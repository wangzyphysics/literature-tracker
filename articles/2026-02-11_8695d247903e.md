## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09526v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Moir\'{e} superlattices formed in WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ heterobilayers have emerged as an exciting platform to explore the quantum many-body physics. The key mechanism is the introduction of moir\'{e} potentials for the band-edge carriers induced by the lateral modulation of interlayer interactions. This trapping potential results in the formation of flat bands, which enhances the strong correlation effect. However, a full understanding of the origin of this intriguing potential remains elusive. In this paper, we present a comprehensive investigation of the origin of moir\'{e} potentials in both R-type and H-type moir\'{e} patterns formed in WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ heterobilayers. We show that both lattice reconstruction and interlayer charge transfer contribute significantly to the formation of moir\'{e} potentials. In particular, the lattice reconstruction induces a nonuniform local strain, which creates an energy modulation of 200 meV for the conduction band-edge state located at WS$_2$ layer and 20 meV for the valence band-edge state located at WSe$_2$ layer. In addition, the lattice reconstruction also introduces a piezopotential energy, whose amplitude ranges from 40 meV to 90 meV depending on the stacking and band-edge carrier. The interlayer charge transfer induces a built-in electric field, resulting in an energy modulation of 80 meV for an R-type moir\'{e} and 40 meV for an H-type moir\'{e}. Taking into account both effects from lattice reconstruction and interlayer charge transfer, the formation of moir\'{e} potential is well understood for both R-type and H-type moir\'{e}s. This trapping potential localizes the wavefunctions of conduction and valence bands around the same moir\'{e} site for an R-type moir\'{e}, while around different moir\'{e} site for an H-type one.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09526v1 公告类型：新 摘要：WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ 异双层中形成的莫尔\'{e}超晶格已成为探索量子多体物理的令人兴奋的平台。关键机制是为带边载流子引入由层间相互作用的横向调制引起的莫尔\'{e}势。这种捕获势导致平带的形成，从而增强了强相关效应。然而，对这种有趣潜力的起源的全面理解仍然难以捉摸。在本文中，我们对 WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ 异双层中形成的 R 型和 H 型莫尔\'{e}图案中莫尔\'{e}电势的起源进行了全面的研究。我们表明，晶格重构和层间电荷转移对莫尔\'{e}电势的形成有显着贡献。特别是，晶格重构会引起不均匀的局部应变，从而为位于 WS$_2$ 层的导带边缘态创建 200 meV 的能量调制，为位于 WSe$_2$ 层的价带边缘态创建 20 meV 的能量调制。此外，晶格重构还引入了压电势能，其幅度范围从 40 meV 到 90 meV，具体取决于堆叠和带边载流子。层间电荷转移会产生内置电场，从而导致 R 型莫尔纹\'{e} 的能量调制为 80 meV，H 型莫尔纹\'{e} 的能量调制为 40 meV。考虑到晶格重构和层间电荷转移的影响，R型和H型莫尔纹的莫尔纹电势的形成都得到了很好的理解。对于 R 型莫尔纹\'{e}，这种捕获势将导带和价带的波函数定位在同一莫尔\'{e} 位点周围，而对于 H 型莫尔纹\'{e} 位点，则将导带和价带的波函数定域在不同的莫尔\'{e} 位点周围。
