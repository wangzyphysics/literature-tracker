## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08239v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Kagome magnets exhibit a range of novel and nontrivial topological properties due to the strong interplay between topology and magnetism, which also extends to their thermoelectric applications. Recent advances in the study of magnetic topological materials have highlighted their intriguing anomalous Hall and thermoelectric effects, arising primarily from large intrinsic Berry curvature. Here, we report observation of a large room-temperature (RT) anomalous Nernst effects (ANE) of S_xy^A ~ 1.3 {\mu}V K^(-1) in the kagome antiferromagnet (AFM) ErMn6Sn6, which is comparable to the largest signals observed in known magnetic materials. Surprisingly, we further found that a significant topological Nernst signal at RT and peaking a maximum of approximately 0.2 {\mu}V K^(-1) at 180 K, exactly coupling with ANE in the spiral AFM state, originates from the real-space nonzero spin chirality caused by incommensurate spin structure. This study demonstrates a potential room-temperature thermoelectric application platform based on Nernst effect, and provides insights for discovering significant anomalous and topological transverse transport effects in the incommensurate AFM system.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08239v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于拓扑和磁性之间的强烈相互作用，Kagome 磁体表现出一系列新颖且重要的拓扑特性，这也延伸到了它们的热电应用。磁性拓扑材料研究的最新进展凸显了它们有趣的反常霍尔效应和热电效应，这些效应主要源于大的固有贝里曲率。在这里，我们报告了在 kagome 反铁磁体 (AFM) ErMn6Sn6 中观察到 S_xy^A ~ 1.3 {\mu}V K^(-1) 的大室温 (RT) 反常能斯特效应 (ANE)，这与已知磁性材料中观察到的最大信号相当。令人惊讶的是，我们进一步发现，RT 处显着的拓扑能斯特信号，在 180 K 处峰值约为 0.2 {\mu}V K^(-1)，与螺旋 AFM 状态下的 ANE 完全耦合，源于由不相称的自旋结构引起的实空间非零自旋手性。这项研究展示了基于能斯特效应的潜在室温热电应用平台，并为发现不相称的 AFM 系统中显着的异常和拓扑横向输运效应提供了见解。
