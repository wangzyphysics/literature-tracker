## Abstract
arXiv:2505.10754v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Continuum computational kinetic plasma models evolve the distribution function of a plasma species $f_s$ on a phase-space grid over time. In many problems of interest the distribution function has limited extent in velocity space; hence, using a uniform, highly refined mesh would be costly and slow. Nonuniform velocity grids can reduce the computational cost by placing more degrees of freedom where $f_s$ is appreciable and fewer where it is not. In this work we introduce a first-of-its kind discontinuous Galerkin approach to nonuniform velocity-space discretization using mapped velocity coordinates. This new method is presented in the context of a gyrokinetic model used to study magnetized plasmas. We create discretizations of collisionless and collisional terms using mappings in a way that exactly conserves particles and energy. Numerical tests of such properties are presented, and we show that this new discretization can reproduce earlier gyrokinetic simulations using grids with up to 6-60 times fewer cells and 22X-60X speed-ups depending on dimensionality, geometry and plasma parameters.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.10754v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：连续计算动力学等离子体模型随时间演化出等离子体物质 $f_s$ 在相空间网格上的分布函数。在许多感兴趣的问题中，分布函数在速度空间中的范围有限；因此，使用统一的、高度精细的网格成本高昂且速度缓慢。非均匀速度网格可以通过在$f_s$明显的地方放置更多的自由度，在$f_s$不明显的地方放置更少的自由度来降低计算成本。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种首创的不连续伽辽金方法，使用映射速度坐标进行非均匀速度空间离散化。这种新方法是在用于研究磁化等离子体的回旋运动模型的背景下提出的。我们使用映射以精确守恒粒子和能量的方式创建无碰撞和碰撞项的离散化。我们对这些特性进行了数值测试，结果表明，这种新的离散化可以使用网格来重现早期的回旋运动模拟，网格数量减少了 6-60 倍，加速速度提高了 22 倍-60 倍，具体取决于维度、几何形状和等离子体参数。
