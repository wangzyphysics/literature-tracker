## Abstract
Papain-like protease (PLpro) and 3-chymotrypsin-like protease (3CLpro or Mpro) are viral enzymes essential for the replication of SARS-CoV-2, the virus causing COVID-19 infection. While 3CLpro has been the main target of many potential antivirals including nirmatrelvir (active ingredient of Paxlovid), PLpro has proven to be more challenging to target and only a handful of inhibitors have been disclosed. However, PLpro inhibitors would enrich the therapeutic arsenal against COVID-19 resistant strains to 3CLpro inhibitors and in future coronavirus pandemics. Combining our experience with 3CLpro covalent inhibitors with our expertise in structure-based covalent drug discovery, we rationally designed PLpro inhibitors achieving a maximum potency (IC50) of 13 µM through fusion of GRL-0617 and VIR-251 PLpro inhibitors. In parallel, we launched an integrated large scale virtual screening/experimental approach, identifying four novel chemical series active at micromolar concentrations against PLpro. We report herein our investigations including rational design, virtual screening, synthesis of selected structures and in vitro assays leading to novel PLpro inhibitors. Surprisingly, these two very distinct approaches led to similar structures.

## 摘要（中文）
木瓜蛋白酶 (PLpro) 和 3-胰凝乳蛋白酶 (3CLpro 或 Mpro) 是 SARS-CoV-2（引起 COVID-19 感染的病毒）复制所必需的病毒酶。虽然 3CLpro 一直是包括 nirmatrelvir（Paxlovid 的活性成分）在内的许多潜在抗病毒药物的主要靶点，但 PLpro 已被证明更具挑战性，并且仅公开了少数抑制剂。然而，PLpro 抑制剂将丰富针对 3CLpro 抑制剂和未来冠状病毒大流行的 COVID-19 耐药菌株的治疗库。结合我们在 3CLpro 共价抑制剂方面的经验和基于结构的共价药物发现的专业知识，我们合理设计了 PLpro 抑制剂，通过 GRL-0617 和 VIR-251 PLpro 抑制剂的融合，实现了 13 µM 的最大效力 (IC50)。与此同时，我们推出了一种集成的大规模虚拟筛选/实验方法，确定了四种在微摩尔浓度下对 PLpro 具有活性的新型化学系列。我们在此报告我们的研究，包括合理设计、虚拟筛选、选定结构的合成以及导致新型 PLpro 抑制剂的体外测定。令人惊讶的是，这两种截然不同的方法产生了相似的结构。
