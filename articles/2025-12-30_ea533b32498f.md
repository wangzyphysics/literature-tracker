## Abstract
arXiv:2404.17641v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Active particles with a (magnetic) dipole moment are of interest for steering self-propelled motion, but also result in novel collective effects due to their dipole-dipole interaction. Here systems of active dipolar particles are studied with Brownian dynamics simulations to systematically characterize the different patterns they form, specifically in the presence of an external (magnetic) field. The combination of three types of order - clustering, orientational alignment and chain formation - is used to classify the patterns observed in these systems. In the presence of an external field, oriented chains and bands are found to be dominant. These structures show some similarities with columnar cluster seen in (passive) ferrofluids and display columnar spacing and number of lanes per cluster that both decrease with increasing field strength.

arXiv:2404.17641v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：具有（磁）偶极矩的活性粒子对于引导自推进运动很有意义，但由于其偶极-偶极相互作用，也导致了新颖的集体效应。这里通过布朗动力学模拟研究活性偶极粒子系统，以系统地表征它们形成的不同模式，特别是在存在外部（磁场）场的情况下。三种类型的顺序（聚类、定向排列和链形成）的组合用于对这些系统中观察到的模式进行分类。在存在外部场的情况下，定向链和带被发现占主导地位。这些结构与（无源）铁磁流体中看到的柱状簇有一些相似之处，并且显示出柱状间距和每个簇的泳道数量，两者都随着场强的增加而减少。
