## Abstract
The coherent-state initial-value representation (IVR) for the semi-classical real-time propagator of a quantum system, developed by Herman and Kluk (HK), is widely used in computational studies of chemical dynamics. On the other hand, the Boltzmann operator e−Ĥ/(kBT), with Ĥ, k B , and T representing the Hamiltonian, Boltzmann constant, and temperature, respectively, plays a crucial role in chemical physics and other branches of quantum physics. One might naturally assume that a semi-classical IVR for the matrix element of this operator in the coordinate representation (i.e., ⟨x̃|e−Ĥ/(kBT)|x⟩, or the imaginary-time propagator) could be derived via a straightforward “real-time → imaginary-time transformation” from the HK IVR of the real-time propagator. However, this is not the case, as such a transformation results in a divergence in the high-temperature limit ( T → ∞ ). In this work, we solve this problem and develop a reasonable HK-like semi-classical IVR for ⟨x̃|e−Ĥ/(kBT)|x⟩, specifically for systems where either the gradient of the potential energy (i.e., the force intensity) has a finite upper bound or the potential becomes harmonic in the long-range limit. The integrand in this IVR is a real Gaussian function of the positions x and x̃, which facilitates its application to realistic problems. Our HK-like IVR is exact for free particles and harmonic oscillators, and its effectiveness for other systems is demonstrated through numerical examples.

## 摘要（中文）
由 Herman 和 Kluk (HK) 开发的量子系统半经典实时传播器的相干态初始值表示 (IVR) 广泛应用于化学动力学的计算研究。另一方面，玻尔兹曼算子 e−Ĥ/(kBT)，其中 Ĥ、k B 和 T 分别代表哈密顿量、玻尔兹曼常数和温度，在化学物理和量子物理的其他分支中起着至关重要的作用。人们可能自然地假设，坐标表示中该算子的矩阵元素的半经典 IVR（即 ⟨x̃|e−Ĥ/(kBT)|x⟩，或虚时间传播器）可以通过实时传播器的 HK IVR 的直接“实时 → 虚时间变换”导出。然而，情况并非如此，因为这种转变会导致高温极限的发散（T→∞）。在这项工作中，我们解决了这个问题，并为 ⟨x̃|e−Ĥ/(kBT)|x⟩ 开发了一个合理的类似 HK 的半经典 IVR，特别是对于势能梯度（即力强度）具有有限上限或势能在长程极限内变成谐波的系统。该 IVR 中的被积函数是位置 x 和 x̃ 的实高斯函数，这有利于其应用于实际问题。我们的类似 HK 的 IVR 对于自由粒子和谐振子来说是精确的，并且通过数值示例证明了它对其他系统的有效性。
