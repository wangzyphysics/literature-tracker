## Abstract
arXiv:2601.23227v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present an open-source Python package, SpinWaveToolkit (SWT), for (semi-)analytical modeling of spin-wave dynamics in thin ferromagnetic films and exchange-coupled magnetic bilayers. SWT combines analytical models based on the Kalinikos-Slavin theory with a semi-analytical dynamic-matrix approach, enabling the calculation of dispersion relations, group velocities, decay lengths, mode profiles, and static equilibrium magnetization states. In addition, SWT implements a quantitative model of micro-focused Brillouin light scattering (BLS) that incorporates vectorial optical focusing, spin-wave Bloch functions, magneto-optical coupling, and Green-function propagation to simulate experimentally measured BLS spectra. The package is validated against finite-element dynamic-matrix simulations performed with TetraX for Damon-Eshbach, backward-volume, forward-volume, and oblique-field geometries, showing excellent agreement while reducing computation times by nearly two orders of magnitude in comparison to the numerical simulations. Thanks to the easiness of the use and fast calculation times, SWT can be used not only for exploratory mapping of the parameter space, but also for the fitting of the measured dispersion relations and related parameters. Thus, it provides a versatile and efficient framework for experiment design, interpretation, and parameter optimization for magnonics research.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.23227v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一个开源 Python 包 SpinWaveToolkit (SWT)，用于铁磁薄膜和交换耦合磁双层中自旋波动力学的（半）分析建模。 SWT 将基于 Kalinikos-Slavin 理论的分析模型与半解析动态矩阵方法相结合，能够计算色散关系、群速度、衰减长度、模式分布和静态平衡磁化状态。此外，SWT 还实现了微聚焦布里渊光散射 (BLS) 的定量模型，该模型结合了矢量光学聚焦、自旋波布洛赫函数、磁光耦合和格林函数传播来模拟实验测量的 BLS 光谱。该软件包针对使用 TetraX 对 Damon-Eshbach、后向体积、前向体积和斜场几何形状执行的有限元动态矩阵模拟进行了验证，显示出良好的一致性，同时与数值模拟相比，计算时间减少了近两个数量级。由于使用方便且计算时间短，SWT 不仅可以用于参数空间的探索性映射，还可以用于测量色散关系和相关参数的拟合。因此，它为磁振子研究的实验设计、解释和参数优化提供了一个通用且有效的框架。
