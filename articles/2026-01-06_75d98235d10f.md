## Abstract
arXiv:2505.11411v5 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Understanding neural network's (NN) generalizability remains a central question in deep learning research. The special phenomenon of grokking, where NNs abruptly generalize long after the training performance reaches a near-perfect level, offers a unique window to investigate the underlying mechanisms of NNs' generalizability. Here we propose an interpretation for grokking by framing it as a computational glass relaxation: viewing NNs as a physical system where parameters are the degrees of freedom and train loss is the system energy, we find memorization process resembles a rapid cooling of liquid into non-equilibrium glassy state at low temperature and the later generalization is like a slow relaxation towards a more stable configuration. This mapping enables us to sample NNs' Boltzmann entropy (states of density) landscape as a function of training loss and test accuracy. Our experiments in transformers on arithmetic tasks suggests that there is NO entropy barrier in the memorization-to-generalization transition of grokking, challenging previous theory that defines grokking as a first-order phase transition. We identify a high-entropy advantage under grokking, an extension of prior work linking entropy to generalizability but much more significant. Inspired by grokking's far-from-equilibrium nature, we develop a toy optimizer WanD based on Wang-landau molecular dynamics, which can eliminate grokking without any constraints and find high-norm generalizing solutions. This provides strictly-defined counterexamples to theory attributing grokking solely to weight norm evolution towards the Goldilocks zone and also suggests new potential ways for optimizer design.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.11411v5 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：理解神经网络（NN）的泛化性仍然是深度学习研究的核心问题。神经网络在训练性能达到近乎完美的水平很久之后突然泛化的特殊现象“grokking”为研究神经网络泛化性的潜在机制提供了一个独特的窗口。在这里，我们提出了一种对 grokking 的解释，将其框架为计算玻璃弛豫：将神经网络视为一个物理系统，其中参数是自由度，训练损失是系统能量，我们发现记忆过程类似于液体在低温下快速冷却到非平衡玻璃态，而后来的概括就像是缓慢弛豫到更稳定的配置。这种映射使我们能够将神经网络的玻尔兹曼熵（密度状态）景观作为训练损失和测试准确性的函数进行采样。我们在 Transformer 上进行算术任务的实验表明，在 grokking 的记忆到泛化的转变中不存在熵障碍，这挑战了之前将 grokking 定义为一阶相变的理论。我们发现了 grokking 下的高熵优势，这是之前将熵与普遍性联系起来的工作的延伸，但更重要。受grokking远离平衡性质的启发，我们开发了一种基于Wang-landau分子动力学的玩具优化器WanD，它可以在没有任何约束的情况下消除grokking并找到高范数泛化解。这为将 grokking 仅归因于向金发姑娘区域的权重范数演化的理论提供了严格定义的反例，并且还为优化器设计提出了新的潜在方法。
