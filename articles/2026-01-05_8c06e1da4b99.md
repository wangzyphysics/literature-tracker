## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00294v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A quantum model exhibits Hilbert space fragmentation (HSF) if its Hilbert space decomposes into exponentially many dynamically disconnected subspaces, known as Krylov subspaces. A model may however have different HSFs depending on the method for identifying them. Here we establish a connection between two vastly distinct methods recently proposed for identifying HSF: the commutant algebra (CA) method and integer characteristic polynomial factorization (ICPF) method. For a Hamiltonian consisting of operators admitting rational number matrix representations, we prove a theorem that, if its center of commutant algebra have all eigenvalues being rational, the HSF from the ICPF method must be equal to or finer than that from the CA method. We show that this condition is satisfied by most known models exhibiting HSF, for which we demonstrate the validity of our theorem. We further discuss representative models for which ICPF and CA methods yield different HSFs. Our results may facilitate the exploration of a unified definition of HSF.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00294v1 公告类型：新 摘要：如果量子模型的希尔伯特空间分解为指数级许多动态断开的子空间（称为克雷洛夫子空间），则量子模型会表现出希尔伯特空间碎片（HSF）。然而，一个模型可能具有不同的 HSF，具体取决于识别它们的方法。在这里，我们在最近提出的两种截然不同的 HSF 识别方法之间建立了联系：交换代数 (CA) 方法和整数特征多项式分解 (ICPF) 方法。对于由允许有理数矩阵表示的算子组成的哈密顿量，我们证明了一个定理：如果其交换代数中心的所有特征值都是有理数，则 ICPF 方法的 HSF 必定等于或优于 CA 方法的 HSF。我们证明大多数已知的 HSF 模型都满足这个条件，为此我们证明了我们定理的有效性。我们进一步讨论 ICPF 和 CA 方法产生不同 HSF 的代表性模型。我们的结果可能有助于探索 HSF 的统一定义。
