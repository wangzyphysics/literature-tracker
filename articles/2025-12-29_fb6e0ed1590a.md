## Abstract
Telechelic polymers are known to form reversible networks through end-group association; however, their application as structuring agents in emulsion-based soft materials remains underexplored. Herein, we systematically investigate the biocompatible amphiphilic triblock copolymer poly(DL-lactic acid)-block-poly(ethylene oxide)-block-poly(DL-lactic acid)(PDLLA-b-PEO-b-PDLLA) as a rheology modifier in toluene-in-water model emulsions. Owing to the selective adsorption of PDLLA end blocks at the oil–water interface and the solvation of the PEO midblock in the aqueous phase, this polymer is expected to form reversible droplet-bridging networks. During the process, the polymer concentration, molecular weight of the mid and end blocks, and the dispersed phase volume fraction were adjusted, and the factors governing network formation were elucidated using oscillatory rheology and stress-relaxation measurements. The results show that anchoring of the PDLLA end blocks and PEO-mediated bridging predominantly control the strength and dynamic reversibility of the network. Step-strain experiments further reveal that the droplet-bridging interactions can be disrupted under large deformation and partially recover when small-strain conditions are restored, confirming the presence of reversible physical associations. Taken together, our findings demonstrate that PDLLA-b-PEO-b-PDLLA can serve as an effective and reprocessable structuring agent for emulsion gels through molecular design, providing guidelines for developing soft materials based on reversible hydrophobic associations.

## 摘要（中文）
已知遥爪聚合物通过端基缔合形成可逆网络；然而，它们作为乳液基软材料结构剂的应用仍未得到充分探索。在此，我们系统地研究了生物相容性两亲性三嵌段共聚物聚（DL-乳酸）-嵌段-聚（环氧乙烷）-嵌段-聚（DL-乳酸）（PDLLA-b-PEO-b-PDLLA）作为甲苯水模型乳液的流变改性剂。由于 PDLLA 末端嵌段在油水界面的选择性吸附以及 PEO 中间嵌段在水相中的溶剂化，该聚合物有望形成可逆的液滴桥接网络。在此过程中，调整了聚合物浓度、中间和末端嵌段的分子量以及分散相体积分数，并利用振荡流变学和应力松弛测量阐明了控制网络形成的因素。结果表明，PDLLA 末端嵌段的锚定和 PEO 介导的桥接主要控制网络的强度和动态可逆性。阶跃应变实验进一步表明，液滴桥接相互作用在大变形下会被破坏，而在小应变条件恢复时会部分恢复，从而证实了可逆物理关联的存在。总而言之，我们的研究结果表明，PDLLA-b-PEO-b-PDLLA 可以通过分子设计作为乳液凝胶的有效且可再加工的结构化剂，为开发基于可逆疏水缔合的软材料提供指导。
