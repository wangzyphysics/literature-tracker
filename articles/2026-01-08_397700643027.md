# The emergence of net chirality in two-dimensional Dirac fermions system with altermagnetic mass

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03564v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In two-dimensional lattice systems, massless Dirac fermions undergo doubling, leading to the cancellation of net chirality. We demonstrate that the recently discovered altermagnetism can induce a unique mass term, the altermagnetic mass term, which gaps out Dirac cones with one chirality while maintaining the other gapless, leading to the emergence of net chirality. The surviving gapless Dirac cones retain identical winding numbers and exhibit the quantum anomalous Hall effect in the presence of the trivial constant mass term. When subjected to an external magnetic field, the altermagnetic mass induces Landau level asymmetry in Dirac fermions, resulting in fully valley-polarized quantum Hall edge states. Our findings reveal that Dirac fermions with the altermagnetic mass harbor rich physical phenomena warranting further exploration.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03564v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在二维晶格系统中，无质量狄拉克费米子经历倍增，导致净手性消失。我们证明，最近发现的交变磁力可以诱导出一种独特的质量项，即交变磁质量项，它使具有一种手性的狄拉克锥有间隙，同时保持另一种无间隙，从而导致净手性的出现。幸存的无间隙狄拉克锥保留了相同的绕数，并在存在微不足道的常数质量项时表现出量子反常霍尔效应。当受到外部磁场时，交变磁质量会引起狄拉克费米子中的朗道能级不对称性，从而产生完全谷极化的量子霍尔边缘态。我们的研究结果表明，具有交变磁质量的狄拉克费米子蕴藏着丰富的物理现象，值得进一步探索。
