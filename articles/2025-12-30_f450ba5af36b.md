## Abstract
arXiv:2412.12424v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The copper metaborate CuB_2O_4(CBO) has been studied within the parametric space of magnetic field (B) and temperature (T), showing a series of distinct phases, including commensurate and incommensurate magnetic orders, as well as magnetic soliton phases. However, no single study has simultaneously demonstrated all these phases. moreover, the existence of additional phases below 2 K or above 2 T for B perpenducular to c has been scarcely explored. This work presents the first comprehensive construction of the complex B-T phase diagram for T = 0.1 to 25 K and B = 0 to 9 T (B perpenducular to c), utilizing thermodynamic probes, magnetic measurements, and neutron scattering on a single batch of CBO crystals. Our findings elucidate new magnetic phases and regimes. First-principles calculations help to gain a deeper understanding of this complex phase diagram. The emergence of various phases within such a narrow B and T regime is attributed to two primary mechanisms: the delicate competition between ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic interactions, and the weak exchange and superexchange interactions within and between the Cu(A) and Cu(B) sublattices. This competition also leads to a magnetically frustrated ground state at absolute zero where the total magnetic entropy release from high to low temperatures is only 0.76 Rln2, lower than Rln2 expected for copper spin 1/2. Using CBO as a model system, the present work elucidates the intricate mechanisms underlying the formation of complex magnetic phases.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2412.12424v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在磁场 (B) 和温度 (T) 的参数空间内研究了偏硼酸铜 CuB_2O_4(CBO)，显示出一系列不同的相，包括相称和不相称磁序以及磁孤子相。然而，没有一项研究能够同时证明所有这些阶段。此外，对于垂直于 c 的 B 而言，是否存在低于 2 K 或高于 2 T 的附加相几乎没有被探索过。这项工作利用热力学探针、磁测量和单批 CBO 晶体上的中子散射，首次全面构建了 T = 0.1 至 25 K 和 B = 0 至 9 T（B 垂直于 c）的复杂 B-T 相图。我们的发现阐明了新的磁相和状态。第一原理计算有助于更深入地理解这个复杂的相图。在如此狭窄的 B 和 T 范围内出现各种相归因于两个主要机制：铁磁和反铁磁相互作用之间的微妙竞争，以及 Cu(A) 和 Cu(B) 亚晶格内部和之间的弱交换和超交换相互作用。这种竞争还导致绝对零的磁挫基态，其中从高温到低温释放的总磁熵仅为 0.76 Rln2，低于铜自旋 1/2 预期的 Rln2。目前的工作以 CBO 作为模型系统，阐明了复杂磁相形成的复杂机制。
