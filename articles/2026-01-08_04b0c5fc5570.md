## Abstract
Sequence-defined polymers demand synthetic strategies that deliver high sequence fidelity, structural complexity, and access to high–molar mass in a limited number of operations. Here we report a hybrid growth platform that combines an IEG-type Cu(I)-catalyzed azide–alkyne cycloaddition (CuAAC) backbone with a protecting-group-free ISG step based on acyl azide esterification. In this orthogonal pairing, acyl azides and alcohols serve as built-in acyl-transfer partners, enabling clean ester formation under mild conditions without external activating agents, while CuAAC provides a robust triazole-forming step with excellent chemoselectivity. Using two bifunctional monomers bearing hydroxyl/alkynyl and azide/acyl-azide motifs, we construct sequence-defined oligotriazoles with high–molar mass in a small number of iterative cycles. Expansion to diversified monomers further demonstrates the modularity of the approach and its capacity to encode more complex architectures. Tandem mass spectrometry reveals characteristic and diverse cleavage patterns associated with ester and triazole linkages, underscoring the potential of this acyl azide/CuAAC platform for digital polymer synthesis and readout.

## 摘要（中文）
序列定义的聚合物需要合成策略，以提供高序列保真度、结构复杂性以及在有限次数的操作中获得高摩尔质量。在这里，我们报告了一种混合生长平台，该平台将 IEG 型 Cu(I) 催化的叠氮-炔环加成 (CuAAC) 主链与基于酰基叠氮化物酯化的无保护基 ISG 步骤相结合。在这种正交配对中，酰基叠氮化物和醇作为内置的酰基转移伴侣，能够在温和条件下干净地形成酯，无需外部活化剂，而 CuAAC 提供了稳健的三唑形成步骤，具有优异的化学选择性。使用带有羟基/炔基和叠氮/酰基叠氮化物基序的两种双功能单体，我们在少量迭代循环中构建了具有高摩尔质量的序列定义的低聚三唑。扩展到多样化单体进一步证明了该方法的模块化及其编码更复杂架构的能力。串联质谱揭示了与酯和三唑键相关的特征性和多样化的裂解模式，强调了该酰基叠氮化物/CuAAC平台用于数字聚合物合成和读出的潜力。
