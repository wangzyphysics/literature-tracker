# Emergent spin-resolved electronic charge density waves and pseudogap phenomena from strong $d$-wave altermagnetism

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11694v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Inspired by recent discovery of metallic $d$-wave altermagnetism in KV$_2$Se$_2$O, we develop a self-consistent microscopic many-body calculation of density-wave order for an itinerant altermagnetic metal. We show that the strong $d$-wave spin-momentum locking inherent to the altermagnetic band structure reconstructs the Fermi surface into spin-selective quasi-1D open sheets. This unique topology of Fermi surface drives an instability toward spin-resolved electronic charge density waves (CDWs), in which the ordering wave vectors for spin-up and spin-down electrons condense along two mutually orthogonal directions, forming spin-resolved stripe phases. As a consequence, this results in pronounced gap openings near the Fermi surface, and the superposition of these spin-resolved stripe orders leads to a checkerboard CDW in the charge channel and an antiphase spin-density-wave modulation in the spin channel. Upon increasing temperature, the density-wave order melts at $T_c$ due to thermal phase fluctuation while the gap opening persists, giving rise to a robust pseudogap regime, which eventually closes at a higher temperature $T_g$. The resulting simulations quantitatively reproduce the key features observed in the spectroscopic measurements, offering a consistent and generic understanding of the reported phenomena in KV$_2$Se$_2$O and, more broadly, in metallic altermagnets with strong spin-momentum locking.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11694v1 公告类型：新 摘要：受最近在 KV$_2$Se$_2$O 中发现的金属 $d$ 波交变磁性的启发，我们开发了一种自洽的流动交变磁性金属密度波阶的微观多体计算。我们表明，交变磁带结构固有的强 $d$ 波自旋动量锁定将费米表面重建为自旋选择性准一维开放片。费米表面的这种独特拓扑导致了自旋分辨电子电荷密度波（CDW）的不稳定性，其中自旋向上和自旋向下电子的有序波矢量沿着两个相互正交的方向凝聚，形成自旋分辨条纹相。结果，这导致费米表面附近出现明显的能隙开口，并且这些自旋分辨条纹顺序的叠加导致电荷通道中的棋盘CDW和自旋通道中的反相自旋密度波调制。当温度升高时，由于热相位波动，密度波阶在 $T_c$ 处熔化，同时间隙打开仍然存在，从而产生强大的赝间隙状态，最终在更高的温度 $T_g$ 下关闭。由此产生的模拟定量地再现了光谱测量中观察到的关键特征，为 KV$_2$Se$_2$O 以及更广泛的具有强自旋动量锁定的金属交替磁体中报告的现象提供了一致和通用的理解。
