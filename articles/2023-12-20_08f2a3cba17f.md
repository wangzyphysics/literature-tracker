## Abstract
Author(s): Koji Azuma, Sophia E. Economou, David Elkouss, Paul Hilaire, Liang Jiang, Hoi-Kwong Lo, and Ilan Tzitrin Quantum technology is now at a point where practical work can begin on creating the quantum internet. However, numerous challenges must be overcome before this vision becomes a reality. A global-scale quantum internet requires the development of the quantum repeater, a device that stores and manipulates qubits while interacting with or emitting entangled photons. This review examines different approaches to quantum repeaters and networks, covering their conceptual frameworks, architectures, and current progress in experimental implementation. [Rev. Mod. Phys. 95, 045006] Published Wed Dec 20, 2023

作者：Koji Azuma、Sophia E. Economou、David Elkouss、Paul Hilaire、Liang Jiang、Hoi-Kwong Lo 和 Ilan Tzitrin 量子技术现在正处于可以开始创建量子互联网的实际工作的阶段。然而，在这一愿景成为现实之前，必须克服许多挑战。全球规模的量子互联网需要开发量子中继器，这是一种在与纠缠光子交互或发射纠缠光子的同时存储和操纵量子位的设备。这篇综述研究了量子中继器和网络的不同方法，涵盖了它们的概念框架、架构和实验实施的当前进展。 [牧师。模组。物理。 95, 045006] 2023 年 12 月 20 日星期三发布
