## Abstract
arXiv:2508.20862v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Optically-pumped atomic magnetometers have previously been used in arrays to reject interference from far away sources and enable the sensitive detection of local sources of radio frequency (RF) signals, useful, for instance, in the detection of low field NMR signals in an unshielded environment. We now demonstrate a complementary scheme in which four magnetometer measurements are used to locate in three dimensions a nearby radio frequency source. The methodology relies on the measurement of a radio frequency vector at two different positions and modeling the source as a magnetic dipole of known orientation. In contrast to coil detection, magnetometers have the advantage of measuring signals in a 2D plane, and do not inductively couple to their environment or each other, making them a strong candidate for localization of hidden RF sources. For this demonstration, we use only a single RF magnetometer to make four measurements of a synchronous and oriented dipole source, but it is to be expected that this could be replaced by four magnetometers working simultaneously. In addition, this work is greatly aided by the introduction of a fully integrated magnetometer, in which all optics, including lasers, are safely enclosed into a compact head with flexible wired connections. The portable, as well as safe, nature of the sensor make it quite valuable for in the field work.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.20862v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：光泵原子磁力计之前已在阵列中使用，以抑制来自远距离源的干扰，并能够灵敏地检测本地射频 (RF) 信号源，例如，可用于在非屏蔽环境中检测低场 NMR 信号。我们现在演示一种补充方案，其中使用四个磁力计测量来在三维空间中定位附近的射频源。该方法依赖于在两个不同位置测量射频矢量并将源建模为已知方向的磁偶极子。与线圈检测相比，磁力计的优点是可以在 2D 平面中测量信号，并且不会与其环境或相互之间产生感应耦合，这使得它们成为隐藏射频源定位的有力候选者。在本次演示中，我们仅使用单个射频磁力计对同步定向偶极子源进行四次测量，但预计这可以由四个同时工作的磁力计代替。此外，完全集成磁力计的引入极大地帮助了这项工作，其中包括激光器在内的所有光学器件都安全地封装在具有灵活有线连接的紧凑头中。传感器的便携性和安全性使其对于现场工作非常有价值。
