## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13893v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The design of high-performance optical systems remains a specialized domain gated by the limited availability of expert engineers, creating a bottleneck that stalls innovation despite the growing demand for imaging hardware. While deep learning has improved parameter optimization, it has yet to address the fundamental challenge of conceptualizing valid optical architectures from functional requirements. Here, we present an end-to-end generative framework that couples the semantic reasoning of Large Language Models (LLMs) with a differentiable ray-tracing engine to democratize the synthesis of diffraction-limited optical prescriptions. By treating optical design as a semantic-to-physical translation task, the system autonomously interprets prompts ranging from high-level end-user requests to rigorous technical specifications. We demonstrate the framework's versatility across three distinct regimes: (1) finite-conjugate industrial metrology systems, where the model autonomously enforces application-specific constraints such as telecentricity to achieve diffraction-limited performance; (2) a suite of infrared objectives (NIR, SWIR, and LWIR), demonstrating the framework's ability to synthesize valid topologies and optical prescriptions for non-visible spectral bands, and (3) complex aspheric mobile lenses, where the system successfully navigates the high-dimensional optimization landscape to produce high-resolution designs suitable for modern sensors. Validated against industry-standard simulation tools, these results establish a new paradigm for automated optical engineering, bridging the gap between semantic intent and physical realization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13893v1 公告类型：新 摘要：高性能光学系统的设计仍然是一个专业领域，受到专家工程师有限的限制，尽管对成像硬件的需求不断增长，但仍造成了阻碍创新的瓶颈。虽然深度学习改进了参数优化，但它尚未解决从功能需求概念化有效光学架构的根本挑战。在这里，我们提出了一个端到端的生成框架，它将大型语言模型（LLM）的语义推理与可微分的光线追踪引擎结合起来，以民主化衍射限制光学处方的合成。通过将光学设计视为语义到物理的转换任务，系统可以自动解释从高级最终用户请求到严格技术规范的提示。我们展示了该框架在三个不同领域的多功能性：(1) 有限共轭工业计量系统，其中模型自动执行特定于应用的约束，例如远心度，以实现衍射极限性能； (2) 一套红外物镜（NIR、SWIR 和 LWIR），展示了该框架合成有效拓扑和非可见光谱带光学处方的能力；(3) 复杂的非球面移动镜头，系统成功地导航高维优化环境，生成适合现代传感器的高分辨率设计。这些结果经过行业标准仿真工具的验证，为自动化光学工程建立了新的范例，弥合了语义意图和物理实现之间的差距。
