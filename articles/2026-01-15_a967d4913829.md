## Abstract
arXiv:2510.15960v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: This work contributes to advancing sustainable energy and waste management strategies by investigating the thermochemical conversion of food-based biomass through pyrolysis, highlighting the role of artificial intelligence (AI) in enhancing process modelling accuracy and optimization efficiency. The main objective is to explore the potential of underutilized biomass resources, such as spent coffee grounds (SCG) and date seeds (DS), for sustainable hydrogen production. Specifically, it aims to optimize the pyrolysis process while evaluating the performance of these resources both individually and as blends. Proximate, ultimate, fibre, TGA/DTG, kinetic, thermodynamic, and Py-Micro GC analyses were conducted for pure DS, SCG, and blends (75% DS - 25% SCG, 50% DS - 50% SCG, 25% DS - 75% SCG). Blend 3 offered superior hydrogen yield potential but had the highest activation energy (Ea: 313.24 kJ/mol), while Blend 1 exhibited the best activation energy value (Ea: 161.75 kJ/mol). The kinetic modelling based on isoconversional methods (KAS, FWO, Friedman) identified KAS as the most accurate. These approaches provide a detailed understanding of the pyrolysis process, with particular emphasis on the integration of artificial intelligence. An LSTM model trained with lignocellulosic data predicted TGA curves with exceptional accuracy (R^2: 0.9996-0.9998).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.15960v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：这项工作通过研究基于食品的生物质通过热解的热化学转化，有助于推进可持续能源和废物管理策略，强调人工智能 (AI) 在提高过程建模准确性和优化效率方面的作用。主要目标是探索未充分利用的生物质资源（例如废咖啡渣（SCG）和枣子（DS））用于可持续氢气生产的潜力。具体来说，它的目的是优化热解过程，同时评估这些资源单独和混合的性能。对纯 DS、SCG 和混合物（75% DS - 25% SCG、50% DS - 50% SCG、25% DS - 75% SCG）进行了近似分析、最终分析、纤维分析、TGA/DTG、动力学分析、热力学分析和 Py-Micro GC 分析。共混物 3 具有优异的产氢潜力，但具有最高的活化能（Ea：313.24 kJ/mol），而共混物 1 则表现出最佳的活化能值（Ea：161.75 kJ/mol）。基于等转换方法（KAS、FWO、Friedman）的动力学模型确定 KAS 是最准确的。这些方法提供了对热解过程的详细了解，特别强调人工智能的集成。使用木质纤维素数据训练的 LSTM 模型以极高的准确度预测 TGA 曲线 (R^2: 0.9996-0.9998)。
