## Abstract
arXiv:2505.17212v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Altermagnetic materials combine compensated magnetic order with momentum-dependent spin splitting, offering a fundamentally new route for spintronic functionality beyond conventional ferromagnets and antiferromagnets. While most studies have focused on three-dimensional compounds, the emergence of altermagnetism in few-layer two-dimensional materials remains largely unexplored. Here, we demonstrate that bilayer MnPS$_3$, a prototypical 2D van der Waals magnet, can host stacking-induced altermagnetic phases. Using density-functional theory and spin-Laue symmetry analysis, we show that interlayer spin alignment and lateral displacement act as coupled symmetry control parameters that switch the system between Type II (collinear AFM) and Type III (altermagnetic) phases. Our systematic exploration reveals how specific stacking geometries enable momentum-dependent spin polarization without net magnetization, even in the absence of spin-orbit coupling. These results establish stacking engineering as a powerful, purely structural route for designing tunable altermagnetic states in 2D magnets, opening pathways toward symmetry-driven spintronic and magnetoelectronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.17212v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：交变磁材料将补偿磁序与动量相关的自旋分裂相结合，为自旋电子功能提供了一条超越传统铁磁体和反铁磁体的全新途径。虽然大多数研究都集中在三维化合物上，但少层二维材料中交变磁性的出现在很大程度上仍未得到探索。在这里，我们证明了双层 MnPS$_3$（一种典型的二维范德华磁体）可以容纳堆叠诱导的交变磁相。利用密度泛函理论和自旋劳厄对称分析，我们表明层间自旋排列和横向位移作为耦合对称控制参数，使系统在 II 型（共线 AFM）和 III 型（交磁）相之间切换。我们的系统探索揭示了即使在没有自旋轨道耦合的情况下，特定的堆叠几何形状如何在没有净磁化的情况下实现动量相关的自旋极化。这些结果确立了堆叠工程作为设计二维磁体中可调谐交变磁态的强大、纯结构途径，为对称驱动的自旋电子和磁电子器件开辟了道路。
