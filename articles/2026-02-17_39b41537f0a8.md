## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14485v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The interplay between conventional superconductivity and unconventional magnetism offers an exciting platform for realizing exotic superconducting phenomena. Here, we investigate Josephson effects in planar and vertical junctions based on CsV$_2$Te$_2$O-family materials, which host hidden $d$-wave altermagnetism with G-type antiferromagnetic order. In monolayer-based planar junctions, the quasi-1D, nearly flat, spin-polarized bands of the altermagnet, when coupled to $s$-wave superconductors, produce a \textit{fully} spin-polarized supercurrent with strong directional anisotropy -- a spin-selective Josephson effect. In multilayers, we uncover an \textit{altermagnetic even-odd effect}: spin-polarized supercurrents persist only in odd-layer planar junctions but cancel exactly in even layers. Thus, layer parity acts as a switch for spin-polarized supercurrent. In vertical junctions, odd-layer barriers enhance equal-spin triplet transport while even layers favor opposite-spin transport, yielding a robust period-two oscillation in the total supercurrent with layer number. These layer-parity-dependent responses represent a general even-odd effect in hidden altermagnets, applicable to diverse magnetic and transport phenomena.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14485v1 公告类型：新 摘要：传统超导性和非常规磁性之间的相互作用为实现奇异的超导现象提供了一个令人兴奋的平台。在这里，我们研究基于 CsV$_2$Te$_2$O 系列材料的平面和垂直结中的约瑟夫森效应，该材料具有 G 型反铁磁序的隐藏 $d$ 波交变磁性。在基于单层的平面结中，互变磁体的准一维、近乎平坦的自旋极化带，当与$s$波超导体耦合时，产生具有强方向各向异性的\textit{完全}自旋极化超电流——一种自旋选择性约瑟夫森效应。在多层结构中，我们发现了一种\textit{交替磁偶奇效应}：自旋极化超电流仅在奇数层平面结中持续存在，但在偶数层中完全抵消。因此，层奇偶校验充当自旋极化超电流的开关。在垂直结中，奇数层势垒增强了等自旋三重态输运，而偶数层则有利于相反自旋输运，从而在总超流中随层数产生强大的第二周期振荡。这些依赖于层奇偶性的响应代表了隐藏交替磁体中的一般奇偶效应，适用于各种磁性和传输现象。
