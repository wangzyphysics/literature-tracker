## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09301v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The rotational diffusion of a rigid spherical body decorated with dimers in an explicit fluid environment is reported. This model acts as a simplified representation of an enveloped virus bearing peplomers immersed in a coarse-grained fluid. Using dissipative particle dynamics, we explicitly study the hydrodynamic effects on the rotational diffusion of this virus-inspired particle subjected to oscillatory shear flow and confined between two solid-like surfaces. Since the rotational response depends on the type of imposed flow, we first characterize the oscillatory shear, identifying distinct flow regimes in terms of the so-called P\'eclet number, $Pe$. Our findings indicate that, under confinement, the rotational diffusivity is strongly modulated by the oscillatory flow amplitude and only weakly affected by the number of peplomers, since their effect is mainly determined by their dimeric structure and associated effective size. For high $Pe$, the rotational diffusion coefficient, $D_{r}$, tends to decrease as the number of peplomers ($N_{s}$) increases, whereas at low $Pe$, rotational diffusion becomes weakly dependent on the number of peplomers. However, at intermediate values of $Pe$, the interplay between oscillatory forcing and thermal fluctuations prevents the emergence of a clear trend between $D_{r}$ and $N_{s}$. Our results provide a clear picture of how, in confined environments, the interplay between fluid flow and thermal fluctuations affects the rotational diffusion of spiked particles, thereby helping to explain how fluid conditions can modify the alignment of peplomers with their potential targets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09301v1 公告类型：新 摘要：报告了在显式流体环境中装饰有二聚体的刚性球体的旋转扩散。该模型是浸入粗粒流体中的带有包膜病毒的简化表示。利用耗散粒子动力学，我们明确研究了这种受病毒启发的粒子受到振荡剪切流并限制在两个类固体表面之间的旋转扩散的流体动力学效应。由于旋转响应取决于施加的流动类型，因此我们首先表征振荡剪切，根据所谓的 P\'eclet 数 $Pe$ 识别不同的流动状态。我们的研究结果表明，在限制下，旋转扩散率受到振荡流振幅的强烈调节，并且仅受聚合物数量的微弱影响，因为它们的影响主要由其二聚体结构和相关的有效尺寸决定。对于高 $Pe$，旋转扩散系数 $D_{r}$ 往往会随着聚合物数量 ($N_{s}$) 的增加而减小，而在低 $Pe$ 下，旋转扩散对聚合物数量的依赖性变得较弱。然而，在 $Pe$ 的中间值下，振荡强迫和热波动之间的相互作用阻止了 $D_{r}$ 和 $N_{s}$ 之间出现明显的趋势。我们的结果清楚地表明，在有限的环境中，流体流动和热波动之间的相互作用如何影响尖峰颗粒的旋转扩散，从而有助于解释流体条件如何改变聚合物与其潜在目标的排列。
