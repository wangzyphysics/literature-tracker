## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08113v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Understanding how nanocluster cocatalysts modify the electronic structure of III-nitride surfaces is central to the rational design of efficient photocatalytic interfaces. Here, we establish design principles for nanocluster cocatalysts on GaN-based semiconductors by systematically analyzing the spatially resolved electronic structure of GaN-, InGaN-, and ScGaN-based slabs decorated with six-atom elemental nanoclusters. Using a region-resolved projected local density of states (PLDOS) framework, we reveal that semiconductor-nanocluster interfaces operate as laterally heterogeneous electronic systems, in which nanocluster-covered regions govern charge injection and band bending, while uncovered nitride regions retain surface states that facilitate surface activation. We further show that cocatalyst effectiveness is controlled not only by hydrogen adsorption energy, but also by interfacial electrostatics, including band alignment, metal-induced gap-state suppression, and in-plane dipoles, with the semiconductor substrate defining the baseline electronic regime. Machine-learning regression models trained on physically motivated global and region-specific descriptors quantify the relative importance of these mechanisms and their correlation with hydrogen adsorption energetics. Together, this work provides transferable design principles for nanocluster cocatalysts on III-nitrides and a generalizable first-principles framework for studying spatially heterogeneous semiconductor-nanocluster interfaces.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08113v1 公告类型：新 摘要：了解纳米团簇助催化剂如何改变 III 族氮化物表面的电子结构对于合理设计高效光催化界面至关重要。在这里，我们通过系统分析装饰有六原子元素纳米团簇的 GaN、InGaN 和 ScGaN 基板的空间分辨电子结构，建立了 GaN 基半导体纳米团簇助催化剂的设计原则。使用区域解析的投影局域密度（PLDOS）框架，我们揭示了半导体-纳米团簇界面作为横向异质电子系统运行，其中纳米团簇覆盖的区域控制电荷注入和能带弯曲，而未覆盖的氮化物区域保留有利于表面激活的表面态。我们进一步表明，助催化剂的有效性不仅受到氢吸附能的控制，而且还受到界面静电的控制，包括能带排列、金属诱导的间隙态抑制和面内偶极子，其中半导体衬底定义了基线电子状态。基于物理驱动的全局和特定区域描述符训练的机器学习回归模型量化了这些机制的相对重要性及其与氢吸附能量的相关性。总之，这项工作为 III 族氮化物上的纳米团簇助催化剂提供了可转移的设计原理，并为研究空间异质半导体纳米团簇界面提供了可推广的第一原理框架。
