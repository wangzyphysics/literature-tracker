## Abstract
arXiv:2601.11388v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Theoretical spectroscopy, and more generally, electronic-structure theory, are powerful concepts for describing the complex many-body interactions in materials. They comprise a variety of methods that can capture all aspects, from ground-state properties to lattice excitations to different types of light-matter interaction, including time-resolved variants. Modern electronic-structure codes implement either a few or several of these methods. Among them, exciting is an all-electron full-potential package that has a very rich portfolio of all levels of theory, with a particular focus on excitations. It implements the linearized augmented planewave plus local orbital (LAPW+LO) basis, which is known as the gold standard for solving the Kohn-Sham equations of density-functional theory (DFT). Based on this, it also offers benchmark-quality results for a wide range of excited-state methods. In this review, we provide a comprehensive overview of the features implemented in exciting in recent years, accompanied by short summaries on the state of the art of the underlying methodologies. They comprise DFT and time-dependent DFT (TDDFT), density-functional perturbation theory (DFPT) for phonons and electron-phonon coupling, and many-body perturbation theory in terms of the $GW$ approach and the Bethe-Salpeter equation (BSE). Moreover, exciting can handle resonant inelastic x-ray scattering (RIXS), pump-probe spectroscopy as well as exciton-phonon coupling (EXPC). Finally, we cover workflows and a view on data and machine learning (ML). All aspects are demonstrated with examples for scientifically relevant materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.11388v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：理论光谱学，更一般地说，电子结构理论，是描述材料中复杂的多体相互作用的强大概念。它们包含多种方法，可以捕获所有方面，从基态特性到晶格激发，再到不同类型的光与物质相互作用，包括时间分辨的变体。现代电子结构代码实现了这些方法中的一些或几种。其中，令人兴奋的是全电子全势包，它拥有非常丰富的各个层次的理论组合，特别关注激发。它实现了线性化增强平面波加局域轨道 (LAPW+LO) 基础，这被称为求解密度泛函理论 (DFT) Kohn-Sham 方程的黄金标准。基于此，它还为各种激发态方法提供基准质量的结果。在这篇评论中，我们对近年来令人兴奋的功能实现进行了全面的概述，并对底层方法的最新技术进行了简短的总结。它们包括 DFT 和瞬态 DFT (TDDFT)、声子和电子声子耦合的密度泛函微扰理论 (DFPT)，以及基于 $GW$ 方法和 Bethe-Salpeter 方程 (BSE) 的多体微扰理论。此外，Exciting 还可以处理共振非弹性 X 射线散射 (RIXS)、泵浦探针光谱以及激子-声子耦合 (EXPC)。最后，我们介绍工作流程以及数据和机器学习 (ML) 的观点。所有方面都通过科学相关材料的示例进行了演示。
