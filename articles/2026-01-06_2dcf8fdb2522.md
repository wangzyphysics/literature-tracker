## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01531v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The competition between Coulomb repulsion and kinetic energy in correlated systems can allow electrons to crystallize into Wigner solids. Despite researches across diverse two-dimensional Wigner platforms, the microscopic melting processes through possible intermediate phases remains largely unknown. Here, we present the visualization of electron-lattice melting in monolayer VCl3 on graphite, where two Wigner crystals coexist with markedly different critical temperatures Tc and lattice periods as stabilized by high magnetic field. One Wigner crystal possesses both record-high Tc and electron density, and undergoes melting through an intermediate nematic phase upon decreasing magnetic field. In contrast, the other Wigner crystal with a lower Tc yields a different intermediate phase during melting, exhibiting an anomalous electron liquid with an energy-independent modulation period. First-principles calculations corroborate the band-selective occupations of interface-transferred electrons in the formation of dual Wigner crystals. Our atomically resolved intermediate phases provide crucial insights into the microscopic melting pathways of Wigner crystals, enabling a phase diagram parameterized by both quantum and thermal fluctuations.

arXiv:2601.01531v1 公告类型：新 摘要：相关系统中库仑斥力和动能之间的竞争可以使电子结晶成维格纳固体。尽管对不同的二维维格纳平台进行了研究，但通过可能的中间相进行的微观熔化过程仍然很大程度上未知。在这里，我们展示了石墨上单层 VCl3 电子晶格熔化的可视化，其中两个维格纳晶体以明显不同的临界温度 Tc 和晶格周期共存，并通过高磁场稳定。一种维格纳晶体具有创纪录的高 Tc 和电子密度，并在磁场减弱时通过中间向列相熔化。相比之下，另一种具有较低 Tc 的维格纳晶体在熔化过程中产生不同的中间相，表现出具有与能量无关的调制周期的反常电子液体。第一性原理计算证实了界面转移电子在双维格纳晶体形成过程中的能带选择性占据。我们的原子分辨中间相为维格纳晶体的微观熔化路径提供了重要的见解，从而实现了由量子和热波动参数化的相图。
