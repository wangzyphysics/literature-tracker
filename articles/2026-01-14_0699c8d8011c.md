## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08716v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Optically pumped atomic magnetometers (OPAMs) offer high sensitivity at room temperature and are increasingly considered for portable magnetic sensing in geomagnetic-field environments. Here we report a handheld-scale, single-beam scalar $^{87}$Rb OPAM with a sensor-head volume of approximately 110 mL. The device operates in an all-optical Bell-Bloom configuration and uses digital lock-in, dispersive tracking of the $^{87}$Rb Larmor resonance, implemented with a hybrid electronics stack that combines in-house control hardware with commercial modules. A single frequency-modulated laser beam performs both pumping and probing without RF coils. In an unshielded indoor environment, the magnetometer exhibits a noise floor below 6 pT/$\sqrt{\mathrm{Hz}}$ near 80 Hz and a measurement bandwidth of 200 Hz. In an unshielded Earth-field deployment, we detect repeatable transient magnetic signatures from a controlled elevator motion sequence and quantify standoff observability over sensor-elevator distances from 1.25 m to 10 m. These results show that compact scalar OPAMs can provide bandwidth and range-resolved event sensitivity suitable for field-deployable magnetic anomaly detection and infrastructure monitoring in realistic geomagnetic environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08716v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光泵原子磁力计 (OPAM) 在室温下具有高灵敏度，并且越来越多地被考虑用于地磁场环境中的便携式磁传感。在这里，我们报告了一种手持式单光束标量 $^{87}$Rb OPAM，其传感器头体积约为 110 mL。该设备采用全光学 Bell-Bloom 配置运行，并使用数字锁定、色散跟踪 $^{87}$Rb 拉莫尔共振，通过将内部控制硬件与商业模块相结合的混合电子堆栈实现。单个调频激光束无需射频线圈即可执行泵浦和探测。在无屏蔽的室内环境中，磁力计在 80 Hz 附近的本底噪声低于 6 pT/$\sqrt{\mathrm{Hz}}$，测量带宽为 200 Hz。在非屏蔽地球场部署中，我们从受控电梯运动序列中检测可重复的瞬态磁特征，并量化 1.25 m 至 10 m 传感器-电梯距离内的隔离可观测性。这些结果表明，紧凑型标量 OPAM 可以提供带宽和范围分辨事件灵敏度，适用于现实地磁环境中的现场部署磁异常检测和基础设施监测。
