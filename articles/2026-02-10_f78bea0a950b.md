## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08743v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Plasma sheaths play a central role in plasma-surface interactions, yet their thickness remains challenging to measure experimentally. Although classical analytical models such as the Child-Langmuir (CL) sheath model provide clear predictions for sheath thickness, experimental validation has been limited because most diagnostics either rely on indirect, multi-step inference (e.g., Langmuir probes) or require invasive and technically demanding techniques. In this work, we demonstrate that the plasma impedance probe (PIP), when operated with a controlled DC bias, enables relatively direct, model-informed measurements of sheath thickness that are reasonably straightforward to implement experimentally. Across a range of discharge conditions, biased-PIP sheath thickness measurements are found to follow CL scaling closely, requiring a single, consistent empirical correction factor of $\alpha \approx 0.74$ to reconcile the measured thickness with CL predictions. Concurrent measurements of plasma density and electron damping show that probe biasing does not significantly perturb the bulk plasma density, supporting the validity of the biased-PIP approach. Building on this validation, we leverage the empirically determined $\alpha$ factor to extend the floating (unbiased) PIP analysis to obtain model-dependent estimates of electron temperature and plasma potential without electrical biasing. A side-by-side comparison demonstrates close agreement between floating-PIP results and those obtained from a biased Langmuir probe. Taken together, these results establish the PIP as a complementary diagnostic to the Langmuir probe, expanding the range of accessible plasma measurements while providing experimental support for classical sheath models.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08743v1 公告类型：新 摘要：等离子体鞘在等离子体-表面相互作用中发挥着核心作用，但其厚度仍然难以通过实验测量。尽管 Child-Langmuir (CL) 鞘层模型等经典分析模型可以清晰地预测鞘层厚度，但实验验证受到限制，因为大多数诊断要么依赖于间接、多步骤推理（例如 Langmuir 探针），要么需要侵入性且技术要求较高的技术。在这项工作中，我们证明等离子体阻抗探针（PIP）在受控直流偏压下运行时，可以对鞘层厚度进行相对直接、基于模型的测量，并且在实验上实现起来相当简单。在一系列放电条件下，偏置 PIP 护套厚度测量结果紧密遵循 CL 缩放比例，需要一个一致的经验校正因子 $\alpha \approx 0.74$ 来使测量的厚度与 CL 预测相一致。等离子体密度和电子阻尼的同时测量表明，探针偏置不会显着扰动整体等离子体密度，支持偏置 PIP 方法的有效性。在此验证的基础上，我们利用经验确定的 $\alpha$ 因子来扩展浮动（无偏）PIP 分析，以获得电子温度和等离子体电势的模型相关估计，而无需电偏压。并排比较表明浮动 PIP 结果与从有偏差的 Langmuir 探针获得的结果非常一致。总而言之，这些结果使 PIP 成为 Langmuir 探针的补充诊断，扩大了可进行的等离子体测量的范围，同时为经典鞘模型提供了实验支持。
