## Abstract
arXiv:2502.04214v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Non-Hermitian systems are widespread in both classical and quantum physics. The dynamics of such systems has recently become a focal point of research, showcasing surprising behaviors that include apparent violation of the adiabatic theorem and chiral topological conversion related to encircling exceptional points (EPs). These have both fundamental interest and potential practical applications. Yet the current literature features a number of apparently irreconcilable results. Here we develop a general theory for slow evolution of non-Hermitian systems and resolve these contradictions. We prove an analog of the adiabatic theorem for non-Hermitian systems and generalize it in the presence of uncontrolled environmental fluctuations (noise). The effect of noise turns out to be crucial due to inherent exponential instabilities present in non-Hermitian systems. Disproving common wisdom, the end state of the system is determined by the final Hamiltonian only, and is insensitive to other details of the evolution trajectory in parameter space. Our quantitative theory, leading to transparent physical intuition, is amenable to experimental tests. It provides efficient tools to predict the outcome of the system's evolution, avoiding the need to follow costly time-evolution simulations. Our approach may be useful for designing devices based on non-Hermitian physics and may stimulate analyses of classical and quantum non-Hermitian-Hamiltonian dynamics, as well as that of quantum Lindbladian and hybrid-Liouvillian systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.04214v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：非厄米系统在经典物理学和量子物理学中都广泛存在。此类系统的动力学最近成为研究的焦点，展示了令人惊讶的行为，包括明显违反绝热定理和与环绕异常点（EP）相关的手性拓扑转换。这些既有根本利益又有潜在的实际应用。然而，当前的文献呈现出许多明显不可调和的结果。在这里，我们发展了非厄米系统缓慢演化的一般理论并解决了这些矛盾。我们证明了非厄米系统的绝热定理的类比，并在存在不受控制的环境波动（噪声）的情况下将其推广。由于非厄米系统中存在固有的指数不稳定性，噪声的影响至关重要。与常识不同的是，系统的最终状态仅由最终的哈密顿量决定，并且对参数空间中演化轨迹的其他细节不敏感。我们的定量理论带来了透明的物理直觉，并且经得起实验测试。它提供了有效的工具来预测系统演化的结果，避免了遵循昂贵的时间演化模拟的需要。我们的方法可能有助于设计基于非厄米物理学的设备，并且可以刺激对经典和量子非厄米-哈密尔顿动力学以及量子林布拉德和混合刘维尔系统的分析。
