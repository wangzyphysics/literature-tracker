## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04730v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We examine the spectral structure of the two-dimensional advection-diffusion operator in flows with mixed phase space at very large Peclet number. Using Fourier discretization combined with symmetry reduction and Krylov-Arnoldi methods, we compute on the order of one hundred leading eigenpairs reliably in the asymptotic, weak-diffusion regime. While the principal eigenvalue is asymptotically diffusive and localized on the largest regular region, the broader spectrum exhibits a rich organization controlled by local Lagrangian phase-space geometry. In particular, exponential mixing in chaotic regions rapidly suppresses correlations, whereas algebraic mixing in integrable regions generates long-lived coherent structures that dominate the slow and intermediate parts of the spectrum. We identify three distinct classes of eigenmodes: advective modes associated with transport on invariant tori, diffusive modes and, within the duffusive branch, tunneling modes arising from weak coupling between dynamically separated regular regions. Drawing on a semiclassical analogy, we assign quantum-number-like labels to these families and predict the appearance, scaling, and ordering of their sub-spectra directly from the Hamiltonian phase-space structure. The coexistence of these families implies that no uniform control of the spectral gap exists across the full spectrum: although the slowest mode is diffusive, arbitrarily small gaps arise between competing families at higher mode numbers. As a result, finite-time advection-diffusion dynamics is generically governed by persistent modal competition rather than single-mode dominance, even at asymptotically large Peclet number.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04730v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们研究了混合相空间流中佩克莱特数非常大的二维平流扩散算子的谱结构。使用傅里叶离散化结合对称性约简和 Krylov-Arnoldi 方法，我们在渐进、弱扩散状态下可靠地计算了一百个主要特征对。虽然主特征值是渐近扩散的并且位于最大规则区域，但更广泛的光谱表现出由局部拉格朗日相空间几何控制的丰富组织。特别是，混沌区域中的指数混合会快速抑制相关性，而可积区域中的代数混合会生成长期相干结构，主导光谱的慢速和中间部分。我们确定了三类不同的本征模态：与不变环面上的传输相关的平流模态、扩散模态以及在扩散分支内由动态分离的规则区域之间的弱耦合产生的隧道模态。利用半经典类比，我们为这些族分配类似量子数的标签，并直接从哈密顿相空间结构预测其子谱的外观、缩放和排序。这些族的共存意味着整个光谱中不存在对光谱间隙的统一控制：尽管最慢的模式是扩散的，但在较高模式数的竞争族之间会出现任意小的间隙。因此，有限时间平流扩散动力学通常由持续模态竞争而不是单模主导控制，即使在渐进大的佩克莱特数下也是如此。
