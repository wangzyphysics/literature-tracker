## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06114v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Quantum many-body systems driven far from equilibrium can exhibit chaos, entanglement, and non-classical correlations, yet directly observing these phenomena in large, closed quantum systems remains challenging. Here we realize the Dicke model -- a fundamental description of light-matter interactions -- in a two-dimensional crystal of approximately 100 trapped ions. The ions' internal state is optically coupled to the center of mass vibrational mode via an optical spin-dependent force, enabling unitary many-body dynamics beyond the mean-field and few-body limits. In the integrable regime, where the phonons can be adiabatically eliminated, we observe a dynamical phase transition between ferromagnetic to paramagnetic spin phases. In contrast, when the spins and phonons are strongly coupled, we observe clear signatures of non-integrable chaotic dynamics, including erratic phase-space trajectories and the exponential growth of excitations and entanglement quantified by the one-body R\'enyi entropy. By quenching from an unstable fixed point in the near-integrable regime, quantum noise can generate correlated spin-phonon excitations. Our numerical calculations, in clear agreement with experiment, reveal the generation of two-mode spin-phonon squeezing, 2.6 dB below the standard quantum limit (4.6 dB relative to the initial thermal state), followed by generalized vacuum Rabi collapses and revivals. Our results establish large ion crystals as scalable analog quantum simulators of non-equilibrium light-matter dynamics and provide a controlled platform for experimental studies of information scrambling and entanglement in closed many-body systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06114v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：远离平衡的量子多体系统可能表现出混沌、纠缠和非经典相关性，但在大型封闭量子系统中直接观察这些现象仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们在大约 100 个捕获离子的二维晶体中实现了迪克模型（光与物质相互作用的基本描述）。离子的内部状态通过光学自旋相关力与质心振动模式光学耦合，从而实现超越平均场和少体极限的单一多体动力学。在可积体系中，声子可以被绝热消除，我们观察到铁磁到顺磁自旋相之间的动态相变。相反，当自旋和声子强耦合时，我们观察到不可积混沌动力学的清晰特征，包括不稳定的相空间轨迹以及由单体 R\'enyi 熵量化的激发和纠缠的指数增长。通过从近可积区域中的不稳定固定点淬灭，量子噪声可以产生相关的自旋声子激发。我们的数值计算与实验完全一致，揭示了双模自旋声子挤压的产生，比标准量子极限低 2.6 dB（相对于初始热状态 4.6 dB），随后是广义真空拉比塌缩和复兴。我们的研究结果将大型离子晶体建立为非平衡光物质动力学的可扩展模拟量子模拟器，并为封闭多体系统中信息加扰和纠缠的实验研究提供了受控平台。
