## Abstract
arXiv:2412.11439v5 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Generating novel molecules with higher properties than the training space, namely the out-of-distribution generation, is important for de novo drug design. However, it is not easy for distribution learning-based models, for example diffusion models, to solve this challenge as these methods are designed to fit the distribution of training data as close as possible. In this paper, we show that Bayesian flow network, especially ChemBFN model, is capable of intrinsically generating high quality out-of-distribution samples that meet several scenarios. A reinforcement learning strategy is added to the ChemBFN and a controllable ordinary differential equation solver-like generating process is employed that accelerate the sampling processes. Most importantly, we introduce a semi-autoregressive strategy during training and inference that enhances the model performance and surpass the state-of-the-art models. A theoretical analysis of out-of-distribution generation in ChemBFN with semi-autoregressive approach is included as well.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2412.11439v5 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：生成比训练空间具有更高特性的新分子，即分布外生成，对于从头药物设计非常重要。然而，基于分布学习的模型（例如扩散模型）要解决这一挑战并不容易，因为这些方法旨在尽可能接近训练数据的分布。在本文中，我们证明贝叶斯流网络，特别是 ChemBFN 模型，能够本质上生成满足多种场景的高质量分布外样本。 ChemBFN 中添加了强化学习策略，并采用了类似常微分方程求解器的可控生成过程来加速采样过程。最重要的是，我们在训练和推理过程中引入了半自回归策略，可以提高模型性能并超越最先进的模型。还包括使用半自回归方法对 ChemBFN 中的分布外生成进行的理论分析。
