## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13390v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Metastability, i.e., partial relaxation to long-lived, quasi-stationary states before true asymptotic equilibrium sets in, emerges ubiquitously in classical and quantum dynamical systems as a result of timescales separation. In open quantum systems, an intrinsically nonequilibrium analogue, dynamical metastability, can originate from the spectral geometry of a non-Hermitian operator. In noninteracting models, this mechanism produces boundary-sensitive anomalous relaxation, transient amplification, and topologically mandated long-lived edge modes, all of which are enhanced as system size grows. Here we extend dynamical metastability into the nonlinear, interacting regime and identify magnetic heterostructures as a natural platform for its exploration. We introduce an interacting spin Lindbladian whose linearized magnon dynamics map onto a dynamically metastable Hatano-Nelson chain, and show that dynamical metastability in the noninteracting limit seeds genuinely nonlinear phenomena, including size-dependent spin dipping and anomalous attraction to unstable equilibria. Long-lived edge states associated to topologically mandated Dirac bosons persist under nonlinearities and disorder. We further analyze the magnetization dynamics in magnetic multilayers within the classical Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert-Slonczewski framework, identifying Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction, nonlocal damping, and spin-transfer torque as control parameters governing bulk-boundary stability mismatch and band topology. While all the distinctive dynamical phenomena previously identified reappear in this experimentally relevant setting, the LLGS framework also supports multistability and limit cycles that are absent in the quantum model. Our results constitute the first systematic study of dynamical metastability in nonlinear dynamics, directly relevant to spin-torque oscillator arrays, magnonic devices, and beyond.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13390v1 公告类型：新 摘要：亚稳态，即在真正的渐近平衡出现之前部分弛豫到长期存在的准稳态，由于时间尺度分离而普遍出现在经典和量子动力系统中。在开放量子系统中，本质上非平衡的类似物，即动态亚稳态，可以源自非厄米算子的谱几何。在非交互模型中，这种机制会产生边界敏感的异常松弛、瞬态放大和拓扑规定的长寿命边缘模式，所有这些都会随着系统规模的增长而增强。在这里，我们将动态亚稳态扩展到非线性、相互作用的领域，并将磁性异质结构确定为其探索的自然平台。我们引入了一种相互作用的自旋 Lindbladian，其线性化磁振子动力学映射到动态亚稳态 Hatano-Nelson 链上，并表明非相互作用极限中的动态亚稳定性产生了真正的非线性现象，包括尺寸相关的自旋下降和对不稳定平衡的异常吸引。与拓扑规定的狄拉克玻色子相关的长寿命边缘态在非线性和无序状态下持续存在。我们进一步分析了经典 Landau-Lifshitz-Gilbert-Slonczewski 框架内磁性多层膜的磁化动力学，将 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用、非局域阻尼和自旋转移扭矩确定为控制体边界稳定性失配和能带拓扑的控制参数。虽然之前确定的所有独特的动力学现象都在这个实验相关的环境中重新出现，但 LLGS 框架还支持量子模型中不存在的多稳定性和极限环。我们的结果构成了非线性动力学中动态亚稳态的第一个系统研究，与自旋力矩振荡器阵列、磁振子器件等直接相关。
