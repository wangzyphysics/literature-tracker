## Abstract
arXiv:2506.05504v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The recent discovery of altermagnets, which exhibit spin splitting without net magnetization, opens new directions for spintronics beyond the limits of ferromagnets, antiferromagnets, and spin orbit coupled systems. We investigate spin selective quantum transport in heterostructures composed of a normal metal and a two dimensional d-wave altermagnet, and identify a universal mechanism for achieving perfect spin polarization. The mechanism is dictated by Fermi surface geometry: closed Fermi surfaces in weak altermagnets yield partial and oscillatory spin filtering, whereas open Fermi surfaces in strong altermagnets intrinsically enforce fully spin polarized conductance. Exploiting these distinct transport regimes, we propose all electrical spin filter and spin valve architectures, where resonant tunneling produces highly spin polarized conductance tunable by gate voltage and interface transparency. Altermagnets with open Fermi surfaces further support gate reversible perfect spin polarization that remains robust against interface scattering, disorder, and temperature. We also demonstrate an electrically controlled spin valve that reproduces the functionality of magnetic tunnel junctions without magnetic fields or relativistic mechanisms. d-wave altermagnets with open Fermi surfaces thus provide a promising platform for low dissipation, scalable, and magnetic field free spintronic devices with potential for integration into next generation quantum and CMOS compatible technologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.05504v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：最近发现的交变磁体在没有净磁化的情况下表现出自旋分裂，为自旋电子学开辟了超越铁磁体、反铁磁体和自旋轨道耦合系统限制的新方向。我们研究了由普通金属和二维 d 波交替磁体组成的异质结构中的自旋选择性量子传输，并确定了实现完美自旋极化的通用机制。该机制由费米表面几何形状决定：弱交变磁体中的闭合费米表面产生部分和振荡自旋过滤，而强交变磁体中的开放费米表面本质上增强了完全自旋极化电导。利用这些不同的传输机制，我们提出了所有电自旋滤波器和自旋阀架构，其中谐振隧道产生可通过栅极电压和界面透明度调节的高自旋极化电导。具有开放费米表面的交变磁体进一步支持栅极可逆完美自旋极化，该极化对界面散射、无序和温度保持鲁棒性。我们还展示了一种电控自旋阀，它可以在没有磁场或相对论机制的情况下重现磁隧道结的功能。因此，具有开放费米表面的 d 波交替磁体为低耗散、可扩展和无磁场的自旋电子器件提供了一个有前景的平台，有可能集成到下一代量子和 CMOS 兼容技术中。
