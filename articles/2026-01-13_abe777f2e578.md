## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06320v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Inferring high-dimensional physical states from sparse, ad-hoc sensor arrays is a fundamental challenge across AI for Science, as they are complicated by irregular geometries and the profound Sim-to-Real gap in physical modeling. Taking earthquake source characterization as a representative challenge, we address limitations in conventional deep learning: CNNs demand fixed grids, while pooling-based architectures (e.g., DeepSets) struggle to capture the relational wave physics. Here, we propose SourceNet, a Transformer-based framework that treats the sensor array as a flexible set to model arbitrary geometries. To bridge the reality gap, we introduce Physics-Structured Domain Randomization (PSDR). Instead of forcing feature alignment, PSDR randomizes the governing physical dynamics by varying velocity structures, propagation effects, and sensor availability, to force the model to learn robust representations invariant to unmodeled environmental heterogeneity. By pre-training on 100,000 synthetic events and fine-tuning on ~2,000 real world events, SourceNet achieves state-of-the-art precision on held-out real data. This demonstrates exceptional data efficiency, and matches classical solvers while enabling real-time processing. Remarkably, interpretability analysis reveals that the model shows scientific-agent-like features: it autonomously discovers geometric information bottlenecks and learns an attention policy that prioritizes sparse sensor placements, effectively recovering principles of optimal experimental design from data alone.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06320v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：从稀疏的临时传感器阵列推断高维物理状态是科学人工智能领域的一项基本挑战，因为它们因不规则的几何形状和物理建模中巨大的模拟与真实差距而变得复杂。以地震源表征为代表性挑战，我们解决了传统深度学习的局限性：CNN 需要固定网格，而基于池的架构（例如 DeepSets）很难捕获相关的波物理现象。在这里，我们提出了 SourceNet，这是一个基于 Transformer 的框架，它将传感器阵列视为一个灵活的集合来对任意几何形状进行建模。为了弥补现实差距，我们引入了物理结构域随机化（PSDR）。 PSDR 不是强制特征对齐，而是通过改变速度结构、传播效应和传感器可用性来随机化控制物理动力学，以迫使模型学习对未建模的环境异质性不变的鲁棒表示。通过对 100,000 个合成事件进行预训练并对约 2,000 个现实世界事件进行微调，SourceNet 在保留的真实数据上实现了最先进的精度。这展示了卓越的数据效率，并与经典求解器相匹配，同时支持实时处理。值得注意的是，可解释性分析表明该模型显示出类似科学代理的特征：它自主发现几何信息瓶颈并学习优先考虑稀疏传感器放置的注意力策略，从而仅从数据中有效地恢复最佳实验设计的原则。
