## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00244v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The continuous casting tundish plays a critical role as a metallurgical reactor in the continuous casting process, with its flow characteristics serving as a key parameter in the production of high-quality steel. These characteristics are typically assessed through residence time distribution (RTD) curves. This study examines the flow behaviour in a single-strand continuous casting tundish through a combination of numerical simulations and experimental validation. Steady-state full order model (FOM) simulations are performed under both isothermal and non-isothermal conditions to evaluate the influence of thermal buoyancy on the velocity field, which is found to be negligible. The resulting flow fields are used to initialize transient tracer transport simulations for determining the RTD and flow volume partitioning. Subsequently, a data-driven reduced order model (ROM) is developed to predict the RTD response. Comparison of RTD curves obtained from experiments, FOM, and ROM shows excellent agreement, with the ROM accurately capturing the key flow characteristics at a fraction of the computational cost. These results highlight the potential of ROM techniques for efficient real-time analysis, design, and optimization of tundish operations in metallurgical processes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00244v1 公告类型：新 摘要：连铸中间包作为冶金反应器在连铸过程中起着至关重要的作用，其流动特性是优质钢生产的关键参数。这些特性通常通过停留时间分布 (RTD) 曲线进行评估。本研究通过数值模拟和实验验证相结合，研究了单流连铸中间包的流动行为。在等温和非等温条件下进行稳态全阶模型（FOM）模拟，以评估热浮力对速度场的影响，发现这种影响可以忽略不计。生成的流场用于初始化瞬态示踪剂传输模拟，以确定 RTD 和流量分配。随后，开发了数据驱动的降阶模型 (ROM) 来预测 RTD 响应。从实验、FOM 和 ROM 获得的 RTD 曲线的比较显示出极好的一致性，ROM 以一小部分计算成本准确地捕获了关键的流动特性。这些结果凸显了 ROM 技术在冶金过程中中间包操作的高效实时分析、设计和优化方面的潜力。
