## Abstract
arXiv:2508.17777v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study the process of magnetic reversals in the presence of a stably-stratified layer below the core-mantle boundary using direct numerical simulations of the incompressible magnetohydrodynamics equations under the Boussinesq approximation in a spherical shell. We show that the dipolar-multipolar transition shifts to larger Rayleigh numbers in the presence of a stably-stratified layer, and that the dipolar strength of the magnetic field at the core-mantle boundary increases due to the skin effect. By imposing an heterogeneous heat flux at the outer boundary, we break the equatorial symmetry of the flow, and show that different heat flux patterns can trigger different dynamo solutions, such as hemispheric dynamos and polarity reversals. Using kinematic dynamo simulations, we show that the stably-stratified layer leads to similar growth rates of the dipole and quadrupole components of the magnetic field, playing the role of a conducting boundary layer, favouring magnetic reversals, and a dynamics predicted by low-dimensional models.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.17777v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们利用球壳中 Boussinesq 近似下的不可压缩磁流体动力学方程的直接数值模拟，研究了在核幔边界下方存在稳定层状层的情况下磁反转的过程。我们表明，在稳定分层层存在的情况下，偶极-多极转变会转变为更大的瑞利数，并且由于趋肤效应，核幔边界处的磁场偶极强度会增加。通过在外边界施加异质热通量，我们打破了流动的赤道对称性，并表明不同的热通量模式可以触发不同的发电机解决方案，例如半球发电机和极性反转。使用运动学发电机模拟，我们表明稳定分层层导致磁场的偶极子和四极子分量的增长率相似，发挥传导边界层的作用，有利于磁反转，以及低维模型预测的动力学。
