## Abstract
arXiv:2511.22398v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The coalescence of liquid lenses is relevant in various applications, including inkjet printing and fog harvesting. However, the dynamics of liquid-lens coalescence have been relatively underexplored, particularly in the case of liquid lenses with larger contact angles. We numerically investigate the coalescence of low-viscosity liquid lenses by means of the pseudopotential multi-component lattice Boltzmann method over a wide range of contact angles. In two-dimensional simulations, our numerical results on the growth of the bridge height are in quantitative agreement with experimental measurements for small contact angles. In addition, by comparing our simulation results with a theoretical approach based on the thin-sheet equations for liquid lenses, we find that the thin-sheet equations accurately capture the bridge-growth dynamics up to contact angles of approximately $\theta < 40^{\circ}$. For the three-dimensional case, the growth of the bridge radius is independent of the equilibrium contact angle of the liquid lenses at the initial stage of growth. The dependency between the growth of the bridge height and the bridge radius exhibits a non-linear to linear transition.

arXiv:2511.22398v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：液体透镜的聚结与各种应用相关，包括喷墨打印和雾收集。然而，液体透镜聚结的动力学研究相对不足，特别是在接触角较大的液体透镜的情况下。我们通过赝势多分量晶格玻尔兹曼方法在较宽的接触角范围内对低粘度液体透镜的聚结进行数值研究。在二维模拟中，我们关于桥高度增长的数值结果与小接触角的实验测量结果定量一致。此外，通过将我们的模拟结果与基于液体透镜薄片方程的理论方法进行比较，我们发现薄片方程准确地捕获了接触角约为 $\theta < 40^{\circ}$ 的桥生长动态。对于三维情况，桥半径的增长与液体透镜在增长初始阶段的平衡接触角无关。桥高度的增长和桥半径之间的相关性表现出非线性到线性的过渡。
