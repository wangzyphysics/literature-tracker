## Abstract
arXiv:2511.09960v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Nanostructured materials, critical for thermal management in semiconductor devices, exhibit a strong size dependence in thermal transport. Specifically, studying the variation of thermal resistance across grain boundaries is of critical importance in designing effective thermal interface materials for heterogeneous integration. Frequency-domain Thermoreflectance (FDTR)-based techniques can provide thermal resistance mapping in the micrometer ({\mu}m) length scale. Scanning Thermal Microscopy (SThM) has the potential for quantification of local thermal transport with orders of magnitude higher spatial resolution (< 100 nm). However, challenges in the accurate quantification of the raw signal to thermal conductivity and surface sensitivity limit its widespread adoption as a characterization standard for understanding nanoscale heat transport and defect-mediated thermal properties modulation in nanostructured films. Here, we introduce a circuit-based probe thermal resistance (Rp) calibration technique that is independent of any parasitic heat transport pathways; thus, the calibration allows accurate determination of the heat dissipated from the SThM probe and the resulting tip temperature increase, thereby enabling extraction of the local thermal resistance of the film. In this study, SThM achieved sub-100 nm spatial resolution in mapping thermal resistance across a 15 nm-thick Al film deposited via e-beam evaporation on SiO2 substrate. Finally, the thermal resistance mapping is converted to thermal conductivity using the finite element modeling-based calibration technique, where the average of the pixel-level Rc values yields the effective thermal conductivity of 2.87 +/- 0.18 W/m.K, in good agreement with published theoretical frameworks explaining heat transport modes in ultra-thin Al.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.09960v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：纳米结构材料对于半导体器件的热管理至关重要，在热传输中表现出强烈的尺寸依赖性。具体来说，研究跨晶界的热阻变化对于设计用于异质集成的有效热界面材料至关重要。基于频域热反射 (FDTR) 的技术可以提供微米 ({\mu}m) 长度尺度的热阻映射。扫描热显微镜 (SThM) 具有量化局部热传输的潜力，其空间分辨率高出几个数量级 (< 100 nm)。然而，对热导率和表面敏感性原始信号进行精确量化的挑战限制了其作为了解纳米结构薄膜中纳米级热传输和缺陷介导的热性能调节的表征标准的广泛采用。在这里，我们介绍一种基于电路的探头热阻 (Rp) 校准技术，该技术独立于任何寄生热传输路径；因此，校准可以准确确定 SThM 探头散发的热量以及由此产生的尖端温度升高，从而能够提取薄膜的局部热阻。在这项研究中，SThM 在绘制通过电子束蒸发沉积在 SiO2 基板上的 15 nm 厚的 Al 薄膜上的热阻时，实现了亚 100 nm 的空间分辨率。最后，使用基于有限元建模的校准技术将热阻映射转换为热导率，其中像素级 Rc 值的平均值产生 2.87 +/- 0.18 W/m.K 的有效热导率，与已发表的解释超薄铝热传输模式的理论框架非常一致。
