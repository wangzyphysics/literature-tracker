## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02259v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a disorder-free one-dimensional single-particle Hamiltonian hosting an exact mobility edge (ME), placing the system outside the assumptions of no-go theorems regarding unbounded potentials. By applying a linear Stark potential selectively to one sublattice of a dimerized chain, we generate an effective Hamiltonian with unbounded, staggered hopping amplitudes. The unbounded nature of the hopping places the model outside the scope of the Simon-Spencer theorem, while the staggered scaling allows it to evade broader constraints on Jacobi matrices. We analytically derive the bulk spectrum in reciprocal space, identifying a sharp ME where the energy magnitude equals the inter-cell hopping strength. This edge separates a continuum of extended states from two distinct localized branches: a standard unbounded Wannier-Stark ladder and an anomalous bounded branch accumulating at the ME. The existence of extended states is supported by finite-size scaling of the inverse participation ratio up to system sizes $L \sim 10^9$. Furthermore, we propose an experimental realization using photonic frequency synthetic dimensions. Our numerical results indicate that the ME is robust against potential experimental imperfections, including frequency detuning errors and photon loss, establishing a practical path for observing MEs in disorder-free systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02259v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种无序一维单粒子哈密顿量，它具有精确的迁移性边缘（ME），使系统不受关于无界势的不可行定理的假设的影响。通过选择性地将线性斯塔克势应用于二聚链的一个子晶格，我们生成了一种具有无界、交错跳跃幅度的有效哈密顿量。跳跃的无界性质使该模型超出了西蒙-斯宾塞定理的范围，而交错缩放使其能够逃避对雅可比矩阵的更广泛的约束。我们通过分析推导了倒易空间中的整体频谱，识别了能量大小等于单元间跳跃强度的尖锐 ME。这条边将扩展状态的连续体与两个不同的局部分支分开：一个标准的无界 Wannier-Stark 梯子和一个在 ME 处累积的异常有界分支。扩展状态的存在由逆参与比的有限尺寸缩放支持，最大系统尺寸为 $L \sim 10^9$。此外，我们提出了使用光子频率合成维度的实验实现。我们的数值结果表明，ME 对潜在的实验缺陷（包括频率失谐误差和光子损失）具有鲁棒性，为在无序系统中观察 ME 建立了一条实用路径。
