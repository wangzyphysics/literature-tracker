## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22947v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Topological magnetic textures are particle-like spin configurations stabilized by competing interactions. Their formation is commonly attributed to fluctuation-driven, first-order nucleation processes requiring activation over a topological energy barrier. Here, we demonstrate an alternative barrier- and fluctuation-free pathway for nucleating topological magnetic textures, triggered in our experiments by an excitation-induced spin reorientation transition. By combining x-ray imaging, scattering and micromagnetic simulations, we show that the system follows a deterministic cascade of symmetry-breaking phase transitions after excitation. First, the system undergoes a second-order phase transition from a homogeneous state to weak stripe domains, then a first-order transition to topologically trivial bubbles, and finally a topological switching event into skyrmionic textures. Through simulations, we generalize our findings and demonstrate that this pathway is active in a vast range of low-anisotropy materials. This previously unrecognized, spontaneous transition pathway suggests strategies for rapid, low-energy generation of topological spin textures and points to a general role of intrinsic modulational instabilities in phase transitions beyond magnetism.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22947v1 公告类型：新 摘要：拓扑磁性纹理是通过竞争相互作用稳定的类粒子自旋配置。它们的形成通常归因于涨落驱动的一阶成核过程，需要在拓扑能垒上激活。在这里，我们展示了一种用于成核拓扑磁性纹理的替代性无势垒和波动路径，该路径在我们的实验中由激发诱导的自旋重定向转变触发。通过结合 X 射线成像、散射和微磁模拟，我们表明该系统在激发后遵循一系列确定性的对称破缺相变。首先，系统经历从均质态到弱条纹域的二阶相变，然后经历到拓扑平凡气泡的一阶相变，最后经历到斯格明子纹理的拓扑切换事件。通过模拟，我们概括了我们的发现，并证明该途径在多种低各向异性材料中都很活跃。这种先前未被认识的自发转变途径提出了快速、低能量生成拓扑自旋纹理的策略，并指出了内在调制不稳定性在磁性以外的相变中的一般作用。
