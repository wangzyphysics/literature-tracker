## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23336v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Hybrid semiconductor-superconductor qubits have recently emerged as a promising alternative to traditional platforms, combining material advantages with device-level tunability. A defining feature is their gate-tunable Josephson coupling, enabling superconducting qubit architectures with full electric-field control and offering a path toward scalable, low-crosstalk quantum processors. This approach seeks to merge benefits of superconducting and semiconductor qubits, for instance by encoding quantum information in the spin of a quasiparticle occupying an Andreev bound state, thus combining long coherence times with fast, flexible control. Progress has accelerated through bottom-up engineering of Andreev states in coupled quantum dot arrays, leading to architectures such as minimal Kitaev chains hosting Majorana zero modes. In parallel, Hamiltonian-protected designs aim to enhance resilience against local noise and decoherence by exploiting superconducting phase dynamics and discrete charge or flux degrees of freedom. This article reviews recent theoretical and experimental advances in hybrid qubits, providing an overview of physical mechanisms, device implementations, and emerging architectures, with emphasis on their potential for (topologically) protected quantum information processing. While many designs remain at proof-of-concept stage, rapid progress suggests practical demonstrations may soon be achievable.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23336v1 公告类型：新 摘要：混合半导体-超导量子位最近已成为传统平台的一种有前景的替代品，它将材料优势与器件级可调谐性相结合。一个决定性的特征是其门可调约瑟夫森耦合，可实现具有完全电场控制的超导量子位架构，并提供通向可扩展、低串扰量子处理器的途径。这种方法寻求融合超导和半导体量子位的优点，例如通过在占据安德烈夫束缚态的准粒子自旋中编码量子信息，从而将长相干时间与快速、灵活的控制结合起来。通过耦合量子点阵列中安德烈夫态的自下而上工程，进展加速，从而产生了托管马约拉纳零模式的最小基塔耶夫链等架构。与此同时，受哈密顿量保护的设计旨在通过利用超导相动力学和离散电荷或通量自由度来增强对抗局部噪声和退相干的弹性。本文回顾了混合量子位的最新理论和实验进展，概述了物理机制、设备实现和新兴架构，重点介绍了它们在（拓扑）受保护的量子信息处理方面的潜力。虽然许多设计仍处于概念验证阶段，但快速进展表明实际演示可能很快就能实现。
