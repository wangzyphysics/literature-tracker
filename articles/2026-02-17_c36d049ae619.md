## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14932v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: While stoichiometric SrRuO$_3$ (SRO) is a metallic itinerant ferromagnet with relatively homogeneous magnetization, Ru deficiency provides a powerful route to alter its electronic transport and depth-dependent magnetic properties. Ru-deficient SRO thin films grown by radio-frequency high oxygen pressure sputtering were investigated using a combination of X-ray reflectivity, polarized neutron reflectometry, off-specular neutron scattering, scanning transmission electron microscopy with energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy, electrical transport, and magnetometry. Structural and compositional analyses reveal that Ru deficiency is intrinsic to the films, with an enhanced deficiency at the interfaces. As a result, coherent electronic transport is suppressed and the saturation magnetization is reduced, while the Curie temperature remains largely unaffected, placing Ru-deficient SRO in a regime consistent with ferromagnetic insulator-like behavior. Depth- and lateral-resolved magnetic measurements further show that the interfacial regions remain ferromagnetic but exhibit enhanced perpendicular magnetic anisotropy, which constrains the local magnetization to remain predominantly out-of-plane and strongly reduces its in-plane projection. Our results establish Ru deficiency as a key control parameter governing transport, magnetization, and anisotropy in SRO thin films and highlight defect and interface engineering as powerful routes to tailor interfacial magnetism in correlated oxide heterostructures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14932v1 公告类型：新 摘要：虽然化学计量的 SrRuO$_3$ (SRO) 是一种具有相对均匀磁化强度的金属流动铁磁体，但 Ru 的缺乏提供了改变其电子传输和深度依赖的磁性的有力途径。通过结合 X 射线反射率、偏振中子反射测量、非镜面中子散射、扫描透射电子显微镜与能量色散 X 射线光谱、电传输和磁力测量，研究了通过射频高氧压溅射生长的缺钌 SRO 薄膜。结构和成分分析表明，Ru 缺陷是薄膜固有的，并且界面处的缺陷增强。结果，相干电子传输受到抑制，饱和磁化强度降低，而居里温度基本不受影响，从而使缺乏 Ru 的 SRO 处于与铁磁绝缘体类行为一致的状态。深度和横向分辨磁测量进一步表明，界面区域仍然是铁磁性的，但表现出增强的垂直磁各向异性，这限制了局部磁化强度主要保持在平面外，并大大减少了其平面内投影。我们的研究结果将 Ru 缺陷确定为控制 SRO 薄膜中输运、磁化和各向异性的关键控制参数，并强调缺陷和界面工程是调整相关氧化物异质结构中界面磁性的有力途径。
