## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01319v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Vascular diseases such as atherosclerosis, thrombosis, and aneurysms can lead to life-threatening medical events. Conventional catheter- or guidewire-based endovascular diagnostic and therapeutic devices often struggle to navigate through highly tortuous vascular pathways. The recently developed multifunctional magnetic milli-spinner offers a promising wireless solution by integrating a central through-hole and side slits into a cylindrical body with helical fins, enabling rapid and stable navigation in complex vascular environments for clot debulking, targeted drug delivery, and aneurysm treatment. Here, we combine computational fluid dynamics simulations with experimental validation to optimize the milli-spinner's structural design for high-speed untethered navigation and high-efficiency clot debulking in tubular flow environments. By systematically examining the effects of through-hole radius, fin number, fin helical angle, and slit dimension on propulsion performance, the optimized magnetic milli-spinner achieves a swimming speed of 55 cm/s (~175 body lengths per second), far exceeding existing untethered magnetic robots in tubular environments (< 80 body lengths per second). This exceptional speed enables stable upstream operation against strong physiological flows representative of major arteries and veins, establishing the magnetic milli-spinner as a robust untethered navigation platform for operation in high-flow, tortuous vasculature, with potential applications in robotic mechanical thrombectomy, embolectomy, and targeted drug delivery.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01319v1 公告类型：新 摘要：动脉粥样硬化、血栓形成和动脉瘤等血管疾病可导致危及生命的医疗事件。传统的基于导管或导丝的血管内诊断和治疗装置通常难以穿过高度曲折的血管通路。最近开发的多功能磁性毫旋转器通过将中心通孔和侧缝集成到带有螺旋翅片的圆柱形主体中，提供了一种有前途的无线解决方案，从而能够在复杂的血管环境中快速稳定地导航，以进行血栓减灭、靶向药物输送和动脉瘤治疗。在这里，我们将计算流体动力学模拟与实验验证相结合，以优化毫旋转器的结构设计，以实现管状流动环境中的高速无束缚导航和高效凝块减积。通过系统地研究通孔半径、鳍片数量、鳍片螺旋角和狭缝尺寸对推进性能的影响，优化后的磁力毫旋转器实现了 55 cm/s（每秒约 175 个身体长度）的游泳速度，远远超过了管状环境中现有的无绳磁性机器人（每秒 < 80 个身体长度）。这种非凡的速度能够针对代表主要动脉和静脉的强生理流进行稳定的上游操作，使磁力毫旋转器成为一个强大的不受束缚的导航平台，用于高流量、曲折的脉管系统中的操作，在机器人机械血栓切除术、栓子切除术和靶向药物输送方面具有潜在的应用。
