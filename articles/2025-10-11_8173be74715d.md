## Abstract
ABSTRACT Continuous and reliable electrocardiogram (ECG) monitoring is crucial for the early diagnosis and intervention of heart diseases, which remain a leading threat to global health and mortality. Traditional ECG devices are often bulky, complex, and require hospital visits, limiting their practicality for daily use. To overcome these challenges, we have developed a wireless, flexible, and user-friendly ECG monitoring system integrated with advanced artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities. Our innovative ECG patch features an island-and-bridge serpentine structure, offering strain insensitivity of up to 100%, robust adhesion (7.6 kPa), and a high signal-to-noise ratio (28 dB). The accompanying mobile application leverages the interpretable attention transformer (IAT) model for heart disease diagnosis with up to 98% accuracy, a generative adversarial network (GAN) combined with convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and gated recurrent units (GRUs) for wear positioning correction with 85% accuracy, and GPT-based consultations with sub-second response times. This system enables real-time diagnosis, accurate wear positioning, and personalized medical advice, effectively bridging the gap between hospital care and at-home monitoring. Our work enhances accessibility to cardiac care, promotes early detection, and reduces the burden on healthcare systems.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 持续可靠的心电图 (ECG) 监测对于心脏病的早期诊断和干预至关重要，心脏病仍然是全球健康和死亡率的主要威胁。传统的心电图设备通常体积庞大、复杂，并且需要去医院就诊，限制了其日常使用的实用性。为了克服这些挑战，我们开发了一种集成了先进人工智能 (AI) 功能的无线、灵活且用户友好的心电图监测系统。我们的创新心电图贴片采用岛桥蛇形结构，具有高达 100% 的应变不敏感性、牢固的附着力 (7.6 kPa) 和高信噪比 (28 dB)。随附的移动应用程序利用可解释注意力变换器 (IAT) 模型进行心脏病诊断，准确率高达 98%，利用生成对抗网络 (GAN) 与卷积神经网络 (CNN) 和门控循环单元 (GRU) 相结合，进行磨损定位校正，准确率达 85%，以及基于 GPT 的亚秒级响应时间咨询。该系统可实现实时诊断、准确的佩戴定位和个性化医疗建议，有效缩小医院护理和家庭监控之间的差距。我们的工作提高了心脏护理的可及性，促进早期发现，并减轻医疗保健系统的负担。
