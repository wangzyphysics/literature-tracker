## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01148v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ultra-low-frequency electromagnetic waves Pc1 (0.2-5 Hz), widely known in the literature as pearls, are excited in the outer radiation belt and propagate to the Earth along the geomagnetic field lines in the form of Alfven waves. The study of pearls is of considerable interest for magnetospheric physics. Both spontaneous and stimulated pearl excitation are observed. The paper examines stimulated (trigger) excitation of pearls. A classification of triggers acting on dynamic systems of the magnetosphere is presented. 2 types, 4 classes and 8 species of triggers have been introduced. Examples of triggers of natural and artificial origin are given. The concept of a trigger cascade is introduced. Particular attention is paid to the anthropogenic periodic trigger of pearls. It manifests itself in the form of the so-called Big Ben effect. The essence of the effect is that a series of pearls is often excited immediately following the hour marker according to world time. It is claimed that the connection between the excitation of pearls and hour markers is not accidental, but represents a rather mysterious geophysical phenomenon. It is assumed that the Big Ben effect occurs as a result of the impact on the radiation belt of an unknown type of endogenous periodic artificial trigger. Key words: oscillations and waves, Alfven waves, ion-cyclotron resonator, radiation belt, instability, classification, natural and artificial, technosphere, Big Ben effect.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01148v1 发布类型：新 摘要：超低频电磁波 Pc1（0.2-5 Hz），在文献中被广泛称为珍珠，在外辐射带中被激发，并以阿尔文波的形式沿着地磁力线传播到地球。珍珠的研究对磁层物理学具有很大的意义。观察到自发珍珠激发和受激珍珠激发。本文研究了珍珠的受激（触发）激发。提出了作用于磁层动态系统的触发器的分类。引入了2类、4类、8种触发器。给出了自然和人工来源的触发因素的例子。引入了触发级联的概念。特别关注珍珠的人为周期性触发。它以所谓的大本钟效应的形式表现出来。这种效果的本质是，根据世界时间，在时标之后立即激发一系列珍珠。据称，珍珠的激发与时标之间的联系并非偶然，而是代表了一种相当神秘的地球物理现象。据推测，大本钟效应的发生是由于未知类型的内源周期性人工触发器对辐射带的影响而产生的。关键词：振荡和波，阿尔文波，离子回旋谐振器，辐射带，不稳定性，分类，自然和人工，技术圈，大本钟效应。
