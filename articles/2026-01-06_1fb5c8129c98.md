## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01420v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnets, an emerging class of magnetic materials, exhibit exotic chiral split magnons that are of great interest for both fundamental physics and spintronic applications. However, detecting and manipulating these magnons is challenging due to their THz frequency response. Here, we report the discovery of gapless chiral split magnons confined within altermagnetic domain walls. Unlike in conventional ferromagnets or antiferromagnets, their spectrum is highly sensitive to the domain wall orientation relative to the crystal axis. These magnons inherit the chiral splitting of their bulk counterparts and are detectable in the microwave regime, offering a distinctive signature for identifying altermagnets. We further show that the interfacial Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction drives hybridization of magnons with opposite chiralities, enabling unidirectional strong magnon-magnon coupling. Moreover, we demonstrate that spin-orbit torque can control the domain wall orientation, providing a practical means to manipulate these chiral magnons. Our findings open pathways for novel magnonic nanocircuitry based on altermagnetic domain walls.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01420v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Altermagnets 是一类新兴的磁性材料，表现出奇特的手性分裂磁振子，对基础物理和自旋电子学应用都很感兴趣。然而，由于它们的太赫兹频率响应，检测和操纵这些磁振子具有挑战性。在这里，我们报告了限制在交变磁畴壁内的无间隙手性分裂磁振子的发现。与传统的铁磁体或反铁磁体不同，它们的光谱对相对于晶轴的磁畴壁方向高度敏感。这些磁振子继承了其块状磁振子的手性分裂，并且可以在微波范围内检测到，为识别交变磁体提供了独特的特征。我们进一步表明，Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 界面相互作用驱动具有相反手性的磁振子的杂化，从而实现单向强磁振子-磁振子耦合。此外，我们证明自旋轨道扭矩可以控制磁畴壁方向，为操纵这些手性磁振子提供了实用的方法。我们的研究结果为基于交变磁畴壁的新型磁纳米电路开辟了道路。
