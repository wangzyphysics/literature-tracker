## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12328v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose and investigate a novel two-dimensional (2D) tight-binding model defined on a diamond-dodecagon lattice geometry that hosts multiple flat bands (FBs) and supports topological phase transitions driven by a magnetic flux. This lattice exhibits three completely flat, non-dispersive bands in the band structure in the absence of magnetic flux due to destructive interference in the electron hoppings, leading to the emergence of compact localized states (CLS). These CLS are analytically constructed and exhibit real-space confinement of the electrons, arising solely due to the lattice's geometrical frustration. It has been shown that these FBs are very robust against the introduction of weak random onsite disorder in the system. By tuning the uniform magnetic flux threaded through the diamond plaquettes, we demonstrate a tunable evolution of the band structure and show that certain bands develop nontrivial topological features with nonzero integer values of the Chern number. Additionally, we have computed the multi-terminal transport properties for this 2D lattice system, which display the flux-tunable resonances and transmission suppression linked to the FBs, establishing a clear interplay between the localization, topology, and transport. Our findings put forward the diamond-dodecagon lattice as a robust and tunable platform for studying the flat-band physics and magnetic flux-controlled topological phenomena, offering promising experimental feasibility in photonic lattices and ultracold atomic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12328v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出并研究了一种在金刚石十二边形晶格几何结构上定义的新型二维 (2D) 紧束缚模型，该模型具有多个平带 (FB) 并支持由磁通量驱动的拓扑相变。由于电子跳跃的破坏性干扰，在没有磁通量的情况下，该晶格在能带结构中呈现出三个完全平坦、非色散的能带，从而导致紧凑局域态（CLS）的出现。这些 CLS 是经过分析构建的，并表现出电子的真实空间限制，这完全是由于晶格的几何挫败而产生的。事实证明，这些 FB 对于系统中引入弱随机现场无序非常稳健。通过调整穿过金刚石小片的均匀磁通量，我们展示了能带结构的可调演化，并表明某些能带发展出具有陈数非零整数值的非平凡拓扑特征。此外，我们还计算了该 2D 晶格系统的多终端传输属性，该属性显示了与 FB 相关的通量可调谐振和传输抑制，从而在定位、拓扑和传输之间建立了清晰的相互作用。我们的研究结果提出金刚石十二边形晶格作为研究平带物理和磁通量控制拓扑现象的稳健且可调谐的平台，为光子晶格和超冷原子系统提供了有希望的实验可行性。
