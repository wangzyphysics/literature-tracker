## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00710v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Carbon mineralization in basaltic rocks may offer rapid, permanent \ce{CO2} storage, yet fundamental controls on reactive transport and precipitation patterns remain poorly understood. This study integrates flow-through experiments at 80\degree C using \ce{CO2}-acidified seawater with geochemical simulation and multi-scale pore imaging to elucidate mineralization dynamics in basaltic glass. Results reveal that carbonate precipitation is nucleation-controlled and stochastic rather than growth-controlled and deterministic, with isolated accumulations forming randomly despite continuous supersaturation. Residence time exerts primary control: reducing flow rate from 0.05 to 0.005\,mL/min proved necessary for visible precipitation. Post-experiment analyses identified calcium carbonate and smectite phases. Multi-scale characterization of three basalt facies revealed that connected porosity fractions (1.3--32\%) differ significantly from total porosity (18--42\%), demonstrating that network topology controls permeability. Micro-CT analysis revealed that pore coordination numbers in basalts (modal = 2) were notably lower than those in reservoir sandstones, creating serial flow paths that are vulnerable to catastrophic permeability loss from modest precipitation. Precipitation-induced clogging scenarios were proposed, where distributed small precipitates cause more severe permeability degradation than large accumulations. The use of seawater complicates geochemistry and reduces mineralization efficiency compared to freshwater. Findings emphasize the need for probabilistic reactive transport modeling frameworks and realistic pore topologies, which are fundamentally different from conventional CCS operations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00710v1 公告类型：新 摘要：玄武岩中的碳矿化可能会提供快速、永久的 \ce{CO2} 储存，但对反应输运和降水模式的基本控制仍然知之甚少。这项研究将使用 \ce{CO2} 酸化海水在 80°C 下进行的流通实验与地球化学模拟和多尺度孔隙成像相结合，以阐明玄武岩玻璃中的矿化动力学。结果表明，碳酸盐沉淀是成核控制和随机的，而不是生长控制和确定性的，尽管存在连续过饱和，但孤立的积累随机形成。停留时间起到主要控制作用：将流速从 0.05 降低到 0.005 mL/min，事实证明对于可见沉淀是必要的。实验后分析确定了碳酸钙和蒙脱石相。三个玄武岩相的多尺度表征表明，连通孔隙度分数 (1.3--32\%) 与总孔隙度 (18--42\%) 显着不同，表明网络拓扑控制渗透率。微 CT 分析显示，玄武岩中的孔隙配位数（模态 = 2）明显低于储层砂岩中的孔隙配位数，形成的连续流动路径容易受到适度降水造成的灾难性渗透率损失的影响。提出了降水引起的堵塞情景，其中分布的小沉淀物比大量堆积物导致更严重的渗透率下降。与淡水相比，海水的使用使地球化学变得复杂并降低了矿化效率。研究结果强调了对概率反应输运建模框架和现实孔隙拓扑的需求，这与传统的 CCS 操作有根本的不同。
