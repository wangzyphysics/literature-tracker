## Abstract
We combine differential scanning calorimetry, broadband dielectric spectroscopy, and 1 H and 2 H nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) for component-selective studies of molecular reorientation and diffusion in mixtures comprising the dipeptide N -acetyl-glycine-methylamide (NAGMA), which is commonly considered as a protein-backbone model, and deuterated water in a broad temperature range of 140–340 K. For a 7.5 m NAGMA–D 2 O mixture, crystallization is largely avoided, revealing a separation of the dipeptide-dominated α process, which describes the glassy slowdown, from the water-caused ν process. The latter shows a dynamical crossover at T g = 175 K and thermally activated motion governed by a temperature-independent Gaussian-like distribution of activation energies with a mean value of E m = 0.49 eV and a standard deviation of σ E = 0.035 eV in the glassy state. Detailed NMR analyses show that, despite the time-scale separation, rotational-translational coupling is found for water dynamics at least down into the weakly supercooled regime. Moreover, NMR reveals that the ν process involves a quasi-isotropic reorientation of basically all water molecules even below T g , while slow or restricted water reorientation does not occur. Based on our findings, we discuss the temperature-dependent coupling of the dipeptide and water motions. For a 2 m NAGMA–D 2 O mixture, partial crystallization leads to an enhanced temperature dependence. Disentangling the rotational motions of the liquid and crystalline water fractions, we find that the liquid fraction exhibits Arrhenius behavior with E a = 0.89 eV until a dynamical crossover again occurs upon cooling, while the reorientation of the ice fraction highly resembles that in hexagonal bulk ice.

## 摘要（中文）
我们结合差示扫描量热法、宽带介电谱以及 1 H 和 2 H 核磁共振 (NMR)，对包含二肽 N-乙酰基-甘氨酸-甲基酰胺 (NAGMA)（通常被认为是蛋白质骨架模型）和氘化水的混合物中的分子重新定向和扩散进行成分选择性研究，温度范围为 140–340 K。对于 7.5 m NAGMA-D 2 O 混合物，结晶很大程度上避免了，揭示了二肽主导的 α 过程（描述玻璃态减速）与水引起的 ν 过程的分离。后者显示出 T g = 175 K 处的动态交叉和由与温度无关的活化能类高斯分布控制的热活化运动，玻璃态下的平均值为 E m = 0.49 eV，标准偏差为 σ E = 0.035 eV。详细的核磁共振分析表明，尽管存在时间尺度的分离，但至少在弱过冷状态下，仍发现水动力学存在旋转平移耦合。此外，核磁共振表明，ν 过程涉及基本上所有水分子的准各向同性重新取向，甚至低于 T g ，而缓慢或受限的水重新取向不会发生。根据我们的发现，我们讨论了二肽和水运动的温度依赖性耦合。对于 2 m NAGMA-D 2 O 混合物，部分结晶导致温度依赖性增强。解开液体和结晶水部分的旋转运动，我们发现液体部分表现出 E a = 0.89 eV 的阿伦尼乌斯行为，直到冷却时再次发生动态交叉，而冰部分的重新定向与六角块状冰高度相似。
