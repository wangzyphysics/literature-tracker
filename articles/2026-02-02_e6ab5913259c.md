## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22533v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Photonic sintering (PS) offers an ultra-fast, contact-free alternative to conventional sintering and has demonstrated its potential for enhancing the sinterability of acceptor-doped barium zirconate (BZY) ceramics. However, a central challenge in the PS process lies in achieving precise control over thermal self-stabilization in the presence of complex microstructural effects arising from photonic-ray--induced thermal profiles. To elucidate the interplay among thermal fields, microstructural evolution, and PS process parameters, this study establishes a two-scale, non-isothermal simulation framework. The framework integrates macroscopic heat-transfer simulations, incorporating effective heat conduction and photonic-ray--induced volumetric heating in the porous media, with microscopic non-isothermal phase-field sintering simulations that resolve microstructure evolution under local thermal profile. Scale bridging is achieved through a temperature field transferring and mapping that satisfies Hill-Mandel condition between the macroscopic and microscopic simulations, while maintaining synchronization between their asynchronous time-stepping schemes. After calibrating model parameters against experimental measurements, the framework successfully reproduces the experimentally observed porosity inhomogeneity along the sample depth. The influence of enhanced localized mass transport is further examined through a parametric investigation of surface and grain boundary diffusivities. Overall, the proposed framework demonstrates its feasibility and physical interpretability in establishing process-microstructure relationships for the scalable fabrication of high-performance protonic ceramics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22533v1 公告类型：新 摘要：光子烧结 (PS) 为传统烧结提供了一种超快、无接触的替代方案，并已证明其在增强受主掺杂锆酸钡 (BZY) 陶瓷的可烧结性方面的潜力。然而，PS 工艺的一个核心挑战在于，在光子射线引起的热分布产生复杂的微观结构效应的情况下，实现对热自稳定的精确控制。为了阐明热场、微观结构演化和 PS 工艺参数之间的相互作用，本研究建立了一个两尺度、非等温模拟框架。该框架集成了宏观传热模拟，将多孔介质中的有效热传导和光子射线诱导的体积加热与微观非等温相场烧结模拟相结合，解决局部热剖面下的微观结构演变。尺度桥接是通过满足宏观和微观模拟之间的希尔-曼德尔条件的温度场传输和映射来实现的，同时保持异步时间步进方案之间的同步。根据实验测量值校准模型参数后，该框架成功地再现了实验观察到的沿样品深度的孔隙度不均匀性。通过表面和晶界扩散率的参数研究进一步检验了增强的局部质量传输的影响。总体而言，所提出的框架证明了其在建立高性能质子陶瓷可扩展制造的工艺-微观结构关系方面的可行性和物理可解释性。
