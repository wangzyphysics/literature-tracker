## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14022v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine-learning potentials (MLPs) have become important tools for modern molecular simulations. However, developing models that simultaneously achieve high accuracy and high computational efficiency remains a significant challenge. In this work, we extend the recently proposed MB-PIPNet framework to general covalently bonded molecular systems by combining monomer-based energy decomposition, permutationally invariant polynomial (PIP) descriptors, and neural networks within a fragmentation-based strategy. Within this framework, the total potential energy is represented as a sum of effective monomeric contributions, where PIPs provide compact and chemically motivated descriptions of both monomer internal structures and their local chemical environments. As a proof-of-concept application, we apply the MB-PIPNet framework to linear alkanes, using n-Tetradecane as a representative system, and benchmark its performance against established atomistic machine-learning models. The resulting MB-PIPNet potential accurately reproduces reference ab initio electronic energies and reliably captures key molecular properties, including torsional potential energy profiles, harmonic vibrational frequencies, and vibrational power spectra obtained from molecular dynamics simulations. Importantly, MB-PIPNet demonstrates a substantial advantage in computational efficiency over other MLP models for combined energy and force evaluations. These results establish MB-PIPNet as a scalable and efficient framework for constructing MLPs, providing an additional route for large-scale quantum and classical simulations of complex molecular systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14022v1 发布类型：新 摘要：机器学习势（MLP）已成为现代分子模拟的重要工具。然而，开发同时实现高精度和高计算效率的模型仍然是一个重大挑战。在这项工作中，我们通过将基于单体的能量分解、置换不变多项式（PIP）描述符和基于碎片的策略中的神经网络相结合，将最近提出的 MB-PIPNet 框架扩展到一般的共价键分子系统。在此框架内，总势能表示为有效单体贡献的总和，其中 PIP 提供了单体内部结构及其局部化学环境的紧凑且化学驱动的描述。作为概念验证应用程序，我们将 MB-PIPNet 框架应用于线性烷烃，使用正十四烷作为代表系统，并根据已建立的原子机器学习模型对其性能进行基准测试。由此产生的 MB-PIPNet 势能准确地再现参考从头开始电子能量，并可靠地捕获关键分子特性，包括从分子动力学模拟获得的扭转势能分布、简谐振动频率和振动功率谱。重要的是，与其他 MLP 模型相比，MB-PIPNet 在能量和力的组合评估方面表现出计算效率的巨大优势。这些结果将 MB-PIPNet 确立为构建 MLP 的可扩展且高效的框架，为复杂分子系统的大规模量子和经典模拟提供了额外的途径。
