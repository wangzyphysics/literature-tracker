## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08324v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study the spectral statistics and wave-function properties of a one-dimensional quantum system subject to a Cantor-type fractal potential. By analyzing the nearest-neighbor level spacings, inverse participation ratio (IPR), and the scaling behavior of the integrated density of states (IDS), we demonstrate how the self-similar geometry of the potential is imprinted on the quantum spectrum. The energy-resolved level spacings form a hierarchical, filamentary structure, in sharp contrast to those of periodic and random systems. The normalized level-spacing distribution exhibits a bimodal structure, reflecting the deterministic recurrence of spectral gaps. A multifractal analysis of eigenstates reveals critical behavior: the generalized fractal dimensions $D_q$ lie strictly between the limits of extended and localized states, exhibiting a distinct $q$-dependence. Consistently, the IPR indicates the coexistence of quasi-extended and localized features, characteristic of critical wave functions. The IDS shows anomalous power-law scaling at low energies, with an exponent close to the Hausdorff dimension of the underlying Cantor set, indicating that the geometric fractality governs the spectral dimensionality. At higher energies, this scaling crosses over to the semiclassical Weyl law. Our results establish a direct connection between deterministic fractal geometry, hierarchical spectral statistics, and quantum criticality.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08324v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究受康托型分形势影响的一维量子系统的光谱统计和波函数性质。通过分析最近邻能级间距、逆参与比（IPR）和积分态密度（IDS）的标度行为，我们演示了势的自相似几何形状如何印在量子光谱上。能量分辨的能级间距形成分层的丝状结构，与周期性和随机系统的结构形成鲜明对比。归一化能级间距分布呈现出双峰结构，反映了光谱间隙的确定性重现。本征态的多重分形分析揭示了关键行为：广义分形维数 $D_q$ 严格位于扩展态和局域态的极限之间，表现出明显的 $q$ 依赖性。一致地，IPR表明准扩展和局域特征的共存，即临界波函数的特征。 IDS 显示出低能量下的反常幂律标度，其指数接近基础康托集的豪斯多夫维数，表明几何分形控制着光谱维数。在更高的能量下，这种缩放比例与半经典韦尔定律相交叉。我们的结果在确定性分形几何、分层光谱统计和量子临界性之间建立了直接联系。
