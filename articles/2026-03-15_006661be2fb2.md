## Abstract
Nature Communications, Published online: 15 March 2026; doi:10.1038/s41467-026-70800-w Aqueous iron-ion batteries represent a compelling energy storage solution due to the cost-effectiveness, suitable redox potential, and high capacity of Fe negative electrodes. This study activates phosphorus redox activity in a carboxyl small molecule positive electrode for iron-ion batteries.
