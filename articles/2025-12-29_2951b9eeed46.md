## Abstract
arXiv:2103.03115v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations are used for many air flow simulations including road vehicle aerodynamics. Numerical diffusion occurs when local flow direction is not aligned with the mesh lines and when there is a non-zero gradient of the dependent variable in the direction normal to the streamline direction. It has been observed that typical numerical discretization schemes for the Navier-Stokes equations such as first order upwinding produce very accurate solutions without numerical diffusion when the mesh is aligned with the streamline directions. On the other hand, numerical diffusion is maximized when the streamline direction is at an angle of 45{\deg} relative to the mesh line. The amount of numerical diffusion can be reduced by mesh refinements such as aligning mesh lines along the local flow direction or by introducing higher order numerical schemes which may introduce potential numerical instability or additional computational cost. Couple test cases of a simple steady-state incompressible and inviscid air flow convection problem were used to investigate whether numerical diffusion occurs when using Physics Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) that rely on automatic differentiation as opposed to numerical techniques used in traditional Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) solvers. Numerical diffusion was not observed when PINNs were used to solve the Partial Differential Equation (PDE) for the simple convection problem irrespective of flow angle. The PINN correctly simulated the streamwise upwinding, which has great potential to improve the accuracy of Navier-Stokes solvers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2103.03115v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：计算流体动力学 (CFD) 模拟用于许多气流模拟，包括道路车辆空气动力学。当局部流动方向与网格线不对齐并且因变量在垂直于流线方向的方向上存在非零梯度时，就会发生数值扩散。据观察，当网格与流线方向对齐时，纳维-斯托克斯方程的典型数值离散化方案（例如一阶逆风）会产生非常精确的解，而无需数值扩散。另一方面，当流线方向相对于网格线成 45{\deg} 角时，数值扩散最大化。数值扩散量可以通过网格细化来减少，例如沿着局部流动方向对齐网格线，或者通过引入可能引入潜在数值不稳定或额外计算成本的高阶数值方案。使用简单稳态不可压缩和无粘性气流对流问题的几个测试用例来研究在使用依赖于自动微分的物理信息神经网络 (PINN) 时是否会发生数值扩散，而不是传统计算流体动力学 (CFD) 求解器中使用的数值技术。当使用 PINN 求解简单对流问题的偏微分方程 (PDE) 时，无论流动角度如何，都没有观察到数值扩散。 PINN 正确模拟了顺流逆风，这对于提高纳维-斯托克斯求解器的精度具有巨大潜力。
