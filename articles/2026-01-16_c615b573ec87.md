## Abstract
arXiv:2507.06586v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study a time-reversal invariant vortex, namely a spin vortex, in helical superconductors by focusing on its emergent gravitational structure. The topology of the time-reversal invariant vortex is classified by a $\mathbb{Z}_2$ invariant: helical Majorana zero modes appear at the vortex core when the winding number is odd, while no such zero modes exist when it is even. We provide a formal mapping to the theory of gravity to describe this $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topological structure. Identifying a superconducting order parameter as a vielbein in the theory of gravity, we explicitly convert the Bogoliubov-de-Genne Hamiltonian into the Dirac Hamiltonian coupled to a nontrivial gravitational field. Then we find that a gravitational curvature is induced at the vortex core, with its total flux quantized in integer multiples of $\pi$, reflecting the $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topology. Although the curvature vanishes everywhere except at the vortex core, the energy spectrum remains sensitive to the total curvature flux, owing to the gravitational Aharonov-Bohm effect. We further demonstrate that our gravitational framework can be applied to the topological phase transition driven by the vortex-linking precess in three-dimensional helical superconductors such as the He-B phase.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.06586v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们通过关注螺旋超导体中的时间反转不变涡旋，即自旋涡旋，重点研究其出现的引力结构。时间反演不变涡旋的拓扑结构按$\mathbb{Z}_2$不变量分类：当缠绕数为奇数时，涡核处出现螺旋马约拉纳零模态，而当缠绕数为偶数时，则不存在这种零模态。我们提供了引力理论的正式映射来描述这个$\mathbb{Z}_2$拓扑结构。将超导序参数识别为引力理论中的 vielbein，我们明确地将 Bogoliubov-de-Genne 哈密顿量转换为与非平凡引力场耦合的狄拉克哈密顿量。然后我们发现在涡核处产生了引力曲率，其总通量量化为$\pi$的整数倍，反映了$\mathbb{Z}_2$拓扑。尽管除了涡核之外，曲率在所有地方都消失，但由于引力阿哈罗诺夫-玻姆效应，能谱仍然对总曲率通量敏感。我们进一步证明，我们的引力框架可以应用于由三维螺旋超导体（例如 He-B 相）中的涡旋过程驱动的拓扑相变。
