## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07481v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Establishing a complete electronic phase diagram for REFeAsO (RE = rare earth, RE1111) ironbased superconductors has remained experimentally challenging. Here, we report a systematic investigation of PrFeAsO1-xFx over the full nominal fluorine-doping range 0 to 1 and construct the first comprehensive electronic phase diagram for this system. The evolution from the nonsuperconducting parent compound to the fluorine-rich limit reveals a broad dome shaped superconducting region. Structural refinement demonstrates a systematic lattice contraction with increasing fluorine content (x), corroborated by Raman spectroscopy through softening of the Pr(A1g) phonon mode and hardening of the Fe(B1g) mode, confirming effective fluorine incorporation at the oxygen sites. The maximum superconducting transition temperature (Tc) reaches up to 52.3 K, approximately 5 K higher than previous reports for Pr1111. Magnetotransport measurements yield large upper critical fields Hc2(0) exceeding 100 T, while analysis of resistive transition broadening reveals thermally activated flux flow with a crossover from single-vortex to collective pinning regimes. Specific-heat measurements exhibit a reduced jump deltaC/{\gamma}Tc < 1.43, reflecting strong superconducting fluctuations and multiband pairing. These results establish clear structure property correlations and provide a unified description of superconductivity across the entire doping range of the Pr1111 system.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07481v1 公告类型：新 摘要：建立 REFeAsO（RE = 稀土，RE1111）铁基超导体的完整电子相图在实验上仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们报告了在 0 到 1 的整个标称氟掺杂范围内对 PrFeAsO1-xFx 的系统研究，并为该系统构建了第一个全面的电子相图。从非超导母体化合物到富氟极限的演变揭示了一个宽阔的圆顶形超导区域。结构细化表明，随着氟含量 (x) 的增加，系统晶格收缩，拉曼光谱通过 Pr(A1g) 声子模式的软化和 Fe(B1g) 模式的硬化证实了这一点，证实了氟在氧位点的有效结合。最高超导转变温度 (Tc) 高达 52.3 K，比之前报道的 Pr1111 高出约 5 K。磁输运测量产生超过 100 T 的大上临界场 Hc2(0)，而电阻转变展宽分析揭示了热激活通量流，具有从单涡流到集体钉扎状态的交叉。比热测量显示跳跃 deltaC/{\gamma}Tc < 1.43 有所降低，反映了强烈的超导波动和多带配对。这些结果建立了清晰的结构特性相关性，并为 Pr1111 系统整个掺杂范围内的超导性提供了统一的描述。
