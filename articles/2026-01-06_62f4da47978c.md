## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01601v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The amorphous structure of nanoporous carbon electrodes in supercapacitors complicates the establishment of clear links between electrode geometry and capacitance. In this work, we examine how specific structural features govern charge storage and explain capacitance variations among carbide-derived carbon (CDC) electrodes. Two key methodological and mechanistic advances are introduced. First, we propose a physically motivated, electric-field-based definition of the ion sphere of influence, which avoids the ambiguity of radial distribution function-based cutoffs and enables a more reliable characterization of local ion--electrode interactions. Second, we introduce an ion-resolved channel-width descriptor that quantifies pore accessibility by identifying the narrowest passage a counter-ion must traverse to enter a pore, directly linking accessibility to ion decoordination. Using atomistic molecular dynamics simulations of supercapacitors comprising realistic CDC electrodes and a room-temperature ionic liquid electrolyte under applied potentials, we show that carbon atoms located at the edges of graphitic sheets consistently accumulate higher charge than basal-plane atoms across all electrode types. The fraction of edge atoms increases with structural disorder and correlates with enhanced capacitance, in agreement with recent experimental findings linking disorder to charge storage. Furthermore, analysis of pore size and channel width reveals that ion decoordination is governed primarily by channel width rather than pore size alone. Together, these results establish edge atom concentration and pore channel width as decisive structural descriptors controlling charge accumulation in nanoporous carbon supercapacitors, providing clear design guidelines for optimizing electrode architectures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01601v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超级电容器中纳米多孔碳电极的无定形结构使电极几何形状和电容之间建立明确联系变得复杂。在这项工作中，我们研究了特定的结构特征如何控制电荷存储并解释碳化物衍生碳（CDC）电极之间的电容变化。介绍了两个关键的方法和机制进展。首先，我们提出了一种物理驱动的、基于电场的离子影响范围定义，它避免了基于径向分布函数的截止的模糊性，并能够更可靠地表征局部离子-电极相互作用。其次，我们引入了一种离子分辨通道宽度描述符，通过识别反离子必须穿过才能进入孔的最窄通道来量化孔的可及性，从而将可及性与离子脱配直接联系起来。通过在施加电势下对包含真实 CDC 电极和室温离子液体电解质的超级电容器进行原子分子动力学模拟，我们发现位于石墨片边缘的碳原子始终比所有电极类型的基面原子积累更高的电荷。边缘原子的比例随着结构无序性的增加而增加，并与电容的增强相关，这与最近将无序性与电荷存储联系起来的实验结果一致。此外，对孔径和通道宽度的分析表明，离子失配主要由通道宽度而不是单独的孔径控制。总之，这些结果将边缘原子浓度和孔道宽度确定为控制纳米多孔碳超级电容器中电荷积累的决定性结构描述符，为优化电极结构提供了明确的设计指南。
