## Abstract
arXiv:2510.25047v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Although magnetic field line chaos, cantori, and turnstiles underlie the physics of tokamak disruptions, runaway electron damage, stellarator non-resonant divertors, and the most important electromagnetic correction to what are called electrostatic micro-instabilities, these concepts are not well known. These concepts will be defined and applications that illustrate their importance will be discussed.
