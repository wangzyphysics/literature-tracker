## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01617v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic structures encode the underlying symmetries of magnetic materials and play a central role in determining their physical properties. However, reliable magnetic structures are known for only limited compounds. Traditional methods based on first-principles calculations are fundamentally limited by the need to calculate a large space of input magnetic configurations. Here we introduce a symmetry-aided strategy based on Landau's phase transition theory. By utilizing the crystallographic space group and the Wyckoff positions of magnetic ions, we narrow down the initial magnetic configurations to a limited number of candidates via the analysis of irreducible representations. The magnetic ground state is subsequently determined by the lowest energy of those well-seleted magnetic configurations via first-principles calculations. Benchmarking calculations are performed on a subset of the MAGNDATA database with q=0 and fewer than 40 atoms per unit cell, comprising 260 materials. Our method correctly identifies the magnetic structure for 207 of these materials, corresponding to an accuracy of 80%. We further apply our highly efficient method to the compounds with fewer than 20 atoms per unit cell in the Inorganic Crystal Structure Database (ICSD), and establish a database containing 2,800 magnetic materials. As a demonstration of its utility, we apply our magnetic structure database to the systematic identification of 1,070 magnetic topological phases and 389 altermagnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01617v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁性结构编码磁性材料的基本对称性，并在确定其物理特性方面发挥核心作用。然而，已知的可靠磁性结构仅适用于有限的化合物。基于第一性原理计算的传统方法从根本上受到计算大空间输入磁配置的限制。在这里，我们介绍一种基于朗道相变理论的对称辅助策略。通过利用晶体学空间群和磁性离子的威科夫位置，我们通过分析不可约表示将初始磁性构型缩小到有限数量的候选者。随后通过第一性原理计算，由这些精心选择的磁配置中的最低能量确定磁基态。基准计算是在 MAGNDATA 数据库的子集上进行的，其中 q=0，每个晶胞原子数少于 40 个，包含 260 种材料。我们的方法正确识别了其中 207 种材料的磁性结构，准确度达到 80%。我们进一步将我们的高效方法应用于无机晶体结构数据库（ICSD）中每晶胞原子数少于20个的化合物，并建立了包含2,800种磁性材料的数据库。为了证明其实用性，我们应用我们的磁结构数据库来系统识别 1,070 个磁拓扑相和 389 个交变磁体。
