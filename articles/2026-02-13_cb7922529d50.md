## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11884v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Large-amplitude decaying kink oscillations of coronal loops are strongly influenced by nonlinear processes, such as Kelvin-Helmholtz instability (KHI) and turbulence, though comprehensive theory and observational confirmation remain limited. Building on the recently developed theory on nonlinear damping by KHI-induced turbulence in impulsively driven transverse loop oscillations, we investigate its observational signatures using 3D magnetohydrodynamic simulations and forward-modelled EUV images. The simulated oscillations exhibit time-varying frequency shifts and damping rates, which are broadly consistent with nonlinear turbulence-damping theory. Additionally, they exhibit excitation of higher-order modes, slightly increased periods relative to the linear kink period, and reduced displacement amplitudes. These features are generally preserved in synthetic observations, though resolving higher-order modes requires higher spatial resolution than currently available. For loops embedded in a hotter background, hotter channels (e.g., 193 Angstroms) are more sensitive to boundary dynamics, thus their oscillations decay faster with smaller displacements and larger phase shifts than those in cooler channels (e.g., 171 Angstroms). Comparisons of simulated and synthetic oscillations show close agreement at the early stage. At later times, synthetic oscillations exhibit smaller displacements and larger phase shifts, due to turbulence-induced asymmetry in the loop cross-section. Bayesian fitting shows that the initial oscillation amplitude and kink period are robustly constrained, whereas parameters controlling the damping profile are degenerate, indicating that additional observables would aid reliable seismological inference. These results provide a quantitative basis for identifying nonlinear damping and detecting KHI-driven turbulence in transverse loop oscillations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11884v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：尽管综合理论和观测证实仍然有限，但日冕环的大振幅衰减扭结振荡受到非线性过程的强烈影响，例如开尔文-亥姆霍兹不稳定性（KHI）和湍流。基于最近开发的关于脉冲驱动横向环路振荡中 KHI 引起的湍流的非线性阻尼理论，我们使用 3D 磁流体动力学模拟和正演模型 EUV 图像研究其观测特征。模拟振荡表现出随时间变化的频移和阻尼率，这与非线性湍流阻尼理论大致一致。此外，它们还表现出高阶模式的激励、相对于线性扭结周期略有增加的周期以及减小的位移幅度。这些特征通常保留在合成观测中，尽管解析高阶模式需要比当前可用的空间分辨率更高的空间分辨率。对于嵌入较热背景中的环路，较热的通道（例如 193 埃）对边界动力学更敏感，因此与较冷的通道（例如 171 埃）相比，它们的振荡衰减得更快，位移更小，相移更大。模拟振荡和合成振荡的比较在早期阶段显示出密切的一致性。后来，由于湍流引起的环路横截面不对称，合成振荡表现出较小的位移和较大的相移。贝叶斯拟合表明，初始振荡幅度和扭结周期受到严格约束，而控制阻尼剖面的参数是退化的，这表明额外的可观测值将有助于可靠的地震推断。这些结果为识别非线性阻尼和检测横向环路振荡中 KHI 驱动的湍流提供了定量基础。
