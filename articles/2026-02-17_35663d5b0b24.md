## Abstract
arXiv:2508.20163v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We propose extended s-wave altermagnets (sAMs) as a class of magnetic states which are fully gapped, spin-compensated, and feature spin-polarized bands. sAMs are formed through valley-exchange symmetries, which act as momentum-space translations beyond standard crystallographic spin-group classifications. Using an effective two-valley model, we demonstrate that sAMs exhibit isotropic spin splitting, enable spin-selective transport in tailored heterostructures, and give rise to descendant pair density wave order. From a microscopic sAM minimal model, we develop the guiding principles to identify sAMs in quantum magnets.

arXiv:2508.20163v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出扩展 s 波交替磁体 (sAM) 作为一类完全带隙、自旋补偿且具有自旋极化带的磁态。 sAM 是通过谷交换对称性形成的，谷交换对称性充当超出标准晶体学自旋群分类的动量空间转换。使用有效的双谷模型，我们证明了 sAM 表现出各向同性的自旋分裂，能够在定制的异质结构中实现自旋选择性输运，并产生后代对密度波序。从微观 sAM 最小模型出发，我们制定了识别量子磁体中 sAM 的指导原则。
