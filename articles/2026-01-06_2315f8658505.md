## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00906v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Resilient intermodal freight networks are vital for sustaining supply chains amid increasing threats from natural hazards and cyberattacks. While transportation resilience has been widely studied, understanding how random and targeted disruptions affect both structural connectivity and functional performance remains a key challenge. To address this, our study evaluates the robustness of the U.S. intermodal freight network, comprising rail and water modes, using a simulation-based framework that integrates graph-theoretic metrics with flow-weighted centrality measures. We examine disruption scenarios including random failures as well as targeted node and edge removals based on static and dynamically updated degree and betweenness centrality. To reflect more realistic conditions, we also consider flow-weighted degree centralities and partial node degradation. Two resilience indicators are used: the size of the giant connected component (GCC) to measure structural connectivity, and flow-weighted network efficiency (NE) to assess freight mobility under disruption. Results show that progressively degrading nodes ranked by Weighted Degree Centrality to 60% of their original functionality causes a sharper decline in normalized NE, for up to approximately 45 affected nodes, than complete failure (100% loss of functionality) applied to nodes targeted by weighted betweenness centrality or selected at random. This highlights how partial degradation of high-tonnage hubs can produce disproportionately large functional losses. The findings emphasize the need for resilience strategies that go beyond network topology to incorporate freight flow dynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00906v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在自然灾害和网络攻击的威胁日益增加的情况下，有弹性的多式联运货运网络对于维持供应链至关重要。尽管交通弹性已得到广泛研究，但了解随机和有针对性的破坏如何影响结构连通性和功能性能仍然是一个关键挑战。为了解决这个问题，我们的研究使用基于模拟的框架，将图论指标与流量加权中心性度量相结合，评估了美国多式联运货运网络（包括铁路和水路模式）的稳健性。我们检查破坏场景，包括随机故障以及基于静态和动态更新的程度和介数中心性的目标节点和边缘删除。为了反映更现实的情况，我们还考虑了流加权度中心性和部分节点退化。使用两个弹性指标：用于衡量结构连通性的巨型连通组件（GCC）的规模，以及用于评估中断情况下的货运流动性的流量加权网络效率（NE）。结果表明，按加权度中心性排序的节点逐步降级至其原始功能的 60%，会导致归一化 NE 急剧下降，最多大约 45 个受影响的节点，而不是应用于按加权介数中心性定位或随机选择的节点的完全故障（100% 功能丧失）。这凸显了大吨位枢纽的部分退化如何导致不成比例的巨大功能损失。研究结果强调需要超越网络拓扑的弹性策略来纳入货运流动态。
