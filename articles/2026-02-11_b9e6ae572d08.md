## Abstract
arXiv:2409.06740v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Inverse materials design has proven successful in accelerating novel material discovery. Many inverse materials design methods use unsupervised learning where a latent space is learned to offer a compact description of materials representations. A latent space learned this way is likely to be entangled, in terms of the target property and other properties of the materials. This makes the inverse design process ambiguous. Here, we present a semi-supervised learning approach based on a disentangled variational autoencoder to learn a probabilistic relationship between features, latent variables and target properties. This approach is data efficient because it combines all labelled and unlabelled data in a coherent manner, and it uses expert-informed prior distributions to improve model robustness even with limited labelled data. It is in essence interpretable, as the learnable target property is disentangled out of the other properties of the materials, and an extra layer of interpretability can be provided by a post-hoc analysis of the classification head of the model. We demonstrate this new approach on an experimental high-entropy alloy dataset with chemical compositions as input and single-phase formation as the single target property. High-entropy alloys were chosen as example materials because of the vast chemical space of their possible combinations of compositions and atomic configurations. While single property is used in this work, the disentangled model can be extended to customize for inverse design of materials with multiple target properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2409.06740v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：逆向材料设计已被证明可以成功地加速新材料的发现。许多逆向材料设计方法使用无监督学习，其中学习潜在空间以提供材料表示的紧凑描述。就目标属性和材料的其他属性而言，以这种方式学习的潜在空间可能会发生纠缠。这使得逆向设计过程变得模糊。在这里，我们提出了一种基于解缠变分自动编码器的半监督学习方法，用于学习特征、潜在变量和目标属性之间的概率关系。这种方法的数据效率很高，因为它以连贯的方式组合了所有标记和未标记的数据，并且即使在标记数据有限的情况下，它也使用专家知情的先验分布来提高模型的稳健性。它本质上是可解释的，因为可学习的目标属性与材料的其他属性分开，并且可以通过对模型的分类头进行事后分析来提供额外的可解释性层。我们在实验高熵合金数据集上演示了这种新方法，其中化学成分作为输入，单相形成作为单一目标属性。选择高熵合金作为示例材料是因为它们的成分和原子构型可能的组合具有巨大的化学空间。虽然本工作中使用单一属性，但解缠结模型可以扩展以定制具有多个目标属性的材料的逆向设计。
