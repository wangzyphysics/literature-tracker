## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13006v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We present an effective potential that allows quantum thermal expectation values of a position-dependent observable to be estimated as a classical ensemble average of the corresponding function. We follow the approach of Feynman and Hibbs, but perform the mean-field treatment of quantum fluctuations about the path starting point rather than the path centroid. Furthermore, rather than performing a full variational optimization of the potential, we explore approximate functional forms that yield a numerical robustness. The resulting closed-form potential is exact in the classical and harmonic limits; benchmarks against exact position distributions for one-dimensional quartic, Morse, and double-well potentials, show good agreement for potentials with harmonic support.

arXiv:2602.13006v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们提出了一种有效势，允许将位置相关可观测量的量子热期望值估计为相应函数的经典系综平均值。我们遵循费曼和希布斯的方法，但对路径起点而不是路径质心的量子涨落进行平均场处理。此外，我们不是对势进行完全变分优化，而是探索产生数值鲁棒性的近似函数形式。由此产生的闭合形式势在经典和谐波极限下是精确的；针对一维四次、莫尔斯和双阱势的精确位置分布的基准，显示出与谐波支持的势良好的一致性。
