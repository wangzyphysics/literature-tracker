## Abstract
Author(s): Yicheng Tang, Pradip Kattel, and Natan Andrei The spin-½ XXZ chain is a well-known model of spontaneous symmetry breaking. But what is its symmetry-protected topological dual model? Here, the authors construct a fermionic dual model via a modified Jordan–Wigner transformation. They demonstrate its topological nature through exact string order parameters, entanglement spectrum degeneracies, and Majorana zero modes, and identify the duals of the ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic phases as two distinct topological phases separated by an extended Luttinger liquid regime.
