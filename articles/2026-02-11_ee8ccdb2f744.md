## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10091v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Motivated by the recent discovery of superconductivity in the bilayer nickelate La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ (LNO) under pressure, we study a mixed-dimensional (mixD) bilayer $t_\parallel$-$J_\parallel$-$J_\perp$ model, which has been proposed as an effective low-energy description of LNO. Using neural quantum states (NQS), and in particular Gutzwiller-projected Hidden Fermion Pfaffian State, we access the ground-state properties on large lattices up to $8\times 8\times 2$ sites. We show that this model exhibits superconductivity across a wide range of dopings and couplings, and analyze the pairing behavior in detail. We identify a crossover from tightly bound, Bose-Einstein-condensed interlayer pairs at strong interlayer exchange to more spatially extended Bardeen-Cooper-Schrieffer-like pairs as the interlayer exchange is decreased. Furthermore, upon tuning the intralayer exchange, we observe a sharp transition from interlayer $s$-wave pairing to intralayer $d$-wave pairing, consistent with a first-order change in the pairing symmetry. We verify that our simulations are accurate by comparing with matrix product state simulations on coupled ladders. Our results represent the first simulation of a fermionic multi-orbital system with NQS, and provide the first evidence for superconductivity in two-dimensonal bilayers using high-precision numerics. These findings provide insight into superconductivity in bilayer nickelates and cold atom quantum simulation platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10091v1 公告类型：新 摘要：受最近发现双层镍酸盐 La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ (LNO) 在压力下超导性的启发，我们研究了混合维 (mixD) 双层 $t_\parallel$-$J_\parallel$-$J_\perp$ 模型，该模型已被提议作为 LNO 的有效低能描述。使用神经量子态（NQS），特别是 Gutzwiller 投影的隐藏费米子普法夫态，我们可以访问高达 $8\times 8\times 2$ 位点的大晶格上的基态属性。我们证明该模型在各种掺杂和耦合下表现出超导性，并详细分析了配对行为。我们发现，随着层间交换的减少，从强层间交换时紧密结合的玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚层间对到更多空间扩展的类巴丁-库珀-施里弗对的交叉。此外，在调整层内交换时，我们观察到从层间 $s$ 波配对到层内 $d$ 波配对的急剧转变，与配对对称性的一阶变化一致。我们通过与耦合梯子上的矩阵乘积状态模拟进行比较来验证我们的模拟是否准确。我们的结果代表了首次使用 NQS 模拟费米子多轨道系统，并使用高精度数值为二维双层中的超导性提供了第一个证据。这些发现为双层镍酸盐和冷原子量子模拟平台的超导性提供了深入的见解。
