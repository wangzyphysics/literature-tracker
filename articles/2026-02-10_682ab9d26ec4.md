## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07976v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: As the first experimentally discovered intrinsic magnetic topological insulator, MnBi2Te4 has attracted widespread attentions, providing a unique platform for the exploration of topological quantum phases, such as quantum anomalous Hall effect and axion insulator state. Despite the increasing number of potential factors affecting samples being identified, obtaining the high-quality device performance with desired topological quantum phases remains a challenge. In this work, by comparing the reflective magnetic circular dichroism (RMCD) of crystals with different defect densities that are characterized by atomically resolved scanning tunneling microscopy, we demonstrate that anti-site defects play an essential role in achieving ideal magnetic states. By measuring RMCD hysteresis loops with operando heating, we find that MnBi2Te4 few-layer samples are highly susceptible to thermal impact, even at temperature as low as 45{\deg}C. The magnetic behavior of heating-treated samples is akin to that of samples fabricated into devices, revealing the thermal impact on devices as well. Starting from few-layers with ideal layer-dependent magnetic order, thermal heating leads to the convergence of magnetization and transition fields between odd- and even-layers. The observed heating-induced magnetic evolution can serve as a valuable reference for assessing the sample quality or the density of anti-site defects. Our findings not only point out the long-standing hidden factor that arose controversies in MnBi2Te4, but also pave the way for controllably engineering the topological quantum phenomena.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07976v1 发布类型：新 摘要：作为第一个通过实验发现的本征磁拓扑绝缘体，MnBi2Te4 引起了广泛的关注，为探索量子反常霍尔效应和轴子绝缘体态等拓扑量子相提供了独特的平台。尽管影响待识别样品的潜在因素越来越多，但获得具有所需拓扑量子相的高质量器件性能仍然是一个挑战。在这项工作中，通过比较原子分辨扫描隧道显微镜表征的具有不同缺陷密度的晶体的反射磁圆二色性（RMCD），我们证明反位点缺陷在实现理想磁态方面发挥着重要作用。通过使用操作加热测量 RMCD 磁滞回线，我们发现 MnBi2Te4 几层样品非常容易受到热影响，即使在低至 45{\deg}C 的温度下也是如此。热处理样品的磁性行为与制造成器件的样品的磁性行为类似，也揭示了对器件的热影响。从具有理想的层相关磁序的几层开始，热加热导致奇数层和偶数层之间磁化强度和过渡场的收敛。观察到的加热引起的磁演化可以作为评估样品质量或反位点缺陷密度的有价值的参考。我们的研究结果不仅指出了MnBi2Te4中长期存在的引起争议的隐藏因素，而且为可控地工程拓扑量子现象铺平了道路。
