## Abstract
arXiv:2509.26267v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In biological tissue, MR transverse relaxation stems from mechanisms spanning multiple scales, from molecular dipole-dipole interactions to mesoscopic field variations driven by tissue microstructure. While mesoscopic relaxation reflects cellular organization, its dynamics in white matter, specifically its dependence on axonal orientation and echo time, remain less investigated. This study employs theoretical frameworks and Monte-Carlo simulations using 3D EM-based white matter (WM) geometries to investigate how compartmentalization and realistic morphology drive these effects. Specifically, we simulate intra-axonal relaxation driven by magnetic fields induced by realistic axonal myelin sheaths and intra-axonal spheres as a model of iron-containing mitochondria. Our results confirm that orientation dependence of mesoscopic relaxation in WM is detectable and agrees with experimental observations. The time-dependence aligns with 1-dimensional short-range structural disorder, but at clinical echo times, this signature may be masked by dominant molecular relaxation. This work moves beyond idealized models to aid the development of more specific biophysical models of mesoscopic relaxation to achieve better biomarkers for neurodegenerative disease.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.26267v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：在生物组织中，MR 横向弛豫源于跨越多个尺度的机制，从分子偶极子-偶极子相互作用到组织微观结构驱动的介观场变化。虽然介观松弛反映了细胞组织，但其在白质中的动态，特别是其对轴突方向和回声时间的依赖性，仍然较少被研究。本研究采用理论框架和蒙特卡罗模拟，使用基于 3D EM 的白质 (WM) 几何形状来研究分区和现实形态如何驱动这些效应。具体来说，我们模拟由真实的轴突髓鞘和轴突内球体诱导的磁场驱动的轴突内松弛，作为含铁线粒体的模型。我们的结果证实，WM 中细观弛豫的方向依赖性是可检测到的，并且与实验观察结果一致。时间依赖性与一维短程结构紊乱一致，但在临床回波时间，该特征可能被占主导地位的分子弛豫所掩盖。这项工作超越了理想化模型，有助于开发更具体的介观松弛生物物理模型，以获得更好的神经退行性疾病生物标志物。
