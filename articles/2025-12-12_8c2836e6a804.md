# Mammo-MX: an x-ray mammography dataset for computer-aided diagnosis of breast cancer

## Abstract
Breast cancer has been the leading cause of cancer-related deaths among Mexican women since 2006, underscoring the need for improved diagnostic tools and accessible datasets for artificial intelligence (AI). We introduce Mammo-MX, a publicly available large-scale mammography dataset specifically focused on Mexican patients, addressing a critical gap in well-labeled data for deep learning applications. The dataset was acquired between 2023-2024 at the Jalisco Breast Clinic using a HOLOGIC® Selenia Dimensions full-field digital mammography system, ensuring high-quality imaging. After rigorous curation, Mammo-MX comprises 13,659 mammograms from 3,368 patients with standard craniocaudal (CC) and mediolateral-oblique (MLO) views. Each study was annotated by expert radiologists according to the BI-RADS classification system, and it includes breast density assessments and extensive acquisition metadata. By offering a robust, well-labeled, and freely accessible dataset, Mammo-MX fills a void in current resources, enabling the development of AI models tailored to the Mexican population while also strengthening the global diversity and generalizability of computer-aided diagnostic tools for breast cancer.

## 摘要（中文）
自 2006 年以来，乳腺癌一直是墨西哥女性癌症相关死亡的主要原因，这凸显了改进诊断工具和可访问的人工智能 (AI) 数据集的必要性。我们推出了 Mammo-MX，这是一个专门针对墨西哥患者的公开大型乳房 X 线摄影数据集，解决了深度学习应用中标记良好的数据的关键缺口。该数据集是 2023 年至 2024 年在哈利斯科州乳腺诊所使用 HOLOGIC® Selenia Dimensions 全视野数字乳房 X 线摄影系统获取的，确保了高质量的成像。经过严格管理，Mammo-MX 包含来自 3,368 名患者的 13,659 张乳房 X 光照片，具有标准头尾 (CC) 和内外侧斜 (MLO) 视图。每项研究均由放射科医生专家根据 BI-RADS 分类系统进行注释，其中包括乳腺密度评估和广泛的采集元数据。通过提供强大、标记良好且可免费访问的数据集，Mammo-MX 填补了当前资源的空白，从而能够开发适合墨西哥人口的人工智能模型，同时还加强了乳腺癌计算机辅助诊断工具的全球多样性和通用性。
