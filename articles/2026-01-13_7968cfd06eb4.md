## Abstract
arXiv:2505.03915v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We present a first-principles approach for calculating phonon-polariton dispersion relations. In this approach, phonon-photon interaction is described by quantization of a Hamiltonian that describes harmonic lattice vibrations coupled with the electromagnetic field inside the material. All Hamiltonian parameters are obtained from first-principles calculations, with diagonalization leading to non-interacting polariton quasiparticles. This method naturally includes retardation effects and resolves non-analytical behavior and ambiguities in phonon frequencies at the Brillouin zone center, especially in non-cubic and optically anisotropic materials. Furthermore, by incorporating higher-order terms in the Hamiltonian, we also account for quasiparticle interactions and spectral broadening. Specifically, we show how anharmonic effects in phonon polaritons lead to a dielectric response that challenges traditional models. The accuracy and consequences of the approach are demonstrated on GaP and GaN as harmonic test systems and PbTe and $\beta$-Ga$_2$O$_3$ as anharmonic test systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.03915v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一种计算声子极化子色散关系的第一原理方法。在这种方法中，声子-光子相互作用是通过哈密顿量的量化来描述的，哈密顿量描述了与材料内部电磁场耦合的谐波晶格振动。所有哈密顿参数都是通过第一性原理计算获得的，对角化导致非相互作用的极化子准粒子。该方法自然包括延迟效应，并解决了布里渊区中心声子频率的非分析行为和模糊性，特别是在非立方和光学各向异性材料中。此外，通过在哈密顿量中纳入高阶项，我们还考虑了准粒子相互作用和光谱展宽。具体来说，我们展示了声子极化子中的非谐波效应如何导致挑战传统模型的介电响应。该方法的准确性和结果在作为谐波测试系统的 GaP 和 GaN 以及作为非谐波测试系统的 PbTe 和 $\beta$-Ga$_2$O$_3$ 上得到了证明。
