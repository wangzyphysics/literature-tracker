## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09391v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We construct a generalized time-dependent Ginzburg-Landau model to demonstrate the possibility of inducing p- and d-wave components in an originally pure s-wave centrosymmetric superconductor via microwave radiation. In this framework, specializing to $O_h$ point-group symmetry, we introduce gradient terms that couple the s-wave superconducting order parameter with other symmetry-allowed components. The singlet-to-singlet gradient terms are quadratic in spatial derivatives, while, in the presence of spin-orbit coupling, linear-in-derivatives terms coupling singlet and triplet order parameters are also permitted. Through the minimal substitution procedure, these terms enable coupling between different superconducting order parameters via the vector potential, thereby leading to the generation of p-wave, d-wave, and other symmetry-allowed components. Such a manipulation of the superconducting state locally via a microwave beam could be considered as one more facet of the concept of quantum printing.

arXiv:2602.09391v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们构建了一个广义的时间相关的 Ginzburg-Landau 模型，以证明通过微波辐射在原始纯 s 波中心对称超导体中感应 p 波和 d 波分量的可能性。在这个专门针对 $O_h$ 点群对称性的框架中，我们引入了梯度项，将 s 波超导序参数与其他允许对称的分量耦合起来。单重态到单重态的梯度项在空间导数中是二次的，而在存在自旋轨道耦合的情况下，也允许耦合单重态和三重态阶参数的线性导数项。通过最小替换过程，这些项能够通过矢量势在不同的超导序参数之间进行耦合，从而导致 p 波、d 波和其他允许对称的分量的生成。这种通过微波束局部操纵超导态可以被视为量子打印概念的另一个方面。
