## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00111v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: On physical grounds, one expects locally interacting quantum many-body systems to feature a finite group velocity. This intuition is rigorously underpinned by Lieb-Robinson bounds that state that locally interacting Hamiltonians with finite-dimensional constituents on suitably regular lattices always exhibit such a finite group velocity. This also implies that causality is always respected by the dynamics of quantum lattice models. It had been a long-standing open question whether interacting bosonic systems also feature finite speeds of sound in information and particle propagation, which was only recently resolved. This work proves a strikingly simple such bound for particle propagation - shown in literally a few elementary, yet not straightforward, lines - for generalized Bose-Hubbard models defined on general lattices, proving that appropriately locally perturbed stationary states feature a finite speed of sound in particle numbers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00111v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：从物理角度来看，人们期望局部相互作用的量子多体系统具有有限的群速度。这种直觉受到李布-罗宾逊界限的严格支持，该界限指出，在适当的规则晶格上具有有限维成分的局部相互作用的哈密顿量总是表现出如此有限的群速度。这也意味着量子晶格模型的动力学始终尊重因果关系。相互作用的玻色子系统是否也具有信息和粒子传播中有限声速的特征，一直是一个长期悬而未决的问题，这个问题最近才得到解决。这项工作证明了在一般晶格上定义的广义 Bose-Hubbard 模型的粒子传播的极其简单的边界（实际上用几条基本但不简单的线表示），证明了适当局部扰动的稳态具有粒子数中的有限声速。
