## Abstract
Reactive machine learning potentials (MLPs) significantly benefits high-throughput exploration of complex reaction networks. However, the development of such MLPs is currently constrained by the scarcity of datasets generated through efficient sampling methods that adequately capture elusive non-equilibrium configurations. To address this, we introduce an integrated molecular dynamics/coordinate driving-active learning (MD/CD-AL) framework that strategically samples reactive configurations, yielding a compact but comprehensive dataset, MDCD20, with ~1.4 million neutral and radical H/C/N/O structures. The MDCD-NN MLP trained on MDCD20 surpasses existing available MLPs trained on much larger datasets in reconstructing 181 elementary reactions with widespread types. It also accelerates automatic reaction network exploration by 10^4-fold in real-world systems relative to its reference quantum chemistry (QM) calculations, tackling complex scenarios such as multiple reaction centers, enantioselectivity and dynamic free-energy landscapes that are beyond the reach of traditional QM methods. This work establishes an efficient paradigm for constructing reactive datasets, enabling the training of reliable MLPs for complex reactive systems at an affordable cost.

## 摘要（中文）
反应式机器学习势 (MLP) 显着有利于复杂反应网络的高通量探索。然而，此类 MLP 的发展目前受到缺乏通过有效采样方法生成的数据集的限制，这些数据集足以捕获难以捉摸的非平衡配置。为了解决这个问题，我们引入了一个集成的分子动力学/坐标驱动主动学习（MD/CD-AL）框架，该框架战略性地对反应构型进行采样，产生一个紧凑但全面的数据集 MDCD20，其中包含约 140 万中性和自由基 H/C/N/O 结构。在 MDCD20 上训练的 MDCD-NN MLP 在重建 181 个具有广泛类型的基本反应方面超过了在更大的数据集上训练的现有 MLP。相对于其参考量子化学 (QM) 计算，它还能将现实系统中的自动反应网络探索速度加快 10^4 倍，解决传统 QM 方法无法达到的复杂场景，例如多反应中心、对映选择性和动态自由能景观。这项工作为构建反应式数据集建立了一个有效的范例，使得能够以可承受的成本为复杂的反应式系统训练可靠的 MLP。
