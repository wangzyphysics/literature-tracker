## Abstract
Author(s): Poulomi Chakraborty, Aaron Hui, Grigory Bednik, and Brian Skinner This work provides theoretical calculations of the Seebeck and Nernst coefficients of nodal line semimetals as a function of temperature and applied magnetic field, finding a remarkable enhancement of the magnetothermoelectric performance at low temperature relative to other materials. [PRX Energy 3, 023007] Published Thu Jun 20, 2024
