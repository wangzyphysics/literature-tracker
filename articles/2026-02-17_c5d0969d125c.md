## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13797v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurately simulating the non-Markovian dynamics of open quantum systems remains a significant challenge. While the recently proposed time-evolving matrix product operator (TEMPO) algorithm based on path integrals successfully circumvents the exponential scaling associated with memory length, its reliance on layer-by-layer tensor contractions and compressions leads to steep scaling with respect to the system Hilbert space dimension. In this work, we introduce the effective Hamiltonian-based TEMPO (EH-TEMPO) algorithm, which reformulates the calculation of the Feynman-Vernon influence functional as an imaginary time evolution governed by an effective Hamiltonian. We demonstrate that this effective Hamiltonian admits a highly compact matrix product operator representation, enabling substantial compression with negligible loss of accuracy. Combining a one-shot global evolution with a backward retrieval approach, EH-TEMPO significantly reduces algorithmic complexity and is naturally suited for GPU acceleration. We benchmark the method against the process tensor TEMPO algorithm using the 7-site Fenna-Matthews-Olson complex model. The results demonstrate that EH-TEMPO achieves numerically exact accuracy with superior efficiency, delivering speedups of up to 17.5x on GPU hardware compared to standard CPU implementations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13797v1 公告类型：新 摘要：准确模拟开放量子系统的非马尔可夫动力学仍然是一个重大挑战。虽然最近提出的基于路径积分的时间演化矩阵乘积算子（TEMPO）算法成功地规避了与内存长度相关的指数缩放，但其对逐层张量收缩和压缩的依赖导致系统希尔伯特空间维度的陡峭缩放。在这项工作中，我们介绍了基于有效哈密顿量的 TEMPO (EH-TEMPO) 算法，该算法将 Feynman-Vernon 影响函数的计算重新表述为由有效哈密顿量控制的虚时间演化。我们证明，这种有效的哈密顿量允许高度紧凑的矩阵乘积算子表示，从而实现大幅压缩，而精度损失可以忽略不计。 EH-TEMPO 将一次性全局进化与向后检索方法相结合，显着降低了算法复杂性，自然适合 GPU 加速。我们使用 7 站点 Fenna-Matthews-Olson 复杂模型针对过程张量 TEMPO 算法对该方法进行基准测试。结果表明，EH-TEMPO 实现了数值精确度和卓越的效率，与标准 CPU 实现相比，在 GPU 硬件上提供高达 17.5 倍的加速。
