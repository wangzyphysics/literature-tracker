## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08201v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Field-angle-resolved specific-heat measurements were performed on a clean single crystal of a spin-triplet superconductor UTe$_2$ with $T_{\rm c}=2.1$ K and a low residual electronic specific heat. At low temperatures, the specific heat exhibits a linear dependence on the magnetic field when the field is applied precisely along the $b$ axis, in stark contrast to its rapid increase at low fields for other orientations. This pronounced anisotropy suggests the presence of nodal quasiparticle excitations with the Fermi velocity predominantly aligned along the $b$ axis. Considering the characteristic field-angle dependences of both the specific heat and the upper critical field, these observations are broadly compatible with theoretical models that assume a superconducting gap structure featuring either point nodes consistent with $B_{\rm 2u}$ symmetry, allowed in the infinitely strong spin-orbit coupling scheme, or line nodes confined to flat regions of the quasi-two-dimensional Fermi surface, consistent with $^3B_{\rm 3u}$ symmetry in the finite spin-orbit classification scheme. These results yield crucial hints for resolving the pairing symmetry of UTe$_2$, paving the way for a deeper understanding of its spin-triplet superconductivity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08201v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在自旋三重态超导体 UTe$_2$ 的干净单晶上进行场角分辨比热测量，其中 $T_{\rm c}=2.1$ K 和低残余电子比热。在低温下，当磁场沿着 $b$ 轴精确施加时，比热表现出与磁场的线性相关性，这与其他方向的低磁场下比热的快速增加形成鲜明对比。这种明显的各向异性表明存在节点准粒子激发，费米速度主要沿 $b$ 轴排列。考虑到比热和上临界场的特征场角依赖性，这些观察结果与假设超导间隙结构的理论模型广泛兼容，该结构具有与 $B_{\rm 2u}$ 对称性一致的点节点（在无限强自旋轨道耦合方案中允许），或限制在准二维费米表面的平坦区域的线节点，与有限自旋轨道分类中的 $^3B_{\rm 3u}$ 对称性一致计划。这些结果为解决 UTe$_2$ 的配对对称性提供了重要的线索，为更深入地理解其自旋三重态超导性铺平了道路。
