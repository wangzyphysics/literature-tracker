## Abstract
Microporous crystalline adsorbents such as zeolites, and metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) have potential use in a wide variety of separations applications. The evaluation of the separation performance requires the measurement of data on unary isotherms of the guest molecules in the microporous host materials; such measurements are usually conducted for a range of temperatures. The experimental data on the unary isotherms may be fitted with a variety of models such as 1-site Langmuir , 1-site Langmuir-Freundlich, and Dual-Site Langmuir-Freundlich (DSLF). The fitted isotherms are then used for calculation of the adsorption selectivity, , using the Ideal Adsorbed Solution Theory (IAST). The isotherm fits at different temperatures allow the determination of the isosteric heats of adsorption . By careful and detailed examination of the experimental data of more than sixty guest/host combinations at different temperatures, we conclude that the saturation capacities and Freundlich exponents are essentially temperature-independent. For any guest/host combination, the optimal fits of the sets of unary isotherm data at different temperatures are achieved by constraining the the saturation capacities and Freundlich exponents to be independent.

## 摘要（中文）
微孔晶体吸附剂如沸石和金属有机框架（MOF）在各种分离应用中具有潜在的用途。分离性能的评价需要测量微孔主体材料中客体分子的一元等温线数据；此类测量通常在一定温度范围内进行。一元等温线的实验数据可以用多种模型拟合，例如 1 位 Langmuir 、1 位 Langmuir-Freundlich 和双位 Langmuir-Freundlich (DSLF)。然后使用理想吸附溶液理论 (IAST)，将拟合的等温线用于计算吸附选择性 。不同温度下的等温线拟合可以确定等量吸附热。通过对不同温度下六十多种客体/主体组合的实验数据进行仔细和详细的检查，我们得出结论，饱和容量和弗罗因德利希指数基本上与温度无关。对于任何客体/主机组合，不同温度下一元等温线数据集的最佳拟合是通过将饱和容量和 Freundlich 指数约束为独立来实现的。
