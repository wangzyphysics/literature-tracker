## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21951v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: When two reservoirs of a distinct salinity are connected by channels or pores, a fluid flow termed diffusio-osmotic is generated. This article investigates the flow emerging in an uniformly charged long slit whose thickness exceeds the local Debye screening length. Attention is focussed on the role of hydrostatic pressure drop $\Delta p$ between reservoirs. For a thick slit we recover the known formula for a local diffusioosmotic slip over a single wall, which is determined by the surface potential, salt concentration and its gradient. An equation for the global fluid flow rate $\mathcal{Q}$ is presented as a sum of the diffusio-osmotic and pressure-driven contributions. Although the diffusio-osmotic term itself remains unaffected by $\Delta p$, the concentration and surface potential profiles along the slit, and consequently, the local slip velocity are dramatically modified. We present an equation relating the local concentration to $\mathcal{Q}$ and employ it to derive an expression describing the surface potential variation in the slit. Since $\mathcal{Q}$ can easily be tuned by $\Delta p$, the variety of possible concentration and surface potential profiles becomes very rich. Our theory provides a simple explanation of recent flow rate measurements and shows that experimental data provide rather direct information about concentration and surface potential profiles in the uniformly charged slit. The relevance of our results for sensing the salt dependence of surface potentials is discussed briefly.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21951v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：当两个不同盐度的储层通过通道或孔隙连接时，会产生称为扩散渗透的流体流动。本文研究了在厚度超过局部德拜屏蔽长度的均匀带电长狭缝中出现的流动。注意力集中在水库之间静水压降 $\Delta p$ 的作用。对于厚缝，我们恢复了单壁上局部扩散渗透滑移的已知公式，该公式由表面电势、盐浓度及其梯度决定。全局流体流速 $\mathcal{Q}$ 的方程表示为扩散渗透和压力驱动贡献的总和。尽管扩散渗透项本身不受 $\Delta p$ 的影响，但沿狭缝的浓度和表面电势分布以及因此局部滑移速度都会发生显着改变。我们提出了一个将局部浓度与 $\mathcal{Q}$ 联系起来的方程，并用它来推导描述狭缝中表面电势变化的表达式。由于$\mathcal{Q}$可以很容易地通过$\Delta p$进行调整，因此可能的浓度和表面电位分布的多样性变得非常丰富。我们的理论对最近的流速测量提供了简单的解释，并表明实验数据提供了有关均匀带电狭缝中的浓度和表面电势分布的相当直接的信息。简要讨论了我们的结果与感测表面电势的盐依赖性的相关性。
