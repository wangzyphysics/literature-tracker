## Abstract
arXiv:2502.07897v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We report Dirac Landau polaritons observed by terahertz (THz) magnetoreflectivity spectroscopy, demonstrating strong coupling between cyclotron transitions of two-dimensional (2D) Dirac fermions in HgTe quantum wells and optical cavity modes. Under pulsed electrical injection we observe efficient nonlinear electroluminescence, with a strongly out-of-equilibrium polariton distribution dominated by emission from the upper polariton branches. Model analysis of the bias-dependent emission intensity and spectral narrowing indicates a polariton occupancy per mode approaching unity, with a possible contribution from stimulated polariton emission in the spectral region of the upper anticrossing. These results open prospects toward Dirac Landau polariton condensates and low-threshold, tunable THz polariton lasers based on cyclotron emission.

arXiv:2502.07897v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们报告了通过太赫兹 (THz) 磁反射光谱观测到的狄拉克朗道极化子，证明了 HgTe 量子阱和光腔模式中二维 (2D) 狄拉克费米子的回旋跃迁之间的强耦合。在脉冲电注入下，我们观察到高效的非线性电致发光，其强烈不平衡的极化子分布主要是来自上极化子分支的发射。依赖于偏差的发射强度和光谱变窄的模型分析表明，每个模式的极化子占据接近统一，可能来自上部反交叉光谱区域中的受激极化子发射。这些结果为狄拉克朗道极化子凝聚体和基于回旋加速器发射的低阈值、可调谐太赫兹极化子激光器开辟了前景。
