## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22566v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We theoretically study the emergence of topological magnons in electron-magnon coupled systems. The magnon dispersion in a ferromagnet usually possesses an effective time reversal symmetry in the absence of Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya (DM) interaction, preventing the appearance of topological magnons. When a spin system is coupled to itinerant electrons, we find that the magnon band structure of the spin system experiences time-reversal symmetry breaking with the electron-magnon interaction via the exchange coupling, where topological magnons arise without requiring strong DM. Specifically, we consider a heterostructure consisting of a ferromagnetic insulator and a transition metal dichalcogenide (TMD) monolayer and investigate topological gap opening in magnon bands. Our findings reveal that even trivial ferromagnets can host topological magnons via coupling to itinerant electronic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22566v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们从理论上研究了电子-磁振子耦合系统中拓扑磁振子的出现。在没有 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya (DM) 相互作用的情况下，铁磁体中的磁振子色散通常具有有效的时间反转对称性，从而防止拓扑磁振子的出现。当自旋系统与流动电子耦合时，我们发现自旋系统的磁振子能带结构通过交换耦合随着电子-磁振子相互作用而经历时间反转对称性破缺，其中拓扑磁振子的出现不需要强DM。具体来说，我们考虑由铁磁绝缘体和过渡金属二硫属化物（TMD）单层组成的异质结构，并研究磁振子带中的拓扑能隙开口。我们的研究结果表明，即使是微不足道的铁磁体也可以通过与流动电子系统的耦合来容纳拓扑磁振子。
