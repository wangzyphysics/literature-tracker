## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03744v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We demonstrate robust and reversible electric-current control of the anomalous Hall effect (AHE) in a two-dimensional WTe2/Fe3GeTe2 (FGT) stack. Applying a current through Td-WTe2 leads to a giant modulation of the AHE of the adjacent FGT layer, with the relative change of the AHE conductivity exceeding 180%. Control experiments show that i) the observed effect is absent in pure FGT, ii) the modulation weakens in thicker FGT films, confirming its interfacial origin, and iii) the modulation peaks for bilayer WTe2, indicating that the Berry-curvature dipole (BCD) plays the dominant role in the modulation. We propose that the charge current I generates an out-of-plane magnetization Mz via BCD in WTe2 and Mz modifies the exchange splitting of FGT via the inverse magnetic proximity effect, thereby altering its Berry curvature and nontrivially influencing the AHE. The demonstrated method of AHE control offers new possibilities for magnetism control, i.e., for the study of AHE-transistors as well as electric-current control of quantum magnets, especially magnetic insulators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03744v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们展示了二维 WTe2/Fe3GeTe2 (FGT) 堆栈中反常霍尔效应 (AHE) 的稳健且可逆的电流控制。通过 Td-WTe2 施加电流会导致相邻 FGT 层的 AHE 发生巨大调制，AHE 电导率的相对变化超过 180%。对照实验表明，i) 在纯 FGT 中不存在观察到的效应，ii) 在较厚的 FGT 薄膜中调制减弱，证实了其界面起源，以及 iii) 双层 WTe2 的调制峰，表明贝里曲率偶极子 (BCD) 在调制中起主导作用。我们提出充电电流 I 通过 WTe2 中的 BCD 产生平面外磁化强度 Mz，并且 Mz 通过逆磁邻近效应修改 FGT 的交换分裂，从而改变其贝里曲率并对 AHE 产生重要影响。所演示的 AHE 控制方法为磁控制（即 AHE 晶体管的研究以及量子磁体（尤其是磁绝缘体）的电流控制）提供了新的可能性。
