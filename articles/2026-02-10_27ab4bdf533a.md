## Abstract
arXiv:2512.16545v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Copper nanoparticles (Cu NPs) have a broad applicability, yet their synthesis is sensitive to subtle changes in reaction parameters. This sensitivity, combined with the time- and resource-intensive nature of experimental optimization, poses a major challenge in achieving reproducible and size-controlled synthesis. While Machine Learning (ML) shows promise in materials research, its application is often limited by scarcity of large high-quality experimental data sets. This study explores ML to predict the size of Cu NPs from microwave-assisted polyol synthesis using a small data set of 25 in-house performed syntheses. Latin Hypercube Sampling is used to efficiently cover the parameter space while creating the experimental data set. Ensemble regression models successfully predict particle sizes with high accuracy ($R^2 = 0.74$), outperforming classical statistical approaches ($R^2 = 0.60$). Additionally, classification models using both random forests and Large Language Models (LLMs) are evaluated to distinguish between large and small particles. While random forests show moderate performance, LLMs offer no significant advantages under data-scarce conditions. Overall, this study demonstrates that carefully curated small data sets, paired with robust classical ML, can effectively predict the synthesis of Cu NPs and highlights that for lab-scale studies, complex models like LLMs may offer limited benefit over simpler techniques.

arXiv:2512.16545v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：铜纳米粒子（Cu NPs）具有广泛的适用性，但其合成对反应参数的细微变化很敏感。这种敏感性，加上实验优化的时间和资源密集型性质，对实现可重复和尺寸控制的合成提出了重大挑战。虽然机器学习 (ML) 在材料研究中展现出良好的前景，但其应用往往因缺乏大型高质量实验数据集而受到限制。本研究利用 25 个内部合成的小数据集探索 ML 来预测微波辅助多元醇合成中 Cu NP 的尺寸。拉丁超立方采样用于在创建实验数据集时有效覆盖参数空间。集成回归模型成功地以高精度预测颗粒大小 ($R^2 = 0.74$)，优于经典统计方法 ($R^2 = 0.60$)。此外，还评估使用随机森林和大型语言模型 (LLM) 的分类模型，以区分大颗粒和小颗粒。虽然随机森林表现出中等的性能，但法学硕士在数据稀缺的条件下没有提供显着的优势。总体而言，这项研究表明，精心策划的小数据集与强大的经典机器学习相结合，可以有效地预测 Cu NP 的合成，并强调对于实验室规模的研究，像 LLM 这样的复杂模型可能比更简单的技术提供有限的好处。
