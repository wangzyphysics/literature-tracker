## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04375v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurate 3D dose calculation for Pencil Beam Scanning Proton Therapy (PBSPT) is typically performed with Monte Carlo (MC) engines, but their runtimes limit adaptive workflows and repeated evaluations. Current deep-learning proton dose engines often require orthogonality between proton rays and the CT grid, forcing computationally expensive beamlet-wise 3D reinterpolation. We propose the Angle-dependent Dose Transformer Algorithm (ADoTA), which eliminates grid rotation by augmenting the model input with a fast analytical beamlet-shape projection that explicitly encodes beam direction. The model was trained on CT data from 108 patients to predict beamlet dose distributions for initial energies of $70$--$270\,\mathrm{MeV}$ over an $80\times110\,\mathrm{mm}^2$ field, and tested on an independent cohort of 50 patients. On the test set, gamma pass rates $(1\%,3\,\mathrm{mm})$ were $99.40\pm0.86\%$ (thorax) and $99.87\pm0.23\%$ (abdomen/pelvis). Single-beamlet inference took $1.72\pm0.8\,\mathrm{ms}$. By avoiding reinterpolation, end-to-end 3D dose computation was reduced by $\approx86\%$ relative to the fastest published reinterpolation-based methods. For full treatment plans, gamma pass rates $\Gamma(2\%,2\,\mathrm{mm})$ with a 10\% dose cut-off reached $98.4\%$ (lung) and $98.9\%$ (prostate). ADoTA provides an angle-aware deep-learning proton dose engine that preserves MC-level accuracy across heterogeneous anatomies while substantially reducing computational overhead.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04375v1 公告类型：新 摘要：笔形束扫描质子治疗 (PBSPT) 的精确 3D 剂量计算通常使用蒙特卡罗 (MC) 引擎执行，但其运行时间限制了自适应工作流程和重复评估。当前的深度学习质子剂量引擎通常需要质子射线和 CT 网格之间的正交性，从而迫使计算成本高昂的逐束 3D 重新插值。我们提出了角度相关剂量变换器算法（ADoTA），该算法通过使用显式编码光束方向的快速分析子束形状投影来增强模型输入，从而消除网格旋转。该模型使用来自 108 名患者的 CT 数据进行训练，以预测初始能量为 $70$--$270\,\mathrm{MeV}$ 在 $80\times110\,\mathrm{mm}^2$ 场上的子束剂量分布，并在由 50 名患者组成的独立队列中进行测试。在测试集上，伽马通过率 $(1\%,3\,\mathrm{mm})$ 分别为 $99.40\pm0.86\%$（胸部）和 $99.87\pm0.23\%$（腹部/骨盆）。单束推断花费了 $1.72\pm0.8\,\mathrm{ms}$。通过避免重新插值，相对于最快的已发布的基于重新插值的方法，端到端 3D 剂量计算减少了 $\approx86\%$。对于完整的治疗计划，剂量截止为 10\% 的伽玛通过率 $\Gamma(2\%,2\,\mathrm{mm})$ 达到 $98.4\%$（肺）和 $98.9\%$（前列腺）。 ADoTA 提供了角度感知深度学习质子剂量引擎，可在异构解剖结构中保持 MC 级精度，同时大幅减少计算开销。
