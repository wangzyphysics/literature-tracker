## Abstract
arXiv:2411.06843v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In one-dimensional quasiperiodic systems, only a few models with exact mobility edges (MEs) have been constructed using generalized self-duality theory, Avila's global theory, or the renormalization group method. This raises an intriguing question that whether we can realize more physical models with exact solvable MEs. In this work, we uncover the hidden self-duality within a class of quasiperiodic network models constituted by periodic and quasiperiodic sites. Although the original Hamiltonians appear to lack self-duality, their effective Hamiltonians obtained by integrating out the periodic sites exhibit self-duality, which yield MEs. The well-studied mosaic model, which is the simplest case of quasiperiodic network models, was previously thought to exhibit MEs due to the absence of self-duality, but we show that they actually arise from the hidden self-duality. Using the effective Hamiltonian, we further introduce the concept of resonant states to understand the shape of MEs. Finally, we present in detail how to determine the MEs in various network models, including some non-Hermitian models, based on the hidden self-duality. These predictions can be experimentally realized using optical and acoustic waveguide arrays. Our work can greatly advance our understanding of MEs in Anderson transition.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2411.06843v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在一维准周期系统中，只有少数具有精确迁移边（ME）的模型使用广义自对偶理论、Avila 全局理论或重正化群方法构建。这就提出了一个有趣的问题：我们是否可以实现更多具有精确可解 ME 的物理模型。在这项工作中，我们揭示了由周期性站点和准周期性站点构成的一类准周期性网络模型中隐藏的自对偶性。尽管原始哈密顿量似乎缺乏自对偶性，但通过整合周期位点而获得的有效哈密顿量表现出自对偶性，从而产生 ME。经过充分研究的马赛克模型是准周期网络模型最简单的情况，之前被认为由于不存在自对偶性而表现出 ME，但我们证明它们实际上是由隐藏的自对偶性产生的。使用有效哈密顿量，我们进一步引入共振态的概念来理解 ME 的形状。最后，我们详细介绍了如何基于隐藏的自对偶性确定各种网络模型（包括一些非厄米特模型）中的 ME。这些预测可以使用光学和声波导阵列通过实验实现。我们的工作可以极大地增进我们对安德森转变中 ME 的理解。
