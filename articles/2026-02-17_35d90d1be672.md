## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14108v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Predicting flows that occur both through and around porous bodies is challenging due to coupled physics across fluid and porous regions and the need to generalize across diverse geometries and boundary conditions. We address this problem using two Physics Informed learning approaches: Physics Informed PointNets (PIPN) and Physics Informed Geometry Aware Neural Operator (P-IGANO). We enforce the incompressible Navier Stokes equations in the free-flow region and a Darcy Forchheimer extension in the porous region within a unified loss and condition the networks on geometry and material parameters. Datasets are generated with OpenFOAM on 2D ducts containing porous obstacles and on 3D windbreak scenarios with tree canopies and buildings. We first verify the pipeline via the method of manufactured solutions, then assess generalization to unseen shapes, and for PI-GANO, to variable boundary conditions and parameter settings. The results show consistently low velocity and pressure errors in both seen and unseen cases, with accurate reproduction of the wake structures. Performance degrades primarily near sharp interfaces and in regions with large gradients. Overall, the study provides a first systematic evaluation of PIPN/PI-GANO for simultaneous through-and-around porous flows and shows their potential to accelerate design studies without retraining per geometry.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14108v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：由于流体和多孔区域之间的耦合物理现象以及需要在不同的几何形状和边界条件下进行推广，预测穿过多孔体及其周围发生的流动具有挑战性。我们使用两种物理信息学习方法来解决这个问题：物理信息点网（PIPN）和物理信息几何感知神经运算符（P-IGANO）。我们在自由流动区域中强制执行不可压缩的纳维斯托克斯方程，在多孔区域中强制执行 Darcy Forchheimer 扩展，并在统一的损失范围内，并根据几何形状和材料参数调节网络。数据集是使用 OpenFOAM 在包含多孔障碍物的 2D 管道以及具有树冠和建筑物的 3D 防风场景上生成的。我们首先通过制造解决方案的方法验证管道，然后评估对未见过的形状的泛化，对于 PI-GANO，评估对可变边界条件和参数设置的泛化。结果显示，在可见和不可见的情况下，速度和压力误差始终较低，并能准确再现尾流结构。性能下降主要是在尖锐界面附近和梯度较大的区域。总体而言，该研究首次对 PIPN/PI-GANO 对于同步贯通和绕行多孔流进行了系统评估，并显示了它们在无需对每个几何形状进行重新训练的情况下加速设计研究的潜力。
