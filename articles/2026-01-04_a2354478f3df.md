## Abstract
The study of artwork is an interesting field that has undergone a major turn the last decades as new scientific analysis methods have been introduced and is becoming a significant part of the investigations. In this study we have employed Raman spectroscopy to analyse the pigments in an old painting of the Roman Emperor Domitian. The painting is of special interest since its creation is historically attributed to Tiziano Vecellio, more known as Titian, but it has recently been found to contain a signature that reads L daVinci. We here perform a rigorous pigment analysis of the painting and signature area to identify and classify the pigments by usage and the potential time of deployment. Microscopic cut outs from selected areas of the painting were analysed in a Raman microscope where 14 different pigments were identified. Out of these, 12 pigments were contemporary with Titian (1488/90-1567) and Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519), one pigment, titanium white, was present during the Middle Ages but came into common use at a later time and one pigment, Prussian blue, is of newer origin. Complimentary X-ray imaging shows that at least one of the areas where Prussian blue was found has likely been altered at a later stage, providing an explanation for the presence of a modern pigment in an old painting. Special emphasis was also applied to resonant and non-resonant Raman response of Prussian green and blue, expressing different ratios of Fe2+ and Fe3+ states, valuable for identification and discrimination of the compounds in pigment identification and in the more recent field of battery research.

## 摘要（中文）
艺术品研究是一个有趣的领域，随着新的科学分析方法的引入，艺术品研究在过去几十年中经历了重大转变，并正在成为研究的重要组成部分。在这项研究中，我们采用拉曼光谱分析了罗马皇帝图密善的一幅旧画中的颜料。这幅画特别令人感兴趣，因为它的创作历史上被认为是提齐亚诺·韦切利奥（Tiziano Vecellio）（更名为提香）的，但最近发现它包含一个写着 L daVinci 的签名。我们在这里对绘画和签名区域进行严格的颜料分析，以根据用途和潜在的部署时间对颜料进行识别和分类。用拉曼显微镜对画作选定区域的显微切片进行分析，鉴定出 14 种不同的颜料。其中，有 12 种颜料与提香（1488/90-1567）和达芬奇（1452-1519）同时代，其中一种颜料钛白出现于中世纪，但后来开始普遍使用，另一种颜料普鲁士蓝起源较新。免费的 X 射线成像显示，至少有一个发现普鲁士蓝的区域可能在后期发生了改变，这为一幅古老绘画中存在现代颜料提供了解释。还特别强调了普鲁士绿和蓝色的共振和非共振拉曼响应，表达了不同比例的 Fe2+ 和 Fe3+ 状态，对于颜料鉴定和最近的电池研究领域中的化合物识别和区分很有价值。
