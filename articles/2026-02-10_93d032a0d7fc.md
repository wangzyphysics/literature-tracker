## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08674v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study the ground-state properties of a family of frustrated spin-1/2 Heisenberg models on two- and three-dimensional decorated lattices composed of connected star-shaped units. Each star is built from edge-sharing triangles with an antiferromagnetic interaction on the shared side and ferromagnetic interactions on the others. At a critical coupling ratio, the ideal star model - defined by equal ferromagnetic interactions - exhibits a macroscopically degenerate ground state, which we map onto a site percolation problem on the Lieb lattice. This mapping enables the calculation of exponential ground-state degeneracy and the corresponding residual entropy for square, triangular, honeycomb, and cubic lattices. Remarkably, the residual entropy remains high for all studied lattices, exceeding 60\% of the maximal value ln(2). Despite a gapless quadratic one-magnon spectrum, the low-temperature thermodynamics is governed by exponentially numerous gapped excitations. For a distorted-star variant of the model, the ground-state manifold is equivalent to that of decoupled ferromagnetic clusters, leading to exponential degeneracy with a lower, yet still substantial, residual entropy. At low temperature the system mimics a paramagnetic crystal of non-interacting spins with high spin value ($s=4$ for a square lattice). The obtained results establish a structural design principle for engineering quantum magnets with a high ground-state degeneracy, suggesting promising candidates for enhanced magnetocaloric cooling and quantum thermal machines.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08674v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了由连接的星形单元组成的二维和三维装饰晶格上的一系列受抑自旋 1/2 海森堡模型的基态特性。每颗恒星都是由共享边的三角形构成，共享边上有反铁磁相互作用，其他边上有铁磁相互作用。在临界耦合比下，理想星模型（由相等的铁磁相互作用定义）表现出宏观简并基态，我们将其映射到利布晶格上的位点渗流问题。该映射可以计算方形、三角形、蜂窝形和立方晶格的指数基态简并性和相应的残余熵。值得注意的是，对于所有研究的晶格来说，残余熵仍然很高，超过了最大值 ln(2) 的 60%。尽管存在无间隙二次单磁振子谱，但低温热力学受指数级数量的带隙激发控制。对于该模型的扭曲星变体，基态流形相当于解耦铁磁团簇的基态流形，导致指数简并，具有较低但仍然很大的残余熵。在低温下，该系统模拟具有高自旋值的非相互作用自旋的顺磁晶体（对于方形晶格，$s=4$）。获得的结果建立了工程具有高基态简并性的量子磁体的结构设计原理，为增强磁热冷却和量子热机提出了有希望的候选者。
