## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08806v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The Kelvin-Helmholtz (KH) instability occurs in multiple heliospheric (solar-wind stream interfaces, planetary magnetospheres, cometary tails, heliopause flanks) and interstellar (protoplanetary disks, relativistic jets, neutron star accretion disks) environments. While the KH instability has been well-studied in the magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) limit, only limited studies were performed in the collisionless regime, which is conducive to development of anisotropic pressures. Collisionless plasmas are often described using the Chew Goldberger and Low (CGL) equations which feature an anisotropic pressure tensor. This paper presents a comprehensive analysis of the CGL version of the KH instability using linearised and numerical techniques. We find that the largest growth rates and the greatest incidence of magnetic effects occur in the MHD limit. In the large relaxation time CGL limit, part of the energy goes into the formation of pressure anisotropies, resulting in smaller amounts of energy being available for bending the field lines. Consequently, when we cross-compare CGL and MHD simulations that are otherwise identical, the current densities are largest in the MHD limit, and the largest magnetic islands also form in that limit. Early and late time formation of pressure anisotropies have also been studied. We also find that the strongest trend for forming intermittencies in the flow also occurs in the MHD limit. The paper also discusses possible consequences of our results for turbulence and reconnection in the heliosheath (the layer between the solar wind termination shock and the heliopause).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08806v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：开尔文-亥姆霍兹 (KH) 不稳定性发生在多个日光层（太阳风流界面、行星磁层、彗尾、日球层顶侧翼）和星际（原行星盘、相对论喷流、中子星吸积盘）环境中。虽然 KH 不稳定性在磁流体动力学 (MHD) 极限下得到了充分研究，但在无碰撞状态下仅进行了有限的研究，这有利于各向异性压力的发展。无碰撞等离子体通常使用具有各向异性压力张量的 Chew Goldberger 和 Low (CGL) 方程来描述。本文使用线性化和数值技术对 CGL 版本的 KH 不稳定性进行了全面分析。我们发现最大的增长率和最大的磁效应发生率发生在 MHD 极限内。在大弛豫时间 CGL 极限下，部分能量会形成压力各向异性，导致可用于弯曲场线的能量较少。因此，当我们交叉比较在其他方面相同的 CGL 和 MHD 模拟时，电流密度在 MHD 限制中最大，并且最大的磁岛也在该限制中形成。还研究了压力各向异性的早期和晚期形成。我们还发现，流动中形成间歇性的最强趋势也出现在 MHD 极限中。本文还讨论了我们的结果对日鞘（太阳风终止激波和日球层顶之间的层）的湍流和重新连接的可能后果。
