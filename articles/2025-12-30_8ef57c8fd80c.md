## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23655v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a diffuse neutron and x-ray scattering study of structural, spin- and charge-density-wave fluctuations in the electrical insulator La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$NiO$_4$. This lamellar nickelate is an isostructural analogue of the high-temperature cuprate superconductor La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$CuO$_4$, for which recent experiments uncovered evidence for unusual structural and superconducting fluctuations indicative of rare-region physics due to inherent inhomogeneity unrelated to common point disorder effects. We find closely analogous nanoscale orthorhombic fluctuation behavior in La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$NiO$_4$, including exponential scaling of the diffuse scattering intensity and power-law scaling of the characteristic length with relative temperature. Moreover, our neutron and x-ray scattering data reveal similar behavior for short-range magnetic and charge fluctuations above the respective ordering temperatures. These observations indicate that rare-region effects are a generic feature of perovskite-related structures and lead to universal fluctuations of both structural and electronic degrees of freedom over extended temperature ranges.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23655v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们对电绝缘体 La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$NiO$_4$ 中的结构、自旋和电荷密度波波动进行了扩散中子和 X 射线散射研究。这种层状镍酸盐是高温铜酸盐超导体La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$CuO$_4$的同构类似物，最近的实验发现了异常结构和超导波动的证据，表明由于与公共点无序效应无关的固有不均匀性而导致稀有区域物理现象。我们在 La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$NiO$_4$ 中发现了密切相似的纳米级正交涨落行为，包括漫散射强度的指数缩放和特征长度随相对温度的幂律缩放。此外，我们的中子和 X 射线散射数据揭示了高于各自有序温度的短程磁和电荷波动的类似行为。这些观察结果表明，稀有区域效应是钙钛矿相关结构的普遍特征，并导致结构和电子自由度在扩展温度范围内的普遍波动。
