## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07424v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The p-type transistor is an indispensable component of semiconductor technology, enabling complementary operation with n-channel transistors for computation, storage, and communication. Achieving both high robustness and high efficiency is highly desirable but challenging for p-type transistors due to limited semiconductors with reliable hole transport and their high activation energies. Here, we achieved a robust yet efficient p-type transistor by heterogeneously integrating an ultra-wide-bandgap semiconductor and a high-k dielectric layer through van der Waals integration. The p-type transistor employs a two-dimensional hole channel on hydrogenated diamond (bandgap 5.6 eV) combined with a high-k (30.5) SrTiO3 perovskite membrane. At room temperature, the transistor exhibits stable operation with a high on-current (~200 mA/mm), low subthreshold swing (70 mV/dec), high hole mobility (566 cm^2/Vs to 572 cm^2/Vs) and high on-off ratio (~10^9). Furthermore, tuning annealing temperature allows operation in either enhancement or depletion mode. The robust p-type transistor with high efficiency holds great potential for future power electronics, UV optoelectronics, and harsh-environment electronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07424v1 发布类型：新 摘要：p 型晶体管是半导体技术中不可或缺的组成部分，能够与 n 沟道晶体管互补运行以进行计算、存储和通信。对于 p 型晶体管来说，实现高鲁棒性和高效率是非常理想的，但也具有挑战性，因为具有可靠空穴传输和高激活能的半导体有限。在这里，我们通过范德华集成异质集成超宽带隙半导体和高 k 介电层，实现了稳健而高效的 p​​ 型晶体管。 p 型晶体管采用氢化金刚石（带隙 5.6 eV）上的二维空穴沟道与高 k (30.5) SrTiO3 钙钛矿膜相结合。在室温下，该晶体管表现出稳定的工作特性，具有高导通电流 (~200 mA/mm)、低亚阈值摆幅 (70 mV/dec)、高空穴迁移率 (566 cm^2/Vs 至 572 cm^2/Vs) 和高开关比 (~10^9)。此外，调节退火温度允许在增强或耗尽模式下运行。这种坚固耐用的高效率 p 型晶体管在未来电力电子、紫外光电子和恶劣环境电子应用中具有巨大潜力。
