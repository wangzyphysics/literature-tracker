## Abstract
arXiv:2505.01759v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The interplay between ferroelectricity, magnetism, and superconductivity provides a rich platform for discovering novel quantum phenomena. Here, we develop an effective theory and propose a heterostructure composed of an antiferromagnetic bilayer MnBi$_{2}$Te$_{4}$ coupled with the s-wave superconductor Fe(Se,Te), enabling the realization of chiral topological superconductivity (CTSC) with switchable chirality. The chirality of the CTSC is controlled by the direction of spontaneous polarization, which arises from interlayer sliding-induced ferroelectricity or charge transfer in the bilayer MnBi$_{2}$Te$_{4}$. This sliding mechanism breaks the $\mathcal{M}_{z}\mathcal{T}$ and $\mathcal{PT}$ symmetries, leading to the anomalous Hall effect in the spin polarized metallic Dirac band and drives the emergence of CTSC when the s-wave superconductivity appears. Our work not only provides a new pathway to achieve and control topological superconductivity but also opens avenues for experimental exploration of Majorana physics and topological quantum computation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.01759v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：铁电性、磁性和超导性之间的相互作用为发现新颖的量子现象提供了丰富的平台。在这里，我们发展了一种有效的理论，并提出了一种由反铁磁双层 MnBi$_{2}$Te$_{4}$ 与 s 波超导体 Fe(Se,Te) 耦合组成的异质结构，从而实现了具有可切换手性的手性拓扑超导（CTSC）。 CTSC 的手性由自发极化的方向控制，自发极化的方向是由层间滑动引起的铁电或双层 MnBi$_{2}$Te$_{4}$ 中的电荷转移引起的。这种滑动机制打破了$\mathcal{M}_{z}\mathcal{T}$和$\mathcal{PT}$对称性，导致自旋极化金属狄拉克带中出现反常霍尔效应，并在s波超导出现时驱动CTSC的出现。我们的工作不仅提供了实现和控制拓扑超导的新途径，而且还为马约拉纳物理和拓扑量子计算的实验探索开辟了途径。
