## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12993v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Neural quantum states (NQS) provide a flexible and highly expressive parameterization of wave functions for strongly correlated problems in quantum chemistry. Despite rapid advances in network architectures, the evaluation of electronic energies remains almost exclusively based on variational Monte Carlo (VMC). While VMC is effective for structured systems such as spin chains, its accuracy and efficiency for electronic Hamiltonians are hindered by sharply peaked distributions, stochastic gradient noise, and slow convergence with sample size. In this letter, we assess the capability of NQS-VMC to efficiently capture correlation in electronic ground states by comparing it to a recently developed NQS-based selected configuration (NQS-SC) approach. We set up a systematic comparison of the ground-state optimizations obtained with NQS-VMC and NQS-SC for molecular systems dominated by either static or dynamical correlation. The comparison demonstrates a clear advantage of NQS-SC over NQS-VMC in both energy accuracy and wave-function coefficients, particularly for statically correlated molecules. Moreover, NQS-SC exhibits robust systematic improvability, whereas NQS-VMC does not. These findings position NQS-SC as the new default approach over NQS-VMC for electronic structure calculations. We further observe that neither NQS-SC nor NQS-VMC can efficiently capture dynamical correlation, highlighting the need for future hybrid methods, such as multiconfigurational perturbation theories built on top of NQS solutions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12993v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：神经量子态 (NQS) 为量子化学中的强相关问题提供了灵活且高度表达的波函数参数化。尽管网络架构取得了快速进步，但电子能量的评估仍然几乎完全基于变分蒙特卡罗（VMC）。虽然 VMC 对于自旋链等结构化系统有效，但其电子哈密顿量的准确性和效率受到尖峰分布、随机梯度噪声和样本大小收敛缓慢的阻碍。在这封信中，我们通过将 NQS-VMC 与最近开发的基于 NQS 的选择配置 (NQS-SC) 方法进行比较，评估了 NQS-VMC 有效捕获电子基态相关性的能力。我们对以静态或动态相关性为主的分子系统使用 NQS-VMC 和 NQS-SC 获得的基态优化进行了系统比较。比较表明，NQS-SC 在能量精度和波函数系数方面均优于 NQS-VMC，特别是对于静态相关分子。此外，NQS-SC 表现出强大的系统可改进性，而 NQS-VMC 则不然。这些发现使 NQS-SC 成为取代 NQS-VMC 的电子结构计算的新默认方法。我们进一步观察到，NQS-SC 和 NQS-VMC 都无法有效地捕获动态相关性，这凸显了对未来混合方法的需求，例如建立在 NQS 解决方案之上的多配置扰动理论。
