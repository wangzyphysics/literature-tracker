## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03774v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine learning force fields (MLFFs) have revolutionized molecular simulations by providing quantum mechanical accuracy at the speed of molecular mechanical computations. However, a fundamental reliance of these models on fixed-cutoff architectures limits their applicability to macromolecular systems where long-range interactions dominate. We demonstrate that this locality constraint causes force prediction errors to scale monotonically with system size, revealing a critical architectural bottleneck. To overcome this, we establish the systematically designed MolLR25 ({Mol}ecules with {L}ong-{R}ange effect) benchmark up to 1200 atoms, generated using high-fidelity DFT, and introduce E2Former-LSR, an equivariant transformer that explicitly integrates long-range attention blocks. E2Former-LSR exhibits stable error scaling, achieves superior fidelity in capturing non-covalent decay, and maintains precision on complex protein conformations. Crucially, its efficient design provides up to 30% speedup compared to purely local models. This work validates the necessity of non-local architectures for generalizable MLFFs, enabling high-fidelity molecular dynamics for large-scale chemical and biological systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03774v1 公告类型：新 摘要：机器学习力场 (MLFF) 通过以分子机械计算的速度提供量子力学精度，彻底改变了分子模拟。然而，这些模型对固定截止结构的基本依赖限制了它们对长程相互作用占主导地位的大分子系统的适用性。我们证明，这种局部性约束会导致力预测误差随着系统大小单调缩放，从而揭示了关键的架构瓶颈。为了克服这个问题，我们建立了系统设计的 MolLR25（具有 {L}ong-{R}ange 效应的 {Mol}ecules） 基准，最多 1200 个原子，使用高保真 DFT 生成，并引入 E2Former-LSR，一种显式集成远程注意力块的等变转换器。 E2Former-LSR 表现出稳定的误差缩放，在捕获非共价衰变方面实现了卓越的保真度，并保持了复杂蛋白质构象的精度。至关重要的是，与纯本地模型相比，其高效设计可提供高达 30% 的加速。这项工作验证了非局部架构对于可推广的 MLFF 的必要性，从而为大规模化学和生物系统提供高保真分子动力学。
