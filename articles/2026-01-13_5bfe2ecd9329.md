## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06440v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a theoretical study of the low-energy physics of a quarter-hole-filled two-orbital bilayer Hubbard model motivated by transition-metal bilayer systems with strong orbital-selective interlayer hybridization. By explicitly treating the strong interlayer bonding of dz2 orbitals within a molecular orbital basis and projecting out high-energy electronic states, we derive a low-energy effective Kugel-Khomskii Hamiltonian describing the interplay between electron spin and emergent layer pseudospin degrees of freedom. We map out a rich ground state phase diagram featuring diverse spin and charge ordered states. These include conventional ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic phases with layer staggered charge densities, a layer-coherent phase characterized by spontaneous interlayer quantum coherence, and a novel maximally spin-layer-entangled phase with a hidden composite spin-layer order. We show that this exotic hidden ordered phase arises from the spontaneous breaking of an emergent O(4) symmetry down to a O(3), manifesting a unique excitation spectrum with three entangled gapless Goldstone modes. Our results uncover a geometrically driven mechanism for realizing composite entanglement in strongly correlated bilayer systems and provide a concrete theoretical framework relevant to bilayer nickelate superconductors and other multi-component correlated materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06440v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了由具有强轨道选择性层间杂化的过渡金属双层系统驱动的四分之一孔填充双轨道双层哈伯德模型的低能物理理论研究。通过明确处理分子轨道基础内 dz2 轨道的强层间键合并投影出高能电子态，我们推导了一个低能有效的 Kugel-Khomskii 哈密顿量，描述了电子自旋和涌现层赝自旋自由度之间的相互作用。我们绘制了丰富的基态相图，具有不同的自旋和电荷有序态。这些包括具有层交错电荷密度的传统铁磁和反铁磁相、以自发层间量子相干为特征的层相干相，以及具有隐藏复合自旋层序的新型最大自旋层纠缠相。我们证明，这种奇异的隐藏有序相源于 O(4) 对称性自发破缺到 O(3)，表现出具有三种纠缠无间隙戈德斯通模式的独特激发光谱。我们的研究结果揭示了在强相关双层系统中实现复合纠缠的几何驱动机制，并提供了与双层镍酸盐超导体和其他多组分相关材料相关的具体理论框架。
