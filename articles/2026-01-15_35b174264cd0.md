## Abstract
arXiv:2506.08112v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Disorder significantly impacts the electronic properties of conducting quantum materials by inducing electron localization and thus altering the local density of states and electric transport. In insulating quantum magnetic materials, the effects of disorder are less understood and can drastically impact fluctuating spin states like quantum spin liquids. In the absence of transport tools, disorder is typically characterized using chemical methods or by semi-classical modeling of spin dynamics. This requires high magnetic fields that may not always be accessible. Here, we show that magnetization plateaus -- incompressible states found in many quantum magnets -- provide an exquisite platform to uncover small amounts of disorder, regardless of the origin of the plateau. Using optical magneto-spectroscopy on the Ising-Heisenberg triangular-lattice antiferromagnet K$_2$Co(SeO$_3$)$_2$ exhibiting a 1/3 magnetization plateau, we identify sharp spectroscopic lines, the fine structure of which serves as a hallmark signature of disorder. Through analytical and numerical modeling, we show that these fingerprints not only enable us to quantify minute amounts of disorder but also reveal its nature -- as dilute vacancies. Remarkably, this model explains all details of the thermomagnetic response of our system, including the existence of multiple plateaus. Our findings provide a new approach to identifying disorder in quantum magnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.08112v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：无序通过诱导电子局域化从而改变局域态密度和电传输，从而显着影响导电量子材料的电子特性。在绝缘量子磁性材料中，无序的影响尚不清楚，并且可以极大地影响量子自旋液体等波动的自旋态。在缺乏运输工具的情况下，通常使用化学方法或自旋动力学的半经典模型来表征无序。这需要高磁场，但并不总是能够达到。在这里，我们展示了磁化平台（在许多量子磁体中发现的不可压缩状态）提供了一个精致的平台来发现少量的无序，无论平台的起源如何。在伊辛-海森堡三角晶格反铁磁体 K$_2$Co(SeO$_3$)$_2$ 上使用光学磁光谱，表现出 1/3 磁化平台，我们识别出尖锐的光谱线，其精细结构可作为无序的标志特征。通过分析和数值模型，我们表明这些指纹不仅使我们能够量化微量的无序，而且还揭示了其本质——稀释空位。值得注意的是，该模型解释了我们系统的热磁响应的所有细节，包括多个平台的存在。我们的发现提供了一种识别量子磁体无序的新方法。
