## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03996v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Coupled networks of mass-spring resonators have attracted growing attention across multiple fundamental and applied research directions, including reservoir computing for artificial intelligence. This has led to the exploration of platforms capable of tasks such as acoustic-wave classification, smart sensing, predictive maintenance, and adaptive vibration control. This work introduces a multiphysics reservoir based on a two-dimensional network of coupled nonlinear mass-spring resonators. Each mass has a magnetic tunnel junction on top of it, working as spin diode, used as a spintronic read-out. As a proof-of-concept, we have benchmarked this reservoir with the task of vowel-recognition reaching accuracy above 95$\%$. Because the device accepts elastic excitations directly, signal injections are simplified, making it well-suited for realtime sensing and edge computation. We also studied the effect of nonlinearity, demonstrating how it influences the reservoir dynamics, and assessed its robustness under node-to-node variation of the elastic constants.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03996v1 公告类型：新 摘要：质量弹簧谐振器的耦合网络在多个基础和应用研究方向（包括人工智能的储层计算）引起了越来越多的关注。这导致了对能够执行声波分类、智能传感、预测性维护和自适应振动控制等任务的平台的探索。这项工作介绍了一种基于耦合非线性质量弹簧谐振器的二维网络的多物理场储层。每个质量块顶部都有一个磁性隧道结，用作自旋二极管，用作自旋电子读数。作为概念验证，我们对这个水库进行了基准测试，元音识别任务的准确度达到 95$\%$ 以上。由于该器件直接接受弹性激励，因此简化了信号注入，使其非常适合实时传感和边缘计算。我们还研究了非线性的影响，展示了它如何影响储层动力学，并评估了其在弹性常数的节点到节点变化下的鲁棒性。
