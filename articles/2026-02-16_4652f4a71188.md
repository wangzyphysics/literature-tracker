# Exploitation of complex Abelian point groups in quantum-chemical calculations

## 中文标题

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12457v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum-chemical calculations often make use of point-group theory to exploit molecular symmetry, resulting in a reduction of the computational cost and in insights into the electronic structure. This exploitation is often limited to subgroups of $D_{2h}$ which are Abelian with real characters. Here, we extend the symmetry exploitation to Abelian point groups with complex characters. Such point groups are often encountered in calculations that involve finite magnetic fields, though their occurrence is not limited to these cases alone. We present the evaluation of integrals over symmetry-adapted orbitals using the double-coset decomposition, as well as the use of these symmetries in the contractions needed within post Hartree Fock calculations in the context of block tensors. Efficiency gains are discussed for four simple hydrocarbons that exhibit a complex Abelian point group in the presence of a magnetic field.

arXiv:2602.12457v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子化学计算通常利用点群理论来利用分子对称性，从而降低计算成本并深入了解电子结构。这种利用通常仅限于 $D_{2h}$ 的子群，这些子群是具有真实字符的阿贝尔群。在这里，我们将对称性利用扩展到具有复杂特征的阿贝尔点群。在涉及有限磁场的计算中经常会遇到这样的点群，尽管它们的出现并不仅限于这些情况。我们提出了使用双陪集分解对对称适应轨道上的积分进行评估，以及在块张量的情况下在后 Hartree Fock 计算中所需的收缩中使用这些对称性。讨论了四种简单碳氢化合物的效率增益，这些碳氢化合物在磁场存在下表现出复杂的阿贝尔点群。
