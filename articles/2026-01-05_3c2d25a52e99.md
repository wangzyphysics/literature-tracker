## Abstract
Chiral spin textures in ferromagnetic materials with Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interactions (DMIs) have attracted significant interest in recent years owing to their potential applications in nanodevices. This work focuses on describing stable conical-helix configurations hosted in ultrathin films with DMI and perpendicular anisotropy. These states are studied for different kinds of DMIs, including symmetry classes\({\mathcal{T}}\),\({{\mathcal{C}}}_{nv}\), isotropic and anisotropic\({{\mathcal{D}}}_{2d}\),\({{\mathcal{D}}}_{n}\),\({{\mathcal{C}}}_{n}\), and\({{\mathcal{S}}}_{4}\). A parameterised analytical model of these configurations is proposed, enabling the determination of optimal parameters characterising the magnetic texture, such as the pitch vector or nucleation field. To substantiate the results, micromagnetic simulations are developed for comparison with the theoretical solutions. Numerical solutions are optimised by implementing finite-difference codes that use next-nearest neighbours and explicit Robin boundary conditions stemming from symmetric exchange and DMI. It is shown that these numerical enhancements decrease anisotropic effects in helical solutions. This study establishes a method to analyse conical-helix textures in thin-film systems with any DMI, which can be simulated with higher precision using the open-access codes developed here.

## 摘要（中文）
近年来，具有 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用 (DMIs) 的铁磁材料中的手性自旋结构因其在纳米器件中的潜在应用而引起了人们的极大兴趣。这项工作的重点是描述具有 DMI 和垂直各向异性的超薄膜中稳定的锥形螺旋结构。针对不同类型的 DMI 研究这些状态，包括对称类\({\mathcal{T}}\)、\({{\mathcal{C}}}_{nv}\)、各向同性和各向异性\({{\mathcal{D}}}_{2d}\)、\({{\mathcal{D}}}_{n}\)、\({{\mathcal{C}}}_{n}\) 和 \({{\mathcal{S}}}_{4}\)。提出了这些配置的参数化分析模型，能够确定表征磁性纹理的最佳参数，例如节距矢量或成核场。为了证实结果，开发了微磁模拟以与理论解决方案进行比较。通过实施有限差分码来优化数值解，该有限差分码使用次近邻以及源自对称交换和 DMI 的显式 Robin 边界条件。结果表明，这些数值增强减少了螺旋解中的各向异性效应。本研究建立了一种使用任何 DMI 分析薄膜系统中的锥形螺旋纹理的方法，可以使用此处开发的开放访问代码以更高的精度进行模拟。
