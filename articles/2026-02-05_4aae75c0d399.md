## Abstract
arXiv:2504.01686v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: This article is dedicated to the 60-th anniversary of the Landau Institute for Theoretical Physics and presents a review of normal and superconducting properties of toroidal, altermagnetic, and noncentrosymmetric metals. Metals with toroidal order are compounds not possessing symmetry in respect of space and time inversion but are symmetric in respect of the product of these operations. An electric current propagating through samples of such a material causes its magnetisation. Superconducting states in toroidal metals are a mixture of singlet and triplet states. Superconductivity is gapless even in ideal crystals without impurities. Altermagnets are antiferromagnetic metals that have a specific spin splitting of electron bands determined by time inversion in combinations with rotations and reflections of a crystal lattice. Similar splitting takes place in metals whose symmetry does not have a spatial inversion operation. Both of these types of materials have an anomalous Hall effect. A current propagating through a noncentrosymmetric metal causes magnetization, but this is not the case in altermagnets. On the other hand, in altermagnets, there is a specific piezomagnetic Hall effect. Superconducting pairing in non-centrosymmetric metals occurs between electrons occupying states in one zone, whereas, in altermagnets, we are dealing with interband pairing, which is unfavorable for the formation of a superconducting state.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.01686v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：本文旨在纪念朗道理论物理研究所成立 60 周年，并对环形金属、交磁金属和非中心对称金属的正常和超导特性进行了回顾。具有环形序的金属是在空间和时间反演方面不具有对称性但在这些操作的产物方面具有对称性的化合物。通过这种材料的样品传播的电流会引起其磁化。环形金属中的超导态是单线态和三线态的混合。即使在没有杂质的理想晶体中，超导性也是无间隙的。交变磁体是反铁磁金属，具有特定的电子带自旋分裂，该自旋分裂是由时间反演与晶格的旋转和反射相结合确定的。类似的分裂发生在对称性不具有空间反转操作的金属中。这两种类型的材料都具有反常霍尔效应。通过非中心对称金属传播的电流会引起磁化，但交流磁体中的情况并非如此。另一方面，在交流磁体中，存在特定的压磁霍尔效应。非中心对称金属中的超导配对发生在一个区域内占据态的电子之间，而在交流磁体中，我们正在处理带间配对，这不利于超导态的形成。
