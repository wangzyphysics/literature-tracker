## Abstract
arXiv:2501.13759v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The Casimir force follows from quantum fluctuations of the electromagnetic field and yields a nonlinear attractive force between closely spaced conductive objects. Its magnitude depends on the conductivity of the objects up to optical frequencies. Measuring the Casimir force in superconductors should allow to isolate frequency-specific contributions to the Casimir effect, as frequencies below the superconducting gap energy are expected to contribute differently than those above it. There is significant interest in this contribution as it is suspected to contribute to an unexplained discrepancy between predictions and measurements of the Casimir force between normal metals, which questions the basic principles on which estimates of the magnitude are based. Here, we observe the Casimir force between superconducting objects through the nonlinear dynamics it imparts to a superconducting drum resonator in a microwave optomechanical system. There is excellent agreement between the experiment and the Casimir force magnitude computed for this device across three orders of magnitude of displacement. Furthermore, the Casimir nonlinearity is intense enough that, with a modified design, this device type should operate in the single-phonon nonlinear regime. Accessing this regime has been a long-standing goal that would greatly facilitate quantum operations of mechanical resonators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2501.13759v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：卡西米尔力源自电磁场的量子涨落，并在紧密间隔的导电物体之间产生非线性吸引力。其大小取决于物体在光频率范围内的电导率。测量超导体中的卡西米尔力应该可以隔离特定频率对卡西米尔效应的贡献，因为低于超导间隙能量的频率预计与高于其的频率的贡献不同。人们对这一贡献非常感兴趣，因为它被怀疑导致了普通金属之间卡西米尔力的预测和测量之间无法解释的差异，这对震级估计所依据的基本原理提出了质疑。在这里，我们通过超导物体赋予微波光机械系统中的超导鼓谐振器的非线性动力学来观察超导物体之间的卡西米尔力。实验与针对该装置在三个数量级的位移上计算的卡西米尔力大小之间存在极好的一致性。此外，卡西米尔非线性足够强烈，通过修改设计，这种设备类型应该在单声子非线性状态下工作。进入这个状态一直是一个长期目标，它将极大地促进机械谐振器的量子操作。
