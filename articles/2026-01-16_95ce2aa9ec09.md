## Abstract
Ion transport in porous carbon electrodes underpins the performance of electrochemical energy-storage and separation technologies, yet exchange dynamics within heterogeneous pore networks remain difficult to quantify. Here, we present a rigorous analytical framework for extracting quantitative ion-exchange kinetics in confined electrolytes from two-dimensional exchange spectroscopy (2D EXSY) NMR, explicitly accounting for asymmetric ion populations, relaxation effects, and microscopic reversibility. By describing exchange with a lognormal distribution of rates, the framework captures the intrinsic heterogeneity of ion motion and overcomes the limitations of conventional discrete-site models. Applied to aqueous LiTFSI electrolytes confined in mesoporous CMK-3 and hierarchically structured ST-CMK-3 carbons, the method resolves a hierarchy of ion-exchange processes spanning fast near-surface exchange to slow in-pore diffusion in two and three-site exchange systems. We report the first observation of three-site ion exchange in porous electrodes, revealing enhanced directional ion mobility and higher effective diffusion coefficients at interconnected micro–mesopore boundaries in hierarchical carbons. These findings establish direct, quantitative links between pore architecture and ion-transport efficiency. This distribution-based framework provides a generalizable route to identifying rate-limiting transport pathways in complex porous energy materials and offers design principles for optimizing porous carbons for high-rate supercapacitors, capacitive deionization, and gas-storage applications.

## 摘要（中文）
多孔碳电极中的离子传输支撑着电化学储能和分离技术的性能，但异质孔隙网络内的交换动态仍然难以量化。在这里，我们提出了一个严格的分析框架，用于从二维交换光谱 (2D EXSY) NMR 中提取受限电解质中的定量离子交换动力学，明确地考虑了不对称离子群、弛豫效应和微观可逆性。通过用速率的对数正态分布描述交换，该框架捕获了离子运动的内在异质性，并克服了传统离散位点模型的局限性。该方法应用于限制在介孔 CMK-3 和分层结构 ST-CMK-3 碳中的水性 LiTFSI 电解质，解决了从快速近表面交换到二位点和三位点交换系统中缓慢孔内扩散的离子交换过程的层次结构。我们报告了多孔电极中三位点离子交换的首次观察，揭示了分级碳中互连的微介孔边界处增强的定向离子迁移率和更高的有效扩散系数。这些发现在孔隙结构和离子传输效率之间建立了直接的定量联系。这种基于分布的框架提供了一种通用的途径来识别复杂多孔能源材料中的限速传输路径，并提供了优化高倍率超级电容器、电容去离子和气体存储应用的多孔碳的设计原理。
