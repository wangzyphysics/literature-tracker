# Detecting exceptional points through dynamics in non-Hermitian systems

## Abstract
arXiv:2212.12403v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Non-Hermitian rotation-time reversal (RT)-symmetric spin models possess two distinct phases, the unbroken phase in which the entire spectrum is real and the broken phase which contains complex eigenspectra, thereby indicating a transition point, referred to as an exceptional point. We report that the dynamical quantities, namely the short- and long-time average of the Loschmidt echo which is the overlap between the initial and the final states, and the corresponding rate function, can faithfully predict the exceptional point. In particular, when the initial state is prepared as the ground state in the unbroken phase of the non-Hermitian Hamiltonian and the system is quenched to either the broken or unbroken phase, we analytically demonstrate that the rate function and the average Loschmidt echo can distinguish between the quench that occurred in the broken or the unbroken phase for the nearest-neighbor non-Hermitian $XY$ model with uniform and alternating magnetic fields, thereby indicating the exceptional point. Furthermore, we exhibit that such quantities are capable of identifying the exceptional point even in models like the non-Hermitian short- and long-range $XYZ$ model with magnetic field which can only be solved numerically, thereby establishing it as detection criteria for recognizing exceptional points.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2212.12403v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：非厄米旋转时间反转（RT）对称自旋模型具有两个不同的相，完整相是实数，破碎相包含复杂的特征谱，从而指示一个过渡点，称为异常点。我们报告说，动力学量，即洛施密特回波的短期和长期平均值（初始状态和最终状态之间的重叠）以及相应的速率函数，可以忠实地预测异常点。特别是，当初始态准备为非厄米哈密顿量的未破碎相中的基态并且系统被淬灭到破碎相或未破碎相时，我们分析证明，速率函数和平均洛施密特回波可以区分具有均匀交变磁场的最近邻非厄米$XY$模型的破碎相或未破碎相中发生的淬灭，从而指示异常点。此外，我们证明，即使在像非厄米短程和长程 $XYZ$ 磁场模型（只能通过数值求解）这样的模型中，这些量也能够识别异常点，从而将其确立为识别异常点的检测标准。
