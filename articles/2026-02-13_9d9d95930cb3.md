## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11847v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Galactic Cosmic Rays (GCRs) have to travel through the heliosphere before they interact with the Earth's atmosphere. During this, they are deflected by the Sun's magnetic field, causing variations in this field to imprint on the flux, spectrum and angular distribution of GCRs detected at or near Earth. Studies of these variations over the past several decades have revealed the impact of both transient phenomena such as solar flares, coronal holes, sunspot activity and coronal mass ejections (CMEs) as well as their effects such as Forbush Decreases (FDs), precursors and Ground-Level Enhancements (GLEs). Periodic variations, such as due to the solar diurnal modulation, the 27-day solar rotation, the 11-year solar cycle, and the 22-year solar magnetic cycle have also been characterized. These Sun-induced phenomena are most prominent in GCR intensity variations up to $\sim$30 GeV/nuc, beyond which the influence of solar modulation decreases rapidly as the gyro-radii of GCRs exceed the characteristic size of the heliosphere ($\sim$100 AU).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11847v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：银河宇宙射线（GCR）在与地球大气层相互作用之前必须穿过日光层。在此期间，它们被太阳磁场偏转，导致该磁场的变化影响在地球或地球附近检测到的 GCR 的通量、光谱和角分布。过去几十年对这些变化的研究揭示了太阳耀斑、日冕洞、太阳黑子活动和日冕物质抛射（CME）等瞬态现象的影响及其福布什减少（FD）、前兆和地面增强（GLE）等影响。周期性变化，例如由于太阳昼夜调制、27天太阳自转、11年太阳周期和22年太阳磁周期等引起的周期性变化也得到了表征。这些太阳引起的现象在高达 $\sim$30 GeV/nuc 的 GCR 强度变化中最为突出，超过该值，当 GCR 的陀螺半径超过日光层的特征尺寸（$\sim$100 AU）时，太阳调制的影响迅速减小。
