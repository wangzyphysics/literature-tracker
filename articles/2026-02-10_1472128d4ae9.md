## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08483v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Superintelligence is commonly envisioned as a quantitative extrapolation of human cognitive abilities driven by scale and computational power. Here we show that qualitative transitions in intelligence instead arise as dynamical phase transitions governed by collective critical dynamics. Building on a unified dynamical field-theoretic framework for cognition, we demonstrate that progressive collective coupling generated by reentrant mixing drives the system toward an infrared critical regime in which an extensive band of slow collective modes emerges. This spectral condensation reorganizes cognitive dynamics from localized relaxation to coherent motion along emergent low-dimensional manifolds. Through numerical analysis of the time-scale density of states, we identify robust power-law scaling of collective relaxation rates with well-defined critical exponents, placing the system within the universality class of self-organized critical many-body dynamics. Criticality alone would generically lead to instability. We further show that homeostatic regulation introduces a gapped stabilizing direction that protects the collective critical sector, yielding a dynamically maintained meta-stable infrared phase in which long-lived inference trajectories persist without collapse. The coexistence of scale-free collective dynamics and global stabilization defines a protected sector-critical regime in which coherence and internal flexibility coexist. Superintelligence therefore corresponds to a distinct dynamical stability class--a self-organized critical phase embedded within a stabilized cognitive manifold--rather than a smooth quantitative continuation of existing cognitive systems.

arXiv:2602.08483v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超级智能通常被设想为由规模和计算能力驱动的人类认知能力的定量外推。在这里，我们表明，智力的质变是作为受集体临界动力学控制的动态相变而出现的。基于统一的动态场理论认知框架，我们证明了由可重入混合产生的渐进集体耦合将系统推向红外临界状态，其中出现了广泛的慢集体模式。这种光谱凝聚重新组织了认知动力学，从局部松弛到沿着新兴低维流形的相干运动。通过对状态时间尺度密度的数值分析，我们确定了具有明确定义的临界指数的集体弛豫率的鲁棒幂律标度，将系统置于自组织临界多体动力学的普遍性类别中。仅仅批评一般就会导致不稳定。我们进一步表明，稳态调节引入了一个有缺口的稳定方向，保护集体关键部分，产生动态维持的亚稳定红外阶段，其中长期推理轨迹持续存在而不会崩溃。无规模集体动态和全球稳定的共存定义了一个受保护的部门关键制度，其中一致性和内部灵活性并存。因此，超级智能对应于一个独特的动态稳定性类别——嵌入稳定认知流形中的自组织关键阶段——而不是现有认知系统的平滑定量延续。
