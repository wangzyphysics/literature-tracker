## Abstract
The performance of solution-processed 2D material networks is typically limited by intersheet junctions, which disrupt charge transport and prevent intrinsic nanosheet properties from scaling. Advances in processing and characterisation now allow direct junction engineering to tune the interlayer transport mechanisms. This Perspective reviews the current understanding of junctions, techniques for measuring their properties, and discusses engineering strategies for their tuning, aiming to unlock scalable fabrication of high-performance devices that mirror intrinsic 2D material properties.

## 摘要（中文）
溶液处理的二维材料网络的性能通常受到片间连接的限制，片间连接会破坏电荷传输并阻止固有的纳米片特性缩放。处理和表征方面的进步现在允许直接连接工程来调整层间传输机制。本视角回顾了当前对结的理解、测量其属性的技术，并讨论了其调整的工程策略，旨在解锁反映固有二维材料属性的高性能器件的可扩展制造。
