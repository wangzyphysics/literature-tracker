## Abstract
Chiral-induced spin selectivity (CISS) is caused by the interplay between the chirality of a system and electron magnetic moments in nonequilibrium. CISS manifests itself in two-terminal junctions (e.g., electrode–molecule(s)–electrode) in magnetoresistance, with an inversion of current–voltage behavior for two enantiomers when reversing the magnetization of the ferromagnetic electrode. This also happens near zero bias, which appears to contradict Onsager’s reciprocal relations, stating that diffusive electron transport must obey microscopic reversibility in the linear regime. It has been suggested that this restriction can be lifted if there are two orbital channels leading to doubly degenerate transmission eigenvalues, in the presence of spin-orbit coupling. This motivates us to analyze the number of nonzero transmission eigenvalues in chiral molecular junctions as typically studied in experiments. In contrast to previous work, we do this based on the most established approach to a realistic first-principles description of such systems, the Landauer–Imry–Büttiker formalism combined with a nonequilibrium Green’s function framework. We find that two molecules, namely [6]-helicene and a pentapeptide α-Helix with thiolated terminal groups, show more than one significant transmission eigenvalue. We also find that other chiral molecules commonly used in CISS experiments exhibit only one significant eigenvalue. This suggests a comparative study of molecules with different numbers of significant transmission eigenvalues, under identical experimental conditions, to assess the relevance of these transmission eigenvalues for the CISS effect.

## 摘要（中文）
手性诱导自旋选择性（CISS）是由系统的手性与非平衡状态下的电子磁矩之间的相互作用引起的。 CISS 表现为磁阻中的两端连接（例如，电极-分子-电极），当反转铁磁电极的磁化时，两种对映体的电流-电压行为发生反转。这种情况也会发生在零偏压附近，这似乎与昂萨格的倒数关系相矛盾，表明扩散电子传输必须服从线性状态下的微观可逆性。有人建议，在存在自旋轨道耦合的情况下，如果有两个轨道通道导致双简并传输特征值，则可以解除此限制。这促使我们分析手性分子连接中非零传输特征值的数量，正如实验中通常研究的那样。与之前的工作相比，我们基于对此类系统进行现实第一原理描述的最成熟的方法来做到这一点，即兰道尔-伊姆里-布蒂克形式主义与非平衡格林函数框架相结合。我们发现两个分子，即[6]-螺旋烯和具有硫醇化末端基团的五肽α-螺旋，显示出不止一个显着的传输特征值。我们还发现 CISS 实验中常用的其他手性分子仅表现出一个显着的特征值。这表明在相同的实验条件下对具有不同数量的显着传输特征值的分子进行比较研究，以评估这些传输特征值与 CISS 效应的相关性。
