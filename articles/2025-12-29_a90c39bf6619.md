## Abstract
arXiv:2502.17876v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Quasiperiodic models are important physical platforms to explore Anderson transitions in low dimensional systems, yet the exact mobility edges (MEs) are generally hard to be determined analytically. To date, the MEs in only a few models can be determined exactly. In this manuscript, we propose a new class of network models characterized by quasiperiodic slowly varying potentials and the absence of hidden self-duality, and exactly determine their MEs. We take the mosaic models with slowly varying potentials as examples to illustrate this result and derive its MEs from the effective Hamiltonian. In this method, we can integrate out the periodic sites to obtain an effective Hamiltonian with energy-dependent potentials $g(E)V$ and effective eigenenergy $f(E)$, which directly yields the MEs at $f(E) = \pm(2t^\kappa \pm g(E)V)$, where $\kappa \in \mathbb{Z}^+$. With this idea in hand, we then generalize our method to more quasiperiodic network models, including those with much more complicated geometries and non-Hermitian features. Finally, we propose the realization of these models using optical waveguides and show that the Anderson transition can be observed even in small physical systems (with lattice sites about $L = 50 - 100$). Our results provide some key insights into the understanding and realization of exact MEs in experiments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2502.17876v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：准周期模型是探索低维系统中安德森跃迁的重要物理平台，但准确的迁移边（ME）通常很难通过分析确定。迄今为止，只有少数模型中的 ME 可以被准确确定。在这篇手稿中，我们提出了一类新的网络模型，其特征是准周期缓慢变化的势和不存在隐藏的自对偶性，并准确地确定了它们的 ME。我们以具有缓慢变化势的镶嵌模型为例来说明这一结果，并从有效哈密顿量导出其 ME。在这种方法中，我们可以对周期位点进行积分，以获得具有能量相关势 $g(E)V$ 和有效本征能量 $f(E)$ 的有效哈密顿量，这直接产生 $f(E) = \pm(2t^\kappa \pm g(E)V)$ 处的 ME，其中 $\kappa \in \mathbb{Z}^+$。有了这个想法，我们然后将我们的方法推广到更多的准周期网络模型，包括那些具有更复杂的几何形状和非厄米特特征的模型。最后，我们建议使用光波导实现这些模型，并表明即使在小型物理系统（晶格位点约为 $L = 50 - 100$）中也可以观察到安德森跃迁。我们的结果为理解和实现实验中的精确 ME 提供了一些关键见解。
