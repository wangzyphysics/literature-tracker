## Abstract
Nature Machine Intelligence, Published online: 25 February 2026; doi:10.1038/s42256-026-01198-9 Wang et al. introduce Mac-Diff, a conditional diffusion model with locality-aware attention across modalities, to generate diverse conformational ensembles for unseen proteins, capturing both dynamic flexibility and structural heterogeneity.
