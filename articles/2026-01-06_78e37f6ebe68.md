## Abstract
This work evaluates the impact of the reduction potential (ER) on the pulsed-mediated formation of high-index facet shaped structures on Cu(111) and on a copper polycrystalline electrode in sodium chloride (NaCl) electrolyte. Cyclic voltammetry (CV), electron backscatter diffraction (EBSD), and scanning electron microscopy (SEM) were combined to correlate grain orientation and surface morphology changes with experimental conditions that drive shape formation. Furthermore, we performed a comprehensive voltammetric analysis across a broad range of stepped single-crystal electrodes, demonstrating that blank cyclic voltammograms in NaCl effectively decouple terrace and step contributions on copper. Our results revealed that while chloride tends to form structures comprising (100) terraces and (111) or (110) defects when oxidation-reduction pulses are applied to refacet copper, the deposition rate, determined by the ER, controls the length of the created (100) terraces and defect density. The oxidation and reduction of 5-hydroxymethylfurfural (HMF) were investigated as model structure-sensitive reactions to probe how variations in the terrace-to-defect ratio affect catalytic behavior. Low-coordinated sites promote oxidation of HMF, whereas (100) terraces adjacent to steps lower the onset potential for HMF reduction. By identifying the active surface facets, this work demonstrates that surface structure engineering is a powerful approach to advance electrocatalysis on copper.

## 摘要（中文）
这项工作评估了氯化钠 (NaCl) 电解质中还原电位 (ER) 对 Cu(111) 和铜多晶电极上脉冲介导的高折射率面形结构形成的影响。结合循环伏安法 (CV)、电子背散射衍射 (EBSD) 和扫描电子显微镜 (SEM)，将晶粒取向和表面形态变化与驱动形状形成的实验条件相关联。此外，我们对各种阶梯式单晶电极进行了全面的伏安分析，证明了 NaCl 中的空白循环伏安图有效地解耦了铜上的阶梯和阶梯贡献。我们的结果表明，虽然当氧化还原脉冲应用于重面铜时，氯化物往往会形成包含（100）阶地和（111）或（110）缺陷的结构，但由ER确定的沉积速率控制所创建的（100）阶地的长度和缺陷密度。将 5-羟​​甲基糠醛 (HMF) 的氧化和还原作为结构敏感反应模型进行研究，以探讨平台与缺陷比的变化如何影响催化行为。低配位位点促进 HMF 氧化，而与台阶相邻的 (100) 阶地降低 HMF 还原的起始电位。通过识别活性表面，这项工作表明表面结构工程是推进铜电催化的有效方法。
