## Abstract
The low-frequency characteristics of infrasound signals enable them to play a critical role in the long-range detection of disasters such as earthquakes, landslides, and chemical explosions. However, the accurate classification of disaster types based on infrasound signals remains a significant challenge. Traditional methods rely on manual feature extraction, often failing to capture spatiotemporal patterns. While deep learning approaches, particularly convolutional neural networks (CNNs), show promise, they are limited by finite depth, inadequate temporal modeling capabilities, and network degradation. Additionally, a single CNN struggles to learn diverse feature representations and exhibits low fault tolerance to anomalies. To address these challenges, a machine learning-driven classification method is proposed featuring three synergistic components: a synergistic feature-deep learning architecture that integrates continuous wavelet-scale average coefficients-based time-scale transformation and deep feature learning to enhance discriminative capability; a triple-branch architecture that integrates multi-scale spatial convolutions and adaptive temporal regulation to model propagation dynamics; and a parallel confidence fusion module that dynamically weights branch outputs for final robust decisions. Experimental results show that the proposed method achieves 98.75% accuracy on a natural earthquake infrasound dataset and maintains a high accuracy on an open-source multi-class dataset, demonstrating strong robustness and generalization capability for natural disaster monitoring.

## 摘要（中文）
次声信号的低频特性使其在地震、山体滑坡、化学爆炸等灾害的远程探测中发挥关键作用。然而，根据次声信号对灾害类型进行准确分类仍然是一个重大挑战。传统方法依赖于手动特征提取，通常无法捕获时空模式。虽然深度学习方法，特别是卷积神经网络 (CNN)，显示出了前景，但它们受到有限深度、时间建模能力不足和网络退化的限制。此外，单个 CNN 很难学习不同的特征表示，并且对异常的容错能力较低。为了应对这些挑战，提出了一种机器学习驱动的分类方法，该方法具有三个协同组件：协同特征深度学习架构，集成了基于连续小波尺度平均系数的时间尺度变换和深度特征学习，以增强判别能力；集成多尺度空间卷积和自适应时间调节来模拟传播动态的三分支架构；并行置信融合模块，动态加权分支输出以实现最终的鲁棒决策。实验结果表明，该方法在天然地震次声数据集上达到了98.75%的准确率，并且在开源多类数据集上保持了较高的准确率，表现出较强的自然灾害监测鲁棒性和泛化能力。
