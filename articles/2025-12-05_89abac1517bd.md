## Abstract
Author(s): Junfa Lin, Jianfeng Guo, Huan Wang, Xiaoyan Wang, Sheng Xu, Xiangyu Zeng, Yu Zhang, Zhihai Cheng, and Tian-Long Xia Novel Hall phenomena are observed in centrosymmetric Mn5Ge3 ferromagnet. Contrary to the typical behavior observed in most ferromagnets, the large anomalous Hall effect is strongly suppressed at low temperatures, giving way to a dominant ordinary Hall effect that is well described by a two-band model. This unique temperature dependence suggests a competition between intrinsic and skew-scattering mechanisms. Furthermore, this work identifies two distinct origins of the topological Hall effect: one arising from non-collinear spin textures and the other from skyrmion bubbles. These findings offer new insights into both the anomalous and topological Hall effects. [Phys. Rev. Materials 9, 124403] Published Fri Dec 05, 2025

作者：林俊发、郭建峰、王欢、王晓燕、徐胜、曾翔宇、张宇、程志海、夏天龙 中心对称 Mn5Ge3 铁磁体中观察到的小说霍尔现象。与大多数铁磁体中观察到的典型行为相反，大的反常霍尔效应在低温下被强烈抑制，让位于由两带模型很好描述的占主导地位的普通霍尔效应。这种独特的温度依赖性表明内在机制和斜散射机制之间存在竞争。此外，这项工作还确定了拓扑霍尔效应的两个不同起源：一个来自非共线自旋纹理，另一个来自斯格明子气泡。这些发现为异常霍尔效应和拓扑霍尔效应提供了新的见解。 [物理。 Rev. Materials 9, 124403] 2025 年 12 月 5 日星期五发布
