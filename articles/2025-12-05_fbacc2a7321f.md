## Abstract
Author(s): Sebin J. Sebastian, Q.-P. Ding, A. A. Tsirlin, R. Nath, and Y. Furukawa The authors investigate here the double-trillium lattice antiferromagnet KSrFe 2 (PO 4 ) 3 using 31 P NMR, thermodynamic measurements, and first-principles calculations to elucidate its unconventional ground state. Below the characteristic temperature T * ~ 3.5 K, the results reveal a short-range spin freezing state with persistent antiferromagnetic fluctuations, contradicting earlier proposals of a gapless spin-liquid state. A nearly field-independent NMR linewidth, strong enhancement of 1/ T 2 below T *, and sizeable antiferromagnetic exchange interactions collectively position KSrFe 2 (PO 4 ) 3 as a rare three-dimensional frustrated magnet hosting intertwined static and dynamic correlations. [Phys. Rev. B 112, L220406] Published Fri Dec 05, 2025

作者：Sebin J. Sebastian, Q.-P。 Ding、A. A. Tsirlin、R. Nath 和 Y. Furukawa 作者利用 31 P NMR、热力学测量和第一性原理计算研究了双延龄晶格反铁磁体 KSrFe 2 (PO 4 ) 3 ，以阐明其非常规基态。在特征温度 T * ~ 3.5 K 以下，结果揭示了具有持续反铁磁涨落的短程自旋冻结态，这与早期提出的无间隙自旋液体态相矛盾。几乎与场无关的 NMR 线宽、低于 T * 的 1/ T 2 的强烈增强以及相当大的反铁磁交换相互作用共同将 KSrFe 2 (PO 4 ) 3 定位为一种罕见的三维受挫磁体，具有相互交织的静态和动态相关性。 [物理。修订版 B 112，L220406] 2025 年 12 月 5 日星期五发布
