## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09738v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report the emergence of highly nonlinear spin and charge pumping in an altermagnetic system driven by magnetic dynamics. The nonrelativistic spin-momentum coupling inherent to altermagnets generates a giant momentum dependent spin splitting, leading to strong spin-flip scattering in the presence of a precessing magnetic order driving the altermagnetic system out of equilibrium. Our simulations reveal the emission of hundreds of harmonics under realistic conditions, with amplitudes far exceeding those obtained in light-driven schemes. Notably, in contrast to ferromagnetic and conventional antiferromagnetic systems, where nonlinear emission typically requires additional spin-orbit coupling, altermagnets intrinsically support high-harmonic spin and charge pumping. These results identify altermagnetic systems as a promising platform for efficient THz emitters and highly nonlinear spintronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09738v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报告了由磁动力学驱动的交变磁系统中出现的高度非线性自旋和电荷泵浦。交变磁体固有的非相对论自旋动量耦合会产生巨大的动量相关自旋分裂，在进动磁序存在的情况下导致强烈的自旋翻转散射，从而驱动交变磁系统失去平衡。我们的模拟揭示了在现实条件下发射的数百种谐波，其幅度远远超过光驱动方案中获得的幅度。值得注意的是，与铁磁和传统反铁磁系统相比，非线性发射通常需要额外的自旋轨道耦合，而交流磁体本质上支持高次谐波自旋和电荷泵浦。这些结果表明交变磁系统是高效太赫兹发射器和高度非线性自旋电子器件的有前途的平台。
