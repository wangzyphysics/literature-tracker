# One-pot organocatalysis of diacrylates to functional polyesters for toughening polylactide

## Abstract
ABSTRACT Conventional chain-growth polymerization of acrylic monomers or diacrylics leads to non-degradable vinyl polymers or crosslinked networks, while existing step-growth polymerization affords saturated or main-chain unsaturated polyesters, hindering post-functionalization. Here, we introduce a hydrogen-transfer polymerization (HTP) strategy via the trialkylphosphine-catalyzed head-to-tail C–C coupling between the α - and β -positions of two vinyl groups of diacrylates with exclusive regioselectivity, producing unsaturated polyesters with side-chain double bonds that can undergo the subsequent thiol-Michael click reaction in a one-pot fashion with the carryover phosphine from the HTP step. The resulting hydroxyl-functionalized polyesters serve as macroinitiators for the efficient synthesis of densely grafted poly( l -lactide) (PLLA) bottlebrush polymers. Such bottlebrush polymers markedly toughen the otherwise brittle PLLA (by ∼10×), while not only uniquely preserving high melting temperature and crystallinity of PLLA but also synergistically increasing the PLLA crystallization rate.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要：丙烯酸单体或二丙烯酸的传统链增长聚合会产生不可降解的乙烯基聚合物或交联网络，而现有的逐步增长聚合会产生饱和或主链不饱和聚酯，阻碍后官能化。在这里，我们引入了一种氢转移聚合（HTP）策略，通过三烷基膦催化的二丙烯酸酯的两个乙烯基的α-和β-位之间的头尾C-C偶联，具有独特的区域选择性，产生具有侧链双键的不饱和聚酯，该聚酯可以以一锅的方式与HTP步骤中残留的膦进行后续的硫醇-迈克尔点击反应。所得羟基官能化聚酯可作为大分子引发剂，用于有效合成密集接枝聚(L-丙交酯)(PLLA)瓶刷聚合物。这种瓶刷聚合物显着增韧了原本易碎的 PLLA（约 10 倍），同时不仅独特地保持了 PLLA 的高熔融温度和结晶度，而且还协同提高了 PLLA 结晶速率。
