## Abstract
Author(s): Shutaro Karube, Yuta Yahagi, Yoshiaki Saito, Chih-Hsiang Tseng, Ryusuke Hisatomi, Yoichi Shiota, and Teruo Ono Anisotropic orbital Hall behavior in epitaxial Ti thin films emerges as a natural consequence of direction-dependent orbital transport. First-principles calculations reveal that the orbital Hall conductivity in Ti(1 1 ¯ 00) differs when electric fields are applied along [0001] versus [11 2 ¯ 0]. Experiments using Ni to probe orbital torque confirm this intrinsic anisotropy, showing corresponding changes in torque generation, and in the critical current required for magnetization switching. These findings uncover orbital-transport anisotropy absent in polycrystalline systems and highlight a fundamental pathway for orbital-driven spintronic phenomena. [Phys. Rev. Materials 10, 014401] Published Wed Jan 07, 2026

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Shutaro Karube、Yuta Yahagi、Yoshiaki Saito、Chih-Hsiang Tseng、Ryusuke Hisatomi、Yoichi Shiota 和 Teruo Ono 外延 Ti 薄膜中的各向异性轨道霍尔行为是方向相关轨道输运的自然结果。第一性原理计算表明，当沿 [0001] 与 [11 2 ¯ 0] 施加电场时，Ti(1 1 ¯ 00) 中的轨道霍尔电导率会有所不同。使用 Ni 探测轨道扭矩的实验证实了这种固有的各向异性，显示了扭矩产生以及磁化切换所需的临界电流的相应变化。这些发现揭示了多晶系统中不存在的轨道输运各向异性，并强调了轨道驱动自旋电子现象的基本途径。 [物理。 Rev. Materials 10, 014401] 2026 年 1 月 7 日星期三发布
