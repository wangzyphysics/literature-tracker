## Abstract
arXiv:2510.11707v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study the honeycomb lattice with a single magnetic impurity modeled by adding imaginary next-nearest-neighbor hopping ih on a single hexagon. This Haldane defect gives a topological mass term to the gapless Dirac cones and generates chirality. For a small density of defects Neehus et al [arXiv:2405.19289] found that the system's chirality reverses at a critical hc ~ 0.95 associated with an unexpected tri-critical point of Dirac fermions at zero defect density. We investigate this zero-density limit by analyzing a single defect and computing two experimentally relevant measures of chirality: (1) orbital magnetization via local Chern marker, a bulk probe of all occupied states; and (2) electronic currents of low-energy states. Both probes show a chirality reversal at a critical hc ~ 0.9--1. Motivated by this consistency we propose a defect-scale toy model whose low energy states reverse their chirality at hc' ~ 0.87. Remarkably, the same pair of zero energy bound states also generate the critical point hc in the full impurity projected T-matrix. Our results show how the chirality reversal produced by an impurity can be observed either in local probes or in the global topology and suggest a possible role of the microscopic defect structure at the critical point.

arXiv:2510.11707v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们通过在单个六边形上添加假想的次近邻跳跃 ih 来研究具有单个磁性杂质的蜂窝晶格。这种霍尔丹缺陷为无间隙狄拉克锥体提供了拓扑质量项并产生手性。对于小密度的缺陷，Neehus 等人 [arXiv:2405.19289] 发现系统的手性在临界 hc ~ 0.95 处反转，与零缺陷密度下狄拉克费米子的意外三临界点相关。我们通过分析单个缺陷并计算两个实验相关的手性测量来研究这种零密度极限：（1）通过局部陈标记（所有占据态的体探针）进行轨道磁化； (2)低能态电子电流。两种探针均在临界 hc ~ 0.9--1 处显示手性反转。受这种一致性的启发，我们提出了一种缺陷尺度玩具模型，其低能态在 hc' ~ 0.87 处反转其手性。值得注意的是，同一对零能量束缚态也在全杂质投影 T 矩阵中生成临界点 hc 。我们的结果显示了如何在局部探针或全局拓扑中观察到杂质产生的手性反转，并表明微观缺陷结构在关键点的可能作用。
