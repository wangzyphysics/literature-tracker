# Incorporating Coulomb interactions with fixed charges in moment tensor potentials and equivariant tensor network potentials

## Abstract
In this work, we incorporate long-range electrostatic interactions in the form of the Coulomb model with fixed charges into the functional form of short-range machine-learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs), particularly in the moment tensor potential and equivariant tensor network potential. We show that the explicit incorporation of the Coulomb interactions with fixed charges leads to a significant reduction of energy fitting errors, namely, more than four times, of short-range MLIPs trained on organic dimers of charged molecules. Furthermore, with our long-range models, we demonstrate a significant improvement in the prediction of the binding curves of the organic dimers of charged molecules. Finally, we show that the results calculated with MLIPs are in good correspondence with those obtained with density functional theory for organic dimers of charged molecules.

## 摘要（中文）
在这项工作中，我们将具有固定电荷的库仑模型形式的长程静电相互作用纳入短程机器学习原子间势（MLIP）的函数形式中，特别是在矩张量势和等变张量网络势中。我们表明，库仑相互作用与固定电荷的明确结合导致能量拟合误差显着减少，即在带电分子有机二聚体上训练的短程 MLIP 的四倍以上。此外，通过我们的长程模型，我们证明了带电分子有机二聚体结合曲线的预测有了显着改进。最后，我们表明，对于带电分子的有机二聚体，使用 MLIP 计算的结果与使用密度泛函理论获得的结果具有良好的一致性。
