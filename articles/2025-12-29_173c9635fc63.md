## Abstract
We report a new charge model and a new general small molecule force field. Here, we address the development and benchmarking of both the Open Force Field (OpenFF) AshGC charge model, as well as the Sage 2.3.0 small molecule force field for drug-like molecules. AshGC is a graph neural network-based method for efficient assignment of charges to both small and large molecules, designed to produce charges of semi- empirical quality at low cost. We describe the model, its architecture, featurization, training process, and performance. AshGC largely generates charges within the range of other accepted AM1-BCC backends such as OpenEye’s oequacpac and AmberTools’ sqm, but we note areas of poor performance such as in smaller molecules and in charged molecules, as well as areas of chemistry underrepresented in the training set. We fur- ther describe the development and performance of Sage 2.3.0, where Lennard-Jones and valence parameters are re-trained with neural network charges for the first time. Our benchmark suite of common force field tasks ranges from optimizing molecular structures in vacuum to protein-ligand binding free energies. Overall, Sage 2.3.0 performs comparably to previous Sage force fields, although we report minor improvements in the prediction of condensed phase properties and a minor decrease in performance in the prediction of non-aqueous solvation free energies. As with other OpenFF force fields, Sage 2.3.0 was validated in protein-ligand benchmarks to be compatible with Amber protein force fields. All data are publicly available, along with scripts, environments, and complete details for reproducing the training and benchmarking of AshGC and Sage 2.3.0 at https://github.com/openforcefield/ashgc-v1.0-fit and github.com/openforcefield/ash-sage-rc2 respectively.

## 摘要（中文）
我们报告了一种新的电荷模型和一种新的通用小分子力场。在这里，我们讨论开放力场 (OpenFF) AshGC 电荷模型以及药物样分子的 Sage 2.3.0 小分子力场的开发和基准测试。 AshGC 是一种基于图神经网络的方法，用于将电荷有效分配给小分子和大分子，旨在以低成本产生半经验质量的电荷。我们描述了模型、其架构、特征、训练过程和性能。 AshGC 产生的电荷主要在其他公认的 AM1-BCC 后端（例如 OpenEye 的 oequacpac 和 AmberTools 的 sqm）范围内，但我们注意到性能较差的领域，例如较小分子和带电分子，以及训练集中代表性不足的化学领域。我们进一步描述了 Sage 2.3.0 的开发和性能，其中首次使用神经网络电荷重新训练 Lennard-Jones 和价参数。我们的常见力场任务基准套件范围从优化真空中的分子结构到蛋白质-配体结合自由能。总体而言，Sage 2.3.0 的性能与以前的 Sage 力场相当，尽管我们报告在凝聚相性质的预测方面有微小的改进，并且在非水溶剂化自由能的预测方面的性能略有下降。与其他 OpenFF 力场一样，Sage 2.3.0 在蛋白质配体基准测试中经过验证，与 Amber 蛋白质力场兼容。所有数据以及脚本、环境和用于重现 AshGC 和 Sage 2.3.0 训练和基准测试的完整详细信息均公开可用，分别位于 https://github.com/openforcefield/ashgc-v1.0-fit 和 github.com/openforcefield/ash-sage-rc2。
