## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01721v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Sub-symmetry (SubSy) protected topological states represent a concept that goes beyond the conventional framework of symmetry-protected topological (SPT) phases, demonstrating that topological boundary states can remain robust even when the pertinent symmetry holds only in a subset of Hilbert space. Typical SPT and SubSy boundary states decay exponentially into the bulk, which means they are not confined in just few lattice sites close to the boundary. Here, we introduce topologically compact edge states protected by SubSy, featuring extreme two-site localization at boundaries of a lattice, without any decay into the bulk. The compactness arises from local destructive interference at the boundary, while topological protection is ensured by SubSy, characterized by quantized winding numbers. Experimentally, we observe compact edge states in laser-written photonic lattices with engineered rhombic-like unit cells, confirming their robustness against perturbations under both chiral symmetry and SubSy conditions. Our results highlight the potential of SubSy protection for achieving topological confinement of light, paving the way for applications in compact waveguides, lasers, and high-sensitivity photonic sensors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01721v1 公告类型：新 摘要：亚对称（SubSy）保护拓扑态代表了一个超越对称保护拓扑（SPT）相的传统框架的概念，证明即使相关对称性仅在希尔伯特空间的子集中成立，拓扑边界态也可以保持鲁棒性。典型的 SPT 和 SubSy 边界态在整体中呈指数衰减，这意味着它们不仅仅局限于靠近边界的少数晶格位置。在这里，我们引入了受 SubSy 保护的拓扑紧凑边缘态，其特点是在晶格边界处具有极端的两点局域化，而不会衰减到体中。紧凑性源于边界处的局部相消干涉，而拓扑保护由 SubSy 保证，其特征是量化的绕数。在实验中，我们观察到具有工程菱形晶胞的激光写入光子晶格中的致密边缘态，证实了它们在手性对称和 SubSy 条件下对抗扰动的鲁棒性。我们的结果凸显了 SubSy 保护在实现光的拓扑限制方面的潜力，为紧凑型波导、激光器和高灵敏度光子传感器的应用铺平了道路。
