## Abstract
The magnetic molecular interferometer (MMI) is a molecular beam scattering apparatus, which allows the polarization of the rotational angular momentum (J) of ortho-H 2 molecules to be controlled using tunable magnetic fields before they collide with a surface, and their J′ polarization to be determined after the collision. In the current work, quantum population distribution functions, or “stereodynamical portraits,” are used to visualize the rotational angular momentum polarization of ortho-H 2 molecules that the MMI creates before the collision with the surface, revealing that the sensitivity of the MMI to stereodynamic effects which depend on the orientation of J with respect to the surface normal can be increased by manipulating the H 2 molecules with two perpendicular magnetic fields rather than just a single field. They can also be used to depict the polarization dependence of a H 2 -surface collision, as shown by the example considered here, where it is found that when H 2 molecules undergo diffractive scattering from a Cu(511) surface, different J polarizations are selected to scatter into different diffraction channels, just as different polarizations of J′ are created after scattering. Signals measured with the MMI are necessarily dependent on both the rotational polarization the MMI creates and the dependence of the molecule-surface collision on this, and it is demonstrated that for flux detection measurements it would be possible to analyze the data directly in terms of the polarization moments which characterize these two properties to gain a more immediate insight into the stereodynamics of the collision than is possible using alternative analysis methods.

## 摘要（中文）
磁分子干涉仪（MMI）是一种分子束散射装置，它允许在邻H 2 分子与表面碰撞之前使用可调磁场控制其旋转角动量（J）的偏振，并在碰撞后确定它们的J'偏振。在当前的工作中，量子布居分布函数或“立体动力学肖像”用于可视化MMI在与表面碰撞之前产生的邻H 2 分子的旋转角动量极化，揭示了MMI对立体动力学效应的敏感性，该效应取决于J相对于表面法线的方向，可以通过用两个垂直磁场而不是仅用单个场操纵H 2 分子来增加。它们还可用于描述 H 2 表面碰撞的偏振依赖性，如此处考虑的示例所示，其中发现当 H 2 分子经历来自 Cu(511) 表面的衍射散射时，选择不同的 J 偏振散射到不同的衍射通道中，就像散射后创建不同的 J' 偏振一样。用 MMI 测量的信号必然取决于 MMI 产生的旋转偏振以及分子表面碰撞对此的依赖性，并且事实证明，对于通量检测测量，可以直接根据表征这两个属性的偏振矩来分析数据，以便比使用其他分析方法更直接地了解碰撞的立体动力学。
