## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00515v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Assembly theory (AT) introduces a concept of causation as a material property, constitutive of a metrology of evolution and selection. The physical scale for causation is quantified with the assembly index, defined as the minimum number of steps necessary for a distinguishable object to exist, where steps are assembled recursively. Observing countable copies of high assembly index objects indicates that a mechanism to produce them is persistent, such that the object's environment builds a memory that traps causation within a contingent chain. Copy number and assembly index underlie the standardized metrology for detecting causation (assembly index), and evidence of contingency (copy number). Together, these allow the precise definition of a selective threshold in assembly space, understood as the set of all causal possibilities. This threshold demarcates life (and its derivative agential, intelligent and technological forms) as structures with persistent copies beyond the threshold. In introducing a fundamental concept of material causation to explain and measure life, AT represents a departure from prior theories of causation, such as interventional ones, which have so far proven incompatible with fundamental physics. We discuss how AT's concept of causation provides the foundation for a theory of physics where novelty, contingency and the potential for open-endedness are fundamental, and determinism is emergent along assembled lineages.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00515v1 公告类型：新 摘要：组装理论（AT）引入了因果关系的概念，作为一种物质属性，构成了进化和选择的计量学。因果关系的物理尺度通过组装指数进行量化，组装指数定义为可区分对象存在所需的最小步骤数，其中步骤是递归组装的。观察高组装索引对象的可数副本表明生成它们的机制是持久的，使得对象的环境构建了一个记忆，将因果关系捕获在偶然链中。拷贝数和组装指数是用于检测因果关系（组装指数）和意外事件证据（拷贝数）的标准化计量的基础。这些共同允许精确定义装配空间中的选择性阈值，将其理解为所有因果可能性的集合。这个阈值将生命（及其衍生的代理、智能和技术形式）划分为具有超出阈值的持久副本的结构。在引入物质因果关系的基本概念来解释和测量生命时，AT 代表了对先前因果关系理论的背离，例如迄今为止已被证明与基础物理学不相容的干预理论。我们讨论 AT 的因果关系概念如何为物理理论提供基础，其中新颖性、偶然性和开放性的潜力是基础，而决定论是沿着组装的谱系出现的。
