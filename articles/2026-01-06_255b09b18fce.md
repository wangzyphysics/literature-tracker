## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02130v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The present paper sketches a framework for solving the wave and Fokker-Planck equations in the ion cyclotron resonance frequency domain fully selfconsistently. It illustrates this can be done by first constructing "building blocks" that are commonly needed by the wave and Fokker-Planck equations, allowing e.g. to account for wave coupling in plasmas containing non-Maxwellian distributions. Up to details, the paper exploits known expressions and methods to solve the two intimately connected aspects of the description of the wave-particle interaction underlying ion cyclotron resonance heating. Two cases are presented: the case where the guiding centre motion is limited to just following magnetic field lines, and the extended case accounting for drifts away from magnetic surfaces but assuming axisymmetry. A limited set of analytical results is included. As combining wave and Fokker-Planck solving is the focus, the computation of the dielectric response for arbitrary distribution functions is illustrated as well.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02130v1 发布类型：新 摘要：本文提出了一个在离子回旋共振频域中完全自洽求解波方程和 Fokker-Planck 方程的框架。它说明这可以通过首先构建波和福克-普朗克方程通常需要的“构建块”来完成，例如允许解释包含非麦克斯韦分布的等离子体中的波耦合。具体而言，本文利用已知的表达式和方法来解决离子回旋共振加热下波粒相互作用描述的两个密切相关的方面。提出了两种情况：引导中心运动仅限于遵循磁场线的情况，以及考虑远离磁性表面但假设轴对称的扩展情况。包括一组有限的分析结果。由于组合波和 Fokker-Planck 求解是重点，因此还说明了任意分布函数的介电响应的计算。
