## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06312v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Binary stoichiometry FeSe (s-FeSe) is a well-known parent of high-temperature unconventional superconductors owing to its charge-neutral layer, highly tunable structure and electronic properties, and rich interplays among multiple electronic phases. Yet the s-FeSe synthesized via high-temperature equilibrium reactions bears the notorious interstitial Fe,where merely 3% of them is sufficient to kill the superconductivity. Here, we successfully synthesized a new non-stoichiometric Fe1.11Se single crystal with a superconducting onset temperature (Tconset) of 30.4 K through a hydrothermal ion-exchange and de-intercalation route. 11% interstitial Fe ions exceed the equilibrium phase diagram limit. Intriguingly, under physical pressure, the Tconset of exhibits a "V"-shaped evolution with a minimum at 2-2.6 GPa, and then upturning into a second superconducting region, reminiscent of the behaviors in FeSe intercalates. Furthermore, a pressure-induced possible magnetic order, previously only observed in pressurized s-FeSe, shows up. These results offer fresh insights into the role of interstitial Fe in governing superconducting and transport properties under non-equilibrium synthesis and tuning strategies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06312v1 公告类型：新 摘要：二元化学计量 FeSe (s-FeSe) 因其电荷中性层、高度可调的结构和电子特性以及多个电子相之间丰富的相互作用而成为众所周知的高温非常规超导体的母体。然而，通过高温平衡反应合成的s-FeSe带有臭名昭著的间隙Fe，其中仅3%就足以破坏超导性。在这里，我们通过水热离子交换和脱嵌路线成功合成了一种新型非化学计量Fe1.11Se单晶，其超导起始温度（Tconset）为30.4 K。 11% 间隙 Fe 离子超出平衡相图极限。有趣的是，在物理压力下，Tconset 表现出“V”形演化，最小值为 2-2.6 GPa，然后向上转变为第二个超导区域，让人想起 FeSe 插层的行为。此外，还出现了压力诱导的可能的磁序，以前仅在加压的 s-FeSe 中观察到。这些结果为非平衡合成和调谐策略下间隙铁在控制超导和输运特性中的作用提供了新的见解。
