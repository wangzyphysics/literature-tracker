## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12933v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Melanoma brain metastases (MBM) are common and spatially heterogeneous lesions, complicating cohort-level analyses due to anatomical variability and differing MRI protocols. We propose a fully differentiable, deep-learning-based deformable registration framework that aligns individual pathological brains to a common atlas while preserving metastatic tissue without requiring lesion masks or preprocessing. Missing anatomical correspondences caused by metastases are handled through a forward-model similarity metric based on distance-transformed anatomical labels, combined with a volume-preserving regularization term to ensure deformation plausibility. Registration performance was evaluated using Dice coefficient (DSC), Hausdorff distance (HD), average symmetric surface distance (ASSD), and Jacobian-based measures. The method was applied to 209 MBM patients from three centres, enabling standardized mapping of metastases to anatomical, arterial, and perfusion atlases. The framework achieved high registration accuracy across datasets (DSC 0.89-0.92, HD 6.79-7.60 mm, ASSD 0.63-0.77 mm) while preserving metastatic volumes. Spatial analysis demonstrated significant over-representation of MBM in the cerebral cortex and putamen, under-representation in white matter, and consistent localization near the gray-white matter junction. No arterial territory showed increased metastasis frequency after volume correction. This approach enables robust atlas registration of pathological brain MRI without lesion masks and supports reproducible multi-centre analyses. Applied to MBM, it confirms and refines known spatial predilections, particularly preferential seeding near the gray-white matter junction and cortical regions. The publicly available implementation facilitates reproducible research and extension to other brain tumours and neurological pathologies.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12933v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：黑色素瘤脑转移 (MBM) 是常见且空间异质的病变，由于解剖变异性和不同的 MRI 协议，使队列水平分析变得复杂。我们提出了一种完全可微的、基于深度学习的变形配准框架，该框架将个体病理大脑与共同的图集对齐，同时保留转移组织，而不需要病变掩模或预处理。由转移引起的解剖对应缺失通过基于距离变换的解剖标签的前向模型相似性度量来处理，并结合体积保持正则化项以确保变形的合理性。使用 Dice 系数 (DSC)、Hausdorff 距离 (HD)、平均对称表面距离 (ASSD) 和基于雅可比的测量来评估配准性能。该方法应用于来自三个中心的 209 名 MBM 患者，实现了解剖、动脉和灌注图谱上转移灶的标准化绘制。该框架在跨数据集（DSC 0.89-0.92、HD 6.79-7.60 mm、ASSD 0.63-0.77 mm）实现了高配准精度，同时保留了转移体积。空间分析表明，MBM 在大脑皮层和壳核中的代表性显着过高，在白质中的代表性不足，并且在灰白质交界处附近的定位一致。体积校正后，没有动脉区域显示转移频率增加。这种方法能够在没有病变掩模的情况下对病理性脑部 MRI 进行稳健的图谱注册，并支持可重复的多中心分析。应用于MBM，它确认并完善了已知的空间偏好，特别是灰白质交界处和皮质区域附近的优先播种。公开实施有助于可重复研究和扩展到其他脑肿瘤和神经病理学。
