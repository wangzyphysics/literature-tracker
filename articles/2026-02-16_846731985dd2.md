## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13113v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Energy flows among coupled subsystems are essential for ultrafast dynamics and high-speed technologies. In magnetic materials, spin fluctuations -- magnons -- mediate these flows in ultrafast magnetism. Yet momentum-resolved access to low-energy magnons governing the microscopic dynamics has been lacking. Using time-resolved resonant diffuse scattering alongside complementary time-resolved X-ray techniques and quantum-kinetic simulations, we unveil the hierarchical energy pathways among correlated systems in the photoexcited antiferromagnet CuO. Above-bandgap excitation triggers near-instantaneous spin disorder, generating non-thermal magnons throughout reciprocal space within femtoseconds. Real-time momentum-resolved tracking reveals picosecond magnon quasi-thermalization, followed by nanosecond recovery via momentum-selective magnon-phonon scattering. The quasiparticle dispersion mismatch creates recovery bottlenecks that control non-equilibrium lifetimes. This microscopic framework transcends phenomenological models and generalizes across materials, establishing design principles for ultrafast control of material properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13113v1 公告类型：新 摘要：耦合子系统之间的能量流动对于超快动力学和高速技术至关重要。在磁性材料中，自旋涨落（磁振子）以超快磁性调节这些流动。然而，一直缺乏控制微观动力学的低能磁振子的动量解析方法。利用时间分辨共振漫散射以及互补的时间分辨 X 射线技术和量子动力学模拟，我们揭示了光激发反铁磁体 CuO 中相关系统之间的分层能量路径。带隙之上的激发会触发近乎瞬时的自旋无序，在飞秒内在整个倒易空间中产生非热磁振子。实时动量分辨跟踪揭示了皮秒磁振子准热化，然后通过动量选择性磁振子-声子散射进行纳秒恢复。准粒子色散失配产生了控制非平衡寿命的恢复瓶颈。这种微观框架超越了现象学模型，并推广到各种材料，建立了超快控制材料特性的设计原则。
