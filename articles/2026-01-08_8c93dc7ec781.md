## Abstract
Nanocarbon materials have emerged as powerful platforms for biological applications, including nucleic acid delivery, bioimaging, and biosensing. While these materials have been explored for imaging and phototherapy, the use of molecularly well-defined nanocarbons with independently tunable charge and hydrophobicity for gene delivery remains largely unexplored. Herein, we report the rational design and synthesis of a new class of cationic perylene tetraesters for efficient nucleic acid delivery. The lead compound, SMZ052, exhibits delivery efficiencies comparable to those of standard commercial reagents across plasmid DNA and siRNA platforms in mammalian cells, and further enables DNA delivery into plant protoplasts as well as hematopoietic stem cells, a cell type that is typically challenging to transfect. These results establish molecular nanocarbons as a versatile and designable platform for gene delivery and highlight their broad potential across biological systems and translational applications.

## 摘要（中文）
纳米碳材料已成为生物应用的强大平台，包括核酸输送、生物成像和生物传感。虽然这些材料已被探索用于成像和光疗，但具有独立可调电荷和疏水性的分子定义明确的纳米碳用于基因传递的用途在很大程度上仍未得到探索。在此，我们报告了用于有效核酸递送的新型阳离子苝四酯的合理设计和合成。先导化合物 SMZ052 在哺乳动物细胞中的质粒 DNA 和 siRNA 平台上表现出与标准商业试剂相当的递送效率，并且进一步能够将 DNA 递送到植物原生质体以及造血干细胞（一种通常难以转染的细胞类型）中。这些结果将分子纳米碳确立为一种多功能且可设计的基因传递平台，并突显了它们在生物系统和转化应用中的广泛潜力。
