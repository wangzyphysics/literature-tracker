## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04773v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Predicting tensorial properties with machine learning models typically requires carefully designed tensorial descriptors. In this work, we introduce an alternative strategy for learning tensorial quantities based on scalar descriptors. We apply this approach to the Born effective charge tensor, showing that scalar (monopole) kernel models can successfully capture its tensorial nature by exploiting the definition of the Born effective charge tensor as the derivative of the polarisation with respect to atomic displacements. We compare this method with tensorial (dipole) kernel models, as established in our previous work, in which the tensorial structure of the Born effective charge is encoded directly in the kernel and obtained via its derivative. Both approaches are then used for charge partitioning, enabling the separation of monopole and dipole contributions. Finally, we demonstrate the effectiveness of the framework by computing finite-temperature infrared spectra for a range of complex materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04773v1 公告类型：新 摘要：使用机器学习模型预测张量属性通常需要精心设计的张量描述符。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种基于标量描述符学习张量的替代策略。我们将这种方法应用于玻恩有效电荷张量，表明标量（单极）核模型可以通过利用玻恩有效电荷张量的定义作为极化相对于原子位移的导数来成功捕获其张量性质。我们将此方法与我们之前的工作中建立的张量（偶极）核模型进行比较，其中玻恩有效电荷的张量结构直接编码在核中并通过其导数获得。然后，这两种方法都用于电荷分配，从而能够分离单极子和偶极子的贡献。最后，我们通过计算一系列复杂材料的有限温度红外光谱来证明该框架的有效性。
