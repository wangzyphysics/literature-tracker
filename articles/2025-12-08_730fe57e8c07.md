## Abstract
Author(s): Amena Khatun and Muhammad Usman A quantum-classical machine learning framework is presented in which a classical autoencoder is used to distill quantum-generated adversarial manipulations. This approach restores quantum classifier accuracy under FGSM and PGD attacks on MNIST and Fashion-MNIST datasets, demonstrating effective mitigation of quantum adversarial manipulations. [Phys. Rev. Research 7, L042054] Published Mon Dec 08, 2025
