## Abstract
arXiv:2504.00121v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Since precisely controlling dissipation in realistic environments is challenging, digital simulation of the Lindblad master equation (LME) is of great significance for understanding nonequilibrium dynamics in open quantum systems. However, achieving long-time simulations for complex systems with multiple dissipation channels remains a major challenge, both theoretically and experimentally. Here, we propose a 1-dilation digital scheme for simulating the LME based on quantum trajectory averaging without postselection. By rigorously matching the stochasticity inherent in quantum trajectories with the probabilistic outcomes of quantum measurements, our method effectively translates the classically established quantum jump algorithm into executable quantum circuits. A key advantage of our method is that it overcomes the exponential suppression of success probability seen in some existing postselection-dependent schemes, especially for long-time evolution or systems with numerous jump operators. Moreover, the scheme can be extended to a 2-dilation framework for the nonlinear LME with postselection, bridging the full LME and non-Hermitian Hamiltonian dynamics. This extended scheme provides a digital approach for exploring the interplay between non-Hermitian Hamiltonians and dissipative terms within a monitored quantum dynamics framework.

arXiv:2504.00121v2 发布类型：replace-cross 摘要：由于在现实环境中精确控制耗散具有挑战性，林德布拉德主方程（LME）的数字模拟对于理解开放量子系统中的非平衡动力学具有重要意义。然而，实现具有多个耗散通道的复杂系统的长时间仿真仍然是理论上和实验上的重大挑战。在这里，我们提出了一种基于量子轨迹平均而不进行后选择来模拟 LME 的 1 膨胀数字方案。通过将量子轨迹固有的随机性与量子测量的概率结果严格匹配，我们的方法有效地将经典建立的量子跳跃算法转化为可执行的量子电路。我们的方法的一个关键优点是它克服了一些现有的后选择相关方案中出现的成功概率的指数抑制，特别是对于长期演化或具有大量跳跃算子的系统。此外，该方案可以扩展到具有后选择的非线性 LME 的 2 扩张框架，从而桥接完整的 LME 和非厄米哈密顿动力学。这种扩展方案提供了一种数字方法，用于探索受监控的量子动力学框架内的非厄米哈密顿量和耗散项之间的相互作用。
