## Abstract
arXiv:2506.02202v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Anisotropy of kinetic coefficients in presence of a magnetic field is represented by Hall currents, which appear in a collisional medium due to action of the Lorentz force on the charged particles between collisions. We derive equations, describing dynamics of the magnetic field in presence of thermo-diffusion with Hall currents, using a standard electrodynamic consideration. The influence of the Hall currents, at presence of thermo-diffusion, on the magnetic field structure is considered in simple models. The equation is derived, which includes additional term for a seed magnetic field creation in the non-magnetized plasma, due to thermo-diffusion. This equation describes the action of the seed magnetic field creation by the mechanism known as "Biermann battery".

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.02202v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：磁场存在下的动力学系数的各向异性由霍尔电流表示，霍尔电流由于碰撞之间洛伦兹力对带电粒子的作用而出现在碰撞介质中。我们使用标准电动力学考虑推导出方程，描述存在霍尔电流热扩散情况下的磁场动力学。在简单模型中考虑了存在热扩散时霍尔电流对磁场结构的影响。推导了该方程，其中包括由于热扩散而在非磁化等离子体中产生种子磁场的附加项。该方程描述了通过称为“比尔曼电池”的机制产生种子磁场的作用。
