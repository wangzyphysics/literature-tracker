## Abstract
arXiv:2601.18895v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: People often express opinions that differ from their privately held views, a phenomenon known in economy as preference falsification. Expressed-private opinion (EPO) models capture this by assigning each agent two dynamical variables: a private (internal) and an expressed (external) opinion. Within the nonlinear $q$-voter model, two EPO variants have been studied so far: with and without self-anticonformity. In both formulations, agents update private and expressed binary opinions, one after another and at the same rate, which has led to two update schemes studied previously: AT (act then think), in which an agent first updates its expressed and then its private opinion, and TA (think then act), in which the order is reversed. To eliminate this ad hoc distinction and quantify the interplay between private and expressed opinions, we introduce the $\alpha$-EPO $q$-voter model with asynchronous updating - in each elementary step, an agent updates its private opinion with probability $\alpha$ or its expressed opinion with complementary probability $1-\alpha$. We derive mean-field theory and, for the first time for EPO $q$-voter dynamics, a pair approximation, and validate them with Monte Carlo simulations on artificial and real organizational networks. Comparing the two model variants, we show that the collective outcome controlled by $\alpha$ strongly depends on self-anticonformity: with self-anticonformity the results are robust to $\alpha$, whereas without it $\alpha$ shifts the agreement-disagreement threshold and, for $q=3$, changes the phase transition from continuous to discontinuous. Based on these results, we briefly discuss implications for real societies that may be relevant to managers and policymakers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.18895v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：人们经常表达与其私下持有的观点不同的观点，这种现象在经济学中被称为偏好伪造。表达私人意见（EPO）模型通过为每个代理分配两个动态变量来捕捉这一点：私人（内部）和表达（外部）意见。在非线性 $q$ 选民模型中，迄今为止已经研究了两种 EPO 变体：有和没有自我反从众。在这两种表述中，代理人以相同的速度依次更新私人意见和表达的二元意见，这导致了先前研究的两种更新方案：AT（行动然后思考），其中代理人首先更新其表达的意见，然后更新其私人意见，以及TA（思考然后行动），其中顺序相反。为了消除这种临时区别并量化私人意见和表达意见之间的相互作用，我们引入了异步更新的 $\alpha$-EPO $q$-投票者模型 - 在每个基本步骤中，代理以概率 $\alpha$ 更新其私人意见，或以补充概率 $1-\alpha$ 更新其表达意见。我们推导了平均场理论，并首次针对 EPO $q$-选民动态推导了一对近似，并通过人工和真实组织网络上的蒙特卡罗模拟对其进行了验证。比较这两个模型变体，我们表明由 $\alpha$ 控制的集体结果强烈依赖于自我反整合：具有自我反整合，结果对 $\alpha$ 是稳健的，而没有它，$\alpha$ 会改变一致-不一致阈值，并且对于 $q=3$，将相变从连续变为不连续。基于这些结果，我们简要讨论了可能与管理者和政策制定者相关的对现实社会的影响。
