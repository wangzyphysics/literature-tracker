## Abstract
Machine-learning force fields (MLFFs) are reshaping computational chemistry and biology by providing near–quantum mechanical accuracy at a computational cost comparable to conventional force fields, enabling applications in biomolecular simulation, catalysis, and materials science. However, toward broader application of MLFFs, a unified and automated computational platform remains lacking. Here, we develop MAPLE, a program specifically designed for MLFF-based molecular modeling, featuring a tailored framework and parallelized algorithms. In this study, we demonstrate the robustness and usability of MAPLE through systematic benchmarking of modern reactive MLFFs and applications to multiple biocatalytic scenarios, highlighting its capability for fast yet accurate simulation of catalytic reactions. By leveraging the accuracy and efficiency of MLFFs together with a highly optimized and flexible software framework, MAPLE serves as a next-generation, physically informed, machine-learning–driven molecular modeling platform with broad applicability to rational catalyst design and drug discovery.

## 摘要（中文）
机器学习力场 (MLFF) 正在重塑计算化学和生物学，它以与传统力场相当的计算成本提供近量子机械精度，从而实现生物分子模拟、催化和材料科学中的应用。然而，为了更广泛地应用 MLFF，仍然缺乏统一和自动化的计算平台。在这里，我们开发了 MAPLE，这是一个专门为基于 MLFF 的分子建模而设计的程序，具有定制的框架和并行算法。在这项研究中，我们通过对现代反应性 MLFF 的系统基准测试以及在多种生物催化场景中的应用，展示了 MAPLE 的稳健性和可用性，强调了其快速而准确地模拟催化反应的能力。通过利用 MLFF 的准确性和效率以及高度优化和灵活的软件框架，MAPLE 作为下一代、物理信息、机器学习驱动的分子建模平台，广泛适用于合理的催化剂设计和药物发现。
