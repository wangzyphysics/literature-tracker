## Abstract
arXiv:2507.09166v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The coarse spatial resolution of gridded climate models, such as general circulation models, limits their direct use in projecting socially relevant variables like extreme precipitation. Most downscaling methods estimate the conditional distributions of extremes by generating large ensembles, complicating the assessment of robustness under distributional transformations, such as those induced by climate change. To better understand and potentially improve robustness, we propose super-resolving the parameters of the target variable's probability distribution directly using analytically tractable mappings. Within a perfect-model framework over Switzerland, we demonstrate that vector generalized linear and additive models can super-resolve the generalized extreme value distribution of summer hourly precipitation extremes from coarse precipitation fields and topography. We introduce the notion of a "robustness gap", defined as the difference in predictive error between present-trained and future-trained models, and use it to diagnose how model structure affects the generalization of each quantile to a pseudo-global warming scenario. By evaluating multiple model configurations, we also identify an upper limit on the super-resolution factor based on the spatial auto- and cross-correlation of precipitation and elevation, beyond which coarse precipitation loses predictive value. Our framework is broadly applicable to variables governed by parametric distributions and offers a model-agnostic diagnostic for understanding when and why empirical downscaling generalizes to climate change and extremes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.09166v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：网格气候模型（例如大气环流模型）的粗略空间分辨率限制了它们在预测极端降水等社会相关变量中的直接使用。大多数降尺度方法通过生成大型集合来估计极端值的条件分布，这使得分布变换（例如气候变化引起的变换）下的稳健性评估变得复杂。为了更好地理解并潜在地提高鲁棒性，我们建议直接使用分析上易于处理的映射来超解析目标变量概率分布的参数。在瑞士的完美模型框架内，我们证明矢量广义线性和加性模型可以超解析来自粗降水场和地形的夏季小时降水极值的广义极值分布。我们引入了“鲁棒性差距”的概念，定义为当前训练模型和未来训练模型之间的预测误差差异，并用它来诊断模型结构如何影响每个分位数对伪全球变暖情景的泛化。通过评估多个模型配置，我们还根据降水和海拔的空间自相关和互相关确定了超分辨率因子的上限，超过该上限，粗降水就会失去预测价值。我们的框架广泛适用于参数分布控制的变量，并提供与模型无关的诊断，以了解经验降尺度何时以及为何适用于气候变化和极端事件。
