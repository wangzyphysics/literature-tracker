## Abstract
Electrochemical nitrate reduction to ammonia (NO₃RR → NH₃) suffers from poor selectivity and strong competition from hydrogen evolution. Here we show that materials-intrinsic electrostatic reprogramming of colloidal copper nitride (Cu₃N) nanocrystals provides a robust lever to bias NO₃RR. Two routes are established in a single electrolyte (0.50 M KNO₃ + 0.10 M KOH): (i) dilute Na incorporation that preserves the anti-ReO₃ (Pm-3m) lattice with small peak shifts, and (ii) Li treatment that amorphizes Cu₃N, yielding short-range–ordered Li–Cu–N. Across both families, cyclic voltammetry, LSV/Tafel analysis, and impedance spectroscopy reveal earlier cathodic onset (≈60–120 mV), reduced charge-transfer resistance (R_ct down to ~16 Ω from ~79 Ω for pristine), and favorable potential-of-zero-charge (PZC) shifts within the operating window. Chronoamperometry with indophenol quantification gives Faradaic efficiencies up to 74% and NH₃ production rates up to 8.8 × 10⁻³ mmol h⁻¹ mg⁻¹ (10% Li–Cu–N), with Tafel slopes of 120–167 mV dec⁻¹, consistent with a first electron-transfer step as rate determining. Electrolyte-cation spike and rinse–swap controls decouple double-layer effects from materials changes: gains on undoped Cu₃N vanish after rinsing, whereas Na-modified and Li-amorphous electrodes retain performance. The results delineate two complementary design paradigms lattice-level Na incorporation and controlled Li-induced amorphization that embed a beneficial interfacial field and accelerate NO₃RR without reactor or electrolyte redesign.

## 摘要（中文）
电化学硝酸盐还原成氨（NO₃RR → NH₃）的选择性较差，且与析氢竞争激烈。在这里，我们证明胶体氮化铜 (Cu₃N) 纳米晶体的材料固有静电重编程提供了偏置 NO₃RR 的强大杠杆。在单一电解质 (0.50 M KNO₃ + 0.10 M KOH) 中建立了两条途径：(i) 稀释 Na 掺入，保留具有小峰移的反 ReO₃ (Pm-3m) 晶格；(ii) Li 处理，使 Cu₃N 非晶化，产生短程有序的 Li-Cu-N。在这两个系列中，循环伏安法、LSV/Tafel 分析和阻抗谱揭示了较早的阴极起始（约 60–120 mV）、降低的电荷转移电阻（R_ct 从原始的约 79 Ω 降至约 16 Ω）以及操作窗口内有利的零电荷电位 (PZC) 变化。采用靛酚定量的计时安培法使法拉第效率高达 74%，NH₃ 生成速率高达 8.8 × 10⁻3 mmol h⁻1 mg⁻1 (10% Li–Cu–N)，塔菲尔斜率为 120–167 mV dec⁻1，与作为速率确定的第一个电子转移步骤一致。电解质阳离子尖峰和冲洗交换控制将双层效应与材料变化解耦：未掺杂的 Cu₃N 的增益在冲洗后消失，而钠改性和锂非晶电极保持性能。结果描绘了两种互补的设计范式：晶格级 Na 掺入和受控 Li 诱导的非晶化，嵌入有益的界面场并加速 NO₃RR，而无需重新设计反应器或电解质。
