## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22399v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming domains from healthcare and agriculture to finance and industry. As progress on Earth meets growing constraints, the next frontier is outer space, where AI can enable autonomous, resilient operations under extreme uncertainty and limited human oversight. This paper introduces Space AI as a unified interdisciplinary field at the intersection of artificial intelligence and space science and technology. We consolidate historical developments and contemporary progress, and propose a systematic framework that organises Space AI into four mission contexts: 1 AI on Earth, covering intelligent mission planning, spacecraft design optimisation, simulation, and ground-based data analytics; 2 AI in Orbit, focusing on satellite and station autonomy, space robotics, on-board/near-real-time data processing, communication optimisation, and orbital safety; (3) AI in Deep Space, enabling autonomous navigation, adaptive scientific discovery, resource mapping, and long-duration human-AI collaboration under communication constraints; and 4 AI for Multi-Planetary Life, supporting in-situ resource utilisation, habitat and infrastructure construction, life-support and ecological management, and resilient interplanetary networks. Ultimately, Space AI can accelerate humanity's capability to explore and operate in space, while translating advances in sensing, robotics, optimisation, and trustworthy AI into broad societal impact on Earth.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22399v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：人工智能 (AI) 正在将领域从医疗保健和农业转变为金融和工业。随着地球上的进步遇到越来越多的限制，下一个前沿是外太空，人工智能可以在极端不确定性和有限的人类监督下实现自主、有弹性的操作。本文介绍了空间人工智能作为人工智能与空间科学技术交叉的统一的跨学科领域。我们整合历史发展和当代进步，提出一个系统框架，将太空人工智能组织成四个任务背景： 1 地球上的人工智能，涵盖智能任务规划、航天器设计优化、模拟和地面数据分析； 2 轨道人工智能，重点关注卫星和空间站自主、空间机器人、星载/近实时数据处理、通信优化和轨道安全； (3) 深空人工智能，实现自主导航、自适应科学发现、资源测绘以及通信限制下的长期人机协作； 4 人工智能促进多行星生命，支持原地资源利用、栖息地和基础设施建设、生命支持和生态管理以及弹性星际网络。最终，太空人工智能可以加速人类在太空探索和操作的能力，同时将传感、机器人、优化和值得信赖的人工智能方面的进步转化为对地球的广泛社会影响。
