## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07875v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A phenomenological description of the Stern--Gerlach experiment yields a mathematical structure equivalent to that of a spin-1/2 particle, described by an irreducible unitary representation of the Poincar\'e group. In the corresponding irreducible two-particle representation, two-particle states have the form of an integral over product states. They describe a correlation between the particles with the structure of the electromagnetic interaction and a coupling constant that numerically equals the electromagnetic coupling constant. This coupling constant is essentially the normalisation factor of these two-particle states. The Standard Model of particle physics describes the electromagnetic interaction by a perturbation algorithm, where the experimental value of the electromagnetic coupling constant is inserted by hand. It is argued that it does not make sense to insert a normalisation factor without checking the range of integration of the corresponding integral and adjusting it if necessary. This adjustment provides the perturbation algorithm with the mathematically consistent structure of a non-local, relativistic, two-particle quantum mechanics. Similarly, multi-particle representations determine a gravitational interaction that, in the quasi-classical limit, is described by the field equations of conformal gravity. A calculated, galaxy-specific value of the gravitational constant matches the experimental value.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07875v1 公告类型：新 摘要：斯特恩-格拉赫实验的现象学描述产生了与自旋 1/2 粒子等效的数学结构，由庞加莱群的不可约酉表示来描述。在相应的不可约二粒子表示中，二粒子态具有乘积态积分的形式。它们描述了粒子与电磁相互作用结构和在数值上等于电磁耦合常数的耦合常数之间的相关性。该耦合常数本质上是这些双粒子态的归一化因子。粒子物理的标准模型通过微扰算法描述电磁相互作用，其中电磁耦合常数的实验值是手动插入的。有人认为，在不检查相应积分的积分范围并在必要时进行调整的情况下插入归一化因子是没有意义的。这种调整为微扰算法提供了非局域、相对论、双粒子量子力学的数学一致结构。类似地，多粒子表示确定引力相互作用，在准经典极限下，由共形引力场方程描述。计算出的星系特定引力常数值与实验值相符。
