## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11526v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Amontons' law postulates a monotonic relationship between frictional force and the normal load applied to a sliding contact. This empirical rule, however, fails in systems where internal degrees of freedom - such as structural or electronic order - play a central role. Here, we demonstrate that friction can emerge entirely from magnetically driven configurational dynamics in the absence of physical contact. Using a two-dimensional array of rotatable magnetic dipoles sliding over a commensurate magnetic substrate, we observe a pronounced non-monotonic dependence of friction on the interlayer separation, and thus on the effective load. The friction peaks at an intermediate distance where competing ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic interactions induce dynamical frustration and hysteretic torque cycles during sliding. Molecular dynamics simulations and a simplified two-sublattice model confirm that energy dissipation is governed by collective magnetic reorientations and their hysteresis. Our results establish the occurrence of sliding-induced changes in collective magnetic order, which has a strong impact on friction, and thus open new possibilities for contactless friction control, magnetic sensing, and the design of reconfigurable, wear-free frictional interfaces and metamaterials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11526v1 公告类型：新 摘要：阿蒙顿定律假定摩擦力与施加到滑动接触的法向载荷之间存在单调关系。然而，这种经验规则在内部自由度（例如结构或电子秩序）发挥核心作用的系统中失效。在这里，我们证明，在没有物理接触的情况下，摩擦可以完全由磁驱动的构型动力学产生。使用在相应磁性基板上滑动的可旋转磁偶极子的二维阵列，我们观察到摩擦力对层间分离以及有效载荷的显着非单调依赖性。摩擦力在中间距离处达到峰值，其中竞争的铁磁和反铁磁相互作用在滑动过程中引起动态挫败和迟滞扭矩循环。分子动力学模拟和简化的两亚晶格模型证实，能量耗散受集体磁重新取向及其磁滞的控制。我们的研究结果证实了滑动引起的集体磁序变化的发生，这对摩擦有很大影响，从而为非接触式摩擦控制、磁传感以及可重构、无磨损摩擦界面和超材料的设计开辟了新的可能性。
