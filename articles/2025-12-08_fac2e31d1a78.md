## Abstract
Author(s): Darren Pereira and Erich J. Mueller The ground state of the hard-core Fermi-Hubbard model with a single hole (and otherwise half-filled) is a ferromagnet on the square lattice, but an antiferromagnet on the triangular lattice, as those spin patterns minimize the kinetic energy of the hole. What is the magnetic behavior as one interpolates between these two geometries? Using a sophisticated quantum Monte Carlo algorithm, the authors determine here the magnetic correlations as the lattice is varied. Their calculations are performed in the thermodynamic limit and at temperatures comparable to current cold-atom experiments. They find a magnetic crossover between the two geometries, and calculate signatures of it that are observable in these experiments. [Phys. Rev. B 112, 245120] Published Mon Dec 08, 2025

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Darren Pereira 和 Erich J. Mueller 具有单孔（或半填充）的硬核费米-哈伯德模型的基态是方形晶格上的铁磁体，但三角形晶格上的反铁磁体，因为这些自旋模式使空穴的动能最小化。当在这两种几何形状之间进行插值时，磁性行为是什么？作者使用复杂的量子蒙特卡罗算法，确定了晶格变化时的磁相关性。他们的计算是在热力学极限和与当前冷原子实验相当的温度下进行的。他们发现了两种几何形状之间的磁交叉，并计算了在这些实验中可观察到的特征。 [物理。修订版 B 112, 245120] 于 2025 年 12 月 8 日星期一发布
