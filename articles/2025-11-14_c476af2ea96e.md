## Abstract
Author(s): Carmel Dansou, Charles Paillard, and Laurent Bellaiche We present a comprehensive investigation of light-induced phase transitions and strain in two-dimensional $\mathrm{NbO}{X}_{2}$ ($X=\mathrm{Cl}$, Br, I) using first-principles calculations. In particular, we identify a light-induced ferroelectric-to-paraelectric phase transition in these 2D systems.… [Phys. Rev. Materials 9, 114001] Published Fri Nov 14, 2025
