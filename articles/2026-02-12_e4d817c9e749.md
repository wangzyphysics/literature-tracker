# Uncovering the excited-state intramolecular proton transfer and triplet formation pathways of 3-methyl-6-hydroxy-m-phthalic acid

## Abstract
Three dominant photorelaxation pathways—internal conversion, intramolecular proton transfer, and triplet formation—originating from the S 1 state of 3-methyl-6-hydroxy-m-phthalic acid are investigated theoretically. Potential energy profiles and spin–orbit coupling parameters associated with the six low-lying excited electronic states (S 1 , S 2 , T 1 , T 2 , T 3 , and T 4 ) are analyzed to elucidate the mechanistic details of these photorelaxation pathways. Full-dimensional quantum wavepacket simulations indicate that the wavepacket initiated on S 1 would move through the S 1 –S 2 conical intersections and accumulate in the S 2 state. This nonadiabatic population transfer feature, combined with small barrier energies on S 1 and S 2 , allows the molecule to display proton transfer from both states. The efficient S 1 –T 3 intersystem crossing is the third event, where the rate of this event increases with the elongation of the proton transfer coordinate (O–H distance). Vibronic coupling in the triplet manifold enables an ultrafast T 3 to T 1 decay. In addition, the proton transfer and triplet formation features are studied in nonpolar and aprotic polar solvents to interpret the experimental findings.

## 摘要（中文）
从理论上研究了源自 3-甲基-6-羟基-间苯二甲酸 S 1 态的三种主要光弛豫途径——内部转换、分子内质子转移和三线态形成。分析与六个低位激发电子态（S 1 、S 2 、T 1 、T 2 、T 3 和 T 4 ）相关的势能分布和自旋轨道耦合参数，以阐明这些光弛豫路径的机制细节。全维量子波包模拟表明，在S 1 上发起的波包将穿过S 1 –S 2 圆锥形交叉点并在S 2 状态下累积。这种非绝热布居转移特征与 S 1 和 S 2 上的小势垒能量相结合，使分子能够显示来自两种状态的质子转移。有效的S 1 –T 3 系间穿越是第三个事件，该事件的发生率随着质子转移坐标（O-H距离）的拉长而增加。三重态流形中的电子振动耦合可实现超快的 T 3 到 T 1 衰减。此外，还研究了非极性和非质子极性溶剂中的质子转移和三重态形成特征，以解释实验结果。
