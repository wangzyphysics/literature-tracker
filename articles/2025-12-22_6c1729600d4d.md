# Structural disorder enables fast magnetization dynamics in nanoparticles

## Abstract
Nature Reviews Materials, Published online: 22 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41578-025-00884-1 An article in Journal of the American Chemical Society demonstrates that engineered disorder enables ultrafast magnetization control in structural highentropy FePt nanoparticles.
