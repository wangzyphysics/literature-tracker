## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08365v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: A green transition in aviation requires a drastic upscaling of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF). The power-to-liquid process for the production of CO2-neutral jet fuel via electricity, called e-SAF, directly replaces fossil jet fuel without having to change infrastructure, aeroplanes, or jet-engines. The process combines green hydrogen with industrial exhaust gas, or captured carbon dioxide, in a circular economy concept. A key element of the e-SAF production plant is the reactor where syngas is produced. Traditional reactors use catalytic technology, which faces severe challenges due to the reduced performance over time because of catalyst degradation, clogging, and breakup due to embrittlement. A high-potential alternative is the catalyst-free reverse water-gas-shift (RWGS) reactor concept. The primary aim of this paper is to investigate the fundamental aspects of the catalyst-free RWGS process, such as reaction kinetics and the interactions between turbulence and chemistry. The secondary aim is to identify how a typical combustion subgrid scale models for Large Eddy Simulations (LES) perform when the chemical reactions are endothermic, in contrast to the strong endothermicity associated with classical combustion. It is found that even small traces of O2 in the CO2 stream can significantly increase the production rate of CO. This is attributed to the increased pool of OH. The effect is strongest at atmospheric pressure and less pronounced at higher pressure. By using the temporal jet framework to study turbulence-chemistry interactions, an algebraic equation for the prediction of the CO conversion time in a turbulent flow as a function of Damkohler number and chemical timescale is employed. Finally, it is concluded that the PaSR LES subgrid model designed for combustion reactions perform well also for the endothermic reverse water-gas-shift reaction.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08365v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：航空业的绿色转型需要大幅提升可持续航空燃料 (SAF) 的规模。通过电力生产二氧化碳中性喷气燃料的电转液工艺称为 e-SAF，可直接替代化石喷气燃料，而无需改变基础设施、飞机或喷气发动机。该工艺将绿色氢气与工业废气或捕获的二氧化碳结合在循环经济概念中。 e-SAF 生产工厂的一个关键要素是生产合成气的反应器。传统反应器使用催化技术，随着时间的推移，由于催化剂降解、堵塞和脆化导致性能下降，该技术面临着严峻的挑战。一种高潜力的替代方案是无催化剂反向水煤气变换（RWGS）反应器概念。本文的主要目的是研究无催化剂 RWGS 过程的基本方面，例如反应动力学以及湍流与化学之间的相互作用。第二个目标是确定大涡模拟 (LES) 的典型燃烧亚网格尺度模型在化学反应吸热时的表现，这与经典燃烧相关的强吸热性相反。研究发现，即使 CO2 流中含有极少量的 O2，也能显着提高 CO 的生成速率。这归因于 OH 池的增加。该效应在大气压下最强，而在较高压力下则不太明显。通过使用时间射流框架来研究湍流-化学相互作用，采用代数方程来预测湍流中 CO 转化时间作为 Damkohler 数和化学时间尺度的函数。最后得出的结论是，为燃烧反应设计的 PaSR LES 子网格模型对于吸热逆水煤气变换反应也表现良好。
