## Abstract
Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) are powerful tools for molecular property prediction, but they are not magic. When applied to molecules unlike their training data, they produce unreliable predictions that are difficult to detect. The Applicability Domain (AD) concept addresses this by defining regions of chemical space where predictions should be trusted. We benchmark embedding-based AD methods across three GNN architectures (GCN, GAT, MPNN) and nine MoleculeNet datasets. Our hybrid AD score combines density estimation (KDE), distance metrics (kNN, Mahalanobis), and prediction uncertainty (entropy, margin). An automated thresh- old optimization selects cutoffs that balance accuracy gains against sample retention. Benchmark results confirm the utility of the framework. Restricting predictions to the In-Domain subset improved AUC up to 0.96 (BACE-GCN) and reduced RMSE to 0.66 (Lipo-GAT), while Out- Domain samples showed 2-3× higher error rates. Coverage ranged from 8% (aggressive filtering) to 99% (conservative), depending on dataset difficulty. Case studies on ESOL and BBBP demonstrate that flagged molecules have interpretable structural features (long aliphatic chains, unusual ring systems, or heteroatom combinations) that correlate with prediction failures.

## 摘要（中文）
图神经网络（GNN）是分子特性预测的强大工具，但它们并不神奇。当应用于与训练数据不同的分子时，它们会产生难以检测的不可靠预测。适用性域 (AD) 概念通过定义应信任预测的化学空间区域来解决此问题。我们在三个 GNN 架构（GCN、GAT、MPNN）和九个 MoleculeNet 数据集上对基于嵌入的 AD 方法进行了基准测试。我们的混合 AD 评分结合了密度估计 (KDE)、距离度量（kNN、马哈拉诺比斯）和预测不确定性（熵、裕度）。自动阈值优化选择可以平衡准确性增益和样本保留的截止值。基准测试结果证实了该框架的实用性。将预测限制为域内子集，将 AUC 提高至 0.96 (BACE-GCN)，并将 RMSE 降低至 0.66 (Lipo-GAT)，而域外样本的错误率提高了 2-3 倍。覆盖范围从 8%（积极过滤）到 99%（保守），具体取决于数据集难度。 ESOL 和 BBBP 的案例研究表明，标记的分子具有可解释的结构特征（长脂肪链、不寻常的环系统或杂原子组合），这些特征与预测失败相关。
