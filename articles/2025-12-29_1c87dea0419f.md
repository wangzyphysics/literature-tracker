## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21939v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic skyrmions exhibit particle-like stability and rich dynamical behaviour arising from their topological nature, making them promising building blocks for future spintronic devices. In this work, we investigate the interaction dynamics of two Neel-type skyrmions in an ultrathin ferromagnetic film through a combined analytical and numerical approach. Full micromagnetic simulations reveal that when the initial separation exceeds twice the skyrmion radius, the pair undergoes a repulsive interaction leading to an outward spiral trajectory whose radial and angular components depend sensitively on the Gilbert damping, consistent with particle-like models of skyrmion motion. The simulations further show a two-component oscillatory behaviour in the out-of-plane magnetization: a fast intrinsic breathing mode superimposed on a slower interaction-induced modulation. Using Thiele collective-coordinate model, an expression for the radial drift, angular decay, and logarithmic growth of the separation distance is derived. The analytical predictions show excellent agreement with numerical results, confirming the exponential form of the long-range interaction potential. A continuum micromagnetic analysis of the skyrmion tail explains the origin of the exponential decay and its role in governing the interaction strength. Together, these findings provide a unified framework for understanding spiral motion, breathing dynamics, and long-range repulsion in interacting skyrmion systems, offering insights relevant for multi-skyrmion information carriers and collective skyrmion-based devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21939v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁性斯格明子表现出粒子般的稳定性和因其拓扑性质而产生的丰富的动力学行为，使它们成为未来自旋电子器件的有前景的构建模块。在这项工作中，我们通过分析和数值相结合的方法研究了超薄铁磁薄膜中两个尼尔型斯格明子的相互作用动力学。完整的微磁模拟表明，当初始分离超过斯格明子半径的两倍时，该对会经历排斥相互作用，导致向外的螺旋轨迹，其径向和角度分量敏感地取决于吉尔伯特阻尼，与斯格明子运动的类粒子模型一致。模拟进一步显示了平面外磁化中的两分量振荡行为：快速的固有呼吸模式叠加在较慢的相互作用引起的调制上。使用蒂勒集体坐标模型，导出了径向漂移、角衰减和间隔距离对数增长的表达式。分析预测与数值结果非常吻合，证实了长程相互作用势的指数形式。对斯格明子尾部的连续微磁分析解释了指数衰变的起源及其在控制相互作用强度中的作用。总之，这些发现为理解相互作用的斯格明子系统中的螺旋运动、呼吸动力学和远程排斥提供了一个统一的框架，提供了与多斯格明子信息载体和基于集体斯格明子的设备相关的见解。
