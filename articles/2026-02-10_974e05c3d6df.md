## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07531v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Cooling the center-of-mass (CM) motion of a macroscopic oscillator to its quantum ground state is a fundamental prerequisite for testing quantum mechanics at macroscopic scales. However, achieving this goal is currently hindered by the stringent requirement for an ultrahigh mechanical quality factor ($Q_c$). Here, we propose a dual-channel cooling scheme based on squeezing-enhanced quantum interference within a hybrid levitated cavity-magnomechanical system to overcome this limitation. By synergizing squeezing effects with quantum interference between the magnon-CM and cavity-CM channels, our scheme simultaneously suppresses Stokes (heating) scattering while enhancing anti-Stokes (cooling) scattering.~We demonstrate that this cooling mechanism reduces the critical $Q_c$ required for ground-state cooling by three orders of magnitude, making it achievable in the experimentally accessible regime of $Q_c \sim 10^4$. Furthermore, the net cooling rate is enhanced by nearly 180-fold compared to that of conventional single-channel cooling. This improvement is accompanied by a two orders of magnitude reduction in both the steady-state CM occupancy and the cooling time. Importantly, this enhanced performance remains robust even deep within the unresolved-sideband regime. Our results provide a feasible path toward preparing macroscopic quantum states by actively controlling the cooling dynamics, thereby relaxing the constraints on intrinsic material properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07531v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：将宏观振荡器的质心 (CM) 运动冷却至其量子基态是在宏观尺度上测试量子力学的基本先决条件。然而，目前对超高机械品质因数 ($Q_c$) 的严格要求阻碍了这一目标的实现。在这里，我们提出了一种基于混合悬浮腔磁力机械系统内挤压增强量子干涉的双通道冷却方案，以克服这一限制。通过协同挤压效应与磁振子-CM 和腔-CM 通道之间的量子干涉，我们的方案同时抑制斯托克斯（加热）散射，同时增强反斯托克斯（冷却）散射。〜我们证明，这种冷却机制将基态冷却所需的临界 $Q_c$ 降低了三个数量级，使其可以在 $Q_c \sim 10^4$ 的实验可访问范围内实现。此外，与传统单通道冷却相比，净冷却速率提高了近180倍。这一改进伴随着稳态 CM 占用率和冷却时间减少两个数量级。重要的是，即使在未解析的边带区域深处，这种增强的性能仍然保持强劲。我们的研究结果为通过主动控制冷却动力学来制备宏观量子态提供了一条可行的途径，从而放宽了对固有材料性能的限制。
