## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11645v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The recent identification of $\alpha$-MnTe as a candidate altermagnet has attracted considerable interest, particularly for its potential application in magnetic random-access memory. However, the development of high-quality thin films - essential for practical implementation - has remained limited. Here, we report the epitaxial growth of centimeter-scale $\alpha$-MnTe thin films on InP(111) substrates via molecular beam epitaxy (MBE). Through X-ray diffraction (XRD) analysis, we construct a MnTe phase diagram that provides clear guidance for stabilizing the pure $\alpha$-MnTe phase, revealing that it is favored under high Te/Mn flux ratios and elevated growth temperatures. Cross-sectional electron microscopy confirms an atomically sharp film-substrate interface, consistent with a layer-by-layer epitaxial growth mode. Remarkably, these high-quality $\alpha$-MnTe films exhibit a pronounced anomalous Hall effect (AHE) originating from Berry curvature, despite a net magnetic moment approaching zero - a signature of robust altermagnetic character. Our work establishes a viable route for synthesizing wafer-scale $\alpha$-MnTe thin films and highlights their promise for altermagnet-based spintronics and magnetic sensing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11645v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近确定 $\alpha$-MnTe 作为候选交替磁体引起了相当大的兴趣，特别是其在磁性随机存取存储器中的潜在应用。然而，对于实际实施至关重要的高质量薄膜的开发仍然有限。在这里，我们报告了通过分子束外延（MBE）在 InP(111) 衬底上外延生长厘米级 $\alpha$-MnTe 薄膜。通过 X 射线衍射 (XRD) 分析，我们构建了 MnTe 相图，为稳定纯 $\alpha$-MnTe 相提供了明确的指导，表明它在高 Te/Mn 通量比和升高的生长温度下受到青睐。横截面电子显微镜证实了原子级清晰的薄膜-基底界面，与逐层外延生长模式一致。值得注意的是，这些高质量的 $\alpha$-MnTe 薄膜表现出明显的源自贝里曲率的反常霍尔效应 (AHE)，尽管净磁矩接近于零 - 这是强大的交变磁特性的标志。我们的工作建立了一条合成晶圆级 $\alpha$-MnTe 薄膜的可行途径，并强调了它们在基于交流磁体的自旋电子学和磁传感方面的前景。
