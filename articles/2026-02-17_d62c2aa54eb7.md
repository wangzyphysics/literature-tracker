## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14548v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Comparison of {\it ab initio} calculations in i.DMFT Method by Di Liu et al. (2025) with benchmark potential curves for HX(Cl,Br) halides shows their inaccuracy in domain around equilibrium and wrong behavior in Van der Waals region of large distances.
