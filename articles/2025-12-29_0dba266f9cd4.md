## Abstract
arXiv:2512.18636v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study the surface density of states in $p$-wave unconventional magnet-spin-singlet $s$-wave superconductor hybrid systems ($p$-wave unconventional magnetic superconductors), by using the effective $p$-wave unconventional magnet model [B. Brekke, et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 133, 236703 (2024)]. Owing to the noncollinear spin structure along the $x$-direction, the quasiparticle energy dispersion has the spin-triplet $p_x$-wave energy gap structure, and then zero-energy flat bands emerge at the [100] edge. Analyzing the pair amplitude at the [100] edge, odd-frequency spin-triplet even-parity pairing is induced in the presence of zero-energy flat bands, while even-frequency spin-singlet even-parity remains. We also demonstrate the Josephson current in superconducting junctions with $p$-wave unconventional magnet-conventional $s$-wave superconductor hybrid systems. By the cooperation of spin-singlet $s$-wave pair potential and the $p$-wave unconventional magnetic order, the current phase relation shows the $\varphi$-junction in the high-transparency but also the temperature dependence of the Josephson current caused by the coupling of the spin-singlet even-parity pairings in the low-transparency limit, even though $p$-wave superconductivity is mainly realized both in the bulk and at the edge. Our calculations provide the possible superconducting phenomena and transport properties in $p$-wave unconventional magnet-$s$-wave superconductor hybrid systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.18636v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们通过使用有效的$p$波非常规磁体模型[B.布雷克等人，物理学。莱特牧师。 133, 236703 (2024)]。由于沿$x$方向的非共线自旋结构，准粒子能量色散具有自旋三重态$p_x$波能隙结构，然后在[100]边缘出现零能量平带。分析[100]边缘处的对振幅，在零能量平带存在的情况下引发奇数频率自旋三重态偶宇称配对，而偶数频率自旋单重态偶宇称仍然存在。我们还通过$p$波非常规磁体-传统$s$波超导混合系统演示了超导结中的约瑟夫森电流。通过自旋单线$s$波对势和$p$波非常规磁序的配合，电流相位关系显示了高透明度下的$\varphi$结，而且还显示了低透明度极限下自旋单线偶宇称对耦合引起的约瑟夫森电流的温度依赖性，尽管$p$波超导主要在体部和边缘实现。我们的计算提供了$p$波非常规磁体-$s$波超导混合系统中可能的超导现象和输运特性。
