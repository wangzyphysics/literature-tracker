## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21949v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The enormous multiplexity of the WST opens up the possibility to trigger alerts for variable objects - an option that has been reserved so far only for imaging surveys. WST can go further by detecting spectroscopic line profile and line strength variations. I review previous alert-issuing surveys that are limited to imaging, and describe some of the new research possibilities that this feature of the data flow system (DFS) would open up. The latter range from variability of emission line stars, such as Bes, WRs and LBVs to variability of active galaxies and quasars, including the so-called changing look objects that shift between Type 1 and Type 2. Furthermore, I describe the requirements that the WST DFS must meet to make this feasible. The most critical aspect is the rapid data processing for timely follow-up. Next, the alert system is tightly connected with the data reduction and archive, because it will need an extensive and continuously updated spectral reference database. The new spectra will have to be compared against these reference spectra to identify variations. The reference spectra can either be "native" from the WST itself or they can originate from other spectroscopic surveys. Two options for the DFS are considered: one is to conduct an automated search of the WST's own archive, and potentially of other spectroscopic archives and a second option is to allow the users to submit reference spectra on their own. The spectroscopic alert system will open up a completely new discovery space that is not accessible to the existing or planned near-future surveys. Finally, I discuss the advantages of moving the variability detection to physical parameters by modeling the observed and reference spectra and comparing the derived fitting parameters. This strategy offers a robust method for alert ranking.

arXiv:2512.21949v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：WST 的巨大多重性开启了触发可变对象警报的可能性 - 迄今为止仅为成像调查保留的选项。 WST 可以通过检测光谱线轮廓和线强度变化来更进一步。我回顾了之前仅限于成像的警报发布调查，并描述了数据流系统 (DFS) 的这一功能将带来的一些新的研究可能性。后者的范围从发射线恒星的可变性（例如 Bes、WR 和 LBV）到活动星系和类星体的可变性，包括在类型 1 和类型 2 之间转换的所谓的外观变化天体。此外，我还描述了 WST DFS 必须满足的要求才能使之可行。最关键的是快速数据处理以便及时跟进。其次，警报系统与数据缩减和归档紧密相连，因为它将需要一个广泛且持续更新的光谱参考数据库。新光谱必须与这些参考光谱进行比较，以识别变化。参考光谱可以是来自 WST 本身的“原生”光谱，也可以源自其他光谱测量。考虑 DFS 的两种选择：一种是对 WST 自己的档案进行自动搜索，也可能对其他光谱档案进行自动搜索，第二种选择是允许用户自行提交参考光谱。光谱警报系统将开辟一个全新的发现空间，这是现有或计划的近期调查无法进入的。最后，我通过对观测光谱和参考光谱进行建模并比较导出的拟合参数，讨论了将变异性检测转移到物理参数的优点。该策略提供了一种稳健的警报排名方法。
