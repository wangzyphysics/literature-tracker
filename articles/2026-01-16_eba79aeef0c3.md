## Abstract
arXiv:2512.03657v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In recent years, the concept of multipoles has been widely used to describe and classify various magnetic and electric responses in solids, providing a systematic way to identify symmetry-allowed or -forbidden physical responses. Conventionally, multipole classifications rely on the magnetic point group of a system, which inherently incorporates the effects of relativistic spin-orbit coupling because the spin orientation is supposed to follow the point-group transformation of the lattice. However, this approach becomes insufficient in situations where relativistic spin-orbit coupling is negligibly weak or where the spin and orbital (lattice) degrees of freedom are decoupled, thereby requiring a more comprehensive symmetry description. In this work, we introduce a multipole description on the basis of the spin-point-group symmetries, enabling a systematic exploration of nonrelativistic phenomena that persist even without spin-orbit coupling in a collinear antiferromagnet. As an application, we theoretically demonstrate spin-current generation driven by elastic waves in a specific collinear antiferromagnet, fully independent of spin-orbit coupling.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.03657v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：近年来，多极子的概念已被广泛用于描述和分类固体中的各种磁电响应，为识别对称性允许或禁止的物理响应提供了系统的方法。传统上，多极分类依赖于系统的磁点群，该系统本质上包含了相对论自旋轨道耦合的效应，因为自旋方向应该遵循晶格的点群变换。然而，在相对论自旋轨道耦合很弱或者自旋和轨道（晶格）自由度解耦的情况下，这种方法就变得不够了，从而需要更全面的对称性描述。在这项工作中，我们引入了基于自旋点群对称性的多极描述，从而能够系统地探索即使在共线反铁磁体中没有自旋轨道耦合的情况下仍然存在的非相对论现象。作为一个应用，我们从理论上证明了在特定的共线反铁磁体中由弹性波驱动的自旋电流的产生，完全独立于自旋轨道耦合。
