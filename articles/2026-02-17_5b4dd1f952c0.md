## Abstract
arXiv:2506.10786v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: N\'eel vector, the order parameter of collinear antiferromagnets, serves as a state variable in associated antiferromagnetic (AFM) spintronic devices to encode information. A deterministic switching of N\'eel vector is crucial for the write-in operation, which, however, remains a challenging problem in AFM spintronics. Here we demonstrate, based on analytical derivation and macro-spin simulations, that N\'eel vector switching can be generally achieved via a current-induced spin torque, provided the spin accumulations responsible for this torque are non-identical between opposite sublattices. This condition occurs widely in AFM films, as symmetry equivalence between sublattice-dependent spin accumulations is usually absent, allowing unequal spin accumulations induced by Edelstein effect or a spin current. Unlike previously studied spin torques induced by uniform or staggered spin accumulations -- where either the field-like or damping-like component dominates exclusively -- the asymmetric spin torque features cooperative contributions from both components, leading to N\'eel vector dynamics that are fundamentally distinct from previous expectations. Crucially, the static states stabilized by the asymmetric spin torque enable versatile N\'eel vector switching strategies -- field-free spin-transfer torque switching during current application, as well as field-free or field-assisted spin-orbit torque switching after the current pulse -- demonstrating that established spin torque techniques from ferromagnetic spintronics can be directly adapted to AFM systems, a capability absent in previous theoretical frameworks. Our work establishes a general mechanism for current-induced N\'eel vector switching, which is in principle feasible for all collinear antiferromagnets, and thus paves the route to realize efficient writing in AFM spintronics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.10786v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：N\'eel 矢量是共线反铁磁体的有序参数，在相关反铁磁 (AFM) 自旋电子器件中作为状态变量来编码信息。 N\'eel矢量的确定性切换对于写入操作至关重要，然而，这仍然是AFM自旋电子学中的一个具有挑战性的问题。在这里，我们基于分析推导和宏观自旋模拟证明，N'eel矢量切换通常可以通过电流感应自旋扭矩来实现，前提是导致该扭矩的自旋累积在相对的子晶格之间不相同。这种情况在 AFM 薄膜中广泛发生，因为亚晶格相关的自旋积累之间通常不存在对称性等价性，从而允许由埃德尔斯坦效应或自旋电流引起不等的自旋积累。与之前研究的由均匀或交错自旋累积引起的自旋扭矩（其中类场分量或类阻尼分量占主导地位）不同，不对称自旋扭矩的特点是两个分量的协同贡献，导致 N'eel 矢量动力学与之前的预期有根本不同。至关重要的是，由不对称自旋扭矩稳定的静态能够实现多种N'eel矢量切换策略——电流应用期间的无场自旋转移扭矩切换，以及电流脉冲后的无场或场辅助自旋轨道扭矩切换——这表明铁磁自旋电子学中已建立的自旋扭矩技术可以直接适用于AFM系统，这是以前的理论框架中不具备的能力。我们的工作建立了一种电流感应磁极矢量切换的通用机制，原则上对于所有共线反铁磁体都是可行的，从而为实现 AFM 自旋电子学的高效写入铺平了道路。
