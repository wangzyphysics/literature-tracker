## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14031v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We demonstrate a non-volatile magnetoelectric magnonic memory (MEMM) that enables fully electrical write/read via direct magnon-driven sensing in an insulating antiferromagnet. A fabricated SrIrO3/La-BiFeO3/SrIrO3 trilayer exhibits sub-100 ps switching, a remnant polarization of 20 uC/cm2, and a readout voltage contrast close to 1mV between high and low-resistance states. To connect device physics to circuit behavior, we develop and experimentally validate a compact circuit model that captures spin Hall injection and spin transport. Simulations with optimized material parameters predict output voltages > 100mV, enabling cascading without external amplification. Using this framework, we design MEMM-based memory and logic blocks, including a 1T1R array, two inverter implementations (complementary two-device and single-device), and a three-input majority gate, and evaluate deep-pipelined operation. The model projects switching energies down to 1 aJ per operation and logic propagation delays of 30-60 ps, indicating MEMM as a promising platform for energy-constrained, high throughput computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14031v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们展示了一种非易失性磁电磁能存储器 (MEMM)，它可以通过绝缘反铁磁体中的直接磁振子驱动传感实现完全电气写入/读取。制造的 SrIrO3/La-BiFeO3/SrIrO3 三层表现出低于 100 ps 的开关、20 uC/cm2 的残余极化以及高电阻状态和低电阻状态之间接近 1mV 的读出电压对比度。为了将器件物理与电路行为联系起来，我们开发并通过实验验证了一个紧凑的电路模型，该模型捕获自旋霍尔注入和自旋输运。使用优化材料参数进行的仿真预测输出电压 > 100mV，无需外部放大即可实现级联。使用该框架，我们设计了基于 MEMM 的存储器和逻辑块，包括 1T1R 阵列、两个逆变器实现（互补的两个器件和单器件）和一个三输入多数门，并评估深度流水线操作。该模型预计每次操作的开关能量降至 1 aJ，逻辑传播延迟为 30-60 ps，这表明 MEMM 是一个有前景的能量受限、高吞吐量计算平台。
