## Abstract
arXiv:2601.10287v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Quantum bianisotropy and chirality are fundamental concepts in light matter interaction that describe how materials with broken symmetries respond to electromagnetic fields at the level of macroscopic quantum electrodynamics. In quantum bianisotropy, magnetoelectric (ME) energy plays a critical role in mediating and enhancing light matter interactions. This concept is essential for bridging the gap between classical electromagnetics (where bianisotropy often involves field nonlocality) and quantum mechanics in metamaterials. The precise manipulation of a quantum emitter's properties at a subwavelength scale is due to near fields, which effectively function as a tunable environment. We show that the ME near field, interpreted as a structure combining the effect of bianisotropy (chirality) with a quantum atmosphere, is a nonMaxwellian field with spacetime symmetry breaking. Quantum ME fields arise from the dynamic modulation and topological coupling of magnetization and electric polarization within ME meta atoms, specific subwavelength structural elements with magnetic and dielectric subsystems in magnetic insulators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.10287v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：量子双各向异性和手性是光物质相互作用中的基本概念，描述了对称性破缺的材料如何在宏观量子电动力学水平上响应电磁场。在量子双各向异性中，磁电（ME）能量在介导和增强光物质相互作用方面发挥着关键作用。这个概念对于弥合经典电磁学（双各向异性通常涉及场非定域性）和超材料中的量子力学之间的差距至关重要。在亚波长尺度上精确控制量子发射器的特性是由于近场，它有效地充当了可调谐环境。我们证明，ME 近场被解释为双各向异性（手性）效应与量子大气相结合的结构，是一个时空对称性破缺的非麦克斯韦场。量子 ME 场源自 ME 元原子内磁化和电极化的动态调制和拓扑耦合，ME 元原子是具有磁性绝缘体中的磁性和介电子系统的特定亚波长结构元件。
