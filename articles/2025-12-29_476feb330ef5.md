## Abstract
The authors would like to correct one minor error in the published article. 1 The Wang–Frenkel potential formula, as presented in Eq. (3), contained an error attributable to a compilation issue. The corrected formula is as follows:UWF(r)=εWFασr2−1rcr2−12,r≤rc0,r>rc(1)The changes are reflected in this Erratum and do not affect the content and conclusions of this publication.
