## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07654v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Anderson localization of particles -- the complete halt of wave transport through multiple scattering and phase coherence -- is a paradigmatic manifestation of quantum interference in disordered media. In three dimensions, the scaling theory predicts a quantum phase transition at a critical energy, the mobility edge, separating localized from diffusive states and underpinning metal-insulator transitions in electronic systems. Despite decades of experimental efforts, a direct observation of this emblematic transition for matter waves has remained elusive. Previous attempts with ultracold atoms were hindered by strong and uncontrolled energy broadening, resulting in indirect, sometimes inaccurate, and model-dependent estimates of the mobility edge. Here we implement a novel energy-resolved scheme to prepare atomic matter waves with a narrow energy distribution and track their expansion dynamics over long timescales. This allows for a direct observation of the three-dimensional Anderson transition in a laser-speckle disordered potential, and for a precise measurement of the mobility edge that is independent of any underlying theoretical modeling. Our measurements show excellent agreement with state-of-the-art numerical predictions over a wide range of disorder strengths, resolving long-standing discrepancies between prior experiments and theory. Beyond the three-dimensional Anderson transition, our approach opens new avenues for quantitative investigations of quantum critical phenomena in spatially disordered systems, including the roles of dimensionality, symmetry class, and interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07654v1 公告类型：新 摘要：粒子的安德森局域化——通过多重散射和相位相干性使波传输完全停止——是无序介质中量子干涉的典型表现。在三维空间中，标度理论预测了临界能量（迁移率边缘）下的量子相变，将局域态与扩散态分离，并支撑电子系统中的金属-绝缘体跃迁。尽管经过数十年的实验努力，对物质波这种标志性转变的直接观察仍然难以实现。先前对超冷原子的尝试受到强烈且不受控制的能量展宽的阻碍，导致对迁移率边缘的间接、有时不准确且依赖于模型的估计。在这里，我们实施了一种新颖的能量解析方案，以制备具有窄能量分布的原子物质波，并在长时间尺度上跟踪它们的膨胀动态。这允许直接观察激光散斑无序势中的三维安德森跃迁，并精确测量独立于任何基础理论模型的迁移率边缘。我们的测量结果与各种无序强度范围内最先进的数值预测非常一致，解决了先前实验与理论之间长期存在的差异。除了三维安德森转变之外，我们的方法为空间无序系统中量子临界现象的定量研究开辟了新途径，包括维度、对称类和相互作用的作用。
