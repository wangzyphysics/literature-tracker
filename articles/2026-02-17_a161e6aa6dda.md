## Abstract
arXiv:2510.12577v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Extracellular matrix (ECM) remodeling is central to a wide variety of healthy and diseased tissue processes. Unfortunately, predicting ECM remodeling under various chemical and mechanical conditions has proven to be excessively challenging, due in part to its complex regulation by intracellular and extracellular molecular reaction networks that are spatially and temporally dynamic. We introduce ECMSim, which is a highly interactive, real-time, and web application designed to simulate heterogeneous matrix remodeling. The current model simulates cardiac scar tissue with configurable input conditions using a large-scale model of the cardiac fibroblast signaling network. Cardiac fibrosis is a major component of many forms of heart failure. ECMSim solves 1.37 million coupled ordinary differential equations (ODEs) and executes approximately 4.84 million operations per time step in real time, encompassing 137 molecular species and 259 regulatory interactions per cell across a 100x100 spatial array (10,000 cells), which accounts for inputs, receptors, intracellular signaling cascades, ECM production, feedback loops, and molecular diffusion. The algorithm is represented by a set of ODEs that are coupled with ECM molecular diffusion. The equations are solved on demand using compiled C++ and the WebAssembly standard. The software enables the investigation of pathological or experimental conditions, hypothetical scenarios, matrix remodeling, or the testing of the effects of an experimental drug(s) with a target receptor.

arXiv:2510.12577v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：细胞外基质 (ECM) 重塑是多种健康和患病组织过程的核心。不幸的是，预测各种化学和机械条件下的 ECM 重塑已被证明极具挑战性，部分原因是其受到空间和时间动态的细胞内和细胞外分子反应网络的复杂调节。我们介绍 ECMSim，它是一个高度交互式、实时的 Web 应用程序，旨在模拟异构矩阵重构。当前模型使用心脏成纤维细胞信号网络的大规模模型来模拟具有可配置输入条件的心脏疤痕组织。心脏纤维化是多种形式的心力衰竭的主要组成部分。 ECMSim 求解 137 万个耦合常微分方程 (ODE)，并在每个时间步实时执行约 484 万次操作，涵盖 137 个分子种类和 100x100 空间阵列（10,000 个细胞）中每个细胞的 259 个调控相互作用，其中考虑了输入、受体、细胞内信号级联、ECM 产生、反馈回路和分子扩散。该算法由一组与 ECM 分子扩散耦合的 ODE 表示。使用编译的 C++ 和 WebAssembly 标准按需求解方程。该软件可以研究病理或实验条件、假设场景、矩阵重塑或测试实验药物与目标受体的效果。
