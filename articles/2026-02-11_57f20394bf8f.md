## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10008v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Simulating low-temperature properties of three-dimensional frustrated quantum magnets is challenging due to the sign problem and the system sizes required to mitigate substantial finite-size effects. However, there are many experimental examples of three-dimensional crystals that could host exotic low-temperature states of matter, such as quantum spin liquids. We calculate the ground-state phase diagrams of frustrated quantum spin models on the body-centered cubic lattice using neural quantum states. First, we study the antiferromagnetic $J_1-J_2$ model where we find a direct first-order phase transition between N\'eel and collinear long-range-ordered phases at $(J_2/J_1)_c = 0.705$, consistent with previous studies. Then, in a tetragonally-distorted variant, proposed as a minimal model of NaCa$_2$Cu$_2$(VO$_4$)$_3$, we find no evidence of a quantum paramagnetic ground state, with a first-order phase transition between N\'eel and chain phases at $(J_{2ab}/J_1)_c = 1.0375$. Therefore, the ground state of the tetragonally-distorted model does not reproduce the low-temperature magnetic properties of NaCa$_2$Cu$_2$(VO$_4$)$_3$, and the inclusion of other effects is necessary to rationalize experimental observations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10008v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于符号问题和减轻实质性有限尺寸效应所需的系统尺寸，模拟三维受挫量子磁体的低温特性具有挑战性。然而，有许多三维晶体的实验例子可以容纳奇异的低温物质态，例如量子自旋液体。我们使用神经量子态计算体心立方晶格上受挫量子自旋模型的基态相图。首先，我们研究反铁磁 $J_1-J_2$ 模型，在 $(J_2/J_1)_c = 0.705$ 处发现 N\'eel 和共线长程有序相之间的直接一阶相变，与之前的研究一致。然后，在四方扭曲变体中，作为 NaCa$_2$Cu$_2$(VO$_4$)$_3$ 的最小模型提出，我们没有发现量子顺磁基态的证据，在 $(J_{2ab}/J_1)_c = 1.0375$ 处，N\'eel 相和链相之间存在一阶相变。因此，四方扭曲模型的基态不能再现NaCa$_2$Cu$_2$(VO$_4$)$_3$的低温磁特性，并且需要包含其他效应以合理化实验观察。
