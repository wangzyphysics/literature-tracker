## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11979v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Coherent control of two-level quantum systems is typically achieved using resonant driving fields, forming the basis for qubit operations. Here, we report a mechanism for inducing complete Rabi oscillations in monochromatically driven two-level quantum systems, when the drive frequency is much larger than the Larmor frequency of the qubit. This effect$\unicode{x2015}$Rabi oscillations induced by nonresonant geometric drive (RING)$\unicode{x2015}$requires that the control field is elliptical, enclosing a nonzero area per cycle. We illustrate the effect with numerical simulations, and provide an analytical understanding via a simple effective Hamiltonian obtained from Floquet theory and perturbation theory. We show that RING enables coherent oscillations without relying on resonant energy exchange, allows for high-pass noise filtering, provides access to non-Abelian phases in finite magnetic fields. We detail a realization in electrically driven spin-orbit qubits and argue that the RING mechanism enables amplification of the Rabi frequency using the same gate voltage amplitudes at higher drive frequencies. Our results broaden the landscape of quantum control techniques, by highlighting a pathway to achieving coherent oscillations under off-resonant driving conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11979v1 公告类型：新 摘要：两级量子系统的相干控制通常是使用谐振驱动场来实现的，形成了量子位操作的基础。在这里，我们报告了一种当驱动频率远大于量子位的拉莫尔频率时在单色驱动的两级量子系统中诱导完整拉比振荡的机制。这种效应$\unicode{x2015}$非共振几何驱动(RING)$\unicode{x2015}$引起的拉比振荡要求控制场是椭圆形的，每个周期包含一个非零区域。我们通过数值模拟说明了这种效应，并通过从 Floquet 理论和微扰理论获得的简单有效的哈密顿量提供了分析理解。我们证明，RING 能够在不依赖谐振能量交换的情况下实现相干振荡，允许高通噪声过滤，提供有限磁场中非阿贝尔相位的访问。我们详细介绍了电驱动自旋轨道量子位的实现，并认为 RING 机制能够在更高的驱动频率下使用相同的栅极电压幅度来放大拉比频率。我们的结果通过强调在非谐振驱动条件下实现相干振荡的途径拓宽了量子控制技术的前景。
