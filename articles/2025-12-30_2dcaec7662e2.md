## Abstract
arXiv:2512.20230v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The rapid emergence of universal Machine Learning Interatomic Potentials (uMLIPs) has transformed materials modeling. However, a comprehensive understanding of their generalization behavior across configurational space remains an open challenge. In this work, we introduce a benchmarking framework to evaluate both the equilibrium and far-from-equilibrium performance of state-of-the-art uMLIPs, including three MACE-based models, MatterSim, and PET-MAD. Our assessment utilizes Equation-of-State (EOS) tests to evaluate near-equilibrium properties, such as bulk moduli and equilibrium volumes, alongside extensive Minima Hopping (MH) structural searches to probe the global Potential Energy Surface (PES). Here, we assess universality within the fundamental limit of unary (elemental) systems, which serve as a necessary baseline for broader chemical generalization and provide a framework that can be systematically extended to multicomponent materials. We find that while most models exhibit high accuracy in reproducing equilibrium volumes for transition metals, significant performance gaps emerge in alkali and alkaline earth metal groups. Crucially, our MH results reveal a decoupling between search efficiency and structural fidelity, highlighting that smoother learned PESs do not necessarily yield more accurate energetic landscapes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.20230v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：通用机器学习原子间势（uMLIP）的迅速出现已经改变了材料建模。然而，全面理解它们在配置空间中的泛化行为仍然是一个开放的挑战。在这项工作中，我们引入了一个基准测试框架来评估最先进的 uMLIP 的平衡和远非平衡性能，包括三个基于 MACE 的模型、MatterSim 和 PET-MAD。我们的评估利用状态方程 (EOS) 测试来评估近平衡特性，例如体积模量和平衡体积，同时进行广泛的最小跳跃 (MH) 结构搜索来探测全局势能面 (PES)。在这里，我们评估一元（元素）系统的基本限制内的普遍性，这是更广泛的化学概括的必要基线，并提供了一个可以系统地扩展到多组分材料的框架。我们发现，虽然大多数模型在再现过渡金属的平衡体积方面表现出高精度，但碱金属和碱土金属族中出现了显着的性能差距。至关重要的是，我们的 MH 结果揭示了搜索效率和结构保真度之间的脱钩，强调更平滑的学习 PES 并不一定会产生更准确的能量景观。
