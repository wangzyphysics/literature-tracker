## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23117v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Polymers-macromolecular systems composed of repeating chemical units-constitute the molecular foundation of living organisms, while their synthetic counterparts drive transformative advances across medicine, consumer products, and energy technologies. While machine learning (ML) models have been trained on millions of quantum chemical atomistic simulations for materials and/or small molecular structures to enable efficient, accurate, and transferable predictions of chemical properties, polymers have largely not been included in prior datasets due to the computational expense of high quality electronic structure calculations on representative polymeric structures. Here, we address this shortcoming with the creation of the Open Polymers 2026 (OPoly26) dataset, which contains more than 6.57 million density functional theory (DFT) calculations on up to 360 atom clusters derived from polymeric systems, comprising over 1.2 billion total atoms. OPoly26 captures the chemical diversity that makes polymers intrinsically tunable and versatile materials, encompassing variations in monomer composition, degree of polymerization, chain architectures, and solvation environments. We show that augmenting ML model training with the OPoly26 dataset improves model performance for polymer prediction tasks. We also publicly release the OPoly26 dataset to help further the development of ML models for polymers, and more broadly, strive towards universal atomistic models.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23117v1 公告类型：新 摘要：聚合物（由重复化学单元组成的大分子系统）构成了生物体的分子基础，而其合成对应物推动了医学、消费品和能源技术的变革性进步。虽然机器学习 (ML) 模型已经过对材料和/或小分子结构进行数百万次量子化学原子模拟的训练，以实现化学性质的高效、准确和可转移的预测，但由于对代表性聚合物结构进行高质量电子结构计算的计算费用，聚合物基本上没有包含在先前的数据集中。在这里，我们通过创建 Open Polymers 2026 (OPoly26) 数据集来解决这一缺陷，该数据集包含超过 657 万次密度泛函理论 (DFT) 计算，涉及来自聚合物系统的多达 360 个原子簇，总共包含超过 12 亿个原子。 OPoly26 捕捉了化学多样性，使聚合物成为本质上可调的多功能材料，包括单体组成、聚合度、链结构和溶剂化环境的变化。我们证明，使用 OPoly26 数据集增强 ML 模型训练可以提高聚合物预测任务的模型性能。我们还公开发布了 OPoly26 数据集，以帮助进一步开发聚合物的 ML 模型，更广泛地说，努力实现通用原子模型。
