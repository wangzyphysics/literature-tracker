## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00726v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a high-order implicit large eddy simulation (ILES) approach for simulating flows at the nearly incompressible regime. Our methodology based on utilization of a nodal discontinuous Galerkin (DG) discretization of the Boltzmann equations. The compactness and low-dissipative nature of the discontinuous Galerkin method are leveraged to mimic traditional large eddy simulations with subgrid-scale models. One of the key requirements of ILES is to provide dissipation only within a narrow band of high wavenumbers. This is validated through numerical experiments on the Taylor-Green Vortex problem in detail at a Reynolds number where varying scales of coherent turbulent structures are present. Furthermore, the approach is validated for external aerodynamic configurations by simulating the flow over a sphere at a Reynolds number of $Re=3700$, capturing the laminar-turbulent transition and the complex multiscale vortex dynamics characteristic of this regime. The results demonstrate the capability of the high-order DG-Boltzmann formulation to accurately capture transitional and turbulent flow features without the use of explicit sub-grid scale modeling, highlighting its potential as a robust and physically consistent framework for ILES of nearly incompressible turbulent flows.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00726v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种高阶隐式大涡模拟（ILES）方法，用于模拟几乎不可压缩状态下的流动。我们的方法基于玻尔兹曼方程的节点不连续伽辽金 (DG) 离散化。利用不连续伽辽金方法的紧凑性和低耗散特性来模拟亚网格尺度模型的传统大涡模拟。 ILES 的关键要求之一是仅在高波数窄带内提供耗散。这通过泰勒-格林涡问题在雷诺数上的详细数值实验得到了验证，其中存在不同尺度的相干湍流结构。此外，通过模拟雷诺数为 $Re=3700$ 的球体上的流动，捕获层流-湍流转变和该状态的复杂多尺度涡动力学特征，该方法针对外部空气动力学配置进行了验证。结果证明了高阶 DG-Boltzmann 公式能够在不使用显式子网格尺度建模的情况下准确捕获过渡流和湍流特征，突显了其作为几乎不可压缩湍流 ILES 的稳健且物理一致框架的潜力。
