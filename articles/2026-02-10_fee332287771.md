## Abstract
arXiv:2509.10307v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Simulating electrochemical interfaces using density functional theory (DFT) requires incorporating the effects of electrochemical potential. The electrochemical potential acts as a new degree of freedom that can effectively tune DFT results as electrochemistry does. Typically, this is implemented by adjusting the number of electrons on the solid surface within the Kohn-Sham (KS) equation, under the framework of an implicit solvent model and the Poisson-Boltzmann equation (PB equation), thereby modulating the potential difference between the solid and liquid. These simulations are often referred to as grand-canonical or fixed-potential DFT calculations. To apply this additional degree of freedom, Legendre transforms are employed in the calculation of free energy, establishing the relationship between the grand potential and the free energy. Other key physical properties, such as atomic forces, vibrational frequencies, and Stark tuning rates, can be derived based on this relationship rather than directly using Legendre transforms. This paper begins by discussing the numerical methodologies for the continuum model of electrolyte double layers and grand-potential algorithms. We then show that atomic forces under grand-canonical ensemble match the Hellmann-Feynman forces observed in canonical ensemble, as previously established. However, vibrational frequencies and Stark tuning rates exhibit distinct behaviors between these conditions. Through finite displacement methods, we confirm that vibrational frequencies and Stark tuning rates exhibit differences between grand-canonical and canonical ensembles.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.10307v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：使用密度泛函理论 (DFT) 模拟电化学界面需要考虑电化学势的影响。电化学势作为一种新的自由度，可以像电化学一样有效地调整 DFT 结果。通常，这是通过在隐式溶剂模型和泊松-玻尔兹曼方程（PB方程）的框架下调整Kohn-Sham（KS）方程中固体表面上的电子数量来实现的，从而调节固体和液体之间的电势差。这些模拟通常称为大正则或定势 DFT 计算。为了应用这种额外的自由度，在计算自由能时采用了勒让德变换，建立了宏势与自由能之间的关系。其他关键物理属性，例如原子力、振动频率和斯塔克调谐率，可以基于这种关系导出，而不是直接使用勒让德变换。本文首先讨论电解质双层连续介质模型的数值方法和巨势算法。然后，我们证明，大正则系综下的原子力与之前建立的正则系综中观察到的赫尔曼-费曼力相匹配。然而，振动频率和斯塔克调谐率在这些条件下表现出不同的行为。通过有限位移方法，我们确认振动频率和斯塔克调谐率在大正则系综和正则系综之间表现出差异。
