## Abstract
arXiv:2503.12708v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: As silicon-based computing approaches fundamental physical limits in energy efficiency, speed, and density, the search for complementary materials to extend or replace CMOS technology has become increasingly urgent. While two-dimensional (2D) transition metal dichalcogenides have been extensively investigated, van der Waals indium selenides--particularly InSe and In2Se3--offer a compelling alternative with distinct advantages for next-generation electronics. Unlike conventional 2D semiconductors, indium selenides combine exceptional electron mobility (exceeding 1,000 cm^2V^-1s^-1), high thermal velocity (>2x10^7 cm/s), thickness-tunable bandgaps (0.97-2.5 eV), and unique phase-dependent ferroelectric properties, enabling both high-performance logic and non-volatile memory functions within a single material system. This perspective critically evaluates the materials properties, fabrication challenges, and device applications of indium selenides, examining their potential to surpass silicon in ultra-scaled transistors through ballistic transport while simultaneously offering ferroelectric memory capabilities impossible in conventional semiconductors. We analyze recent breakthroughs in ballistic InSe transistors, tunnel field-effect transistors, and In2Se3-based ferroelectric devices for information storage, and identify key research priorities for addressing persistent challenges in scalable synthesis, phase control, and oxidation prevention. By bridging fundamental materials science with practical device engineering, we provide a roadmap for translating the exceptional properties of indium selenides into commercially viable, low-power computing technologies that can overcome the limitations of silicon while enabling novel computing architectures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2503.12708v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：随着硅基计算在能源效率、速度和密度方面接近基本物理极限，寻找补充材料来扩展或取代 CMOS 技术变得越来越紧迫。虽然二维 (2D) 过渡金属二硫属化物已得到广泛研究，但范德华硒化物（尤其是 InSe 和 In2Se3）为下一代电子产品提供了具有明显优势的引人注目的替代品。与传统的二维半导体不同，硒化铟结合了卓越的电子迁移率（超过 1,000 cm^2V^-1s^-1）、高热速度（>2x10^7 cm/s）、厚度可调带隙（0.97-2.5 eV）和独特的相相关铁电特性，在单一材料系统中实现高性能逻辑和非易失性存储功能。这种观点批判性地评估了硒化铟的材料特性、制造挑战和器件应用，研究了它们通过弹道传输在超大规模晶体管中超越硅的潜力，同时提供传统半导体不可能实现的铁电存储功能。我们分析了弹道 InSe 晶体管、隧道场效应晶体管和用于信息存储的 In2Se3 基铁电器件的最新突破，并确定了解决可扩展合成、相位控制和氧化预防方面持续存在的挑战的关键研究重点。通过将基础材料科学与实际设备工程联系起来，我们提供了一个路线图，将硒化铟的卓越特性转化为商业上可行的低功耗计算技术，这些技术可以克服硅的局限性，同时实现新颖的计算架构。
