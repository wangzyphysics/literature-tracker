## Abstract
Author(s): Atul Pandey, Prajwal Rigvedi, Edouard Lesne, Jitul Deka, Jiho Yoon, Wolfgang Hoppe, Chris Koerner, Banabir Pal, James M. Taylor, Stuart S. P. Parkin, and Georg Woltersdorf Chiral antiferromagnets like Mn 3 Sn are promising materials for ultrafast, low-power spintronic technologies, but they exhibit resistance to full magnetic ordering. This work shows that even in the presence of strong magnetic fields, regions of Mn 3 Sn thin films fail to align as they get pinned by grain boundaries. [Phys. Rev. Research 7, L042058] Published Tue Dec 09, 2025
