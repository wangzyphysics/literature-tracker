## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22637v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study presents a comparative numerical analysis of Gold (Au) and high-index semiconductor nanoparticles for refractive index sensing in the visible range. While Au nanoparticles demonstrate high sensitivity ($\approx 150$ nm per refractive index unit), their performance is constrained by ohmic losses. In contrast, high-index dielectrics are shown to exhibit comparable extinction efficiencies driven exclusively by scattering, thereby minimizing thermal losses. Multipolar decomposition reveals that semiconductors support simultaneous electric and magnetic Mie resonances, the interference of which enables directional scattering phenomena unattainable in small metallic particles. These findings suggest that high-index nanostructures offer a robust, low-loss alternative to plasmonics for advanced sensing applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22637v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项研究对用于可见光范围折射率传感的金 (Au) 和高折射率半导体纳米粒子进行了比较数值分析。虽然金纳米粒子表现出高灵敏度（每折射率单位约 150 纳米），但其性能受到欧姆损耗的限制。相比之下，高折射率电介质表现出可比的仅由散射驱动的消光效率，从而最大限度地减少热损失。多极分解表明，半导体支持同时发生的电和磁米氏共振，其干扰能够实现小金属颗粒中无法实现的定向散射现象。这些发现表明，高折射率纳米结构为先进传感应用提供了一种强大的、低损耗的等离激元替代方案。
