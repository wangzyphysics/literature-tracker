## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07580v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study the use of large language models (LLMs) for physics instrument design and compare their performance to reinforcement learning (RL). Using only prompting, LLMs are given task constraints and summaries of prior high-scoring designs and propose complete detector configurations, which we evaluate with the same simulators and reward functions used in RL-based optimization. Although RL yields stronger final designs, we find that modern LLMs consistently generate valid, resource-aware, and physically meaningful configurations that draw on broad pretrained knowledge of detector design principles and particle--matter interactions, despite having no task-specific training. Based on this result, as a first step toward hybrid design workflows, we explore pairing the LLMs with a dedicated trust region optimizer, serving as a precursor to future pipelines in which LLMs propose and structure design hypotheses while RL performs reward-driven optimization. Based on these experiments, we argue that LLMs are well suited as meta-planners: they can design and orchestrate RL-based optimization studies, define search strategies, and coordinate multiple interacting components within a unified workflow. In doing so, they point toward automated, closed-loop instrument design in which much of the human effort required to structure and supervise optimization can be reduced.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07580v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究大型语言模型 (LLM) 在物理仪器设计中的使用，并将其性能与强化学习 (RL) 进行比较。仅使用提示，法学硕士就会获得任务约束和先前高分设计的摘要，并提出完整的检测器配置，我们使用基于强化学习的优化中使用的相同模拟器和奖励函数对其进行评估。尽管强化学习产生了更强大的最终设计，但我们发现现代法学硕士始终能够生成有效的、资源感知的和具有物理意义的配置，这些配置利用了探测器设计原理和粒子-物质相互作用的广泛预训练知识，尽管没有经过特定任务的训练。基于这一结果，作为迈向混合设计工作流程的第一步，我们探索将 LLM 与专用信任域优化器配对，作为未来管道的先驱，其中 LLM 提出并构造设计假设，而 RL 执行奖励驱动的优化。基于这些实验，我们认为法学硕士非常适合作为元规划者：他们可以设计和编排基于强化学习的优化研究，定义搜索策略，并在统一的工作流程中协调多个交互组件。在此过程中，他们指出了自动化、闭环仪器设计，可以减少构建和监督优化所需的大量人力。
