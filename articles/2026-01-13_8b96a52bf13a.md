## Abstract
The room temperature, high-resolution (8-16 MHz) rovibrational absorption spectrum of low-pressure dimethyl sulfide [Konnov et al., J. Chem. Phys. 2025, 37, 109690] shows spectral congestion at transition energies as low as ~ 1000 cm-1 . The origin of this congestion is investigated theoretically by developing a rotation-torsion-vibration Hamiltonian and dipole moment surface that allow us to model the excitation of the methyl rocking vibrations that are excited in the experiment. The vibrations that are described by this Hamiltonian include the two torsions and the seven additional low-frequency vibrations with harmonic frequencies below 1100 cm-1. The effect of Coriolis coupling is minimized by defining a body-fixed embedding that satisfies the Eckart condition at the nine equivalent minima on the potential surface. The couplings between the torsions and vibrations, especially for those states excited in hot-band transitions, lead to substantial state mixing. Not only does this state mixing lead to spectral congestion due to intensity borrowing, it also leads to the quartet of transitions, which are associated with the torsional tunneling of the two methyl groups, becoming spectrally distinct at the experimental resolution. These combined effects lead to model spectrum with similar levels of congestion as are observed experimentally, allowing us to deconvolute the various contributions to this low-energy spectral congestion.

## 摘要（中文）
低压二甲硫醚的室温高分辨率 (8-16 MHz) 振动吸收光谱 [Konnov 等人，J. Chem.物理。 2025, 37, 109690] 显示跃迁能量低至 ~ 1000 cm-1 时的光谱拥塞。通过开发旋转-扭转-振动哈密顿量和偶极矩表面，我们可以从理论上研究这种拥塞的根源，这使我们能够对实验中激发的甲基摇摆振动的激发进行建模。该哈密顿量描述的振动包括两个扭转和谐波频率低于 1100 cm-1 的七个附加低频振动。通过定义在势面上九个等效最小值处满足埃卡特条件的身体固定嵌入，可以最大限度地减少科里奥利耦合的影响。扭转和振动之间的耦合，特别是对于热带跃迁中激发的那些状态，导致大量的状态混合。这种状态混合不仅会因强度借用而导致光谱拥塞，还会导致与两个甲基的扭转隧道相关的四重跃迁，在实验分辨率下光谱变得不同。这些综合效应导致模型频谱具有与实验观察到的相似拥塞水平，使我们能够对这种低能量频谱拥塞的各种贡献进行解卷积。
