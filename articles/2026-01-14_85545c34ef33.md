# Spectral mechanism and nearly reducible transfer matrices for pseudotransitions in one-dimensional systems

## Abstract
arXiv:2511.18109v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: While true phase transitions are forbidden in one-dimensional systems with short-range interactions, several models have recently been shown to exhibit sharp yet analytic thermodynamic anomalies that mimic thermal phase transitions. We show that this behavior arises from transfer matrices that are mathematically irreducible but possess a nearly block-diagonal structure due to the weak contribution of off-diagonal Boltzmann weights in the low-temperature regime. This results in weakly coupled competing sectors whose eigenvalue competition produces abrupt crossovers without nonanalyticity, a mechanism we term nearly block-diagonal irreducible. A key thermodynamic signature of such pseudotransitions is that the residual entropy at the interface remains bounded between the residual entropies of the competing sectors. We develop a general spectral framework to describe this behavior and apply it to two representative models: the Ising chain with internal degeneracy (Doniach model) and a hexagonal nanowire chain with mixed spin-1/2 and spin-1 components. In the first case, we derive exact expressions for the pseudo-critical temperature and residual entropy. In the second, we reduce the full $1458\times1458$ transfer matrix via symmetry decomposition and construct a low-rank effective matrix that accurately captures the crossover between quasi-ferromagnetic and quasi-core-ferromagnetic regimes. Our results demonstrate that pseudotransitions can be understood as spectral phenomena emerging from irreducible but functionally decoupled structures within the transfer matrix.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.18109v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：虽然在具有短程相互作用的一维系统中禁止真正的相变，但最近有几个模型被证明表现出模拟热相变的尖锐但分析的热力学异常。我们证明这种行为是由传递矩阵引起的，传递矩阵在数学上是不可约的，但由于低温状态下非对角玻尔兹曼权重的贡献较弱，因此具有近块对角结构。这导致弱耦合的竞争部门，其特征值竞争产生突然的交叉而没有非分析性，我们将这种机制称为近块对角不可约。这种赝转变的一个关键热力学特征是界面处的残余熵仍然限制在竞争扇区的残余熵之间。我们开发了一个通用的光谱框架来描述这种行为，并将其应用于两个代表性模型：具有内简并性的伊辛链（Doniach 模型）和具有混合自旋 1/2 和自旋 1 分量的六边形纳米线链。在第一种情况下，我们推导出伪临界温度和残余熵的精确表达式。在第二步中，我们通过对称分解减少了完整的 $1458\times1458$ 传输矩阵，并构造了一个低秩有效矩阵，可以准确地捕获准铁磁和准铁磁状态之间的交叉。我们的结果表明，赝跃迁可以理解为从转移矩阵内不可约但功能解耦结构中出现的光谱现象。
