## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22369v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The coherence of quantum dot qubits fabricated in semiconductors is often limited by charge noise from defects in gate dielectrics, which are material- and process-dependent. Characterizing these defects is an important step towards reducing their impact and improving qubit coherence. The identification of individual defects requires atomic-scale spatial resolution, however, and sufficient spectral sensitivity to determine their electronic structure. Electrostatic force microscopy (EFM) provides highly resolved maps of the surface potential of dielectrics, and importantly, is also sensitive to single-electron charging processes that reflect the spectral structure of underlying defects. In this work, we use cryogenic EFM to characterize aluminum oxide grown by atomic layer deposition (ALD) on bulk silicon. These measurements reveal defects close to the surface that exchange electrons with the EFM tip as they transition through different charge states. Detailed electrostatic modeling opens the door to powerful techniques for mapping tip-backgate charging voltages onto defect transition energies, allowing defects such as aluminum vacancies, and carbon, oxygen, or hydrogen impurities to be identified, by comparing to density functional theory (DFT). These results point towards EFM as a powerful tool for exploring defect structures in solid-state qubits.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22369v1 公告类型：新 摘要：半导体中制造的量子点量子位的相干性通常受到栅极电介质缺陷产生的电荷噪声的限制，而栅极电介质缺陷与材料和工艺有关。表征这些缺陷是减少其影响和提高量子位一致性的重要一步。然而，单个缺陷的识别需要原子级的空间分辨率和足够的光谱灵敏度来确定其电子结构。静电力显微镜 (EFM) 提供了电介质表面电位的高分辨率图，重要的是，它对反映潜在缺陷光谱结构的单电子充电过程也很敏感。在这项工作中，我们使用低温 EFM 来表征通过原子层沉积 (ALD) 在体硅上生长的氧化铝。这些测量揭示了靠近表面的缺陷，当电子通过不同的电荷状态转变时，这些缺陷会与 EFM 尖端交换电子。详细的静电建模为将尖端背栅充电电压映射到缺陷跃迁能量上的强大技术打开了大门，通过与密度泛函理论 (DFT) 进行比较，可以识别铝空位以及碳、氧或氢杂质等缺陷。这些结果表明 EFM 是探索固态量子位缺陷结构的强大工具。
