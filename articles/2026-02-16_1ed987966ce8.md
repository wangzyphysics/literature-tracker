# An optimal lower bound for the low density Fermi gas in three dimensions

## Abstract
arXiv:2410.08904v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We consider the dilute Fermi gas in three dimensions interacting through a positive, radially symmetric, compactly supported and integrable potential in the thermodynamic limit. We establish a second order lower bound for the ground state energy density with an error term which is optimal in the sense that it matches the order of the next correction term conjectured by Huang-Yang in 1957.
