## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06123v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Lattice gauge theories (LGTs) describe fundamental interactions in particle physics. A central phenomenon in these theories is confinement, which binds quarks and antiquarks into hadrons through the formation of string-like flux tubes of gauge fields. Simulating confinement dynamics is a challenging task, but recent advances in quantum simulation are enabling the exploration of LGTs in regimes beyond the reach of classical computation. For analog devices, a major difficulty is the realization of strong plaquette interactions, which generate string fluctuations that can drive a roughening transition. Understanding string roughening -- where strong transversal functions lead to an effective restoration of translational symmetry at long distances -- is of central importance in the study of confinement. In this work, we show that string roughening emerges naturally in an analog Rydberg quantum simulator. We first map a triangular Rydberg array onto a (2+1)D U(1) LGT where plaquette terms appear as first-order processes. We study flux strings connecting static charges and demonstrate that, near a deconfined quantum critical point, the string exhibits logarithmic growth of its transverse width as the separation between charges increases, along with the universal L\"uscher correction to the confining potential -- both signatures of string roughening. Finally, we investigate the real-time dynamics of an initially rigid string, observing large fluctuations after quenching into the roughening regime, as well as string breaking via particle-pair creation. Our results indicate that rough strings can be realized in experimentally accessible quantum simulators, opening the door to detailed studies of how strong fluctuations influence string-breaking dynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06123v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：晶格规范理论（LGT）描述了粒子物理学中的基本相互作用。这些理论的一个核心现象是禁闭，它通过形成规范场的弦状通量管将夸克和反夸克束缚成强子。模拟约束动力学是一项具有挑战性的任务，但量子模拟的最新进展使得在经典计算无法达到的范围内探索 LGT 成为可能。对于模拟器件来说，一个主要困难是实现强板相互作用，这会产生可以驱动粗糙化过渡的弦波动。了解弦粗糙化（强横向函数导致远距离平移对称性的有效恢复）对于约束研究至关重要。在这项工作中，我们展示了弦粗糙化在模拟里德堡量子模拟器中自然出现。我们首先将三角形里德伯阵列映射到 (2+1)D U(1) LGT，其中斑块项显示为一阶过程。我们研究了连接静电荷的通量弦，并证明，在解禁量子临界点附近，随着电荷之间的间距增加，弦的横向宽度呈现对数增长，同时对限制电势进行通用的 L\"uscher 校正——这都是弦粗糙化的特征。最后，我们研究了初始刚性弦的实时动力学，观察到淬火到粗糙化状态后的大波动，以及通过粒子对创建实现弦断裂。我们的结果表明，粗糙弦可以在实验中实现易于使用的量子模拟器，为详细研究强波动如何影响断弦动力学打开了大门。
