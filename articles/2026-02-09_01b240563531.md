# 3D Spin-orbital liquids

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06628v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-orbital liquids provide an exactly solvable route to three-dimensional Z2 quantum spin liquids beyond the original Kitaev setting. Built from higher-dimensional Clifford-algebra representations, spin-orbital Hamiltonians can be realized on both three- and four-coordinated lattices, giving rise to phases with 3 and 2 itinerant Majorana flavors. We demonstrate that these models host a rich set of gapless Majorana metals, characterized, in particular, by topological Fermi surfaces, nodal lines, and Weyl semimetal phases. We analyze the stability of these structures under physically motivated perturbations and identify generic splitting patterns and topological transitions driven by symmetry breaking and flavor mixing. This yields a unified organizing framework for three-dimensional Majorana metals in fractionalized spin liquids.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06628v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋轨道液体为超越原始 Kitaev 设置的三维 Z2 量子自旋液体提供了一条完全可解的途径。自旋轨道哈密顿量由高维克利福德代数表示构建而成，可以在三配位和四配位晶格上实现，从而产生具有 3 和 2 巡回马约拉纳风味的相。我们证明这些模型包含一组丰富的无带隙马约拉纳金属，其特征尤其是拓扑费米面、节线和外尔半金属相。我们分析了这些结构在物理驱动的扰动下的稳定性，并确定了由对称破缺和风味混合驱动的通用分裂模式和拓扑转变。这为分级自旋液体中的三维马约拉纳金属产生了统一的组织框架。
