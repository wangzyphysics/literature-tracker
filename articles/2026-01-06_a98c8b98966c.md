## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01173v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: To overcome the limitations of conventional liquid density sensors, MEMS-based approaches have been developed. However, the viscosity-density coupling effect often compromises accuracy in high-viscosity liquids. Although various decoupling strategies have been proposed, they often suffer from complexity and inefficiency. This study presents an AlN-based PMUT liquid density sensor insensitive to viscosity interference. The sensor employs two identical PMUTs, functioning as transmitter and receiver, respectively. An ultrasonic wave generated by the transmitter is reflected by the liquid surface and detected by the receiver. Theoretical calculations demonstrate that when the excitation frequency remains constant, the amplitude of the received electrical signal exhibits a specific relationship with liquid density, while the viscosity-induced signal amplitude variation becomes negligible. Therefore, after calibrating the PMUT liquid density sensor by fitting the relationship between received signal amplitude and density across 0-100% glycerol solutions, the device can measure densities within this range regardless of liquid type and viscosity. Experimental results show that the sensor can accurately measure the density of the propylene glycol solutions using the glycerol-calibrated fitting formula, with the calculated density error rate between measured propylene glycol solutions using the glycerol-calibrated fitting formula and actual densities remains below 0.125%, demonstrating measurement insensitivity to viscosity differences between the two liquids. The proposed method achieves maximum error rates of less than 2.5% in high-viscosity environments (80%-100% glycerol solutions), which is 20% that of other density measurement methods based on resonant frequency. The developed PMUT liquid density sensor exhibits a measurable density range from 0.789 to 1.261 g/cm3.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01173v1 公告类型：新 摘要：为了克服传统液体密度传感器的局限性，开发了基于 MEMS 的方法。然而，粘度-密度耦合效应通常会影响高粘度液体的精度。尽管已经提出了各种解耦策略，但它们常常面临复杂性和低效率的问题。本研究提出了一种对粘度干扰不敏感的基于 AlN 的 PMUT 液体密度传感器。该传感器采用两个相同的 PMUT，分别充当发射器和接收器。发射器产生的超声波被液体表面反射并被接收器检测到。理论计算表明，当激励频率保持恒定时，接收到的电信号的幅度与液体密度呈现特定的关系，而粘度引起的信号幅度变化可以忽略不计。因此，通过拟合 0-100% 甘油溶液中接收信号幅度和密度之间的关系来校准 PMUT 液体密度传感器后，无论液体类型和粘度如何，该设备都可以测量该范围内的密度。实验结果表明，该传感器能够利用甘油校准拟合公式准确测量丙二醇溶液的密度，利用甘油校准拟合公式计算出的测量丙二醇溶液的密度误差率与实际密度之间的误差率保持在0.125%以下，表明测量对两种液体之间的粘度差异不敏感。该方法在高粘度环境（80%-100%甘油溶液）中最大误差率小于2.5%，是其他基于共振频率的密度测量方法的20%。开发的 PMUT 液体密度传感器的可测量密度范围为 0.789 至 1.261 g/cm3。
