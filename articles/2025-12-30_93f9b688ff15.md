## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23116v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The simulation of turbulent flows remains a central challenge, as even our most powerful computers cannot resolve the finest scales of motion in many flows of practical interest. As a result, the effects of unresolved scales on large eddies must be modelled via closures and coarse-graining procedures. Large-eddy simulation (LES) traditionally coarse-grains Navier-Stokes equations using Smagorinsky's effective viscosity model. This has the merit of simplicity but fails to account for strong non-equilibrium effects, as they typically arise in most flows in the vicinity of solid walls, the reason being that the notion of eddy viscosity assumes scale separation between small and large eddies, an assumption that fails for high-Reynolds flows far from equilibrium. The lattice Boltzmann method (LBM) offers an alternative by coarse-graining at the kinetic level, potentially capturing non-equilibrium effects beyond reach of hydrodynamic closures. This paper addresses whether LBM-Smagorinsky LES of forced homogeneous isotropic turbulence (FHIT) exhibits kinetic behavior. We test whether the turbulent Knudsen number $K_t$, measuring scale separation, reaches order one (kinetic regime) or remains asymptotically small (hydrodynamic regime). Using reference DNS ($800^3$) and iso-Reynolds LES ($100^3$) at $Re = 2 \times 10^4$, we quantify $K_t$ via spatial maps, temporal statistics, energy spectra, and higher-order moments. Results show $K_t \sim O(10^{-3})$, strictly positive without negative excursions, with spectra and flatness following canonical LES behavior. We conclude that despite its kinetic formulation, LBM-Smagorinsky LES operates in the hydrodynamic regime, with small FHIT eddies remaining in local equilibrium with larger ones, validating Smagorinsky viscosity and confirming that LBM-LES functions as conventional hydrodynamic LES while preserving LBM efficiency and locality.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23116v1 公告类型：新 摘要：湍流的模拟仍然是一个核心挑战，因为即使我们最强大的计算机也无法解析许多实际感兴趣的流动中的最精细的运动尺度。因此，必须通过闭包和粗粒度程序对未解决的尺度对大涡流的影响进行建模。大涡模拟 (LES) 传统上是使用 Smagorinsky 有效粘度模型的粗粒纳维-斯托克斯方程。这具有简单的优点，但未能考虑到强烈的非平衡效应，因为它们通常出现在固体壁附近的大多数流动中，原因是涡流粘度的概念假设小涡流和大涡流之间存在尺度分离，这一假设对于远离平衡的高雷诺流来说是失败的。格子玻尔兹曼方法 (LBM) 提供了一种在动力学水平上进行粗粒度化的替代方案，有可能捕获超出流体动力学闭包范围的非平衡效应。本文讨论了强迫均匀各向同性湍流 (FHIT) 的 LBM-Smagorinsky LES 是否表现出动力学行为。我们测试测量尺度分离的湍流努森数 $K_t$ 是否达到一阶（动力学状态）或保持渐近小（流体动力学状态）。使用参考 DNS ($800^3$) 和 iso-Reynolds LES ($100^3$) 在 $Re = 2 \times 10^4$ 时，我们通过空间图、时间统计、能谱和高阶矩来量化 $K_t$。结果显示 $K_t \sim O(10^{-3})$，严格为正，没有负偏移，频谱和平坦度遵循规范的 LES 行为。我们得出的结论是，尽管有动力学公式，LBM-Smagorinsky LES 在流体动力学状态下运行，小 FHIT 涡流与较大涡流保持局部平衡，验证了 Smagorinsky 粘度并确认 LBM-LES 与传统流体动力学 LES 一样起作用，同时保留了 LBM 效率和局部性。
