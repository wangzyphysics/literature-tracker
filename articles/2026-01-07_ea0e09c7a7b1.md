## Abstract
Equilateral triangular lattices (TLs) with antiferromagnetic interactions are ideal templates for inducing magnetic frustration, offering a platform to explore exotic quantum spin states, which are crucial for advancing quantum science and technology. However, few examples meet these structural criteria, and even fewer realize these fascinating physical properties. Here, we report four novel equilateral TL magnets by deliberately choosing the structural template and building blocks, namely, the molecular-brick chemical strategy. Magnetic ions are fixed on an equilateral TL via a three-fold rotation symmetry in the a-b plane and are eclipsed stacked along the c-axis. Magnetic susceptibility and specific heat measurements reveal a long-range magnetic ordering in BaCoBe2(BO3)2F2 around 180 mK, while BaNiBe2(BO3)2F2 remains disordered at 140 mK. Furthermore, crystal orbital Hamilton population analysis shows that different molecular bricks exhibit distinct bonding characteristics, resulting in diverse magnetic properties. Our results demonstrate that the molecular-brick chemical strategy offers valuable insights for investigating geometrically frustrated magnets.

## 摘要（中文）
具有反铁磁相互作用的等边三角形晶格（TL）是诱导磁挫败的理想模板，为探索奇异的量子自旋态提供了一个平台，这对于推进量子科学和技术至关重要。然而，很少有例子能够满足这些结构标准，实现这些令人着迷的物理特性的例子就更少了。在这里，我们通过精心选择结构模板和构建块，即分子砖化学策略，报告了四种新型等边TL磁体。磁性离子通过 a-b 平面中的三重旋转对称性固定在等边 TL 上，并沿 c 轴重叠堆叠。磁化率和比热测量表明，BaCoBe2(BO3)2F2 在 180 mK 左右存在长程磁有序，而 BaNiBe2(BO3)2F2 在 140 mK 时仍保持无序状态。此外，晶体轨道汉密尔顿布居分析表明，不同的分子砖表现出不同的键合特性，从而导致不同的磁性能。我们的结果表明，分子砖化学策略为研究几何受挫磁体提供了宝贵的见解。
