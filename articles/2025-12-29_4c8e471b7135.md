## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21682v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a combined experimental and theoretical study of the triangular-lattice quantum antiferromagnet CsFeCl$_3$ under high magnetic fields and high pressure. Pulsed-field magnetization for the magnetic field along the symmetric $c$ direction at ambient pressure reveals a magnetization process from a nonmagnetic singlet ground state with a nearly linear increase between 3.7 and 10.7 T, a plateau-like region, and then a sharp stepwise metamagnetic transition near 32 T. Wide frequency--field range electron spin resonance indicates that the low-field regime originates from the $J = 1$ manifold, while the high-field metamagnetic transition suggests a level crossing between the $J = 1$ and $J = 2$ lowest states. Pulsed-field magnetic susceptibilities measured with a proximity detector oscillator under high pressure show that the low-field nonmagnetic singlet phase is gradually suppressed, while the high-field metamagnetic transition evolves into an increasingly rich pattern of fractional steps. While the observations at low to intermediate fields can be understood within the established spin-1 description, the high-field regime requires a new perspective, which we provide through a projected spin-1/2 framework built from Zeeman-selected crystal-field states not related by time reversal. This construction naturally allows emergent three-body interactions on triangular plaquettes and explains the asymmetric evolution of the fractional steps in the magnetization. Our findings reveal that high-field effective spin models in quantum magnets with separated yet accessible crystal-field multiplets are not constrained to even-body couplings, but can naturally host odd-body terms, opening a broader avenue for realizing field-asymmetric magnetization processes and exotic phases beyond conventional even-body physics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21682v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了高磁场和高压下三角晶格量子反铁磁体 CsFeCl$_3$ 的实验和理论相结合的研究。环境压力下沿对称 $c$ 方向的磁场的脉冲场磁化揭示了从非磁性单重态基态开始的磁化过程，在 3.7 到 10.7 T 之间几乎线性增加，这是一个平台状区域，然后在 32 T 附近出现急剧的逐步变磁转变。宽频率场范围电子自旋共振表明低场状态源自 $J = 1$ 流形，而高场变磁转变表明在$J = 1$ 和 $J = 2$ 最低状态。在高压下用接近探测器振荡器测量的脉冲场磁化率表明，低场非磁性单重态相逐渐受到抑制，而高场变磁转变演变成越来越丰富的分数阶模式。虽然可以在已建立的自旋 1 描述中理解低到中场的观察结果，但高场状态需要新的视角，我们通过根据塞曼选择的与时间反转无关的晶体场状态构建的投影自旋 1/2 框架提供该视角。这种结构自然地允许三角形板上出现三体相互作用，并解释了磁化中分数阶的不对称演化。我们的研究结果表明，具有分离但可访问的晶体场多重态的量子磁体中的高场有效自旋模型并不局限于偶数体耦合，而是可以自然地容纳奇体项，为实现场不对称磁化过程和超越传统偶数体物理的奇异相开辟了更广阔的途径。
