## Abstract
Light-driven hydrogen evolution is a promising strategy for solar fuel production. While numerous systems for molecular photocatalysis exist, their implementation into reusable soft materials remains challenging due to catalyst leaching, solubility issues, and limited structural integration. Here, we report the first hydrogel system in which a molecular photocatalyst is covalently embedded as crosslinker. Using a bipyridine- annulated perylene dye bearing terminal alkene groups, we employ thiol-ene click chemistry to incorporate the dye into a polyethylene glycol (PEG)-based hydrogel network. Post-synthetic platinum coordination yields “Dyedrogels” – mechanically robust hydrogels of varying shape that retain their photocatalytic functionality under visible-light irradiation in pure water, without any solubilizing additives. Structural and optical characterization confirms a homogeneous distribution of the catalyst, while indicating only negligible platinum leaching. Despite moderate hydrogen evolution turnover numbers (TON = 0.03), the stability, modularity, and reusability of this platform underscore its value as a device-like material for future flow-reactor designs. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first demonstration of a photocatalytically active hydrogel in which the molecular catalyst itself constitutes a covalent crosslink within the polymer network.

## 摘要（中文）
光驱动的析氢是太阳能燃料生产的一种有前景的策略。尽管存在许多分子光催化系统，但由于催化剂浸出、溶解度问题和有限的结构集成，将它们应用到可重复使用的软材料中仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们报告了第一个水凝胶系统，其中分子光催化剂作为交联剂共价嵌入。使用带有末端烯基的联吡啶环化苝染料，我们采用硫醇-烯点击化学将染料合并到基于聚乙二醇（PEG）的水凝胶网络中。合成后的铂配位产生“染料凝胶”——机械坚固的不同形状的水凝胶，在纯水中可见光照射下保留其光催化功能，无需任何增溶添加剂。结构和光学表征证实了催化剂的均匀分布，同时表明铂的浸出量可以忽略不计。尽管析氢周转次数适中（TON = 0.03），但该平台的稳定性、模块化和可重复使用性强调了其作为未来流动反应器设计的类设备材料的价值。据我们所知，这是光催化活性水凝胶的首次演示，其中分子催化剂本身在聚合物网络内构成共价交联。
