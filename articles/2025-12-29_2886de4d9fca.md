## Abstract
arXiv:2506.23956v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The search for candidate materials for fractional Chern insulators (FCIs) has mainly focused on the topological and geometrical structures of single-particle Chern bands. However, there are inherent limitations in approaches that neglect interaction effects, highlighting the need for complementary methods. In this work, we discuss how the Chern number defined for the effective interaction projected onto a Chern band is related to the stabilization of FCIs. Specifically, by formulating both the effective interaction and the two-particle problem using a common matrix, we establish a connection between the two-particle band structure and the effective interaction. This formulation allows us to characterize the effective interaction through the topology of the two-particle band. To investigate the relationship between topological effective interactions and FCIs, we perform numerical calculations primarily based on exact diagonalization. We find a notable correlation between the fact that the dominant two-particle bands carry a unit Chern number and the realization of a robust FCI at the filling fraction $\nu = 1/3$. This result is consistent with the presumed correspondence between pseudopotentials in the fractional quantum Hall effect and the two-particle band structure. From another perspective, our findings suggest that the topology inherent in the interaction itself can obstruct trivial ground states. We also discuss this in the context of scattering channels. Extending such topological two-body interactions could pave the way for realizing exotic states beyond FCIs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.23956v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：分数陈绝缘体（FCI）候选材料的搜索主要集中在单粒子陈能带的拓扑和几何结构上。然而，忽视相互作用效应的方法存在固有的局限性，凸显了补充方法的必要性。在这项工作中，我们讨论了为投射到陈能带上的有效相互作用定义的陈数与 FCI 的稳定性如何相关。具体来说，通过使用公共矩阵来制定有效相互作用和双粒子问题，我们在双粒子能带结构和有效相互作用之间建立了联系。这个公式使我们能够通过双粒子带的拓扑结构来表征有效的相互作用。为了研究拓扑有效相互作用与 FCI 之间的关系，我们主要基于精确对角化进行数值计算。我们发现，主要的双粒子带带有单位陈数这一事实与在填充分数 $\nu = 1/3$ 下实现稳健的 FCI 之间存在显着的相关性。该结果与分数量子霍尔效应中的赝势与双粒子能带结构之间的假定对应关系一致。从另一个角度来看，我们的研究结果表明，相互作用本身固有的拓扑结构可以阻碍微不足道的基态。我们还在散射通道的背景下讨论这个问题。扩展这种拓扑二体相互作用可以为实现 FCI 之外的奇异状态铺平道路。
