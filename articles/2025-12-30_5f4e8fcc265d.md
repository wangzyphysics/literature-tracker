## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23556v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Lunar-based gravitational-wave interferometry is a fascinating endeavor, and was proposed as a promising approach to bridge the observational gap between space-borne and ground-based detectors. In this work, we adopt the Fisher-matrix method to examine the angular-resolution performance of the newly proposed Crater Interferometry Gravitational-wave Observatory (CIGO) on the lunar crater rim near the north pole, together with TianQin and LISA, for monochromatic sources in the 0.1-10 Hz band. We find that above 0.1 Hz, CIGO achieves better localization accuracy than the other two space-based missions and dominates the combined detector network's performance, provided that lunar noise mitigation is achieved in the 0.1-2.87 Hz frequency range. We further explore an upgraded Tetrahedron configuration, TCIGO, with a fourth station at the bottom of a crater, which forms a regular tetrahedral constellation on the lunar surface. The result shows that TCIGO yields a five-fold improvement in angular-resolution capability over CIGO and gets better sky coverage across the target frequency band.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23556v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：基于月球的引力波干涉测量是一项令人着迷的尝试，被提出作为一种有前途的方法来弥合星载和地面探测器之间的观测差距。在这项工作中，我们采用费舍尔矩阵方法，与天琴和LISA一起检查了新提出的位于北极附近月球陨石坑边缘的陨石坑干涉引力波观测站（CIGO）对于0.1-10 Hz频段单色源的角分辨率性能。我们发现，在 0.1 Hz 以上，CIGO 比其他两个天基任务实现了更好的定位精度，并且在组合探测器网络的性能中占据主导地位，前提是在 0.1-2.87 Hz 频率范围内实现了月球噪声缓解。我们进一步探索升级的四面体配置 TCIGO，在陨石坑底部设有第四个站，在月球表面形成规则的四面体星座。结果表明，TCIGO 的角分辨率能力比 CIGO 提高了五倍，并且在目标频段上获得了更好的天空覆盖范围。
