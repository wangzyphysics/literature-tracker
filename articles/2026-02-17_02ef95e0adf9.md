## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13825v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In this work, we report implementation and performance evaluation of memristor-driven fundamental logic gates, including NOT, AND, NAND, OR, NOR, and XOR, and novel and optimized design of the sequential logic circuits, such as D flip-flop, T-flip-flop, JK-flip-flop, and SR-flip-flop. The design, implementation, and optimization of these logic circuits were performed in SPECTRE in Cadence Virtuoso and integrated with 90 nm CMOS technology node. Additionally, we discuss an optimized design of memristor-driven logic gates and sequential logic circuits, and draw a comparative analysis with the other reported state-of-the-art work on sequential circuits. Moreover, the utilized memristor framework was experimentally pre-validated with the experimental data of Y2O3-based memristive devices, which shows significantly low values of variability during switching in both device-to-device (D2D) and cycle-to-cycle (C2C) operation. The performance metrics were calculated in terms of area, power, and delay of these sequential circuits and were found to be reduced by more than ~24%, 60%, and 58%, respectively, as compared to the other state-of-the-art work on sequential circuits. Therefore, the implemented memristor-based design significantly improves the performance of various logic designs, which makes it more area and power-efficient and shows the potential of memristor in designing various low-power, low-cost, ultrafast, and compact circuits.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13825v1 发布类型：cross 摘要：在这项工作中，我们报告了忆阻器驱动的基本逻辑门（包括 NOT、AND、NAND、OR、NOR 和 XOR）的实现和性能评估，以及时序逻辑电路（例如 D 触发器、T 触发器、JK 触发器和 SR 触发器）的新颖和优化设计。这些逻辑电路的设计、实现和优化在 Cadence Virtuoso 的 SPECTRE 中进行，并与 90 nm CMOS 技术节点集成。此外，我们还讨论了忆阻器驱动逻辑门和时序逻辑电路的优化设计，并与其他报道的时序电路最先进的工作进行了比较分析。此外，所使用的忆阻器框架已经通过基于 Y2O3 的忆阻器件的实验数据进行了实验预先验证，该器件在器件到器件 (D2D) 和周期到周期 (C2C) 操作的切换过程中显示出非常低的可变性值。性能指标是根据这些时序电路的面积、功耗和延迟来计算的，结果发现与时序电路的其他最先进的工作相比，性能指标分别降低了约 24%、60% 和 58% 以上。因此，所实现的基于忆阻器的设计显着提高了各种逻辑设计的性能，使其更具面积和功率效率，并显示了忆阻器在设计各种低功耗、低成本、超快和紧凑电路中的潜力。
