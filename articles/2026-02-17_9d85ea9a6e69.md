## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13721v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Unidirectional magnetoresistance, or electric magnetochiral anisotropy (eMChA), is a nonlinear magnetotransport phenomenon that arises in noncentrosymmetric conductors , where changes in resistance $R(B)$ are: (i) chiral, $\Delta R(B)/R(0)=2\,\chi\, {\bf I}\cdot{\bf B}$, or (ii) polar, $\Delta R(B)/R(0)=2\,\gamma\, {\bf I}\cdot({\bf P}\times{\bf B})$, with eMChA coefficients $\chi$ and $\gamma$. In [Phys. Rev. Lett. 135, 106602 (2025)], we showed that the eMChA in the conduction band of tellurene is polar ($\chi=0$, $\gamma\neq 0$) and emerges from the quantum metric dipole due to its Weyl node and from the lone pair polarization ${\bf P}$. Here, we extend our work to the valence band of tellurene, where the eMChA is usually said to be chiral ($\chi \neq 0, \gamma = 0$). We show that also a polar coefficient $\gamma \neq 0$ emerges naturally through a downfolding procedure, in which remote Weyl-node containing bands induce momentum-space gradients of the quantum metric in the low-energy levels, activating finite metric dipoles. Combining semiclassical Boltzmann transport with a ${\bf k}\cdot{\bf p}$ description of tellurene, our numerical calculations agree quantitatively with doping ($\mu$) dependent second-harmonic measurements of the longitudinal voltage $V^{2\omega}_\parallel(\mu)$ in perpendicular field. The combined chiral and polar characters ($\chi\neq0, \gamma\neq 0)$ of the eMChA in tellurene also explains the shift in the angular ($\phi$) dependence of $V^{2\omega}_\parallel(\phi)$ for in plane fields. Our results demonstrate that the polar eMChA can arise in topologically trivial bands through multiband effects and establishes tellurene as a platform for quantum-geometric rectification in both electron and hole regimes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13721v1 公告类型：新 摘要：单向磁阻或电磁手性各向异性 (eMChA) 是一种在非中心对称导体中出现的非线性磁输运现象，其中电阻 $R(B)$ 的变化为： (i) 手性，$\Delta R(B)/R(0)=2\,\chi\, {\bf I}\cdot{\bf B}$，或 (ii) 极坐标，$\Delta R(B)/R(0)=2\,\gamma\，{\bf I}\cdot({\bf P}\times{\bf B})$，eMChA 系数为 $\chi$ 和 $\gamma$。在[物理。莱特牧师。 135, 106602 (2025)]，我们证明碲烯导带中的 eMChA 是极性的 ($\chi=0$, $\gamma\neq 0$)，并且由于其 Weyl 节点而从量子度量偶极子和孤对极化 ${\bf P}$ 中出现。在这里，我们将工作扩展到碲烯的价带，其中 eMChA 通常被认为是手性的 ($\chi \neq 0, \gamma = 0$)。我们还表明，极性系数 $\gamma \neq 0$ 通过下折叠过程自然出现，其中包含能带的远程 Weyl 节点在低能级引起量子度量的动量空间梯度，激活有限度量偶极子。将半经典玻尔兹曼输运与碲烯的 ${\bf k}\cdot{\bf p}$ 描述相结合，我们的数值计算与垂直场中纵向电压 $V^{2\omega}_\parallel(\mu)$ 的掺杂 ($\mu$) 相关二次谐波测量定量一致。碲烯中 eMChA 的组合手性和极性特征 ($\chi\neq0, \gamma\neq 0)$ 也解释了平面场中 $V^{2\omega}_\parallel(\phi)$ 的角度 ($\phi$) 依赖性的变化。我们的结果表明，极性 eMChA 可以通过多带效应在拓扑平凡能带中出现，并将碲烯建立为电子和空穴体系中量子几何校正的平台。
