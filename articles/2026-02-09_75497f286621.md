## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06591v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The self-assembly of polymer grafted nanoparticles is more and more used in the field of functional materials. However, there is still a lack of analysis on the dynamic transformation paths of different self-assembly morphologies, which makes it impossible to achieve further precise regulation and targeted design in experiments and industrial production. In this work the effects of patchy property, grafted chain length, ratio and grafting density on the self-assembly behavior and structure of polymer grafted flexible patchy nanoparticles are investigated by dissipative particle dynamics simulation method through the construction of coarse-grained model of polymer grafted ternary nanoparticles. The influence and regulation mechanisms of these factors on the self-assembly structure transformation of flexible patchy nanoparticles are systematically studied, and a variety of structures such as dendritic structure, columnar structure, and bilayer membrane are obtained. The self-assembly structure of flexible patchy nanoparticles obtained in this work (such as bilayer membrane structure) provides a potential application basis for designing drug carriers. By precisely regulating the specific structural characteristics of the system, it is possible to achieve efficient loading of drugs and targeted delivery functions, thus significantly improving the bioavailability and effect of drugs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06591v1 发布类型：新增 摘要：聚合物接枝纳米颗粒的自组装技术越来越多地应用于功能材料领域。然而，目前仍缺乏对不同自组装形态动态转化路径的分析，导致无法在实验和工业生产中实现进一步的精准调控和针对性设计。本工作通过构建聚合物接枝三元纳米粒子的粗粒模型，采用耗散粒子动力学模拟方法，研究了片状性质、接枝链长、接枝比例和接枝密度对聚合物接枝柔性片状纳米粒子自组装行为和结构的影响。系统研究了这些因素对柔性片状纳米粒子自组装结构转变的影响和调控机制，获得了树枝状结构、柱状结构、双层膜等多种结构。本工作获得的柔性片状纳米粒子的自组装结构（例如双层膜结构）为设计药物载体提供了潜在的应用基础。通过精确调控系统的具体结构特征，可以实现药物的高效负载和靶向递送功能，从而显着提高药物的生物利用度和疗效。
