## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22310v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A minimal model of a graphene topological insulator heterostructure is considered, where a moire superlattice modulates the Rashba spin orbit interaction. In the spin degenerate, spin orbit free limit, the reduced Brillouin zone contains flat, spin degenerate moire minibands, with periodicity determined by superlattice folding. The inclusion of spin orbit interaction lifts the spin degeneracy and reduces the effective spectral periodicity by a factor of two. Through spin orbit interaction, the moire potential entangles spin, sublattice, and leg degrees of freedom, reshaping the miniband structure in momentum space and generating emergent helicity spectral functions. As the Rashba coupling is renormalized by the moire pattern, it induces helicity fragmentation, in which the helicity weight is distributed across a dense manifold of moire minibands, forming an extended network of helicity carrying states and significantly enhancing helicity fluctuations at the bare response level. The emergence of Dirac like miniband crossings at finite spin orbit interaction demonstrates that moire heterostructures can support relativistic quasiparticles through band reconstruction. This model provides a microscopic mechanism by which proximity induced spin orbit coupling can be amplified via moire engineering.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22310v1 公告类型：新 摘要：考虑石墨烯拓扑绝缘体异质结构的最小模型，其中莫尔超晶格调制 Rashba 自旋轨道相互作用。在自旋简并、自旋轨道自由极限中，缩减的布里渊区包含平坦的自旋简并莫尔微带，其周期性由超晶格折叠决定。自旋轨道相互作用的加入提高了自旋简并性并将有效光谱周期性降低了两倍。通过自旋轨道相互作用，莫尔势纠缠自旋、亚晶格和腿自由度，重塑动量空间中的微带结构并生成涌现的螺旋谱函数。当 Rashba 耦合通过莫尔图案重新归一化时，它会引起螺旋度碎片，其中螺旋权重分布在密集的莫尔微带上，形成螺旋承载态的扩展网络，并显着增强裸响应水平的螺旋度波动。有限自旋轨道相互作用中狄拉克式微带交叉的出现表明莫尔异质结构可以通过能带重建来支持相对论性准粒子。该模型提供了一种微观机制，可以通过莫尔工程放大邻近引起的自旋轨道耦合。
