## Abstract
Nature, Published online: 17 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41586-025-09887-y After spinal cord injury, lesion-remote astrocytes acquire heterogeneous, spatially restricted reactivity states that shape neuroinflammation, neural repair and neurological recovery.
