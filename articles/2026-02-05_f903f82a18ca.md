## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04469v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Defects in semiconductors have recently attracted renewed interest owing to their potential in novel quantum applications. Here we investigate the electronic and magnetic properties induced by 60{\deg} dislocations in Ge. Using large-scale DFT calculations, we determine the band structure for both the shuffle and glide sets in their lowest-energy configurations. We also perform charged-defect calculations to aid in the interpretation of complex photoluminescence spectra observed in epitaxial Ge layers. The band structure for the shuffle set reveals defect-induced dispersive bands localized within the band gap near the {\Gamma} point, whereas for the glide set, we observe strong overlap with the conduction band. Defect-induced band splitting evident away from {\Gamma} reveals Rashba-Dresselhaus spin-orbit coupling, an effect previously reported only for screw dislocations. Remarkably, we find evidence that specific dislocation arrangements can stabilize antiferromagnetic ordering with sizable local magnetic moments and considerable exchange splitting between opposite spin states. These results uncover rich physics in Ge dislocations through the combination of spin-orbit coupling and magnetic ordering, potentially enabling novel defect-based functionalities in Ge devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04469v1 公告类型：新 摘要：半导体缺陷由于其在新型量子应用中的潜力而最近重新引起了人们的兴趣。在这里，我们研究了 Ge 中 60{\deg} 位错引起的电子和磁性。使用大规模 DFT 计算，我们确定了最低能量配置下的洗牌组和滑行组的能带结构。我们还进行带电缺陷计算，以帮助解释在外延 Ge 层中观察到的复杂光致发光光谱。洗牌组的能带结构揭示了缺陷引起的色散带位于{\Gamma}点附近的带隙内，而对于滑移组，我们观察到与导带的强烈重叠。缺陷引起的能带分裂明显远离 {\Gamma}，揭示了 Rashba-Dresselhaus 自旋轨道耦合，这种效应以前只报道过螺旋位错。值得注意的是，我们发现证据表明特定的位错排列可以稳定反铁磁有序性，具有相当大的局部磁矩和相反自旋态之间相当大的交换分裂。这些结果通过自旋轨道耦合和磁有序的结合揭示了Ge位错中丰富的物理现象，有可能在Ge器件中实现基于缺陷的新型功能。
