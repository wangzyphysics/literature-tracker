## Abstract
Cluster-assembled materials (CAMS) are an emerging category of modular solids that interconnect atomic precision with functional tunability. This work highlights ligand-induced dimensionality control in Ag12 cluster-based assemblies, achieved through functionalization of the organic linker. Two distinct cluster assemblies were fabricated from the geometrically distinct Ag12 core utilizing pyrazine and 2 amino-pyrazine linkers. A three-dimensional (SSICG20) framework was achieved through the accumulation of Ag12 cluster core pyrazine as a bridging ligand, while the latter one constructs a two-dimensional (SSICG21) sheet-like structure. Both frameworks exhibit distinct architectures with modulating charge transfer capability, particularly evidenced through their applicability in the charge transport measurements via linear device preparation and electro catalytic urea oxidation reaction. In investigations of the I-V measurements, both SSICG20 and SSICG21 demonstrated stable ON/OFF ratios throughout 1000 cycles, followed by SSICG20 showing superior memory response. Aligned with its superior switching response, SSICG20 showed promising catalytic efficiency in the electro catalytic urea oxidation reaction in harsh alkaline seawater conditions, with a significantly reduced onset potential of 110 mV at a current density of 10 mA, surpassing its 2D counterpart. A sustainable solar-driven overall water electrolysis experiment was also employed, which showed quite an optimistic efficiency. Collectively, this work provides a fundamental insight into the interplay of charge transportation on silver cluster assembly via dimensionality Modulation.

## 摘要（中文）
簇组装材料（CAMS）是一类新兴的模块化固体，它将原子精度与功能可调性联系起来。这项工作强调了基于 Ag12 簇的组装中配体诱导的维度控制，这是通过有机连接体的功能化实现的。利用吡嗪和 2 个氨基-吡嗪连接体，从几何形状不同的 Ag12 核心制造出两个不同的簇组件。通过Ag12簇核吡嗪作为桥连配体的积累实现了三维（SSICG20）框架，而后者构建了二维（SSICG21）片状结构。两种框架都表现出具有调节电荷转移能力的独特架构，特别是通过线性器件制备和电催化尿素氧化反应在电荷转移测量中的适用性证明了这一点。在 I-V 测量研究中，SSICG20 和 SSICG21 在 1000 个周期内均表现出稳定的开/关比，其次是 SSICG20，显示出卓越的记忆响应。与其卓越的开关响应相一致，SSICG20 在恶劣的碱性海水条件下的电催化尿素氧化反应中表现出良好的催化效率，在 10 mA 的电流密度下，起始电位显着降低至 110 mV，超过了其 2D 同类产品。还采用了可持续的太阳能驱动的整体水电解实验，显示出相当乐观的效率。总的来说，这项工作通过维度调制提供了对银簇组装上电荷传输相互作用的基本见解。
