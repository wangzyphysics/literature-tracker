## Abstract
Nature Electronics, Published online: 18 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41928-025-01513-z An 18-gram haptic feedback ring can deliver powerful force sensations while detecting multi-directional touch inputs, potentially transforming the way we can interact with digital environments.
