## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01270v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Plasma activated water (PAW) offers a sustainable, nonthermal solution for degrading persistent organic pollutants in industrial effluents. This study employed a gliding arc plasma system to generate PAW for treating diluted waste water containing dyes, pesticides, and pharmaceuticals. Experimental parameters such as exposure time, dilution ratio, and pollutant concentration were varied, with analysis conducted using UV Vis spectroscopy, HPLC, TOC, and COD. Results showed high degradation efficiencies, up to 90% for dyes, 85% for pesticides, and 80% for pharmaceuticals following pseudo first order kinetics driven by hydroxyl and nitrate or nitrite radicals. The findings demonstrate PAWs potential as a green, scalable wastewater treatment strategy that minimizes chemical use, supports water reuse, and enhances environmental safety, with future scope for pilot scale applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01270v1 公告类型：新 摘要：等离子体活化水 (PAW) 为降解工业废水中的持久性有机污染物提供了一种可持续的非热解决方案。本研究采用滑动电弧等离子体系统来产生 PAW，用于处理含有染料、农药和药品的稀释废水。改变暴露时间、稀释比和污染物浓度等实验参数，并使用紫外可见光谱、高效液相色谱、TOC 和 COD 进行分析。结果显示，遵循由羟基和硝酸盐或亚硝酸盐自由基驱动的伪一级动力学，染料的降解效率高达 90%，农药的降解效率高达 85%，药物的降解效率高达 80%。研究结果表明，PAW 作为一种绿色、可扩展的废水处理策略的潜力，可以最大限度地减少化学品的使用，支持水的再利用，并提高环境安全，并具有未来中试规模应用的空间。
