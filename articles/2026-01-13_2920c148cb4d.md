## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06820v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Efficient exploration of vast compositional and processing spaces is essential for accelerated materials discovery. Bayesian optimization (BO) provides a principled strategy for identifying optimal materials with minimal experiments, yet its adoption in materials science is hindered by implementation complexity and limited domain-specific tools. Here, we present Bgolearn, a comprehensive Python framework that makes BO accessible and practical for materials research through an intuitive interface, robust algorithms, and materials-oriented workflows. Bgolearn supports both single-objective and multi-objective Bayesian optimization with multiple acquisition functions (e.g., expected improvement, upper confidence bound, probability of improvement, and expected hypervolume improvement etc.), diverse surrogate models (including Gaussian processes, random forests, and gradient boosting etc.), and bootstrap-based uncertainty quantification. Benchmark studies show that Bgolearn reduces the number of required experiments by 40-60% compared with random search, grid search, and genetic algorithms, while maintaining comparable or superior solution quality. Its effectiveness is demonstrated not only through the studies presented in this paper, such as the identification of maximum-elastic-modulus triply periodic minimal surface structures, ultra-high-hardness high-entropy alloys, and high-strength, high-ductility medium-Mn steels, but also by numerous publications that have proven its impact in material discovery. With a modular architecture that integrates seamlessly into existing materials workflows and a graphical user interface (BgoFace) that removes programming barriers, Bgolearn establishes a practical and reliable platform for Bayesian optimization in materials science, and is openly available at https://github.com/Bin-Cao/Bgolearn.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06820v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对广阔的成分和加工空间的有效探索对于加速材料发现至关重要。贝叶斯优化 (BO) 提供了一种通过最少的实验来识别最佳材料的原则性策略，但其在材料科学中的采用却因实施复杂性和有限的特定领域工具而受到阻碍。在这里，我们介绍 Bgolearn，这是一个全面的 Python 框架，它通过直观的界面、强大的算法和面向材料的工作流程使 BO 易于使用且实用，可用于材料研究。 Bgolearn 支持单目标和多目标贝叶斯优化，具有多种获取函数（例如预期改进、置信上限、改进概率和预期超体积改进等）、多种代理模型（包括高斯过程、随机森林和梯度提升等）以及基于 bootstrap 的不确定性量化。基准研究表明，与随机搜索、网格搜索和遗传算法相比，Bgolearn 减少了 40-60% 所需的实验数量，同时保持了相当或更高的解决方案质量。其有效性不仅通过本文提出的研究得到证明，例如最大弹性模量三周期最小表面结构、超高硬度高熵合金和高强度、高延展性中锰钢的识别，而且还通过许多出版物证明了其在材料发现中的影响。 Bgolearn 具有无缝集成到现有材料工作流程中的模块化架构和消除编程障碍的图形用户界面 (BgoFace)，为材料科学中的贝叶斯优化建立了实用且可靠的平台，并可在 https://github.com/Bin-Cao/Bgolearn 上公开获取。
