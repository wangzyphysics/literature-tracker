## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09546v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate spin-wave propagation in magnetic insulators in the presence of lattice dislocations. Within a continuum magnetoelastic framework, we show that the strain fields generated by dislocations induce equilibrium magnetic textures. The morphology of these textures depends sensitively on the dislocation type and acts as a localized scattering potential for spin-wave excitations. As a result, the scattering response exhibits pronounced asymmetries and interference effects governed by the magnetoelastic coupling and the dislocation type. By combining numerical simulations with analytical scattering theory, we compute differential cross sections and frequency-dependent transmission coefficients. Furthermore, analysis of the effective potential landscape reveals that the defect forms a barrier that modulates spin-wave transport and, crucially, breaks the intrinsic reflectionless nature of magnetic domain walls. Our findings identify lattice dislocations as tunable scattering centers, opening new avenues for defect engineering in magnonic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09546v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究存在晶格位错的情况下磁绝缘体中的自旋波传播。在连续磁致弹性框架内，我们表明位错产生的应变场会引起平衡磁织构。这些纹理的形态敏感地取决于位错类型，并充当自旋波激发的局部散射势。因此，散射响应表现出明显的不对称性和由磁弹性耦合和位错类型控制的干涉效应。通过将数值模拟与分析散射理论相结合，我们计算微分横截面和频率相关的传输系数。此外，对有效势景观的分析表明，缺陷形成了调节自旋波传输的势垒，并且最重要的是，打破了磁畴壁固有的无反射性质。我们的研究结果将晶格位错确定为可调散射中心，为磁波器件中的缺陷工程开辟了新途径。
