## Abstract
Nano-structured optomechanical crystals (OMC) form an interface between mechanical modes with long coherence times and telecom optical photons, ideal for long-distance distribution of quantum information. However, the implementation of scalable quantum networks based on OMCs has been inhibited by thermal mechanical noise. Here, we overcome this limitation using a quasi-two-dimensional OMC and generate single photons via single phonon-photon conversion. In this work, we verify the low thermal noise and high purity of the generated single photons through a Hanbury Brown-Twiss experiment with\({g}^{(2)}(0)=0.3{5}_{-0.08}^{+0.10}\). We perform Hong-Ou-Mandel interference of the emitted photons showcasing the indistinguishability and coherence with visibilityV= 0.52 ± 0.15 after 1.43 km fiber delay. Lastly, we use two-photon interference to measure the temporal wavepackets of optomechanically generated single photons demonstrating narrow bandwidths as low as 10 MHz. Our results pave the way for multinode quantum networks of mechanical oscillators and hybrid entanglement generation between mechanical oscillators and telecom quantum emitters.

## 摘要（中文）
纳米结构光机械晶体 (OMC) 在长相干时间的机械模式和电信光学光子之间形成了接口，非常适合量子信息的长距离分发。然而，基于 OMC 的可扩展量子网络的实现一直受到热机械噪声的抑制。在这里，我们使用准二维 OMC 克服了这一限制，并通过单声子-光子转换生成单光子。在这项工作中，我们通过 Hanbury Brown-Twiss 实验验证了生成的单光子的低热噪声和高纯度。\({g}^{(2)}(0)=0.3{5}_{-0.08}^{+0.10}\)。我们对发射的光子进行 Hong-Ou-Mandel 干涉，展示了 1.43 km 光纤延迟后可见度 V= 0.52 ± 0.15 的不可区分性和相干性。最后，我们使用双光子干涉来测量光机械生成的单光子的时间波包，显示出低至 10 MHz 的窄带宽。我们的结果为机械振荡器的多节点量子网络以及机械振荡器和电信量子发射器之间的混合纠缠生成铺平了道路。
