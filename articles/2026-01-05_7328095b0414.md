## Abstract
Author(s): K. Gofryk, S. Zhou, N. Poudel, N. Dice, D. Murray, T. Pavlov, and C. Marianetti Using a combination of plasma focused ion beam micromachining, low-temperature magnetotransport measurements, and first-principles calculations, this study demonstrates that PuB 6 exhibits hallmark characteristics of a topological Kondo insulator, including a transition in electrical resistivity from high-temperature thermally activated behavior, indicative of a narrow gap at the Fermi level, to a distinctive low-temperature resistivity plateau. Furthermore, the observed surface-to-volume dependence of resistivity at low temperatures provides evidence for the presence of topologically protected surface states. [Phys. Rev. Research 8, L012002] Published Mon Jan 05, 2026

作者：K. Gofryk、S. Zhou、N. Poudel、N. Dice、D. Murray、T. Pavlov 和 C. Marianetti 这项研究结合等离子体聚焦离子束微加工、低温磁输运测量和第一性原理计算，证明 PuB 6 表现出拓扑 Kondo 绝缘体的标志性特征，包括高温热激活行为引起的电阻率转变，表明费米能级处存在狭窄间隙，直至形成独特的低温电阻率平台。此外，观察到的低温电阻率的表面与体积依赖性为拓扑保护表面态的存在提供了证据。 [物理。 Rev. Research 8, L012002] 2026 年 1 月 5 日星期一发布
