## Abstract
arXiv:2512.14793v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: First-principles density functional simulations were employed to investigate the geometries, electrical properties, and hyperfine structures of various beryllium-doped diamond configurations, including interstitial (Be$_i$), substitutional (Be$_s$), and beryllium-nitrogen (Be-N) complexes. The incorporation of Be into the diamond lattice is more favorable as a substitutional dopant than as an interstitial dopant, although both processes are endothermic. Interstitial Be could potentially exhibit motional averaging from planar to axial symmetry with an activation energy of 0.1 eV. The most stable Be$_s$ configuration has $T_{d}$ symmetry with a spin state of $S=1$. Co-doping with nitrogen reduces the formation energy of Be$_s$-N$_{n}$ $(n=1-4)$ complexes, which further decreases as the number of nitrogen atoms increases. This is attributed to the smaller covalent radius of nitrogen compared to carbon, resulting in reduced lattice distortion. Be$_s$-N$_3$ and Be$_s$-N$_4$ co-doping introduces shallow donors, while Be$_s$ exhibits $n$-type semiconductivity, but the deep donor level renders it impractical for room-temperature applications. These findings provide valuable insights into the behavior of beryllium as a dopant in diamond and highlight the potential of beryllium-nitrogen co-doping for achieving $n$-type diamond semiconductors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.14793v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：采用第一原理密度泛函模拟来研究各种铍掺杂金刚石构型的几何形状、电学性质和超精细结构，包括填隙 (Be$_i$)、替代 (Be$_s$) 和铍氮 (Be-N) 配合物。 Be 并入金刚石晶格作为替代掺杂剂比作为间隙掺杂剂更有利，尽管这两种过程都是吸热的。间隙 Be 可能表现出从平面对称到轴对称的运动平均，激活能为 0.1 eV。最稳定的 Be$_s$ 配置具有 $T_{d}$ 对称性，自旋状态为 $S=1$。与氮共掺杂降低了Be$_s$-N$_{n}$ $(n=1-4)$配合物的形成能，并且随着氮原子数量的增加进一步降低。这是由于氮的共价半径比碳更小，从而减少了晶格畸变。 Be$_s$-N$_3$ 和 Be$_s$-N$_4$ 共掺杂引入了浅施主，而 Be$_s$ 表现出 $n$ 型半导体性，但深施主能级使其在室温应用中不切实际。这些发现为了解铍作为金刚石中的掺杂剂的行为提供了宝贵的见解，并强调了铍-氮共掺杂在实现 $n$ 型金刚石半导体方面的潜力。
