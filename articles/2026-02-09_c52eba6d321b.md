## Abstract
arXiv:2510.09327v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The design and fabrication of room-temperature ferrotoroidic materials and magnetic semiconductors are recognized worldwide as a great challenge, and of both theoretical and practical importance in the field of condensed matter physics and information storage. Reported herein are ferrotoroidic crystal powder and film formed by supramolecular self-assembly based on uranyl and cyclodextrin, with the Curie temperature above room temperature. Experimental measurements and calculations demonstrate spontaneous vortex-like alignment of magnetic moments and furthermore a macroscopic long-range arrangement in the crystal, which breaks simultaneously space-inversion and time-reversal symmetries, exhibiting strong superexchange, spin-orbit coupling as well as anomalous Hall effect (AHE). The electrical measurements show the film with a superhigh carrier mobility of 3200 cm2*V-1*s-1 and a Hall resistivity as high as 0.32 mV*A-1*cm at room temperature. This work is expected to pave greatly the applied research on new-generation magnetoresistive random access memory (MRAM), especially as flexible material.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.09327v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：室温铁磁材料和磁性半导体的设计和制造被全世界公认为是一项巨大的挑战，在凝聚态物理和信息存储领域具有重要的理论和实践意义。本文报道了基于双氧铀和环糊精通过超分子自组装形成的铁环晶体粉末和薄膜，其居里温度高于室温。实验测量和计算证明了磁矩的自发涡旋状排列以及晶体中的宏观长程排列，它同时打破了空间反转和时间反转对称性，表现出强烈的超交换、自旋轨道耦合以及反常霍尔效应（AHE）。电学测量表明，该薄膜在室温下具有3200 cm2*V-1*s-1的超高载流子迁移率和高达0.32 mV*A-1*cm的霍尔电阻率。这项工作有望为新一代磁阻随机存取存储器（MRAM），特别是作为柔性材料的应用研究铺平道路。
