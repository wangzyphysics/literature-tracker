## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14790v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnets are collinear magnetic materials with 'alter'nating local crystalline environments, characterized by joint spin and crystalline symmetries that enable ferromagnetic-like transport properties but with vanishing net magnetization. Hexagonal FeS (h-FeS) is a recently identified altermagnet candidate that shows a spontaneous anomalous Hall effect (AHE) accompanied by a tiny net magnetization. Here, we show that both the spontaneous AHE and magnetization can be effectively suppressed by an in-plane compressive strain. Since neutron diffraction measurements show that the applied uniaxial strain only modifies the in-plane domain population but does not affect the in-plane magnetic structure, the major effect of the applied strain is to tune the small $c$-axis ferromagnetic moment. Our results demonstrate a strong correlation between the tiny net magnetization and the spontaneous AHE in h-FeS, and show that uniaxial strain provides an effective knob to tune both properties in this altermagnet candidate for spintronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14790v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交变磁体是具有“交替”局部晶体环境的共线磁性材料，其特征是联合自旋和晶体对称性，可实现类似铁磁的输运特性，但净磁化强度消失。六方 FeS (h-FeS) 是最近发现的一种候选交替磁体，它表现出自发的反常霍尔效应 (AHE)，并伴有微小的净磁化强度。在这里，我们证明自发 AHE 和磁化强度都可以通过面内压缩应变得到有效抑制。由于中子衍射测量表明，所施加的单轴应变仅改变面内磁畴布居，但不影响面内磁结构，因此所施加应变的主要作用是调整小$c$轴铁磁矩。我们的结果表明，h-FeS 中微小的净磁化强度与自发 AHE 之间存在很强的相关性，并表明单轴应变提供了一个有效的旋钮来调整这种用于自旋电子应用的交替磁体候选物的两种特性。
