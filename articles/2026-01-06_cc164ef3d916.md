## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01367v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Low-energy heavy-ion beams are fundamentally limited by severe space-charge divergence, which constrains the transportable beam current to a few microamperes in conventional electrostatic accelerators. This limitation is particularly critical for high-mass ions, where the generalized perveance increases rapidly because of their low velocity. Here, we demonstrate that this apparent space-charge limit can be overcome by shaping the electrostatic potential configuration of an existing multistage accelerator, thereby transforming the acceleration column itself into a combined acceleration-focusing column. By optimizing the interstage voltage configuration, a strong electrostatic lens effect is superimposed on the accelerating field to counteract space-charge-driven expansion. We formulate a generalized design framework that quantitatively maps the transport 'design window' in terms of beam current, ion mass, and acceleration voltage. For gold ions at 64 keV, this approach enables stable transport of beam currents exceeding 100 microA, more than an order of magnitude higher than the conventional limit. Numerical phase-space analysis shows that this improvement is achieved by prioritizing envelope control over emittance preservation, a trade-off intrinsic to space-charge-dominated regimes. Our results establish a universal and practical guideline for high-current heavy-ion beam transport, relevant to fusion plasma diagnostics, ion implantation, and massive molecular ion applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01367v1 公告类型：新 摘要：低能重离子束从根本上受到严重的空间电荷发散的限制，这将传统静电加速器中的可传输束流限制在几微安。这种限制对于高质量离子尤其重要，因为其低速度，广义渗透率迅速增加。在这里，我们证明了可以通过塑造现有多级加速器的静电势配置来克服这种明显的空间电荷限制，从而将加速柱本身转变为组合的加速聚焦柱。通过优化级间电压配置，在加速场上叠加强静电透镜效应，以抵消空间电荷驱动的膨胀。我们制定了一个通用的设计框架，可以根据束流、离子质量和加速电压定量映射传输“设计窗口”。对于 64 keV 的金离子，这种方法能够稳定传输超过 100 微安的束流，比传统极限高出一个数量级以上。数值相空间分析表明，这种改进是通过优先考虑包络控制而不是发射度保持来实现的，这是空间电荷主导体系固有的权衡。我们的研究结果为高电流重离子束传输建立了通用且实用的指南，与聚变等离子体诊断、离子注入和大分子离子应用相关。
