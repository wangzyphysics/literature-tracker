## Abstract
arXiv:2507.17386v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We present a simple theory accounting for two central observations in a recent experiment on quantum coarsening and collective dynamics on a programmable quantum simulator [T. Manovitz et al., Nature \textbf{638}, 86 (2025)]: an apparent speeding up of the coarsening process as the phase transition is approached; and persistent oscillations of the order parameter after quenches within the ordered phase. Our theory, based on the Hamiltonian structure of the equations of motion in the classical limit of the quantum model, finds a speeding up already deep within the ordered phase, with subsequent slowing down as the domain wall tension vanishes upon approaching the critical line. Further, the oscillations are captured within a mean-field treatment of the order parameter field. For quenches within the ordered phase, small spatially-varying fluctuations in the initial mean-field lead to a remarkable long-time effect, wherein the system dynamically destroys its long-range order and has to coarsen to re-establish it. We term this phenomenon \emph{symmetry re-breaking}, as the resulting late-time magnetization can have a sign opposite to the initial magnetization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.17386v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一个简单的理论，解释了最近在可编程量子模拟器上进行的量子粗化和集体动力学实验中的两个中心观察结果 [T. Manovitz et al., Nature \textbf{638}, 86 (2025)]：随着相变的接近，粗化过程明显加速；以及有序相内淬火后有序参数的持续振荡。我们的理论基于量子模型经典极限中运动方程的哈密顿结构，发现有序相内部已经加速，随后随着畴壁张力在接近临界线时消失而减速。此外，在阶次参数场的平均场处理中捕获振荡。对于有序相内的猝灭，初始平均场中微小的空间变化波动会导致显着的长期效应，其中系统动态地破坏其长程有序并必须粗化以重新建立它。我们将这种现象称为 \emph{对称性重新破缺}，因为由此产生的后期磁化强度可能具有与初始磁化强度相反的符号。
