## Abstract
Enzymes are powerful and sustainable catalysts, but their widespread application is limited by the difficulty of identifying functional starting points for optimization, creating a major bottleneck in early- stage biocatalyst discovery. Designing libraries of such starting enzymes remains particularly challenging. Here, we use the GenSLM protein language model to generate novelβ-subunit of tryptophan synthase (TrpB) enzymes that express inEscherichia coliand are both stable and catalytically active. Many generated TrpBs also display significant substrate promiscuity, outperforming their natural counterparts on non-native substrates. Some even surpass laboratory-evolved TrpBs. Comparison of the most-active and most-promiscuous generated TrpB to its closest natural homolog confirms that the enhanced versatility is absent from the natural enzyme, highlighting the creative potential of generative models. These results demonstrate that the generated TrpBs not only preserve natural structure and function but also acquire non-natural properties, establishing generative models as powerful tools for biocatalyst discovery and engineering.

## 摘要（中文）
酶是强大且可持续的催化剂，但其广泛应用受到难以确定优化功能起点的限制，从而成为早期生物催化剂发现的主要瓶颈。设计此类起始酶的文库仍然特别具有挑战性。在这里，我们使用 GenSLM 蛋白质语言模型生成新的色氨酸合酶 (TrpB) β 亚基，这些酶在大肠杆菌中表达，既稳定又具有催化活性。许多生成的 TrpB 还表现出显着的底物混杂性，在非天然底物上的表现优于其天然对应物。有些甚至超过了实验室进化的TrpB。将最活跃和最混杂的生成的 TrpB 与其最接近的天然同源物进行比较，证实天然酶不具有增强的多功能性，这凸显了生成模型的创造潜力。这些结果表明，生成的 TrpB 不仅保留了自然结构和功能，而且还获得了非自然特性，建立了生成模型作为生物催化剂发现和工程的强大工具。
