## Abstract
arXiv:2510.24903v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: In this work, we examine the conditions for the emergence of chimera-like states in Ising systems. We study an Ising chain with periodic boundaries in contact with a thermal bath at temperature T, that induces stochastic changes in spin variables. To capture the non-locality needed for chimera formation, we introduce a model setup with non-local diffusion of spin values through the whole system. More precisely, diffusion is modeled through spin-exchange interactions between units up to a distance R, using Kawasaki dynamics. This setup mimics, e.g., neural media, as the brain, in the presence of electrical (diffusive) interactions. We explored the influence of such non-local dynamics on the emergence of complex spatiotemporal synchronization patterns of activity. Depending on system parameters we report here for the first time chimera-like states in the Ising model, characterized by relatively stable moving domains of spins with different local magnetization. We analyzed the system at T=0, both analytically and via simulations and computed the system's phase diagram, revealing rich behavior: regions with only chimeras, coexistence of chimeras and stable domains, and metastable chimeras that decay into uniform stable domains. This study offers fundamental insights into how coherent and incoherent synchronization patterns can arise in complex networked systems as it is, e.g., the brain.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.24903v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在这项工作中，我们研究了伊辛系统中出现类嵌合态的条件。我们研究了具有周期性边界的伊辛链，该链与温度为 T 的热浴接触，这会引起自旋变量的随机变化。为了捕获嵌合体形成所需的非局域性，我们引入了一种模型设置，其自旋值在整个系统中进行非局域扩散。更准确地说，扩散是使用川崎动力学通过距离 R 的单元之间的自旋交换相互作用进行建模的。这种设置在存在电（扩散）相互作用的情况下模仿神经媒体等大脑。我们探讨了这种非局部动态对复杂的时空同步活动模式的出现的影响。根据系统参数，我们首次在伊辛模型中报告类似嵌合体的状态，其特征是具有不同局部磁化强度的相对稳定的自旋移动域。我们通过分析和模拟分析了 T=0 时的系统，并计算了系统的相图，揭示了丰富的行为：只有嵌合体的区域、嵌合体和稳定域的共存，以及衰变为均匀稳定域的亚稳态嵌合体。这项研究提供了关于如何在复杂的网络系统（例如大脑）中出现相干和不相干同步模式的基本见解。
