## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21385v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: While quantum simulators promise to explore quantum many-body physics beyond classical computation, their capabilities are limited by the available native interactions in the hardware. On many platforms, accessible Hamiltonians are largely restricted to one- and two-body interactions, limiting access to multi-body Hamiltonians and to systems governed by simultaneous, non-commuting interaction terms that are central to condensed matter, quantum chemistry, and high-energy physics. We introduce and experimentally demonstrate a hybrid digital-analog protocol that overcomes these limitations by embedding analog evolution between shallow entangling-gate layers. This method produces effective Hamiltonians with simultaneous non-commuting three- and four-body interactions that are generated non-perturbatively and without Trotter error -- capabilities not practically attainable on near-term hardware using purely digital or purely analog schemes. We implement our scheme on a trapped-ion quantum processor and use it to realize a topological spin chain exhibiting prethermal strong zero modes persisting at high temperature, as well as models featuring three- and four-body interactions. Our hardware-agnostic and scalable method opens new routes to realizing complex many-body physics across quantum platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21385v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：虽然量子模拟器承诺探索经典计算之外的量子多体物理，但它们的功能受到硬件中可用的本机交互的限制。在许多平台上，可访问的哈密顿量在很大程度上仅限于一体和两体相互作用，从而限制了对多体哈密顿量以及受同时、非交换相互作用项控制的系统的访问，这些相互作用项是凝聚态物质、量子化学和高能物理的核心。我们引入并通过实验演示了一种混合数模协议，该协议通过在浅纠缠门层之间嵌入模拟演化来克服这些限制。这种方法产生有效的哈密顿量，具有同时非交换的三体和四体相互作用，这些相互作用是非扰动生成的并且没有特罗特误差——使用纯数字或纯模拟方案在近期硬件上实际上无法实现这种能力。我们在俘获离子量子处理器上实现了我们的方案，并用它来实现拓扑自旋链，该拓扑自旋链表现出在高温下持续存在的预热强零模式，以及具有三体和四体相互作用的模型。我们与硬件无关且可扩展的方法开辟了跨量子平台实现复杂多体物理的新途径。
