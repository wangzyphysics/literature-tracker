## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22537v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the Ferrofluid Drop Targeting (FDT) for the treatment of the Retinal Detachment (RD), considering, for the first time, the real 3D geometry of an eye and magnets configurations as well as the viscoelastic rheology of the medium, i.e., the Vitreous Humor (VH). A Front-Tracking Method (FTM) is extended to handle a general 3D unstructured Eulerian grid and strong wall effects. The challenges include the accuracy and robustness of the solver when the drop spreads on the retina under the effect of a magnetic field, which necessitates the design of a multi-region Eulerian grid and defining a threshold distance between the front and wall, along with the choice of an effective front smoothing and volume correction FTM sub-algorithms near the walls. After model validations, the effect of different design parameters on important objectives, such as the travel time, settling time, retinal coverage area, and impact compressive stress, are studied. The results reveal that, in addition to the magnetic Bond number, the ratio of the drop-to-VH magnetic permeabilities plays a key role in the terminal shape parameters, like the retinal coverage. Additionally, simultaneously increasing these two parameters, significantly increase the total FDT force, coverage area, and stress concentration, while decreasing the drop-VH surface tension can mitigate the stress concentration on the retina.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22537v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了用于治疗视网膜脱离 (RD) 的铁磁流体滴靶向 (FDT)，首次考虑了眼睛的真实 3D 几何结构和磁体配置以及介质（即玻璃体液 (VH)）的粘弹性流变学。前向跟踪方法 (FTM) 被扩展以处理一般的 3D 非结构化欧拉网格和强墙效应。挑战包括当水滴在磁场作用下在视网膜上扩散时求解器的准确性和鲁棒性，这需要设计多区域欧拉网格并定义正面和墙壁之间的阈值距离，以及选择有效的正面平滑和靠近墙壁的体积校正FTM子算法。模型验证后，研究了不同设计参数对重要目标（例如行程时间、稳定时间、视网膜覆盖面积和冲击压应力）的影响。结果表明，除了磁键数之外，下降磁导率与 VH 磁导率的比率在终端形状参数（如视网膜覆盖范围）中起着关键作用。此外，同时增加这两个参数，显着增加总FDT力、覆盖面积和应力集中，同时降低drop-VH表面张力可以减轻视网膜上的应力集中。
