## Abstract
Author(s): Chuntian Cao, Arun Kingan, Ryan C. Hill, Jason Kuang, Lei Wang, Chunyi Zhang, Matthew R. Carbone, Hubertus van Dam, Shinjae Yoo, Shan Yan, Esther S. Takeuchi, Kenneth J. Takeuchi, Xifan Wu, AM Milinda Abeykoon, Amy C. Marschilok, and Deyu Lu A neural network potential model is developed for ZnCl 2 electrolytes that provides atomic scale insights into the solvation structure and ionic conductivity. The results agree well with experiment and shed light on the performance of aqueous zinc-ion batteries across a wide concentration range of ZnCl 2 electrolytes. [PRX Energy 4, 023004] Published Fri Apr 11, 2025
