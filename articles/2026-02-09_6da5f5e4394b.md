## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06324v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Antiperovskites share the same structure as perovskites, but allow completely different chemistries and nominal charge states of anions to be stabilized. This gives rise to many interesting phenomena, including septet superconductivity and topological crystalline insulating phases in these systems. Despite this, the work on the crystal structural trends in these compounds are more limited compared to perovskites. In this study, we consider the family of antiperovskite oxides with tetrel elements (Si, Ge, Sn, Pb) and alkaline earth metals (Ca, Sr, Ba), and perform a detailed study of their crystal structures using first principles density functional theory. We show how tolerance factor arguments can be constructed to predict their structure in a way parallel to the perovskites, and furthermore, how heterostructuring (or cation-order) can be used to induce ferroelectricity in these systems which may provide an experimental knob to modify electronic structure. We conclude by a discussion of the electronic structure of antiperovskites, and show that they display interesting trends not observed in regular perovskites, including significant antibonding interactions between face-center ions, which might need to be taken into account building effective electronic models of these compounds.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06324v1 公告类型：新 摘要：反钙钛矿与钙钛矿具有相同的结构，但允许稳定完全不同的化学性质和阴离子的标称电荷状态。这产生了许多有趣的现象，包括这些系统中的七重超导性和拓扑晶体绝缘相。尽管如此，与钙钛矿相比，这些化合物晶体结构趋势的研究更加有限。在这项研究中，我们考虑了具有四元素（Si、Ge、Sn、Pb）和碱土金属（Ca、Sr、Ba）的反钙钛矿氧化物家族，并使用第一原理密度泛函理论对其晶体结构进行了详细研究。我们展示了如何构建容差因子参数来以与钙钛矿平行的方式预测其结构，此外，如何使用异质结构（或阳离子顺序）在这些系统中诱导铁电性，这可能提供修改电子结构的实验旋钮。最后，我们讨论了反钙钛矿的电子结构，并表明它们表现出在常规钙钛矿中未观察到的有趣趋势，包括面心离子之间显着的反键相互作用，在建立这些化合物的有效电子模型时可能需要考虑到这一点。
