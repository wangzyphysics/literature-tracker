## Abstract
Staphylococcus aureus is a major human pathogen responsible for a broad spectrum of infections and is recognized by the World Health Organization as a high-priority antimicrobial-resistant organism. The global transcription factor (TF) catabolite control protein A (CcpA), a central regulator of metabolism and virulence, represents a promising drug target. However, efforts to design inhibitors are hindered by their closed crystallographic conformation and the absence of well-defined ligand-binding cavities. Here, we present a structure-guided alignment strategy to identify potential ligand-binding sites in CcpA from S. aureus (Sa-CcpA), using a curated set of co-crystallized TFs as structural templates. This methodology identified a putative ligand-binding site in Sa-CcpA that was not predicted by conventional cavity-detection methods. Phylogenetic analysis showed that residues within this predicted cavity are conserved in a subset of particular pathogenic species in the order Bacillales, suggesting an evolutionarily constrained and functionally relevant region. To assess the stability of this newly identified site, multiple molecular dynamics simulations were performed, followed by docking analyses of two known Sa-CcpA inhibitors. Together, our results suggest that this structure-informed alignment strategy is a promising approach to uncover previously unrecognized ligand-binding regions in TFs. Although demonstrated here using Sa-CcpA as a case study, this approach may support future efforts to rationalize inhibitor design targeting transcriptional regulators.

## 摘要（中文）
金黄色葡萄球菌是一种主要的人类病原体，可引起广泛的感染，并被世界卫生组织认定为高度优先的耐药微生物。全局转录因子 (TF) 分解代谢物控制蛋白 A (CcpA) 是代谢和毒力的中心调节因子，是一个有前途的药物靶点。然而，设计抑制剂的努力因其封闭的晶体构象和缺乏明确的配体结合腔而受到阻碍。在这里，我们提出了一种结构引导的比对策略，使用一组精心设计的共结晶 TF 作为结构模板，识别金黄色葡萄球菌 (Sa-CcpA) CcpA 中潜在的配体结合位点。该方法鉴定了 Sa-CcpA 中一个推定的配体结合位点，这是传统空腔检测方法无法预测的。系统发育分析表明，这个预测的空腔内的残基在芽孢杆菌目的特定致病物种的子集中是保守的，这表明这是一个进化受限且功能相关的区域。为了评估这个新识别位点的稳定性，进行了多次分子动力学模拟，然后对两种已知的 Sa-CcpA 抑制剂进行了对接分析。总之，我们的结果表明，这种基于结构的比对策略是一种很有前途的方法，可以发现 TF 中以前未识别的配体结合区域。尽管此处使用 Sa-CcpA 作为案例研究进行了演示，但这种方法可能支持未来合理化针对转录调节因子的抑制剂设计的努力。
