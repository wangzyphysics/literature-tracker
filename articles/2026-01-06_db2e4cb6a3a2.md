## Abstract
arXiv:2510.21681v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: After major disasters, formal inquiries become arenas where responsibility is publicly contested. While extensive research has examined blame attribution through qualitative and actor-centred approaches, the relational structure of blame within formal accountability processes remains poorly understood. Using evidence from the Grenfell Tower Inquiry, this study analyses the web of blame presented during the Phase 2 closing proceedings, in which Counsel to the Inquiry synthesised how core participants publicly attributed responsibility to one another. We represent this synthesis as a directed network and examine its structural properties using standard tools from network analysis. The resulting configuration is interconnected, with pronounced reciprocity and local clustering, indicating that responsibility claims were articulated within a dense institutional environment rather than as isolated, one-to-one accusations. Comparisons with neutral benchmark models show that several observed features depart from expectations based on simple structural constraints alone, revealing patterned organisation in the public articulation of blame within the Inquiry. By applying network-analytic methods to an institutionalised representation of blame attribution, this study provides a systematic structural account of responsibility relations in a major public inquiry. The findings contribute to research on crisis governance and blame avoidance by demonstrating how accountability processes generate patterned distributions of responsibility that reflect institutional arrangements and governance contexts.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.21681v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：重大灾难发生后，正式调查成为公开争夺责任的舞台。虽然大量研究通过定性和以行为者为中心的方法检验了责任归因，但正式问责流程中责任的关系结构仍然知之甚少。这项研究利用格伦菲尔大厦调查的证据，分析了第二阶段结束程序中提出的指责网络，其中调查律师总结了核心参与者如何公开相互推卸责任。我们将这种综合表示为有向网络，并使用网络分析的标准工具检查其结构特性。由此产生的配置是相互关联的，具有明显的互惠性和局部集群性，这表明责任主张是在密集的制度环境中阐明的，而不是孤立的、一对一的指控。与中性基准模型的比较表明，一些观察到的特征偏离了仅基于简单结构约束的预期，揭示了调查中公开阐明指责的模式组织。通过将网络分析方法应用于责任归因的制度化表示，本研究为重大公共调查中的责任关系提供了系统的结构说明。研究结果通过展示问责流程如何产生反映制度安排和治理背景的责任模式分配，有助于危机治理和避免指责的研究。
