## Abstract
Author(s): Maryam A. Nasir and W. A. Atkinson Charged domain walls in ferroelectrics form conducting two-dimensional channels that can be manipulated by electric fields. They exhibit memristive behaviour that makes them, for example, candidate synapses in neuromorphic computers. Here, the authors show that for common domain-wall geometries, electrons are subject to nontrivial spin-orbit coupling. This result suggests that reconfigurable electronics with spin-orbitronic functionality may be possible. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 035425] Published Thu Jan 15, 2026
