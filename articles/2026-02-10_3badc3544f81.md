## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08566v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Homing and navigation are fundamental behaviors in biological systems that enable agents to reliably reach a target under uncertainty. We present a Reinforcement Learning (RL) framework to model adaptive homing in continuous two-dimensional domain. In this framework, the agent's state is given by its angular deviation from home, actions correspond to alignment or stochastic reorientation, and learning is driven by a radial-distance-based cost that penalizes motion away from the target. For a single self-propelled agent moving with constant speed, we find that the mean homing time $\langle T_{\mathrm{home}} \rangle$ exhibits a non-monotonic dependence on the rotational diffusion strength $D_r$, with an optimal noise level $D_r^{*}$, revealing a subtle interplay between exploration and goal-directed correction. Extending to two agents with soft repulsion, one agent consistently reaches home faster than the other, while in multi-agents system, repulsion ensures separation and the fastest agent becomes progressively faster as group size increases. Finally comparing the mean homing time $\langle T_{\mathrm{home}} \rangle$ of an Active Brownian Particle (ABP) and RL agent over an identical range of $D_r$, we find that RL trajectories are shorter, less noisy, and consistently faster. Our results show that cost-driven learning, stochastic reorientation, and inter-agent interactions enable efficient adaptive navigation, linking individual and collective homing. This reinforcement learning framework captures key biological features such as feedback-based route learning, randomness to escape unfavorable orientations, and mutual coordination.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08566v1 公告类型：新 摘要：寻的和导航是生物系统中的基本行为，使代理能够在不确定的情况下可靠地到达目标。我们提出了一个强化学习（RL）框架来模拟连续二维域中的自适应归航。在此框架中，代理的状态由其与家乡的角度偏差给出，动作对应于对齐或随机重新定向，学习由基于径向距离的成本驱动，该成本会惩罚远离目标的运动。对于以恒定速度移动的单个自推进智能体，我们发现平均归航时间 $\langle T_{\mathrm{home}} \rangle$ 对旋转扩散强度 $D_r$ 表现出非单调依赖性，并具有最佳噪声水平 $D_r^{*}$，揭示了探索和目标导向校正之间的微妙相互作用。扩展到具有软排斥力的两个智能体，一个智能体始终比另一个智能体更快地到达目的地，而在多智能体系统中，排斥力确保分离，并且随着群体规模的增加，最快的智能体逐渐变得更快。最后，比较活性布朗粒子 (ABP) 和 RL 智能体在相同 $D_r$ 范围内的平均归航时间 $\langle T_{\mathrm{home}} \rangle$，我们发现 RL 轨迹更短、噪音更小并且始终更快。我们的结果表明，成本驱动的学习、随机重新定向和代理间交互可以实现高效的自适应导航，将个人和集体归航联系起来。该强化学习框架捕获了关键的生物学特征，例如基于反馈的路线学习、逃避不利方向的随机性以及相互协调。
