# On flying through the base of a pseudo-streamer

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03620v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Near the 10 solar radius perihelion of Parker Solar Probe orbit 24, a confined region containing an enhanced plasma density of 25,000 particles per cubic centimeter and broadband electrostatic waves was encountered. The solar wind velocity of 200 kilometers per second and ion temperature of 25 eV were significantly reduced as compared to their values in the ambient solar wind. These anomalous plasma conditions were observed on closed magnetic field lines, as determined from observations of the suprathermal electron strahl. Because the polarity of the radial magnetic field did not change sign on the two sides of the crossing and the crossed region contained a double-peaked plasma structure, the spacecraft must have passed through the base of a pseudo-streamer whose structure extended out to 10 solar radii. In the plasma frame, an electric field as large as 400 millivolts per meter was detected during the crossing. The current associated with this electric field was less than one milliampere per square meter, corresponding to a drift velocity less than 2.5 kilometers per second. It also contained a turbulent plasma with density fluctuations divided by density as large as 0.3, suggesting that the resistive term in the generalized ohm's law was significant. Also, the density as a function of time had a non-zero slope when the electric field was non-zero, suggesting that the pressure gradient term also mattered. As compared to earlier remote sensing and theoretical results, it is surprising that the plasma in this pseudo-streamer had a remarkably low flow velocity and that the pseudo-streamer base extended out to 10 solar radii.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03620v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在帕克太阳探测器 24 号轨道的 10 个太阳半径近日点附近，遇到了一个包含每立方厘米 25,000 个粒子的增强等离子体密度和宽带静电波的受限区域。与环境太阳风中的值相比，每秒200公里的太阳风速度和25 eV的离子温度显着降低。这些异常等离子体条件是在闭合磁场线上观察到的，这是根据超热电子斯特拉尔的观察确定的。由于交叉点两侧的径向磁场极性没有改变符号，且交叉区域包含双峰等离子体结构，因此航天器肯定穿过了伪流光的底部，该伪流光的结构延伸至10个太阳半径。在等离子框架中，在穿越过程中检测到了每米400毫伏的电场。与该电场相关的电流小于每平方米 1 毫安，对应的漂移速度小于每秒 2.5 公里。它还包含密度波动除以密度高达 0.3 的湍流等离子体，这表明广义欧姆定律中的电阻项很重要。此外，当电场非零时，密度作为时间的函数具有非零斜率，这表明压力梯度项也很重要。与早期的遥感和理论结果相比，令人惊讶的是，该伪流光中的等离子体具有极低的流速，并且伪流光底座延伸至10个太阳半径。
