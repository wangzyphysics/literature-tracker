## Abstract
6PPD-quinone (6PPD-Q) has been recognized as a pollutant of surface waters in recent years and has been shown to contribute to Urban Runo􀆯 Mortality Syndrome (URMS) in sensitive species. While there is published in silico, in vitro and semi-quantitative data available, little is known about the bioaccumulation and biotransformation (metabolism) of 6PPD-Q in intact salmonid species. This research focuses on the bioaccumulation and metabolism of carbon-14 ([14C]) radiolabeled 6PPD-Q using techniques and strategies commonly employed in regulatory science for the registration of agrochemicals, chemicals and as used in pharmaceutical environmental risk assessments. The OECD 305 test guideline (TG) is used as the basis for generating data on bioaccumulation but also lends itself to the generation of biotransformation data. Rainbow trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss) is commonly used as the model species for such studies and has also been shown to be acutely sensitive to 6PPD-Q. The use of [14C]-6PPD-Q allows the quantification of total residues without the need for reference standards and provides a means of assessing the bioaccumulation potential of 6PPD-Q. The use of radiolabeled material also allows assessment of extraction e􀆯iciency and quantitative metabolite profiling and identification without the need for reference standards providing full coverage of the metabolites generated from 6PPD-Q exposure in rainbow trout. The bioaccumulation potential and metabolic routes in rainbow trout are presented herein.

## 摘要（中文）
近年来，6PPD-醌 (6PPD-Q) 已被认为是地表水的污染物，并已被证明会导致敏感物种的城市鲁诺死亡综合症 (URMS)。虽然已经发表了计算机模拟、体外和半定量数据，但人们对 6PPD-Q 在完整鲑鱼物种中的生物累积和生物转化（代谢）知之甚少。本研究重点关注碳 14 ([14C]) 放射性标记 6PPD-Q 的生物蓄积和代谢，使用监管科学中常用的农用化学品、化学品注册和制药环境风险评估中常用的技术和策略。 OECD 305 测试指南 (TG) 用作生成生物累积数据的基础，但也适用于生成生物转化数据。虹鳟鱼 (Oncorhynchus mykiss) 通常用作此类研究的模型物种，并且已被证明对 6PPD-Q 高度敏感。使用 [14C]-6PPD-Q 无需参考标准即可定量总残留量，并提供了评估 6PPD-Q 生物蓄积潜力的方法。使用放射性标记材料还可以评估提取效率以及定量代谢物分析和鉴定，而无需参考标准，从而完全覆盖虹鳟鱼中 6PPD-Q 暴露产生的代谢物。本文介绍了虹鳟鱼的生物累积潜力和代谢途径。
