## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22371v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Understanding the magnetic ground state of Ruddlesden-Popper nickelates is crucial, as these materials exhibit superconductivity under high pressure and host competing electronic orders that may play a key role in the pairing mechanism. In this work, we investigate the magnetic properties of the alternating monolayer-trilayer phase of La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ (1313-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$) using muon-spin rotation/relaxation ($\mu$SR) under both ambient and hydrostatic pressure conditions. The monolayer-trilayer phase develops incommensurate magnetic order below approximately 150 K, with a mean ordering temperature of $T_{SDW} \simeq 123$ K and a transition width of $\Delta T_{SDW} \simeq 15$ K. The abrupt onset of the internal magnetic field indicates a first-order-like transition. Hydrostatic pressure ($p$) suppresses the magnetic ordering temperature at a rate of $dT_{SDW}/d p \simeq -3.9$ K/GPa, demonstrating a progressive destabilization of the ordered state. By comparison with the bilayer 2222-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ and the trilayer 3333-La$_4$Ni$_3$O$_{10}$ systems, and within a unified phenomenological framework, systematic trends are identified linking the pressure dependence of $T_{SDW}$, the (in)commensurability of the magnetic order, and the character of the magnetic transition. These trends consistently indicate a gradual reduction of electronic correlation strength from the bilayer to the monolayer-trilayer and trilayer nickelates. This hierarchy suggests that the higher superconducting transition temperature observed in the 2222 phase may be closely connected to its more strongly correlated electronic nature. These results position the alternating monolayer-trilayer 1313-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ as an intermediate member linking the magnetic behavior of the bilayer 2222-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ and the trilayer 3333-La$_4$Ni$_3$O$_{10}$ Ruddlesden-Popper compounds.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22371v1 公告类型：新 摘要：了解 Ruddlesden-Popper 镍酸盐的磁性基态至关重要，因为这些材料在高压下表现出超导性，并具有可能在配对机制中发挥关键作用的竞争电子序。在这项工作中，我们利用μ子自旋旋转/弛豫 ($\mu$SR) 在环境压力和静水压力条件下研究了 La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ (1313-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$) 交替单层-三层相的磁性。单层-三层相在大约 150 K 以下形成不相称的磁序，平均有序温度为 $T_{SDW} \simeq 123$ K，转变宽度为 $\Delta T_{SDW} \simeq 15$ K。内部磁场的突然开始表明发生了类似一阶的转变。静水压力 ($p$) 以 $dT_{SDW}/d p \simeq -3.9$ K/GPa 的速率抑制磁有序温度，表明有序状态逐渐失稳。通过与双层 2222-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ 和三层 3333-La$_4$Ni$_3$O$_{10}$ 系统进行比较，并在统一的现象学框架内，确定了将 $T_{SDW}$ 的压力依赖性、磁序的（内）可通约性和磁转变特征联系起来的系统趋势。这些趋势一致表明电子关联强度从双层到单层-三层和三层镍酸盐逐渐降低。这种层次结构表明，在 2222 相中观察到的较高超导转变温度可能与其更强相关的电子性质密切相关。这些结果将交替的单层-三层 1313-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ 定位为连接双层 2222-La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ 和三层 3333-La$_4$Ni$_3$O$_{10}$ Ruddlesden-Popper 化合物的磁性行为的中间成员。
