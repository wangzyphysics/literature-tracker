## Abstract
arXiv:2512.15470v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Starting from limited measurements of a turbulent flow, data assimilation (DA) attempts to estimate all the spatio-temporal scales of motion. Success is dependent on whether the system is observable from the measurements, or how much of the initial turbulent field is encoded in the available measurements. Adjoint-variational DA minimises the discrepancy between the true and estimated measurements by optimising the initial velocity or vorticity field (the `state space'). Here we propose to instead optimise in a lower-dimensional latent space which is learned by implicit rank minimising autoencoders. Assimilating in latent space, rather than state space, redefines the observability of the measurements and identifies the physically meaningful perturbation directions which matter most for accurate prediction of the flow evolution. When observing coarse-grained measurements of two-dimensional Kolmogorov flow at moderate Reynolds numbers, the proposed latent-space DA approach estimates the full turbulent state with a relative error improvement of two orders of magnitude over the standard state-space DA approach. The small scales of the estimated turbulent field are predicted more faithfully with latent-space DA, greatly reducing erroneous small-scale velocities typically introduced by state-space DA. Furthermore, latent-space DA is demonstrated to be robust to noisy measurements at the range of Reynolds numbers considered. These findings demonstrate that the observability of the system from available data can be greatly improved when turbulent measurements are assimilated in the right space, or coordinates.

arXiv:2512.15470v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：从湍流的有限测量开始，数据同化 (DA) 尝试估计运动的所有时空尺度。成功取决于系统是否可以从测量中观察到，或者有多少初始湍流场被编码在可用的测量中。伴随变分 DA 通过优化初始速度或涡度场（“状态空间”）来最小化真实测量值和估计测量值之间的差异。在这里，我们建议在通过隐式秩最小化自动编码器学习的低维潜在空间中进行优化。在潜在空间而不是状态空间中进行同化，重新定义了测量的可观测性，并确定了物理上有意义的扰动方向，这对于准确预测流动演化最重要。当观察中等雷诺数的二维柯尔莫哥洛夫流的粗粒度测量时，所提出的潜在空间 DA 方法估计完全湍流状态，相对误差比标准状态空间 DA 方法提高了两个数量级。使用潜在空间 DA 可以更准确地预测估计湍流场的小尺度，从而大大减少通常由状态空间 DA 引入的错误小尺度速度。此外，潜空间 DA 被证明对所考虑的雷诺数范围内的噪声测量具有鲁棒性。这些发现表明，当湍流测量被同化在正确的空间或坐标中时，系统根据可用数据的可观测性可以大大提高。
