## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09513v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Cuprate high-temperature superconductors, such as Yttrium Barium Copper Oxide (YBCO), are extremely promising for emerging technologies such as low-power computing, data storage, quantum sensors and superconducting electronics. However, the realization of high-performance functional nanostructures presents formidable challenges due to the difficulty of applying conventional nanofabrication methods to such sensitive materials, making the search for alternative methods a key enabling factor. Since YBCO's superconducting and normal-state properties are highly dependent on oxygen stoichiometry, precise nanoscale control of the oxygen content represents a highly appealing approach for creating advanced nanoengineered devices. In this work, we demonstrate the precise fabrication of sub-micrometer, grayscale patterns over large areas in epitaxial YBCO thin films, achieving finely tuned optical and superconducting transport properties by locally controlling the stoichiometry through maskless direct laser writing under ambient conditions. Cryogenic magneto-optical imaging and transport measurements in irradiated devices directly demonstrate the spatial tuning of the critical temperature and carrier density with the patterning conditions. Correlated Raman microscopy and reflectometry of the patterned regions indicate a laser-power dependent oxygen depletion in the irradiated regions. The proposed laser-controlled stoichiometry approach provides a direct and scalable method to navigate the phase diagram of high-Tc superconducting oxides, offering new possibilities for integrating functional nanostructures into superconducting devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09513v1 公告类型：新 摘要：铜氧化物高温超导体，例如钇钡铜氧化物 (YBCO)，对于低功耗计算、数据存储、量子传感器和超导电子器件等新兴技术极具前景。然而，由于难以将传统的纳米制造方法应用于此类敏感材料，高性能功能纳米结构的实现面临着巨大的挑战，这使得寻找替代方法成为关键的推动因素。由于 YBCO 的超导和常态特性高度依赖于氧化学计量，因此对氧含量的精确纳米级控制代表了创建先进纳米工程器件的一种极具吸引力的方法。在这项工作中，我们展示了在外延 YBCO 薄膜中大面积亚微米灰度图案的精确制造，通过在环境条件下无掩模直接激光写入局部控制化学计量，实现精细调节的光学和超导传输特性。辐照器件中的低温磁光成像和传输测量直接证明了临界温度和载流子密度与图案化条件的空间调谐。相关的拉曼显微镜和图案区域的反射测量表明，在照射区域中存在与激光功率相关的氧消耗。所提出的激光控制化学计量方法提供了一种直接且可扩展的方法来导航高温超导氧化物的相图，为将功能纳米结构集成到超导器件中提供了新的可能性。
