## Abstract
arXiv:2512.16556v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We study the learning dynamics of the soft committee machine (SCM) with Rectified Linear Unit (ReLU) activation using a statistical-mechanics approach within the annealed approximation. The SCM consists of a student network with $N$ input units and $K$ hidden units trained to reproduce the output of a teacher network with $M$ hidden units. We introduce a reduced set of macroscopic order parameters that yields a unified description valid from the conventional regime $K \ll N$ to the ultra-wide limit $K \ge N$. The control parameter $\alpha$, proportional to the ratio of training samples to adjustable weights, serves as an effective measure of dataset size. For small $\gamma = M/N$, we recover a continuous phase transition at $\alpha_{c} \approx 2\pi$ from an unspecialized, permutation-symmetric state to a specialized state in which student units align with the teacher. For finite $\gamma$, the transition disappears and the generalization error decreases smoothly with dataset size, reaching a low plateau when $\gamma=1$. In the asymptotic limit $\alpha \to \infty$, the error scales as $\varepsilon_{g} \propto 1/\alpha$, independent of $\gamma$ and $K$. The results highlight the central role of network dimensions in SCM learning and provide a framework extendable to other activations and quenched analyses.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.16556v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们使用退火近似中的统计力学方法，研究具有修正线性单元 (ReLU) 激活的软委员会机 (SCM) 的学习动态。 SCM 由具有 $N$ 输入单元和 $K$ 隐藏单元的学生网络组成，经过训练可以重现具有 $M$ 隐藏单元的教师网络的输出。我们引入了一组简化的宏观阶次参数，产生从传统机制 $K \ll N$ 到超宽极限 $K \ge N$ 有效的统一描述。控制参数 $\alpha$ 与训练样本与可调整权重的比率成正比，可作为数据集大小的有效度量。对于小的 $\gamma = M/N$，我们在 $\alpha_{c} \approx 2\pi$ 恢复连续相变，从非专门化的排列对称状态到学生单元与教师对齐的专门化状态。对于有限的 $\gamma$，过渡消失，泛化误差随着数据集大小的增加而平滑减小，当 $\gamma=1$ 时达到一个较低的平台。在渐近极限 $\alpha \to \infty$ 中，误差缩放为 $\varepsilon_{g} \propto 1/\alpha$，与 $\gamma$ 和 $K$ 无关。结果强调了网络维度在 SCM 学习中的核心作用，并提供了一个可扩展到其他激活和淬火分析的框架。
