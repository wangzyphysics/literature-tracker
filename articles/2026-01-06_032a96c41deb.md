## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02185v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We theoretically propose a nonlinear spin-wave Doppler effect, in which the time-dependent motion of a magnetic energy boundary acts as an active frequency modulator, directly converting boundary-induced phase dynamics into instantaneous spectral synthesis for propagating spin-wave modes. In contrast to the conventional linear Doppler effect governed by constant relative velocity, this mechanism enables dynamic phase-to-frequency transduction, generating high-order harmonics, magnonic frequency combs, and coherent chirped sidebands, without requiring nonlinear magnon-magnon coupling or multi-magnon scattering. Micromagnetic simulations on voltage-controlled anisotropy boundaries in ferroelectric/ferromagnetic (FE/FM) heterostructures demonstrate that the comb spacing and spectral topology are determined solely by boundary kinematics, confirming direct Doppler phase coupling between boundary motion and spin-wave propagation. These results establish moving magnetic-energy boundaries as a new class of on-chip spectral synthesizers and define a coherent and energy-efficient framework for flexible tuning of magnonic frequencies, fundamentally distinct from traditional passive scattering or nonlinear multi-magnon mechanisms.

arXiv:2601.02185v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们从理论上提出了一种非线性自旋波多普勒效应，其中磁能边界的时间相关运动充当主动频率调制器，直接将边界引起的相位动力学转换为传播自旋波模式的瞬时谱合成。与由恒定相对速度控制的传统线性多普勒效应相比，该机制能够实现动态相频转换，生成高阶谐波、磁振子频率梳和相干啁啾边带，而不需要非线性磁振子-磁振子耦合或多磁振子散射。对铁电/铁磁 (FE/FM) 异质结构中压控各向异性边界的微磁模拟表明，梳状间距和光谱拓扑仅由边界运动学决定，证实了边界运动和自旋波传播之间的直接多普勒相位耦合。这些结果将移动磁能边界确立为新型片上光谱合成器，并定义了用于灵活调谐磁振频率的相干且节能的框架，从根本上区别于传统的无源散射或非线性多磁振子机制。
