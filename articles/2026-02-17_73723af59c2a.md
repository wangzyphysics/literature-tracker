## Abstract
Author(s): Andi Gumarilang and Kohji Nakamura Magnetism and magnon excitation in the two-dimensional van der Waals CrI 3 is driven by not only the isotropic spin-exchange interactions, but also the anisotropic spin-exchange interactions, where the nonmagnetic ligand Iodine atoms play important roles. This allows indirect modulation of interactions between Chromium atoms by tuning the electronic states of Iodine atoms via stacking order control. Here, combining first-principles calculation and linear spin-wave theory, the authors theoretically demonstrate that multiple stacking orders between monolayer CrI 3 and Chromium host different modulation of spin-exchange interactions and topological magnon phases, identified by the existence of chiral edge states. The modulation of spin-exchange interactions is mainly driven by the interfacial charge transfer from the Chromium atoms to the Iodine atoms, which indirectly changes the electronic states of Chromium d orbitals from symmetry point of view. ## 摘要（中文）
作者：Andi Gumarilang 和 Kohji Nakamura 二维范德华 CrI 3 中的磁性和磁振子激发不仅由各向同性自旋交换相互作用驱动，而且还由各向异性自旋交换相互作用驱动，其中非磁性配体碘原子发挥重要作用。这允许通过堆叠顺序控制调整碘原子的电子状态来间接调节铬原子之间的相互作用。在这里，作者结合第一性原理计算和线性自旋波理论，从理论上证明了单层 CrI 3 和铬之间的多个堆叠顺序对自旋交换相互作用和拓扑磁振子相进行了不同的调制，这是通过手性边缘态的存在来识别的。自旋交换相互作用的调节主要是由铬原子到碘原子的界面电荷转移驱动的，这从对称性角度间接改变了铬d轨道的电子态。 [物理。修订版材料 10, 024407] 2026 年 2 月 17 日星期二发布
