## Abstract
arXiv:2508.12879v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: CMOS-compatible HfO2-based ferroelectric tunnel junction (FTJ) has attracted significant attention as a promising candidate for in-memory computing (IMC) due to its extremely low power consumption. However, conventional FTJs face inherent challenges that hinder their practical applications. Insufficient current density and limited on-off current ratios in FTJs are primarily constrained by their dependence on direct and Fowler-Nordheim tunneling mechanisms. Building on previous experimental results, this paper proposes a trap-assisted tunneling (TAT)-based FTJ that leverages the TAT mechanism to overcome these limitations. A comprehensive FTJ model integrating ferroelectric switching, direct, Fowler-Nordheim tunneling, and TAT mechanisms is developed, enabling detailed analyses of the trap conditions and their impact on performance. Through systematic optimization of trap parameters and device structure, the simulated TAT-based FTJ achieves ultra-high current density and a remarkable on-off current ratio, meeting the nanoscale IMC requirements. The results highlight the potential of TAT-based FTJs as high-performance memory solutions for IMC applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.12879v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：CMOS 兼容的 HfO2 基铁电隧道结 (FTJ) 由于其极低的功耗，作为内存计算 (IMC) 的有前途的候选者而引起了广泛关注。然而，传统的 FTJ 面临着阻碍其实际应用的固有挑战。 FTJ 中电流密度不足和有限的开关电流比主要受到其对直接隧道机制和福勒-诺德海姆隧道机制的依赖的限制。基于之前的实验结果，本文提出了一种基于陷阱辅助隧道（TAT）的 FTJ，利用 TAT 机制来克服这些限制。开发了集成铁电开关、直接、Fowler-Nordheim 隧道和 TAT 机制的综合 FTJ 模型，可以详细分析陷阱条件及其对性能的影响。通过对陷阱参数和器件结构的系统优化，模拟的基于TAT的FTJ实现了超高电流密度和显着的开关电流比，满足纳米级IMC要求。结果凸显了基于 TAT 的 FTJ 作为 IMC 应用的高性能存储器解决方案的潜力。
