## Abstract
arXiv:2507.01119v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The electromagnetic properties of type-II superconductors depend on vortices -- magnetic flux lines whose motion introduces dissipation that can be mitigated by pinning from material defects. The material disorder landscape is tuned by the choice of materials growth technique and incorporation of impurities that serve as vortex pinning centers. For example, metal organic deposition (MOD) and pulsed laser deposition (PLD) produce high-quality superconducting films with uncorrelated versus correlated disorder, respectively. Here, we study vortex dynamics in PLD-grown EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ films containing varying concentrations of BaHfO$_3$ inclusions and compare our results with those of MOD-grown (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ films. Despite both systems exhibiting behavior consistent with strong pinning theory, which predicts the critical current density $J_c$ based on vortex trapping by randomly distributed spherical inclusions, we find striking differences in the vortex dynamics owing to the correlated versus uncorrelated disorder. Specifically, we find that the EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ films grown without inclusions exhibit surprisingly slow vortex creep, comparable to the slowest creep rates achieved in (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ films containing high concentrations of BaHfO$_3$. Whereas adding inclusions to (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ is effective in slowing creep, BaHfO$_3$ increases creep in EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ even while concomitantly improving $J_c$. Lastly, we find evidence of variable range hopping and that $J_c$ is maximized at the BaHfO$_3$ concentration that hosts creep of large vortex bundles or a Bose glass state.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.01119v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：II 型超导体的电磁特性取决于涡流 - 磁通线的运动会引入耗散，可以通过材料缺陷的钉扎来减轻耗散。通过选择材料生长技术和掺入充当涡旋钉扎中心的杂质来调整材料无序景观。例如，金属有机沉积（MOD）和脉冲激光沉积（PLD）分别生产具有不相关和相关无序的高质量超导薄膜。在这里，我们研究了含有不同浓度 BaHfO$_3$ 夹杂物的 PLD 生长的 EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 薄膜中的涡流动力学，并将我们的结果与 MOD 生长的 (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 薄膜的结果进行比较。尽管两个系统都表现出与强钉扎理论一致的行为，该理论根据随机分布的球形夹杂物的涡流捕获来预测临界电流密度 $J_c$，但我们发现由于相关与不相关的无序，涡流动力学存在显着差异。具体来说，我们发现在没有夹杂物的情况下生长的 EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 薄膜表现出令人惊讶的缓慢涡流蠕变，与含有高浓度 BaHfO$_3$ 的 (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 薄膜中实现的最慢蠕变速率相当。虽然在 (Y,Gd)Ba$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 中添加夹杂物可有效减缓蠕变，但 BaHfO$_3$ 会增加 EuBa$_2$Cu$_3$O$_y$ 的蠕变，甚至同时改善 $J_c$。最后，我们发现了可变范围跳跃的证据，并且 $J_c$ 在 BaHfO$_3$ 浓度下最大化，该浓度承载大涡束或玻色玻璃态的蠕变。
