## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06099v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the non-thermal material dynamics of strongly excited silicon during ultra-fast laser ablation. In contrast to metals, silicon shows strongly excitation-dependent interatomic bonding strengths, which gives rise to a number of unique material dynamics like non-thermal melting, Coulomb explosions and altered carrier heat conduction due to charge carrier confinement. In this study, we report novel non-thermal ablation mechanisms in the ultra-fast single shot laser ablation of silicon and perform large scale massive multi-parallel simulations on experimentally achievable length scales with atomistic resolution. For this, we model the ultra-fast carrier dynamics utilizing the Thermal-Spike-Model coupled to Molecular Dynamics simulations and include the accompanied excitation-dependent nonthermal bonding strength manipulation by application of the excitation-dependent modified Tersoff Potential. Further, we present first results on the systematic construction of the excitation-dependent phase diagram of silicon by thermodynamic integration.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06099v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了超快激光烧蚀过程中强激发硅的非热材料动力学。与金属相比，硅表现出强烈依赖于激发的原子间键合强度，这产生了许多独特的材料动力学，例如非热熔化、库仑爆炸以及由于载流子限制而改变的载流子热传导。在这项研究中，我们报告了硅的超快单次激光烧蚀中的新型非热烧蚀机制，并在实验上可实现的原子分辨率长度尺度上进行大规模大规模多并行模拟。为此，我们利用与分子动力学模拟相结合的热尖峰模型对超快载流子动力学进行建模，并通过应用与激发相关的修改特索夫电势来进行伴随的与激发相关的非热键合强度操纵。此外，我们提出了通过热力学积分系统构建硅的激发相关相图的初步结果。
