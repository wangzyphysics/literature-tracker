# Path-integral calculation of the second and third density virial coefficients of 4 He

## Abstract
We present a comprehensive study of the second and third density virial coefficients, B ( T ) and C ( T ), of 4 He across an extended temperature range from 1 K to 10 000 K, utilizing the path-integral Monte Carlo (PIMC) method and the most accurate interaction potentials. This work reports the first complete determination of B ( T ) within the PIMC framework, demonstrating excellent agreement and comparable uncertainties with results from the established phase-shift method, thus providing crucial cross-method validation. We generate a significantly extended and high-accuracy dataset for C ( T ), complementing existing literature values. A comprehensive uncertainty analysis quantifies contributions from both two-body and three-body potentials. The results reveal subtle differences from previous work for C ( T ) below 5 K, while reducing the uncertainty by approximately a factor of two in this low-temperature regime. This study provides crucial reference data to enhance the accuracy of primary standards for both temperature and pressure based on 4 He.

## 摘要（中文）
我们利用路径积分蒙特卡罗 (PIMC) 方法和最准确的相互作用势，对 4 He 在 1 K 至 10 000 K 的扩展温度范围内的第二和第三密度维里系数 B ( T ) 和 C ( T ) 进行了全面研究。这项工作报告了 PIMC 框架内首次完整测定 B ( T )，与已建立的相移方法的结果表现出良好的一致性和可比较的不确定性，从而提供了关键的交叉方法验证。我们为 C ( T ) 生成了一个显着扩展的高精度数据集，补充了现有的文献值。全面的不确定性分析量化了二体和三体电位的贡献。结果表明，C (T) 低于 5 K 时与之前的工作存在细微差别，同时在此低温状态下将不确定性降低了大约两倍。这项研究为提高基于 4 He 的温度和压力初级标准的准确性提供了重要的参考数据。
