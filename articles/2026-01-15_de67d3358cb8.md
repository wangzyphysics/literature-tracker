## Abstract
arXiv:2507.03277v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The discovery of superconductivity in multilayer nickelates under pressure has intensified interest in understanding the magnetic and electronic properties of Ruddlesden-Popper nickelates. Using density functional theory with Hubbard corrections, we investigate the magnetic ground state, electronic structure evolution under pressure, and Sr-doping effects in La$_2$NiO$_4$. We find that at ambient pressure, tetragonal La$_2$NiO$_4$ exhibits G-type antiferromagnetic order with negligible interlayer magnetic coupling. Under hydrostatic pressure, the system undergoes a continuous insulator-metal transition at ~50 GPa while maintaining robust magnetic order up to 75 GPa, contrasting sharply with the rapid magnetic suppression in La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$. Sr doping induces a systematic evolution from G-type to A-type, to striped antiferromagnetic orders, and eventually to ferromagnetic order, accompanied by metallization. Furthermore, LaSrNiO$_4$ displays weak charge and orbital orders. These results reveal the unique pressure and doping effects of single-layer nickelates and provide insights into the magnetic mechanisms underlying nickelate superconductivity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.03277v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在压力下多层镍酸盐中超导性的发现增强了人们对了解 Ruddlesden-Popper 镍酸盐的磁性和电子特性的兴趣。利用密度泛函理论和哈伯德修正，我们研究了 La$_2$NiO$_4$ 中的磁基态、压力下的电子结构演化以及 Sr 掺杂效应。我们发现，在环境压力下，四方晶系 La$_2$NiO$_4$ 表现出 G 型反铁磁序，层间磁耦合可忽略不计。在静水压力下，系统在约 50 GPa 的压力下经历连续的绝缘体-金属转变，同时保持高达 75 GPa 的强大磁序，这与 La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ 中的快速磁抑制形成鲜明对比。 Sr 掺杂引起从 G 型到 A 型、条纹反铁磁有序、最终到铁磁有序的系统演化，并伴有金属化。此外，LaSrNiO$_4$ 显示出弱电荷和轨道序数。这些结果揭示了单层镍酸盐独特的压力和掺杂效应，并为了解镍酸盐超导性的磁性机制提供了见解。
