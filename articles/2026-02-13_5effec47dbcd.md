## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12142v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum gas microscopes probe quantum many-body lattice states via projective measurements in the occupation basis, enabling access to various density and spin correlations. Phase information, however, cannot be directly obtained in these setups. Recent experiments went beyond this by measuring local current operators and local phase fluctuations. Here we propose how Fourier-space manipulation in a matter-wave microscope allows access to various long-range off-diagonal correlators in experimentally realistic settings, realizing a many-body phase microscope. We demonstrate in particular how the fermionic d-wave superconducting order parameter in arbitrary Hubbard-type models, the non-equal time Green's function yielding the spectral function, or the hidden order of composite bosons in a fractional Chern insulator can be directly measured. Our results show the great potential of matter-wave microscopy for accessing exotic correlators including phases and coherences and characterizing intriguing quantum many-body states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12142v1 发布类型：新 摘要：量子气体显微镜通过占据基础上的投影测量来探测量子多体晶格态，从而能够获得各种密度和自旋相关性。然而，在这些设置中无法直接获得相位信息。最近的实验超越了这一点，测量了本地电流算子和本地相位波动。在这里，我们提出了物质波显微镜中的傅里叶空间操纵如何允许在实验现实设置中访问各种远程非对角相关器，从而实现多体相位显微镜。我们特别演示了如何直接测量任意哈伯德型模型中的费米子 d 波超导阶参数、产生谱函数的非等时格林函数或分数陈绝缘体中复合玻色子的隐藏阶。我们的结果表明，物质波显微镜在获取奇异相关器（包括相位和相干性）以及表征有趣的量子多体态方面具有巨大潜力。
