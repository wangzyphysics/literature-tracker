## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14334v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto are key targets in the search for habitability due to the potential presence of subsurface oceans. Detecting complex organic molecules (COMs), essential for prebiotic chemistry, is crucial to assessing their potential. Though COMs remain undetected on these moons, ESA's JUICE and NASA's Europa Clipper missions aim to fill this gap with their science payloads. This study explores the formation and transport of COMs within Jupiter's circumplanetary disk (CPD), a critical environment for the formation of the Galilean moons. Using a time-dependent model that couples the evolving CPD structure with the dynamics of icy particles of varying sizes and release times, we assess two primary COM formation pathways: thermal processing of ices and UV photochemistry. The results indicate that heating, particularly of NH3:CO2 ices, occurs efficiently before substantial irradiation, making it the dominant pathway for COM formation in the Jovian CPD. However, the relative efficiencies of these two processes are governed by particle density, disk viscosity, accretion rate, and UV flux, which collectively determine drift timescales and exposure to favorable thermodynamic environments. Existing models indicate that Europa's accretion was relatively cold and prolonged, possibly allowing some COMs to survive incorporation, whereas Ganymede and Callisto likely formed under even cooler conditions conducive to preserving COM-rich material. These results highlight the potential inheritance of complex organics by the Galilean moons and offer a framework for interpreting upcoming compositional data from JUICE and Europa Clipper.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14334v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：由于地下海洋的潜在存在，木卫二、木卫三和木卫四是寻找宜居性的关键目标。检测复杂有机分子 (COM) 对于生命起源化学至关重要，对于评估其潜力至关重要。尽管这些卫星上的 COM 仍未被发现，但 ESA 的 JUICE 和 NASA 的 Europa Clipper 任务旨在利用其科学有效载荷来填补这一空白。这项研究探讨了木星环行星盘 (CPD) 内 COM 的形成和运输，木星环行星盘是伽利略卫星形成的关键环境。使用与时间相关的模型，将不断变化的 CPD 结构与不同尺寸和释放时间的冰颗粒的动力学耦合起来，我们评估了两种主要的 COM 形成途径：冰的热处理和紫外线光化学。结果表明，加热，特别是 NH3:CO2 冰的加热，在大量辐射之前有效发生，使其成为木星 CPD 中 COM 形成的主要途径。然而，这两个过程的相对效率受颗粒密度、盘粘度、吸积率和紫外线通量的控制，它们共同决定了漂移时间尺度和暴露在有利的热力学环境中。现有模型表明，木卫二的吸积相对寒冷且持续时间较长，可能使一些 COM 能够在合并过程中幸存下来，而木卫三和木卫四可能是在更冷的条件下形成的，有利于保存富含 COM 的物质。这些结果强调了伽利略卫星对复杂有机物的潜在继承性，并为解释即将来自 JUICE 和 Europa Clipper 的成分数据提供了一个框架。
