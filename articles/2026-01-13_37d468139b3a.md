## Abstract
arXiv:2510.19533v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Fermionic cold atoms in optical traps provide viable quantum simulators of correlation effects in electronic systems. For dressed Rydberg atoms in two-dimensional traps with out-of-plane dipole moments, a realistic model of the pairwise interaction is of repulsive dipolar $1/r^3$ form at long range, softened to a constant at short range. This study provides parameterizations of fixed-node diffusion Monte Carlo energy data for ferromagnetic (one-component) and paramagnetic (two-component) two-dimensional homogeneous Fermi fluids of interacting dipolar atoms. We find itinerant ferromagnetism to be unstable within our parameter spaces for dipolar interactions both with and without softening. Our parameterization of the energy as a function of density will enable density functional theory to support experimental studies of inhomogeneous fermionic cold atom systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.19533v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：光陷阱中的费米子冷原子为电子系统中的相关效应提供了可行的量子模拟器。对于具有平面外偶极矩的二维陷阱中的修饰里德伯原子，成对相互作用的现实模型在长距离上具有排斥偶极 $1/r^3$ 形式，在短距离上软化为常数。本研究提供了相互作用偶极原子的铁磁（单组分）和顺磁（双组分）二维均匀费米流体的固定节点扩散蒙特卡罗能量数据的参数化。我们发现，无论有软化还是没有软化，巡回铁磁性在偶极相互作用的参数空间内都是不稳定的。我们将能量参数化为密度的函数，将使密度泛函理论能够支持非均匀费米子冷原子系统的实验研究。
