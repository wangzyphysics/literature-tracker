## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09245v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Coherent anti-Stokes Raman scattering (CARS) microscopy offers label-free chemical imaging capabilities, but its performance is constrained by small Raman scattering cross-section, strong non-resonant background (NRB), and limited signal-to-noise ratio (SNR). Here, we introduce a high-energy, low-photodamage CARS (HELP-CARS) platform designed to overcome these physical limitations. By employing a 1-MHz non-collinear optical parametric amplifier (NOPA) with extensive pulse chirping, HELP-CARS increases the coherent Raman excitation efficiency by ~300 times and improves the signal-to-nonresonant background ratio by 11 times, while inducing negligible damage during live cell imaging. Furthermore, to remove non-independent noise and physically entangled non-resonant background, we incorporate self-supervised deep-learning denoising and background removal based on the Kramers-Kronig relationship, yielding sensitivity improvement by an additional order of magnitude. Together, these advances provide a micromolar imaging sensitivity (160 uM for Dimethyl sulfoxide-d6) corresponding to 1000 molecules in the focal volume. Such high sensitivity enables high-fidelity chemical imaging in both fingerprint and silent windows. Hyperspectral HELP-CARS imaging of deuterated fatty acids allowed first observation of chemical separation with single lipid droplet. Together, HELP-CARS offers a powerful and generalizable approach for ultrasensitive and quantitative vibrational imaging of biological systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09245v1 公告类型：新 摘要：相干反斯托克斯拉曼散射 (CARS) 显微镜提供无标记化学成像功能，但其性能受到拉曼散射截面小、非共振背景 (NRB) 强和信噪比 (SNR) 有限的限制。在这里，我们介绍了一种高能量、低光损伤的 CARS (HELP-CARS) 平台，旨在克服这些物理限制。通过采用具有广泛脉冲啁啾的 1 MHz 非共线光学参量放大器 (NOPA)，HELP-CARS 将相干拉曼激发效率提高了约 300 倍，并将信号与非共振背景比提高了 11 倍，同时在活细胞成像期间引起的损伤可以忽略不计。此外，为了消除非独立噪声和物理纠缠的非共振背景，我们结合了基于 Kramers-Kronig 关系的自监督深度学习去噪和背景消除，从而将灵敏度提高了一个数量级。总之，这些进步提供了相当于焦点体积中 1000 个分子的微摩尔成像灵敏度（二甲亚砜-d6 为 160 uM）。如此高的灵敏度可以在指纹和静音窗口中实现高保真化学成像。氘代脂肪酸的高光谱 HELP-CARS 成像首次能够观察到单个脂滴的化学分离。 HELP-CARS 共同为生物系统的超灵敏和定量振动成像提供了一种强大且通用的方法。
