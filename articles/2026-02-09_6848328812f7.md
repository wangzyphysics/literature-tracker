## Abstract
arXiv:2512.09536v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: For more than 40 years the quest to understand how large-scale magnetic fields emerge from turbulent flows in rotating astrophysical systems, such as the Sun, has been a major focus of computational astrophysics research. Using a parameter scan and phenomenological analysis of maximally simplified three-dimensional cartesian magnetohydrodynamic simulations of large-scale non-linear helical turbulent dynamos, I present results in this Letter that strongly point to an asymptotic ultimate regime of the large-scale solar dynamo at large magnetic Reynolds numbers, $Rm$, involving helicity fluxes between hemispheres. I obtained corresponding numerical solutions at both $Pm>1$ and $Pm<1$, and show that they can currently only be achieved in clean, simplified numerical set-ups. The analysis further strongly suggests that all global simulations to date lie in non-asymptotic turbulent magnetohydrodynamic regimes highly sensitive to changes in kinetic and magnetic Reynolds numbers. Ideas are presented to attempt to reach the ultimate regime in such 'realistic' global spherical models at a reasonable numerical cost. Overall, the results clarify the current state, and some hard limitations of the brute-force numerical modelling approach applied to this, and other similar astrophysical turbulence problems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.09536v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：40 多年来，了解太阳等旋转天体物理系统中的湍流如何产生大规模磁场一直是计算天体物理学研究的主要焦点。使用参数扫描和对大型非线性螺旋湍流发电机的最大简化三维笛卡尔磁流体动力学模拟的唯象分析，我在这封信中提出了结果，这些结果强烈指出了大型太阳发电机在大磁雷诺数 $Rm$ 下的渐近极限状态，涉及半球之间的螺旋通量。我在 $Pm>1$ 和 $Pm<1$ 处获得了相应的数值解，并表明它们目前只能通过干净、简化的数值设置来实现。该分析进一步强烈表明，迄今为止的所有全球模拟都处于对动力学和磁雷诺数的变化高度敏感的非渐近湍流磁流体动力学状态。提出了一些想法，试图以合理的数值成本达到这种“现实”全球球形模型的最终状态。总体而言，结果阐明了当前状态，以及应用于此问题和其他类似天体物理湍流问题的强力数值建模方法的一些硬限制。
