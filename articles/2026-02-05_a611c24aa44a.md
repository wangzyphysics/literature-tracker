## Abstract
Author(s): Lukas P. Weise, Tobias A. Kampmann, and Jan Kierfeld Large-scale simulations of mutually attractive semiflexible polymers show that they generically form a network of bundles, which slowly coarsens over time. Similar to foams, the coarsening process is driven by a reduction of the bundle surface suggesting that such structures are frequently observed in cytoskeletal systems because they represent long-lived intermediate structures in a slow coarsening process toward a ground state consisting of a single bundle. [Phys. Rev. Research 8, L012033] Published Thu Feb 05, 2026

作者：Lukas P. Weise、Tobias A. Kampmann 和 Jan Kierfeld 对相互吸引的半柔性聚合物的大规模模拟表明，它们通常形成束网络，随着时间的推移，该网络会慢慢变粗。与泡沫类似，粗化过程是由束表面的减少驱动的，这表明这种结构在细胞骨架系统中经常观察到，因为它们代表了在缓慢粗化过程中朝向由单个束组成的基态的长寿命中间结构。 [物理。 Rev. Research 8, L012033] 2026 年 2 月 5 日星期四发布
