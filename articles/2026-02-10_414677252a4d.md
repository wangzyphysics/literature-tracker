## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08950v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Topological insulators and skyrmion-hosting, chiral magnetic multilayers are two well-explored areas of modern condensed matter physics, each offering unique advantages for spintronics applications. In this paper, we demonstrate the optimization process for the growth of a Bi$_2$Se$_3$/buffer/[Pt/CoB/Ru]$_{\times N}$ heterostructure that combines these two material classes: the Bi$_2$Se$_3$ epilayer was grown by molecular beam epitaxy before transfer under ultrahigh vacuum to a separate growth chamber where the polycrystalline metallic multilayer was sputter deposited. The structure of the samples was characterized by co-fitted X-ray and polarized neutron reflectometry measurements and scanning transmission electron microscopy. Polarized neutron models and standard magnetometry show that a buffer layer exceeding a critical thickness is required to obtain the desired uniform, perpendicular magnetic anisotropy in every magnetic layer in the multilayer. Samples with both Ta and Mo buffers were used requiring thicknesses of 1.5 and 0.9 nm respectively. In minimizing the Bi$_2$Se$_3$ terracing, buffered samples yield well-defined, out-of-plane, magnetic domains suitable for spin-orbit torque induced manipulation as determined by X-ray photoemission electron microscopy.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08950v1 公告类型：新 摘要：拓扑绝缘体和斯格明子手性磁性多层膜是现代凝聚态物理的两个深入探索的领域，每个领域都为自旋电子学应用提供了独特的优势。在本文中，我们展示了结合这两种材料类别的 Bi$_2$Se$_3$/buffer/[Pt/CoB/Ru]$_{\times N}$ 异质结构生长的优化过程：Bi$_2$Se$_3$ 外延层通过分子束外延生长，然后在超高真空下转移到单独的生长室，在该生长室中溅射沉积多晶金属多层。通过联合 X 射线和偏振中子反射测量以及扫描透射电子显微镜对样品的结构进行了表征。极化中子模型和标准磁力测量表明，需要超过临界厚度的缓冲层才能在多层中的每个磁性层中获得所需的均匀、垂直磁各向异性。使用具有 Ta 和 Mo 缓冲剂的样品，厚度分别需要 1.5 和 0.9 nm。在最大限度地减少 Bi$_2$Se$_3$ 梯田的过程中，缓冲样品产生了明确的平面外磁域，适用于由 X 射线光电子显微镜确定的自旋轨道扭矩诱导操纵。
