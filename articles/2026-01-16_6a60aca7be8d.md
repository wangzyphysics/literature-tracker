## Abstract
arXiv:2601.10210v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In the thermodynamic limit, the Dicke-Ising chain maps exactly onto an effective self-consistent matter Hamiltonian with the photon field acting solely as a self-consistent effective field. As a consequence, no quantum correlations between photons and spins are needed to understand the quantum phase diagram. This enables us to determine the quantum phase diagram in the thermodynamic limit using numerical linked-cluster expansions combined with density matrix renormalization group calculations (NLCE+DMRG) to solve the resulting self-consistent matter Hamiltonian. This includes magnetically ordered phases with significantly improved accuracy compared to previous estimates. For ferromagnetic Ising couplings, we refine the location of the multicritical point governing the change in the order of the superradiant phase transition, reaching a relative accuracy of $10^{-4}$. For antiferromagnetic Ising couplings, we confirm the existence of the narrow antiferromagnetic superradiant phase in the thermodynamic limit. The effective matter Hamiltonian framework identifies the antiferromagnetic superradiant phase as the many-body ground state of an antiferromagnetic transverse-field Ising model with longitudinal field. This phase emerges through continuous Dicke-type polariton condensation from the antiferromagnetic normal phase, followed by a first-order transition to the paramagnetic superradiant phase. Thus, NLCE+DMRG provides a precise determination of the Dicke-Ising phase diagram in one dimension by solving the self-consistent effective matter Hamiltonian.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.10210v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在热力学极限下，迪克伊辛链精确映射到有效自洽物质哈密顿量，其中光子场仅充当自洽有效场。因此，理解量子相图不需要光子和自旋之间的量子相关性。这使我们能够使用数值链接簇展开结合密度矩阵重正化群计算（NLCE + DMRG）来确定热力学极限下的量子相图，以求解所得的自洽物质哈密顿量。这包括磁排序相，与之前的估计相比，其精度显着提高。对于铁磁伊辛耦合，我们细化了控制超辐射相变顺序变化的多临界点的位置，达到了 10^{-4}$ 的相对精度。对于反铁磁伊辛耦合，我们证实了热力学极限内窄反铁磁超辐射相的存在。有效物质哈密顿框架将反铁磁超辐射相识别为具有纵向场的反铁磁横向场伊辛模型的多体基态。该相是通过从反铁磁正相连续迪克型极化激元凝聚而出现的，然后是向顺磁超辐射相的一级转变。因此，NLCE+DMRG 通过求解自洽有效物质哈密顿量，提供了一维 Dicke-Ising 相图的精确确定。
