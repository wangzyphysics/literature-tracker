## Abstract
arXiv:2412.17183v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We present the design for a thermodynamic computer that can perform arbitrary nonlinear calculations in or out of equilibrium. Simple thermodynamic circuits, fluctuating degrees of freedom in contact with a thermal bath and confined by a quartic potential, display an activity that is a nonlinear function of their input. Such circuits can therefore be regarded as thermodynamic neurons, and can serve as the building blocks of networked structures that act as thermodynamic neural networks, universal function approximators whose operation is powered by thermal fluctuations. We simulate a digital model of a thermodynamic neural network, and show that its parameters can be adjusted by genetic algorithm to perform nonlinear calculations at specified observation times, regardless of whether the system has attained thermal equilibrium. This work expands the field of thermodynamic computing beyond the regime of thermal equilibrium, enabling fully nonlinear computations, analogous to those performed by classical neural networks, at specified observation times.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2412.17183v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一种热力学计算机的设计，它可以在平衡状态或非平衡状态下执行任意非线性计算。简单的热力学电路，与热浴接触的波动自由度并受到四次势的限制，显示出其输入的非线性函数的活动。因此，此类电路可以被视为热力学神经元，并且可以作为网络结构的构建块，这些网络结构充当热力学神经网络、其操作由热波动驱动的通用函数逼近器。我们模拟了热力学神经网络的数字模型，结果表明，无论系统是否达到热平衡，都可以通过遗传算法调整其参数，以在指定的观测时间执行非线性计算。这项工作将热力学计算领域扩展到热平衡范围之外，实现了完全非线性计算，类似于经典神经网络在指定观察时间执行的计算。
