## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22611v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The fast-ion (FI) on turbulent transport is one of the key topics of magnetic confinement fusion. This work focus on the impact of FI pressure gradients on turbulence in a high-\b{eta} plasma scenario using gyrokinetic simulations. Linear analyses reveal that FIs strongly stabilize ion temperature gradient (ITG) modes via the thermal-ion dilution, while their influence on trapped electron modes (TEMs) is minimal. At elevated FI pressure gradients, a transition to a FI-driven BAE (FI-BAE) regime occurs, as evidenced by mode structure and frequency alignment within the Alfv\'enic gap. Electron \b{eta} scans further demonstrate the emergence of kinetic ballooning modes (KBMs) at higher \b{eta}, whereas an ITG-TEM hybrid turbulence dominates near experimental \b{eta} values. Nonlinear simulations show that moderate FI pressure suppresses transport via zonal flow (ZF) shear, whereas strong FI drive weakens ZFs and enhances transport by destabilizing FI-BAEs. These results highlight the dual role of FIs in regulating turbulence and offer insight into multiscale transport physics relevant for high-performance plasmas.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22611v1 发布类型：new 摘要：湍流输运中的快离子（FI）是磁约束聚变的关键课题之一。这项工作的重点是使用回旋模拟来研究 FI 压力梯度对高 b{eta} 等离子体场景中湍流的影响。线性分析表明，FI 通过热离子稀释强烈稳定离子温度梯度 (ITG) 模式，而它们对俘获电子模式 (TEM) 的影响很小。在升高的 FI 压力梯度下，会发生向 FI 驱动的 BAE (FI-BAE) 状态的转变，如 Alfv\'enic 间隙内的模式结构和频率对齐所证明的那样。电子\b{eta}扫描进一步证明了在较高\b{eta}处出现了动力学气球模式（KBM），而ITG-TEM混合湍流在实验\b{eta}值附近占主导地位。非线性模拟表明，适度的 FI 压力会通过层流 (ZF) 剪切抑制输运，而强 FI 驱动会削弱 ZF，并通过破坏 FI-BAE 的稳定性来增强输运。这些结果强调了 FI 在调节湍流中的双重作用，并提供了对与高性能等离子体相关的多尺度输运物理的深入了解。
