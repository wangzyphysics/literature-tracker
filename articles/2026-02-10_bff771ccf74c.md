## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08358v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-triplet superconductivity is an intriguing quantum coherent state with both spin and orbital degrees of freedom, which holds significant potential for future applications in quantum technology. However, how the spin of the triplet pairs responds to an external magnetic field remains poorly understood. This is mainly due to the absence of suitable spin-triplet superconductors. Here, we report results of Knight-shift and ac-susceptibility measurements on UTe$_2$. We demonstrate that the spin susceptibility, which slightly decreases compared to the normal-state value below the superconducting (SC) transition temperature $T_{\rm c}$, is rapidly restored and nearly recovers to the normal-state values around 5 T, well below the SC upper critical field $H_{c2}$ when the magnetic field is applied along the $c$ axis ($H \parallel c$). In addition, we found that $H_{\rm c2}$ of superconductivity becomes larger when the SC spin aligns with the magnetic field. By considering the results on $H \parallel b$, our results suggest the presence of a close relationship between the spin configuration of the triplet pair and $H_{\rm c2}$, as well as the anisotropic pinning interaction acting on the triplet pairs. These phenomena, which have never been observed in spin-singlet superconductors, represent characteristic features unique to spin-triplet superconductors. We discuss the similarities between superconductivity in UTe$_2$ and superfluid $^3$He, focusing on their spin-triplet pairing states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08358v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋三重态超导是一种有趣的量子相干态，具有自旋和轨道自由度，在量子技术的未来应用中具有巨大的潜力。然而，三重态对的自旋如何响应外部磁场仍然知之甚少。这主要是由于缺乏合适的自旋三重态超导体。在这里，我们报告了 UTe$_2$ 的奈特位移和交流磁化率测量结果。我们证明，当沿 $c$ 轴（$H \parallel c$）施加磁场时，与低于超导（SC）转变温度 $T_{\rm c}$ 的正常状态值相比，自旋磁化率略有下降，但在 5 T 左右几乎恢复到正常状态值，远低于 SC 上临界场 $H_{c2}$。此外，我们发现当SC自旋与磁场一致时，超导性的$H_{\rm c2}$变得更大。通过考虑 $H \parallel b$ 上的结果，我们的结果表明三重态对的自旋构型与 $H_{\rm c2}$ 之间存在密切关系，以及作用于三重态对的各向异性钉扎相互作用。这些现象在自旋单线态超导体中从未观察到，但代表了自旋三线态超导体独有的特征。我们讨论 UTe$_2$ 和超流体 $^3$He 中的超导性之间的相似性，重点关注它们的自旋三重态配对状态。
