## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08585v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Excitons in monolayer transition-metal dichalcogenides (TMDs) have garnered significant attention because of their large binding energies due to weakly screened Coulomb interaction, and direct bandgap at the K/K$^\prime$ point in the hexagonal Brillouin zone featuring spin-polarised bands due to spin-orbit coupling and lack of inversion symmetry. This makes them prospective for next-generation optoelectronic and quantum devices. However, despite the intense research activity, the reported values for exciton binding energies, quasiparticle gaps, and spectral features exhibit substantial variation across both experimental and theoretical studies. In this article, we present a comprehensive and critical assessment of the current understanding of excitonic properties in single-layer TMDs, integrating results from the angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES), photoluminescence (PL) measurements, and other experimental techniques with first-principles theoretical insights. Special emphasis is placed on the comparison and reconciliation of discrepancies observed across different experimental setups and sample qualities. Furthermore, we highlight our state-of-the-art GW-BSE calculations, which include both equilibrium and laterally strained systems, to systematically analyse the behaviour of direct and indirect excitons. By evaluating the effect of strain as a tunable control variable, we demonstrate its potential to engineer excitonic properties, supported by cross-validation against prior theoretical predictions and experimental findings. In doing so, we clarify the sources of discrepancies in the literature and offer a unified perspective on excited-state engineering strategies in two-dimensional TMDs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08585v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：单层过渡金属二硫化物 (TMD) 中的激子因其弱屏蔽库仑相互作用而具有较大的结合能，并且由于自旋轨道耦合和缺乏反转而在六角形布里渊区的 K/K$^\prime$ 点处具有直接带隙，具有自旋极化带，因此引起了极大的关注对称性。这使它们有望成为下一代光电和量子设备。然而，尽管研究活动很激烈，但所报道的激子结合能、准粒子能隙和光谱特征的值在实验和理论研究中都表现出很大的变化。在本文中，我们将角分辨光电子能谱 (ARPES)、光致发光 (PL) 测量和其他实验技术的结果与第一性原理理论见解相结合，对当前对单层 TMD 激子特性的理解进行了全面而批判性的评估。特别强调不同实验设置和样本质量之间观察到的差异的比较和协调。此外，我们还重点介绍了最先进的 GW-BSE 计算，其中包括平衡系统和横向应变系统，以系统地分析直接和间接激子的行为。通过评估应变作为可调控制变量的影响，我们证明了其设计激子特性的潜力，并得到了与先前理论预测和实验结果的交叉验证的支持。在此过程中，我们澄清了文献中差异的来源，并为二维 TMD 中的激发态工程策略提供了统一的视角。
