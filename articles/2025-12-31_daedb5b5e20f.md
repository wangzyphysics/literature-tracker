## Abstract
Abstract Equivariant graph neural networks have shown remarkable success in molecular property prediction, but their performance on novel molecular geometries remains limited without extensive training data. We present a computationally efficient approach to cross-geometry pretraining for molecular systems that improves transfer learning performance within severe computational constraints (single GPU, <100 GPU-hours). Using hybrid EGNN-PaiNN architecturesand strategic pretraining on diverse molecular geometries from QM9 and MD17 datasets, we achieve 47% average improvement in few-shot force prediction compared to training from scratch. Our methodology demonstrates that effective geometry transfer can be achieved through careful dataset sampling and multi-task learning strategies, even under limited computational budgets. We provide complete code and reproducible training protocols to facilitate adoption by resource-constrained research groups. While current experiments focus on small organic molecules, the approach establishes a foundation for extending equivariant pretraining to broader molecular systems.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 等变图神经网络在分子特性预测方面取得了显着的成功，但如果没有大量的训练数据，它们在新型分子几何形状上的性能仍然有限。我们提出了一种计算高效的分子系统跨几何预训练方法，可在严格的计算限制（单 GPU，<100 GPU 小时）内提高迁移学习性能。使用混合 EGNN-PaiNN 架构以及对来自 QM9 和 MD17 数据集的不同分子几何形状进行战略预训练，与从头开始训练相比，我们在几次射击力预测方面实现了 47% 的平均改进。我们的方法表明，即使在有限的计算预算下，也可以通过仔细的数据集采样和多任务学习策略来实现有效的几何迁移。我们提供完整的代码和可重复的培训协议，以方便资源有限的研究小组采用。虽然目前的实验主要集中在有机小分子上，但该方法为将等变预训练扩展到更广泛的分子系统奠定了基础。
