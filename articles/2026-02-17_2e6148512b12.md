## Abstract
arXiv:2409.01873v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: We consider quantum transport in a tight-binding model on the Bethe lattice of finite generation, which we expect to be the first step toward analyzing electronic transport in a light-harvesting molecule. We seek conditions under which the electronic current from the peripheral sites to the central site reaches its maximum. As a new feature, we add complex potentials for sources at peripheral sites and a drain at the central site, and solve a non-Hermitian eigenvalue problem, instead of simulating an initial-value problem. Solving the eigenvalue problem clearly reveals which electronic channels contribute most to the quantum transport. We find that the number of eigenstates that can penetrate from the peripheral sites to the central site is limited among the total number of eigenstates. All the other eigenstates are localized around the peripheral sites and cannot reach the central site. The former eigenstates can carry current, reducing the problem to quantum transport on a parity-time symmetric tight-binding chain. We find that the current has a maximum with respect to the strengths of the sources and the drain. Counterintuitively, the current decreases as we increase the strengths beyond the maximum and vanishes in the limit of infinite strength. Moreover, we find that the current maximum is given by a zero mode. When the number of links is common to all generations, the current takes the maximum value at the exceptional point where two eigenstates coalesce to a zero mode, which emerges because of the non-Hermiticity due to the PT-symmetric complex potentials. By introducing randomness either into the hopping amplitude or the number of links in each generation of the tree, we obtain a random-hopping tight-binding model, and find that the current reaches its maximum not exactly, but approximately, for a zero mode, although it is no longer located at an exceptional point in general.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2409.01873v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：我们考虑有限代 Bethe 晶格紧束缚模型中的量子输运，我们期望这是分析光捕获分子中电子输运的第一步。我们寻求从外围站点到中心站点的电子电流达到最大值的条件。作为一项新功能，我们为外围站点的源和中心站点的漏添加了复杂的势，并解决了非厄米特特征值问题，而不是模拟初始值问题。解决特征值问题清楚地揭示了哪些电子通道对量子传输贡献最大。我们发现，能够从外围位点渗透到中心位点的本征态数量在本征态总数中是有限的。所有其他本征态都集中在外围站点周围，无法到达中心站点。以前的本征态可以承载电流，从而减少宇称时间对称紧束缚链上的量子传输问题。我们发现，相对于源极和漏极的强度，电流具有最大值。与直觉相反，当我们将强度增加到超过最大值时，电流会减小，并在无限强度的极限下消失。此外，我们发现当前的最大值是由零模式给出的。当所有代的链接数量相同时，电流在两个本征态合并到零模式的特殊点处取最大值，这是由于 PT 对称复势导致的非厄米性而出现的。通过将随机性引入每一代树中的跳跃幅度或链接数量，我们获得了随机跳跃紧束缚模型，并发现对于零模式，电流不完全达到最大值，而是近似达到最大值，尽管它通常不再位于异常点。
