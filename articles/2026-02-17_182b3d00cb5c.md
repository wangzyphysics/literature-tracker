## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13251v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Project Based Learning (PBL), recognized as an active learning strategy, has been linked to self efficacy of student in prior studies, including those within Physics Education Research. Meanwhile, technological advancements have significantly facilitated the optimization of diverse learning modes, including online learning. However, comprehensive investigations addressing questions such as how students perceive PBL and their self efficacy, what forms of PBL design they prefer, and what benefits and challenges they encounter during its implementation remain underexplored in existing literature. This study sought to uncover the experiences of ten students through longitudinal observation in an online PBL Physics class, focusing on its influence on their self efficacy within a phenomenological study. Data were collected via semi structured, in depth interviews. Social Cognitive Theory, Self Efficacy theory of Bandura, and theoretical framework for the impact of project based learning on educational outcomes were employed as guiding frameworks to shape the interpretation of students experiences and perspectives. Through Interpretative Phenomenological Analysis (IPA), the researchers identified four primary themes representing the experience of students. There are, variety of unique learning experiences based on students perceptions about their character, pivotal experience as a catalyst in the learning process, student self efficacy in Project Based Learning, and self reflection based on prior experience. The findings contribute novel insights for educators in implementing PBL, particularly in online contexts, by highlighting strategies to accommodate diverse student personality characteristics. Moreover, the study provides helpful references for longitudinal research on the interplay between PBL and self efficacy.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13251v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基于项目的学习 (PBL) 被认为是一种主动学习策略，在之前的研究（包括物理教育研究中的研究）中已与学生的自我效能相关。同时，技术的进步极大地促进了包括在线学习在内的多种学习模式的优化。然而，现有文献中尚未充分探讨针对学生如何看待 PBL 及其自我效能、他们喜欢什么形式的 PBL 设计以及他们在实施过程中遇到的好处和挑战等问题的全面调查。本研究试图通过纵向观察在线 PBL 物理课来揭示 10 名学生的经历，重点关注其在现象学研究中对他们自我效能的影响。数据是通过半结构化的深度访谈收集的。社会认知理论、班杜拉的自我效能理论以及基于项目的学习对教育成果影响的理论框架被用作指导框架，以形成对学生经历和观点的解释。通过解释现象学分析（IPA），研究人员确定了代表学生经历的四个主要主题。基于学生对自己性格的看法，有各种独特的学习体验，作为学习过程催化剂的关键经验，基于项目的学习中的学生自我效能，以及基于先前经验的自我反思。研究结果强调了适应不同学生个性特征的策略，为教育工作者实施 PBL（特别是在在线环境中）提供了新颖的见解。此外，该研究为PBL与自我效能相互作用的纵向研究提供了有益的参考。
