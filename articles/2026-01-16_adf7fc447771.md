## Abstract
The ability to track therapeutic cells is critical for advancing adoptive cell therapy (ACT). Positron emission tomography (PET) offers sensitive and quantitative imaging, yet improved cell radiolabeling strategies are sorely needed. We report a metabolic glycoengineering (MGE) approach that installs azide moieties on Jurkat T lymphocyte surface using the canonical derivative Ac₄ManNAz, enabling bioorthogonal radiolabeling via strain-promoted azide– alkyne cycloaddition (SPAAC) and inverse electron-demand Diels–Alder (IEDDA) chemistry. A dual-clickable sulfo-DBCO-PEG₄-TCO intermediate was synthesized to bridge these functionalities, allowing radiolabeling with aluminum [¹⁸F]fluoride-tetrazine (Al[¹⁸F]F-Tz) through cell-surface TCOs. Optimization of the labeling conditions yielded robust in vitro radiolabeling without compromising cell viability. Both Al[¹⁸F]F-Tz and in vitro fluorine-18-labeled Jurkat cells were subsequently evaluated in vivo in athymic nude mice, alongside preliminary pretargeting studies with TCO-modified cells. In vitro fluorine-18-labeled cells exhibited predictable trafficking and biodistribution, supporting the suitability of this radiolabeling strategy for cell tracking with PET, whereas pretargeted imaging requires further optimization, revealing mostly the hepatobiliary excretion of the Al[¹⁸F]F-Tz tracer. Overall, these findings underscore the potential of MGE-mediated bioorthogonal radiolabeling for noninvasive tracking of immune cells and provide a foundation for further development of pretargeted imaging approaches for cell transplants.

## 摘要（中文）
追踪治疗细胞的能力对于推进过继细胞疗法（ACT）至关重要。正电子发射断层扫描 (PET) 提供灵敏的定量成像，但迫切需要改进的细胞放射性标记策略。我们报告了一种代谢糖工程 (MGE) 方法，该方法使用经典衍生物 Ac₄ManNAz 在 Jurkat T 淋巴细胞表面安装叠氮化物部分，通过应变促进的叠氮化物-炔环加成 (SPAAC) 和逆电子需求 Diels-Alder (IEDDA) 化学实现生物正交放射性标记。合成了一种双可点击的磺基-DBCO-PEG4-TCO中间体来桥接这些功能，允许通过细胞表面TCO用铝[18F]氟化物-四嗪（Al[18F]F-Tz）进行放射性标记。标记条件的优化产生了强大的体外放射性标记，而不影响细胞活力。随后在无胸腺裸鼠体内评估 Al[18F]F-Tz 和体外氟 18 标记的 Jurkat 细胞，同时对 TCO 修饰细胞进行初步预靶向研究。体外氟 18 标记细胞表现出可预测的运输和生物分布，支持这种放射性标记策略用于 PET 细胞追踪的适用性，而预靶向成像需要进一步优化，主要揭示 Al[18F]F-Tz 示踪剂的肝胆排泄。总体而言，这些发现强调了 MGE 介导的生物正交放射性标记在免疫细胞无创追踪方面的潜力，并为进一步开发细胞移植的预靶向成像方法奠定了基础。
