## Abstract
arXiv:2506.06681v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We report the quantization, quantum oscillations, and singular behavior of sublattice symmetry-breaking sublattice charge order (SCO) in two-dimensional Dirac insulators at charge neutrality under perpendicular magnetic fields $B$. SCO is induced by staggered sublattice potentials, such as those originating from substrates, strains, hydrogenation, and chemical doping. In small non-quantizing magnetic fields that result in less than a flux quantum threading the system, and small sublattice symmetry breaking potentials, SCO exhibits perturbative singular magnetic field dependence, $\sim |B|$, originating from hopping between neighboring sites of the same sublattice. At intermediate magnetic fields, when the cyclotron gap between the zeroth Landau level and the first Landau level, $\omega_c$, is smaller than the sublattice potential, $\omega_c\lesssim \Delta$, SCO shows $\textit{universally}$ quantized plateaus owing to discrete Landau-level degeneracy. As the magnetic flux increases by one flux quantum, one electron (per spin) is transferred from the sublattice with a higher chemical potential to the sublattice with a lower chemical potential. One electron transfer between sublattices per flux quantum results from the sublattice polarization of the zeroth Landau level in gapped Dirac materials, realizing the topological Thouless pump effect. At stronger magnetic fields, $\omega_c\gtrsim \ \Delta$, corresponding to integer quantum Hall regimes, SCO displays singularities based on the physics of quantum magneto-oscillations. Our findings suggest new ways to experimentally detect the presence of the energy gap in Dirac materials, irrespective of the gap size.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.06681v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们报告了垂直磁场 $B$ 下电荷中性的二维狄拉克绝缘体中亚晶格对称破缺的亚晶格电荷序 (SCO) 的量子化、量子振荡和奇异行为。 SCO 是由交错的亚晶格电势引起的，例如源自基底、应变、氢化和化学掺杂的亚晶格电势。在导致小于穿过系统的通量量子和小的亚晶格对称破缺势的小非量子化磁场中，SCO表现出扰动奇异磁场依赖性$\sim |B|$，源自同一子晶格的相邻位点之间的跳跃。在中间磁场下，当第零朗道能级和第一朗道能级之间的回旋间隙 $\omega_c$ 小于亚晶格势 $\omega_c\lesssim \Delta$ 时，SCO 由于离散朗道能级简并而表现出 $\textit{universally}$ 量子化平台。当磁通量增加一个通量量子时，一个电子（每次自旋）从具有较高化学势的亚晶格转移到具有较低化学势的亚晶格。每个通量量子的亚晶格之间的一次电子转移是由带隙狄拉克材料中第零朗道能级的亚晶格极化引起的，实现了拓扑Thouless泵浦效应。在更强的磁场 $\omega_c\gtrsim \ \Delta$ 下，对应于整数量子霍尔体系，SCO 显示基于量子磁振荡物理学的奇点。我们的研究结果提出了通过实验检测狄拉克材料中能隙存在的新方法，无论能隙大小如何。
