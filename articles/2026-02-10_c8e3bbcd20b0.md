## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08893v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Due to their interactive nature, serious games offer valuable opportunities for supporting learning in educational contexts. Recent advances in large language models (LLMs) have further opened the door to new forms of personalized scaffolding in education. In this study, we combine both worlds and study three types of AI-based scaffolding designs in a serious game: (i) no scaffolding, (ii) chat-based (verbal) scaffolding provided by an AI-based non-player character (NPC), and (iii) combined chat-(verbal) and action-based (visual) scaffolding in which the AI may both try to explain or demonstrate the next step towards a solution. The scaffolding conditions are embedded in Qookies, a serious game designed to introduce fundamental concepts of quantum technologies. A total of 152 school students, university students, and members of the general public were randomly assigned to one of the three conditions. The results show that all groups experience significant learning gains, confirming the overall effectiveness of the serious game itself. No significant differences in learning outcomes emerged between scaffolding conditions. However, intrinsic cognitive load was lower in the combined chat-and-action (verbal+visual) scaffolding condition compared to the chat (verbal)-only condition, suggesting that visual demonstrations may offer more accessible support. Interaction analyses further revealed that players engaged with the AI character primarily for level-related questions and action recommendations, while deeper interactions were relatively rare.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08893v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于其互动性，严肃游戏为支持教育环境中的学习提供了宝贵的机会。大语言模型 (LLM) 的最新进展进一步为新形式的个性化教育支架打开了大门。在这项研究中，我们结合了这两个世界，研究了严肃游戏中三种基于人工智能的脚手架设计：(i) 无脚手架，(ii) 由基于人工智能的非玩家角色 (NPC) 提供的基于聊天（语言）的脚手架，以及 (iii) 组合聊天（语言）和基于动作（视觉）的脚手架，其中人工智能可能会尝试解释或演示解决方案的下一步。脚手架条件嵌入在 Qookies 中，这是一款旨在介绍量子技术基本概念的严肃游戏。共有 152 名中学生、大学生和公众被随机分配到这三种情况之一。结果表明，所有小组都获得了显着的学习收益，证实了严肃游戏本身的整体有效性。支架条件之间的学习成果没有显着差异。然而，与仅聊天（口头）条件相比，在组合聊天和动作（口头+视觉）支架条件下，内在认知负荷较低，这表明视觉演示可以提供更容易获得的支持。交互分析进一步表明，玩家与 AI 角色的互动主要是为了与关卡相关的问题和行动建议，而更深层次的交互相对较少。
