## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07747v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report the first combined study of the low-energy electronic band structure of epitaxial Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ films using angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES) and density functional theory (DFT). The complete Fermi-surface topography of the near-Fermi-level bands is determined from in-situ ARPES measurements. To investigate the effects of substrate-induced strain on the band structure, Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ thin films are epitaxially grown on SrTiO$_3$ (STO) and (LaAlO$_{3}$)$_{0.3}$(Sr$_{2}$TaAlO$_{6}$)$_{0.7}$ (LSAT) substrates using molecular beam epitaxy. The combination of the measured Fermi-surfaces along with the theoretical interpretation, clearly show dramatic changes in the Fermi surface topologies that result from the underlying strain states of the films on the two substrates. We find that the Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ films prepared on STO are tensile strained with tetragonal symmetry, whereas those grown on LSAT are compressively strained with orthorhombic symmetry. Within $\sim15~\text{meV}$ below the Fermi level, we observe two flat bands along $\Gamma$-$X$ in the orthorhombic phase and around $\Gamma$ in the tetragonal phase. These features could be favorable for van Hove singularities near the Fermi level, and highlight the emergence of magnetic instabilities in epitaxial Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ films.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07747v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报告了首次使用角分辨光电子能谱 (ARPES) 和密度泛函理论 (DFT) 对外延 Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ 薄膜的低能电子能带结构进行的联合研究。近费米能带的完整费米表面形貌是通过原位 ARPES 测量确定的。为了研究基底引起的应变对能带结构的影响，使用分子束外延在 SrTiO$_3$ (STO) 和 (LaAlO$_{3}$)$_{0.3}$(Sr$_{2}$TaAlO$_{6}$)$_{0.7}$ (LSAT) 基底上外延生长 Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ 薄膜。测量的费米表面与理论解释相结合，清楚地显示了费米表面拓扑的巨大变化，这是由两个基板上薄膜的潜在应变状态引起的。我们发现在 STO 上制备的 Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ 薄膜具有四方对称的拉伸应变，而在 LSAT 上生长的薄膜则具有正交对称的压缩应变。在费米能级以下的 $\sim15~\text{meV}$ 内，我们观察到正交相中沿 $\Gamma$-$X$ 和四方相中 $\Gamma$ 周围的两个平带。这些特征可能有利于费米能级附近的范霍夫奇点，并突出了外延 Sr$_3$Ru$_2$O$_7$ 薄膜中磁不稳定性的出现。
