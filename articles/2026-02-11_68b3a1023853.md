## Abstract
arXiv:2503.10712v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We compute the magnetoelectric conductivity for ideal nodal-line semimetals (NLSMs), with a finite but tiny mass-gap, in distinct planar-Hall set-ups. Each differing configuration results from the relative orientation of the nodal-ring's plane with respect to the plane spanned by the electric ($\mathbf E $) and magnetic ($\mathbf B$) fields. The net conductivity tensor has components comprising the Drude, anomalous-Hall, in-plane (with $\mathbf E$ and $\mathbf B$) longitudinal and transverse, and Lorentz-force-operator-induced parts. Our results feature the signatures of the inherent topology of a gapped NLSM, revealed through nonzero values of the Berry curvature and the orbital magnetic moment. In particular, we show that both of these vector fields, arising in the momentum space, give rise to terms of comparable magnitudes in the resulting response. Our explicit theoretical expressions will help identify unique signatures of NLSMs in contemporary experiments.

arXiv:2503.10712v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们在不同的平面霍尔设置中计算具有有限但微小质量间隙的理想节点线半金属 (NLSM) 的磁电导率。每种不同的配置都是由节环平面相对于电场 ($\mathbf E $) 和磁场 ($\mathbf B$) 所跨越的平面的相对方向产生的。净电导率张量的分量包括德鲁德、反常霍尔、面内（具有 $\mathbf E$ 和 $\mathbf B$）纵向和横向部分以及洛伦兹力算子引起的部分。我们的结果具有带隙 NLSM 固有拓扑的特征，通过贝里曲率和轨道磁矩的非零值揭示。特别是，我们表明，在动量空间中出现的这两个矢量场在所得响应中产生了相当大小的项。我们明确的理论表达将有助于识别当代实验中 NLSM 的独特特征。
