## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09672v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Relying on atomic scattering factors from evaluated databases, a new model for the reflectivity of x rays on solid surfaces has been developed for FLUKA v4-6.0. This model accounts for the variation of reflectivity as a function of the photon energy, its incidence angle, and linear polarisation; surface roughness effects are also taken into account. FLUKA reflectivities agree well with those obtained from state-of-the-art codes used for the characterization of optical devices, both for homogeneous solids and for multilayer mirrors. This new capability renders FLUKA a nearly one-stop shop for synchrotron radiation simulations: emission from bending magnets and wigglers, photon transport and interaction, electromagnetic (and hadronic when applicable) shower development in complex geometries, as well as x-ray reflection at designated solid surfaces can now be all accounted for in a single FLUKA run. This streamlined FLUKA simulation workflow greatly simplifies the plethora of simulation tools that Monte Carlo practitioners previously needed to rely on. Two application scenarios of this new reflectivity model are showcased: first, the use of a multilayer mirror to deflect x rays from an optical hutch onto an experimental hall at the MINERVA beamline of the ALBA synchrotron and, second, the assessment of the photon flux near the interaction point at the CERN's Future Circular Collider (in its electron-positron stage) as a result of upstream x-ray reflections.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09672v1 公告类型：新 摘要：依靠评估数据库中的原子散射因子，为 FLUKA v4-6.0 开发了固体表面上 X 射线反射率的新模型。该模型解释了反射率随光子能量、入射角和线偏振的变化；还考虑了表面粗糙度的影响。 FLUKA 反射率与从用于表征光学器件（均匀固体和多层镜）的最先进代码中获得的反射率非常吻合。这一新功能使 FLUKA 几乎成为同步加速器辐射模拟的一站式商店：弯曲磁铁和摆动器的发射、光子传输和相互作用、复杂几何形状中的电磁（和强子，如果适用）簇射发展，以及指定固体表面的 X 射线反射现在都可以在单次 FLUKA 运行中得到解释。这种简化的 FLUKA 仿真工作流程极大地简化了蒙特卡洛从业者以前需要依赖的大量仿真工具。展示了这种新反射率模型的两个应用场景：首先，使用多层镜将来自光学棚的 X 射线偏转到 ALBA 同步加速器的 MINERVA 光束线的实验大厅上；其次，评估 CERN 未来圆形对撞机（处于其电子-正电子阶段）相互作用点附近由于上游 X 射线反射而产生的光子通量。
