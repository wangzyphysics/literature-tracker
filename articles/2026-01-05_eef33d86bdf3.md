## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00330v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Effective graph resistance is a fundamental structural metric in network science, widely used to quantify global connectivity, compare network architectures, and assess robustness in flow-based systems. Despite its importance, current formulations rely mainly on spectral or pseudo-inverse Laplacian representations, offering limited physical insight into how structural features shape this quantity or how it can be efficiently optimized. Here, we establish an exact and physically transparent relationship between effective graph resistance and the cumulative heat dissipation generated by Laplacian diffusion dynamics. We show that the total heat dissipated during relaxation to equilibrium precisely equals the effective graph resistance. This dynamical viewpoint uncovers a natural multi-scale decomposition of the Laplacian spectrum: early-time dissipation is governed by degree-based local structure, intermediate times isolate eigenvalues below the spectral mean, and long times are dominated by the algebraic connectivity. These multi-scale properties yield continuous and interpretable strategies for modifying network structure and constructing optimized ensembles, enabling improvements that are otherwise NP-hard to achieve via combinatorial methods. Our results unify structural and dynamical perspectives on network connectivity and provide new tools for analyzing, comparing, and optimizing complex networks across domains.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00330v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：有效图阻力是网络科学中的基本结构度量，广泛用于量化全局连接、比较网络架构以及评估基于流的系统的鲁棒性。尽管它很重要，但当前的公式主要依赖于谱或伪逆拉普拉斯表示，对于结构特征如何塑造这个量或如何有效地优化它提供了有限的物理洞察力。在这里，我们在有效图形电阻与拉普拉斯扩散动力学产生的累积散热之间建立了精确且物理透明的关系。我们证明，弛豫到平衡期间耗散的总热量恰好等于有效图电阻。这种动力学观点揭示了拉普拉斯谱的自然多尺度分解：早期耗散由基于度的局部结构控制，中间时间隔离低于谱平均值的特征值，而长时间则由代数连通性主导。这些多尺度特性产生了连续且可解释的策略，用于修改网络结构和构建优化的集成，从而实现通过组合方法难以实现的改进。我们的结果统一了网络连接的结构和动态视角，并提供了跨领域分析、比较和优化复杂网络的新工具。
