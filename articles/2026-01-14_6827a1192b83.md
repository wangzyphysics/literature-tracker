## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08381v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: When we consider canonical averages for classical discrete systems, typically referred to as substitutional alloys, the map from many-body interatomic interactions to thermodynamic equilibrium configurations generally exhibits complicated nonlinearity. This canonical nonlinearity is fundamentally rooted in deviations of the discrete configurational density of states (CDOS) from continuous Gaussian families, and has conventionally been characterized by the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence on discrete statistical manifold. Thus, the previous works inevitablly missed intrinsic nonlinearities induced by discretization of Gaussian families, which remains invisible within conventional information-geometric descriptions. In the present work, we identify and quantify such unavoidable canonical nonlinearity by employing the 2-Wasserstein distance with a cost function aligned with the Fisher metric for Gaussian families. We derive an explicit expression for the Wasserstein distance in the limit of vanishing discretization scale d to 0: W2 = d*sqrt(Tr(Gamma)^(-1)/12), where Gamma denotes covariance matrix of the CDOS. We further show that this limiting Wasserstein distance admits a clear geometric interpretation on the statistical manifold, equivalent to a KL divergence associated with the expected parallel translations of continuous Gaussian. Our framework thus provides a transport-information-geometric characterization of discretization-induced nonlinearity in classical discrete systems, with future potential applications to configurational thermodynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08381v1 公告类型：新 摘要：当我们考虑经典离散系统（通常称为替代合金）的规范平均值时，从多体原子间相互作用到热力学平衡配置的映射通常表现出复杂的非线性。这种正则非线性从根本上根源于离散构型态密度（CDOS）与连续高斯族的偏差，并且通常用离散统计流形上的库尔贝克-莱布勒（KL）散度来表征。因此，以前的工作不可避免地错过了由高斯族离散化引起的内在非线性，这在传统的信息几何描述中仍然是不可见的。在目前的工作中，我们通过采用 2-Wasserstein 距离以及与高斯族费舍尔度量一致的成本函数来识别和量化这种不可避免的规范非线性。我们在消失离散化尺度 d 到 0 的极限下推导出 Wasserstein 距离的显式表达式：W2 = d*sqrt(Tr(Gamma)^(-1)/12)，其中 Gamma 表示 CDOS 的协方差矩阵。我们进一步表明，这种限制 Wasserstein 距离允许对统计流形进行清晰的几何解释，相当于与连续高斯的预期平行平移相关的 KL 散度。因此，我们的框架提供了经典离散系统中离散化引起的非线性的传输信息几何表征，以及未来在构型热力学中的潜在应用。
