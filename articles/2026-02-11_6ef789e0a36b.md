## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09647v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurate extraction of the complex dielectric constant in the terahertz (THz) band is essential for material characterization and non-destructive evaluation yet remains challenging due to the ill-posed nature of electromagnetic inverse problems and the limited availability of reliable reference data. In this work, a field-material couple neural network (FMCNN) is proposed to retrieve the complex dielectric constant directly from THz measurements. The FMCNN consists of a field neural network and a material neural network that are strongly coupled through the frequency-domain Maxwell equations in the form of a Helmholtz equation, with the governing physics enforced by partial differential equation (PDE) and boundary condition constraints. This formulation enables prior-free and data-free inversion, requiring only measured test data as input. The extracted dielectric constants are validated by comparison with results from a one-dimensional normal-incidence model and the Drude-Lorentz model, showing good agreement over a broad frequency range, particularly above 0.2 THz. These results demonstrate that the FMCNN provides a physics-consistent and data-efficient approach for material parameter extraction in the THz band, offering an alternative to conventional model-based methods.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09647v1 公告类型：新 摘要：准确提取太赫兹 (THz) 频段的复杂介电常数对于材料表征和无损评估至关重要，但由于电磁反演问题的不适定性质和可靠参考数据的可用性有限，仍然具有挑战性。在这项工作中，提出了一种场材料耦合神经网络（FMCNN）来直接从太赫兹测量中检索复介电常数。 FMCNN 由场神经网络和材料神经网络组成，它们通过亥姆霍兹方程形式的频域麦克斯韦方程强耦合，并由偏微分方程 (PDE) 和边界条件约束强制执行控制物理。该公式可实现无先验和无数据反演，仅需要测量的测试数据作为输入。通过与一维法向入射模型和 Drude-Lorentz 模型的结果进行比较，验证了提取的介电常数，在较宽的频率范围内（特别是 0.2 THz 以上）显示出良好的一致性。这些结果表明，FMCNN 为太赫兹频段的材料参数提取提供了一种物理一致且数据高效的方法，为传统的基于模型的方法提供了替代方案。
