## Abstract
arXiv:2511.10235v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Optical coherence tomography (OCT) microscope (OCM) uses a high-numerical-aperture objective to achieve cellular-level lateral resolution. However, its practical imaging depth range is limited by the depth of focus (DOF). Although computational refocusing can potentially provide sharp images outside the DOF, signal reduction by the confocal effect still limits the imaging depth in practice in point-scanning OCT. In addition, standard OCT cannot visualize intra-tissue activities. To overcome these limitations, we demonstrated a spatially coherent full-field OCM (SC-FFOCM) with computational refocusing. In addition, a repetitive acquisition protocol was designed to visualize intra-tissue activities (i.e., dynamic OCT imaging). The in-focus lateral resolution is 1.4 um, and the axial resolution is 6.5 um (in air) at full-width at half-maximum intensity. Three-dimensional structure and the dynamic OCT imaging using SC-FFOCM with computational refocusing was applied to human breast adenocarcinoma spheroids (MCF-7 cell line). Volumetric dynamic imaging with cellular-level lateral resolution was demonstrated over the full depth of the spheroid.

arXiv:2511.10235v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：光学相干断层扫描 (OCT) 显微镜 (OCM) 使用高数值孔径物镜来实现细胞级横向分辨率。然而，其实际成像深度范围受到焦深（DOF）的限制。尽管计算重聚焦有可能在自由度之外提供清晰的图像，但共焦效应造成的信号减少仍然限制了点扫描 OCT 实践中的成像深度。此外，标准 OCT 无法可视化组织内活动。为了克服这些限制，我们展示了具有计算重聚焦功能的空间相干全场 OCM (SC-FFOCM)。此外，重复采集协议旨在可视化组织内活动（即动态 OCT 成像）。焦点内横向分辨率为 1.4 um，轴向分辨率为 6.5 um（空气中），半高强度全宽。使用 SC-FFOCM 和计算重聚焦的三维结构和动态 OCT 成像应用于人乳腺腺癌球体（MCF-7 细胞系）。在球体的整个深度上展示了具有细胞水平横向分辨率的体积动态成像。
