## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08793v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This perspective introduces attosecond path qubits: measurement-defined two-level subsystems that arise naturally in strong-field physics from the coherent interference of distinguishable quantum pathways. These effective qubits are dynamical superposition states formed during laser-driven sub-cycle electron motion and appear in diverse settings, including short-long trajectories in high-harmonic generation (HHG), early-late ionization bursts in multicolor fields, polarization-selective recombination channels, and momentum or energy-bin interference in above-threshold ionization (ATI). Attosecond qubits can be prepared, coherently manipulated, and read out using existing attosecond techniques, providing a compact Hilbert-space description of strong-field dynamics beyond semiclassical trajectory pictures. A density-matrix formulation makes explicit the roles of coherence, dephasing, and decoherence, arising from continuum propagation, Coulomb interaction, infrared dressing, and macroscopic averaging, within a reduced description that remains far from thermalization. The attosecond qubits framework identifies new opportunities for ultrafast coherence metrology and for probing open quantum dynamics in strongly driven, time-dependent Hamiltonians.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08793v1 公告类型：新 摘要：该视角介绍了阿秒路径量子位：测量定义的两级子系统，在强场物理中自然出现，源自可区分量子路径的相干干涉。这些有效量子位是激光驱动子周期电子运动过程中形成的动态叠加态，并出现在不同的环境中，包括高次谐波产生（HHG）中的短长轨迹、多色场中的早晚电离爆发、偏振选择性复合通道以及阈值以上电离（ATI）中的动量或能量仓干扰。可以使用现有的阿秒技术来准备、连贯地操纵和读出阿秒量子位，从而提供超越半经典轨迹图的强场动力学的紧凑希尔伯特空间描述。密度矩阵公式在远离热化的简化描述中明确了由连续传播、库仑相互作用、红外修整和宏观平均引起的相干、相移和退相干的作用。阿秒量子位框架为超快相干计量学和探索强驱动、时间相关的哈密顿量中的开放量子动力学提供了新的机会。
