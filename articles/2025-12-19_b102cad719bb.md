# Data-driven multiscale modeling for correcting dynamical systems

用于校正动力系统的数据驱动的多尺度建模

## Abstract
We propose a multiscale approach for predicting quantities in dynamical systems which is explicitly structured to extract information in both fine-to-coarse and coarse-to-fine directions. Our approach improves model accuracy and stability with minimally increased computation compared to non-multiscale approaches with analogous network architecture. We evaluate our approach on an idealized fluid subgrid parameterization (known as closure) task in which our multiscale networks correct chaotic underlying models to reflect the contributions of unresolved, fine-scale dynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
我们提出了一种用于预测动态系统中的数量的多尺度方法，该方法具有明确的结构，可以在从精细到粗略和从粗略到精细的方向上提取信息。与具有类似网络架构的非多尺度方法相比，我们的方法以最小程度增加的计算量提高了模型的准确性和稳定性。我们在理想化流体子网格参数化（称为闭包）任务上评估我们的方法，其中我们的多尺度网络纠正混沌基础模型以反映未解决的精细尺度动力学的贡献。
