# Biorthogonal scattering and generalized unitarity in non-Hermitian systems

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06349v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the two-port scattering process in non-Hermitian dimer models via quantum measurements using external leads. We focus on two exemplary dimer models that preserve parity-time symmetry via spatial gain-loss balance and exhibit non-reciprocity due to directional hopping. The scattering matrix is constructed using the biorthogonality of the left and right scattering states of the Hamiltonian, allowing us to calculate the reflection and transmission probabilities. Our analysis compares the reflection and transmission coefficients derived from the left, right, and combined scattering states, revealing that, unlike in Hermitian systems, the non-Hermitian scattering process does not adhere to unitarity when considering only the right scattering states. Furthermore, non-Hermitian scattering can enhance the reflection and transmission probabilities, with distinct physical contributions arising independently from complex eigenvalues and the non-orthogonality of eigenstates. Our results clarify how biorthogonality restores generalized unitarity and identify distinct physical origins of enhanced transport in PT-symmetric and non-reciprocal dimers, providing new insights into quantum transport in non-Hermitian systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06349v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们通过使用外部引线的量子测量研究非厄米二聚体模型中的双端口散射过程。我们重点关注两个示例性二聚体模型，它们通过空间增益损失平衡保持奇偶时间对称性，并由于定向跳跃而表现出非互易性。散射矩阵是利用哈密顿量的左右散射状态的双正交性构建的，使我们能够计算反射和透射概率。我们的分析比较了从左、右和组合散射状态导出的反射和透射系数，表明与厄米系统不同，非厄米散射过程在仅考虑右散射状态时不遵循幺正性。此外，非厄米散射可以增强反射和透射概率，其独特的物理贡献独立于复特征值和本征态的非正交性。我们的结果阐明了双正交性如何恢复广义幺正性，并确定了 PT 对称和非互易二聚体中增强输运的不同物理起源，为非厄米系统中的量子输运提供了新的见解。
