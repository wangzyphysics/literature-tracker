## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09086v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Solid state materials can display varieties of atomic structural orders ranging from crystalline to amorphous, underlying their properties and diverse functionalities. Procrystal has emerged as a new category of solids, featuring a long-range ordered lattice framework tiled with disordered atomic or molecular structures on the lattice sites, arousing great interest due to its novel structural and physical properties. However, the electronic analogue of a procrystal, dubbed as an electronic procrystalline (EPC) state, has never been experimentally observed. Here, we report the observation of an EPC state in a moire superstructure formed between a monolayer metallic NiTe2 and a superconductor NbSe2 with incommensurate lattice wavevectors. The observed EPC state exhibits a long-range periodic charge modulation at the moire scale inlaid with short-range irregular orders within each moire cell. Strikingly, the short-range charge orders inside the moire unit cells have proximately root3*root3 quasi-period, which is absent in pristine NiTe2. Intriguingly, the EPC order is also observed in the superconducting state of the moire superstructure. Furthermore, the emergent EPC state and short-range charge order, coexisting with the proximity induced superconductivity, can be precisely modulated with the thickness of NiTe2. Our findings uncover the potential of moire platform for understanding and tuning novel correlated quantum phases with this exotic procrystalline order.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09086v1 公告类型：新 摘要：固态材料可以显示从晶体到非晶态的各种原子结构顺序，这是其特性和多样化功能的基础。原晶体作为一类新型固体出现，具有长程有序的晶格骨架，晶格位点上铺有无序的原子或分子结构，因其新颖的结构和物理性质引起了人们的极大兴趣。然而，被称为电子原晶（EPC）态的原晶体的电子类似物从未在实验中被观察到。在这里，我们报告了在具有不相称晶格波矢的单层金属 NiTe2 和超导体 NbSe2 之间形成的莫尔超结构中的 EPC 态的观察。观察到的 EPC 状态表现出莫尔尺度的长程周期性电荷调制，每个莫尔单元内镶嵌有短程不规则有序。引人注目的是，莫尔晶胞内的短程电荷顺序具有近似 root3*root3 准周期，这在原始 NiTe2 中是不存在的。有趣的是，在莫尔超结构的超导状态下也观察到了 EPC 级。此外，与邻近感应超导共存的新兴EPC态和短程电荷序可以通过NiTe2的厚度精确调节。我们的研究结果揭示了莫尔平台在理解和调整具有这种奇异的前晶序的新型相关量子相方面的潜力。
