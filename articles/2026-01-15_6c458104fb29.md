## Abstract
Choline-peptides ([Ch][Pep]) have recently been presented as a means to advance biocompatible ionic liquids (ILs). Although these ILs found their applications in blends with water, the resulting systems are yet unexplored at the molecular level, a knowledge that is key to understanding and rationalizing novel applications. Thus, building on prior molecular dynamics insights for neat [Ch][Pep], we examined 5 {[Ch][Pep],H 2 O} binaries with a focus on their nano-structuration. Upon addition, water disperses unevenly within the polar IL network, preferentially near anionic charge centers. With increasing dilution, the ion pairs become progressively isolated and their interactions weakened, yielding five structural regimes with two key transitions: ∼70% n/n (ionic polar network disruption) and ∼95% n/n (ion pair dissociation). For the series of phenylalaninate and di-phenylalaninate-based ILs ([Ch][P] and [Ch][PP]), the apolar domains conformed by phenyl rings (Phe r ) remain continuous at low dilutions but present different disruption regimes: While the ∼95% n/n dilution of [Ch][PP] is still dominated by a unique aggregate, that of [Ch][P] presents a broken apolar network. In fact, both the uneven breakage of Phe r –Phe r and Phe r –[Ch] + complexes in [Ch][PP] and a greater and more stretchable polar network lead to an apolar aggregate more regularly distributed at the 96% n/n blend. Greater dilutions do prompt the fracture of the [Ch][PP] Phe r -based domain into the small clusters still observable in 99.6% n/n . Overall, this work describes how water reorganizes both polar and apolar motifs in {[Ch][Pep],H 2 O} and delineates different composition-dependent regimes, insights that can be, for instance, used to sketch more suitable hydrotrope solvents for pharmaceutical formulation.

## 摘要（中文）
胆碱肽（[Ch][Pep]）最近被提出作为推进生物相容性离子液体（IL）的一种手段。尽管这些离子液体在与水的混合物中得到应用，但所产生的系统尚未在分子水平上得到探索，而这一知识对于理解和合理化新应用至关重要。因此，基于先前对纯 [Ch][Pep] 的分子动力学见解，我们检查了 5 个 {[Ch][Pep],H 2 O} 二元化合物，重点关注其纳米结构。添加后，水在极性离子液体网络内分散不均匀，优先在阴离子电荷中心附近分散。随着稀释度的增加，离子对逐渐孤立，它们的相互作用减弱，产生具有两个关键转变的五种结构方案：∼70% n/n（离子极性网络破坏）和∼95% n/n（离子对解离）。对于基于苯丙氨酸和二苯丙氨酸的离子液体（[Ch][P] 和 [Ch][PP]）系列，苯环（Pher ）一致的非极性结构域在低稀释度下保持连续，但呈现出不同的破坏机制：虽然 [Ch][PP] 的 ~95% n/n 稀释仍然由独特的聚集体主导，但 [Ch][P] 呈现出破碎的非极性网络。事实上，[Ch][PP] 中 Pher –Pher 和 Pher –[Ch] + 复合物的不均匀断裂以及更大且更具拉伸性的极性网络导致非极性聚集体在 96% n/n 共混物中分布更加规则。更大的稀释度确实会促使基于 [Ch][PP] Pher 的结构域断裂成小簇，在 99.6% n/n 中仍可观察到。总体而言，这项工作描述了水如何重组 {[Ch][Pep],H 2 O} 中的极性和非极性基序，并描绘了不同的依赖于组成的机制，这些见解可用于绘制更适合药物制剂的水溶助长剂溶剂。
