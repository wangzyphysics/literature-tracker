# Nodal-line-enhanced quantum geometric effects: anomalous and nonlinear Hall effects in the parity-mixed antiferromagnet NbMnP

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08317v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The anomalous Hall effect has been understood in terms of the geometric nature of Bloch bands and impurity scattering, and has been observed in a wide variety of magnetic materials such as ferromagnets and antiferromagnets. Recently, a large anomalous Hall effect was reported in the noncollinear antiferromagnetic metal NbMnP whose magnetic order is a mixture of the even-parity and the odd-parity magnetic components. Such a magnetic structure is expected to exhibit the anomalous Hall effect and the nonlinear Hall effect from the symmetry breaking of the antiferromagnet ordering. Here, we theoretically investigate the intrinsic anomalous and nonlinear Hall effect of NbMnP induced by the quantum geometry of Bloch band using the first-principles calculation and the Wannier interpolation method. We found that the intrinsic Hall response of NbMnP is predominantly governed by the strongly enhanced Berry curvature and Berry-connection-polarization dipole on a specific mirror plane. These enhanced geometric quantities originate from the spin-orbit-coupling-induced gap openings along the nodal lines. Our results indicate that NbMnP serves as a model system for investigating transport phenomena originating from nodal-lines in parity-mixed antiferromagnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08317v1 公告类型：新 摘要：人们已经根据布洛赫能带和杂质散射的几何性质来理解反常霍尔效应，并且在铁磁体和反铁磁体等各种磁性材料中观察到了反常霍尔效应。最近，在非共线反铁磁金属NbMnP中报道了大的反常霍尔效应，其磁序是偶宇称和奇宇称磁性成分的混合。这种磁结构预计会因反铁磁体有序性的对称性破缺而表现出反常霍尔效应和非线性霍尔效应。在这里，我们利用第一性原理计算和 Wannier 插值方法从理论上研究了 Bloch 带的量子几何结构引起的 NbMnP 固有的反常和非线性霍尔效应。我们发现 NbMnP 的本征霍尔响应主要受特定镜面上强烈增强的贝里曲率和贝里连接偏振偶极子的控制。这些增强的几何量源自自旋轨道耦合引起的沿节点线的间隙开口。我们的结果表明，NbMnP 可作为研究源于宇称混合反铁磁体中的节点线的输运现象的模型系统。
