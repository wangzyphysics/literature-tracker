## Abstract
arXiv:2506.19039v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Geological data show that, early in its history, the Earth had a large-scale magnetic field with an amplitude comparable to the one of the present geomagnetic field. However, its origin remains enigmatic and various mechanisms have been proposed to explain the Earth's field over geological time scales. Here, we critically evaluate whether tidal forcing could explain the ancient geodynamo, by combining constraints from geophysical models of the Earth-Moon system and predictions from turbulence studies. Our analysis shows that lunar tidal forcing could have been sufficiently strong before $-3.25$ Gy to trigger turbulence within the Earth's core, and potentially to sustain dynamo action during that interval. Then, we propose new scaling laws for the magnetic field amplitude $B$. We expect the latter to scale as $B \propto \beta^{4/3}$, where $\beta$ is the equatorial ellipticity of the liquid core, if the turbulence involves weak interactions of three-dimensional inertial waves. Alternatively, in the regime of strong tidal forcing, the expected scaling becomes $B \propto \beta$. When extrapolated to the Earth's core, it suggests that tidal forcing alone was too weak to possibly explain the ancient geomagnetic field. Therefore, our study indirectly favours another origin for the early Earth's dynamo on long time scales (e.g. exsolution of light elements atop the core, or thermal convection due to secular cooling).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.19039v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：地质资料显示，地球在其历史早期就曾存在过大范围的磁场，其幅度与现在的地磁场相当。然而，它的起源仍然是个谜，人们提出了各种机制来解释地质时间尺度上的地球场。在这里，我们通过结合地月系统地球物理模型的约束和湍流研究的预测，批判性地评估潮汐强迫是否可以解释古代地球发电机。我们的分析表明，在 $-3.25$ Gy 之前，月球潮汐强迫可能足够强，足以引发地核内的湍流，并有可能在该时间段内维持发电机作用。然后，我们提出了磁场幅度 $B$ 的新标度定律。如果湍流涉及三维惯性波的弱相互作用，我们预计后者的尺度为 $B \propto \beta^{4/3}$，其中 $\beta$ 是液体核心的赤道椭圆率。或者，在强潮汐力的情况下，预期缩放变为 $B \propto \beta$。当推断到地核时，它表明潮汐力本身太弱，无法解释古代地磁场。因此，我们的研究间接支持早期地球发电机在长时间尺度上的另一个起源（例如，地核顶部轻元素的溶出，或由于长期冷却而产生的热对流）。
