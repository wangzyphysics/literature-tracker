## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09970v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetoelectric multiferroics, materials with intrinsically coupled electric polarization and magnetic order, promise ultralow-power switching, nonvolatile memory, and energy-efficient signal transduction. Yet practical deployment demands ultrathin films down to the atomic limit, where both orders typically degrade. Maintaining both order parameters at the thinnest scales in complex oxides remains a tremendous challenge, as uncompensated bound charge drives nanoscale depolarization in most ferroelectrics, while off-stoichiometry, reduced anisotropy, and charge transfer can produce magnetic dead layers in ultrathin oxides at substrate interfaces. Here, we realize a multiferroic phase of BiFeO3 that not only sustains both order parameters at room temperature with no dead layer but also exhibits signatures of emergent altermagnetism in the four-unit-cell, ultrathin limit. First-principles calculations, spin symmetry analysis, atomic-resolution imaging, and angle-resolved magnetic imaging reveal that short-circuit electrostatic boundary conditions, together with epitaxial strain, drive a continuous second-order, thickness-driven phase transition that enables the formation of multiferroic topological textures. Moreover, the imposed boundary conditions stabilize a d-wave altermagnetic time-reversal symmetry breaking, with corresponding signatures observed in magnetic circular dichroism. Collectively, these results establish a pathway to stabilize unconventional multiferroicity at device-relevant thicknesses, reframing scaling limits for oxide electronics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09970v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁电多铁性材料是具有本质耦合电极化和磁序的材料，有望实现超低功耗开关、非易失性存储器和节能信号传输。然而，实际部署需要超薄膜达到原子极限，在这种情况下，两个订单通常都会退化。在复杂氧化物中保持最薄尺度的两个有序参数仍然是一个巨大的挑战，因为未补偿的束缚电荷会驱动大多数铁电体中的纳米级去极化，而非化学计量、降低的各向异性和电荷转移会在衬底界面处的超薄氧化物中产生磁性死层。在这里，我们实现了 BiFeO3 的多铁性相，它不仅在室温下维持两个有序参数且没有死层，而且还在四晶胞、超薄极限中表现出紧急交变磁性的特征。第一原理计算、自旋对称分析、原子分辨率成像和角度分辨磁成像表明，短路静电边界条件与外延应变一起驱动连续的二阶、厚度驱动的相变，从而能够形成多铁性拓扑纹理。此外，施加的边界条件稳定了 d 波交磁时间反转对称性破缺，并在磁圆二色性中观察到相应的特征。总的来说，这些结果建立了一条在器件相关厚度下稳定非常规多铁性的途径，重新制定了氧化物电子器件的缩放限制。
