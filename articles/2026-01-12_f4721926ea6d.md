## Abstract
We report the controlled synthesis of fully programmable macroscopic chirality in hierarchical inorganic assemblies through the introduction of directional spiral airflow during evaporation-induced self-assembly of sub-1 nm gadolinium oxyhydroxide (GdOOH) nanowires. Extending previous observation of spontaneous centimeter-scale macroscopic helical assemblies (MHAs) without chiral dopants and with random handedness, we demonstrated 100% deterministic control over helicity, with left- or right-handed structures formation depending on the direction of airflow. This effect is independent of gas composition, confirming a purely mechanical shear-driven mechanism. Moreover, the resulting MHAs can be readily dissolved to regenerate the homogenous nanowire dispersion, allowing repeated cycles of assembly with inverted handedness and creating true dynamic programmability in a dopant-free inorganic system. This simple yet strong approach overcomes the random nature of macroscopic inorganic chirality, revealing supramolecular-like reversibility. These findings open new pathways for engineerable chiroptical materials with potential applications in photonics, catalysis, and beyond.

## 摘要（中文）
我们报告了通过在亚 1 nm 羟基氧化钆 (GdOOH) 纳米线的蒸发诱导自组装过程中引入定向螺旋气流，在分层无机组件中实现了完全可编程宏观手性的受控合成。扩展了先前对无手性掺杂剂和随机旋性的自发厘米级宏观螺旋组装体（MHA）的观察，我们证明了对螺旋性的 100% 确定性控制，左旋或右旋结构的形成取决于气流方向。这种效应与气体成分无关，证实了纯机械剪切驱动机制。此外，所得的 MHA 可以很容易地溶解，以再生均匀的纳米线分散体，从而允许以反旋手性进行重复的组装循环，并在无掺杂剂的无机系统中创建真正的动态可编程性。这种简单而强大的方法克服了宏观无机手性的随机性，揭示了类超分子的可逆性。这些发现为可工程手性光学材料开辟了新的途径，在光子学、催化等领域具有潜在的应用。
