## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00236v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The potential application of non-thermal plasma in treating textile industrial wastewater motivates researchers to develop innovative techniques at a laboratory scale to achieve the goal of wastewater mineralization. In line with this objective, a dielectric barrier post-discharge plasma reactor with a liquid electrode has been built for the study of synthetic dye degradation. The plasma reactor was optimized by altering various operating conditions to achieve a higher degradation efficiency at given discharge conditions. The reaction kinetics of crystal violet degradation were studied, and the same plasma reactor was tested for other synthetic dyes (wastewater model samples). The results suggest that the proposed dielectric barrier post-discharge plasma reactor may offer a promising solution for treating dye effluents from the textile industry.

arXiv:2601.00236v1 公告类型：新 摘要：非热等离子体在处理纺织工业废水中的潜在应用促使研究人员在实验室规模上开发创新技术，以实现废水矿化的目标。为了实现这一目标，建立了带有液体电极的介质阻挡后放电等离子体反应器，用于研究合成染料的降解。通过改变各种操作条件来优化等离子体反应器，以在给定的放电条件下实现更高的降解效率。研究了结晶紫降解的反应动力学，并测试了同一等离子体反应器用于其他合成染料（废水模型样品）。结果表明，所提出的介质阻挡后放电等离子体反应器可以为处理纺织工业的染料废水提供有前途的解决方案。
