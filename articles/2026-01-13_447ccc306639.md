## Abstract
Bio-oil derived from lignocellulosic biomass represents a sustainable alternative to fossil fuels as an energy source and chemical feedstock. Fast pyrolysis of lignocellulosic biomass yields a rich mixture of oxygenated organic species with low energy density and low stability, which can be electrochemically upgraded to increase energy density and stability and produce value-added chemicals. Here, we focus on electrocatalytic hydrotreatment of furfural (FF) and 4-propylphenol (4-PP), two constituents of raw bio-oil. In a binary mixture, the presence of FF has been shown to inhibit the reduction of 4-PP. The underlying mechanisms of these competing reactions are probed using density functional theory (DFT) and experimental electrochemical deposition techniques. The binding energies of FF and 4-PP, as well as their respective reduction products and associated reaction thermodynamics, are examined comparatively on multiple facets of single-crystal platinum and ruthenium catalysts. FF is shown to bind competitively compared to 4-PP, particularly on Pt(111) and Ru(0001), and hydrogenation products such as tetrahydrofurfuryl alcohol (THFA) and 4-propylcyclohexanol (4-PCOL) were found to bind less strongly to the surfaces than the reactants. FF reduction side products, including dimers furoin (FUI) and hydrofuroin (HF), were also found to bind competitively with 4-PP. No thermodynamic barriers were observed for FF hydrogenation to THFA, whereas 4-PCOL formation is favored on Pt facets, and propylcyclohexane (PC) formation is favored on Ru. The presence of barriers to 4-PP hydrogenation indicates that FF hydrogenation is thermodynamically favored on Pt and Ru. Further, limiting potential calculations suggests that the FF reduction pathways are generally favorable around 0 V vs. RHE. In contrast, the 4-PP reduction pathways require a more cathodic bias, achieved at potentials less than -0.1 V vs. RHE. Adsorption behavior of FF-derived reduction products on polycrystalline Pt is experimentally validated using a Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM). Such experiments yield estimates of aqueous-phase free energies of adsorption, which are correlated with the gas phase for comparison with computational results. Overall, the DFT model results suggest that the observed inhibition of 4-PP by FF is due to competitive adsorption and favorable FF reaction thermodynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
源自木质纤维素生物质的生物油代表了化石燃料作为能源和化学原料的可持续替代品。木质纤维素生物质的快速热解产生丰富的含氧有机物质混合物，具有低能量密度和低稳定性，可以通过电化学升级以提高能量密度和稳定性并生产增值化学品。在这里，我们重点关注粗生物油的两种成分糠醛 (FF) 和 4-丙基苯酚 (4-PP) 的电催化加氢处理。在二元混合物中，FF 的存在已被证明可以抑制 4-PP 的还原。使用密度泛函理论（DFT）和实验电化学沉积技术探讨了这些竞争反应的基本机制。在单晶铂和钌催化剂的多个方面对 FF 和 4-PP 的结合能以及它们各自的还原产物和相关反应热力学进行了比较研究。与 4-PP 相比，FF 具有竞争性结合，特别是在 Pt(111) 和 Ru(0001) 上，并且发现四氢糠醇 (THFA) 和 4-丙基环己醇 (4-PCOL) 等氢化产物与表面的结合不如反应物牢固。 FF 还原副产物，包括二聚体弗洛因 (FUI) 和氢弗洛因 (HF)，也被发现与 4-PP 竞争性结合。 FF 氢化成 THFA 时没有观察到热力学障碍，而 Pt 晶面上有利于形成 4-PCOL，Ru 上有利于形成丙基环己烷 (PC)。 4-PP 氢化势垒的存在表明 FF 氢化在热力学上有利于 Pt 和 Ru。此外，极限电势计算表明 FF 还原路径通常在 0 V 左右相对于 RHE 有利。相反，4-PP 还原途径需要更多的阴极偏压，与 RHE 相比，在低于 -0.1 V 的电位下实现。使用石英晶体微天平 (QCM) 对 FF 衍生还原产物在多晶 Pt 上的吸附行为进行了实验验证。这些实验得出了水相吸附自由能的估计值，该自由能与气相相关，以便与计算结果进行比较。总体而言，DFT 模型结果表明观察到的 FF 对 4-PP 的抑制是由于竞争吸附和有利的 FF 反应热力学。
