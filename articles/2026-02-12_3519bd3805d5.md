## Abstract
Hydrogen-bond (H-bond) networks dictate the self-assembly, dynamics, and macroscopic behavior of hydroxyl-containing liquids, yet their molecular determinants remain poorly defined. Here, we identify branched O 2 H 1 centers (hydroxyl sites accepting two and donating one H-bonds) as key structural motifs that define H-bond network diversity in alcohols. Near-infrared spectroscopy, supported by theoretical analysis, quantitatively distinguishes OH species in three hexanol isomers, revealing characteristic O 2 H 1 absorptions near 6250 cm −1 . Molecular dynamics simulations link the O 2 H 1 content to network topology and reorientation dynamics, where linear 1-hexanol forms disordered, flexible networks rich in O 2 H 1 centers (12%), whereas 3-hexanol, with fewer O 2 H 1 sites (4%), exhibits ordered, chain-like networks with longer H-bond lifetimes. The O 2 H 1 fraction also modulates CuCl 2 solubility and ion transport, establishing a direct molecular-level connection between H-bond motifs and the macroscopic functionality of alcohol-included liquids.

## 摘要（中文）
氢键（H-键）网络决定了含羟基液体的自组装、动力学和宏观行为，但其分子决定因素仍不清楚。在这里，我们将支链 O 2 H 1 中心（接受两个氢键并提供一个氢键的羟基位点）确定为定义醇中氢键网络多样性的关键结构基序。在理论分析的支持下，近红外光谱定量区分了三种己醇异构体中的 OH 物质，揭示了 6250 cm -1 附近的特征 O 2 H 1 吸收。分子动力学模拟将 O 2 H 1 含量与网络拓扑和重新取向动力学联系起来，其中线性 1-己醇形成富含 O 2 H 1 中心 (12%) 的无序、灵活的网络，而 3-己醇具有较少的 O 2 H 1 位点 (4%)，表现出有序的链状网络，具有更长的氢键寿命。 O 2 H 1 组分还调节 CuCl 2 溶解度和离子传输，在氢键基序和含醇液体的宏观功能之间建立直接的分子水平连接。
