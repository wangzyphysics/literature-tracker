## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00133v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Foundation models (FMs) have recently shown remarkable in-context learning (ICL) capabilities across diverse scientific domains. In this work, we introduce a unified in-context learning foundation model (ICL-FM) framework for materials property prediction that integrates both composition-based and structure-aware representations. The proposed approach couples the pretrained TabPFN transformer with graph neural network (GNN)-derived embeddings and our novel MagpieEX descriptors. MagpieEX augments traditional features with cation-anion interaction data to explicitly measure bond ionicity and charge-transfer asymmetry, capturing interatomic bonding characteristics that influence vibrational and thermal transport properties. Comprehensive experiments on the MatBench benchmark suite and a standalone lattice thermal conductivity (LTC) dataset demonstrate that ICL-FM achieves competitive or superior performance to state-of-the-art (SOTA) models with significantly reduced training costs. Remarkably, the training-free ICL-FM outperformed sophisticated SOTA GNN models in five out of six representative composition-based tasks, including a significant 9.93\% improvement in phonon frequency prediction. On the LTC dataset, the FM effectively models complex phenomena such as phonon-phonon scattering and atomic mass contrast. t-SNE analysis reveals that the FM acts as a physics-aware feature refiner, transforming raw, disjoint feature clusters into continuous manifolds with gradual property transitions. This restructured latent space enhances interpolative prediction accuracy while aligning learned representations with underlying physical laws. This study establishes ICL-FM as a generalizable, data-efficient paradigm for materials informatics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00133v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基础模型 (FM) 最近在不同科学领域表现出了卓越的情境学习 (ICL) 能力。在这项工作中，我们引入了一个用于材料属性预测的统一上下文学习基础模型（ICL-FM）框架，该框架集成了基于成分和结构感知的表示。所提出的方法将预训练的 TabPFN 变压器与图神经网络 (GNN) 衍生的嵌入和我们新颖的 MagpieEX 描述符结合起来。 MagpieEX 通过阳离子-阴离子相互作用数据增强了传统功能，可明确测量键离子性和电荷转移不对称性，捕获影响振动和热传输特性的原子间键合特征。 MatBench 基准套件和独立晶格热导率 (LTC) 数据集的综合实验表明，ICL-FM 实现了与最先进 (SOTA) 模型相比具有竞争力或优越的性能，同时显着降低了训练成本。值得注意的是，免训练的 ICL-FM 在六项基于合成的代表性任务中的五项中优于复杂的 SOTA GNN 模型，其中声子频率预测显着提高了 9.93%。在 LTC 数据集上，FM 有效地模拟了声子-声子散射和原子质量对比等复杂现象。 t-SNE 分析表明，FM 充当物理感知特征细化器，将原始、不相交的特征簇转换为具有逐渐属性转换的连续流形。这种重构的潜在空间提高了插值预测的准确性，同时将学习的表示与基本的物理定律保持一致。这项研究将 ICL-FM 确立为材料信息学的可推广、数据高效的范例。
