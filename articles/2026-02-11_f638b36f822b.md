## Abstract
arXiv:2506.19915v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The emergence of the arrow of time in quantum many-body systems stems from the inherent tendency of Hamiltonian evolution to scramble quantum information and increase entanglement. While, in principle, one might counteract this temporal directionality by engineering a perfectly inverted Hamiltonian to reverse entanglement growth, such a scenario is fundamentally unstable because even minor imperfections in the backward evolution can be exponentially amplified, a hallmark of quantum many-body chaos. Therefore, successfully reversing quantum many-body dynamics demands a deep understanding of the underlying structure of quantum information scrambling and chaotic dynamics. In this letter, by using solid-state nuclear magnetic resonance on a macroscopic ensemble of randomly interacting spins, we measure the out-of-time-ordered correlator (OTOC) and validate key predictions of scramblon theory, a universal theoretical framework for information scrambling. Crucially, this theory enables us to isolate and mitigate errors in the OTOC caused by imperfections in the backward evolution. As a result, this protocol uncovers the anticipated exponential behavior of quantum many-body chaos and extracts the quantum Lyapunov exponent in a many-body experimental system for the first time. Our results push the fundamental limits of dynamical reversibility of complex quantum systems, with implications for quantum simulation and metrology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.19915v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：量子多体系统中时间箭头的出现源于哈密顿演化扰乱量子信息、增加纠缠的内在倾向。虽然原则上，人们可以通过设计一个完美反转的哈密顿量来逆转纠缠增长来抵消这种时间方向性，但这种情况从根本上来说是不稳定的，因为即使向后演化中的微小缺陷也可能呈指数级放大，这是量子多体混沌的标志。因此，成功逆转量子多体动力学需要深入理解量子信息置乱和混沌动力学的底层结构。在这封信中，通过对随机相互作用的自旋宏观系综使用固态核磁共振，我们测量了乱序相关器（OTOC）并验证了扰乱理论（信息扰乱的通用理论框架）的关键预测。至关重要的是，该理论使我们能够隔离和减轻 OTOC 中由向后进化的缺陷引起的错误。因此，该协议揭示了量子多体混沌的预期指数行为，并首次在多体实验系统中提取了量子李亚普诺夫指数。我们的结果突破了复杂量子系统动态可逆性的基本极限，对量子模拟和计量学具有重要意义。
