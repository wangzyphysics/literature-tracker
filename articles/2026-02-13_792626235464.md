## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11695v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Quantum coherence offers potential for energy conversion technologies. It influences light absorption and emission, affecting energy conversion limits and efficiency. As a result, quantum coherence is being harnessed to boost performance in quantum heat engines, photocells, and photosynthetic-inspired platforms. Of particular interest in this context is the generation of Fano coherences, i.e., the formation of quantum coherences due to the interaction with the continuum of modes characterizing an incoherent process. We aim to formalize mathematically the possibility of achieving steady-state Fano coherence in a V-type three-level quantum system using polarized incoherent radiation, without requiring the energy difference between the excited levels to tend to zero. We perform this analysis by deriving the Bloch-Redfield equation from first-principles by quantizing the incoherent radiation. The resulting reduced dynamics of the system are analysed, so as to determine the lifetime of Fano coherence and identify the conditions under which it becomes stationary. We characterise distinct dynamical regimes, ranging from weak to strong pumping, in which steady-state Fano coherence emerges, and we quantitatively determine its magnitude. For each regime, we analyse the generation of Fano coherence as a function of both the intensity of the incoherent pumping and the energy splitting between the excited levels. We also assess how obtaining Fano coherence is modified by symmetric or asymmetric decay rates. These findings indicate that a three-level quantum system driven by polarized incoherent light can act as a robust resource for coherence-assisted energy conversion and storage. Finally, we discuss the experimental challenges associated with the implementation of the proposed model using an ensemble of Rubidium atoms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11695v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：量子相干性为能量转换技术提供了潜力。它影响光的吸收和发射，影响能量转换极限和效率。因此，量子相干性被用来提高量子热机、光电池和光合作用平台的性能。在这种情况下特别令人感兴趣的是法诺相干性的产生，即由于与表征非相干过程的模式连续统的相互作用而形成量子相干性。我们的目标是在数学上形式化使用偏振非相干辐射在 V 型三能级量子系统中实现稳态法诺相干性的可能性，而不需要激发能级之间的能量差趋于零。我们通过量化非相干辐射，从第一原理推导出布洛赫-雷德菲尔德方程来进行此分析。分析由此产生的系统动力学降低，以确定法诺相干性的寿命并确定其变得静止的条件。我们描述了不同的动态机制，从弱泵浦到强泵浦，其中出现稳态法诺相干性，并定量确定其大小。对于每种状态，我们分析法诺相干性的生成作为非相干泵浦强度和激发能级之间能量分裂的函数。我们还评估了对称或不对称衰减率如何改变法诺相干性的获得。这些发现表明，由偏振非相干光驱动的三能级量子系统可以作为相干辅助能量转换和存储的强大资源。最后，我们讨论了与使用铷原子集合实现所提出的模型相关的实验挑战。
