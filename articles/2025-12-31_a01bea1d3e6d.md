## Abstract
Accurate prediction of drug metabolites is crucial for pharmaceutical research, yet traditional methods struggle with generalizability and complex chemical structures. Recent deep learning models, particularly LAGOM, have significantly improved metabolite prediction by framing it as a direct SMILES-to-SMILES translation, setting a new benchmark for this challenging one-to-many problem. Building on this, this study introduces MetaSense, an enhanced Transformer model designed for more precise and diverse metabolite generation. MetaSense integrates a novel Structure-Aware Attention (SAA) mechanism within its encoder to identify potential metabolic sites by modulating attention weights with local structural features. Concurrently, an Adaptive Diversity Beam Search (ADBS) strategy is employed in the decoder to optimize the generation of multiple, diverse, and chemically plausible metabolites through dynamic diversity penalties and online chemical validity filtering. Evaluated on the independent GLORYx external test set, MetaSense consistently outperforms LAGOM, achieving a higher F-one score, Recall, and Precision, with a comparable total number of predictions. These results validate the effectiveness of SAA and ADBS, demonstrating MetaSense's enhanced capability to provide more accurate, diverse, and chemically valid metabolite predictions, thereby offering a more practical and reliable tool for drug discovery.

## 摘要（中文）
药物代谢物的准确预测对于药物研究至关重要，但传统方法在普遍性和复杂的化学结构方面存在困难。最近的深度学习模型，特别是 LAGOM，通过将代谢物预测构建为直接 SMILES 到 SMILES 的转换，显着改进了代谢物预测，为这一具有挑战性的一对多问题设定了新的基准。在此基础上，本研究引入了 MetaSense，这是一种增强的 Transformer 模型，旨在实现更精确和多样化的代谢物生成。 MetaSense 在其编码器中集成了一种新颖的结构感知注意力（SAA）机制，通过利用局部结构特征调节注意力权重来识别潜在的代谢位点。同时，解码器中采用自适应多样性波束搜索 (ADBS) 策略，通过动态多样性惩罚和在线化学有效性过滤来优化多种、多样化且化学上合理的代谢物的生成。在独立的 GLORYx 外部测试集上进行评估，MetaSense 始终优于 LAGOM，实现了更高的 F-one 分数、召回率和精度，并且预测总数相当。这些结果验证了 SAA 和 ADBS 的有效性，证明 MetaSense 提供更准确、多样化和化学上有效的代谢物预测的增强能力，从而为药物发现提供更实用和可靠的工具。
