# Light-induced Magnetization by Quantum Geometry

## 中文标题
量子几何的光诱导磁化

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09637v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a mechanism for the inverse Faraday and the inverse Cotton--Mouton effects arising from quantum geometry, characterized by the quantum metric quadrupole and the weighted quantum metric. Within a semiclassical framework based on the Boltzmann transport theory, we establish a general formalism describing light-induced magnetization in electronic systems as a second-order response to the electric field of light. Using continuum and tight-binding models, we discuss the symmetry constraints on these effects and estimate the magnitudes of the resulting magnetizations. Our results highlight a direct manifestation of quantum-geometric quantities in nonlinear magneto-optical responses and suggest a viable pathway for experimental detection.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09637v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种由量子几何产生的逆法拉第和逆科顿-穆顿效应的机制，其特征是量子度量四极和加权量子度量。在基于玻尔兹曼输运理论的半经典框架内，我们建立了一种通用形式，将电子系统中的光感磁化描述为对光电场的二阶响应。使用连续介质和紧束缚模型，我们讨论了这些效应的对称约束并估计了由此产生的磁化强度。我们的结果强调了量子几何量在非线性磁光响应中的直接表现，并提出了一条可行的实验检测途径。
