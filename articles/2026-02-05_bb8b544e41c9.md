## Abstract
arXiv:2504.10532v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Classical Electrodynamics in ponderable media remains defined by a century-long debate over force and energy localization. While the prevailing view treats competing formulations (Minkowski, Abraham, etc.) as equivalent conventions, this monograph argues that global conservation is insufficient for physical validity. A formulation must be mechanically coherent: power transfer must strictly equal the work rate of the force acting on the mass target. We formalize this requirement as the Force--Energy Consistency Criterion (FECC) -- a ``Kinematic Lock'' ($ P = \mathbf{f} \cdot \mathbf{v} $) -- and use it to audit standard macroscopic tensors. The analysis demonstrates that only the Macroscopic Vacuum (Lorentz) Formulation offers a mechanically consistent description of total energy-momentum transfer. The internal distribution of this energy is shown to be macroscopically indeterminate. By reinterpreting spatial averaging as spectral filtering, we reconstruct the theory from the microscopic baseline. This perspective identifies a universal host interface that routes electromagnetic energy into mechanical work, heat, and reversible storage, revealing a structural isomorphism where thermodynamics, mechanics, and electrodynamics emerge as coupled spectral projections.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.10532v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：有重量介质中的经典电动力学仍然由长达一个世纪的关于力和能量局部化的争论所定义。虽然主流观点将相互竞争的表述（闵可夫斯基、亚伯拉罕等）视为等效约定，但本专着认为全球守恒不足以保证物理有效性。公式必须具有机械连贯性：功率传输必须严格等于作用在质量目标上的力的工作速率。我们将这一要求形式化为力-能量一致性准则（FECC）——“运动锁”（$ P = \mathbf{f} \cdot \mathbf{v} $）——并用它来审核标准宏观张量。分析表明，只有宏观真空（洛伦兹）公式才能对总能量动量传递提供机械上一致的描述。该能量的内部分布在宏观上是不确定的。通过将空间平均重新解释为光谱过滤，我们从微观基线重建了理论。这种观点确定了一个通用主机接口，它将电磁能转化为机械功、热量和可逆存储，揭示了热力学、力学和电动力学作为耦合光谱投影出现的结构同构。
