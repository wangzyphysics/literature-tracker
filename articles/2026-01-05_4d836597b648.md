## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00018v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: This paper introduces an advanced Computational Analytical Micromechanics (CAM) framework for linear thermoelastic composites (CMs) with periodic microstructures. The approach is based on an exact new Additive General Integral Equation (AGIE), formulated for compactly supported loading conditions, such as body forces and localized thermal effects (for example laser heating). In addition, new general integral equations (GIEs) are established for arbitrary mechanical and thermal loading. A unified iterative scheme is developed for solving the static AGIEs, where the compact support of loading serves as a new fundamental training parameter. At the core of the methodology lies a generalized Representative Volume Element (RVE) concept that extends Hill classical definition of the RVE. Unlike conventional RVEs, this generalized RVE is not fixed geometrically but emerges naturally from the characteristic scale of localized loading, thereby reducing the analysis of an infinite periodic medium to a finite, data-driven domain. This formulation automatically filters out nonrepresentative subsets of effective parameters while eliminating boundary effects, edge artifacts, and finite-size sample dependencies. Furthermore, the AGIE-based CAM framework integrates seamlessly with machine learning (ML) and neural network (NN) architectures, supporting the development of accurate, physics-informed surrogate nonlocal operators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00018v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：本文介绍了一种用于具有周期性微结构的线性热弹性复合材料 (CM) 的先进计算分析微力学 (CAM) 框架。该方法基于全新的加性一般积分方程 (AGIE)，专为紧凑支撑的负载条件而制定，例如体积力和局部热效应（例如激光加热）。此外，还为任意机械和热载荷建立了新的通用积分方程（GIE）。开发了一种统一的迭代方案来解决静态 AGIE，其中负载的紧凑支持作为新的基本训练参数。该方法的核心在于广义的代表性体积元素 (RVE) 概念，它扩展了 Hill 经典的 RVE 定义。与传统的 RVE 不同，这种广义 RVE 不是几何固定的，而是从局部载荷的特征尺度中自然出现，从而将无限周期介质的分析减少到有限的数据驱动域。该公式自动过滤掉有效参数的非代表性子集，同时消除边界效应、边缘伪影和有限大小的样本依赖性。此外，基于 AGIE 的 CAM 框架与机器学习 (ML) 和神经网络 (NN) 架构无缝集成，支持开发准确的、基于物理的代理非局部算子。
