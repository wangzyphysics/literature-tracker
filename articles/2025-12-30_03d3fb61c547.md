## Abstract
arXiv:2512.05973v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In order to find reliable and efficient numerical approximation schemes, we suggest to identify the Functional Renormalization Group flow equations of one-particle irreducible two-point functions as Hamilton-Jacobi(-Bellman)-type partial differential equations. Based on this reformulation and reinterpretation we adopt a numerical scheme for the solution of field-dependent flow equations as nonlinear partial differential equations. We demonstrate this novel approach by first applying it to a simple fermion-boson system in zero spacetime dimensions - which itself presents as an interesting playground for method development. Afterwards, we show, how the gained insights can be transferred to more interesting problems: One is the bosonic $\mathbb{Z}_2$-symmetric model in three Euclidean dimensions within a truncation that involves the field-dependent effective potential and field-dependent wave-function renormalization. The other example is the $(1 + 1)$-dimensional Gross-Neveu model within a truncation that involves a field-dependent potential and a field-dependent fermion mass/Yukawa coupling at nonzero temperature, chemical potential, and finite fermion number.

arXiv:2512.05973v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：为了找到可靠、高效的数值逼近方案，我们建议将单粒子不可约两点函数的泛函重整化群流方程识别为 Hamilton-Jacobi(-Bellman) 型偏微分方程。基于这种重新表述和重新解释，我们采用数值格式将场相关流动方程求解为非线性偏微分方程。我们首先将其应用于零时空维度的简单费米子-玻色子系统来展示这种新颖的方法，该系统本身就是方法开发的一个有趣的游乐场。之后，我们展示了如何将所获得的见解转移到更有趣的问题：一个是截断内三个欧几里德维度的玻色 $\mathbb{Z}_2$ 对称模型，涉及场相关的有效势和场相关的波函数重整化。另一个例子是截断内的 $(1 + 1)$ 维 Gross-Neveu 模型，涉及非零温度、化学势和有限费米子数下的场相关势和场相关费米子质量/汤川耦合。
