## Abstract
Author(s): Jose Reina-Gálvez, Hoang-Anh Le, Hong Thi Bui, Soo-hyon Phark, Nicolás Lorente, and Christoph Wolf Atomic dimers are the smallest building blocks for spin-based qubits. In this work, a quantum magnet under an ac voltage enables electric-field-driven control of a nearby spin-1/2 qubit through modulation of the exchange coupling. [Phys. Rev. Research 7, L042055] Published Mon Dec 08, 2025
