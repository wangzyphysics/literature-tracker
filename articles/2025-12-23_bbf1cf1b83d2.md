## Abstract
Electronic resonances are metastable states with finite lifetimes, encountered in processes such as photodetachment, electron transmission spectroscopy, and Auger decay. Resonances appear in Hermitian quantum mechanics as increased density of states in the continuum rather than as discrete energy levels. To describe resonances accurately, including their coupling to the continuum, methods based on non-Hermitian quantum mechanics can be used, which yield complex energies. In this study, we combine the complex absorbing potential and complex basis functions techniques with the RI-CC2 method. The second-order coupled cluster method (CC2) offers a good balance between accuracy and computational cost by approximating equation-of-motion coupled-cluster singles and doubles (EOM-CCSD) theory, making it suitable for studying electronic resonances in larger molecules. The resolution-of-the-identity (RI) approximation further reduces computational demands without significant loss in accuracy. We investigate the numerical performance of the new complex-energy RI-CC2 methods, focusing on temporary anions. Negative electron affinities and decay widths can be computed using the electron-attachment (EA) variant of RI-CC2. For N 2 , C 2 H 4 , CH 2 O, and HCOOH, EA-CC2 yields electron affinities about 0.1–0.2 eV smaller than EOM-EA-CCSD, while deviations reach 0.5 eV for larger anions such as uracil, naphthalene, cyanonaphthalene, and pyrene. As a result of these trends, EA-CC2 is in better agreement with experiment for the negative electron affinities than EOM-EA-CCSD for all studied anions. The corresponding resonance widths from EA-CC2 calculations are about 0.05–0.25 eV smaller compared to EOM-EA-CCSD. Semi-empirical spin-scaling increases electron affinities by 0.3–0.5 eV and broadens resonance widths, improving the agreement with EOM-EA-CCSD but worsening the agreement with experiment.

## 摘要（中文）
电子共振是具有有限寿命的亚稳态，在光脱离、电子透射光谱和俄歇衰变等过程中会遇到。在厄米量子力学中，共振表现为连续体中状态密度的增加，而不是离散的能级。为了准确地描述共振，包括它们与连续体的耦合，可以使用基于非厄米量子力学的方法，该方法产生复杂的能量。在本研究中，我们将复吸收势和复基函数技术与 RI-CC2 方法相结合。二阶耦合团簇方法 (CC2) 通过近似运动方程耦合团簇单数和双数 (EOM-CCSD) 理论，在精度和计算成本之间提供了良好的平衡，使其适合研究较大分子中的电子共振。身份分辨率 (RI) 近似进一步降低了计算需求，而不会显着降低准确性。我们研究了新的复能 RI-CC2 方法的数值性能，重点关注临时阴离子。负电子亲和力和衰变宽度可以使用 RI-CC2 的电子附着 (EA) 变体来计算。对于 N 2 、C 2 H 4 、CH 2 O 和 HCOOH，EA-CC2 产生的电子亲和势比 EOM-EA-CCSD 小约 0.1-0.2 eV，而对于较大的阴离子（例如尿嘧啶、萘、氰萘和芘），偏差达到 0.5 eV。由于这些趋势，对于所有研究的阴离子，EA-CC2 与负电子亲和势的实验结果比 EOM-EA-CCSD 更好。与 EOM-EA-CCSD 相比，EA-CC2 计算得出的相应共振宽度大约小 0.05–0.25 eV。半经验自旋缩放将电子亲和势增加了 0.3-0.5 eV，并拓宽了共振宽度，改善了与 EOM-EA-CCSD 的一致性，但恶化了与实验的一致性。
