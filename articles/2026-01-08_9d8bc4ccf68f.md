## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03688v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Dielectric cylindrical waveguides are widely used for confining and guiding of electromagnetic waves in relatively wide range of frequencies. They have found numerous technological and scientific applications in telecommunications, medicine, material science, photonics and quantum optics. The electromagnetic field Green function is the central object in investigations of different types of radiation processes in those structures. In this paper, we review and further develop the recurrence procedure for evaluating the electromagnetic field Green function in a medium made of any number of homogeneous cylindrical layers. The general results are specified for a cylindrical waveguide immersed in a homogeneous medium. Expressions are provided for all the components of the Green tensor in both regions inside and outside the cylinder. As an application of the results for the Green function, we consider the radiation of a charged particle rotating around a dielectric cylinder. The intensities for all types of radiation processes are discussed. They include the synchrotron-Cherenkov radiation at large distances from the cylinder and the radiation on guided and surface polaritonic modes confined inside or near the surface of the cylinder. The paper provides explicit formulas for the electromagnetic fields and the spectral-angular densities of those radiations. It also includes a numerical and comparative analysis.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03688v1 公告类型：新 摘要：介质圆柱形波导广泛用于在相对较宽的频率范围内限制和引导电磁波。他们在电信、医学、材料科学、光子学和量子光学领域发现了许多技术和科学应用。电磁场格林函数是研究这些结构中不同类型辐射过程的中心对象。在本文中，我们回顾并进一步开发了用于评估由任意数量的均匀圆柱形层组成的介质中的电磁场格林函数的递归程序。一般结果是针对浸入均匀介质中的圆柱形波导指定的。为圆柱体内部和外部区域中格林张量的所有分量提供了表达式。作为格林函数结果的应用，我们考虑围绕介电圆柱体旋转的带电粒子的辐射。讨论了所有类型的辐射过程的强度。它们包括距圆柱体较远距离处的同步加速器切伦科夫辐射以及限制在圆柱体内部或表面附近的引导和表面极化模式的辐射。该论文提供了电磁场和这些辐射的光谱角密度的明确公式。它还包括数值分析和比较分析。
