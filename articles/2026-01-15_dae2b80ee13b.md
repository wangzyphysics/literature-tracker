## Abstract
arXiv:2405.04729v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Many factors in perovskite X-ray detectors, such as crystal lattice and carrier dynamics, determine the final device performance (e.g., sensitivity and detection limit). However, the relationship between these factors remains unknown due to the complexity of the material. In this study, we employ machine learning to reveal the relationship between 15 intrinsic properties of halide perovskite materials and their device performance. We construct a database of X-ray detectors for the training of machine learning. The results show that the band gap is mainly influenced by the atomic number of the B-site metal, and the lattice length parameter b has the greatest impact on the carrier mobility-lifetime product ({\mu}{\tau}). An X-ray detector (m-F-PEA)2PbI4 were generated in the experiment and it further verified the accuracy of our ML models. We suggest further study on random forest regression for X-ray detector applications.

arXiv:2405.04729v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：钙钛矿 X 射线探测器中的许多因素，例如晶格和载流子动力学，决定了最终的器件性能（例如灵敏度和检测限）。然而，由于材料的复杂性，这些因素之间的关系仍然未知。在这项研究中，我们利用机器学习来揭示卤化物钙钛矿材料的 15 种固有特性与其器件性能之间的关系。我们构建了一个用于机器学习训练的 X 射线探测器数据库。结果表明，带隙主要受B位金属原子序数的影响，而晶格长度参数b对载流子迁移率-寿命乘积({\mu}{\tau})影响最大。实验中生成了 X 射线探测器 (m-F-PEA)2PbI4，进一步验证了我们的 ML 模型的准确性。我们建议进一步研究 X 射线探测器应用的随机森林回归。
