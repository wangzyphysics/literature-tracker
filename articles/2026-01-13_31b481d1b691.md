## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07777v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Fully connected Blume-Emery-Griffiths neural networks performing pattern recognition and associative memory have been heuristically studied in the past (mainly via the replica trick and under the replica symmetric assumption) as generalization of the standard Hopfield reference. In these notes, at first, by relying upon Guerra interpolation, we re-obtain the existing picture rigorously. Next we show that, due to dilution in the patterns, these networks are able to switch from serial recall (where one pattern is retrieved per time) to parallel recall (where several patterns are retrieved at once) and the larger the dilution, the stronger this emerging multi-tasking capability. In particular, we inspect the regimes of mild dilution (where solely a low storage of pattern can be enabled) and extreme dilution (where a medium storage of patterns can be sustained) separately as they give rise to different outcomes: the former displays hierarchical recall (distributing the amplitudes of the retrieved signals with different amplitudes), the latter -instead- performs a equal-strength recall (where a O(1) fraction of all the patterns is simultaneously retrieved with the same amplitude per pattern). Finally, in order to implement graded responses in the neurons, variations on theme obtained by enlarging the possible values of neural activity these neurons may sustain are also discussed generalizing the Ghatak-Sherrington model for inverse freezing in Hebbian terms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07777v1 公告类型：新 摘要：执行模式识别和联想记忆的完全连接的 Blume-Emery-Griffiths 神经网络过去已被启发式研究（主要通过复制技巧并在复制对称假设下）作为标准 Hopfield 参考的推广。在这些笔记中，我们首先依靠Guerra插值，严格地重新获得现有的图像。接下来我们表明，由于模式的稀释，这些网络能够从串行召回（每次检索一个模式）切换到并行召回（一次检索多个模式），并且稀释越大，这种新兴的多任务处理能力就越强。特别是，我们分别检查轻度稀释（仅可启用模式的低存储）和极端稀释（可维持模式的中等存储）的机制，因为它们会产生不同的结果：前者显示分层召回（以不同幅度分布检索到的信号的幅度），后者执行等强度召回（其中以每个模式相同的幅度同时检索所有模式的 O(1) 部分）。最后，为了在神经元中实现分级响应，还讨论了通过扩大这些神经元可能维持的神经活动的可能值而获得的主题变化，以赫布术语概括逆冻结的 Ghatak-Sherrington 模型。
