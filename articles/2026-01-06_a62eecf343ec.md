## Abstract
arXiv:2507.16053v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Fluctuations and structure across a wide range of spatial and temporal scales are frequently studied in the solar wind. The properties of the low-frequency fluctuations are of relevance to turbulent energy injection into the plasma and the transport of high-energy cosmic rays. Correlation analysis of decade-long intervals of interplanetary data permits study of fluctuations at time scales much longer than suitably defined correlation times, and therefore at frequencies well below those associated with the Kolmogorov inertial range of {\it in situ} turbulence. At the frequencies of interest, we study the familiar occurrence of the $1/f$ spectral signature. We also study point spectral features due to solar rotation and their relation with the $1/f$ signal. We report novel properties at timescales ranging from minutes up to years, using data selected by wind speed, phase of solar cycle, and cartesian components of the magnetic field. A surprising finding is that the power in solar rotation harmonics is consistent with an extension of the $1/f$ spectrum, down to frequencies as low as around $\unit[5 \times 10^{-7}]{Hz}$. The presence of a broadband $1/f$ spectrum across different wind types supports the interpretation that $1/f$ signals may be related to or even originate from the solar dynamo.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.16053v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：人们经常研究太阳风在广泛的时空尺度上的波动和结构。低频波动的特性与注入等离子体的湍流能量和高能宇宙射线的传输有关。对行星际数据长达十年的时间间隔的相关分析允许研究比适当定义的相关时间长得多的时间尺度上的波动，因此频率远低于与原位湍流的柯尔莫哥洛夫惯性范围相关的频率。在感兴趣的频率处，我们研究了常见的 $1/f$ 频谱特征。我们还研究了太阳自转引起的点光谱特征及其与 $1/f$ 信号的关系。我们使用根据风速、太阳周期相位和磁场的笛卡尔分量选择的数据，在从几分钟到几年的时间尺度上报告新奇的特性。一个令人惊讶的发现是，太阳旋转谐波的功率与 $1/f$ 频谱的扩展一致，低至 $\unit[5 \times 10^{-7}]{Hz}$ 左右的频率。不同风型中宽带 $1/f$ 频谱的存在支持了 $1/f$ 信号可能与太阳能发电机相关甚至源自太阳能发电机的解释。
