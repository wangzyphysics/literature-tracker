## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21725v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Multi-terminal topological devices are a new generation of electronic devices with quantized properties robust against imperfections. In magnetic topological insulators, dissipationless edge states give functional devices in zero magnetic field, of interest for quantum metrology (resistance standard) or topological electronics (Chern networks). Here we show that a new generation of simple quantum circuits (disk, ring) with non-Hermitian topology, based on the interconnection of 1D Chern states in the quantum anomalous Hall regime, can have a much stronger quantization of their invariant than that of the Chern invariant itself, when measured in a non-metrology grade setup - that is, in industry-relevant conditions. Remarkably, the chirality-related topological skin effect is realized without the need of a magnetic field or an electrical gate, with a record degree of localization for a quantum Hall device. This new type of topological quantum devices based on magnets, with an exponential response that can be switched at small magnetic fields (about 200mT), can operate at liquid-Helium temperature with a good quantization and have some potential as cryogenic sensors for applications in high-precision impedance or magnetic field measurements.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21725v1 发布类型：新 摘要：多端拓扑器件是新一代电子器件，具有抗缺陷鲁棒性量化特性。在磁性拓扑绝缘体中，无耗散边缘态赋予零磁场下的功能器件，这对量子计量学（电阻标准）或拓扑电子学（陈省身网络）很有意义。在这里，我们展示了具有非厄米拓扑的新一代简单量子电路（圆盘、环），基于量子反常霍尔体系中一维陈氏态的互连，当在非计量级设置（即在工业相关条件下）测量时，其不变量的量子化比陈不变量本身的量子化强得多。值得注意的是，无需磁场或电门即可实现与手性相关的拓扑集肤效应，并且具有量子霍尔器件创纪录的局域化程度。这种基于磁体的新型拓扑量子器件具有指数响应，可以在小磁场（约200mT）下切换，可以在液氦温度下工作并具有良好的量子化，并且具有作为低温传感器应用于高精度阻抗或磁场测量的潜力。
