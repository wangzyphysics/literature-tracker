## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09193v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Curved electromagnetic beams at terahertz (THz) frequencies have recently emerged as a powerful example of wavefront engineering, with applications in imaging and high-capacity wireless communications. Unlike canonical self-accelerating solutions such as Airy beams, general curved-beam propagation enables arbitrary, application-specific trajectories that are not constrained by analytic beam families. Here, we demonstrate a passive and reconfigurable approach for generating trajectory-engineered THz curved beams using inverse-designed bilayer diffractive optical elements (DOEs). Two phase-only diffractive layers are optimized using gradient-based inverse design to produce predetermined curved propagation paths. Reconfiguration is achieved by a 180{\deg} rotation of the second layer, which modifies the effective phase profile of the cascaded structure without altering the incident wave or individual layer designs. The proposed system can produce distinct curved trajectories with controlled transverse displacement and beam confinement, as confirmed by scalar diffraction simulations and experimental measurements. Overall, this work establishes inverse-designed cascaded DOEs as a compact and scalable platform for reconfigurable trajectory control of THz beams, providing a flexible alternative to analytic self-accelerating beams for radiative near-field THz communications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09193v1 公告类型：新 摘要：太赫兹 (THz) 频率的弯曲电磁束最近已成为波前工程的有力示例，在成像和大容量无线通信中得到应用。与艾里光束等标准自加速解决方案不同，一般弯曲光束传播可以实现任意的、特定于应用程序的轨迹，不受解析光束族的限制。在这里，我们展示了一种使用逆向设计的双层衍射光学元件（DOE）生成轨迹工程太赫兹弯曲光束的被动且可重构的方法。使用基于梯度的逆设计优化两个纯相位衍射层，以产生预定的弯曲传播路径。重新配置是通过第二层旋转 180{\deg} 来实现的，这会修改级联结构的有效相位分布，而不改变入射波或各个层的设计。正如标量衍射模拟和实验测量所证实的，所提出的系统可以产生具有受控横向位移和光束限制的独特弯曲轨迹。总体而言，这项工作将逆向设计的级联DOE建立为一个紧凑且可扩展的平台，用于太赫兹光束的可重构轨迹控制，为辐射近场太赫兹通信的分析自加速光束提供了灵活的替代方案。
