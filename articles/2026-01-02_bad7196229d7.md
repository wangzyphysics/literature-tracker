## Abstract
Author(s): Suman Jyoti De, T. Pereg-Barnea, and Kartiek Agarwal A new technique using diamond NV centers detects time-reversal symmetry breaking in 2D materials by comparing spin relaxation due to opposite circular magnetic polarizations, enabling nanoscale probes of Hall conductivity and other chiral quantum effects. [Phys. Rev. X 16, 011001] Published Fri Jan 02, 2026
