## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12069v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We theoretically investigate the collisions between ultracold polar molecules in the presence of two lasers ensuring a Raman resonant transition on individual molecules to suppress photon scattering, taking the example of bosonic $^{23}$Na$^{39}$K molecules. By varying laser detunings and intensities, we enable a repulsive long-range interaction potential between molecules. After solving a set of coupled Schr\"odinger equations with the Hamiltonian written in the basis of laser-dressed states of the molecule pair at infinite distance, we identify quasi-resonant conditions under which elastic collisions are favored over inelastic and reactive ones, by a factor of about 2, thus demonstrating a promising pathway for efficient two-photon optical shielding of ultracold molecular collisions. The results are analyzed in terms of scattering length of the colliding laser-dressed molecules, which exhibit prominent resonances assigned to the interaction of the entrance channel with other specific channels, consistent with the existence of a quasi-bound level of the long-range molecular pair induced by the lasers.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12069v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们从理论上研究了在两个激光器存在的情况下超冷极性分子之间的碰撞，确保单个分子上的拉曼共振跃迁以抑制光子散射，以玻色子 $^{23}$Na$^{39}$K 分子为例。通过改变激光失谐和强度，我们实现了分子之间的排斥性长程相互作用势。在求解了一组基于无限距离分子对激光修饰态基础上的哈密顿量的耦合薛定格方程后，我们确定了准共振条件，在该条件下，弹性碰撞比非弹性和反应性碰撞更有利，大约是非弹性和反应性碰撞的两倍，从而证明了超冷分子碰撞的有效双光子光学屏蔽的有前途的途径。结果根据碰撞激光修饰分子的散射长度进行了分析，其表现出显着的优势归因于入口通道与其他特定通道的相互作用的共振，与激光诱导的长程分子对的准结合水平的存在一致。
