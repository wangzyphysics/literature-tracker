## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09083v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study one-dimensional disordered systems with average non-invertible symmetries, where quenched disorder may locally break part of the symmetry while preserving it upon disorder averaging. A canonical example is the random transverse-field Ising model, which at criticality exhibits an average Kramers-Wannier duality. We consider the general setting in which the full symmetry is described by a $G$-graded fusion category $\mathcal{B}$, whose identity component $\mathcal{A}$ remains exact, while the components with nontrivial $G$-grading are realized either exactly or only on average. We develop a topological holographic framework that encodes the symmetry data of the 1D system in a 2D topological order $\mathcal{Z}[\mathcal{A}]$ (the Drinfeld center of $\mathcal{A}$), enriched by an exact or, respectively, average $G$ symmetry. Within this framework, we obtain a complete classification of anomalies and average symmetry-protected topological (SPT) phases: when the components with nontrivial $G$-grading are realized only on average, the symmetry is anomaly-free if and only if $\mathcal{Z}[\mathcal{A}]$ admits a magnetic Lagrangian algebra that is invariant under the permutation action of $G$ on anyons. When an anomaly is present, we show that the ground state of a single disorder realization is long-range entangled with probability one in the thermodynamic limit, and is expected to exhibit power-law Griffiths singularities in the low-energy spectrum. Finally, we present an explicit, exactly solvable lattice model based on a symmetry-enriched string-net construction. It yields trivial ground state ensemble in the anomaly-free case, and exhibits exotic low-energy behavior in the presence of an average anomaly.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09083v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究具有平均不可逆对称性的一维无序系统，其中猝灭无序可能会局部破坏部分对称性，同时在无序平均时保留对称性。一个典型的例子是随机横向场 Ising 模型，该模型在临界点表现出平均 Kramers-Wannier 对偶性。我们考虑一般设置，其中完全对称性由 $G$ 分级融合类别 $\mathcal{B}$ 描述，其恒等分量 $\mathcal{A}$ 保持精确，而具有非平凡 $G$ 分级的分量要么精确实现，要么仅平均实现。我们开发了一个拓扑全息框架，以二维拓扑顺序 $\mathcal{Z}[\mathcal{A}]$ （$\mathcal{A}$ 的 Drinfeld 中心）编码一维系统的对称数据，并分别通过精确或平均 $G$ 对称性进行丰富。在此框架内，我们获得了异常和平均对称保护拓扑（SPT）相的完整分类：当具有非平凡$G$分级的分量仅在平均上实现时，当且仅当$\mathcal{Z}[\mathcal{A}]$承认磁拉格朗日代数，该代数在$G$对任意子的置换作用下保持不变时，对称性是无异常的。当存在异常时，我们表明单一无序实现的基态与热力学极限中的概率一长程纠缠，并且预计在低能谱中表现出幂律格里菲斯奇点。最后，我们提出了一个基于对称性丰富的弦网结构的显式的、精确可解的晶格模型。它在无异常的情况下产生微不足道的基态系综，并在存在平均异常的情况下表现出奇异的低能量行为。
