## Abstract
arXiv:2505.24290v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: High-harmonic generation (HHG), the hallmark effect of attosecond science, is a nonperturbative nonlinear process leading to the emission of high-harmonic light from gases and solids. In gases, extreme driving laser pulse intensities can deplete the ground state, suppressing harmonic emission during the trailing edge of the pulse. Here, we report a similar effect, pronounced ultrafast carrier saturation dynamics and harmonic emission suppression during nonperturbative harmonic generation (NPHG) in a gapless Dirac semimetal -- highly oriented pyrolytic graphite (HOPG). Remarkably, HOPG supports NPHG at laser intensities as low as $\sim 10^{10}$ W cm$^{-2}$, facilitated by its vanishing bandgap. Ultrafast carrier saturation strongly modulates the interplay between interband and intraband currents, a key characteristic of NPHG in Dirac materials. Using two-color spectroscopy, we reveal the excitation dynamics of Dirac electron-hole pairs as it affects the emission of harmonics during the presence of the driving laser pulse. The excitation of out-of-equilibrium hot carriers and the concomitant saturation near the Dirac points leads to a marked suppression of interband harmonics and induces measurable temporal shifts. These observations are supported by simulations based on semiconductor Bloch equations. Our finding reveal that field-driven carrier saturation plays a critical role in gapless solid NPHG. We demonstrate the potential of NPHG and HHG as a sensitive, all-optical probe of ultrafast carrier dynamics, offering novel opportunities for ultrafast optoelectronics in Dirac materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.24290v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：高次谐波产生（HHG）是阿秒科学的标志效应，是一种非微扰非线性过程，导致气体和固体发射高次谐波光。在气体中，极端的驱动激光脉冲强度会耗尽基态，从而抑制脉冲后沿期间的谐波发射。在这里，我们报告了类似的效应，即在无间隙狄拉克半金属——高取向热解石墨（HOPG）中的非微扰谐波产生（NPHG）过程中显着的超快载流子饱和动力学和谐波发射抑制。值得注意的是，HOPG 在低至 $\sim 10^{10}$ W cm$^{-2}$ 的激光强度下支持 NPHG，这得益于其消失的带隙。超快载流子饱和强烈调制带间和带内电流之间的相互作用，这是狄拉克材料中 NPHG 的一个关键特性。使用双色光谱，我们揭示了狄拉克电子空穴对的激发动力学，因为它影响驱动激光脉冲存在期间谐波的发射。非平衡热载流子的激发以及狄拉克点附近的伴随饱和导致带间谐波的显着抑制并引起可测量的时间偏移。这些观察结果得到了基于半导体布洛赫方程的模拟的支持。我们的发现表明，场驱动载流子饱和在无间隙固体 NPHG 中起着至关重要的作用。我们展示了 NPHG 和 HHG 作为超快载流子动力学的灵敏全光学探针的潜力，为狄拉克材料中的超快光电子学提供了新的机会。
