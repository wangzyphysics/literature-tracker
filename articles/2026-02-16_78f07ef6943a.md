## Abstract
arXiv:2508.06027v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We theoretically investigate the mechanism of spin current generation induced by elastic waves in nonrelativistic magnets referred to as altermagnets. By analyzing an $f$-wave altermagnet formed by a three-sublattice noncollinear antiferromagnetic structure breaking the spatial inversion symmetry on a two-dimensional triangular lattice within the linear response theory, we show that the nonrelativistic antisymmetric spin-split band structure can give rise to spin current generation when either longitudinal or transverse elastic wave is applied. We find that the momentum dependence of the antisymmetric spin splitting leads to a characteristic direction-dependent spin current response. We also compare the present nonrelativistic magnetic-order-driven mechanism with the relativistic one in a nonmagnetic Rashba system. These findings highlight the potential of invesion-symmetry-breaking altermagnets as a spin current generator driven by elasticity without relying on the relativistic spin-orbit coupling.

arXiv:2508.06027v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们从理论上研究了非相对论性磁体（称为交替磁体）中弹性波引起的自旋电流产生的机制。通过在线性响应理论中分析由打破二维三角晶格空间反演对称性的三子晶格非共线反铁磁结构形成的$f$波交变磁体，我们发现，当施加纵向或横向弹性波时，非相对论反对称自旋分裂能带结构可以产生自旋电流。我们发现反对称自旋分裂的动量依赖性导致了特征方向依赖的自旋电流响应。我们还将当前的非相对论磁序驱动机制与非磁 Rashba 系统中的相对论机制进行了比较。这些发现凸显了反转对称性破缺交替磁体作为由弹性驱动的自旋电流发生器而不依赖相对论自旋轨道耦合的潜力。
