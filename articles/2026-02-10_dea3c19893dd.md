# A theoretical framework for random acceleration molecular dynamics simulations

## Abstract
The dissociation of a ligand bound to a receptor is a rare event occurring on a timescale that is far longer than can be afforded by standard simulation methodologies. A number of specialized approaches have been designed to overcome this challenge. One of these, Random Acceleration Molecular Dynamics (RAMD), is a biased simulation method that is particularly effective at accelerating the dissociation of ligands bound to protein receptors. However, no theoretical framework is currently available to extract unbiased estimates from the RAMD simulation results. In this study, we develop a simple theory for its interpretation based on the assumption that the RAMD simulations can be approximated by an overdamped Langevin dynamics with an additional force of fixed magnitude changing direction randomly at exponentially distributed times. We show that the dynamics of the model is consistent with a Smoluchowski equation with an effective temperature and diffusion coefficient that depend quadratically on the magnitude of the random force. These observations explain why the RAMD simulations yield accelerated dynamics. Importantly, we derive an analytical expression for the unbiased escape time of a particle over a free-energy barrier based on the modified Smoluchowski equation. The theoretical framework was used to analyze RAMD simulations monitoring the dissociation of the trypsin–benzamidine protein–ligand complex. The results indicate that the theoretical framework is sound and could potentially be used to unbias the results from RAMD simulations.

## 摘要（中文）
与受体结合的配体解离是一种罕见的事件，其发生的时间尺度远远长于标准模拟方法所能提供的时间尺度。人们设计了许多专门的方法来克服这一挑战。其中之一是随机加速分子动力学 (RAMD)，它是一种有偏差的模拟方法，在加速与蛋白质受体结合的配体解离方面特别有效。然而，目前没有理论框架可用于从 RAMD 模拟结果中提取无偏估计。在这项研究中，我们基于这样的假设开发了一个简单的理论来解释它：RAMD 模拟可以通过过阻尼朗之万动力学来近似，并带有固定大小的附加力，在指数分布的时间随机改变方向。我们证明模型的动力学与 Smoluchowski 方程一致，其有效温度和扩散系数与随机力的大小成二次关系。这些观察结果解释了为什么 RAMD 模拟会产生加速动力学。重要的是，我们基于修正的 Smoluchowski 方程推导了粒子在自由能势垒上的无偏逃逸时间的解析表达式。该理论框架用于分析监测胰蛋白酶-苯甲脒蛋白-配体复合物解离的 RAMD 模拟。结果表明理论框架是合理的，并且有可能用于消除 RAMD 模拟结果的偏差。
