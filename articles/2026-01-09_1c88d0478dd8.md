## Abstract
We present the next generation of AMP, a neural network potential (NNP) with anisotropic message passing designed to study large biomolecular systems at DFT accuracy in the condensed phase using a multiscale approach similar to quantum-mechanics/molecular-mechanics (QM/MM) with electrostatic embedding. We trained AMPv3 on our recently published biomolecular multiscale simulation (BMS25) dataset and demonstrated the model’s high eﬃciency, which enabled us to simulate proteins involving thousands of atoms at DFT accuracy in addition to explicit MM solvent for up to hundred nanoseconds, which presents a major leap for contemporary NNPs. We observe excellent scaling to large systems on a single GPU. AMPv3-BMS25 (or short AMP-BMS) shows promising performance on benchmarks and we demonstrate that the model can be used to accurately estimate experimental properties, including solvation free energies of small molecules and structural features of proteins. Finally, AMP-BMS/MM was employed to predict the free-energy profiles of reactions catalyzed by the enzymes chorismate mutase and fluoroacetate dehalogenase. In total, AMP-BMS/MM was used to simulate proteins in the condensed phase for a cumulative 23 µs simulation time or 48 billion integration steps. This work establishes AMP-BMS as a highly eﬃcient and accurate model for multiscale simulations of biomolecules.

## 摘要（中文）
我们提出了下一代 AMP，一种具有各向异性消息传递的神经网络势 (NNP)，旨在使用类似于具有静电嵌入的量子力学/分子力学 (QM/MM) 的多尺度方法，以凝聚相的 DFT 精度研究大型生物分子系统。我们在最近发布的生物分子多尺度模拟 (BMS25) 数据集上训练了 AMPv3，并证明了该模型的高效率，这使我们能够以 DFT 精度模拟涉及数千个原子的蛋白质以及高达数百纳秒的显式 MM 溶剂，这为当代 NNP 带来了重大飞跃。我们观察到在单个 GPU 上可以出色地扩展到大型系统。 AMPv3-BMS25（或简称 AMP-BMS）在基准测试中表现出良好的性能，我们证明该模型可用于准确估计实验特性，包括小分子的溶剂化自由能和蛋白质的结构特征。最后，采用 AMP-BMS/MM 来预测分支酸变位酶和氟乙酸脱卤酶催化反应的自由能曲线。总共，AMP-BMS/MM 用于模拟凝聚相中的蛋白质，累计模拟时间为 23 µs 或 480 亿个积分步骤。这项工作将 AMP-BMS 建立为生物分子多尺度模拟的高效且准确的模型。
