## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22802v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Transport of spin angular momentum over large distance has been a long sought-after goal in the field of spintronics. While the majority of the research effort has been devoted to the spin transport properties of magnetically ordered materials, spin transport in magnetically frustrated materials has received little attention. Here, we report an anomalous state in frustrated hyperkagome magnetic insulator Gd3Ga5O12, where spin angular momenta can be transported over a long distance of 480 {\mu}m, far exceeding the transport distance of any diffusive spin current in magnetically ordered materials, to the best of our knowledge. Monte Carlo simulations reveal significant spin fluctuations, spin-spin correlations and an absence of conventional magnons in such anomalous state; while the response of the anomalous state to perturbation is found to be akin to an overdamped forced oscillator. We find close relation of such state to the correlated ``director'' state in the material. Our result provides an effective electrical technique to characterize spin-spin correlations and frustrations; it also unveils the potential of frustrated magnets as powerful channel materials for spin transport.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22802v1 发布类型：新 摘要：自旋角动量的远距离传输一直是自旋电子学领域长期以来备受追捧的目标。虽然大部分研究工作都致力于磁有序材料的自旋输运特性，但磁阻材料中的自旋输运却很少受到关注。在这里，我们报告了受抑超卡戈麦磁绝缘体 Gd3Ga5O12 中的异常状态，其中自旋角动量可以传输 480 μm 的长距离，据我们所知，远远超过磁有序材料中任何扩散自旋电流的传输距离。蒙特卡罗模拟揭示了显着的自旋波动、自旋-自旋相关性以及在这种异常状态下不存在传统磁振子；同时发现异常状态对扰动的响应类似于过阻尼强迫振荡器。我们发现这种状态与材料中相关的“指挥”状态密切相关。我们的结果提供了一种有效的电学技术来表征自旋-自旋相关性和挫折；它还揭示了受阻磁体作为自旋传输的强大通道材料的潜力。
