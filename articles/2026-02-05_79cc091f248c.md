# Where Does Tracing of Cosmic Ray in Real Atmosphere Terminate?

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.03885v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In backtracing simulations, which are widely employed to determine cosmic-ray particle trajectories in the geomagnetic field, the atmosphere is typically approximated as an artificial sharp boundary at some low altitude where the traced trajectory terminates. In this paper, we extend beyond this simplified assumption and investigate two realistic physical processes that terminate cosmic-ray particle propagation in the atmosphere: Bethe-Bloch energy loss mechanisms and hard scattering interactions with atmospheric atoms using total cross sections based on the Glauber-Gribov formalism. The former mechanism dominates at low rigidities (for protons below $\sim0.57$~GV), while the latter becomes dominant at higher rigidities. Consequently, we introduce two dimensionless variables up to detailed numerical criteria: the relative rigidity shift due to Bethe-Bloch effects ($\Delta\mathfrak{R}/\mathfrak{R}$), and the expected number of hard scattering events ($\langle N\rangle$). Using the corrected US Standard Atmosphere 1976 model, we demonstrate that the altitude dependence can be factorized as approximately $\exp(-0.14h/\textrm{km})$. Additionally, the effect of the local curvature radius of the trajectory near perigee can be similarly factorized. Our calculations indicate that the simplified sharp-boundary altitude should be at least $50$ km with $\Delta\mathfrak{R}/\mathfrak{R}+\langle N\rangle\lesssim1$ for protons, increasing by more than $15$ km for heavy nuclei such as iron.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.03885v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在广泛用于确定地磁场中宇宙射线粒子轨迹的回溯模拟中，大气通常被近似为追踪轨迹终止处的某个低海拔处的人工尖锐边界。在本文中，我们超越了这个简化的假设，并研究了终止宇宙射线粒子在大气中传播的两个现实物理过程：Bethe-Bloch 能量损失机制以及使用基于 Glauber-Gribov 形式主义的总横截面与大气原子的硬散射相互作用。前一种机制在低刚性时占主导地位（对于低于 $\sim0.57$~GV 的质子），而后者在较高刚性时占主导地位。因此，我们引入了两个符合详细数值标准的无量纲变量：Bethe-Bloch 效应引起的相对刚度变化 ($\Delta\mathfrak{R}/\mathfrak{R}$) 和硬散射事件的预期数量 ($\langle N\rangle$)。使用修正后的 1976 年美国标准大气模型，我们证明高度依赖性可以分解为大约 $\exp(-0.14h/\textrm{km})$。此外，近地点附近轨迹的局部曲率半径的影响可以类似地分解。我们的计算表明，对于质子，简化的锐边界高度应至少为 $\Delta\mathfrak{R}/\mathfrak{R}+\langle N\rangle\lesssim1$ $50$ km，对于铁等重核，则增加超过 $15$ km。
