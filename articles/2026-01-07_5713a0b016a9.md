## Abstract
The stromal cell compartment plays a central part in the maintenance of tissue homeostasis by coordinating with the immune system throughout inception, amplification and resolution of inflammation1. Chronic inflammation can impede the phased regulation of tissue restitution, resulting in the scarring complication of fibrosis. In inflammatory bowel disease, stromal fibroblasts have been implicated in treatment-refractory disease and fibrosis2,3; however, their mechanisms of activation have remained undefined. Through integrative single-cell and spatial profiling of intestinal tissues from patients with inflammatory bowel disease, we uncovered a pathological cell nexus centred on inflammation-associated fibroblasts. These fibroblasts were induced by proinflammatory macrophages (FCN1+IL1B+) and, in turn, produced profibrotic cytokine IL-11. We investigated the inflammation-associated fibroblast activation program at a mechanistic level using genome-wide CRISPR knockout and activation screens and identified the transcription factor GLIS3 as a key regulator of a gene regulatory network governing expression of inflammatory and fibrotic genes. We further demonstrated that the magnitude of the GLIS3 gene expression program in intestinal biopsies could be used to stratify patients with ulcerative colitis by disease severity, and that fibroblast-specific deletion ofGlis3in mice alleviated pathological features of chronic colitis. Taken together, our findings identify a critical immune–stromal cell circuit that functions as a central node in the inflammation–fibrosis cycle.

## 摘要（中文）
基质细胞区室在炎症发生、放大和消退的整个过程中与免疫系统协调，在维持组织稳态中发挥着核心作用1。慢性炎症会阻碍组织恢复的阶段性调节，导致纤维化的疤痕并发症。在炎症性肠病中，基质成纤维细胞与难治性疾病和纤维化有关2,3；然而，它们的激活机制仍未明确。通过对炎症性肠病患者肠道组织进行综合单细胞和空间分析，我们发现了以炎症相关成纤维细胞为中心的病理细胞关系。这些成纤维细胞由促炎性巨噬细胞 (FCN1+IL1B+) 诱导，进而产生促纤维化细胞因子 IL-11。我们使用全基因组 CRISPR 敲除和激活筛选在机制水平上研究了与炎症相关的成纤维细胞激活程序，并确定转录因子 GLIS3 是控制炎症和纤维化基因表达的基因调控网络的关键调节因子。我们进一步证明，肠道活检中 GLIS3 基因表达程序的大小可用于根据疾病严重程度对溃疡性结肠炎患者进行分层，并且小鼠中成纤维细胞特异性删除 Glis3 可减轻慢性结肠炎的病理特征。综上所述，我们的研究结果确定了一个关键的免疫基质细胞回路，它作为炎症-纤维化循环的中心节点。
