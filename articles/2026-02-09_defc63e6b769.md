## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06684v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The beam-breakup (BBU) instability in plasma accelerators is seeded by a transverse offset between the driver and a trailing bunch. The BBU instability induces oscillations in the trailing bunch, which are detrimental to its beam quality. When the instability is large, assuming little mitigation from ion motion and energy spread, the beam suffers emittance growth, and charge can be kicked transversely out of the plasma channel. The detrimental effect on beam quality is substantially worse at high efficiencies, which places constraints on the achievable power efficiency in applications such as linear colliders, where maintaining the beam quality is required. In this paper, we present the first experimental signatures of the BBU instability in data taken in the E302 experiment at the FACET-II facility at SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory. We use a specific beam-optical setup and a novel method to probe for transverse instabilities on diagnostic screens downstream of a magnetic dipole spectrometer. We complement the analysis with full 3D particle-in-cell (PIC) simulations of the plasma interaction using similar driver and trailing bunch parameters on a simulated FACET-II spectrometer.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06684v1 公告类型：新 摘要：等离子体加速器中的束流破碎 (BBU) 不稳定性是由驱动器和尾随束之间的横向偏移造成的。 BBU 的不稳定性会引起尾随束的振荡，这对其光束质量不利。当不稳定性很大时，假设离子运动和能量扩散几乎没有减轻，束流会遭受发射度增长，并且电荷可能会被横向踢出等离子体通道。在高效率下，对光束质量的不利影响要严重得多，这限制了线性对撞机等需要保持光束质量的应用中可实现的功率效率。在本文中，我们在 SLAC 国家加速器实验室 FACET-II 设施的 E302 实验中获取的数据中展示了 BBU 不稳定性的第一个实验特征。我们使用特定的光束光学装置和新颖的方法来探测磁偶极子光谱仪下游诊断屏幕上的横向不稳定性。我们在模拟 FACET-II 光谱仪上使用类似的驱动器和尾随束参数，通过等离子体相互作用的完整 3D 粒子内细胞 (PIC) 模拟来补充分析。
