## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09178v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Stoichiometric materials of Eu(III) offer a promising platform for quantum memories attributable to their unique capability to display a distinctive, nondegenerate J = 0 transition, which enables precise mapping of optical quantum states into their hyperfine structure for reliable storage and retrieval on demand. However, placing Eu(III) into chiral polar structures, which are necessary for achieving narrow spectral linewidths and long optical lifetimes, is a daunting task. Here, we discover Eu2(SeO3)2(SO4)(H2O)2, a rare Eu(III) material that exhibits chiral polar symmetries encompassing both local and global structures. This unique structure is shaped by an appropriate combination of asymmetric ligands. The chirality fosters dipole-dipole interactions and J-mixing, as characterized by second-harmonic generation, photoluminescence, and magnetic susceptibility. The broken inversion symmetry is supported by the phase-matching behavior of second-harmonic generation. The J = 0 transition is observed at 578 nm with a narrow linewidth at 78 K and a microsecond-scale optical lifetime. The analysis of magnetic susceptibility data using Van Vleck theory results in an effective magnetic moment of 3.33 {\mu}B/Eu3+ and J-mixing. Heat capacity data reveal underlying phonon dynamics in the material. This study demonstrates a pathway toward realizing new stoichiometric Eu3+ compounds with potential for optically addressable quantum memory applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09178v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Eu(III) 的化学计量材料为量子存储器提供了一个有前景的平台，这归因于其独特的能力，能够显示独特的非简并 J = 0 跃迁，从而能够将光学量子态精确映射到其超精细结构中，以便根据需要进行可靠的存储和检索。然而，将Eu(III)置于手性极性结构中是实现窄光谱线宽和长光学寿命所必需的，是一项艰巨的任务。在这里，我们发现了Eu2(SeO3)2(SO4)(H2O)2，这是一种罕见的Eu(III)材料，它表现出包含局部和整体结构的手性极性对称性。这种独特的结构是由不对称配体的适当组合形成的。手性促进偶极-偶极相互作用和 J 混合，其特征在于二次谐波产生、光致发光和磁化率。二次谐波产生的相位匹配行为支持了破缺的反演对称性。在 578 nm 处观察到 J = 0 跃迁，在 78 K 处具有窄线宽和微秒级的光学寿命。使用 Van Vleck 理论对磁化率数据进行分析，得出有效磁矩为 3.33 {\mu}B/Eu3+ 和 J 混合。热容数据揭示了材料中潜在的声子动力学。这项研究展示了实现新化学计量 Eu3+ 化合物的途径，该化合物具有光学可寻址量子存储应用的潜力。
