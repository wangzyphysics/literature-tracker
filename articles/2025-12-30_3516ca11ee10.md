## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23290v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnetism simultaneously possesses nonrelativistic spin responses and zero net magnetization, thus combining advantages of ferromagnetism and antiferromagnetism. This superiority originates from its unique dual feature, i.e., opposite-magnetic sublattices in real space and alternating spin polarization in momentum space enforced by the same crystal symmetry. Therefore, the determination of an altermagnetic order and its unique spin response inherently necessitates atomic-scale spin-resolved measurements in real and momentum spaces, an experimental milestone yet to be achieved. Here, via utilizing the helical edge (hinge) modes of a higher order topological insulator as the spin sensor, we realize spin-resolved scanning tunneling microscopy which enables us to pin down the dual-space feature of a layered $d$-wave altermagnet, KV$_2$Se$_2$O. In real space, atomic-registered mapping demonstrates the checkerboard antiferromagnetic order together with density-wave lattice modulation, and in momentum space, spin-resolved spectroscopic imaging provides a direct visualization of d-wave spin splitting of the band structure. Critically, using this new topology-guaranteed spin filter we directly reveal the unidirectional, spin-polarized quasiparticle excitations originating from the crystal symmetry-paired X and Y valleys around opposite magnetic sublattices simultaneously --the unique spin response for $d$-wave altermagnetism. Our experiments establish a solid basis for the exploration and utilization of altermagnetism in layered materials and further facilitate access to atomic-scale spin sensing and manipulating of 2D quantum materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23290v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交变磁性同时具有非相对论性自旋响应和零净磁化强度，从而结合了铁磁性和反铁磁性的优点。这种优越性源于其独特的双重特征，即实空间中的相反磁性亚晶格和由相同晶体对称性强制的动量空间中的交替自旋极化。因此，确定交变磁序及其独特的自旋响应本质上需要在真实空间和动量空间中进行原子尺度的自旋分辨测量，这是一个尚未实现的实验里程碑。在这里，通过利用高阶拓扑绝缘体的螺旋边缘（铰链）模式作为自旋传感器，我们实现了自旋分辨扫描隧道显微镜，这使我们能够确定层状 $d$ 波交替磁体 KV$_2$Se$_2$O 的双空间特征。在真实空间中，原子配准映射展示了棋盘反铁磁序以及密度波晶格调制，而在动量空间中，自旋分辨光谱成像提供了能带结构的d波自旋分裂的直接可视化。至关重要的是，使用这种新的拓扑保证的自旋滤波器，我们直接揭示了源自相反磁性亚晶格周围的晶体对称配对X和Y谷同时产生的单向、自旋极化准粒子激发——$d$波交变磁性的独特自旋响应。我们的实验为层状材料中交变磁性的探索和利用奠定了坚实的基础，并进一步促进了原子尺度自旋传感和二维量子材料的操纵。
