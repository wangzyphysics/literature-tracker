## Abstract
Author(s): Zesheng Zhang, Xin-Zhi Li, and Wen-Yu He Orbital magnetization is established as the origin of the nonlinear Hall effect by deriving an explicit formula connecting the electric-field-induced orbital magnetization to the second-order nonlinear Hall conductivity. The applied electric field is shown to play dual roles in generating the nonlinear Hall effect by first generating a nonequilibrium orbital magnetization and then perturbing the associated edge states, and a unique nonlinear Hall effect is predicted in isotropic chiral metals driven by a noncollinear bichromatic electric field. [Phys. Rev. Research 7, L042064] Published Fri Dec 19, 2025

## 摘要（中文）
作者：张泽生、李新志、何文宇 通过推导将电场引起的轨道磁化强度与二阶非线性霍尔电导率联系起来的显式公式，将轨道磁化强度确定为非线性霍尔效应的起源。研究表明，所施加的电场在产生非线性霍尔效应方面发挥着双重作用，首先产生非平衡轨道磁化强度，然后扰动相关的边缘态，并且在非共线双色电场驱动的各向同性手性金属中预测了独特的非线性霍尔效应。 [物理。 Rev. Research 7, L042064] 2025 年 12 月 19 日星期五发布
