## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04362v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Symmetry constraints determine which physical responses are allowed in a given system. Magnetization induced by strain fields, such as in piezomagnetic and flexomagnetic effects, has typically been considered in materials that break time-reversal symmetry. Here, we propose that nonuniform strain can induce magnetization even in nonmagnetic metals and semiconductors that preserve time-reversal symmetry. This mechanism differs from the conventional flexomagnetic effect: the strain gradient acts as a driving force on the electrons, generating magnetization in a manner closely analogous to current-induced magnetization. Treating the strain field as an external field, we derive a general expression for the magnetization induced by a strain gradient and demonstrate that this response is symmetry-allowed even in time-reversal symmetric systems. We apply our formulation to nonmagnetic systems that lack spatial inversion symmetry while preserving time-reversal symmetry, using a decorated square lattice, monolayer MoS$_2$, and monolayer Janus MoSSe as representative examples. We find a finite magnetization response to strain gradients, which is consistent with symmetry arguments, supporting the validity of our theoretical framework. These results offer a pathway for controlling magnetization in nonmagnetic materials using strain fields.

arXiv:2602.04362v1 公告类型：新 摘要：对称约束决定给定系统中允许哪些物理响应。在打破时间反转对称性的材料中，通常考虑由应变场引起的磁化，例如压磁和柔磁效应。在这里，我们提出，即使在保持时间反转对称性的非磁性金属和半导体中，不均匀应变也可以引起磁化。这种机制与传统的柔磁效应不同：应变梯度充当电子的驱动力，以与电流感应磁化非常相似的方式产生磁化。将应变场视为外部场，我们推导了应变梯度引起的磁化强度的一般表达式，并证明即使在时间反转对称系统中，这种响应也是对称的。我们将我们的公式应用于缺乏空间反转对称性同时保留时间反转对称性的非磁性系统，使用装饰方形晶格、单层 MoS$_2$ 和单层 Janus MoSSe 作为代表性示例。我们发现了对应变梯度的有限磁化响应，这与对称性论证一致，支持了我们理论框架的有效性。这些结果提供了利用应变场控制非磁性材料磁化强度的途径。
