## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09563v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We extend a recently developed "tangent fermion" method to discretize the Hamiltonian of a helical Luttinger liquid on a one-dimensional lattice, including two-particle backscattering processes that may open a gap in the spectrum. The fermion-doubling obstruction of the sine dispersion is avoided by working with a tangent dispersion, preserving the time-reversal symmetry of the Hamiltonian. The numerical results from a tensor network calculation on a finite lattice confirm the expectation from infinite-system analytics, that a gapped phase with spontaneously broken time-reversal symmetry emerges when the Fermi level is tuned to the Dirac point and the Luttinger parameter crosses a critical value.

arXiv:2601.09563v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们扩展了最近开发的“切线费米子”方法，在一维晶格上离散化螺旋 Luttinger 液体的哈密顿量，包括可能在光谱中打开间隙的双粒子反向散射过程。通过使用正切色散，可以避免正弦色散的费米子倍增阻碍，从而保留哈密顿量的时间反转对称性。有限晶格上张量网络计算的数值结果证实了无限系统分析的预期，即当费米能级调整到狄拉克点并且鲁廷格参数跨越临界值时，会出现时间反转对称性自发破缺的带隙相。
