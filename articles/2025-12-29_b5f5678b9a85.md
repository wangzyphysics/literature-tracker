## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21807v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Compact, lightweight, and energy-efficient cold atom systems are crucial for advancing quantum technologies, yet their realization remains constrained by the bulky optical and magnetic components required in current atom trapping architectures. Here, we demonstrate a low-SWaP magneto-optical trap that seamlessly integrates planar optical and magnetic components into a unified platform. A monolithic dual-functional metasurface simultaneously polarized and shapes the cooling beam, replacing traditional lens-waveplate assemblies and converting a linearly polarized Gaussian beam into a circularly polarized flat-top beam. In parallel, a planar coil chip substitutes bulky anti-Helmholtz cols and generated the required quadrupole magnetic field with drastically reduced power consumption. Under D2 line cooling of 87Rb atoms, the fully planar system delivers nearly an order-of-magnitude improvement in trapping performance while operating at a fraction of the size, weight, and power of traditional systems. This compact, bulky-component-free approach offers a scalable, energy-efficient pathway toward chip-scale cold atom platforms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21807v1 公告类型：新 摘要：紧凑、轻量且节能的冷原子系统对于推进量子技术至关重要，但其实现仍然受到当前原子捕获架构所需的庞大光学和磁性组件的限制。在这里，我们演示了一种低 SWaP 磁光陷阱，它将平面光学和磁性组件无缝集成到统一平台中。单片双功能超表面同时对冷却光束进行偏振和整形，取代了传统的透镜波片组件，并将线偏振高斯光束转换为圆偏振平顶光束。同时，平面线圈芯片取代了笨重的反亥姆霍兹线圈，并产生了所需的四极磁场，同时大大降低了功耗。在 87Rb 原子的 D2 线冷却下，全平面系统的捕获性能几乎提高了一个数量级，同时其尺寸、重量和功率仅为传统系统的一小部分。这种紧凑、无笨重元件的方法为芯片级冷原子平台提供了一条可扩展、节能的途径。
