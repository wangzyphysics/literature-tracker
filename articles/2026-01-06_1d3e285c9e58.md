## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01357v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The rapid evolution of large language models (LLMs) is transforming artificial intelligence into autonomous research partners, yet a critical gap persists in complex scientific domains such as combustion modeling. Here, practical AI assistance requires the seamless integration of domain literature knowledge with robust execution capabilities for expertise-intensive tools such as computational fluid dynamics (CFD) codes. To bridge this gap, we introduce FlamePilot, an LLM agent designed to empower combustion modeling research through automated and self-corrective CFD workflows. FlamePilot differentiates itself through an architecture that leverages atomic tools to ensure the robust setup and execution of complex simulations in both OpenFOAM and extended frameworks such as DeepFlame. The system is also capable of learning from scientific articles, extracting key information to guide the simulation from initial setup to optimized results. Validation on a public benchmark shows FlamePilot achieved a perfect 1.0 executability score and a 0.438 success rate, surpassing the prior best reported agent scores of 0.625 and 0.250, respectively. Furthermore, a detailed case study on Moderate or Intense Low-oxygen Dilution (MILD) combustion simulation demonstrates its efficacy as a collaborative research copilot, where FlamePilot autonomously translated a research paper into a configured simulation, conducted the simulation, post-processed the results, proposed evidence-based refinements, and managed a multi-step parameter study to convergence under minimal human intervention. By adopting a transparent and interpretable paradigm, FlamePilot establishes a foundational framework for AI-empowered combustion modeling, fostering a collaborative partnership where the agent manages workflow orchestration, freeing the researcher for high-level analysis.

arXiv:2601.01357v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：大型语言模型 (LLM) 的快速发展正在将人工智能转变为自主研究伙伴，但在复杂的科学领域（例如燃烧建模）仍然存在关键差距。在这里，实用的人工智能辅助需要将领域文献知识与计算流体动力学 (CFD) 代码等专业知识密集型工具的强大执行能力无缝集成。为了弥补这一差距，我们引入了 FlamePilot，这是一种 LLM 代理，旨在通过自动化和自我校正的 CFD 工作流程来增强燃烧建模研究。 FlamePilot 的独特之处在于它的架构利用原子工具来确保 OpenFOAM 和 DeepFlame 等扩展框架中复杂模拟的稳健设置和执行。该系统还能够从科学文章中学习，提取关键信息来指导模拟从初始设置到优化结果。公共基准验证显示，FlamePilot 获得了完美的 1.0 可执行性分数和 0.438 的成功率，分别超过了之前报告的最佳代理分数 0.625 和 0.250。此外，关于中度或强烈低氧稀释 (MILD) 燃烧模拟的详细案例研究证明了其作为协作研究副驾驶的功效，其中 FlamePilot 自动将研究论文转化为配置的模拟，进行模拟，对结果进行后处理，提出基于证据的改进，并管理多步骤参数研究，以在最少的人为干预下收敛。通过采用透明且可解释的范例，FlamePilot 为人工智能驱动的燃烧建模建立了基础框架，促进了代理管理工作流程编排的协作伙伴关系，使研究人员能够进行高级分析。
