## Abstract
arXiv:2512.12338v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We analyze the equilibrium states of quantum lattice model with local multi-well potentials for Sn$_2$P$_2$S$_6$ ferroelectric crystals using the mean and Gaussian curvatures ($H$, $K$), curvedness ($C$) and shape index ($S$). From the energy gap, pressure and temperature variations of $H$, $K$, $C$ and $S$, we have reported the geometric construction of the free energy surfaces for the ferroelectric and paraelectric phases. Their behaviors are explicitly observed near the ferroelectric-paraelectric phase transitions. It is found that $H$, $C$ and $S$ display a cusp singularity at the criticality while $K$ converges to zero on both sides of the critical and tricritical points.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.12338v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们使用平均曲率和高斯曲率 ($H$, $K$)、弯曲度 ($C$) 和形状指数 ($S$) 分析 Sn$_2$P$_2$S$_6$ 铁电晶体的局域多阱势量子晶格模型的平衡态。根据 $H$、$K$、$C$ 和 $S$ 的能隙、压力和温度变化，我们报告了铁电相和顺电相自由能表面的几何结构。在铁电-顺电相变附近可以清楚地观察到它们的行为。发现$H$、$C$和$S$在临界点处呈现尖点奇点，而$K$在临界点和三临界点两侧均收敛于零。
