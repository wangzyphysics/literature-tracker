# Plasmon-Enhanced Graphene Terahertz Photo-thermoelectric Response

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22063v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Terahertz (THz) technology shows great potential in 6G communications and imaging, but faces challenges related to detector sensitivity, noise, and cryogenic operation. Here, we integrate interferometric enhancement of absorption (IEA) from a metal reflection layer with a graphene plasmon polariton atomic cavity (PPAC)-based photodetector. The hybrid configuration enhances the in-plane electric field and improves the plasmon-induced thermal gradient. Numerical simulations and photoresponse measurements were employed to systematically investigate the influence of a metal reflective layer on the photothermoelectric behavior of the device, which reveals the IEA design significantly boosts the THz absorption rate in graphene nanostructures and promotes asymmetry in the lateral diffusion of hot carriers. Compared with the bare device, the responsivity of the device is enhanced by approximately 30-folds, while maintaining a response time below 130 {\mu}s. We further demonstrate the potential of the device to distinguish concealed liquids, advancing high-responsivity, room-temperature, and compact terahertz imaging technology.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22063v1 公告类型：新 摘要：太赫兹 (THz) 技术在 6G 通信和成像方面显示出巨大潜力，但面临与探测器灵敏度、噪声和低温操作相关的挑战。在这里，我们将金属反射层的干涉增强吸收（IEA）与基于石墨烯等离子体激元原子腔（PPAC）的光电探测器集成。混合配置增强了面内电场并改善了等离激元引起的热梯度。采用数值模拟和光响应测量系统地研究了金属反射层对器件光热电行为的影响，结果表明IEA设计显着提高了石墨烯纳米结构的太赫兹吸收率，并促进了热载流子横向扩散的不对称性。与裸器件相比，该器件的响应度提高了约30倍，同时保持响应时间低于130μs。我们进一步证明了该设备区分隐藏液体的潜力，推进了高响应度、室温和紧凑型太赫兹成像技术。
