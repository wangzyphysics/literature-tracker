## Abstract
arXiv:2507.11268v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: The electron and muon magnetic moments have played, and continue to play, important roles in testing the fundamental mathematical description of physical reality called the Standard Model of particle physics (SM). The electron magnetic moment is the most precisely measured property of an elementary particle and the most precise SM prediction, setting up the most precise confrontation ever between experiment and theory. It enables the most precise test of quantum field theory, and of the fundamental CPT symmetry invariance of the SM with leptons. The stable electron is studied with quantum methods while the electron remains for months in its quantum ground and first excited states. The muon magnetic moment is one of the most precisely measured property of an unstable elementary particle. Although less precise measured than the electron, it provides greater sensitivity to physics beyond the Standard Model -- a powerful tool for testing the existence of new particles and forces. Because muons decay quickly, they must be studied as they orbit at nearly the speed of light in a large storage ring. The extremely high precision of the electron and muon magnetic moment measurements has driven major advances in theoretical physics, inspiring new techniques in quantum field theory, precision calculations, and lattice gauge theory. Only experimental limits currently exist on the size of the magnetic moments of the tau and neutrino leptons.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.11268v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：电子和μ子磁矩在测试物理现实的基本数学描述（称为粒子物理（SM）标准模型）中已经并将继续发挥重要作用。电子磁矩是基本粒子最精确测量的特性，也是最精确的 SM 预测，在实验和理论之间建立了有史以来最精确的对抗。它能够对量子场论以及带有轻子的 SM 的基本 CPT 对称不变性进行最精确的测试。使用量子方法研究稳定电子，同时电子在其量子基态和第一激发态中保持数月。 μ子磁矩是不稳定基本粒子最精确测量的特性之一。虽然测量精度不如电子，但它提供了超出标准模型的更高的物理灵敏度——这是测试新粒子和力是否存在的强大工具。由于 μ 子衰变速度很快，因此必须对它们进行研究，因为它们在一个大型存储环中以接近光速的速度运行。电子和μ子磁矩测量的极高精确度推动了理论物理学的重大进步，激发了量子场论、精密计算和晶格规范理论的新技术。目前，tau 轻子和中微子磁矩的大小仅存在实验限制。
