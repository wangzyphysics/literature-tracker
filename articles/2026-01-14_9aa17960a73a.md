## Abstract
arXiv:2509.00618v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: This study proposes a Duffing oscillator based measurement framework for detecting frequency components associated with postsynaptic potential related activity in EEG recordings. The Duffing oscillator is employed as a nonlinear measurement system whose high sensitivity to weak periodic inputs enables robust frequency estimation in the presence of strong noise. Because postsynaptic potentials are embedded in noisy EEG recordings, they are treated as weak signals. Based on the detected weak-signal frequency distributions across EEG channels, topographic maps of brain activity are constructed to visualize spatial variations in neural activation. For this purpose, EEG signals recorded from two musicians and two audience members during a music experiment were analysed. The frequency components of the weak signals were searched within the 4 37 Hz range across all EEG channels. Subsequently, topographic brain maps were generated according to the number of detected weak-signal components. The results indicate that Duffing oscillator based weak signal detection is an effective tool for EEG brain mapping. Compared with Fourier and wavelet-based methods, the proposed technique provides a more detailed representation of brain activation. These findings suggest that the proposed approach has potential for investigating various pathological conditions and for enhancing the understanding of cognitive and behavioural functions of the human brain.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.00618v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：本研究提出了一种基于杜芬振荡器的测量框架，用于检测与脑电图记录中突触后电位相关活动相关的频率分量。杜芬振荡器用作非线性测量系统，其对弱周期性输入的高灵敏度能够在存在强噪声的情况下实现稳健的频率估计。由于突触后电位嵌入在嘈杂的脑电图记录中，因此它们被视为微弱信号。根据检测到的脑电图通道上的微弱信号频率分布，构建大脑活动的地形图，以可视化神经激活的空间变化。为此，分析了两名音乐家和两名观众在音乐实验期间记录的脑电图信号。在所有 EEG 通道的 4 37 Hz 范围内搜索弱信号的频率分量。随后，根据检测到的弱信号分量的数量生成脑地形图。结果表明，基于杜芬振荡器的微弱信号检测是脑电图脑图绘制的有效工具。与基于傅里叶和小波的方法相比，所提出的技术提供了更详细的大脑激活表示。这些发现表明，所提出的方法具有研究各种病理状况并增强对人脑认知和行为功能的理解的潜力。
