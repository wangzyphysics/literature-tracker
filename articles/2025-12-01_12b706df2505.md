## Abstract
Author(s): A. Bagard, C. Hu, X. Henning, T. Fix, M. Lenertz, A. Dinia, S. Colis, and M. V. Rastei A method for measuring the depletion region width at oxide heterostructure interfaces is developed. It is demonstrated on bismuth ferrite–chromite thin films that exhibit p-type behavior and form a p–n-like junction when grown on a Nb-doped strontium titanate substrate. Using surface photovoltage measurements with Kelvin probe force microscopy under illumination on films of different thicknesses, a maximum SPV response is identified, revealing the depletion region width. This width also enables estimation of the acceptor concentration in the film, providing a non-destructive approach to probing ferroelectric heterostructures. [Phys. Rev. Materials 9, 123801] Published Mon Dec 01, 2025

## 摘要（中文）
作者：A. Bagard、C. Hu、X. Henning、T. Fix、M. Lenertz、A. Dinia、S. Colis 和 M. V. Rastei 开发了一种测量氧化物异质结构界面处耗尽区宽度的方法。它在铁氧体-亚铬酸铋薄膜上得到了证明，当在掺铌钛酸锶基板上生长时，铁氧体-亚铬酸铋薄膜表现出 p 型行为并形成 p-n 状结。使用开尔文探针力显微镜在照明下对不同厚度的薄膜进行表面光电压测量，确定了最大 SPV 响应，揭示了耗尽区宽度。该宽度还可以估计薄膜中的受体浓度，从而提供一种非破坏性的方法来探测铁电异质结构。 [物理。修订版材料 9, 123801] 于 2025 年 12 月 1 日星期一发布
