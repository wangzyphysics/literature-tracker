## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03724v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Turbulence transition often arises from a subcritical transition between bistable states characterized by invariant sets of deterministic dynamical systems, and such transitions can be triggered by system noise as rare events. In this study, we employ the Onsager-Machlup (OM) formulation of stochastic dynamics to examine the Hamilton equations governing the most probable transition paths (MPPs). We introduce an effective potential function, termed the OM potential, which depends on the noise strength. Focusing on the Dauchot-Manneville model as a minimal system with an edge state, we comprehensively analyze the MPP between laminar and turbulent states for different transition times. We find that the MPPs cross the separatrix at nearly the same point regardless of the transition time, and the obtained OM action values suggest that the transition to turbulence occurs more frequently than the transition to the laminar state. Moreover, we numerically demonstrate that the noise-induced transition paths follow the OM potential landscape and its bifurcation diagram, indicating that the qualitative behavior of the MPPs is determined by the OM potential. Our methodology formulated in general dynamical systems provides a theoretical basis for predicting noise-induced transitions among invariant sets of the dynamics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03724v1 公告类型：新 摘要：湍流转变通常源于双稳态之间的亚临界转变，其特征是确定性动力系统的不变集，并且这种转变可以由系统噪声作为罕见事件触发。在本研究中，我们采用随机动力学的 Onsager-Machlup (OM) 公式来检查控制最可能转变路径 (MPP) 的哈密顿方程。我们引入了一个有效的势函数，称为 OM 势，它取决于噪声强度。我们将 Dauchot-Manneville 模型作为具有边缘态的最小系统，全面分析不同过渡时间的层流态和湍流态之间的 MPP。我们发现，无论转变时间如何，MPP 都会在几乎相同的点穿过分界线，并且获得的 OM 作用值表明，向湍流的转变比向层流状态的转变更频繁地发生。此外，我们在数值上证明了噪声引起的转变路径遵循 OM 势势及其分叉图，这表明 MPP 的定性行为是由 OM 势决定的。我们在一般动力系统中制定的方法为预测动态不变集之间噪声引起的转变提供了理论基础。
