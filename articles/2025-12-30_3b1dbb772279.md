## Abstract
arXiv:2507.10531v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Ferromagnetic exponential random graph models (ERGMs) are nonlinear exponential tilts of Erd\H{o}s-R\'enyi models, under which the presence of certain subgraphs such as triangles may be emphasized. These models are mixtures of metastable wells which each behave macroscopically like new Erd\H{o}s-R\'enyi models themselves, exhibiting the same laws of large numbers for the overall edge count as well as all subgraph counts. However, the microscopic fluctuations of these quantities remained elusive for some time. Building on a recent breakthrough by Fang, Liu, Shao and Zhao [FLSZ24] driven by Stein's method, we prove quantitative central limit theorems (CLTs) for these quantities and more in metastable wells under ferromagnetic ERGMs. One main novelty of our results is that they apply also in the supercritical (low temperature) regime of parameters, which has previously been relatively unexplored. To accomplish this, we develop a novel probabilistic technique based on the careful analysis of the evolution of relevant quantities under the ERGM Glauber dynamics. Our technique allows us to deliver the main input to the method developed by [FLSZ24], which is the fact that the fluctuations of subgraph counts are driven by those of the overall edge count. This was first shown for the triangle count by Sambale and Sinulis [SS20] in the Dobrushin (very high temperature) regime via functional-analytic methods. We feel our technique clarifies the underlying mechanisms at play, and it also supplies improved bounds on the Wasserstein and Kolmogorov distances between the observables at hand and the limiting Gaussians, as compared to the results of [FLSZ24] in the subcritical (high temperature) regime beyond the Dobrushin regime. Moreover, our technique is flexible enough to also yield quantitative CLTs for vertex degrees and local subgraph counts, which have not appeared before in any parameter regime.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.10531v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：铁磁指数随机图模型（ERGM）是 Erd\H{o}s-R\'enyi 模型的非线性指数倾斜，在该模型下可能会强调某些子图（例如三角形）的存在。这些模型是亚稳态井的混合物，每个模型在宏观上都像新的 Erd\H{o}s-R\'enyi 模型本身一样，在整体边计数以及所有子图计数方面表现出相同的大数定律。然而，这些量的微观波动在一段时间内仍然难以捉摸。在Fang、Liu、Shao 和Zhao [FLSZ24] 最近在Stein 方法的推动下取得的突破的基础上，我们证明了铁磁ERGM 下亚稳态井中这些量以及更多量的定量中心极限定理(CLT)。我们的结果的一个主要新颖之处在于它们也适用于超临界（低温）参数范围，而这在以前相对未被探索过。为了实现这一目标，我们基于对 ERGM Glauber 动力学下相关量演化的仔细分析，开发了一种新颖的概率技术。我们的技术使我们能够向[FLSZ24]开发的方法提供主要输入，即子图计数的波动是由整体边缘计数的波动驱动的。 Sambale 和 Sinulis [SS20] 在 Dobrushin（非常高的温度）条件下通过泛函分析方法首次证明了这一点。我们认为我们的技术阐明了起作用的潜在机制，并且与 [FLSZ24] 在超出 Dobrushin 区域的亚临界（高温）区域中的结果相比，它还提供了现有可观测量与极限高斯之间的 Wasserstein 和 Kolmogorov 距离的改进界限。此外，我们的技术足够灵活，还可以生成顶点度数和局部子图计数的定量 CLT，这在任何参数体系中都没有出现过。
