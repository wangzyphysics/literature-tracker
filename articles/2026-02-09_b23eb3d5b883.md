## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06236v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Transition-metal nitrides in {\eta}-carbide type structures exhibit unusual bonding motifs and proximity to magnetic instabilities. Yet they remain unexplored in thin-film form due to the difficulty of stabilizing nitrogen-poor ternaries among competing phases. Here, we report the thin-film synthesis and phase-stability mapping of the {\eta}-nitride systems Fe-W-N and Fe-Mo-N. Amorphous Fe-M-N (M = W, Mo) combinatorial libraries deposited by reactive co-sputtering crystallize upon rapid thermal annealing, enabling systematic identification of synthesis windows as a function of composition and annealing temperature. Using laboratory powder X-ray diffraction and synchrotron grazing incidence wide angle X-ray scattering, we establish that Fe3Mo3N-based {\eta}-carbide phases form over a substantially broader compositional and thermal range than W-based compositions, where {\eta}-structures are stabilized only when the films are Fe-rich. These trends are rationalized using mixed chemical-potential vs. composition phase diagrams that capture the narrow nitrogen chemical-potential stability of {\eta}-nitrides. Magnetic measurements reveal that ferromagnetism is induced in Fe-rich Fe3.54Mo2.46N with a small exchange-bias-like response that is absent in Fe3W3N-based compositions, highlighting the sensitivity of magnetic behavior to modest deviations from stoichiometry. This work establishes practical thin-film synthesis routes for {\eta}-nitride materials and demonstrates how composition can be tuned to access emergent magnetic phenomena in these complex nitrides.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06236v1 公告类型：新摘要：{\eta}-碳化物类型结构中的过渡金属氮化物表现出不寻常的键合图案和接近磁不稳定性。然而，由于在竞争相之间难以稳定贫氮三元化合物，因此它们仍未以薄膜形式被探索。在这里，我们报告了 {\eta}-氮化物系统 Fe-W-N 和 Fe-Mo-N 的薄膜合成和相稳定性图。通过反应共溅射沉积的非晶 Fe-M-N（M = W、Mo）组合库在快速热退火时结晶，从而能够系统地识别作为成分和退火温度函数的合成窗口。利用实验室粉末 X 射线衍射和同步加速器掠入射广角 X 射线散射，我们确定 Fe3Mo3N 基 eta 碳化物相的形成范围比 W 基成分要宽得多，其中 eta 结构仅当薄膜富含 Fe 时才稳定。使用混合化学势与成分相图来合理化这些趋势，该相图捕获了{\eta}-氮化物的窄氮化学势稳定性。磁性测量表明，富铁 Fe3.54Mo2.46N 中会产生铁磁性，并具有小交换偏置样响应，而 Fe3W3N 基组合物中不存在这种响应，这凸显了磁性行为对化学计量适度偏差的敏感性。这项工作为{\eta}-氮化物材料建立了实用的薄膜合成路线，并演示了如何调整成分以获取这些复杂氮化物中出现的磁性现象。
