## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02114v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The global transition from traditional power plants to renewable energy sources introduces new challenges in grid stability, primarily because inverter-based technologies provide insufficient inertia. To address this, we introduce an artificial intelligence algorithm that autonomously stabilizes power grids by adaptively tuning admittance regulators in response to disturbances. This Adaptive Admittance Controller (AAC) algorithm not only stabilizes the system in real time but also identifies the best regulator locations, thereby unifying grid planning and real time control within a single framework. When tested on a real UK power grid, the AAC markedly reduces frequency deviations and rapidly restores nominal operation. In addition, the algorithm isolates a small number of key regulators and intervenes only on these, lowering both system complexity and cost. The AAC algorithm further reduces the nonlinearity effect, quickly stabilizing the frequency and power flow. This intelligent control scheme enables power grids to reliably return to stable operating conditions under a broad spectrum of fault scenarios. The proposed framework can also be used to mitigate cascading failures by adaptively controlling critical links in a variety of networked infrastructures, such as cascades of traffic congestion on road networks or fuse failures in energy-saving systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02114v1 公告类型：新 摘要：全球从传统发电厂向可再生能源的转型给电网稳定性带来了新的挑战，这主要是因为基于逆变器的技术提供的惯性不足。为了解决这个问题，我们引入了一种人工智能算法，该算法通过自适应调整导纳调节器以响应干扰来自动稳定电网。这种自适应导纳控制器（AAC）算法不仅可以实时稳定系统，还可以识别最佳调节器位置，从而将电网规划和实时控制统一在一个框架内。在真实的英国电网上进行测试时，AAC 显着减少了频率偏差并快速恢复正常运行。此外，该算法隔离了少量关键调节器并仅对其进行干预，从而降低了系统复杂性和成本。 AAC算法进一步降低非线性效应，快速稳定频率和潮流。这种智能控制方案使电网能够在各种故障情况下可靠地恢复到稳定运行状态。所提出的框架还可用于通过自适应控制各种网络基础设施中的关键链路来减轻级联故障，例如道路网络上的级联交通拥堵或节能系统中的熔断器故障。
