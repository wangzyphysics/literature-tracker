## Abstract
Nature Machine Intelligence, Published online: 11 March 2026; doi:10.1038/s42256-026-01197-w Combining soft robotics with neuromorphic engineering is a promising approach in embodied intelligence. Giulia d’Angelo et al. contribute to progress in this field by developing a framework for benchmarking neuromorphic controllers on soft robotic platforms.
