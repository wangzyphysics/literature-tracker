## Abstract
arXiv:2601.08086v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Traditional 2D hydraulic models face significant computational challenges that limit their applications that are time-sensitive or require many model evaluations. This study presents a physics-informed Deep Operator Network (DeepONet) framework for computational hydraulics modeling that learns the solution operator of the 2D shallow water equations (SWEs) to create fast surrogate models. The framework can operate in two modes: a purely data-driven SWE-DeepONet that learns from numerical solver such as SRH-2D, and a physics-informed PI-SWE-DeepONet that additionally incorporates the continuous SWEs as constraints during training. Based on a real-world case, steady flows in a reach of the Sacramento River in California, it is demonstrated that PI-SWE-DeepONet possesses much enhanced prediction capability than SWE-DeepONet when applied to out-of-distribution scenarios. The physics-informed model is shown to exhibit slower error growth and larger breakdown distances in comparison with SWE-DeepONet. The gain of the physics-informed training, however, comes with costs, chief among which are the simulated results have slightly higher errors for in-distribution cases. It reflects the existence of a tension between the two competing training objectives: fitting the results from the traditional hydraulic model and satisfying the continuous governing equations. In this study, guidelines are developed for selecting the appropriate approach based on a real-world case: PI-SWE-DeepONet is preferred for out-of-distribution predictions, uncertain training data, or when physical consistency is a priority, while SWE-DeepONet is recommended if the modeling objective is to replicate faithfully the traditional hydraulic model results within the training distribution. Other challenges are also discussed, such as the loss weighting approach.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.08086v1 公告类型：新 摘要：传统的二维水力模型面临着巨大的计算挑战，限制了其对时间敏感或需要多次模型评估的应用。本研究提出了一种用于计算水力学建模的物理信息深度算子网络 (DeepONet) 框架，该框架学习二维浅水方程 (SWE) 的解算子以创建快速代理模型。该框架可以以两种模式运行：纯数据驱动的 SWE-DeepONet，它从 SRH-2D 等数值求解器中学习；以及基于物理的 PI-SWE-DeepONet，它在训练期间另外将连续 SWE 作为约束。基于加利福尼亚州萨克拉门托河河段稳定水流的实际案例，证明当应用于分布外场景时，PI-SWE-DeepONet 比 SWE-DeepONet 具有更强的预测能力。与 SWE-DeepONet 相比，基于物理的模型表现出更慢的误差增长和更大的击穿距离。然而，基于物理的训练的收益是有代价的，其中最主要的是模拟结果对于分布情况的误差略高。它反映了两个相互竞争的训练目标之间存在的紧张关系：拟合传统水力模型的结果并满足连续控制方程。在本研究中，制定了根据实际情况选择适当方法的指南：对于分布外预测、不确定的训练数据或优先考虑物理一致性时，首选 PI-SWE-DeepONet，而如果建模目标是在训练分布内忠实地复制传统水力模型结果，则建议使用 SWE-DeepONet。还讨论了其他挑战，例如损失加权方法。
