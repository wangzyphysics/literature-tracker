## Abstract
Hybrid perovskites have become a leading candidate for next-generation photovoltaic applications, but they still suffer from several intrinsic and extrinsic sources of instability. Residual tensile stress has emerged as a major source of extrinsic instability, as it originates from thin film fabrication and choice of substrate. Here, we propose an experimentally validated mechanical stress-diffusion framework which links dynamic stress relaxation behavior of perovskites to vacancy-mediated grain boundary (GB) halide diffusional, namely, Coble creep. Using temperature-dependent in situ analysis of stress and strain relaxation across the perovskite composition, we quantitatively link these mechanisms. We extend this framework to link these mechanisms to material stability in the context of ambient manufacturing in the presence of humidity. The Coble creep mechanism is circumvented by the fast diffusion of water molecules, with potentially severe impact on material degradation. We show how pre-relaxation of perovskite films in a dry environment (0% RH and N2) extends lifetime from <100 hours to >1,000 hours in moderately humid air by removing the driving force for moisture uptake. This work identifies the fundamental mechanisms of stress relaxation and diffusion responsible for moisture uptake and proposes stress relaxation approaches which facilitate ambient manufacturing of existing materials and fabrication approaches under moderate humidity conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
混合钙钛矿已成为下一代光伏应用的主要候选者，但它们仍然受到一些内在和外在不稳定因素的影响。残余拉伸应力已成为外部不稳定的主要来源，因为它源于薄膜制造和基板的选择。在这里，我们提出了一种经过实验验证的机械应力扩散框架，它将钙钛矿的动态应力松弛行为与空位介导的晶界（GB）卤化物扩散（即 Coble 蠕变）联系起来。通过对钙钛矿组合物的应力和应变松弛进行温度依赖性原位分析，我们定量地将这些机制联系起来。我们扩展了这个框架，将这些机制与存在湿度的环境制造环境下的材料稳定性联系起来。 Coble 蠕变机制被水分子的快速扩散所规避，这对材料降解可能产生严重影响。我们展示了钙钛矿薄膜在干燥环境（0% RH 和 N2）中的预松弛如何通过消除吸湿驱动力，将其在中等潮湿空气中的使用寿命延长至 1,000 小时。这项工作确定了导致水分吸收的应力松弛和扩散的基本机制，并提出了应力松弛方法，以促进现有材料的环境制造和中等湿度条件下的制造方法。
