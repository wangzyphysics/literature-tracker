## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09283v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Choosing a representative element volume (REV) from finite cylindrical $\mu$CT scans becomes ambiguous when a key field variable exhibits a slow axial trend, because estimated statistics can change systematically with subvolume size and position rather than converging under simple averaging. A practical workflow is presented to size an REV under such nonstationary conditions by first suppressing axial drift/trend to obtain a residual field suitable for second-order analysis, and then selecting the smallest analysis diameter for which low-wavenumber content stabilizes within a prescribed tolerance. The approach is demonstrated on \textit{Thalassinoides}-bearing rocks, whose branching, connected burrow networks impose heterogeneity on length scales comparable to typical laboratory core diameters, making imaging-based microstructural statistics and downstream digital-rock proxies highly sensitive to the chosen subvolume. From segmented data, a scalar ``burrowsity'' field--capturing burrow-related pore spaces and infills--is defined, and axial detrending (with optional normalization) is applied to mitigate acquisition drift and nonstationary trends. Representativeness is then posed as a diameter-convergence problem on nested inscribed cylinders: the two-point covariance and its isotropic spectral counterpart $\widehat{C}$ are estimated, and the smallest diameter at which the low-wavenumber plateau becomes stable is selected. Applied to a segmented \textit{Thalassinoides} core, the method identifies a minimum analysis cylinder of approximately $D_{\mathrm{REV}}\approx 93~\mathrm{mm}$ and $H_{\mathrm{REV}}\approx 83~\mathrm{mm}$, enabling reproducible correlation-scale reporting and connectivity-sensitive property estimation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09283v1 公告类型：新 摘要：当关键场变量表现出缓慢的轴向趋势时，从有限圆柱 $\mu$CT 扫描中选择代表性单元体积 (REV) 变得不明确，因为估计的统计数据可能会随着子体积大小和位置而系统地变化，而不是在简单平均下收敛。提出了一种实用的工作流程，在这种非平稳条件下确定 REV 的大小，首先抑制轴向漂移/趋势以获得适合二阶分析的残余场，然后选择使低波数内容稳定在规定公差内的最小分析直径。该方法在含有 \textit{Thalassinoides} 的岩石上进行了演示，其分支、连接的洞穴网络在长度尺度上强加了与典型实验室岩心直径相当的异质性，使得基于成像的微观结构统计数据和下游数字岩石代理对所选子体积高度敏感。根据分段数据，定义了标量“洞穴性”字段（捕获与洞穴相关的孔隙空间和填充物），并应用轴向去趋势（带有可选的标准化）来减轻采集漂移和非平稳趋势。然后将代表性提出为嵌套内切圆柱上的直径收敛问题：估计两点协方差及其各向同性光谱对应物$\widehat{C}$，并选择低波数平台变得稳定的最小直径。应用于分段的 \textit{Thalassinoides} 核心，该方法识别出大约 $D_{\mathrm{REV}}\approx 93~\mathrm{mm}$ 和 $H_{\mathrm{REV}}\approx 83~\mathrm{mm}$ 的最小分析柱面，从而实现可重复的相关尺度报告和连接敏感属性估计。
