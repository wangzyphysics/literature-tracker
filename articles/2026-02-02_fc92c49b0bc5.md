## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22280v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Kitaev iridates have been proposed as candidates for realizing an elusive quantum spin liquid (QSL) state, in which strong spin-orbit coupling and bond-directional exchange generate a highly frustrated and entangled ground state. However, all physical systems proposed to host this ground state, including Li$_2$IrO$_3$, Na$_2$IrO$_3$, and RuCl$_3$, develop magnetic order at low temperatures due to competing interactions. Nonetheless, theoretical modeling of experimental data has shown that Kitaev interactions are still present, motivating the application of perturbations such as pressure, magnetic field, and chemical doping to drive the system into the QSL phase. Here we study $\beta$-Li$_{2}$Ru$_{x}$Ir$_{1-x}$O$_{3}$ with dilute levels of Ru, $x \lesssim 10\%$. Through a combination of magnetometry, resonant elastic X-ray scattering, ac-heat capacity, and muon spin relaxation/resonance, we show that weak magnetic disorder suppresses long-range antiferromagnetic order and stabilizes an anisotropic spin glass that retains key signatures of Kitaev exchange. This Kitaev spin glass shows pronounced directional anisotropy in its magnetic susceptibility and thermoremenant magnetization. These results demonstrate that dilute magnetic disorder can access an anisotropic Kitaev spin glass: a proximate phase that freezes the Kitaev frustration landscape. This could provide a new window into the degeneracy, anisotropy, and competing interactions underlying the Kitaev QSL.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22280v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Kitaev iridates 已被提议作为实现难以捉摸的量子自旋液体（QSL）态的候选者，其中强自旋轨道耦合和键方向交换产生高度受抑和纠缠的基态。然而，所有提议承载这种基态的物理系统，包括 Li$_2$IrO$_3$、Na$_2$IrO$_3$ 和 RuCl$_3$，都会由于竞争相互作用而在低温下形成磁序。尽管如此，实验数据的理论模型表明，Kitaev 相互作用仍然存在，激发了压力、磁场和化学掺杂等扰动的应用，以驱动系统进入 QSL 阶段。在这里，我们研究 $\beta$-Li$_{2}$Ru$_{x}$Ir$_{1-x}$O$_{3}$ 与稀释水平的 Ru，$x \lesssim 10\%$。通过磁力测量、共振弹性 X 射线散射、交流热容和 μ 子自旋弛豫/共振的结合，我们表明弱磁无序抑制长程反铁磁有序并稳定保留基塔耶夫交换关键特征的各向异性自旋玻璃。这种基塔耶夫自旋玻璃的磁化率和热剩磁化强度表现出明显的方向各向异性。这些结果表明，稀磁紊乱可以进入各向异性的基塔耶夫自旋玻璃：冻结基塔耶夫挫败景观的邻近相。这可以为了解 Kitaev QSL 背后的简并性、各向异性和竞争相互作用提供一个新的窗口。
