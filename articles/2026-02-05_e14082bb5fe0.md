## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04313v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We compute the critical exponents of three-dimensional magnets with strong dipole-dipole interactions using the functional renormalization group (FRG) within the local potential approximation including the wave function renormalization (LPA$^\prime$). The system is governed by the Aharony fixed point, which is scale-invariant but lacks conformal invariance. Our nonperturbative FRG analysis identifies this fixed point and determines its scaling behavior. The resulting critical exponents are found to be close to those of the Heisenberg $O(3)$ universality class, as computed within the same FRG/LPA$^\prime$ framework. This proximity confirms the distinct yet numerically similar nature of the two universality classes.

arXiv:2602.04313v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用局部势近似中的函数重正化群 (FRG) 计算具有强偶极-偶极相互作用的三维磁体的临界指数，包括波函数重正化 (LPA$^\prime$)。该系统由 Aharony 不动点控制，该不动点具有尺度不变性，但缺乏共形不变性。我们的非微扰 FRG 分析识别了这个固定点并确定了其缩放行为。结果发现，在相同的 FRG/LPA$^\prime$ 框架内计算得出的临界指数接近海森堡 $O(3)$ 普适性类别的指数。这种接近性证实了两个普遍性类别在数量上不同但相似的性质。
