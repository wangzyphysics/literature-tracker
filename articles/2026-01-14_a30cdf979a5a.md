## Abstract
Exposure to harsh radiation environments leads to displacement damage in semiconductor materials, ultimately degrading the device performance. Molecular dynamics (MD) is a powerful method for simulating the dynamic processes of radiation-induced defect generation, clustering, and evolution, which are often beyond the reach of experimental observation. In this work, we introduce a machine-learning Gaussian approximation potential for germanium, specifically designed to describe properties related to radiation-induced collision cascades and the resulting damage. A repulsive potential is incorporated to accurately capture short-range interactions during cascade simulations. In addition, we reproduce elastic, thermal, and vibrational properties, and accurately capture the energetics of vacancies and self-interstitials. These high-fidelity predictions are comparable to ab initio calculations while requiring only a compact training database. This potential is expected to facilitate accurate MD simulations of radiation damage in germanium with greatly improved accuracy compared to empirical potentials.

## 摘要（中文）
暴露在恶劣的辐射环境中会导致半导体材料发生位移损坏，最终降低器件性能。分子动力学（MD）是模拟辐射引起的缺陷产生、聚集和演化动态过程的强大方法，而这些过程往往超出了实验观察的范围。在这项工作中，我们引入了锗的机器学习高斯近似势，专门用于描述与辐射引起的碰撞级联和由此产生的损坏相关的特性。引入排斥势以在级联模拟期间准确捕获短程相互作用。此外，我们再现弹性、热和振动特性，并准确捕获空位和自填隙的能量。这些高保真度预测可与从头计算相媲美，同时仅需要紧凑的训练数据库。与经验潜力相比，这种潜力有望促进对锗辐射损伤的精确MD模拟，其精度大大提高。
