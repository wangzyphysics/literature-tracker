## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07843v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The integration of plasma activated water (PAW) with herbal infusions offers a sustainable approach to enhancing the functional bioactivity of plant derived compounds. In this study, neem (Azadirachta indica) and tulsi (Ocimum sanctum) infusions were treated with PAW generated using an atmospheric pressure gliding arc discharge system. The aim was to investigate plasma induced modifications in phytochemicals and their subsequent effects on antimicrobial and antioxidant properties. Spectroscopic (UV Vis, FTIR) and chromatographic (HPLC) analyses demonstrated structural alterations in key polyphenolic constituents, accompanied by mild acidification and changes in redox potential. Total phenolic content (TPC) and flavonoid levels increased significantly following 10 min PAW treatment, while prolonged exposure (15 min) led to partial degradation, suggesting an optimum treatment window. Antioxidant assays (DPPH, ABTS, FRAP) confirmed improved radical scavenging capacity, correlating with enhanced reducing power of modified phytochemicals. Antimicrobial evaluation against Escherichia coli and Staphylococcus aureus revealed synergistic inhibitory effects, with reduced minimum inhibitory concentrations (MIC) for PAW-treated infusions. Collectively, the results highlight the potential of PAW to modulate herbal bioactives, extending their efficacy in natural preservation systems and biomedical formulations. This green plasma-herbal synergy provides a promising pathway toward eco-friendly food safety and healthcare applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07843v1 公告类型：新 摘要：等离子活化水 (PAW) 与草药输液的结合提供了一种可持续的方法来增强植物衍生化合物的功能性生物活性。在这项研究中，印楝 (Azadirachta indica) 和罗勒 (Ocimum sainttum) 浸液采用大气压滑行电弧放电系统产生的 PAW 进行处理。目的是研究等离子体诱导的植物化学物质修饰及其对抗菌和抗氧化特性的后续影响。光谱（UV Vis、FTIR）和色谱（HPLC）分析表明，关键多酚成分发生了结构变化，并伴有轻度酸化和氧化还原电位的变化。 PAW 处理 10 分钟后，总酚含量 (TPC) 和类黄酮水平显着增加，而长时间暴露（15 分钟）会导致部分降解，表明最佳处理窗口。抗氧化测定（DPPH、ABTS、FRAP）证实自由基清除能力得到改善，这与改良植物化学物质的还原能力增强相关。针对大肠杆菌和金黄色葡萄球菌的抗菌评估显示出协同抑制作用，经 PAW 处理的输注液的最低抑制浓度 (MIC) 降低。总的来说，这些结果凸显了 PAW 调节草药生物活性物质、扩展其在自然保存系统和生物医学配方中的功效的潜力。这种绿色血浆-草药协同作用为环保食品安全和医疗保健应用提供了一条有前途的途径。
