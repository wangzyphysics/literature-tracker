## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03367v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Understanding and modeling the constitutive behavior of concrete is crucial for civil and defense applications, yet widely used phenomenological models such as Karagozian \& Case concrete (KCC) model depend on empirically calibrated failure surfaces that lack flexibility in model form and associated uncertainty quantification. This work develops a physics-informed framework that retains the modular elastoplastic structure of KCC model while replacing its empirical failure surface with a constrained Gaussian Process Regression (GPR) surrogate that can be learned directly from experimentally accessible observables. Triaxial compression data under varying confinement levels are used for training, and the surrogate is then evaluated at confinement levels not included in the training set to assess its generalization capability. Results show that an unconstrained GPR interpolates well near training conditions but deteriorates and violates essential physical constraints under extrapolation, even when augmented with simulated data. In contrast, a physics-informed GPR that incorporates derivative-based constraints aligned with known material behavior yields markedly better accuracy and reliability, including at higher confinement levels beyond the training range. Probabilistic enforcement of these constraints also reduces predictive variance, producing tighter confidence intervals in data-scarce regimes. Overall, the proposed approach delivers a robust, uncertainty-aware surrogate that improves generalization and streamlines calibration without sacrificing the interpretability and numerical efficiency of the KCC model, offering a practical path toward an improved constitutive models for concrete.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03367v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：理解和建模混凝土的本构行为对于民用和国防应用至关重要，但广泛使用的唯象模型，如 Karagozian \& Case 混凝土 (KCC) 模型依赖于经验校准的破坏面，而这些破坏面在模型形式和相关的不确定性量化方面缺乏灵活性。这项工作开发了一个基于物理的框架，该框架保留了 KCC 模型的模块化弹塑性结构，同时用约束高斯过程回归 (GPR) 替代项替换其经验失效面，该替代项可以直接从实验可观测到的数据中学习。使用不同限制水平下的三轴压缩数据进行训练，然后在训练集中未包含的限制水平上评估替代物，以评估其泛化能力。结果表明，无约束探地雷达的插值效果非常接近训练条件，但即使在使用模拟数据进行增强时，也会在外推条件下恶化并违反基本的物理约束。相比之下，基于物理学的探地雷达结合了与已知材料行为一致的基于导数的约束，可产生明显更好的准确性和可靠性，包括在超出训练范围的更高限制水平下。这些约束的概率执行还减少了预测方差，在数据稀缺的情况下产生更严格的置信区间。总体而言，所提出的方法提供了一种稳健的、不确定性感知的替代方法，可以提高泛化性并简化校准，而不会牺牲 KCC 模型的可解释性和数值效率，从而为改进混凝土本构模型提供了一条实用途径。
