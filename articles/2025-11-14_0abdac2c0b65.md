## Abstract
A multi-enzyme molecular machine enables the complete conversion of cellulose into starch, potentially unlocking agricultural residues and grass as a new feed and food source for non-ruminant animals and humans.

## 摘要（中文）
多酶分子机器能够将纤维素完全转化为淀粉，从而有可能将农业残留物和草作为非反刍动物和人类的新饲料和食物来源。
