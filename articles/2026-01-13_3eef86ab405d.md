## Abstract
arXiv:2509.15322v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We consider overdamped physical systems evolving under a feedback-controlled fluctuating potential and in contact with a thermal bath at temperature $T$. A Markovian description of the dynamics, which keeps only the last value of the control action, is advantageous -- both from the theoretical and the practical side -- for the entropy balance. Novel second-law equalities and bounds for the extractable work are obtained, the latter being both tighter and easier to evaluate than those in the literature based on the whole chain of controller actions. The Markovian framework also allows us to prove that the bound for the extractable work that incorporates the unavailable information is saturated in a wide class of physical systems, for error-free measurements. These results are illustrated in model systems. For imperfect measurements, there appears an interval of measurement uncertainty, including the point at which work ceases to be extracted, where the new Markovian bound is tighter than the unavailable information bound.

arXiv:2509.15322v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们考虑过阻尼物理系统在反馈控制的波动电势下演化并与温度为 $T$ 的热浴接触。动态的马尔可夫描述仅保留控制动作的最后一个值，无论从理论还是实践方面来说，对于熵平衡都是有利的。获得了可提取工作的新颖的第二定律等式和界限，后者比文献中基于整个控制器操作链的那些更严格且更容易评估。马尔可夫框架还允许我们证明，对于无差错测量，包含不可用信息的可提取工作的界限在各种物理系统中是饱和的。这些结果在模型系统中得到了说明。对于不完美的测量，会出现一个测量不确定度的区间，包括停止提取功的点，其中新的马尔可夫界限比不可用的信息界限更紧。
