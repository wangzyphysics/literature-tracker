## Abstract
Ferrocene is a benchmark metallocene known for its robust chemical stability. Yet, mechanical activation of ferrocene or its irradiation when heavily distorted by strain leads to Fe–cyclopentadienyl bond scission. However, a direct link between simultaneous mechanical force transduction and photoactivation has never been established. Here, we show that light can harness stored mechanical energy to induce the metal–ligand bond dissociation. A family of ferrocene–cycloparaphenylene nanohoops (Fc[n]CPP, n = 6–8) releases Fe²⁺ efficiently under visible light, even when the ferrocene unit itself is unstrained. X-ray crystal structure analysis, quantum yield measurements, and excited-state calculations reveal that reactivity scales with strain localized in the aromatic hoop-like ligand, not with distortion at the metal center. This correlation identifies the hoop as an internal stress reservoir that lowers the triplet-state barrier for Fe–cyclopentadienyl cleavage. By coupling mechanical bias to photochemical reactivity, this work establishes a concept of photomechanophores, molecular units in which light and mechanical activation operate in concert. Thereby, we lay foundation of a general framework for designing tunable photocages and new force-responsive materials that can be modulated by light.

## 摘要（中文）
二茂铁是一种基准茂金属，以其强大的化学稳定性而闻名。然而，二茂铁的机械活化或其辐照在因应变严重变形时会导致 Fe-环戊二烯基键断裂。然而，同时机械力传导和光活化之间的直接联系尚未建立。在这里，我们证明光可以利用储存的机械能来诱导金属-配体键解离。二茂铁-环对亚苯基纳米环家族（Fc[n]CPP，n = 6-8）即使在二茂铁单元本身不受应变的情况下，也能在可见光下有效释放 Fe2+。 X射线晶体结构分析、量子产率测量和激发态计算表明，反应性随芳香环配体中局部应变的变化而变化，而不是随着金属中心的变形而变化。这种相关性将环确定为内应力库，可降低 Fe-环戊二烯基裂解的三重态势垒。通过将机械偏压与光化学反应性耦合，这项工作建立了光机械载体的概念，光机械载体是光和机械激活协同作用的分子单元。因此，我们为设计可调谐光笼和可通过光调制的新型力响应材料奠定了总体框架的基础。
