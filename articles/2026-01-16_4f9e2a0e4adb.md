## Abstract
arXiv:2601.10489v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: An $S=1$ Haldane chain is a one-dimensional (1D) quantum magnet where strong fluctuations result in quantum disordered singlet ground state with a gapped excitation spectrum. The gap magnitude is primarily set by the dominant intrachain interaction ($J_\text{1D}$). An applied magnetic field closes the gap at $B_\text{c1}$ and drives the system into a gapless Tomonaga-Luttinger liquid (TLL) regime, followed by, at lower temperatures, a Bose-Einstein condensate (BEC) ground state, persisting up to $B_\text{c2} \propto 4 J_\text{1D}/g\mu_B$. Almost all previously studied experimental realizations of such systems were based on transition-metal complexes which typically suffer from intrinsic anisotropies or large $J_\text{1D}$ values, limiting the access to the full theoretical phase diagram. We report a comprehensive study of TLL and BEC phases in the organic Haldane chain system 3,5-bis(N-tert-butylaminoxyl)-3'-nitrobiphenyl (BoNO). The absence of anisotropy and a moderate $J_\text{1D}$ enable exploration of the complete $B-T$ phase diagram. Through $^1$H nuclear magnetic resonance, combined with theoretical analysis, we characterize the TLL properties, map the BEC phase boundary $T_c (B)$, determine the associated critical exponent $\nu \approx 0.66$ at $B_\text{c2}$, and demonstrate universal quasiparticle scaling in the quantum-critical regime. These results provide full experimental validation of theoretical predictions for field-induced phases in an $S=1$ Haldane chain, made over two decades ago.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.10489v1 公告类型：新 摘要：$S=1$ Haldane 链是一种一维 (1D) 量子磁体，其中强烈的波动导致量子无序单重态基态具有带隙激发光谱。间隙大小主要由占主导地位的链内交互 ($J_\text{1D}$) 设置。施加的磁场闭合$B_\text{c1}$处的间隙，并驱动系统进入无间隙Tomonaga-Luttinger液体（TLL）状态，随后在较低温度下形成玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚（BEC）基态，持续到$B_\text{c2} \propto 4 J_\text{1D}/g\mu_B$。几乎所有先前研究的此类系统的实验实现都是基于过渡金属配合物，过渡金属配合物通常具有固有的各向异性或较大的 $J_\text{1D}$ 值，从而限制了对完整理论相图的访问。我们报告了有机霍尔烷链系统 3,5-双(N-叔丁基氨氧基)-3'-硝基联苯 (BoNO) 中 TLL 和 BEC 相的综合研究。由于不存在各向异性且$J_\text{1D}$ 适中，因此可以探索完整的$B-T$ 相图。通过$^1$H核磁共振，结合理论分析，我们表征了TLL特性，绘制了BEC相界$T_c (B)$，确定了$B_\text{c2}$处的相关临界指数$\nu \approx 0.66$，并证明了量子临界状态下的通用准粒子标度。这些结果为二十多年前做出的 $S=1$ Haldane 链中场诱导相的理论预测提供了全面的实验验证。
