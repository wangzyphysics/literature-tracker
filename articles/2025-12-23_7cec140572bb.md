## Abstract
To investigate the state-to-state dynamics of the P(D2) + H2(XΣg+1)( v = 0, j = 0) reaction, we reconstructed the PH 2 (X 2 B 1 ) potential energy surface (PES) using the permutation invariant polynomial neural network (PIP-NN) method based on 40 595 ab initio points. The aug-cc-pV Q Z basis sets with Davidson correction were employed throughout the calculations. The reference wave function for the multi-reference configuration interaction calculations was constructed from a full valence complete-active-space self-consistent field wave function. To achieve higher PES accuracy, the double many-body expansion–scaled external correlation (DMBE–SEC) method was applied to extrapolate to the one-electron complete basis set limit, yielding a total root-mean square deviation of 2.4 meV for the final NN-PES. Based on the refined PH 2 (X 2 B 1 ) NN-PES, geometries, energies, and harmonic frequencies of stationary points were obtained and analyzed in detail, showing excellent agreement with other theoretical results. Subsequently, quantum time-dependent wave packet (TDWP) and quasi-classical trajectory (QCT) methods were utilized to compute reaction probability, integral cross section (ICS), differential cross section (DCS), product rovibrational distribution, and rate constant on the developed NN-PES. The TDWP results reveal rich resonant structures, while the QCT calculations provide a qualitatively correct description of the coarse-grained reaction cross sections. These results also demonstrate a threshold-type microscopic reaction mechanism characterized by dominant forward–backward scattering, attributable to long-lived collision complex formation. Collectively, these findings provide fundamental mechanistic insights into the microscopic reaction mechanism and dynamics of phosphorus chemistry in interstellar environments.

## 摘要（中文）
为了研究 P(D2) + H2(XΣg+1)( v = 0, j = 0) 反应的状态动态，我们使用基于 40 595 个从头点的排列不变多项式神经网络 (PIP-NN) 方法重建了 PH 2 (X 2 B 1 ) 势能面 (PES)。整个计算过程中均采用带有戴维森校正的 aug-cc-pV Q Z 基组。用于多参考构型相互作用计算的参考波函数是由全价完整活性空间自洽场波函数构造的。为了获得更高的 PES 精度，采用双多体展开尺度外相关 (DMBE-SEC) 方法外推至单电子完全基组极限，最终 NN-PES 的总均方根偏差为 2.4 meV。基于精化的PH 2 (X 2 B 1 ) NN-PES，获得并详细分析了驻点的几何形状、能量和谐波频率，与其他理论结果表现出良好的一致性。随后，利用量子时间相关波包（TDWP）和准经典轨迹（QCT）方法来计算所开发的 NN-PES 上的反应概率、积分截面（ICS）、微分截面（DCS）、产物振动分布和速率常数。 TDWP 结果揭示了丰富的共振结构，而 QCT 计算提供了粗粒度反应截面的定性正确描述。这些结果还证明了一种阈值型微观反应机制，其特征是主要的前向-后向散射，归因于长寿命的碰撞复合物的形成。总的来说，这些发现为星际环境中磷化学的微观反应机制和动力学提供了基本的机制见解。
