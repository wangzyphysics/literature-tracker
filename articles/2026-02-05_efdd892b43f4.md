## Abstract
arXiv:2509.03907v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: A variety of experimental observations in monolayer transition metal dichalcogenide superconductors with Ising spin-orbit coupling suggest the presence of an unconventional superconducting pairing mechanism. Some of these experiments include observation of Leggett modes and a nodal superconducting gap in STM experiments, a large in-plane upper critical field compared to the Pauli limit, and the observation of a two-fold gap anisotropy in magnetoresistance measurements. Here, we propose a superconducting pairing mechanism mediated by spin and charge fluctuations and identify the dominant superconducting instability relevant to monolayer TaS$_2$. We then explore the effect of an additional electron-phonon pairing contribution, and compare our results with recent experimental findings. In particular, our theory stabilizes a superconducting ground state with nodal-like density of states that agrees with STM experiments. The theory obtains a large in-plane upper critical field due to a combination of Ising spin-orbit coupling and even-odd parity mixing in the superconducting state. Further, we find that an in-plane magnetic field splits the degeneracy of the superconducting ground state, and the resulting two-fold symmetric superconducting order parameter could explain the gap anisotropy observed in magnetoresistance experiments. Overall, the proposed theoretical pairing model can reconcile diverse experimental observations and remains consistent with observations on other dichalcogenide superconductors such as monolayer NbSe$_2$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.03907v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：伊辛自旋轨道耦合单层过渡金属二硫族化物超导体的各种实验观察表明存在非常规的超导配对机制。其中一些实验包括在 STM 实验中观察 Leggett 模式和节点超导间隙、与泡利极限相比较大的面内上临界场以及在磁阻测量中观察两倍间隙各向异性。在这里，我们提出了一种由自旋和电荷波动介导的超导配对机制，并确定了与单层 TaS$_2$ 相关的主要超导不稳定性。然后，我们探索额外的电子声子配对贡献的影响，并将我们的结果与最近的实验结果进行比较。特别是，我们的理论稳定了具有类节点态密度的超导基态，这与 STM 实验一致。该理论通过伊辛自旋轨道耦合和超导态奇偶校验混合的结合，获得了较大的面内上临界场。此外，我们发现面内磁场分裂了超导基态的简并性，由此产生的两倍对称超导序参数可以解释磁阻实验中观察到的间隙各向异性。总体而言，所提出的理论配对模型可以协调不同的实验观察结果，并与其他二硫属化物超导体（例如单层 NbSe$_2$）的观察结果保持一致。
