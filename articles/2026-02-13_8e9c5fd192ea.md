## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12227v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Operating matter-wave interferometers as quantum detectors for fundamental physics or inertial sensors in real-world applications with unprecedented accuracies relies on noise rejection, often implemented by correlating two sensors. Such sensors can be spatially separated (gradiometry or gravitational-wave detection) or consist of different internal states (magnetometry or quantum clock interferometry), in which case a signal-amplitude modulation may serve as a signature of a differential phase. In this work, we introduce Phase Estimation from Amplitude Collapse (PEAC) by applying targeted fitting methods for different magnetically sensitive substates of an atom interferometer. We demonstrate that PEAC provides higher trueness (up to 80% bias reduction) than standard tools for perfectly correlated signals. At its working point near, but not exactly at phase settings resulting in vanishing amplitude, it achieves precision competitive with standard methods, contrasting prior claims of optimal operation at vanishing amplitude. PEAC presents a generally applicable complementary evaluation method for correlated interferometers without phase stability, increasing the overall accuracy and enabling applications beyond atom interferometry.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12227v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：将物质波干涉仪用作基础物理的量子探测器或现实世界应用中的惯性传感器，具有前所未有的精度，这依赖于噪声抑制，通常通过关联两个传感器来实现。此类传感器可以在空间上分离（梯度测量或引力波检测）或由不同的内部状态组成（磁力测量或量子时钟干涉测量），在这种情况下，信号幅度调制可以充当微分相位的签名。在这项工作中，我们通过对原子干涉仪的不同磁敏感子态应用有针对性的拟合方法，引入了振幅塌陷相位估计（PEAC）。我们证明，对于完全相关的信号，PEAC 比标准工具提供更高的真实度（偏差减少高达 80%）。在其工作点附近但不完全在导致幅度消失的相位设置处，它实现了与标准方法相媲美的精度，这与先前声称的在幅度消失时的最佳操作形成鲜明对比。 PEAC 为没有相位稳定性的相关干涉仪提供了一种普遍适用的补充评估方法，提高了整体精度并实现了原子干涉测量之外的应用。
