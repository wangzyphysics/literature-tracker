## Abstract
The discovery of chemically novel or structurally anomalous metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) is essential for expanding reticular design space and enhancing dataset reliability. We present CHEM-AD (Chemically Unusual Metal–organic Frameworks via Autoencoder-based Detection), a label-free, CPU-efficient pipeline that detects anomalous MOFs using 81 engineered descriptors (32 geometric/chemical/topological scalars plus a 49-dimensional metal-composition encoding). A compact symmetric autoencoder (∼ 1.8 × 105 trainable parameters) learns the latent distribution of typical MOFs and assigns anomaly scores based on reconstruction error. Applied to 26,025 entries from MOFxDB, CHEM-AD identifies 488 outliers (∼1.87%) featuring distinctive topologies, unusual pore metrics (PLD: 2.56–29.48 ˚A; LCD: 4.89–63.59 ˚A), and extreme densities (0.057–4.27 g cm−3). These anomalies consistently occupy peripheral clusters in PCA embeddings and exhibit substantial Mahalanobis distances from normal MOFs, indicating multivariate deviation. Feature attribution reveals connectivity (e.g., edge/node counts, degree dispersion) as the primary driver of anomalies, followed by window-limited geometry and linker–metal composition. We categorize results into three groups: (A) topologically unusual yet plausible candidates, (B) anomalies with chemically resolvable issues, and (C) likely structural artifacts. The full pipeline executes in under six minutes on standard CPUs and does not require 3D structure fitting or graph parsing. CHEM-AD generalizes to other porous materials, providing a scalable framework for discovery, database curation, and robust preprocessing in materials informatics.

## 摘要（中文）
化学新颖或结构异常的金属有机框架（MOF）的发现对于扩大网状设计空间和提高数据集可靠性至关重要。我们提出了 CHEM-AD（基于自动编码器检测的化学异常金属有机框架），这是一种无标签、CPU 高效的管道，可使用 81 个工程描述符（32 个几何/化学/拓扑标量加上 49 维金属成分编码）检测异常 MOF。紧凑的对称自动编码器（∼ 1.8 × 105 可训练参数）学习典型 MOF 的潜在分布，并根据重建误差分配异常分数。应用于 MOFxDB 中的 26,025 个条目，CHEM-AD 识别出 488 个异常值 (∼1.87%)，这些异常值具有独特的拓扑结构、不寻常的孔隙指标（PLD：2.56–29.48 ℃；LCD：4.89–63.59 ℃）和极端密度（0.057–4.27 g cm−3）。这些异常始终占据 PCA 嵌入中的外围簇，并与正常 MOF 表现出很大的马哈拉诺比斯距离，表明存在多变量偏差。特征归因揭示了连接性（例如，边缘/节点计数、程度分散）是异常的主要驱动因素，其次是窗口限制的几何结构和连接体-金属成分。我们将结果分为三组：(A) 拓扑上不寻常但合理的候选者，(B) 具有化学可解决问题的异常，以及 (C) 可能的结构伪影。完整的管道在标准 CPU 上执行时间不到六分钟，并且不需要 3D 结构拟合或图形解析。 CHEM-AD 可以推广到其他多孔材料，为材料信息学中的发现、数据库管理和强大的预处理提供可扩展的框架。
