## Abstract
Nature Computational Science, Published online: 22 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s43588-025-00914-6 Quantum machine learning is being actively explored to assess whether quantum resources can enhance learning and inference, yet major obstacles remain. Here, we discuss pressing challenges and outline potential pathways toward future practical applications.
