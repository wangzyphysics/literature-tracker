## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07548v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The application of generative modeling to many-body physics offers a promising pathway for analyzing high-dimensional state spaces of spin systems. However, unlike computer vision tasks where visual fidelity suffices, physical systems require the rigorous reproduction of higher-order statistical moments and thermodynamic quantities. While Score-Based Generative Models (SGMs) have emerged as a powerful tool, their standard formulation on Euclidean embedding space is ill-suited for continuous spin systems, where variables inherently reside on a manifold. In this work, we demonstrate that training on the Euclidean space compromises the model's ability to learn the target distribution as it prioritizes to learn the manifold constraints. We address this limitation by proposing the use of Manifold-Aware Score-Based Generative Modeling framework applied to the 64x64 2D XY model (a 4096-dimensional torus). We show that our method estimates the theoretical Boltzmann score with superior precision compared to standard diffusion models. Consequently, we successfully capture the Berezinskii-Kosterlitz Thouless (BKT) phase transition and accurately reproduce second-moment quantities, such as heat capacity without explicit feature engineering. Furthermore, we demonstrate zero-shot generalization to unseen lattice sizes, accurately recovering the physics of variable system scales without retraining. Since this approach bypasses domain-specific feature engineering, it remains intrinsically generalizable to other continuous spin systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07548v1 公告类型：新 摘要：生成建模在多体物理中的应用为分析自旋系统的高维状态空间提供了一条有前途的途径。然而，与视觉保真度就足够的计算机视觉任务不同，物理系统需要严格再现高阶统计矩和热力学量。虽然基于分数的生成模型（SGM）已经成为一种强大的工具，但它们在欧几里德嵌入空间上的标准公式并不适合连续自旋系统，因为变量本质上驻留在流形上。在这项工作中，我们证明了欧几里得空间上的训练会损害模型学习目标分布的能力，因为它优先学习流形约束。我们建议使用应用于 64x64 2D XY 模型（4096 维环面）的基于流形感知分数的生成建模框架来解决此限制。我们表明，与标准扩散模型相比，我们的方法以更高的精度估计了理论玻尔兹曼分数。因此，我们成功捕获了 Berezinskii-Kosterlitz Thouless (BKT) 相变，并准确地再现了二阶矩量，例如热容量，而无需明确的特征工程。此外，我们演示了对看不见的晶格尺寸的零样本泛化，无需重新训练即可准确恢复可变系统尺度的物理特性。由于这种方法绕过了特定领域的特征工程，因此它本质上仍然可以推广到其他连续自旋系统。
