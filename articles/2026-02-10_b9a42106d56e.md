## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07649v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Short THz pulses are highly attractive both for fundamental research and practical applications in this underdeveloped region of the electromagnetic spectrum. Typically, THz pulses are generated using nonlinear optical methods, starting from a high-power visible or near-IR mode-locked source, but usually suffer from low conversion efficiencies. Here, we present a direct on-chip THz source of coherent short pulse trains based on active mode-locking of an inverse-designed planarized Y-coupled THz quantum cascade laser. By employing quasi-resonant microwave modulation of the asymmetric laser cavity, mode-locked pulses as short as 2.3 ps are generated, with emission bandwidths spanning 500 and 700 GHz at a central frequency of 2.9 THz.

arXiv:2602.07649v1 公告类型：新 摘要：短太赫兹脉冲对于电磁频谱欠发达区域的基础研究和实际应用都极具吸引力。通常，太赫兹脉冲是使用非线性光学方法生成的，从高功率可见光或近红外锁模源开始，但通常转换效率较低。在这里，我们提出了一种基于逆向设计的平面化 Y 耦合太赫兹量子级联激光器的主动锁模的直接片上相干短脉冲序列太赫兹源。通过采用非对称激光腔的准谐振微波调制，可生成短至 2.3 ps 的锁模脉冲，发射带宽在 2.9 THz 的中心频率下跨越 500 和 700 GHz。
