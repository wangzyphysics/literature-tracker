## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22658v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The nominal divide between $p$- and $d$-electron systems often obscures a deep underlying unity in condensed matter physics. This review elucidates the orbital homology between the $p$ and $t_{2g}$ orbital manifolds, establishing the correspondence that extends from minimal model Hamiltonians to the complex behaviors of real quantum materials. We demonstrate that despite their distinct atomic origins, these orbitals host nearly identical hopping physics and spin-orbit coupling, formalized through an effective ${l=1}$ angular momentum algebra for the $t_{2g}$ case. This equivalence allows one to transpose physical intuition and theoretical models developed for $p$-orbital systems directly onto the more complex $t_{2g}$ materials, and vice versa. We showcase how this paradigm provides a unified understanding of emergent phenomena, including non-trivial band topology, itinerant ferromagnetism, and unconventional superconductivity, across a wide range of platforms, from transition metal compounds, two-dimensional oxide heterostructures, and iron-based superconductors, to $p$-orbital ultracold gases. Ultimately, this $p$-$t_{2g}$ homology serves not only as a tool for interpretation but also as a robust design principle for engineering novel quantum states.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22658v1 公告类型：新 摘要：$p$- 和 $d$-电子系统之间的名义鸿沟常常掩盖了凝聚态物理中深层的基础统一。这篇综述阐明了 $p$ 和 $t_{2g}$ 轨道流形之间的轨道同源性，建立了从最小模型哈密顿量到真实量子材料的复杂行为的对应关系。我们证明，尽管它们具有不同的原子起源，但这些轨道具有几乎相同的跳跃物理和自旋轨道耦合，通过 $t_{2g}$ 情况的有效 ${l=1}$ 角动量代数形式化。这种等价性允许人们将为 $p$ 轨道系统开发的物理直觉和理论模型直接转换到更复杂的 $t_{2g}$ 材料上，反之亦然。我们展示了这种范式如何在从过渡金属化合物、二维氧化物异质结构和铁基超导体到 $p$ 轨道超冷气体的广泛平台上提供对新兴现象的统一理解，包括非平凡带拓扑、巡回铁磁性和非常规超导性。最终，这种 $p$-$t_{2g}$ 同源性不仅可以作为解释工具，而且可以作为工程新型量子态的稳健设计原理。
