## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04792v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We demonstrate the existence of a dynamical quantum phase transition (DQPT) in a dissipative collective-spin model that exhibits the boundary time crystal (BTC) phase. We initialize the system in the ground state of the Hamiltonian in either the BTC or the non-BTC phase, and drive it across the BTC transition. The driving is done by an abrupt quench or by a finite-time linear ramp of a Hamiltonian control parameter under Markovian Lindblad dynamics. We diagnose DQPTs through zeros of the fidelity-based Loschmidt echo between the initial state and the evolving mixed state, which induce nonanalytic cusp-like features in the associated rate function. For quenches into the BTC phase, the Loschmidt echo exhibits repeated zeros due to the emergent time-periodic steady state, whereas for quenches into the non-BTC phase, the overlap vanishes and remains zero once the dynamics relaxes to a stationary state. We further show that the DQPT persists under the ramp protocol followed by unitary evolution with the final Hamiltonian. Finally, we analyze the finite-size scaling of the first critical time and find convergence to a constant in the thermodynamic limit, with distinct power-law approaches for the quench and the ramp protocols.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04792v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们证明了耗散集体自旋模型中动态量子相变 (DQPT) 的存在，该模型表现出边界时间晶体 (BTC) 相。我们在 BTC 或非 BTC 阶段将系统初始化为哈密顿量的基态，并驱动它跨越 BTC 转变。在马尔可夫 Lindblad 动力学下，驱动是通过突然失超或哈密顿控制参数的有限时间线性斜坡来完成的。我们通过初始状态和演变的混合状态之间基于保真度的洛施密特回波的零点来诊断 DQPT，这会在相关速率函数中引入非解析尖点特征。对于进入 BTC 相的淬灭，由于出现了时间周期稳态，洛施密特回波表现出重复的零，而对于进入非 BTC 相的淬灭，一旦动力学松弛到稳态，重叠就会消失并保持为零。我们进一步表明，DQPT 在斜坡协议下持续存在，然后是最终哈密顿量的酉演化。最后，我们分析了第一临界时间的有限尺寸缩放，并发现收敛到热力学极限中的常数，并针对淬火和斜坡协议采用了不同的幂律方法。
