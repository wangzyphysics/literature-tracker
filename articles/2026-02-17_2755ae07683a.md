## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14853v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The traditional limitations of neural networks in reliably generalizing beyond the convex hulls of their training data present a significant problem for computational physics, in which one often wishes to solve PDEs in regimes far beyond anything which can be experimentally or analytically validated. In this paper, we show how it is possible to circumvent these limitations by constructing formally-verified neural network solvers for PDEs, with rigorous convergence, stability, and conservation properties, whose correctness can therefore be guaranteed even in extrapolatory regimes. By using the method of characteristics to predict the analytical properties of PDE solutions a priori (even in regions arbitrarily far from the training domain), we show how it is possible to construct rigorous extrapolatory bounds on the worst-case L^inf errors of shallow neural network approximations. Then, by decomposing PDE solutions into compositions of simpler functions, we show how it is possible to compose these shallow neural networks together to form deep architectures, based on ideas from compositional deep learning, in which the large L^inf errors in the approximations have been suppressed. The resulting framework, called BEACONS (Bounded-Error, Algebraically-COmposable Neural Solvers), comprises both an automatic code-generator for the neural solvers themselves, as well as a bespoke automated theorem-proving system for producing machine-checkable certificates of correctness. We apply the framework to a variety of linear and non-linear PDEs, including the linear advection and inviscid Burgers' equations, as well as the full compressible Euler equations, in both 1D and 2D, and illustrate how BEACONS architectures are able to extrapolate solutions far beyond the training data in a reliable and bounded way. Various advantages of the approach over the classical PINN approach are discussed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14853v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：神经网络在可靠地泛化训练数据凸包之外的传统局限性给计算物理学带来了一个重大问题，其中人们通常希望在远远超出任何可以通过实验或分析验证的范围内求解偏微分方程。在本文中，我们展示了如何通过构建经过形式验证的偏微分方程神经网络求解器来规避这些限制，该求解器具有严格的收敛性、稳定性和守恒性，因此即使在外推机制下也可以保证其正确性。通过使用特征方法先验地预测 PDE 解的分析属性（即使在远离训练域的区域），我们展示了如何在浅层神经网络近似的最坏情况 L^inf 误差上构造严格的外推界。然后，通过将偏微分方程解分解为更简单函数的组合，我们展示了如何基于组合深度学习的思想将这些浅层神经网络组合在一起形成深层架构，其中近似值中的大 L^inf 误差已被抑制。由此产生的框架称为 BEACONS（有界误差、代数可组合神经求解器），包括神经求解器本身的自动代码生成器，以及用于生成机器可检查正确性证书的定制自动定理证明系统。我们将该框架应用于各种线性和非线性偏微分方程，包括一维和二维的线性平流方程和无粘伯格斯方程，以及完全可压缩欧拉方程，并说明 BEACONS 架构如何能够以可靠且有界的方式推断远远超出训练数据的解。讨论了该方法相对于经典 PINN 方法的各种优点。
