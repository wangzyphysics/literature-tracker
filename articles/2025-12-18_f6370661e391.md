## Abstract
Author(s): Swarnim Shashank, Askar B. Abdikamalov, Honghui Liu, Abdurakhmon Nosirov, Cosimo Bambi, Indu K. Dihingia, and Yosuke Mizuno The authors use accretion-disk general relativistic magnetohydrodynamic simulations to simulate NuSTAR spectra and assess the ability of widely used X-ray reflection models to recover black hole spins. They demonstrate that only high spins are likely to be correctly predicted, and discuss limitations of lamppost models. [Phys. Rev. D 112, 123030] Published Thu Dec 18, 2025
