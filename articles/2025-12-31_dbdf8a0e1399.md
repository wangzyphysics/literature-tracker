## Abstract
Multifold topological semimetals host fermions with opposite chiralities at topological band crossings1,2,3. Chiral fermionic transport in topological systems often relies on high magnetic fields or magnetic dopants to suppress trivial transport and create an imbalance in occupancy of opposite Chern-number states4,5. Here we use the quantum geometry6,7of topological bands to filter fermions by chirality into distinct Chern-number-polarized states. This allows for the real-space separation of currents with opposite fermionic chiralities, which we have demonstrated by observing their quantum interference in the absence of any magnetic field. Devices fabricated from single-crystal PdGa in a three-arm geometry exhibit quantum-geometry-induced anomalous velocities8,9of chiral fermions, thereby exhibiting a nonlinear Hall effect. The resultant transverse chiral currents with opposite anomalous velocities are thereby spatially separated into the outer arms of the device. These chiral currents in opposing Chern number states also carry orbital magnetizations with opposite signs. The mesoscopic phase coherence of these chiral currents facilitated their quantum interference10in a Mach–Zehnder interferometer. Our findings establish a chiral fermionic valve that exhibits three key properties: spatially separates chiral fermions into Chern-number polarized states by using their quantum geometry, enables tuneable current-induced magnetization and provides a platform for controllable quantum interference of chiral quasiparticles using an electric current and magnetic field.

## 摘要（中文）
多重拓扑半金属在拓扑带交叉处具有相反手性的费米子1,2,3。拓扑系统中的手性费米子输运通常依赖于高磁场或磁性掺杂剂来抑制微不足道的输运并造成相反陈数态占据的不平衡4,5。在这里，我们使用拓扑带的量子几何6,7通过手性将费米子过滤成不同的陈数偏振态。这允许具有相反费米子手性的电流在实空间分离，我们通过在没有任何磁场的情况下观察它们的量子干涉来证明这一点。采用三臂几何结构的单晶 PdGa 制成的器件表现出量子几何诱导的手性费米子的反常速度8,9，从而表现出非线性霍尔效应。由此产生的具有相反反常速度的横向手性电流在空间上被分离到装置的外臂中。这些处于相反陈数态的手性电流也带有相反符号的轨道磁化强度。这些手性电流的介观相位相干性促进了它们在马赫-曾德尔干涉仪中的量子干涉。我们的研究结果建立了一种手性费米子阀，它具有三个关键特性：利用其量子几何结构在空间上将手性费米子分离成陈数极化态，实现可调谐的电流感应磁化，并为使用电流和磁场的手性准粒子的可控量子干涉提供平台。
