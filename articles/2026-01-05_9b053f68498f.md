## Abstract
arXiv:2509.07579v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) have shown promise in solving partial differential equations (PDEs) relevant to multiscale modeling, but they often fail when applied to materials with discontinuous coefficients, such as media with piecewise constant properties. This paper introduces a dual formulation for the PINN framework to improve the reliability of the homogenization of periodic thermo-conductive composites, for both strong and variational (weak) formulations. The dual approach facilitates the derivation of guaranteed upper and lower error bounds, enabling more robust detection of PINN failure. We compare standard PINNs applied to smoothed material approximations with variational PINNs (VPINNs) using both spectral and neural network-based test functions. Our results indicate that while strong-form PINNs may outperform VPINNs in controlled settings, they are sensitive to material discontinuities and may fail without clear diagnostics. In contrast, VPINNs accommodate piecewise constant material parameters directly but require careful selection of test functions to avoid instability. Dual formulation serves as a reliable indicator of convergence quality, and its integration into PINN frameworks enhances their applicability to homogenization problems in micromechanics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2509.07579v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：物理信息神经网络 (PINN) 在求解与多尺度建模相关的偏微分方程 (PDE) 方面已显示出前景，但在应用于具有不连续系数的材料（例如具有分段常数属性的介质）时，它们常常会失败。本文介绍了 PINN 框架的双重公式，以提高周期性导热复合材料均匀化的可靠性，无论是强公式还是变分（弱）公式。双方法有助于推导有保证的误差上限和下限，从而能够更稳健地检测 PINN 故障。我们使用基于谱和神经网络的测试函数，将应用于平滑材料近似的标准 PINN 与变分 PINN (VPINN) 进行比较。我们的结果表明，虽然强形式 PINN 在受控设置中可能优于 VPINN，但它们对材料不连续性很敏感，并且在没有明确诊断的情况下可能会失败。相比之下，VPINN 直接适应分段恒定材料参数，但需要仔细选择测试函数以避免不稳定。对偶公式可以作为收敛质量的可靠指标，并且将其集成到 PINN 框架中增强了它们对微观力学均质化问题的适用性。
