## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06791v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Recently, the topic of altermagnetism has attracted tremendous attention and RuO2 have been demonstrated to be one of the most promising altermagnetic candidates. However, disputes still remain on the existence of magnetic order in RuO2. Here in this work, we employ X-ray linear dichroism (XLD), a widely utilized technique for characterizing antiferromagnets, in conjunction with photoemission electron microscopy and multiple scattering calculation to provide clear evidence of the absence of magnetic order in epitaxial RuO2 films. The observed XLD signal is nearly invariant with temperature and independent on cooling field direction, in stark contrast to the substantial magnetic order-related XLD signal predicted by multiple scattering calculation. This finding strongly suggests a nonmagnetic origin for RuO2. Furthermore, we observed significantly distinct XLD signals at the Ru M3 and O K edges in RuO2 films grown on TiO2 substrate with different surface orientations, which can be attributed to the low-symmetry crystal field. These results unequivocally demonstrate the absence of magnetic order in RuO2 and establishes XLD measurement as a robust technique for probing the low-symmetry magnetic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06791v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近，交变磁学话题引起了极大的关注，RuO2 已被证明是最有前途的交变磁候选材料之一。然而，对于RuO2中是否存在磁序仍存在争议。在这项工作中，我们采用 X 射线线性二色性 (XLD)（一种广泛使用的表征反铁磁体的技术），结合光电电子显微镜和多重散射计算，为外延 RuO2 薄膜中不存在磁序提供了明确的证据。观察到的 XLD 信号几乎不随温度变化，并且与冷却场方向无关，这与多重散射计算预测的大量与磁序相关的 XLD 信号形成鲜明对比。这一发现强烈表明 RuO2 的非磁性起源。此外，我们在不同表面取向的 TiO2 基底上生长的 RuO2 薄膜中，在 Ru M3 和 O K 边缘观察到明显不同的 XLD 信号，这可归因于低对称性晶体场。这些结果明确证明了 RuO2 中不存在磁序，并将 XLD 测量确立为探测低对称性磁性材料的可靠技术。
