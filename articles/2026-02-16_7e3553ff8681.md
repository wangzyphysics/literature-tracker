## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12396v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-orbit coupling (SOC) leads to splitting of otherwise spin-degenerate bands in noncentrosymmetric materials, even if time-reversal symmetry is present. While this gives rise to well-known phenomena such as the Rashba and Dresselhaus effects, various other terms are allowed based on the point group of the crystal and the electronic Hamiltonian. In this study, we utilize point group representations to illustrate that four different types of SOC terms (Rashba, Dresselhaus, Weyl, and Ising) can emerge in periodic solids. We construct reciprocal space energy expressions for each type of SOC-induced splitting of opposite spin bands, and follow a similar procedure to also obtain minimal tight-binding models that capture all types of spin-splittings for subgroups of the cubic parent group $m\bar{3}m$. Furthermore, we also obtain a complete list of nodal features in the electronic band structure in these systems, distinguishing between crystallographic-symmetry-imposed nodal lines and those imposed by time-reversal-symmetry only. Finally, we conclude by presenting a list of materials that host each type of inversion-breaking SOC effects. Our classification of the spin-splitting symmetries in non-magnetic systems with SOC is the counterpart of the recent classification of spin-splitting symmetries in unconventional magnetic systems without SOC, such as altermagnets and odd-parity magnets. More broadly, our work provides a basis for studying superconductivity and other collective electronic phenomena that are impacted by SOC-induced band splittings in noncentrosymmetric materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12396v1 公告类型：新 摘要：即使存在时间反演对称性，自旋轨道耦合 (SOC) 也会导致非中心对称材料中其他自旋简并能带的分裂。虽然这会产生众所周知的现象，例如拉什巴效应和德雷塞尔豪斯效应，但基于晶体的点群和电子哈密顿量，还允许使用各种其他术语。在本研究中，我们利用点群表示来说明周期固体中可以出现四种不同类型的 SOC 项（Rashba、Dresselhaus、Weyl 和 Ising）。我们为每种类型的 SOC 引起的相反自旋带分裂构建了倒数空间能量表达式，并遵循类似的过程来获得最小紧束缚模型，该模型捕获立方母群 $m\bar{3}m$ 子群的所有类型的自旋分裂。此外，我们还获得了这些系统中电子能带结构中节点特征的完整列表，区分了晶体学对称性强加的节点线和仅由时间反转对称性强加的节点线。最后，我们列出了具有每种类型的反转破坏 SOC 效应的材料列表。我们对具有 SOC 的非磁性系统中自旋分裂对称性的分类与最近对不具有 SOC 的非常规磁性系统（例如交流磁体和奇宇称磁体）中自旋分裂对称性的分类相对应。更广泛地说，我们的工作为研究受非中心对称材料中 SOC 引起的能带分裂影响的超导性和其他集体电子现象提供了基础。
