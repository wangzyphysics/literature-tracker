## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07752v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The Finitely Extensible Nonlinear Elastic (FENE) dumbbell model is a widely used mathematical model for complex fluids. Direct simulation of the FENE Fokker--Planck equation is computationally challenging due to high dimensionality and singularity of its potential. In this paper, we develop two fast Jacobi-Spherical Harmonic spectral methods for the spatially homogeneous FENE Fokker--Planck equation. These methods effectively resolve the singularity near the boundary by combining properly designed Jacobi polynomials with a weighted variational formulation. A semi-implicit backward differentiation formula of second-order (BDF2) is employed for time marching, and its energy stability is rigorously proved. The resulting linear algebraic system possesses a sparse structure and can be efficiently solved. Numerical results verify the spectral convergence and efficiency of the direct spectral solvers, establishing them as a reliable tool for generating reference solutions for challenging benchmark problems. Furthermore, to achieve an optimal trade-off between accuracy and efficiency, we compare several closure approximation models, including the industry workhorse Peterlin approximation (FENE-P), the quasi-equilibrium approximation (FENE-QE), and a novel neural network implementation for FENE-QE proposed in this paper (FENE-QE-NN). Numerical experiments in extensional and shear flows demonstrate the superior accuracy and efficiency of the proposed methods compared to traditional approaches.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07752v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：有限可扩展非线性弹性（FENE）哑铃模型是一种广泛使用的复杂流体数学模型。 FENE Fokker-Planck 方程的直接模拟由于其高维性和奇异性而在计算上具有挑战性。在本文中，我们针对空间齐次 FENE Fokker-Planck 方程开发了两种快速雅可比球谐谱方法。这些方法通过将正确设计的雅可比多项式与加权变分公式相结合，有效地解决了边界附近的奇异性。采用二阶半隐式后向微分公式(BDF2)进行时间推进，并严格证明了其能量稳定性。由此产生的线性代数系统具有稀疏结构并且可以有效地求解。数值结果验证了直接谱求解器的谱收敛性和效率，使它们成为为具有挑战性的基准问题生成参考解决方案的可靠工具。此外，为了实现精度和效率之间的最佳权衡，我们比较了几种闭包近似模型，包括行业主力 Peterlin 近似（FENE-P）、准平衡近似（FENE-QE）以及本文提出的 FENE-QE 的新型神经网络实现（FENE-QE-NN）。拉伸流和剪切流的数值实验表明，与传统方法相比，所提出的方法具有更高的准确性和效率。
