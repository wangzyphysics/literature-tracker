## Abstract
ABSTRACT The development of chemically recyclable polymers is critical for addressing the environmental and resource limitations of the traditional linear plastic economy. Owing to its intrinsic reversibility, ring-opening polymerization (ROP) offers great potential for the development of recyclable polymers. However, achieving a balance between monomer polymerizability, polymer performance and recyclability remains a key challenge. This study explores the design and synthesis of a novel cis -fused cyclobutane-butyrolactone monomer (C4GBL) for use in ROP, leading to the formation of a polymer with 1,2-cyclobutane units in the main chain. C4GBL was synthesized using ethylene and bio-based maleic anhydride, and the resultant polymer exhibits high thermal stability ( T d , 5% = 380°C). Thermodynamic analysis of the ROP of C4GBL revealed a ceiling temperature ( T c ) of −24°C, indicating that both polymerization and chemical recycling are feasible under appropriately tuned conditions. Density functional theory optimization reveals that the rigid fused cyclobutane forces the lactone segment of C4GBL into a near-eclipsed conformation, enhancing ring strain and polymerization reactivity. Collectively, this work establishes the cis -fused ring strategy as a new design principle for recyclable polyesters.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 化学可回收聚合物的开发对于解决传统线性塑料经济的环境和资源限制至关重要。由于其固有的可逆性，开环聚合（ROP）为可回收聚合物的开发提供了巨大的潜力。然而，实现单体聚合性、聚合物性能和可回收性之间的平衡仍然是一个关键挑战。本研究探索了用于 ROP 的新型顺式稠合环丁烷-丁内酯单体 (C4GBL) 的设计和合成，从而形成主链上具有 1,2-环丁烷单元的聚合物。 C4GBL是使用乙烯和生物基马来酸酐合成的，所得聚合物表现出高热稳定性（T d , 5% = 380°C）。 C4GBL ROP的热力学分析显示最高温度（T c ）为-24°C，表明在适当调整的条件下聚合和化学回收都是可行的。密度泛函理论优化表明，刚性稠合环丁烷迫使 C4GBL 的内酯链段形成近乎重叠的构象，从而增强环应变和聚合反应活性。总的来说，这项工作将顺式稠环策略确立为可回收聚酯的新设计原则。
