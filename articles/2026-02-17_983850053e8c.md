## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13884v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Highly charged ions (HCIs) combine compact electronic structure with strong relativistic effects, offering both robustness against external perturbations and enhanced sensitivity to variations of the fine-structure constant. Recent advances in sympathetic cooling and trapping enable precision measurements of highly charged ions; however, fully exploiting their potential requires accurate theoretical predictions. In particular, reliable calculations of clock wavelengths are essential for experimentally locating HCI clock transitions. Here, we treat Cf17+ as a univalent ion and perform calculations within the relativistic coupled-cluster framework, iteratively including nonlinear single-double contributions and valence and core triple excitations. We also assess quantum-electrodynamic corrections and basis-set and partial-wave truncation effects. Our results establish the impact of different correlation contributions on the low-lying energy spectrum and provide a quantitatively reliable prediction of the 5f_5/2 - 6p_1/2 clock transition, highlighting the critical role of core-valence correlations and iterative triples for precision spectroscopy and optical clock development.

arXiv:2602.13884v1 公告类型：新 摘要：高电荷离子 (HCI) 将紧凑的电子结构与强相对论效应相结合，既具有抵抗外部扰动的鲁棒性，又增强了对精细结构常数变化的敏感性。交感冷却和捕获方面的最新进展使得能够精确测量高电荷离子；然而，充分发挥其潜力需要准确的理论预测。特别是，时钟波长的可靠计算对于实验定位 HCI 时钟跃迁至关重要。在这里，我们将 Cf17+ 视为单价离子，并在相对论耦合团簇框架内进行计算，迭代地包括非线性单双贡献以及价态和核心三重激发。我们还评估量子电动力学校正以及基组和部分波截断效应。我们的结果确定了不同相关性贡献对低位能谱的影响，并提供了 5f_5/2 - 6p_1/2 时钟跃迁的定量可靠预测，强调了核价相关性和迭代三元组对于精密光谱和光学时钟开发的关键作用。
