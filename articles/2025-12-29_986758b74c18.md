## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22017v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present a set of necessary conditions for the appearance of charge and spin Josephson diode effects across strongly spin-polarized inhomogeneous magnetic materials (FM) placed between two spin-singlet superconductors. Noncoplanarity of the FM's spin texture gives rise to quantum geometric phases, $\Delta\varphi'$, that enter the Josephson current-phase relation (CPR) similarly to the superconducting phase difference, resulting in charge and spin Josephson diode effects. Our study shows that such effects appear if the CPR possesses no phase-inversion center, achieved under the following conditions. First, noncoplanarity of the spin texture is necessary to break the spatial inversion symmetry. Second, both spin bands have to contribute to the transport, i.e., the effect is absent in half-metallic junctions. Third, different band-specific densities of states are required, and this condition is ensured by the strong spin polarization of the FM. Finally, higher harmonics in the CPR are necessary, i.e., the effect is absent in the tunneling limit. However, even in this case, the CPR must not have a phase-inversion center, which is ensured by the restriction of the quantum geometric phase to values $\Delta\varphi'\neq k\pi/2, k\in\mathbb{Z}$. We formulate a minimal phenomenological model that incorporates all these points, qualitatively illustrating our theory.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22017v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了在两个自旋单线超导体之间的强自旋极化非均匀磁性材料 (FM) 上出现电荷和自旋约瑟夫森二极管效应的一组必要条件。 FM自旋纹理的非共面性产生了量子几何相位$\Delta\varphi'$，它与超导相位差类似地进入约瑟夫森电流相位关系（CPR），从而产生电荷和自旋约瑟夫森二极管效应。我们的研究表明，如果 CPR 不具有转相中心，则在以下条件下实现，就会出现这种效应。首先，自旋纹理的非共面性对于打破空间反转对称性是必要的。其次，两个自旋带都必须对传输做出贡献，即在半金属结中不存在这种效应。第三，需要不同的能带特定态密度，而FM的强自旋极化保证了这一条件。最后，CPR 中的高次谐波是必要的，即隧道效应限制中不存在该效应。然而，即使在这种情况下，CPR 也不能具有相反转中心，这是通过将量子几何相位限制为值 $\Delta\varphi'\neq k\pi/2, k\in\mathbb{Z}$ 来确保的。我们制定了一个包含所有这些要点的最小现象学模型，定性地说明了我们的理论。
