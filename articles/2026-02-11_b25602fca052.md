## Abstract
arXiv:2602.10076v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The distinctive symmetry properties of pure altermagnets make them natural candidates for piezomagnetism. Previous work motivated by the piezomagnetic properties of altermagnets has primarily focused on the spin magnetization response to applied strain. In this paper we study orbital piezomagnetic effects--the orbital magnetization response to applied strain--in minimal lattice models of pure insulating altermagnets in two dimensions. We obtain general microscopic expressions for the orbital magnetization in the presence of strain, as well as the orbital piezomagnetic polarizability, i.e., the defining response characteristic of pure altermagnets. We apply these expressions to three specific tetragonal lattice models, two corresponding to $d$-wave altermagnets and one describing a $g$-wave altermagnet. Whereas the $d$-wave altermagnets are associated with a linear piezomagnetic polarizability, the $g$-wave altermagnet exhibits a nonlinear piezomagnetic effect. Our analysis reveals how the polarizabilities are related to and determined by the Berry curvature of the occupied bands. Connections to materials of current interest are discussed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.10076v1 公告类型：新 摘要：纯交变磁体独特的对称特性使其成为压磁性的天然候选者。以前由交流磁体的压磁特性推动的工作主要集中在对施加应变的自旋磁化响应。在本文中，我们研究了二维纯绝缘交变磁体的最小晶格模型中的轨道压磁效应（轨道磁化强度对施加应变的响应）。我们获得了存在应变时轨道磁化强度以及轨道压磁极化率的一般微观表达式，即纯交流磁体的定义响应特性。我们将这些表达式应用于三个特定的四方晶格模型，两个对应于 $d$ 波交变磁体，一个描述 $g$ 波交变磁体。 $d$ 波交变磁体与线性压磁极化率相关，而 $g$ 波交变磁体则表现出非线性压磁效应。我们的分析揭示了极化率如何与占据能带的贝里曲率相关并由其决定。讨论了与当前感兴趣的材料的联系。
