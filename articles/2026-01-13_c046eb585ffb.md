## Abstract
Ligand exchange is an elegant route to tailor semiconducting nanocrystal surfaces, yet it is constrained by ground-state thermodynamics, and thus access is limited to the most stable interfacial structures. Light-driven ligand desorption, by contrast, offers a non-equilibrium handle to dynamically reconfigure surfaces and potentially reach thermodynamically inaccessible states. Here we study photoinduced displacement of perylene bisimide (PBI) ligands from 5-ML (monolayer) CdSe nanoplatelets (NPLs). Upon NPL excitation, the displacement efficiency is enhanced by up to 7 times compared with zero dimensional nanocrystals and can be tuned by NPL lateral dimensions and PBI surface loading. Time-resolved spectroscopy reveals 100-ps electron transfer from the NPL band edge to the PBI LUMO that initiates ligand dissociation. The overall yield is ultimately limited by the probability that a PBI ligand lies within the exciton diffusion range, set by vacant [100] basal-plane binding sites. These results establish nanoplatelets as efficient light-programmable platforms for dynamic ligand chemistry beyond thermodynamic control.

## 摘要（中文）
配体交换是定制半导体纳米晶体表面的一种优雅途径，但它受到基态热力学的限制，因此仅限于最稳定的界面结构。相比之下，光驱动的配体解吸提供了一种非平衡处理，可以动态地重新配置表面，并有可能达到热力学上无法达到的状态。在这里，我们研究 5-ML（单层）CdSe 纳米片（NPL）中苝双酰亚胺（PBI）配体的光诱导位移。在 NPL 激励下，与零维纳米晶体相比，位移效率提高了 7 倍，并且可以通过 NPL 横向尺寸和 PBI 表面载荷进行调节。时间分辨光谱揭示了从 NPL 带边缘到 PBI LUMO 的 100 ps 电子转移，从而引发配体解离。总产率最终受到 PBI 配体位于激子扩散范围内的概率的限制，该范围由空的 [100] 基底平面结合位点设定。这些结果将纳米血小板确立为高效的光可编程平台，用于超越热力学控制的动态配体化学。
