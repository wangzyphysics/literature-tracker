## Abstract
arXiv:2507.03550v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We theoretically investigate the inertial dynamics of magnetic skyrmions driven by circularly polarized light (CPL) via the inverse Faraday effect (IFE). By incorporating an inertial mass term into the Thiele equation and analytically deriving the optically induced magnetic fields and forces, we demonstrate fundamentally distinct dynamical regimes under continuous-wave (CW) versus pulsed excitation. Skyrmion inertia qualitatively transforms trajectories from smooth spirals to polygonal orbits under continuous driving, while enabling sustained post-pulse gyration that reveals the system's intrinsic relaxation dynamics. The handedness of the trajectory is determined by the topological charge and light helicity: left-circularly polarized (LCP) light attracts skyrmions toward the beam center, while right-circularly polarized (RCP) light repels them. Systematic parameter analysis reveals how Gilbert damping, optical intensity, frequency, and skyrmion mass control the transition between oscillatory and overdamped dynamical phases. Our work identifies inertia, topological charge, and light helicity as essential factors in ultrafast all-optical skyrmion manipulation and provides a theoretical model for designing topological spin textures with ultrafast light.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.03550v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们从理论上研究了圆偏振光 (CPL) 通过反法拉第效应 (IFE) 驱动的磁斯格明子的惯性动力学。通过将惯性质量项纳入蒂勒方程并分析推导光感磁场和力，我们证明了连续波 (CW) 与脉冲激励下根本不同的动力学机制。斯格明子惯性在连续驱动下将轨迹从平滑螺旋定性地转变为多边形轨道，同时实现持续的脉冲后回转，从而揭示系统的内在弛豫动力学。轨迹的旋向性由拓扑电荷和光螺旋性决定：左圆偏振（LCP）光将斯格明子吸引向光束中心，而右圆偏振（RCP）光则排斥它们。系统参数分析揭示了吉尔伯特阻尼、光强度、频率和斯格明子质量如何控制振荡和过阻尼动态相位之间的过渡。我们的工作将惯性、拓扑电荷和光螺旋性确定为超快全光斯格明子操纵的基本因素，并为利用超快光设计拓扑自旋纹理提供了理论模型。
