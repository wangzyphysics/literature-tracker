## Abstract
Considering the harsh surface conditions on Mars, terrestrial organisms that can survive and remain detectable after exposure to similar conditions provide invaluable models for guiding current and future search-for-life missions. To this end, we evaluated the extremophilic archaeon Acidianus manzaensis grown on ESA01-E Mars analog material as a model organism. We exposed cell-mineral mixtures to one month of desiccation and two weeks of Mars-like conditions in a Mars simulation chamber to evaluate the potential for cell survival after exposure to these conditions. In the search for reliable biomarkers in other planetary environments, such as Mars, molecules that are stable over geological timescales while preserving information indicative of their potential biological origin are crucial. Thiophene-bearing quinones fulfill these requirements, and thiophenes, which are their basic moieties, have been discovered on Mars. Therefore, we analyzed the thiophene-bearing quinone composition of A. manzaensis using mass spectrometry-based metabolomics and discussed potential molecular alterations. Successful re-cultivation after one month of desiccation and two weeks of exposure to Mars-like conditions proved the durability of the organism and its capability for cell recovery after exposure to extreme conditions, paving the way for further investigation.

## 摘要（中文）
考虑到火星上恶劣的表面条件，在暴露于类似条件后仍能生存并保持可检测性的陆地生物为指导当前和未来的生命搜寻任务提供了宝贵的模型。为此，我们评估了在 ESA01-E 火星模拟材料上生长的嗜极古菌 Acidianus manzaensis 作为模式生物。我们将细胞-矿物质混合物暴露在火星模拟室中一个月的干燥环境和两周的类似火星的条件下，以评估暴露于这些条件后细胞存活的潜力。在火星等其他行星环境中寻找可靠的生物标志物时，在地质时间尺度上保持稳定同时保留表明其潜在生物起源的信息的分子至关重要。含噻吩的醌满足这些要求，并且在火星上发现了作为其基本部分的噻吩。因此，我们使用基于质谱的代谢组学分析了 A. manzaensis 中含有噻吩的醌组成，并讨论了潜在的分子改变。经过一个月的干燥和暴露于类似火星的条件两周后的成功重新培养证明了该生物体的耐久性及其在暴露于极端条件后细胞恢复的能力，为进一步研究铺平了道路。
