## Abstract
Author(s): Changhee Lee, Nico A. Hackner, and P. M. R. Brydon Odd-parity magnets present a novel magnetic state which may turn out to be important in spintronics applications. Here, the authors use symmetry analysis to show that at sufficiently high temperatures odd-parity magnets are generically unstable to the formation of an incommensurate magnetic state. This places severe constraints on the phase diagram of odd-parity magnets. In particular, their phase cannot be reached from the nonmagnetic state in a single phase transition. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 064420] Published Fri Feb 13, 2026
