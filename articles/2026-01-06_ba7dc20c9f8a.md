## Abstract
Peptides and peptidomimetics are promising in diverse biological applications, but intrinsic visible emission without appended fluorophores remains a great challenge due to the lack of π-conjugation in their non-aromatic backbones. Although clusteroluminescence (CL) of nonconjugated materials provides a pathway for intrinsic emission, most non-aromatic clusterolumigens (CLgens) are limited to blue emission, and the underlying factor hindering emission redshift remains unclear. Herein, we identified that intramolecular/intermolecular hydrogen H-bonds between heteroatoms (e.g., –NH2, C=O) compete with through-space conjugation (TSC) and suppress emission redshift. To address this issue, we designed and synthesized ten peptidomimetic polyurethanes (P1-L/H–P5-L) with gradient H-bond densities. Comprehensive characterizations including infrared spectroscopy, femtosecond transient absorption spectroscopy, molecular dynamics simulations, and quantum-chemical calculations confirmed that selective removal of H-bonds enhanced TSC. Notably, H-bond-deficient P4-L and P5-L achieved intrinsic NIR emission at 763 nm and 784 nm, respectively, which is among the longest emission wavelengths reported for non-aromatic CLgens. This work reveals the inhibitory role of H-bonds in TSC and provides a novel strategy for developing intrinsic NIR-emitting peptidomimetics, holding great potential for biological applications.

## 摘要（中文）
肽和肽模拟物在多种生物应用中具有广阔的前景，但由于其非芳香族主链中缺乏π-共轭，不附加荧光团的内在可见光发射仍然是一个巨大的挑战。尽管非共轭材料的簇发光（CL）提供了本征发射的途径，但大多数非芳香族簇发光（CLgens）仅限于蓝色发射，并且阻碍发射红移的潜在因素仍不清楚。在此，我们发现杂原子（例如-NH2、C=O）之间的分子内/分子间氢键与穿越空间共轭（TSC）竞争并抑制发射红移。为了解决这个问题，我们设计并合成了十种具有梯度氢键密度的拟肽聚氨酯（P1-L/H–P5-L）。包括红外光谱、飞秒瞬态吸收光谱、分子动力学模拟和量子化学计算在内的综合表征证实，选择性去除氢键可增强 TSC。值得注意的是，氢键缺陷的 P4-L 和 P5-L 分别在 763 nm 和 784 nm 处实现了本征近红外发射，这是非芳香族 CLgen 报道的最长发射波长之一。这项工作揭示了氢键在 TSC 中的抑制作用，并为开发内在的近红外发射肽模拟物提供了一种新策略，具有巨大的生物应用潜力。
