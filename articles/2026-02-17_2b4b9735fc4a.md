## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13590v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnetism arises from composite real-space and spin-space symmetries, combining zero net magnetization with pronounced momentum-dependent spin splitting. This review highlights the pivotal role of angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES), along with its spin-resolved (SARPES) and circular-dichroism (CD-ARPES) variants, in directly visualizing nonrelativistic band splitting and spin textures in altermagnets. Within the spin-group framework, we distinguish ferromagnetic, antiferromagnetic, and altermagnetic orders and elucidate the symmetry origin of spin polarization. We then systematically review representative systems: the debated d-wave prototype RuO2, layered d-wave altermagnets KV2Se2O and Rb1-delta V2Te2O, and a series of g-wave compounds including MnTe (domain-tunable) and CrSb (topological), together with the noncoplanar antiferromagnet MnTe2 and other emerging candidate platforms. Overall, ARPES has become a key probe for resolving symmetry-driven spin splitting. Future advances in micro/nano-beam and in-situ spectroscopies, combined with strain and domain engineering, heterostructure design, and exploration of broader unconventional magnetic states, are expected to drive the joint evolution of altermagnetism and photoemission spectroscopy, paving the way for spintronic and correlated quantum research.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13590v1 公告类型：新 摘要：交变磁力源自复合实空间和自旋空间对称性，将零净磁化强度与明显的动量相关自旋分裂相结合。本综述强调了角分辨光电子能谱 (ARPES) 及其自旋分辨 (SARPES) 和圆二色性 (CD-ARPES) 变体在直接可视化交变磁体中的非相对论能带分裂和自旋纹理方面的关键作用。在自旋群框架内，我们区分了铁磁、反铁磁和交磁序，并阐明了自旋极化的对称起源。然后，我们系统地回顾了代表性系统：有争议的 d 波原型 RuO2、层状 d 波交替磁体 KV2Se2O 和 Rb1-delta V2Te2O、一系列 g 波化合物，包括 MnTe（域可调）和 CrSb（拓扑），以及非共面反铁磁体 MnTe2 和其他新兴候选平台。总体而言，ARPES 已成为解决对称驱动的自旋分裂的关键探针。微/纳米束和原位光谱学的未来进展，结合应变和域工程、异质结构设计以及更广泛的非常规磁态的探索，预计将推动交变磁学和光电子能谱的共同发展，为自旋电子学和相关量子研究铺平道路。
