## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09180v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Anisotropic van der Waals materials provide a powerful platform for nanoscale optoelectronics, enabling strong light$-$matter interaction and deep electromagnetic field confinement mediated by polaritons, hybrid light$-$matter excitations with unique dispersion properties. While polaritonic phenomena in van der Waals heterostructures have been extensively explored in the mid-infrared frequency range, their behaviour at the visible frequencies remains largely unexplored, in part due to the lack of knowledge on natural materials supporting anisotropic and highly confined visible-range polaritons. In this context, MoOCl$_2$, an anisotropic van der Waals metal, is particularly interesting, since it supports hyperbolic plasmon polaritons (PPs) that enable directional propagation and subwavelength light compression. Here, we investigate the strategy for launching anisotropic PPs in MoOCl$_2$ in the visible frequency range using gold rod nanoantennas. The nanoantennas, placed on top of the MoOCl$_2$ crystal, excite in-plane anisotropic PP modes, effectively overcoming the momentum mismatch between waves in free-space and nanoscale PPs. We demonstrate a strong electromagnetic field confinement, angle-dependent absorption, and controlled anisotropic PP launching enabled by gold nanoantennas, highlighting the potential of MoOCl$_2$ as a compact platform for nanoscale waveguiding and optical signal processing. By providing a practical antenna-based strategy for exciting visible-range PPs, this work addresses the lack of compact elements for optical signal manipulation and opens new opportunities for optoelectronic devices based on van der Waals polaritonics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09180v1 公告类型：新 摘要：各向异性范德华材料为纳米级光电子学提供了强大的平台，可实现由极化子介导的强光物质相互作用和深层电磁场限制，以及具有独特色散特性的混合光物质激发。虽然范德华异质结构中的极化子现象已在中红外频率范围内得到广泛探索，但它们在可见光频率下的行为仍然很大程度上未被探索，部分原因是缺乏对支持各向异性和高度受限的可见光范围极化子的天然材料的了解。在这种情况下，MoOCl$_2$（一种各向异性范德华金属）特别有趣，因为它支持双曲等离子体激元（PP），可实现定向传播和亚波长光压缩。在这里，我们研究了使用金棒纳米天线在可见光频率范围内在 MoOCl$_2$ 中发射各向异性 PP 的策略。放置在 MoOCl$_2$ 晶体顶部的纳米天线激发面内各向异性 PP 模式，有效克服自由空间波和纳米级 PP 之间的动量不匹配。我们展示了由金纳米天线实现的强电磁场限制、角​​度相关吸收和受控各向异性 PP 发射，突出了 MoOCl$_2$ 作为纳米级波导和光信号处理的紧凑平台的潜力。通过为令人兴奋的可见光范围 PP 提供实用的基于天线的策略，这项工作解决了用于光信号操纵的紧凑元件的缺乏，并为基于范德华极化的光电器件开辟了新的机会。
