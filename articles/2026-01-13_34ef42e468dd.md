## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07753v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The non-thermal optical control of magnetic order offers a promising route to ultrafast, energy-efficient information technologies. Although optical manipulation of magnetism in metals has been extensively studied, experimentally demonstrated effects have so far been limited to heat-driven dynamics or helicity-dependent mechanisms. Here, we report ultrafast non-thermal control of spin order in noncollinear antiferromagnetic Mn-based antiperovskite nitrides Mn3NiN and Mn3GaN, driven solely by the polarization orientation of linearly polarized femtosecond laser pulses. Using time-resolved magneto-optical pump-probe experiments based on the Voigt effect, we observe sub-picosecond changes in magnetic order followed by picosecond relaxation. The magneto-optical response depends on the relative orientation of the pump and probe polarization planes, with linear-polarization dependence reaching up to 95%, a value unprecedented in metallic magnets. This phenomenon is observed in two different materials and persists over a wide range of excitation wavelengths, fluences, and temperatures, demonstrating its robustness. Symmetry analysis and microscopic modeling indicate that optically induced torques alone cannot fully explain the observed dynamics. We therefore propose laser-induced formation of transient spin-spiral states as a possible excitation mechanism.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07753v1 公告类型：新 摘要：磁序的非热光控制为超快、节能信息技术提供了一条有前途的途径。尽管金属磁性的光学操纵已被广泛研究，但迄今为止实验证明的效应仅限于热驱动动力学或螺旋度相关机制。在这里，我们报告了非共线反铁磁锰基反钙钛矿氮化物 Mn3NiN 和 Mn3GaN 中自旋顺序的超快非热控制，仅由线性偏振飞秒激光脉冲的偏振方向驱动。使用基于福伊特效应的时间分辨磁光泵浦探针实验，我们观察到磁序的亚皮秒变化，随后是皮秒弛豫。磁光响应取决于泵浦和探针偏振面的相对方向，线性偏振依赖性高达 95%，这在金属磁体中是前所未有的。这种现象在两种不同的材料中观察到，并且在很宽的激发波长、注量和温度范围内持续存在，证明了其鲁棒性。对称分析和微观建模表明，光感生扭矩本身无法完全解释观察到的动力学。因此，我们提出激光诱导瞬态自旋螺旋态的形成作为一种可能的激发机制。
