## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07683v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present implementations of two physically-embedded computation-universal logical operations using a 2-bit logical unit composed of coupled quantum flux parametrons -- Josephson-junction superconducting circuits. To illustrate universality, we investigate NAND gates built from these two distinct elementary operations. On the one hand, Controlled Erasure (CE) is designed using fixed-point analysis and assumes that information must be stored in locally-metastable distributions. On the other, Erasure-Flip (EF) leverages momentum as a computational resource and significantly outperforms the metastable approach, simultaneously achieving higher fidelity and faster computational speed without incurring any additional energetic cost. Notably, the momentum degree of freedom allows the EF to achieve universality by using both nontrivial reversible and irreversible logic simultaneously in different logical subspaces. These results not only provide a practical, high-performance protocol ripe for experimental realization but also underscore the broader potential of momentum-based computing paradigms.

arXiv:2602.07683v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用由耦合量子通量参数组成的 2 位逻辑单元（约瑟夫森结超导电路）提出了两种物理嵌入式计算通用逻辑运算的实现。为了说明通用性，我们研究了由这两种不同的基本运算构建的 NAND 门。一方面，受控擦除（CE）是使用定点分析设计的，并假设信息必须存储在局部亚稳态分布中。另一方面，Erasure-Flip (EF) 利用动量作为计算资源，显着优于亚稳态方法，同时实现更高的保真度和更快的计算速度，而不会产生任何额外的能量成本。值得注意的是，动量自由度允许 EF 通过在不同的逻辑子空间中同时使用非平凡的可逆和不可逆逻辑来实现普适性。这些结果不仅提供了一种适合实验实现的实用、高性能协议，而且还强调了基于动量的计算范式的更广泛潜力。
