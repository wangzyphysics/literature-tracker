## Abstract
arXiv:2510.16243v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Balancing plasma performance and coil cost is a significant challenge when designing a stellarator power plant. Most current stellarator designs are produced by two-stage optimization: stage-1 for the equilibrium and stage-2 for a coil design that reproduces its magnetic configuration. Because few proxies connect both stages, two-stage optimization can produce plasmas that have high-quality physical properties but overly complex coils. In recent years, single-stage optimization is increasingly used to optimize the plasma and coils simultaneously, in order to improve the plasma-coil balance. However, all existing single-stage tools are specialized for filament coils, cannot model coil systems containing permanent magnets (PM) or dipole arrays, and remain challenged by numerical problems.The quasi-single-stage (QSS) optimization finds a middle-ground by integrating a coil optimization subproblem into stage-1 optimization. We present a flexible, differentiable coil complexity proxy based on the newly developed QUADCOIL coil optimization code. QUADCOIL is fast and can target realistic coil metrics and constraints that are unavailable to codes with comparable speed. We demonstrate the effectiveness and flexibility of the QUADCOIL proxy by presenting two QSS optimization studies. The first study produces a permanent magnet solution for the MUSE stellarator with 29% fewer magnets than previous solutions. The second study produces a coil solution for the ARIES-CS stellarator with approx 30% reductions in both peak and average coil force.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.16243v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在设计仿星器发电厂时，平衡等离子体性能和线圈成本是一个重大挑战。目前大多数仿星器设计都是通过两阶段优化产生的：第一阶段用于平衡，第二阶段用于再现其磁性配置的线圈设计。由于很少有代理连接两个级，因此两级优化可以产生具有高质量物理特性但线圈过于复杂的等离子体。近年来，越来越多地采用单级优化来同时优化等离子体和线圈，以改善等离子体-线圈平衡。然而，所有现有的单级工具都是专门用于灯丝线圈的，无法对包含永磁体 (PM) 或偶极子阵列的线圈系统进行建模，并且仍然受到数值问题的挑战。准单级 (QSS) 优化通过将线圈优化子问题集成到第一阶段优化中找到了中间立场。我们提出了一种基于新开发的 QUADCOIL 线圈优化代码的灵活、可微分的线圈复杂度代理。 QUADCOIL 速度很快，可以针对具有相当速度的代码无法实现的实际线圈指标和约束。我们通过提出两项 QSS 优化研究来证明 QUADCOIL 代理的有效性和灵活性。第一项研究为 MUSE 仿星器提供了永磁体解决方案，其磁体比以前的解决方案少了 29%。第二项研究为 ARIES-CS 仿星器提供了线圈解决方案，峰值线圈力和平均线圈力均降低了约 30%。
