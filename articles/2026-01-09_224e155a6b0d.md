## Abstract
Novel triazin-5-ones as potential antiseizure medicines Anxiety and related issues are major health and social concerns in developed countries, affecting 10–20% of the population. The lowest effective inhibitory concentrations for GABA-AT were observed for two compounds: 3h (30.50±0.61 µM) and 3r (44.76±0.49 µM). Their IC50 values were similar to those of lamotrigine. In vitro tests showed that compounds 3h and 3f had the lowest IC50 values for GABA-AT inhibition, at 30.50±0.61 and 44.58±0.54, respectively. The anticonvulsant activity of 6-(4-methoxybenzyl)-3-R-4H-[1,2,4] triazin-5-ones in a corral seizure model depends on modifications to substituents at positions three and six of the heterocyclic 1,2,4-triazine ring. Leading compounds, 3f (3-(4-Chlorophenylamino)-6-(4-methoxybenzyl)-4H-[1,2,4]triazin-5-one) and 3h (3-(2,3-Dimethylphenylamino)-6-(4-methoxybenzyl)-4H-[1,2,4]triazin-5-one), completely inhibit clonic and tonic-clonic seizures. Compound 3r, which contains both tryptamine and triazine fragments, has a clear pharmacophore structure. However, the spatial structure of the pharmacophore in compound 3h, with the lowest binding energy to GABAa and GABA-AT and the best performance in tests, is not yet fully understood.

## 摘要（中文）
新型三嗪-5-酮作为潜在的抗癫痫药物焦虑和相关问题是发达国家主要的健康和社会问题，影响着 10-20% 的人口。观察到两种化合物对 GABA-AT 的最低有效抑制浓度：3h (30.50±0.61 µM) 和 3r (44.76±0.49 µM)。它们的 IC50 值与拉莫三嗪相似。体外测试表明，化合物3h和3f对GABA-AT抑制的IC50值最低，分别为30.50±0.61和44.58±0.54。 6-(4-甲氧基苄基)-3-R-4H-[1,2,4]三嗪-5-酮在畜栏癫痫模型中的抗惊厥活性取决于杂环1,2,4-三嗪环第三和第六位取代基的修饰。主要化合物3f (3-(4-氯苯基氨基)-6-(4-甲氧基苯甲基)-4H-[1,2,4]三嗪-5-一)和3h (3-(2,3-二甲基苯氨基)-6-(4-甲氧基苯甲基)-4H-[1,2,4]三嗪-5-一)完全抑制阵挛性和强直阵挛性癫痫发作。化合物3r同时含有色胺和三嗪片段，具有清晰的药效团结构。然而，化合物3h中与GABAa和GABA-AT的结合能最低且在测试中表现最好的药效基团的空间结构尚未完全清楚。
