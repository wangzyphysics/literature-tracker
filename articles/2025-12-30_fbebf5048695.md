## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22155v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This dissertation presents a systematic theoretical investigation into realizing a condensed matter analogue of the Chiral Magnetic Effect (CME) in a quasi-planar, 2+1D system. The research establishes a conceptual bridge between the anomalous transport phenomena of high-energy physics and the emergent electronic properties of engineered honeycomb lattices. The central objective is the formulation of a low-energy effective Hamiltonian that incorporates the necessary ingredients for a CME-like effect. This is achieved by moving beyond pristine graphene, whose inherent sublattice symmetry precludes the formation of a mass gap necessary for defining robust pseudo-chiral states. The core of this work is a model based on a honeycomb lattice with explicitly broken sublattice symmetry, which introduces a band gap and endows the quasi-particles system with a well-defined pseudo-chirality based on sublattice polarization. A time-reversal symmetry-breaking parameter is introduced to asymmetrically modify the valley gaps, creating a controllable non-equilibrium imbalance analogous to the chiral chemical potential in relativistic systems. A key finding is the validation of the physical model consistency; through commutator calculations, the total angular momentum - comprising both orbital and an emergent lattice spin component - is shown to be a conserved quantity. This research successfully transforms the abstract possibility of a 2D CME into a concrete, self-consistent theoretical framework, detailing the precise symmetry conditions required for its manifestation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22155v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本文对在准平面 2+1D 系统中实现手性磁效应 (CME) 的凝聚态物质模拟进行了系统的理论研究。该研究在高能物理的反常输运现象与工程蜂窝晶格的新兴电子特性之间建立了一座概念桥梁。中心目标是制定低能量有效哈密顿量，其中包含类似日冕物质抛射效应的必要成分。这是通过超越原始石墨烯来实现的，其固有的亚晶格对称性阻止了定义鲁棒伪手性态所需的质量间隙的形成。这项工作的核心是基于具有明显破缺的亚晶格对称性的蜂窝晶格的模型，该模型引入了带隙并赋予准粒子系统基于亚晶格极化的明确的伪手性。引入时间反转对称性破缺参数来不对称地修改谷间隙，产生类似于相对论系统中的手性化学势的可控非平衡不平衡。一个重要的发现是物理模型一致性的验证；通过换向器计算，总角动量（包括轨道分量和出现的晶格自旋分量）被证明是一个守恒量。这项研究成功地将二维日冕物质抛射的抽象可能性转化为具体的、自洽的理论框架，详细说明了其表现所需的精确对称条件。
