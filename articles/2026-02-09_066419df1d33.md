# Wavefront-Dislocation Evolution via Quadratic Band Touching Annihilation

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06397v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Wavefront dislocations (WDs) -- phase singularities observed in quasiparticle interference (QPI) experiments -- have been widely interpreted as the definitive real-space signatures of Berry phases in graphene-family systems. Here, we disentangle the roles of topological charge and pseudospin texture in WD experiments. By investigating various way of the annihilation of quadratic band touchings (QBTs) in bilayer graphene and magneto-spin-orbit graphene systems, we demonstrate that WD evolution is governed exclusively by changes in the underlying pseudospin winding, while remaining insensitive to the topological charge (i.e., vorticity) of the band touching itself. Our results imply that WD measures wavefunction pseudospin texture rather than a diagnostic of topological charge and provide solid-state platforms in which WD evolution can be engineered and observed.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06397v1 公告类型：新 摘要：波前位错 (WD)——在准粒子干涉 (QPI) 实验中观察到的相奇点——已被广泛解释为石墨烯族系统中贝里相的明确实空间特征。在这里，我们阐明了拓扑电荷和赝自旋结构在 WD 实验中的作用。通过研究双层石墨烯和磁自旋轨道石墨烯系统中二次能带接触（QBT）湮灭的各种方式，我们证明了WD演化完全受底层赝自旋缠绕的变化控制，同时对能带接触本身的拓扑电荷（即涡度）不敏感。我们的结果表明，WD 测量的是波函数赝自旋结构，而不是拓扑电荷的诊断，并提供了可以设计和观察 WD 演化的固态平台。
