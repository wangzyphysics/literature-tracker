# SiPM-based photodetectors for spectroscopic measurements in wide dynamic range

## 中文标题
基于 SiPM 的光电探测器，用于宽动态范围内的光谱测量

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21719v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The results of measurements of the characteristics of photodetectors based on large-area SiPM matrices with a wide dynamic range are presented. The potential application of SiPM-based photodetectors for electromagnetic calorimetry in the energy range from hundreds of keV to tens of GeV is explored. The potential application of SiPM in gamma-spectroscopy is also discussed.
