## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02317v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Single-photon emitters (SPEs) are central to quantum communication, computing, and metrology, yet their development remains constrained by trade-offs in purity, indistinguishability, and tunability. This review presents a mechanism-based classification of SPEs, offering a physics-oriented framework to clarify the performance limitations of conventional sources, including quantum emitters and nonlinear optical processes. Particular attention is given to hybrid organic-inorganic perovskite quantum dots (HOIP QDs), which provide size- and composition-tunable emission with narrow linewidths and room-temperature operation. Through comparative analysis of physical mechanisms and performance metrics, we show how HOIP QDs may address key limitations of established SPE platforms. Recognizing the constraints of current deterministic sources, we introduce a performance framework to guide the development of scalable SPEs, and examine the theoretical potential of bright squeezed vacuum (BSV) states, discussing how BSV mechanisms could serve as a promising avenue for multiplexable, high-purity photon generation beyond conventional heralded schemes. The review concludes by outlining future directions for integrating HOIP- and BSV-based concepts into scalable quantum photonic architectures.

arXiv:2601.02317v1 公告类型：新 摘要：单光子发射器 (SPE) 是量子通信、计算和计量学的核心，但其发展仍然受到纯度、不可区分性和可调谐性权衡的限制。这篇综述提出了基于机制的 SPE 分类，提供了一个面向物理的框架来阐明传统光源（包括量子发射器和非线性光学过程）的性能限制。特别关注混合有机-无机钙钛矿量子点（HOIP QD），它提供尺寸和成分可调的发射，具有窄线宽和室温操作。通过物理机制和性能指标的比较分析，我们展示了 HOIP QD 如何解决现有 SPE 平台的关键限制。认识到当前确定性源的限制，我们引入了一个性能框架来指导可扩展SPE的开发，并研究了亮压缩真空（BSV）态的理论潜力，讨论了BSV机制如何成为超越传统预示方案的可复用、高纯度光子生成的有前途的途径。该评论最后概述了将基于 HOIP 和 BSV 的概念集成到可扩展的量子光子架构中的未来方向。
