## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04200v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ising machines and related probabilistic hardware have emerged as promising platforms for NP-hard optimization and sampling. However, many practical problems involve constraints that induce dense or all-to-all couplings, undermining scalability and hardware efficiency. We address this constraint-induced density through two complementary approaches. First, we introduce a hardware-aware native formulation for multi-state probabilistic digits (p-dits) that avoids the locally dense intra-variable couplings required by binary Ising encodings. We validate p-dit dynamics by reproducing known critical behavior of the 2D Potts model. Second, we propose mean-field constraints (MFC), a hybrid scheme that replaces dense pairwise constraint couplings with dynamically updated single-node biases. Applied to balanced graph partitioning, MFC achieves solution quality comparable to exact all-to-all constraint formulations while dramatically reducing graph density. Finally, we demonstrate the practical impact of restored sparsity by an FPGA implementation, enabling orders-of-magnitude acceleration over CPU-based solvers. Together, these results outline a pathway for scaling constrained optimization on probabilistic hardware.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04200v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Ising 机和相关概率硬件已成为 NP 难优化和采样的有前途的平台。然而，许多实际问题涉及导致密集或全面耦合的约束，从而破坏可扩展性和硬件效率。我们通过两种互补的方法来解决这种约束引起的密度问题。首先，我们引入了多状态概率数字（p-dits）的硬件感知本机公式，避免了二进制伊辛编码所需的局部密集变量内耦合。我们通过重现 2D Potts 模型的已知关键行为来验证 p-dit 动力学。其次，我们提出平均场约束（MFC），这是一种混合方案，用动态更新的单节点偏差代替密集的成对约束耦合。应用于平衡图分区时，MFC 实现了与精确的所有约束公式相当的解决方案质量，同时显着降低了图密度。最后，我们展示了通过 FPGA 实现恢复稀疏性的实际影响，从而能够比基于 CPU 的求解器实现数量级的加速。总之，这些结果概述了在概率硬件上扩展约束优化的途径。
