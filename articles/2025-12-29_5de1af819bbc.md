## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21609v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Graphene-based moire superlattices exhibit novel quantum phenomena driven by pronounced interactions, leading to topological corrected states like orbital Chern insulators exhibiting quantum anomalous Hall effect (QAHE). Typically, intrinsic Chern insulators are stabilized at odd moir\'e fillings, as even fillings often result in valley-balanced, topologically trivial states at zero magnetic field. In our work, we report the observation of an intrinsic Chern insulator with C = 1 state at moire filling v = 2 in rhombohedral octalayer graphene (R8G)/hBN moire superlattice. Observing such Chern insulators in particular with C = 1 at v = 2 is intriguing, as each moir\'e band carries Chern number C = 1 or -1. We further demonstrate such a state can originate from the entanglement between the low-energy moire flat bands and high-energy remote bands according to the Hartree-Fock calculation. Our findings extend the known topological phase diagram of rhombohedral multilayer graphene (RMG) moire systems and establish this platform as highly promising for investigating strong electron correlations and multiband hybridized transport.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21609v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基于石墨烯的莫尔超晶格表现出由明显相互作用驱动的新颖量子现象，导致拓扑校正状态，例如表现出量子反常霍尔效应 (QAHE) 的轨道陈绝缘体。通常，本征陈绝缘体在奇数摩尔纹填充下稳定，因为偶数填充通常会在零磁场下产生谷平衡、拓扑平凡状态。在我们的工作中，我们报告了在菱形八层石墨烯 (R8G)/hBN 莫尔超晶格中莫尔填充 v = 2 时观察到 C = 1 状态的本征陈绝缘体。观察这样的陈绝缘体，尤其是在 v = 2 时 C = 1 的情况下，是很有趣的，因为每个莫尔带都带有陈数 C = 1 或 -1。根据 Hartree-Fock 计算，我们进一步证明这种状态可能源于低能量莫尔平带和高能量远程带之间的纠缠。我们的研究结果扩展了已知的菱形多层石墨烯（RMG）莫尔系统的拓扑相图，并建立了该平台对于研究强电子相关性和多带杂化传输非常有前景。
