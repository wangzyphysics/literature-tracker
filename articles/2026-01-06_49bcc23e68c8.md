## Abstract
arXiv:2512.08027v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Thea Energy, Inc. has developed the preconceptual design for "Helios," a fusion power plant based on the planar coil stellarator architecture. In this overview paper, the design is summarized and the reader is referred to the other papers for more detail. The Helios design is based around a two-field-period quasi-axisymmetric ("QA") stellarator equilibrium with aspect ratio 4.5 and a novel tokamak-like X-point divertor. The natural stability, low recirculating power, and steady-state capability of the stellarator are leveraged. Stability and transport are calculated using state-of-the-art, high-fidelity codes and grounded in measured performance of existing experiments. The electromagnetic coil set is high-temperature superconducting ("HTS") and consists of 12 large, plasma-encircling coils like the toroidal field coils of a tokamak, and 324 smaller, field-shaping coils. All coils are planar and convex. A maximum of 20 T on-coil is enforced, a value which has been achieved in existing large-bore HTS coils. There is a minimum of 1.2 m between plasma and coils, leaving space for tritium breeding blanket and neutron shielding. Because of this thick shielding, all coils have a minimum 40-year operational lifetime, the same minimum lifetime of the power plant system. 1.1 GW of thermal power and 390 MW of net electric power are produced. The shaping coils are individually controllable, enabling a uniquely configurable magnetic field for relaxed manufacturing and assembly tolerances and plasma control. A practical maintenance architecture is a primary driver of the design; maintenance is performed on entire toroidal sectors that are removed from between the encircling coils. A biennial maintenance cycle is estimated to take approximately 84 days, resulting in an 88% capacity factor. Rigorous engineering constraints such as temperature and stress limits are enforced.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.08027v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：Thea Energy, Inc. 开发了“Helios”的预概念设计，这是一个基于平面线圈仿星器架构的聚变发电厂。在这篇概述论文中，总结了设计，读者可以参考其他论文以了解更多详细信息。 Helios 的设计基于纵横比为 4.5 的两场周期准轴对称 (“QA”) 仿星器平衡和新型托卡马克式 X 点偏滤器。利用了仿星器的自然稳定性、低循环功率和稳态能力。稳定性和传输是使用最先进的高保真代码计算的，并基于现有实验的测量性能。电磁线圈组是高温超导（“HTS”），由 12 个大型等离子体环绕线圈（如托卡马克的环形场线圈）和 324 个较小的场成形线圈组成。所有线圈都是平面和凸面的。线圈上的最大负载为 20 T，该值已在现有大口径高温超导线圈中实现。等离子体和线圈之间至少有 1.2 m 的距离，为氚增殖层和中子屏蔽留出空间。由于这种厚屏蔽层，所有线圈的使用寿命至少为 40 年，与发电厂系统的最短使用寿命相同。生产火力1.1吉瓦，净发电量390兆瓦。整形线圈可单独控制，从而实现独特的可配置磁场，从而放宽制造和装配公差以及等离子体控制。实用的维护架构是设计的主要驱动力；对从环绕线圈之间移除的整个环形扇区进行维护。每两年一次的维护周期预计大约需要 84 天，容量系数为 88%。执行严格的工程约束，例如温度和应力限制。
