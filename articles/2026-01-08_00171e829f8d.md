## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03344v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: The origin of non-thermal power-law distribution functions ubiquitously observed in astrophysical/space (e.g., the solar wind) and laboratory kinetic plasmas, is not well understood. Another puzzling phenomenon is temperature inversion in the solar corona. These two issues are deeply connected. We develop a self-consistent quasilinear theory (QLT) for electromagnetically driven kinetic plasmas, deriving a Fokker-Planck equation for the simultaneous relaxation of multiple species, with (i) a drive diffusion coefficient for the heating of dressed particles directly by the drive and indirectly by waves, and (ii) Balescu-Lenard diffusion and drag coefficients for internal turbulence and Coulomb collisions. Both electron and ion distributions relax towards a universal attractor with a $v^{-5}$ $(E^{-2})$ tail, akin to a $\kappa = 1.5$ distribution, under a super-Debye (but sub-Larmor) drive with a steep power-spectrum. This is an outcome of Debye screening: large-scale fields accelerate the unscreened, fast particles but not the screened, slow ones. The universality may be broken by shallow power-spectra and incomplete relaxation. Collisions cannot decelerate suprathermal particles, rendering a high $v$ tail immune to Maxwellianization. Such a tail may be generated in the solar corona by chromospheric convection despite collisional losses. The suprathermal particles escape sun's gravity (velocity filtration), inverting the temperature profile and raising it to $10^6$ K. A proper analysis of velocity filtration with a $\kappa \approx 1.5-2$ distribution inspired by QLT provides a reasonable fit to the spectroscopic data of heavy ions and explains the abrupt temperature rise, a consequence of the divergence of pressure in the $\kappa \to 1.5$ limit.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03344v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在天体物理/空间（例如太阳风）和实验室动力等离子体中普遍观察到的非热幂律分布函数的起源尚不清楚。另一个令人费解的现象是日冕的逆温现象。这两个问题是紧密相连的。我们开发了电磁驱动动力等离子体的自洽拟线性理论（QLT），推导了多个物质同时弛豫的福克-普朗克方程，其中（i）直接通过驱动和间接通过波加热修饰颗粒的驱动扩散系数，以及（ii）内部湍流和库仑碰撞的Balescu-Lenard扩散和阻力系数。在功率谱陡峭的超德拜（但亚拉莫尔）驱动下，电子和离子分布都松弛向具有 $v^{-5}$ $(E^{-2})$ 尾部的通用吸引子，类似于 $\kappa = 1.5$ 分布。这是德拜筛选的结果：大范围的场加速未筛选的快粒子，但不会加速筛选的慢粒子。浅功率谱和不完全弛豫可能会破坏普遍性。碰撞无法使超热粒子减速，从而使高 $v$ 尾部不受麦克斯韦化的影响。尽管存在碰撞损失，但这样的尾部可能在日冕中通过色球对流产生。超热粒子逃离太阳重力（速度过滤），反转温度分布并将其提高到 $10^6$ K。受 QLT 启发，对 $\kappa \ 约 1.5-2$ 分布的速度过滤进行正确分析，为重离子的光谱数据提供了合理的拟合，并解释了温度突然升高，这是 $\kappa \ 到 1.5$ 限制中压力发散的结果。
