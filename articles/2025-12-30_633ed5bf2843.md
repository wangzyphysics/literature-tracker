## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22528v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Frustrated Kondo lattices are ideal platforms for studying how both the Kondo effect and quantum fluctuations compete with the magnetic exchange interactions that drive magnetic ordering. Here, we investigate the effect of tuning the heavy-fermion compound CePdIn, which crystallizes in the geometrically frustrated ZrNiAl-type structure, using applied magnetic fields and hydrostatic pressure. At ambient pressure, CePdIn exhibits two magnetic transitions, one at $T_{\rm{N}} \approx 1.65$ K and another at $T_{\rm{M}} \approx 1.15$ K, which are both suppressed by applied $c$-axis fields. Upon applying pressure in zero magnetic field, there is a non-monotonic evolution of $T_{\rm{N}}$, which decreases to 0.8 K at 2.3 GPa, before abruptly increasing to 1.5 K at 2.6 GPa. At higher pressures, $T_{\rm{N}}$ has a weak pressure dependence, and vanishes near 5 GPa. Together with the high-pressure phase being more robust to applied fields, these results suggest two distinct antiferromagnetic phases in CePdIn, which are separated near 2.6 GPa, and this change may be driven by the evolution of the underlying electronic structure due to enhanced Kondo hybridization under pressure.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22528v1 公告类型：新 摘要：受挫近藤晶格是研究近藤效应和量子涨落如何与驱动磁有序的磁交换相互作用竞争的理想平台。在这里，我们研究了使用外加磁场和静水压力调节重费米子化合物 CePdIn 的效果，该化合物在几何受挫 ZrNiAl 型结构中结晶。在环境压力下，CePdIn 表现出两个磁转变，一个在 $T_{\rm{N}} \approx 1.65$ K 处，另一个在 $T_{\rm{M}} \approx 1.15$ K 处，这两种磁转变都被施加的 $c$ 轴场抑制。在零磁场中施加压力时，$T_{\rm{N}}$ 会出现非单调演化，在 2.3 GPa 时降至 0.8 K，然后在 2.6 GPa 时突然增加至 1.5 K。在较高压力下，$T_{\rm{N}}$ 具有较弱的压力依赖性，并在 5 GPa 附近消失。再加上高压相对应用场更加鲁棒，这些结果表明 CePdIn 中存在两个不同的反铁磁相，它们在 2.6 GPa 附近分离，并且这种变化可能是由压力下近藤杂化增强导致的底层电子结构的演化驱动的。
