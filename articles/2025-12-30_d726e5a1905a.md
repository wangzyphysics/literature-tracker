## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22779v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The development of sophisticated integrated photonic circuits demands microresonators that combine exceptional optical confinement with dynamic operational flexibility. Here, we demonstrate a racetrack resonator on the thin-film lithium niobate platform that achieves an electro-optically tunable coupling strength while maintaining a stable, high intrinsic Q factor on the order of 10^6. By incorporating a Mach-Zehnder interferometer into the coupling region, the device facilitates a continuous and reversible transition across the entire coupling spectrum from under-coupling and critical coupling to deep over-coupling. To ensure high spectral purity, we employ Euler bends to facilitate an adiabatic transition between the straight and curved waveguide sections. This design effectively suppresses the excitation of higher-order modes, resulting in a clean transmission spectrum characterized by exclusive fundamental mode operation. At the critical coupling point, the resonator exhibits a high extinction ratio exceeding 30 dB. The integration of stable ultra-high Q, single-mode purity, and full-range coupling reconfigurability positions this device as a vital component for adaptive microwave photonics, high-efficiency nonlinear optics, and programmable quantum photonic networks.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22779v1 公告类型：新 摘要：复杂集成光子电路的发展需要微谐振器将卓越的光学限制与动态操作灵活性相结合。在这里，我们展示了薄膜铌酸锂平台上的跑道谐振器，该谐振器实现了电光可调耦合强度，同时保持稳定、高内在 Q 因子（大约 10^6）。通过将马赫-曾德干涉仪合并到耦合区域，该装置有助于在整个耦合谱上从欠耦合和临界耦合到深度过耦合的连续且可逆的过渡。为了确保高光谱纯度，我们采用欧拉弯曲来促进直波导部分和弯曲波导部分之间的绝热过渡。这种设计有效地抑制了高阶模式的激励，从而产生了以独特的基模操作为特征的干净的传输光谱。在临界耦合点，谐振器表现出超过 30 dB 的高消光比。稳定的超高 Q、单模纯度和全范围耦合可重构性的集成使该器件成为自适应微波光子学、高效非线性光学和可编程量子光子网络的重要组件。
