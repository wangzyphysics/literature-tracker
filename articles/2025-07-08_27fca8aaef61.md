## Abstract
Author(s): Alexander P. Petrović, Christina Psaroudaki, Peter Fischer, Markus Garst, and Christos Panagopoulos Skyrmions are topological field configurations that were first discussed in the context of high-energy theory. In recent years, skyrmionic spin patterns in solid-state systems have received much attention, in part for their promising application potential. This Colloquium discusses quantum-mechanical aspects of such magnetic skyrmions, both for the interactions that underlie skyrmion formation and for quantum features of the skyrmions themselves. [Rev. Mod. Phys. 97, 031001] Published Tue Jul 08, 2025
