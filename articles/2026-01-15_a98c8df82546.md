## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09439v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In optoacoustic imaging, recovering the absorption coefficients of tissue by inverting the light transport remains a challenging problem. Improvements in solving this problem can greatly benefit the clinical value of optoacoustic imaging. Existing variational inversion methods require an accurate and differentiable model of this light transport. As neural surrogate models allow fast and differentiable simulations of complex physical processes, they are considered promising candidates to be used in solving such inverse problems. However, there are in general no guarantees that the derivatives of these surrogate models accurately match those of the underlying physical operator. As accurate derivatives are central to solving inverse problems, errors in the model derivative can considerably hinder high fidelity reconstructions. To overcome this limitation, we present a surrogate model for light transport in tissue that uses Sobolev training to improve the accuracy of the model derivatives. Additionally, the form of Sobolev training we used is suitable for high-dimensional models in general. Our results demonstrate that Sobolev training for a light transport surrogate model not only improves derivative accuracy but also reduces generalization error for in-distribution and out-of-distribution samples. These improvements promise to considerably enhance the utility of the surrogate model in downstream tasks, especially in solving inverse problems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09439v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在光声成像中，通过反转光传输来恢复组织的吸收系数仍然是一个具有挑战性的问题。解决这一问题的改进可以极大地提高光声成像的临床价值。现有的变分反演方法需要这种光传输的精确且可微分的模型。由于神经代理模型允许对复杂的物理过程进行快速且可微分的模拟，因此它们被认为是解决此类逆问题的有希望的候选者。然而，通常不能保证这些代理模型的导数与底层物理算子的导数准确匹配。由于准确的导数是解决反演问题的核心，因此模型导数中的错误会极大地阻碍高保真度重建。为了克服这一限制，我们提出了一种组织中光传输的替代模型，该模型使用 Sobolev 训练来提高模型导数的准确性。此外，我们使用的 Sobolev 训练形式一般适用于高维模型。我们的结果表明，针对光传输代理模型的 Sobolev 训练不仅提高了导数准确性，而且还减少了分布内和分布外样本的泛化误差。这些改进有望显着增强代理模型在下游任务中的实用性，特别是在解决逆问题时。
