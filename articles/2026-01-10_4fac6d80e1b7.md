## Abstract
Electrospray ionization (ESI)-based native mass spectrometry (MS) has been extensively used to study non-covalent protein complexes and their structural aspects in the gas phase. As such it has a stable place in the portfolio of modern structural biology and biopharmaceutical analysis techniques. It is also promising as an approach to deliver pro-teins and their assemblies into the gas phase for single particle experiments at advanced light sources such as free electron lasers and synchrotrons. Here we report on a novel radiofrequency ion transfer interface enabling gas flow laminarization at low pressure for efficient capture, focusing, thermalization, trapping and gating of ions into a mass analyser, boosting up to 19-fold the observable signal intensities especially for notoriously challenging multi-megadalton assemblies such as in-tact viral capsids. The new ion transfer interface offers unique opportunities for interdisciplinary approaches intersecting native MS-guided structural biology, molecular and cluster physics as well as (bio)analytical applications. Additionally, when combined with sensitive detection of individual particles, this technology could eventually also open new avenues in clinical diagnostics, enabling for example the analysis of viruses at very low concentrations directly from dilute patient-derived samples as well as viral therapeutic vectors along the biopharmaceutical production chain, thereby helping to un-lock the full potential of native MS.

## 摘要（中文）
基于电喷雾电离 (ESI) 的天然质谱 (MS) 已广泛用于研究气相中的非共价蛋白质复合物及其结构方面。因此，它在现代结构生物学和生物制药分析技术组合中占有稳定的地位。它也有望作为一种将蛋白质及其组装体输送到气相中的方法，以便在自由电子激光器和同步加速器等先进光源下进行单粒子实验。在这里，我们报告了一种新型射频离子传输接口，能够在低压下实现气流层化，从而有效捕获、聚焦、热化、捕获离子并将其门控到质量分析仪中，将可观察信号强度提高高达 19 倍，特别是对于众所周知的具有挑战性的多兆道尔顿组件，例如完整的病毒衣壳。新的离子转移接口为跨学科方法提供了独特的机会，这些方法涉及本地 MS 引导的结构生物学、分子和簇物理学以及（生物）分析应用。此外，当与单个颗粒的灵敏检测相结合时，该技术最终还可以在临床诊断中开辟新途径，例如能够直接从稀释的患者来源样本以及生物制药生产链上的病毒治疗载体中分析极低浓度的病毒，从而有助于释放天然 MS 的全部潜力。
