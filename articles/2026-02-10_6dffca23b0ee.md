## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07162v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Three-dimensional (3D) multi-slab imaging is a promising approach for high-resolution in vivo diffusion MRI (dMRI) due to its compatibility with short TR (1-2 s), providing optimal signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) efficiency. A major challenge, however, is slab boundary artifacts arising from non-ideal slab-selective RF excitation. Non-rectangular slab profiles reduce signal intensity at slab boundaries, while profile overlap across adjacent slabs introduces inter-slab crosstalk, where repeated excitation shortens the local TR and limits T1 recovery. To mitigate slab boundary artifacts without increasing scan time, we build on slab profile encoding and propose Slab-shifting for Harmonized 3D Acquisition and Reconstruction with Profile Encoding Networks (SHARPEN). For different diffusion directions, SHARPEN applies inter-volume field-of-view shifts along the slice direction to provide complementary slab profile encoding without prolonging acquisition. Slab profiles are estimated using a lightweight self-supervised neural network that exploits consistency across shifted acquisitions and known physical properties of slab profiles and diffusion images, and corrected images are reconstructed accordingly. SHARPEN was validated using simulated and prospectively acquired high-resolution in vivo data and demonstrates accurate slab profile estimation and robust boundary artifact correction, even in the presence of inter-volume motion. SHARPEN does not require high-quality reference training data and supports subject-specific training. Its efficient GPU-based implementation delivers faster and more accurate correction than NPEN, yielding slice-wise quantitative profiles that closely match those from reference 2D acquisitions. SHARPEN enables high-quality dMRI at 0.7 mm isotropic resolution on a 3T clinical scanner, highlighting its potential to advance submillimeter dMRI for neuroscience research.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07162v1 公告类型：新 摘要：三维 (3D) 多板成像是高分辨率体内扩散 MRI (dMRI) 的一种有前景的方法，因为它与短 TR（1-2 s）兼容，可提供最佳的信噪比（SNR）效率。然而，一个主要挑战是非理想板层选择性射频激励产生的板层边界伪影。非矩形板轮廓会降低​​板边界处的信号强度，而相邻板之间的轮廓重叠会引入板间串扰，其中重复激励会缩短局部 TR 并限制 T1 恢复。为了在不增加扫描时间的情况下减轻板层边界伪影，我们以板层轮廓编码为基础，并提出使用轮廓编码网络 (SHARPEN) 进行协调 3D 采集和重建的板层移位。对于不同的扩散方向，SHARPEN 沿切片方向应用体积间视场偏移，以提供互补的平板轮廓编码，而无需延长采集时间。使用轻量级自监督神经网络估计板坯剖面，该网络利用板坯剖面和扩散图像的移位采集和已知物理特性的一致性，并相应地重建校正图像。使用模拟和前瞻性获取的高分辨率体内数据对 SHARPEN 进行了验证，即使在存在体积间运动的情况下，也能证明准确的平板轮廓估计和强大的边界伪影校正。 SHARPEN 不需要高质量的参考训练数据，支持特定主题的训练。其高效的基于 GPU 的实施可提供比 NPEN 更快、更准确的校正，产生与参考 2D 采集的结果非常匹配的切片定量配置文件。 SHARPEN 可在 3T 临床扫描仪上实现 0.7 毫米各向同性分辨率的高质量 dMRI，突显其在神经科学研究中推进亚毫米 dMRI 的潜力。
