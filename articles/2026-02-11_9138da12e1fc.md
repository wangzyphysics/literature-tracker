## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09683v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the formation, stability, and dynamics of 3D ring-shaped and multipole vortical quantum droplets (QDs) in non-rotating dipolar Bose-Einstein condensates held in a toroidal trapping potential. The QD dynamics are investigated in the framework of the extended Gross-Pitaevskii equation, which includes long-range dipole-dipole interactions (DDI) and the beyond-mean-field Lee-Huang-Yang (LHY) term, revealing the emergence of self-bound states. Stable stationary solutions for multipole QDs with different values of the topological charge (vorticity $S$) are shaped as necklace-like modes, with the number of \textquotedblleft beads" (multipole's order) $n=2S$, up to $S=6$. The stability area of the multipoles shrinks with the increase of $S$. For higher values of $S$ the centrifugal effect associated with the phase winding destabilizes the annular density and drives the formation of fragmented multipole droplet states. The dependence of the chemical potential, total energy and peak density on the norm (number of particles) and $S$ is produced. These findings uncover the stabilizing effect of the LHY correction and DDI anisotropy in maintaining complex QD states in the non-rotating configurations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09683v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了处于环形捕获势的非旋转偶极玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体中 3D 环形和多极涡旋量子液滴 (QD) 的形成、稳定性和动力学。在扩展的 Gross-Pitaevskii 方程的框架中研究了 QD 动力学，该方程包括长程偶极-偶极相互作用 (DDI) 和超平均场 Lee-Huang-Yang (LHY) 项，揭示了自束缚态的出现。具有不同拓扑电荷值（涡度 $S$）的多极量子点的稳定稳态解被成形为项链状模式，其数量为 \textquotedblleft 珠子"（多极的阶数）$n=2S$，最高为 $S=6$。多极的稳定区域随着 $S$ 的增加而缩小。对于较高的 $S$ 值，与相绕组相关的离心效应会使环形密度不稳定并驱动地层产生了化学势、总能量和峰值密度对范数（粒子数）和 $S$ 的依赖性。这些发现揭示了 LHY 校正和 DDI 各向异性在非旋转配置中维持复杂 QD 状态的稳定作用。
