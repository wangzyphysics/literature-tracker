## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07128v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Half-metals, featuring ideal 100\% spin polarization, are widely regarded as key materials for spintronic and quantum technologies; however, the half-metallic state is intrinsically fragile, as it relies on a delicate balance of exchange splitting and band filling and is therefore highly susceptible to disorder, external perturbations, and thermal effects. Here we introduce the concept of hidden half-metallicity, whereby the global electronic structure of a symmetry-enforced net-zero-magnetization magnet is non-half-metallic, while each of its two symmetry-related sectors is individually half-metallic, enabling robust 100\% spin polarization through a layer degree of freedom. Crucially, the vanishing net magnetization of the entire system suppresses stray fields and magnetic instabilities, rendering the half-metallic functionality inherently more robust than in conventional ferromagnetic half-metals. Using first-principles calculations, we demonstrate this mechanism in a $PT$-symmetric bilayer $\mathrm{CrS_2}$, and further show that an external electric field drives the system into a seemingly forbidden fully compensated ferrimagnetic metal in which hidden half-metallicity persists. Finally, we briefly confirm the realization of hidden half-metallicity in altermagnets, establishing a general paradigm for stabilizing half-metallic behavior by embedding it in symmetry-protected hidden sectors and opening a new route toward the design and discovery of unprecedented half-metallic phases.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07128v1 发布类型：新 摘要：半金属具有理想的 100% 自旋极化，被广泛认为是自旋电子和量子技术的关键材料；然而，半金属态本质上是脆弱的，因为它依赖于交换分裂和能带填充的微妙平衡，因此非常容易受到无序、外部扰动和热效应的影响。在这里，我们引入了隐藏半金属性的概念，即对称强制净零磁化磁体的整体电子结构是非半金属的，而其两个对称相关扇区中的每一个都是单独的半金属性，从而通过层自由度实现稳健的 100% 自旋极化。至关重要的是，整个系统消失的净磁化抑制了杂散场和磁不稳定性，使半金属功能本质上比传统的铁磁半金属更强大。使用第一性原理计算，我们在 $PT$ 对称双层 $\mathrm{CrS_2}$ 中演示了这种机制，并进一步表明外部电场驱动系统进入看似禁止的完全补偿亚铁磁金属，其中隐藏的半金属性仍然存在。最后，我们简要确认了交替磁体中隐藏半金属性的实现，通过将其嵌入对称保护的隐藏扇区中来建立稳定半金属行为的通用范式，并为设计和发现前所未有的半金属相开辟了新途径。
