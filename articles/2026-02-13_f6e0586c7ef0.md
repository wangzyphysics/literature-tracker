## Abstract
arXiv:2508.17655v5 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Nonlinear dynamical systems with continuous variables can be used for solving combinatorial optimization problems with discrete variables. Numerical simulations of them are also useful as heuristic algorithms with a desirable property, namely, parallelizability, which allows us to execute them in a massively parallel manner, leading to ultrafast performance. However, the dynamical-system approaches with continuous variables are usually less accurate than conventional approaches with discrete variables such as simulated annealing. To improve the solution accuracy of a quantum-inspired algorithm called simulated bifurcation (SB), which was found from classical simulation of a quantum nonlinear oscillator network exhibiting quantum bifurcation, here we generalize it by introducing nonlinear control of individual bifurcation parameters and show that the generalized SB (GSB) can achieve surprisingly high performance, namely, almost 100% success probabilities for some large-scale problems. As a result, the time to solution for a 2,000-variable problem is shortened to 10 ms by a GSB-based machine, which is two orders of magnitude shorter than the best known value, 1.3 s, previously obtained by an SB-based machine. To examine the reason for the ultrahigh performance, we investigated chaos in the GSB changing the nonlinear-control strength and found that the dramatic increase of success probabilities happens near the edge of chaos. That is, the GSB can find a solution with high probability by harnessing the edge of chaos. This finding suggests that dynamical-system approaches to combinatorial optimization will be enhanced by harnessing the edge of chaos, opening a broad possibility for physics-inspired approaches to combinatorial optimization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2508.17655v5 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：具有连续变量的非线性动力系统可用于解决具有离散变量的组合优化问题。它们的数值模拟也可用作具有理想属性的启发式算法，即并行性，这使我们能够以大规模并行方式执行它们，从而实现超快的性能。然而，使用连续变量的动力系统方法通常不如使用离散变量的传统方法（例如模拟退火）准确。为了提高被称为模拟分岔（SB）的量子启发算法的求解精度，该算法是从表现出量子分岔的量子非线性振荡器网络的经典模拟中发现的，这里我们通过引入单个分岔参数的非线性控制来对其进行推广，并表明广义SB（GSB）可以实现令人惊讶的高性能，即对于一些大规模问题几乎100％的成功概率。因此，基于 GSB 的机器解决 2,000 个变量问题的时间缩短至 10 毫秒，比之前基于 SB 的机器获得的最佳值 1.3 秒缩短了两个数量级。为了检验超高性能的原因，我们研究了 GSB 中改变非线性控制强度的混沌，发现成功概率的急剧增加发生在混沌边缘附近。也就是说，GSB可以利用混沌的边缘以高概率找到解决方案。这一发现表明，通过利用混沌边缘，组合优化的动力系统方法将得到增强，为物理启发的组合优化方法提供了广阔的可能性。
