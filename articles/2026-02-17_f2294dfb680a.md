## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13698v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In topological bands, it is impossible to construct exponentially localized Wannier functions while preserving the symmetries. Instead, in quantum Hall systems, one can define an overcomplete basis of spatially localized coherent states. In this work, we propose a unified framework for understanding the quantum Hall effect and Chern insulators from the perspective of localized bases, by extending the overcomplete basis of coherent states to Chern bands in terms of coherent-like states. Specifically, by representing both coherent states and coherent-like states as wave packets defined on a band, the difference between them can be encoded solely in the functional form of the wave packet in momentum space. Furthermore, for filling factor $\nu=1/3$, we define a local repulsive interaction Hamiltonian based on these bases and discuss properties of its ground states. In particular, by relating this Hamiltonian to previously studied models, we show that in quantum Hall systems it possesses exactly zero-energy ground states with topological degeneracy, thereby confirming that it serves as a model for fractional quantum Hall systems. In addition, we numerically verify that the Hamiltonian possesses topological degeneracy for representative Chern insulator models. An advantage of this formulation is that it allows fractional quantum Hall systems and various fractional Chern insulator systems to be discussed within a unified framework using the same Hamiltonian form. In addition, we discuss that coherent-like states can also be defined in $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topological insulators. Corresponding to the fermionic time-reversal symmetry of the system, Kramers-degenerate coherent-like states can be naturally defined. The localized basis constructed from coherent-like states is expected to be useful for describing strongly correlated topological phases in flat-band systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13698v1 发布类型：new 摘要：在拓扑带中，不可能在保持对称性的同时构造指数局域 Wannier 函数。相反，在量子霍尔系统中，人们可以定义空间局域相干态的超完备基础。在这项工作中，我们提出了一个从局域基的角度理解量子霍尔效应和陈绝缘体的统一框架，通过将相干态的超完备基础扩展到类相干态的陈能带。具体来说，通过将相干态和类相干态表示为在带上定义的波包，它们之间的差异可以仅以动量空间中波包的函数形式进行编码。此外，对于填充因子$\nu=1/3$，我们基于这些基础定义了局部排斥相互作用哈密顿量，并讨论了其基态的性质。特别是，通过将该哈密顿量与之前研究的模型联系起来，我们表明在量子霍尔系统中它完全具有拓扑简并的零能量基态，从而证实它可以作为分数量子霍尔系统的模型。此外，我们还通过数值验证了哈密顿量对于代表性的陈绝缘体模型具有拓扑简并性。该公式的优点是它允许使用相同的哈密顿形式在统一框架内讨论分数量子霍尔系统和各种分数陈绝缘体系统。此外，我们讨论类相干态也可以在$\mathbb{Z}_2$拓扑绝缘体中定义。对应于系统的费米子时间反转对称性，可以自然地定义克莱默简并类相干态。由类相干态构建的局域基础预计可用于描述平带系统中的强相关拓扑相位。
