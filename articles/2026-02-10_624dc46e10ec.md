## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07750v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate a diabatization procedure that localizes charges (in real space) and localizes spins (in spin space) for open-shell systems that exhibit charge transfer in the presence of spin-orbit coupling. The procedure is applied to a two-state crossing between pairs of Kramers-restricted doublet states (which can also be considered effectively a four-state crossing). To generate the relevant electronic states, we employ the recently developed electron/hole-transfer Dynamically-weighted State-Averaged Constrained CASSCF (eDSC/hDSC) method that treats systems with an odd number of electrons. To generate the relevant diabatic states, we employ a two-step optimization over complex-unitary rotations that sequentially maximizes dipole and spin moments through iterative Jacobi sweeps; the resulting update rules are effectively equivalent to those of approximate joint diagonalization (AJD) applied to charge and spin. The method converges rapidly and yields smooth diabatic potential energy surfaces that preserve dipole and spin properties (e.g., a slowly varying pseudospin texture) along the reaction coordinate while maintaining time-reversal symmetry.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07750v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了一种对开壳系统的电荷（在实空间中）局域化和自旋（在自旋空间中）局域化的程序，该系统在自旋轨道耦合的情况下表现出电荷转移。该过程适用于克莱默限制双态态对之间的二态交叉（也可以有效地视为四态交叉）。为了生成相关的电子态，我们采用了最近开发的电子/空穴转移动态加权状态平均约束 CASSCF (eDSC/hDSC) 方法，该方法可处理具有奇数电子的系统。为了生成相关的非绝热态，我们对复酉旋转采用两步优化，通过迭代雅可比扫描依次最大化偶极子和自旋矩；由此产生的更新规则实际上等同于应用于电荷和自旋的近似联合对角化（AJD）的规则。该方法快速收敛并产生平滑的非绝热势能表面，沿反应坐标保留偶极子和自旋特性（例如，缓慢变化的赝自旋纹理），同时保持时间反转对称性。
