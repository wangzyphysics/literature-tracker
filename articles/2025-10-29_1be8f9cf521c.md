## Abstract
ABSTRACT Photocatalysis, as a sustainable, green and low-cost technology that converts solar energy into chemical energy, holds great potential to address the issues of environmental pollution and energy shortages. However, the rapid charge recombination in semiconductors limits their potential for achieving high photocatalytic activity. Heterojunctions, including inorganic-inorganic, organic-inorganic, and organic-organic heterojunctions, have proven to be an effective strategy for inhibiting charge recombination. By virtue of the high crystallinity, tunable structure and accessible pore systems of covalent organic frameworks (COFs), COF-based heterojunction photocatalysts have shown appealing prospects for promoting solar-to-energy conversion by improving charge separation, enhancing light-harvesting ability and facilitating photoredox reactions. In this review, the state-of-the-art progress in COF-based heterojunction photocatalysts including their mechanism, classification, synthetic strategies and applications are summarized. In particular, the synergistic effect and charge transfer mechanism as well as the structure–activity relationships of COF-based heterojunctions are thoroughly reviewed. Finally, the challenges and outlooks of COF-based heterojunction photocatalysts are also discussed. It is believed that this review can stimulate inspiration for the future development of efficient COF-based heterojunction photocatalysts.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 光催化作为一种​​可持续、绿色、低成本的将太阳能转化为化学能的技术，在解决环境污染和能源短缺问题方面具有巨大潜力。然而，半导体中的快速电荷复合限制了它们实现高光催化活性的潜力。异质结，包括无机-无机、有机-无机和有机-有机异质结，已被证明是抑制电荷复合的有效策略。凭借共价有机框架（COF）的高结晶度、可调节结构和易接近的孔隙系统，基于COF的异质结光催化剂在通过改善电荷分离、增强光捕获能力和促进光氧化还原反应来促进太阳能到能源转换方面显示出诱人的前景。本文综述了COF基异质结光催化剂的最新进展，包括其机理、分类、合成策略和应用。特别是，对COF基异质结的协同效应和电荷转移机制以及构效关系进行了深入的综述。最后，还讨论了基于COF的异质结光催化剂的挑战和前景。相信这篇综述可以为未来开发高效的基于COF的异质结光催化剂激发灵感。
