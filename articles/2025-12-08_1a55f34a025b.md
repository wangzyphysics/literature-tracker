## Abstract
Author(s): Lei Geng, Aaram J. Kim, and Philipp Werner Strong-coupling expansions for impurity models up to third order are implemented here on the real-time axis using a quantics tensor cross interpolation framework. This approach provides an accurate impurity solver that can describe both equilibrium systems and nonequilibrium steady states in the intermediate to strong-interaction regime. [Phys. Rev. B 112, 245119] Published Mon Dec 08, 2025
