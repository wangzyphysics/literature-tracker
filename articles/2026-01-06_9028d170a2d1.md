## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01728v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The mechanical properties of oocytes are regarded as important indicators of their developmental potential. During fertilization, deviations from the normal mechanical range can hinder sperm penetration, ultimately reducing fertilization efficiency and compromising embryo quality. However, current methods for measuring oocyte mechanics often suffer from serious cellular damage, low automation levels, and large measurement errors. To address these limitations, we developed an AI-guided micronewton-scale mechanical measurement system for safe and automated oocyte quality assessment. The system integrates voice interaction with automated experimental workflows to control a magnetically actuated microgripper, which applies defined loading forces to induce micron-scale compressive deformation of the oocyte. Combined with AI-assisted object detection and image segmentation algorithms, the system captures cellular deformation in real time, enabling precise calculation of the oocyte's compressive modulus. This measurement system enables automated, quantitative, and non-destructive evaluation of oocyte mechanical properties, providing an effective approach for oocyte quality screening in in vitro fertilization (IVF) and other assisted reproductive technologies (ART).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01728v1 公告类型：新 摘要：卵母细胞的机械特性被视为其发育潜力的重要指标。在受精过程中，偏离正常机械范围会阻碍精子穿透，最终降低受精效率并损害胚胎质量。然而，目前测量卵母细胞力学的方法往往存在细胞损伤严重、自动化水平低、测量误差大等问题。为了解决这些限制，我们开发了一种人工智能引导的微牛顿级机械测量系统，用于安全、自动化的卵母细胞质量评估。该系统将语音交互与自动化实验工作流程相结合，以控制磁驱动微夹具，该微夹具应用定义的加载力来诱导卵母细胞发生微米级压缩变形。结合人工智能辅助的目标检测和图像分割算法，系统实时捕获细胞变形，从而能够精确计算卵母细胞的压缩模量。该测量系统能够对卵母细胞机械特性进行自动化、定量和无损评估，为体外受精（IVF）和其他辅助生殖技术（ART）中卵母细胞质量筛查提供有效方法。
