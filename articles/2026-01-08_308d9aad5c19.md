## Abstract
arXiv:2504.05166v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Spin-orbit coupling (SOC) in noncentrosymmetric materials is the source of incommensurate magnetic structures. In semiconductors, it drives the Rashba spin splitting and spin momentum locking, while in magnetic insulators based on transition metals, it induces anisotropic spin exchange interactions, like the Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya (DM) interaction which drives chiral magnetism and skyrmion formation. Here, we establish a direct connection between SOC and spin exchange interactions by deriving an effective spin model from the SOC Hubbard model at half-filling. Using a strong-coupling expansion up to fourth order by including the full set of terms, we identify Heisenberg, Ising-like, and ring exchange interactions, as well as a variety of four-body terms for realistic Hubbard parameters, which impose strong constraints on the relative strengths of the spin interactions. Our spin model shows excellent agreement in energy with the SOC Hubbard model down $U/t \sim 5$ near the metal-insulator transition point, providing insight to which kind of magnetic interactions relevant across this regime are responsible for the emergence of complex magnetic textures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.05166v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：非中心对称材料中的自旋轨道耦合（SOC）是不通约磁结构的根源。在半导体中，它驱动 Rashba 自旋分裂和自旋动量锁定，而在基于过渡金属的磁绝缘体中，它引起各向异性自旋交换相互作用，例如驱动手性磁性和斯格明子形成的 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya (DM) 相互作用。在这里，我们通过从半填充时的 SOC Hubbard 模型推导出有效的自旋模型，在 SOC 和自旋交换相互作用之间建立了直接联系。通过包含全套项，使用强耦合展开至四阶，我们识别了海森堡、类伊辛和环交换相互作用，以及实际哈伯德参数的各种四体项，这些项对自旋相互作用的相对强度施加了强烈的约束。我们的自旋模型显示出与 SOC Hubbard 模型在金属-绝缘体转变点附近 $U/t \sim 5$ 的能量高度一致，从而深入了解与该状态相关的哪种磁相互作用导致了复杂磁性纹理的出现。
