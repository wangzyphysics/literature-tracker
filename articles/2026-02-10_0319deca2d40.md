## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07352v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We present the observation of three semi-forbidden transitions in singly-ionized ytterbium from the metastable $^{2}\mathrm{F}^o_{7/2}$ state. Owing to the long lifetimes of both the upper and lower states involved, these transitions are narrow and complement those already frequently used in this atom for quantum information and searches for new physics. We report the absolute frequencies of these electric quadrupole transitions, their isotope shifts, hyperfine structure, and measurements of the quadrupole transition moments. We find that the spontaneous lifetimes of these excited states are limited by slow magnetic dipole emission to lower-lying, odd-parity states, the lowest of which is accessible by laser excitation from ${}^2\mathrm{F}_{7/2}^o$ and is an attractive candidate for hosting a long-lived qubit.

arXiv:2602.07352v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了从亚稳态 $^{2}\mathrm{F}^o_{7/2}$ 状态观察到的单电离镱中的三个半禁止跃迁。由于所涉及的上态和下态的寿命都很长，这些跃迁很窄，并且补充了该原子中已经经常用于量子信息和寻找新物理的跃迁。我们报告了这些电四极跃迁的绝对频率、它们的同位素位移、超精细结构以及四极跃迁矩的测量。我们发现这些激发态的自发寿命受到慢磁偶极子发射到较低位置的奇宇称态的限制，其中最低的状态可以通过 ${}^2\mathrm{F}_{7/2}^o$ 的激光激发来达到，并且是托管长寿命量子位的有吸引力的候选者。
