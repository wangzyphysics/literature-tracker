## Abstract
One new Fe(III) complex functionalised with an electropolymerisable unit was prepared and characterised. The complex (1) was obtained as a Cl- salt, with dichloromethane as solvate. The magnetic properties of the compound were determined using SQUID magnetometry, combined with temperature-dependent Raman. This analysis revealed that in its powdered form compound 1 displays an incomplete and very gradual spin crossover, predominantly remaining in the low spin state over the temperature range measured. The compound was tested for its efficacy in the fabrication of conducting polymers via electropolymerisation. Our findings indicate that compound 1 is efficient in the fabrication of spin crossover coatings. Additionally, the surface roughness of the polymers was found to vary with the addition of a surfactant, S-camphorsulfonic acid (CSA), during the electropolymerisation process. Interestingly, the polymers exhibit magnetic behaviours distinct from those of the powder forms, and the presence of CSA neither affects nor alters their magnetic properties. The polymers formed, both with and without CSA, demonstrate very gradual and complete spin crossover behaviour.

## 摘要（中文）
制备并表征了一种用电聚合单元功能化的新型 Fe(III) 络合物。得到络合物(1)，其为Cl-盐，其中二氯甲烷为溶剂化物。使用 SQUID 磁力测定法结合温度依赖性拉曼测定该化合物的磁性。该分析表明，粉末形式的化合物 1 显示出不完全且非常渐进的自旋交叉，在测量的温度范围内主要保持低自旋状态。测试了该化合物在通过电聚合制造导电聚合物中的功效。我们的研究结果表明，化合物 1 在旋转交叉涂层的制造中是有效的。此外，发现聚合物的表面粗糙度随着电聚合过程中表面活性剂S-樟脑磺酸（CSA）的添加而变化。有趣的是，聚合物表现出与粉末形式不同的磁性行为，并且 CSA 的存在既不影响也不改变它们的磁性。所形成的聚合物，无论有或没有CSA，都表现出非常渐进和完全的自旋交叉行为。
