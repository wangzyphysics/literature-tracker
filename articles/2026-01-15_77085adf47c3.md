## Abstract
arXiv:2510.04843v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: We present a first-principles machine-learning computational framework to investigate anharmonic effects in polarization-orientation (PO) Raman spectra of molecular crystals, focusing on anthracene and naphthalene. By combining machine learning models for interatomic potentials and polarizability tensors, we enable efficient, large-scale simulations that capture temperature-dependent vibrational dynamics beyond the harmonic approximation. Our approach reproduces key qualitative features observed experimentally. We show, systematically, what are the fingerprints of anharmonic lattice dynamics, thermal expansion, and Raman tensor symmetries on PO-Raman intensities. However, we find that the simulated polarization dependence of Raman intensities shows only subtle deviations from quasi-harmonic predictions, failing to capture the pronounced temperature-dependent changes that have been reported experimentally in anthracene. We propose that part of these inconsistencies stem from the impossibility to deconvolute certain vibrational peaks when only experimental data is available. This work therefore provides a foundation to improve the interpretation of PO-Raman experiments in complex molecular crystals with the aid of theoretical simulations.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.04843v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：我们提出了一个第一原理机器学习计算框架，用于研究分子晶体偏振取向 (PO) 拉曼光谱中的非谐效应，重点关注蒽和萘。通过结合原子间势和极化张量的机器学习模型，我们能够实现高效的大规模模拟，捕获超出谐波近似的温度依赖性振动动力学。我们的方法再现了实验观察到的关键定性特征。我们系统地展示了 PO-拉曼强度上的非调和晶格动力学、热膨胀和拉曼张量对称性的指纹。然而，我们发现拉曼强度的模拟偏振依赖性仅显示与准谐波预测的细微偏差，未能捕获蒽实验报告的明显的温度依赖性变化。我们认为，这些不一致的部分原因在于，当只有实验数据可用时，无法对某些振动峰进行解卷积。因此，这项工作为借助理论模拟改进复杂分子晶体中 PO 拉曼实验的解释奠定了基础。
