## Abstract
Combination of altermagnetism and ferroic orders, such as ferroelectric switchable altermagnetism [Phys. Rev. Lett.134, 106801 (2025) andPhys. Rev. Lett.134, 106802 (2025)], offers a powerful route to achieve nonvolatile switching of altermagnetic spin splitting. In this work, by synergizing altermagnetism and ferroelasticity, we propose the concept offerroelastic altermagnetsin which the ferroelastic crystal reorientation can drive multistate nonvolatile switching of the altermagnetic spin splitting viaaltermagnetoelastic effect. Using monolayers RuF4and CuF2as material candidates, we demonstrate 2-state and 3-state altermagnetic spin splitting switching as driven by ferroelastic strain states. Transport calculation shows that multistate spin conductivities can be ferroelastically encoded in ferroelastic altermagnets, thus suggesting the potential of ferroelastic altermagnets as nonvolatile nanomechanical spin switches. The proposed concept of ferroelastic altermagnetism enriches the emerging landscape of multiferroic altermagnetism and shall pave a way towards straintronic-spintronic device applications.

## 摘要（中文）
交变磁性和铁序的组合，例如铁电可切换交变磁性[Phys。 Rev. Lett.134, 106801 (2025) 和 Phys。 Rev. Lett.134, 106802 (2025)] 提供了一种实现互磁自旋分裂的非易失性切换的强大途径。在这项工作中，通过协同交变磁性和铁弹性，我们提出了铁弹性交变磁体的概念，其中铁弹性晶体重新取向可以通过交变磁弹性效应驱动交变磁自旋分裂的多态非易失性切换。使用单层 RuF4 和 CuF2 作为候选材料，我们演示了由铁弹性应变状态驱动的二态和三态互磁自旋分裂切换。输运计算表明，多态自旋电导率可以在铁弹性交变磁体中进行铁弹性编码，从而表明铁弹性交变磁体作为非易失性纳米机械自旋开关的潜力。所提出的铁弹性交变磁性概念丰富了多铁性交变磁性的新兴领域，并为应变电子自旋电子器件的应用铺平了道路。
