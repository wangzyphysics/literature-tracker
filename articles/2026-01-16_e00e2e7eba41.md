## Abstract
arXiv:2601.10151v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the polar Kerr and Faraday effects in two-dimensional multiband chiral superconductors. We show that the clapping modes--the relative phase and amplitude oscillations between two chiral components of the superconducting order parameter--lie well within the quasiparticle excitation gap in multiband systems and dominate these magneto-optical responses in the microwave regime. The Kerr and Faraday rotation angles exhibit the resonant enhancement with sign reversals in the microwave regime as a function of the light frequency, reaching peak values on the order of 100 nrad--10 $\mu$rad in thin films of candidate chiral superconductors. These resonances are accessible in superconducting atomic layer materials and provide a generic probe of chiral superconductivity in two-dimensional systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.10151v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了二维多带手性超导体中的极克尔效应和法拉第效应。我们表明，拍手模式（超导有序参数的两个手征分量之间的相对相位和振幅振荡）很好地位于多带系统中的准粒子激发间隙内，并主导了微波区域中的这些磁光响应。克尔和法拉第旋转角作为光频率的函数，在微波范围内表现出符号反转的共振增强，在候选手性超导体的薄膜中达到 100 nrad--10 $\mu$rad 数量级的峰值。这些共振可以在超导原子层材料中实现，并提供二维系统中手性超导性的通用探针。
