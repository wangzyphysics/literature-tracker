## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04245v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The piezomagnetic effect-strain-induced linear modulation of magnetization, arises in magnets with broken time-reversal symmetry (BTRS), offering a pathway to bidirectional strain-based control of magnetism, which is an essential straintronic and spintronic functionality in solids. Metallic antiferromagnets with BTRS provide an ideal platform to study this effect through transport measurements, yet experimental demonstrations are limited. Van der Waals (vdW) nanomagnets, with their mechanical flexibility, are particularly promising for realizing large piezomagnetic responses and effective transport control. Here we demonstrate piezomagnetic control of electronic transport in nano-devices of the vdW antiferromagnets CoNb$_3$S$_6$ and CoTa$_3$S$_6$, archetypal vdW metals with BTRS that exhibit a spontaneous Hall effect. Applying uniaxial strain linearly modulates both the antiferromagnetic transition temperature and coercive field, consistent with strain-driven tuning of exchange coupling, key signatures of the piezomagnetic effect. Moreover, spontaneous Hall effect is controllable via strain, evidencing piezomagnetic tuning of Berry curvature and its associated geometric transport. These findings establish piezomagnetism as a powerful route to manipulate antiferromagnetic transport, opening avenues for straintronic and spintronic applications in vdW magnetic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04245v1 公告类型：新 摘要：时间反转对称性破缺 (BTRS) 的磁体中出现压磁效应-应变引起的磁化线性调制，为基于应变的双向磁性控制提供了途径，这是固体中必不可少的应变电子和自旋电子功能。具有 BTRS 的金属反铁磁体提供了一个通过输运测量研究这种效应的理想平台，但实验演示有限。范德华（vdW）纳米磁体具有机械灵活性，特别有希望实现大压磁响应和有效的输运控制。在这里，我们演示了 vdW 反铁磁体 CoNb$_3$S$_6$ 和 CoTa$_3$S$_6$ 纳米器件中电子传输的压磁控制，这是典型的 vdW 金属，具有 BTRS，表现出自发霍尔效应。应用单轴应变线性调制反铁磁转变温度和矫顽场，与交换耦合的应变驱动调谐一致，这是压磁效应的关键特征。此外，自发霍尔效应可以通过应变进行控制，这证明了贝里曲率及其相关几何输运的压磁调谐。这些发现将压磁学确立为操纵反铁磁输运的强大途径，为应变电子学和自旋电子学在 vdW 磁系统中的应用开辟了途径。
