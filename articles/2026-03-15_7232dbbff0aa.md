## Abstract
Nature Communications, Published online: 15 March 2026; doi:10.1038/s41467-026-70793-6 The thermoelectric performance of rhombohedral GeTe-based material is boosted by the TiB2 nanoparticle-induced interfacial constraint effect that results in reduced equivalent deformation potential and substantial interfacial thermal resistance.

《自然通讯》，在线发布：2026 年 3 月 15 日； doi:10.1038/s41467-026-70793-6 TiB2 纳米颗粒诱导的界面约束效应提高了菱面体 GeTe 基材料的热电性能，从而降低了等效变形势并显着提高了界面热阻。
