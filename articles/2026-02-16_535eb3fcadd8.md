## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12990v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Identifying whether a many-body ground state is gapped or gapless is a fundamental yet challenging problem, especially in quantum spin liquids. In this work, we develop a gap-diagnostic scheme based on the polarization amplitude defined via a twist operator, evaluated within the infinite density-matrix renormalization group (iDMRG) framework. As a benchmark, analysis of the spin-$1/2$ XXZ chain demonstrates that the polarization amplitude clearly distinguishes the gapless Tomonaga-Luttinger liquid from the gapped N\'eel phase. We then extend this framework to infinite cylinders of the spin-$1/2$ XY-$J_\chi$ model on the square lattice. We find that the polarization amplitude sharply detects the transition between the gapless XY phase and the gapped chiral spin liquid phase. These results show that polarization amplitudes provide a strong energy-gap diagnostic in two-dimensional frustrated quantum magnets, including quantum spin liquids.

arXiv:2602.12990v1 公告类型：新 摘要：识别多体基态是有隙还是无隙是一个基本但具有挑战性的问题，特别是在量子自旋液体中。在这项工作中，我们开发了一种基于通过扭曲算子定义的偏振幅度的间隙诊断方案，并在无限密度矩阵重整化群（iDMRG）框架内进行评估。作为基准，对自旋 $1/2$ XXZ 链的分析表明，极化幅度可以清楚地区分无间隙 Tomonaga-Luttinger 液体和有间隙 N'eel 相。然后，我们将这个框架扩展到方晶格上自旋 $1/2$ XY-$J_\chi$ 模型的无限圆柱体。我们发现偏振振幅可以敏锐地检测到无间隙 XY 相和有间隙手性自旋液相之间的转变。这些结果表明，极化幅度在二维受挫量子磁体（包括量子自旋液体）中提供了强大的能隙诊断。
