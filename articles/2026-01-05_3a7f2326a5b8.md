## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00085v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Ruthenates, materials with a single magnetic Ruthenium (Ru) atom, often display an exotic array of ground states ranging from superconductivity to altermagnetism. In this work, we investigated the magnetic ground state of a least explored member of the 4d3 double perovskite ruthenate series A2ScRuO6 (A = Ca, Sr, Ba): Ca2ScRuO6. Interestingly, temperature-dependent bulk susceptibility curve shows ferrimagnetic-like behaviour above the magnetic ordering at around 40 K, which were corroborated by the identification of the mixed valence states, Ru5+ and Ru4+ via X-ray absorption spectroscopy. Structural analysis further revealed atomic-site exchange between the Ru and Sc sites, which results in the Ru mixed valence states. Neutron powder diffraction measurements detected the presence of magnetic Bragg peaks at a low temperature near 4 K and a moderate magnetoelastic coupling near the ordering temperature of 40 K. However, the corresponding symmetry analysis shows a weak Type I antiferromagnetic ground state with a reduced magnetic moment of 1.1{\mu}B/Ru atom. Our findings establish an unusual magnetic ground state in the Mott insulating Ca2ScRuO6, where a long range ordered antiferromagnet coexists with small magnetic clusters, which manifests a ferrimagnetic-like high temperature inverse magnetic susceptibility. This system presents a unique platform to study long-range magnetic order in the presence of antisite disorder.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00085v1 公告类型：新 摘要：钌酸盐是具有单个磁性钌 (Ru) 原子的材料，通常表现出一系列奇异的基态，范围从超导性到交变磁性。在这项工作中，我们研究了 4d3 双钙钛矿钌酸盐系列 A2ScRuO6（A = Ca、Sr、Ba）中最少探索成员的磁性基态：Ca2ScRuO6。有趣的是，与温度相关的整体磁化率曲线在 40 K 左右显示出高于磁序的类似亚铁磁性的行为，这通过 X 射线吸收光谱对混合价态 Ru5+ 和 Ru4+ 的识别得到了证实。结构分析进一步揭示了 Ru 和 Sc 位点之间的原子位点交换，从而产生了 Ru 混合价态。中子粉末衍射测量检测到在 4 K 附近的低温下存在磁布拉格峰，在 40 K 有序温度附近存在中等磁弹性耦合。然而，相应的对称性分析显示弱 I 型反铁磁基态，磁矩降低为 1.1{\mu}B/Ru 原子。我们的研究结果在莫特绝缘 Ca2ScRuO6 中建立了一种不寻常的磁基态，其中长程有序反铁磁体与小磁团共存，表现出类似亚铁磁的高温逆磁化率。该系统提供了一个独特的平台来研究存在反位无序情况下的远程磁序。
