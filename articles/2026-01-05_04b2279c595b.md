## Abstract
Enterotoxins cause severe diseases worldwide, exacerbated by limited access to clean water, food, and medical care. Cholera is a diarrheal disease driven by cholera enterotoxin (CTX) and is secreted by Vibrio cholerae in the small intestinal (SI) epithelium. CTX consists of an enzymatic A subunit (CTA) and a pentameric B subunit (CTB), which mediates cellular uptake by binding to glycans. While CTB primarily recognizes GM1 glycosphingolipid or galactose ligands, GM1 is undetectable in the human SI, and fucose ligands are a key alternative binding target. Previously, we developed linear norbornenyl glycopolymers as biomimetic inhibitors that block CTB binding through interchain crosslinking, forming CTB-glycopolymer aggregates. To assess the polymers’ potential efficacy under flow conditions, we evaluated CTB-glycopolymer binding kinetics under dynamic conditions using surface plasmon resonance (SPR). Copolymer with a random display of galactose and fucose (pGal50Fuc50) showed nanomolar avidity with stable complex formation driven primarily by a slow dissociation rate in the multivalent phase (kd1). In contrast, an equimolar mixture of homopolymers (pGal100 + pFuc100) exhibited similar binding avidity and comparable inhibitory efficacy but did not display slow-dissociation. Moreover, random copolymer pGal25Fuc75 dissociated slowly even from low-CTB-density surfaces. These results highlight the superior stability of copolymers in forming CTB–copolymer aggregates under low CTB abundance and flow conditions. These findings underscore the importance of co-displaying galactose and fucose for complex formation and for developing clinically-useful therapies.

## 摘要（中文）
肠毒素在全世界范围内引起严重疾病，而清洁水、食物和医疗服务的获取机会有限则加剧了这种疾病。霍乱是一种由霍乱肠毒素（CTX）驱动的腹泻疾病，由小肠（SI）上皮中的霍乱弧菌分泌。 CTX 由酶促 A 亚基 (CTA) 和五聚体 B 亚基 (CTB) 组成，通过与聚糖结合介导细胞摄取。虽然 CTB 主要识别 GM1 鞘糖脂或半乳糖配体，但 GM1 在人类 SI 中检测不到，而岩藻糖配体是关键的替代结合靶标。此前，我们开发了线性降冰片烯基糖聚合物作为仿生抑制剂，通过链间交联阻断 CTB 结合，形成 CTB-糖聚合物聚集体。为了评估聚合物在流动条件下的潜在功效，我们使用表面等离子共振 (SPR) 评估了动态条件下的 CTB-糖聚合物结合动力学。随机显示半乳糖和岩藻糖的共聚物 (pGal50Fuc50) 显示出纳摩尔亲和力，并具有稳定的复合物形成，这主要是由多价相 (kd1) 中的缓慢解离速率驱动的。相比之下，等摩尔均聚物混合物（pGal100 + pFuc100）表现出相似的结合亲合力和相当的抑制功效，但没有表现出缓慢解离。此外，无规共聚物 pGal25Fuc75 即使在低 CTB 密度表面也能缓慢解离。这些结果凸显了在低 CTB 丰度和流动条件下，共聚物在形成 CTB-共聚物聚集体时具有优异的稳定性。这些发现强调了共同展示半乳糖和岩藻糖对于复杂形成和开发临床有用疗法的重要性。
