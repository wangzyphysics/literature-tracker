## Abstract
ABSTRACT Sodium-ion batteries (SIBs) show significant promise for their abundance and potential fast kinetics. However, ether-based electrolytes are limited by low anodic stability, and carbonate electrolytes typically suffer from reduced conductivity and sluggish desolvation at lower temperatures. Here, a weak-solvation dilution strategy that utilizes methyl difluoroacetate (MDFA) as a weakly solvated diluting agent to prepare an optimized carbonate/ester hybrid electrolyte is reported. MDFA reduces the bulk viscosity and takes part in the solvation structures to promote bulk ionic transportation and desolvation. Meanwhile, the reduced interaction between sodium and the solvent system limits salt dissolution to improve the interphase durability. The optimized electrolyte has an energy density of 60 Wh kg −1 at −70°C with a full cell using Na 4 Fe 3 (PO 4 ) 2 P 2 O 7 (NFPP) and hard carbon (HC), which exceeds the current limitation of carbonate electrolytes. It also enables Na(Ni 1/3 Fe 1/3 Mn 1/3 )O 2 ||HC (NNFMO||HC) pouch cells to maintain 83% of room temperature capacity at −30°C and function with an appliance at −50°C. This strategy provides the possibility of operating high-power SIBs under low temperatures, which could also be extended to other batteries.

## 摘要（中文）
摘要 钠离子电池（SIB）因其丰度和潜在的快速动力学而显示出巨大的前景。然而，醚基电解质受到低阳极稳定性的限制，而碳酸酯电解质通常会在较低温度下出现电导率降低和去溶剂化缓慢的问题。本文报道了一种弱溶剂化稀释策略，利用二氟乙酸甲酯（MDFA）作为弱溶剂化稀释剂来制备优化的碳酸酯/酯混合电解质。 MDFA 降低整体粘度并参与溶剂化结构以促进整体离子传输和去溶剂化。同时，钠与溶剂系统之间相互作用的减少限制了盐的溶解，从而提高了界面耐久性。优化后的电解质在-70°C下使用Na 4 Fe 3 (PO 4 ) 2 P 2 O 7 (NFPP)和硬碳(HC)的全电池具有60 Wh kg -1 的能量密度，这超出了碳酸盐电解质的当前限制。它还使 Na(Ni 1/3 Fe 1/3 Mn 1/3 )O 2 ||HC (NNFMO||HC) 软包电池在 -30°C 下保持 83% 的室温容量，并在 -50°C 下与电器一起工作。这种策略提供了在低温下运行高功率SIB的可能性，这也可以扩展到其他电池。
