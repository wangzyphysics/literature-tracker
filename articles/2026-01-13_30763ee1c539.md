## Abstract
Sustainable and efficient synthetic strategies for bioactive bis and tris(indolyl) alkane (BIM and TIM) derivatives remain a central challenge because of their acid sensitivity. Herein, we demonstrate an eco-friendly synthesis of 3,3′-bisindolylalkane esters from α-keto carboxylic acids and indoles employing potassium hydrogen sulfate as a green promoter, with methanol acting as both reagent and solvent, in excellent yields. Beyond that, the methodology was further extended to the esterification of α-keto carboxylic acids and N-protected L-amino acid derivatives. Synthetic applications of the developed methodology were demonstrated in the concise total synthesis of Streptindole and Arsindoline B, a bioactive natural product. Complementary computational investigations, including SwissADME analyses, DFT, MEP, molecular docking, and MD simulations, demonstrated promising pharmacokinetic profiles, favourable electronic properties, and strong EGFR binding affinities, highlighting the potential of selected novel compounds as effective anticancer agents. Guided by these in silico predictions, experimental anticancer evaluation using in vitro MTT assays identified compound C26 as the most potent and selective derivative against A549 and T24 cancer cell lines, with reduced toxicity toward normal MCF-10a cells, underscoring its potential as a promising anticancer lead.

## 摘要（中文）
由于其酸敏感性，生物活性双和三（吲哚基）烷烃（BIM 和 TIM）衍生物的可持续和高效合成策略仍然是一个主要挑战。在此，我们展示了使用硫酸氢钾作为绿色促进剂，甲醇作为试剂和溶剂，以 α-酮羧酸和吲哚为原料，以环保方式合成 3,3'-双吲哚基烷酯，收率优异。除此之外，该方法还进一步扩展到 α-酮基羧酸和 N-保护的 L-氨基酸衍生物的酯化。所开发方法的合成应用在 Streptindole 和 Arsindoline B（一种生物活性天然产物）的简明全合成中得到了证明。补充计算研究，包括 SwissADME 分析、DFT、MEP、分子对接和 MD 模拟，证明了有前景的药代动力学特征、有利的电子特性和强大的 EGFR 结合亲和力，凸显了所选新型化合物作为有效抗癌药物的潜力。在这些计算机预测的指导下，使用体外 MTT 测定进行的实验抗癌评估将化合物 C26 确定为针对 A549 和 T24 癌细胞系的最有效和最具选择性的衍生物，并且对正常 MCF-10a 细胞的毒性降低，强调了其作为有前途的抗癌先导药物的潜力。
