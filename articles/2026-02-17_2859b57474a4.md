# Nematostriction in frustrated two-dimensional Heisenberg models

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14964v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the nematic phase transition in the Heisenberg $J_1$-$J_2$-model on square and triangular lattices, accounting for finite lattice compressibility and bond-length-dependent magnetic exchange. Using Nematic Bond Theory, a diagrammatic self-consistent method, we study the nematostriction that happens when the onset of nematic order in the spin-system drives a concomitant structural phase transition. We analyze the mechanisms by which the magnetoelastic couplings renormalize the critical temperature and modify the phonon spectrum. The magnetoelastic feeback can also alter fundamentally the nature of the phase transition. Specifically, on the square lattice, the transition shifts from continuous to weakly first-order (discontinuous) beyond a critical magnetoelastic coupling threshold. Conversely, on the triangular lattice, the transition remains discontinuous regardless of coupling strength.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14964v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究了方形和三角形晶格上海森堡 $J_1$-$J_2$ 模型中的向列相变，解释了有限晶格压缩性和键长相关的磁交换。使用向列键理论（一种图解自洽方法），我们研究了当自旋系统中向列序的开始驱动伴随的结构相变时发生的向列伸缩。我们分析了磁弹性耦合重正化临界温度和修改声子谱的机制。磁致弹性反馈还可以从根本上改变相变的性质。具体来说，在方形晶格上，过渡从连续转变为弱一阶（不连续），超过临界磁弹性耦合阈值。相反，在三角晶格上，无论耦合强度如何，过渡都保持不连续。
