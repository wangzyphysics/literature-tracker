## Abstract
Nature Nanotechnology, Published online: 26 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41565-025-02068-y Exciton propagation in CrSBr is strongly influenced by magnetic properties, particularly peaking at the Néel temperature. Its transport is not governed by classical diffusion but rather by an interaction with the spin degree of freedom, specifically through a magnon–exciton drag effect.

《自然纳米技术》，在线发布：2025 年 12 月 26 日； doi:10.1038/s41565-025-02068-y CrSBr 中的激子传播受到磁性的强烈影响，特别是在尼尔温度下达到峰值。它的输运不受经典扩散的控制，而是受到与自旋自由度的相互作用的控制，特别是通过磁振子-激子阻力效应。
