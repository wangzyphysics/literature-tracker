## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03856v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Meteotsunamis are atmospherically driven sea-level oscillations that can trigger hazardous coastal flooding, particularly in resonant bays. This study assesses the GPU-based Meteo-HySEA model for meteotsunami simulation in the Adriatic Sea, benchmarking its performance against the CPU-based AdriSC-ADCIRC system. Three documented events (2014, 2017, 2020) were simulated using WRF downscaling of ERA reanalyses and validated with tide-gauge and microbarograph observations. Both models are limited by the underestimation of mesoscale pressure disturbances in the atmospheric forcing. Meteo-HySEA generally reproduces the timing and spatial variability of sea-level oscillations and often yields larger amplitudes than ADCIRC, but it tends to overestimate dominant wave periods, particularly in enclosed basins. Differences in oscillation persistence underscore the need for further validation against high-resolution tide-gauge data to assess whether Meteo-HySEA captures harbor seiches more realistically or ADCIRC better represents physical energy dissipation. Crucially, GPU acceleration provides order-of-magnitude gains in computational efficiency, enabling rapid high-resolution, multi-grid simulations including inundation, and thus offering strong potential for operational early warning.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03856v1 公告类型：新 摘要：气象海啸是大气驱动的海平面波动，可能引发危险的沿海洪水，特别是在共振海湾。本研究评估了用于亚得里亚海气象海啸模拟的基于 GPU 的 Meteo-HySEA 模型，并根据基于 CPU 的 AdriSC-ADCIRC 系统对其性能进行了基准测试。使用 ERA 再分析的 WRF 降尺度模拟了三个记录的事件（2014 年、2017 年、2020 年），并通过验潮仪和微气压计观测进行了验证。这两种模型都受到大气强迫中中尺度压力扰动的低估的限制。 Meteo-HySEA 通常再现海平面振荡的时间和空间变化，并且通常产生比 ADCIRC 更大的振幅，但它往往会高估主波周期，特别是在封闭盆地中。振荡持续性的差异强调需要根据高分辨率验潮数据进行进一步验证，以评估 Meteo-HySEA 是否更真实地捕获港口水流，或者 ADCIRC 是否更好地代表物理能量耗散。至关重要的是，GPU 加速可提高计算效率，实现快速高分辨率、多网格模拟（包括洪水），从而为业务预警提供强大潜力。
