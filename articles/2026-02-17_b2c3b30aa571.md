## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13341v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Superparamagnetism in nanoscale magnetic layers is a critical property for a wide range of spintronic-based sensor and computing applications. While conventional magnetization measurements can detect superparamagnetic signatures, they often require the application of high perturbative fields and are difficult to implement for magnetic layers integrated within functional devices. In this study, we non-invasively investigate the superparamagnetic spin dynamics of a nanoscale CoFeB layer of thickness 1.1 nm, deposited on a diamond substrate, by probing its low-frequency near-field electromagnetic (EM) fluctuations using nitrogen-vacancy (NV) centers-based quantum dephasometry. Our measurements reveal an unconventional, non-monotonic temperature dependence of the dephasing time of NV centers, which we attribute to EM fluctuations produced by thermally driven superparamagnetic domain flipping in CoFeB. Our findings are further supported by the theoretical interpretation of the dephasing dynamics of NV centers and the complementary SQUID-based magnetization characterizations of the CoFeB layer. Additionally, exploiting the technique of NV dephasometry, we extract the spectral density of the EM fluctuations in CoFeB, which is used to isolate different components of the EM fluctuations acting on NV centers. We also measure the CoFeB-to-NV distance-dependent coherence times of NV centers to investigate the effect of the dimensionality of the CoFeB layer on the generated near-field EM fluctuations. These results provide critical insight into the magnetization dynamics and near-field EM environment of nanoscale magnetic layers. It also has significant implications for the development of hybrid quantum spintronic devices and applications involving nanoscale opto-magnetic materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13341v1 公告类型：新 摘要：纳米级磁性层中的超顺磁性是各种基于自旋电子的传感器和计算应用的关键特性。虽然传统的磁化测量可以检测超顺磁特征，但它们通常需要应用高扰动场，并且对于集成在功能设备内的磁性层来说很难实现。在这项研究中，我们通过使用基于氮空位 (NV) 中心的量子相移测量法探测其低频近场电磁 (EM) 波动，非侵入性地研究了沉积在金刚石基板上的厚度为 1.1 nm 的纳米级 CoFeB 层的超顺磁自旋动力学。我们的测量揭示了 NV 中心相移时间的非常规、非单调温度依赖性，我们将其归因于 CoFeB 中热驱动的超顺磁畴翻转产生的电磁波动。我们的研究结果得到了 NV 中心失相动力学的理论解释和 CoFeB 层基于 SQUID 的互补磁化特性的进一步支持。此外，利用 NV 相移技术，我们提取了 CoFeB 中电磁波动的谱密度，用于隔离作用于 NV 中心的电磁波动的不同分量。我们还测量了 NV 中心的 CoFeB 到 NV 距离相关的相干时间，以研究 CoFeB 层的维数对生成的近场电磁波动的影响。这些结果为纳米级磁性层的磁化动力学和近场电磁环境提供了重要的见解。它还对混合量子自旋电子器件的开发和涉及纳米级光磁材料的应用具有重大影响。
