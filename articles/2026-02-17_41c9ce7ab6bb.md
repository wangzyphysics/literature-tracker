## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14152v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: A key question for most applications involving reconfigurable linear wave systems is how accurately a desired linear operator can be realized by configuring the system's tunable elements. The relevance of this question spans from hybrid-MIMO analog combiners via computational meta-imagers to programmable wave-domain signal processing. Yet, no electromagnetically consistent bounds have been derived for the fidelity with which a desired operator can be realized in a real-world reconfigurable wave system. Here, we derive such bounds based on an electromagnetically consistent multiport-network model (capturing mutual coupling between tunable elements) and accounting for real-world hardware constraints (lossy, 1-bit-programmable elements). Specifically, we formulate the operator-synthesis task as a quadratically constrained fractional-quadratic problem and compute rigorous fidelity upper bounds based on semidefinite relaxation. We apply our technique to three distinct experimental setups. The first two setups are, respectively, a free-space and a rich-scattering $4\times 4$ MIMO channel at 2.45 GHz parameterized by a reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) comprising 100 1-bit-programmable elements. The third setup is a $4\times 4$ MIMO channel at 19 GHz from four feeds of a dynamic metasurface antenna (DMA) to four users. We systematically study how the achievable fidelity scales with the number of tunable elements, and we probe the tightness of our bounds by trying to find optimized configurations approaching the bounds with standard discrete-optimization techniques. We observe a strong influence of the coupling strength between tunable elements on our fidelity bound. For the two RIS-based setups, our bound attests to insufficient wave-domain flexibility for the considered operator synthesis.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14152v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：对于大多数涉及可重构线性波系统的应用来说，一个关键问题是通过配置系统的可调谐元件可以如何准确地实现所需的线性算子。这个问题的相关性涵盖了从混合 MIMO 模拟组合器、计算元成像器到可编程波域信号处理。然而，对于在现实世界的可重构波系统中实现所需算子的保真度，尚未得出电磁一致的界限。在这里，我们基于电磁一致的多端口网络模型（捕获可调元件之间的相互耦合）并考虑现实世界的硬件约束（有损、1 位可编程元件）得出这样的界限。具体来说，我们将算子综合任务表述为二次约束分数二次问题，并基于半定松弛计算严格的保真度上限。我们将我们的技术应用于三个不同的实验设置。前两个设置分别是 2.45 GHz 的自由空间和丰富散射的 4\times 4$ MIMO 通道，由包含 100 个 1 位可编程元件的可重构智能表面 (RIS) 进行参数化。第三种设置是 19 GHz 的 4\times 4$ MIMO 通道，通过动态超表面天线 (DMA) 的四个馈源向四个用户提供信号。我们系统地研究了可实现的保真度如何随可调元件的数量而变化，并通过尝试使用标准离散优化技术找到接近边界的优化配置来探测边界的紧密度。我们观察到可调元件之间的耦合强度对保真度范围有很大影响。对于两种基于 RIS 的设置，我们的界限证明了所考虑的算子综合的波域灵活性不足。
