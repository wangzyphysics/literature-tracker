## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21559v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a fractionally quantized polarization induced by interlayer sliding in bilayer altermagnets, unveiling a previously unrecognized multiferroic phase termed sliding fractional quantum multiferroicity (SFQM). This unconventional magnetic phase uniquely integrates sliding ferroelectricity with fractional quantum ferroelectricity, enabling highly efficient switching and nonvolatile electrical control of spin.~Unlike conventional multiferroics, SFQM simultaneously exhibits lattice-scale atomic displacements, ultralow switching barriers, and spin splitting, giving rise to a large fractionally quantized polarization and strong magnetoelectric coupling. Through symmetry analysis and first-principles calculations, we identify bilayer altermagnet Ca(CoN)$_2$ and its family materials as promising candidates hosting SFQM. In contrast to gate-controlled schemes, the spin-layer coupling in SFQM is intrinsically induced by spontaneous electrical and layer polarization, requiring no sustained gate field and exhibiting nonvolatile character. This mechanism enables nonvolatile electrical control of spin through biaxial sliding, where displacements along the \textit{x}- and \textit{y}-axes generate opposite polarization directions in the layer-dependent electrical polarization. Furthermore, SFQM exhibits a fully switchable anomalous Hall effect and a pronounced magneto-optical response, which can be utilized for its detection and distinction. These findings highlight the promising role of sliding-mediated couplings among unconventional magnetism, fractional quantum ferroelectricity, and stacking order in realizing electrically controllable two-dimensional multiferroics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21559v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种由双层互变磁体中的层间滑动引起的分数量化极化，揭示了一种以前未被识别的多铁性相，称为滑动分数量子多铁性（SFQM）。这种非常规磁相独特地将滑动铁电性与分数量子铁电性相结合，实现了自旋的高效开关和非易失性电控制。〜与传统的多铁性材料不同，SFQM同时表现出晶格级原子位移、超低开关势垒和自旋分裂，从而产生大的分数量子化极化和强磁电耦合。通过对称性分析和第一性原理计算，我们确定双层交变磁体 Ca(CoN)$_2$ 及其家族材料是承载 SFQM 的有希望的候选材料。与栅极控制方案相比，SFQM 中的自旋层耦合本质上是由自发电极化和层极化引起的，不需要持续的栅极场并表现出非易失性特征。这种机制能够通过双轴滑动对自旋进行非易失性电控制，其中沿 \textit{x} 轴和 \textit{y} 轴的位移在层相关的电极化中产生相反的极化方向。此外，SFQM 表现出完全可切换的反常霍尔效应和明显的磁光响应，可用于其检测和区分。这些发现凸显了非常规磁性、分数量子铁电性和堆叠顺序之间的滑动介导耦合在实现电控二维多铁性方面的有希望的作用。
