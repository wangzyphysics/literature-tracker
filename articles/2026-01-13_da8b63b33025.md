## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07216v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The design of quantum states at heterointerfaces has enabled a variety of emergent phenomena. Among them, molecular intercalation superlattices have attracted attention as tunable hybrid materials, formed by inserting organic molecules into van der Waals crystals, where molecular structure and chemistry provide new degrees of freedom. Traditionally, the intercalated molecules have been regarded as inactive spacers, while possible molecular ordering and its impact on the host lattice have remained largely unexplored. Here, we report the discovery of a cooperative superstructure (CSS) phase in molecule intercalated NbSe2, where ordering of the guest molecules induce a concomitant superstructure in the NbSe2 host lattice, characterized by a moir\'e structure due to incommensurability between the molecular layer and the inorganic lattice. Synchrotron X-ray diffraction reveals the emergence of CSS phase, accompanied by crystal symmetry lowering. Complementary resistivity and thermal-quench measurements show that the transition is governed by unusually slow order-disorder kinetics, so that the CSS phase can be selectively accessed under standard laboratory cooling rates. This kinetic behavior arises from slow molecular dynamics coupled to the host lattice, contrasting with fast charge or magnetic ordering in inorganic solids. Our findings establish molecular ordering as a route for engineering heterointerfaces, enabling thermally programmable superstructures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07216v1 公告类型：新 摘要：异质界面处的量子态设计使得各种涌现现象成为可能。其中，分子插层超晶格作为可调谐杂化材料引起了人们的关注，它是通过将有机分子插入范德华晶体中而形成的，其中分子结构和化学提供了新的自由度。传统上，插层分子被认为是非活性间隔基，而可能的分子排序及其对主晶格的影响在很大程度上仍未被探索。在这里，我们报告了分子插层 NbSe2 中的协同超结构 (CSS) 相，其中客体分子的排序在 NbSe2 主晶格中诱导了伴随的超结构，其特征是由于分子层和无机晶格之间的不可通约性而产生莫尔结构。同步加速器X射线衍射揭示了CSS相的出现，并伴随着晶体对称性的降低。互补电阻率和热淬火测量表明，转变受异常缓慢的有序-无序动力学控制，因此可以在标准实验室冷却速率下选择性地获得 CSS 相。这种动力学行为源于与主晶格耦合的慢分子动力学，与无机固体中的快速电荷或磁有序形成对比。我们的研究结果将分子排序确立为工程异质界面的一种途径，从而实现热可编程超结构。
