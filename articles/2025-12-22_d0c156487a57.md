## Abstract
In this study, fluid velocity is measured in a porous medium using a magnetic resonance steady state free precession (SSFP) technique. As shown theoretically and experimentally in this study, the SSFP measurement under steady flow conditions produces a periodic steady state magnetization with a strong flow sensitivity. The Bloch–Torrey equations were solved analytically using a Fourier series to predict the distortion of the steady state response due to flow. The analytical solution accurately describes the measured steady state longitudinal magnetization and transverse magnetization phase to permit the determination of the fluid velocity in a Bentheimer sandstone core plug non-invasively.

## 摘要（中文）
在本研究中，使用磁共振稳态自由进动 (SSFP) 技术测量多孔介质中的流体速度。正如本研究中的理论和实验所示，稳定流条件下的 SSFP 测量会产生具有强流敏感性的周期性稳态磁化强度。使用傅里叶级数对 Bloch-Torrey 方程进行分析求解，以预测由于流动而导致的稳态响应的失真。该解析解准确地描述了测量的稳态纵向磁化强度和横向磁化强度相位，从而可以非侵入性地确定本特海默砂岩岩心塞中的流体速度。
