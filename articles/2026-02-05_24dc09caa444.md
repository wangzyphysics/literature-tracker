## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04075v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine learning models have recently emerged to predict whether hypothetical solid-state materials can be synthesized. These models aim to circumvent direct first-principles modeling of solid-state phase transformations, instead learning from large databases of successfully synthesized materials. Here, we assess the alignment of several recently introduced synthesis prediction models with material and reaction thermodynamics, quantified by the energy with respect to the convex hull and a metric accounting for thermodynamic selectivity of enumerated synthesis reactions. A dataset of successful synthesis recipes was used to determine the likely bounds on both quantities beyond which materials can be deemed unlikely to be synthesized. With these bounds as context, thermodynamic quantities were computed using the CHGNet foundation potential for thousands of new hypothetical materials generated using the Chemeleon generative model. Four recently published machine learning models for synthesizability prediction were applied to this same dataset, and the resultant predictions were considered against computed thermodynamics. We find these models generally overpredict the likelihood of synthesis, but some model scores do trend with thermodynamic heuristics, assigning lower scores to materials that are less stable or do not have an available synthesis recipe that is calculated to be thermodynamically selective. In total, this work identifies existing gaps in machine learning models for materials synthesis and introduces a new approach to assess their quality in the absence of extensive negative examples (failed syntheses).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04075v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近出现了机器学习模型来预测是否可以合成假设的固态材料。这些模型旨在规避固态相变的直接第一原理建模，而是从成功合成材料的大型数据库中学习。在这里，我们评估了最近引入的几个合成预测模型与材料和反应热力学的一致性，通过凸包的能量和计算所列举的合成反应的热力学选择性的度量来量化。成功合成配方的数据集被用来确定两个数量的可能界限，超过该界限，材料就被认为不可能合成。以这些界限为背景，使用 CHGNet 基础势计算了使用 Chemeleon 生成模型生成的数千种新假设材料的热力学量。最近发布的四个用于可合成性预测的机器学习模型被应用于同一数据集，并且根据计算热力学考虑所得预测。我们发现这些模型通常会高估合成的可能性，但一些模型分数确实具有热力学启发式趋势，为不太稳定或不具有经计算具有热力学选择性的可用合成配方的材料分配较低的分数。总的来说，这项工作确定了材料合成机器学习模型中现有的差距，并引入了一种在没有大量负面例子（合成失败）的情况下评估其质量的新方法。
