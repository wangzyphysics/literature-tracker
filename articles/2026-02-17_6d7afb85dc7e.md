## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14887v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: TaSb$_2$, a member of the transition metal dipnictide family of materials, hosts the very rare dual topological phase - weak topological insulating state and topological crystalline insulating state along different crystallographic orientations. So far, studies on the electronic structure of transition metal dipnictides have focused on their overall electronic structure and the bulk open-orbit Fermi surfaces. Using angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy, density functional theory calculations, and transport measurements, we distinguish the intertwined bulk and surface states on the weakly topological $(20\bar{1})$ plane of TaSb$_2$. We identify multiple electron- and hole-like bulk bands, yielding a near-perfect carrier compensation. Crucially, we observe open-orbit FSs parallel to $\bar{L}$-$\bar{Y}$ direction that are entirely of surface origin. Circular-dichroism ARPES reveals $k \rightarrow -k$ spectral reversal, indicating spin-momentum locking and the topological nature of these surface states. Consistent with this, magnetotransport measurements display weak antilocalization, establishing TaSb$_2$ as a platform for spin-polarized topological transport on a weakly topological surface.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14887v1 公告类型：新 摘要：TaSb$_2$ 是过渡金属二镍化物材料家族的成员，具有非常罕见的双拓扑相 - 沿不同晶体取向的弱拓扑绝缘态和拓扑晶体绝缘态。迄今为止，对过渡金属二氮化物电子结构的研究主要集中在其整体电子结构和体开轨道费米面。利用角分辨光电子能谱、密度泛函理论计算和输运测量，我们区分了 TaSb$_2$ 弱拓扑 $(20\bar{1})$ 平面上相互交织的体态和表面态。我们识别出多个类电子和类空穴的体能带，产生近乎完美的载流子补偿。至关重要的是，我们观察到平行于 $\bar{L}$-$\bar{Y}$ 方向的开轨道 FS，它们完全是表面原点。圆二色性 ARPES 揭示了 $k \rightarrow -k$ 光谱反转，表明自旋动量锁定和这些表面态的拓扑性质。与此一致的是，磁输运测量显示出弱反局域化，将 TaSb$_2$ 建立为弱拓扑表面上自旋极化拓扑输运的平台。
