## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22791v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study energy dissipation and propagation of information encoded by Josephson vortices in two types of circular shift register: a) a uniform register composed of sections of discrete Josephson transmission lines (JTL) forming a closed loop with a flux pump allowing to change the number of moving fluxon; b) a nonuniform register composed of sections of the regular JTL and sections of JTLs utilizing nSQUIDs - dc-SQUIDs with negative inductance between their arms - instead of single Josephson junctions. nSQUIDs are parametric devices with a flexible double-well potential that were proposed as components for reversible computing. For the uniform register, we demonstrate the energy dissipation per bit-shift operation below the Landauer's thermodynamic limit $E_T=k_BTln2$ up to propagation delays of ~0.7 ns, corresponding to the circular information motion with frequencies up to ~1.4 GHz. This does not contradict Landauer's minimum energy requirement for computations since information is not destroyed. For the nonuniform register, we find the minimum energy dissipation per bit-shift of about 16$E_T$ and attribute this to a nonuniform movement of vortices and energy barriers between the regular JTL and nSQUID sections. Differences of Josephson vortex propagation in both types of circular registers are discussed based on the measured current-voltage characteristics, extracted effective resistance and the terminal speed of Josephson vortices, and their dependences on the number of moving vorticies. nSQUID inductance connecting JJs to the ground leads to an unusual type of lossless discrete transmission line with frequency-dependent impedance and propagation speed, both different from the regular JTLs.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22791v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究两种类型的循环移位寄存器中由约瑟夫森涡旋编码的信息的能量耗散和传播：a）由离散约瑟夫森传输线（JTL）部分组成的统一寄存器，形成一个带有通量泵的闭环，允许改变移动通量子的数量； b) 非均匀寄存器，由常规 JTL 部分和利用 nSQUID（臂之间具有负电感的 dc-SQUID）的 JTL 部分组成，而不是单个约瑟夫森结。 nSQUID 是具有灵活双阱潜力的参数设备，被提议作为可逆计算的组件。对于统一寄存器，我们展示了低于兰道尔热力学极限 $E_T=k_BTln2$ 的每次位移操作的能量耗散，直至传播延迟约为 0.7 ns，对应于频率高达约 1.4 GHz 的圆形信息运动。这与兰道尔计算的最低能量要求并不矛盾，因为信息不会被破坏。对于非均匀寄存器，我们发现每个位移位的最小能量耗散约为 16$E_T$，并将其归因于常规 JTL 和 nSQUID 部分之间涡流和能量势垒的不均匀运动。基于测量的电流-电压特性、提取的有效电阻和约瑟夫森涡旋的终端速度以及它们对移动涡旋数量的依赖性，讨论了两种类型的圆形寄存器中约瑟夫森涡旋传播的差异。将 JJ 连接到地的 nSQUID 电感形成了一种不寻常的无损离散传输线，其阻抗和传播速度取决于频率，这两者都与常规 JTL 不同。
