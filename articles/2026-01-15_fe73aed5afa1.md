## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09149v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study aims to conduct a bibliometric analysis of scientific publications discussing technology integration in the Project-Based Learning (PjBL) model during the 2015-2024 period. With the evolving 21st century educational paradigm, PjBL has emerged as one of the most promising pedagogical approaches. In this context, the integration of technology in PjBL becomes an important catalyst that expands the scope and depth of learning experiences while preparing students to face challenges in the digital era. This research applies a bibliometric analysis methodology and systematic literature review, utilizing the Publish or Perish (POP) application for data collection and VOSviewer for data analysis and visualization. The results reveal emerging research trends, collaboration patterns, and focus areas in the scientific literature related to technology integration in PjBL. The bibliometric analysis uncovers research dynamics and knowledge gaps that need to be addressed. These findings provide valuable insights for researchers, practitioners, and policymakers in optimizing the implementation of technology-based PjBL and formulating educational policies responsive to technological developments and 21st-century learning needs. The results of this study are expected to contribute significantly to the academic community and educational practitioners in developing more effective technology based PjBL implementation strategies and fostering interdisciplinary collaboration to advance education in the digital age.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09149v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本研究旨在对 2015-2024 年期间讨论基于项目的学习 (PjBL) 模型中的技术集成的科学出版物进行文献计量分析。随着 21 世纪教育范式的不断发展，PjBL 已成为最有前途的教学方法之一。在此背景下，PjBL 中的技术整合成为重要的催化剂，扩大了学习体验的范围和深度，同时让学生做好应对数字时代挑战的准备。本研究采用文献计量分析方法和系统文献综述，利用 Publish or Perish (POP) 应用程序进行数据收集，并利用 VOSviewer 进行数据分析和可视化。结果揭示了与 PjBL 技术集成相关的科学文献中的新兴研究趋势、合作模式和重点领域。文献计量分析揭示了研究动态和需要解决的知识差距。这些发现为研究人员、从业者和政策制定者优化基于技术的 PjBL 的实施以及制定响应技术发展和 21 世纪学习需求的教育政策提供了宝贵的见解。这项研究的结果预计将为学术界和教育从业者制定更有效的基于技术的 PjBL 实施策略并促进跨学科合作以推进数字时代的教育做出重大贡献。
