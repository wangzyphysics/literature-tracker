## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02182v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Astrophysical dynamos feature various spatial structures and dynamical regimes, ranging from hemispherical magnetic fields to the random reversals of the geodynamo. The recently observed Tayler-Spruit dynamo has been invoked to explain angular momentum transport in stellar radiative zones and magnetar formation in a proto-neutron star spun-up by fallback accretion. Whether this dynamo mechanism can lead to different dynamical regimes remains an open question. Using three-dimensional direct numerical simulations, we model the dynamics of a stably stratified spherical Couette flow, with the outer sphere rotating faster than the inner one. While the generation of strong stationary and hemispherical dynamos has been observed in our previous studies, we report for the first time the existence of reversals and complex temporal dynamics. We observe that the dynamics is strongly correlated with the equatorial symmetry breaking of the flow. Focusing on a fiducial dynamo simulation, we propose a simple interpretation of its dynamics, which consists in the coupling of two large-scale magnetic modes with two opposite equatorial symmetries by the flow symmetry breaking. While this interpretation captures the simplest observed dynamics, the nonlinear interaction between a higher number of magnetic modes is certainly required to describe some of the more complex regimes. The wide diversity of dynamical regimes generated by the Tayler-Spruit dynamo may have interesting implications for the geometry of the neutron star magnetic fields, and therefore neutron star emissions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02182v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：天体物理发电机具有各种空间结构和动力机制，范围从半球磁场到地球发电机的随机反转。最近观察到的泰勒-斯普雷特发电机被用来解释恒星辐射区中的角动量传输以及通过后退吸积旋转的原中子星中的磁星形成。这种发电机机制是否能够导致不同的动力机制仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。使用三维直接数值模拟，我们模拟了稳定分层球形库埃特流的动力学，其中外球体的旋转速度比内球体快。虽然在我们之前的研究中已经观察到了强大的静止和半球形发电机的产生，但我们首次报告了逆转和复杂的时间动力学的存在。我们观察到动力学与流动的赤道对称性破缺密切相关。着眼于基准发电机模拟，我们提出了对其动力学的简单解释，其中包括通过流对称破缺将两个大尺度磁模式与两个相反的赤道对称性耦合。虽然这种解释捕获了最简单的观察到的动力学，但肯定需要更多磁模式之间的非线性相互作用来描述一些更复杂的状态。泰勒-斯普雷特发电机产生的动力学体系的广泛多样性可能对中子星磁场的几何形状以及中子星发射产生有趣的影响。
