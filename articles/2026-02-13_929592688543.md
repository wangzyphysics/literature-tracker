## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11866v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Spin-orbit coupling (SOC) is incorporated into the phaseless plane-wave-based auxiliary-field quantum Monte Carlo (pw-AFQMC) method. This integration is implemented using optimized multiple-projector norm-conserving pseudopotentials, which are derived from the fully-relativistic (FR) atomic all-electron Dirac-like equation. The inclusion of SOC enables accurate phaseless pw-AFQMC calculations that capture both electronic correlation and SOC effects concurrently, greatly improving the method's applicability for studying systems containing heavy atoms. We discuss the form of FR pseudopotentials and detail the corresponding formulations of phaseless pw-AFQMC with a two-component Hamiltonian in the spinor basis. The accuracy of our approach is demonstrated by computing the dissociation energy of molecule I2 and the cohesive energy of bulk Pb, highlighting the large influence of SOC in both. Subsequently, we determine the transition pressure of the III-V compound InP from its zinc-blende to rock-salt phase by constructing and analyzing their respective equations of state.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11866v1 公告类型：新 摘要：自旋轨道耦合（SOC）被纳入基于无相平面波的辅助场量子蒙特卡罗（pw-AFQMC）方法。这种积分是使用优化的多投影仪范数守恒赝势来实现的，该赝势源自完全相对论 (FR) 原子全电子类狄拉克方程。包含 SOC 可以实现精确的无相 pw-AFQMC 计算，同时捕获电子相关性和 SOC 效应，大大提高了该方法研究含有重原子的系统的适用性。我们讨论了 FR 赝势的形式，并详细介绍了旋量基上具有二分量哈密顿量的无相 pw-AFQMC 的相应公式。我们的方法的准确性通过计算分子 I2 的解离能和块体 Pb 的内聚能来证明，突出了 SOC 对两者的巨大影响。随后，我们通过构建和分析各自的状态方程，确定了 III-V 族化合物 InP 从闪锌矿相到岩盐相的转变压力。
