## Abstract
arXiv:2504.17292v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Statistical properties of the pair dispersion of Lagrangian particles (tracers) in incompressible turbulent flows provide insights into transport and mixing. We explore the same in transonic to supersonic compressible turbulence of an isothermal ideal gas in two dimensions, driven by large-scale solenoidal and irrotational stirring forces, via direct numerical simulations. We find that the scaling exponents of the order-$p$ negative moments of the distribution of exit times -- in particular, the doubling and halving times of pair separations -- are nonlinear functions of $p$. Furthermore, the doubling and halving time statistics are different. The halving-time exponents are universal -- they satisfy their multifractal model-based prediction, irrespective of the nature of the stirring. However, the doubling-time exponents are not. In the solenoidally-stirred flows, the doubling time exponents can be expressed solely in terms of the multifractal scaling exponents obtained from the structure functions of the solenoidal component of the velocity. Moreover, they depend strongly on the Mach number, Ma, as elongated patches of high vorticity emerge along shock fronts at high Ma. In contrast, in the irrotationally-stirred flows, the doubling-time exponents do not satisfy any known multifractal model-based relation, and are independent of Ma. Our findings are of potential relevance to astrophysical disks and molecular clouds wherein turbulent transport and mixing of gases often govern chemical kinetics and the rates of formation of stars and planetesimals.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2504.17292v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：不可压缩湍流中拉格朗日粒子（示踪剂）对色散的统计特性提供了对传输和混合的见解。我们通过直接数值模拟，在大规模螺线管和无旋搅拌力驱动下，在二维等温理想气体的跨音速到超音速可压缩湍流中探索同样的问题。我们发现退出时间分布的$p$阶负矩的标度指数——特别是对分离的倍增和减半时间——是$p$的非线性函数。此外，倍增和减半时间统计也不同。减半时间指数是通用的——它们满足基于多重分形模型的预测，无论搅拌的性质如何。然而，倍增时间指数则不然。在螺线管搅拌流中，倍增时间指数可以仅用从速度的螺线管分量的结构函数获得的多重分形标度指数来表示。此外，它们强烈依赖于马赫数 Ma，因为高 Ma 处的激波锋面会出现细长的高涡度斑块。相反，在无旋搅拌流中，倍增时间指数不满足任何已知的基于多重分形模型的关系，并且与 Ma 无关。我们的发现与天体物理圆盘和分子云具有潜在相关性，其中气体的湍流传输和混合通常控制化学动力学以及恒星和星子的形成速率。
