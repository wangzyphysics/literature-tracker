## Abstract
Bismuth-based bicyclic peptides (Bi-BCPs) have garnered growing interest as a promising drug modality due to their notable conformational rigidity and cell permeability, underscoring their considerable potential for diverse biological applications. However, a generalizable synthetic strategy for producing Bi-BCPs with exceptional cell permeability remains lacking. To address this challenge, we developed a “plug-and-display” strategy that enables the modular synthesis of a novel class of cell-permeable, bifunctional Bi-BCPs. These Bi-BCPs adopt a “cycle A–Bi–cycle B” architecture, in which one cycle serves as a functional motif while the other acts as a cell-penetrating sequence (CPS), termed the “plug.” The two cycles are linked via a bismuth metal ion to form the bicyclic scaffold. Using the integrin-binding RGD peptide as a model functional motif, we conducted comprehensive structure–cell permeability relationship studies by systematically varying the A–B conjugation pattern and CPS parameters—including sequence type, charge number, and chiral configuration. Our results demonstrated that chiral configuration exerts the most pronounced influence on cell permeability, and we identified the iterative (Rr)4 motif as the optimal plug for enhancing this property in Bi-BCPs. Furthermore, the (Rr)4 plug was successfully applied to construct glioma-targeting radioligands, which exhibited significantly improved tumor uptake and retention. Additionally, combining the (Rr)4 plug with hydrophobic tetrapeptides yielded novel super-amphiphilic Bi-BCPs with markedly enhanced anti-tumor and anti-microbial activities. In summary, this study presents a facile and robust “plug-and-display” strategy for constructing unprecedented, multifunctional Bi-BCPs with superior cell permeability, opening new avenues for the development of Bi-BCP–based therapeutics.

## 摘要（中文）
基于铋的双环肽（Bi-BCP）由于其显着的构象刚性和细胞通透性，作为一种有前景的药物形式引起了越来越多的兴趣，强调了它们在多种生物应用中的巨大潜力。然而，仍然缺乏生产具有优异细胞渗透性的 Bi-BCP 的通用合成策略。为了应对这一挑战，我们开发了一种“即插即用”策略，能够模块化合成一类新型细胞渗透性双功能 Bi-BCP。这些 Bi-BCP 采用“循环 A-双-循环 B”架构，其中一个循环充当功能基序，而另一个循环充当细胞穿透序列 (CPS)，称为“插头”。这两个循环通过铋金属离子连接形成双环支架。使用整合素结合 RGD 肽作为模型功能基序，我们通过系统地改变 A-B 缀合模式和 CPS 参数（包括序列类型、电荷数和手性构型）进行了全面的结构-细胞渗透性关系研究。我们的结果表明，手性构型对细胞通透性产生最显着的影响，并且我们将迭代 (Rr)4 基序确定为增强 Bi-BCP 中这一特性的最佳插塞。此外，(Rr)4 栓塞成功应用于构建神经胶质瘤靶向放射性配体，其显着改善了肿瘤的摄取和保留。此外，将 (Rr)4 插塞与疏水性四肽结合产生了新型超两亲性 Bi-BCP，其抗肿瘤和抗微生物活性显着增强。总之，本研究提出了一种简便而强大的“即插即用”策略，用于构建前所未有的、具有优异细胞渗透性的多功能Bi-BCP，为基于Bi-BCP的疗法的开发开辟了新途径。
