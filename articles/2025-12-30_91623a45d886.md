## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23622v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Mechanistic network models can capture salient characteristics of empirical networks using a small set of domain-specific, interpretable mechanisms. Yet inference remains challenging because the likelihood is often intractable. We show that, for a broad class of growing network models, information about model parameters is localized in the network, i.e., the likelihood can be expressed in terms of small subgraphs. We take a Bayesian perspective to inference and develop neural density estimators (NDEs) to approximate the posterior distribution of model parameters using graph neural networks (GNNs) with limited receptive size, i.e., the GNN can only "see" small subgraphs. We characterize nine growing network models in terms of their localization and demonstrate that localization predictions agree with NDEs on simulated data. Even for non-localized models, NDEs can infer high-fidelity posteriors matching model-specific inference methods at a fraction of the cost. Our findings establish information localization as a fundamental property of network growth, theoretically justifying the analysis of local subgraphs embedded in larger, unobserved networks and the use of GNNs with limited receptive field for likelihood-free inference.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23622v1 公告类型：交叉 摘要：机械网络模型可以使用一小组特定于领域的可解释机制来捕获经验网络的显着特征。然而，推断仍然具有挑战性，因为可能性往往很棘手。我们表明，对于一大类不断增长的网络模型，有关模型参数的信息在网络中是本地化的，即可能性可以用小子图来表示。我们采用贝叶斯观点来推理和开发神经密度估计器（NDE），以使用接受大小有限的图神经网络（GNN）来近似模型参数的后验分布，即 GNN 只能“看到”小子图。我们在本地化方面描述了九个不断增长的网络模型，并证明本地化预测与模拟数据上的 NDE 一致。即使对于非局部模型，NDE 也可以以一小部分成本推断出与特定于模型的推理方法相匹配的高保真后验。我们的研究结果将信息本地化确立为网络增长的基本属性，从理论上证明了对嵌入在更大的、未观察到的网络中的局部子图的分析以及使用具有有限感受野的 GNN 进行无似然推理的合理性。
