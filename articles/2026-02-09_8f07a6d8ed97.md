## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06573v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We realize a superconductor-coupled quantum dot (QD) in an InSb nanosheet, a 2D platform promising for studies of topological superconductivity. The device consists of a superconductor-QD-superconductor junction, where a bottom bilayer gate defines the QD and allows tuning of its coupling to the superconducting leads. The QD exhibits large $g$-factors and strong spin-orbit coupling. Transport measurements reveal Coulomb diamond-shaped differential conductance features with even-odd alternating sizes and pronounced conductance lines associated with the superconducting gap, confirming a few-electron, superconductor-coupled regime. At an odd electron occupation, Kondo signatures emerge, including a zero-bias peak that splits with magnetic field and is logarithmically suppressed at elevated temperatures. We further observe a doublet-singlet quantum phase transition, manifested by a clear change of Andreev bound states from crossing to anticrossing as the coupling strength increases. These results underscore the rich physics of InSb nanosheet QDs and their promise for topological quantum devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06573v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们在 InSb 纳米片中实现了超导耦合量子点 (QD)，这是一个有望用于拓扑超导研究的 2D 平台。该器件由超导-量子点-超导结组成，其中底部双层栅极定义了量子点，并允许调节其与超导引线的耦合。量子点表现出较大的 $g$ 因子和较强的自旋轨道耦合。输运测量揭示了库仑菱形微分电导特征，具有奇偶交替尺寸和与超导间隙相关的明显电导线，证实了少电子、超导体耦合状态。在奇数电子占据时，近藤特征出现，包括随磁场分裂的零偏压峰，并在高温下受到对数抑制。我们进一步观察到双重态-单重态量子相变，表现为随着耦合强度的增加，安德烈夫束缚态从交叉到反交叉的明显变化。这些结果强调了 InSb 纳米片量子点的丰富物理特性及其在拓扑量子器件中的应用前景。
