## Abstract
Photothermal therapy (PTT) offers a minimally invasive approach for treating hypoxic tumors, while photodynamic therapy (PDT) is limited by its oxygen dependence. Indocyanine green (ICG) is a clinically approved near-infrared (NIR) dye with potential for PTT and PDT, but its application is hindered by poor photostability, rapid clearance, and aggregation, resulting in unfavorable pharmacokinetics and limited phototherapeutic performances. Here, we address these limitations through a combination of chemical transformation of ICG by dimerization, controlled supramolecular assembly into dye aggregates, and a tailored formulation strategy. We systematically compared three forms, monomeric ICG, its covalent dimer (dICG), and the corresponding J-aggregate (dIJA), each formulated into polyion complex nanoparticles (PNPs) using poly(2-ethyl-2-oxazoline)-b-poly(L-ornithine) as the polyelectrolyte carrier matrix. Subsequent genipin-mediated core crosslinking produced crosslinked nanoparticles (CPNPs) with enhanced stability, high encapsulation efficiency, and preserved optical characteristics. CPNPs consistently outperformed free dye and non-crosslinked PNPs, showing improved colloidal stability, enhanced photostability, and higher photothermal performances. In FaDu cells, ICG-loaded CPNPs mediated oxygen-dependent photodynamic effects, whereas dICG- and dIJA-loaded CPNPs induced oxygen-independent photothermal cytotoxicity under NIR irradiation. Our integrated comparison demonstrates that controlling the molecular form and supramolecular assembly of ICG derivatives enables tunable NIR phototherapy, identifying dICG- and dIJA-based CPNPs as promising platforms for the treatment of hypoxic tumors by photoacoustic imaging (PAI) guided PTT.

## 摘要（中文）
光热疗法（PTT）为治疗缺氧肿瘤提供了一种微创方法，而光动力疗法（PDT）则因其氧依赖性而受到限制。吲哚菁绿（ICG）是一种临床批准的近红外（NIR）染料，具有用于PTT和PDT的潜力，但其应用受到光稳定性差、快速清除和聚集的阻碍，导致不利的药代动力学和有限的光治疗性能。在这里，我们通过二聚化对 ICG 进行化学转化、受控超分子组装成染料聚集体以及定制的配方策略来解决这些限制。我们系统地比较了三种形式，单体ICG、其共价二聚体（dICG）和相应的J-聚集体（dIJA），每种形式均使用聚（2-乙基-2-恶唑啉）-b-聚（L-鸟氨酸）作为聚电解质载体基质配制为聚离子复合纳米颗粒（PNP）。随后京尼平介导的核心交联产生了交联纳米粒子（CPNP），其稳定性增强，封装效率高，并保留了光学特性。 CPNP 的性能始终优于游离染料和非交联 PNP，表现出更好的胶体稳定性、增强的光稳定性和更高的光热性能。在 FaDu 细胞中，负载 ICG 的 CPNP 介导氧依赖性光动力效应，而负载 dICG 和 dIJA 的 CPNP 在近红外辐射下诱导不依赖于氧的光热细胞毒性。我们的综合比较表明，控制 ICG 衍生物的分子形式和超分子组装可以实现可调谐的 NIR 光疗，并将基于 dICG 和 dIJA 的 CPNP 确定为通过光声成像 (PAI) 引导的 PTT 治疗缺氧肿瘤的有前景的平台。
