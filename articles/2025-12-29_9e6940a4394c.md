## Abstract
arXiv:2507.09260v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The optical selection rules dictate symmetry-allowed/forbidden transitions, playing a decisive role in engineering exciton quantum states and designing optoelectronic devices. While both the real (quantum metric) and imaginary (Berry curvature) parts of quantum geometry contribute to optical transitions, the conventional theory of optical selection rules in solids incorporates only Berry curvature. Here, we propose quantum metric-based optical selection rules. We unveil a universal quantum metric-oscillator strength correspondence for linear polarization of light and establish valley-contrasted optical selection rules that lock orthogonal linear polarizations to distinct valleys. Tight-binding and first-principles calculations confirm our theory in two models (altermagnet and Kane-Mele) and monolayer $d$-wave altermagnet $\mathrm{V_2SeSO}$. This work provides a quantum metric paradigm for valley-based spintronic and optoelectronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2507.09260v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：光学选择规则规定了对称性允许/禁止的跃迁，在工程激子量子态和设计光电器件中发挥着决定性作用。虽然量子几何的实部（量子度量）和虚部（贝里曲率）都有助于光学跃迁，但固体中光学选择规则的传统理论仅包含贝里曲率。在这里，我们提出了基于量子度量的光学选择规则。我们推出了光线性偏振的通用量子度量-振荡器强度对应关系，并建立了谷对比光学选择规则，将正交线性偏振锁定到不同的谷。紧束缚和第一性原理计算在两个模型（交变磁体和凯恩-梅勒）和单层 $d$ 波交变磁体 $\mathrm{V_2SeSO}$ 中证实了我们的理论。这项工作为基于谷的自旋电子和光电应用提供了量子度量范式。
