## Abstract
Glycerol oxidation reaction (GOR) is a promising valorisation route to upgrade the biodiesel by-product while co-producing green hydrogen at the cathode in electrolysers. However, the working mechanism of transition-metal-based catalysts such as Ni(OH)₂ remains poorly understood. Here, we employed a multi-operando spectroelectrochemical approach combining UV–Vis optical spectroscopy, soft X-ray absorption spectroscopy, and time-resolved stepped-potential spectroscopy to investigate the active oxidising species and charge-transfer dynamics under OER and GOR conditions. We identified the NiOOH (Ni3+) as the active species for GOR, whereas the formation of higher valent NiOO (Ni4+) species is completely suppressed in the presence of glycerol. The accumulation of surface adsorbed glycerol molecules is rate-determining, (τ ~ 27.9 s at 1.47 VRHE), occuring slower than the intrinsic catalytic step of glycerol reaction ( τ ~ 3.2 s at 1.47 VRHE), which involves oxidation and bond cleavage. In contrast, OER has significantly slower kinetics (τ ~167 s at 1.47 VRHE), resulting in the dominance of GOR and suppresion of oxygen evolution in the presence of glycerol. The potential-independent production of formic acid during GOR follows an apparent first-order dependence on NiOOH concentration, suggesting continuous C-C bond cleavage activated by reactive *O species. These findings link oxidising species with charge-transfer dynamics, providing insight for the rational design of Ni-based catalysts for glycerol and other biomass-derived molecule oxidations.

## 摘要（中文）
甘油氧化反应（GOR）是一种很有前途的增值途径，可以升级生物柴油副产品，同时在电解槽的阴极共同生产绿色氢气。然而，人们对 Ni(OH)2 等过渡金属基催化剂的工作机制仍知之甚少。在这里，我们采用了结合紫外-可见光谱、软X射线吸收光谱和时间分辨阶梯电位光谱的多操作光谱电化学方法来研究OER和GOR条件下的活性氧化物质和电荷转移动力学。我们确定 NiOOH (Ni3+) 是 GOR 的活性物质，而高价 NiOO (Ni4+) 物质的形成在甘油存在下被完全抑制。表面吸附的甘油分子的积累是决定速率的（τ ~ 27.9 s at 1.47 VRHE），其发生速度慢于甘油反应的固有催化步骤（τ ~ 3.2 s at 1.47 VRHE），其中涉及氧化和键断裂。相比之下，OER 的动力学明显较慢（τ ~167 s，1.47 VRHE），导致 GOR 占主导地位并在甘油存在的情况下抑制氧气释放。 GOR 过程中甲酸的电势独立生成遵循对 NiOOH 浓度的明显一阶依赖性，表明反应性 *O 物质激活了连续的 C-C 键断裂。这些发现将氧化物质与电荷转移动力学联系起来，为甘油和其他生物质衍生分子氧化的镍基催化剂的合理设计提供了见解。
