## Abstract
The accurate characterisation of nucleophilicity has attracted the attention of research groups worldwide for many years. So far, no universally applicable descriptor that correlates closely with experimental observations has been established. In this study, the behaviour of the two conceptual DFT nucleophilicity indices is examined for a broad set of chemical compounds using semi-empirical methods. As exposed, the tested indices do not reliably capture experimental nucleophilicity trends when applied outside of small reference sets, leading to weak or negative correlations with Mayr's reactivity scale. To address these shortcomings, an empirical nucleophilicity index, $N_{emp}$, is constructed through symbolic regression, incorporating frontier orbital energies, CDFT-based reactivity descriptors, atom-specific Fukui functions, local charge information, and solvent characteristics. This model yields a markedly better correspondence with the experimental data, with additional improvements obtained for well-defined subsets of solvents and molecular scaffolds. Collectively, these findings expose the inherent limitations of existing global CDFT nucleophilicity indices and demonstrate the potential of data-driven strategies to build transferable reactivity descriptors.

## 摘要（中文）
亲核性的准确表征多年来吸引了全世界研究小组的关注。到目前为止，还没有建立与实验观察密切相关的普遍适用的描述符。在本研究中，使用半经验方法检查了多种化合物的两个概念性 DFT 亲核性指数的行为。正如所暴露的，当应用于小参考集之外时，测试的指数不能可靠地捕获实验亲核性趋势，导致与 Mayr 反应性量表的弱相关或负相关。为了解决这些缺点，通过符号回归构建了经验亲核性指数 $N_{emp}$，结合了前沿轨道能量、基于 CDFT 的反应性描述符、原子特定的 Fukui 函数、局域电荷信息和溶剂特征。该模型与实验数据具有明显更好的对应性，并且对于明确定义的溶剂和分子支架子集获得了额外的改进。总的来说，这些发现揭示了现有全球 CDFT 亲核性指数的固有局限性，并证明了数据驱动策略构建可转移反应性描述符的潜力。
