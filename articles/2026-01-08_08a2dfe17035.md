## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03366v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Compensated magnetic orders that can split the spin-degeneracy of electronic bands have become a very active field of research. As opposed to spin-orbit coupling, the splitting resulting from these "altermagnets" is not a small relativistic correction and, in contrast to ferromagnets, not accompanied by a net magnetization and large stray fields. In particular, the theoretical analysis of the interplay of altermagnetism and superconductivity has taken center stage, while experimental investigations of their coexistence remain in their infancy. We here study heterostructures consisting of Nb thins films interfaced with the $T_1$ and $T_2$ phases of Mn$_3$Pt. These non-collinear magnetic states can be thought of as descendants from the same altermagnetic order in the absence of spin-orbit coupling. We demonstrate the non-trivial impact on the superconducting state of Nb, which exhibits a zero-field superconducting diode effect, despite the compensated ($T_2$) and nearly-compensated ($T_1$) magnetic order; the diode efficiencies can reach large values (up to 50$\%$). The diode effect is found to be highly sensitive to the form of the magnetic order, illustrating its potential as a symmetry probe. The complex magnetic field and temperature dependence hint at a rich interplay of multiple contributing mechanisms. Our results define a new materials paradigm for dissipationless spintronics and magnetization-free diode functionality, while motivating further exploration of non-collinear altermagnetic superconductors.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03366v1 公告类型：新 摘要：能够分裂电子能带自旋简并性的补偿磁序已成为一个非常活跃的研究领域。与自旋轨道耦合相反，这些“交替磁体”产生的分裂不是一个小的相对论校正，并且与铁磁体相反，不伴随净磁化和大的杂散场。特别是，对交变磁性和超导性相互作用的理论分析已占据中心舞台，而对它们共存的实验研究仍处于起步阶段。我们在这里研究由 Nb 薄膜与 Mn$_3$Pt 的 $T_1$ 和 $T_2$ 相界面组成的异质结构。这些非共线磁态可以被认为是在没有自旋轨道耦合的情况下来自相同交变磁序的后代。我们证明了对 Nb 超导状态的重要影响，尽管具有补偿 ($T_2$) 和近补偿 ($T_1$) 磁序，但 Nb 仍表现出零场超导二极管效应；二极管效率可以达到很大的值（高达 50$\%$）。人们发现二极管效应对磁序的形式高度敏感，这说明了它作为对称探针的潜力。复杂的磁场和温度依赖性暗示了多种贡献机制的丰富相互作用。我们的结果定义了无耗散自旋电子学和无磁化二极管功能的新材料范例，同时激发了对非共线交变磁超导体的进一步探索。
