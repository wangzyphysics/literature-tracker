## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06511v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Altermagnet (AM), a recently identified class of collinear magnet, has garnered significant attention due to its unique combination of zero net magnetization and spin-split energy bands, leading to a variety of novel physical phenomena. Using numerically exact lattice Monte Carlo simulations, we investigate AM-like phases within the Ising-Kondo lattice model which is commonly employed to describe heavy-fermion materials. By incorporating an alternating next-nearest-neighbor hopping (NNNH) term, which arises from the influence of non-magnetic atoms in altermagnetic candidate materials, our results reveal key signatures of AM-like states, including spin-splitting quasiparticle bands and spectral functions, and demonstrate that d-wave AM remains stable across a broad range of interaction strengths, doping levels, NNNH amplitudes and temperatures, highlighting its robustness. Furthermore, through an analysis of non-magnetic impurity effects, we further confirm the d-wave symmetry of the AM phase. These findings establish a solid theoretical foundation for exploring AM-like phases in f-electron compounds, paving the way for future investigations into their exotic magnetic and electronic properties.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06511v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Altermagnet (AM) 是最近发现的一类共线磁体，由于其零净磁化强度和自旋分裂能带的独特组合而引起了广泛关注，导致了各种新颖的物理现象。使用数值精确的晶格蒙特卡罗模拟，我们研究了 Ising-Kondo 晶格模型中的类 AM 相，该模型通常用于描述重费米子材料。通过纳入由交变磁性候选材料中非磁性原子的影响产生的交替次近邻跳跃（NNNH）项，我们的结果揭示了类 AM 态的关键特征，包括自旋分裂准粒子带和光谱函数，并证明 d 波 AM 在广泛的相互作用强度、掺杂水平、NNNH 振幅和温度范围内保持稳定，突出了其鲁棒性。此外，通过非磁性杂质效应的分析，我们进一步证实了AM相的d波对称性。这些发现为探索 f 电子化合物中的类 AM 相奠定了坚实的理论基础，为未来研究其奇异的磁和电子特性铺平了道路。
