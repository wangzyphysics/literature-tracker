## Abstract
arXiv:2512.15731v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Electromagnetic waves propagating through vacuum can polarize virtual electron-positron pairs; this polarization, in turn, nonlinearly modifies their propagation. A semi-classical nonlinear wave equation describing the propagation is derived from the Euler--Heisenberg Lagrangian density, which captures vacuum polarization effects up to the one-loop level. Here, we present a reduced-action-integral approach that enables rapid modeling of nonlinear phenomena arising from the Euler--Heisenberg Lagrangian. Application of the variational principle to the reduced action provides equations of motion for familiar light-pulse parameters, such as spot size, phase, polarization, and phase-front curvature, without requiring full-field simulations. Three examples demonstrate the utility of the approach: phase modulation, birefringence, and frequency mixing.

arXiv:2512.15731v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：在真空中传播的电磁波可以使虚拟电子-正电子对极化；这种极化反过来会非线性地改变它们的传播。描述传播的半经典非线性波动方程是从欧拉-海森堡拉格朗日密度导出的，它捕获了高达单环水平的真空偏振效应。在这里，我们提出了一种简化作用积分方法，可以对欧拉-海森堡拉格朗日产生的非线性现象进行快速建模。将变分原理应用于简化作用，可以为熟悉的光脉冲参数（例如光斑尺寸、相位、偏振和相前曲率）提供运动方程，而无需进行全场模拟。三个例子展示了该方法的实用性：相位调制、双折射和混频。
