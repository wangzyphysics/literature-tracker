## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22835v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Interest in planar Hall effect (PHE) sensors has re-emerged in recent years due to their promising potential for a wide range of applications, particularly in biotechnology. Sensor sensitivity can be enhanced by lowering the effective anisotropy field; however, this favors magnetic domain formation during magnetization reversal, leading to hysteretic responses. Therefore, precise control of magnetic anisotropy and magnetization reversal is essential to balance sensitivity and stability in PHE sensors. In this work, we investigate the magnetic anisotropy and magnetization reversal mechanisms of Ni-Fe- and Co-Fe-based multilayers grown on various metallic buffer layers and deposited with and without an external magnetic field, in order to evaluate the effects of the buffer layers and field-assisted deposition on the resulting magnetic anisotropy. NiFe films exhibit a dominant uniaxial anisotropy mainly determined by the applied field during growth, with an anisotropy constant of approximately $3,\mathrm{kerg,cm^{-3}}$, largely independent of the buffer layer. In contrast, the magnetic anisotropy of CoFe films is dominated by the buffer layer, resulting in a biaxial magnetic response. In particular, Ag-buffered films deposited under an external magnetic field exhibit a biaxial anisotropy with values up to $14.88,\mathrm{kerg,cm^{-3}}$. The magnetization reversal mechanism of each system was deduced from the analysis of the angular dependence of the coercive field.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22835v1 公告类型：新 摘要：近年来，人们对平面霍尔效应 (PHE) 传感器的兴趣重新兴起，因为它们具有广泛的应用前景，特别是在生物技术领域。通过降低有效各向异性场可以提高传感器灵敏度；然而，这有利于磁化反转期间磁畴的形成，导致磁滞响应。因此，精确控制磁各向异性和磁化反转对于平衡 PHE 传感器的灵敏度和稳定性至关重要。在这项工作中，我们研究了在各种金属缓冲层上生长并在有和没有外部磁场的情况下沉积的 Ni-Fe 和 Co-Fe 基多层膜的磁各向异性和磁化反转机制，以评估缓冲层和场辅助沉积对所产生的磁各向异性的影响。 NiFe 薄膜表现出主要由生长过程中施加的场决定的主要单轴各向异性，各向异性常数约为 3,\mathrm{kerg,cm^{-3}}$，很大程度上与缓冲层无关。相比之下，CoFe 薄膜的磁各向异性主要由缓冲层决定，从而产生双轴磁响应。特别是，在外部磁场下沉积的银缓冲薄膜表现出双轴各向异性，其值高达 14.88,\mathrm{kerg,cm^{-3}}$。通过分析矫顽场的角度依赖性，推导出每个系统的磁化反转机制。
