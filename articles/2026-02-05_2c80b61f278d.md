## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04732v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Active adaptive matter has attracted considerable interest due to its rich, largely unexplained dynamics and its relevance to a wide range of synthetic and biological materials. An important subclass of such systems consists of active particles that can remodel the network in which they move. Here, we introduce a minimal yet versatile model of active particles moving on an adjustable network. In this model, particles undergo discrete run-and-tumble motion along the links of a triangular lattice and leave behind a trail of temporarily blocked links. These closed links cannot be traversed by other particles and reopen only after a characteristic healing time. The resulting trail-mediated blocking mechanism is fundamentally distinct from more familiar interactions such as excluded-volume effects. In the high-persistence limit, we find a qualitative contrast between the two mechanisms: while steric blocking leads to reduced diffusivity with increasing persistence, trail-induced blocking causes diffusivity to increase monotonically. We characterize this fundamental difference and the associated, unexpected transport properties, and discuss potential applications of our findings.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04732v1 公告类型：新 摘要：主动自适应物质因其丰富的、很大程度上无法解释的动力学及其与广泛的合成和生物材料的相关性而引起了极大的兴趣。此类系统的一个重要子类由活性粒子组成，这些粒子可以重塑它们移动的网络。在这里，我们介绍了在可调节网络上移动的活性粒子的最小但通用的模型。在这个模型中，粒子沿着三角形晶格的链接经历离散的奔跑和翻滚运动，并留下暂时阻塞的链接的痕迹。这些关闭的链接不能被其他粒子遍历，并且仅在特征愈合时间后重新打开。由此产生的线索介导的阻断机制从根本上不同于更熟悉的相互作用，例如排除体积效应。在高持久性极限下，我们发现两种机制之间存在定性对比：虽然空间阻塞导致扩散率随着持久性的增加而降低，但尾迹引起的阻塞导致扩散率单调增加。我们描述了这种根本差异以及相关的、意想不到的传输特性，并讨论了我们的研究结果的潜在应用。
