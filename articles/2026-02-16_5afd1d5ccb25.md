## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12673v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The fast ignition paradigm for inertial confinement fusion (ICF) allows for extremely high gains but requires fuel to be heated very quickly to outpace hotspot disassembly and energy losses. This demands lasers with high power and intensity, posing engineering challenges that have called into question the fundamental practicality of fast ignition. Magnetized liner inertial fusion (MagLIF) circumvents these problems through its large-aspect-ratio cylindrical geometry and strong axial magnetic fields that allow for ignition at lower areal densities. Furthermore, MagLIF's large aspect ratio and higher yields relax other constraints on energy deposition and repetition rate while its axial magnetic fields can be used to collimate ignitor electrons and thereby increase allowed standoff distance and save on ignitor energy. This tremendous overall relaxation of the engineering constraints that have historically limited the practicality of fast ignition suggests that the paradigm may be considerably more viable in a MagLIF context.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12673v1 公告类型：新 摘要：惯性约束聚变 (ICF) 的快速点火范例可实现极高的增益，但需要非常快速地加热燃料以超过热点分解和能量损失。这需要具有高功率和高强度的激光器，带来了工程挑战，使人们对快速点火的基本实用性产生了质疑。磁化线性惯性聚变（MagLIF）通过其大纵横比的圆柱形几何形状和强轴向磁场（允许在较低的面密度下点火）避免了这些问题。此外，MagLIF 的大纵横比和更高的产量放宽了对能量沉积和重复率的其他限制，同时其轴向磁场可用于准直点火器电子，从而增加允许的隔离距离并节省点火器能量。历史上限制快速点火实用性的工程约束的全面放松表明，该范例在 MagLIF 环境中可能更加可行。
