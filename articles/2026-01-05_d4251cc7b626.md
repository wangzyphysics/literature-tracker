## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00363v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Dynamic and stable magnetic textures offer a powerful platform for controlling magnon states in the broader context of spin electronics. In this work, we uncover a novel class of dynamical, crystal-like localization patterns in real space, arising from the hybridization of magnons with topologically non-trivial spin textures that possess helicity as an internal degree of freedom. By tuning the magnon wavelength to match the size of these textures, specifically, atomic-scale skyrmions in centrosymmetric frustrated magnets, we achieve strong interference effects. This leads to the emergence of magnon superlattices, shaped by the internal skyrmion structure and the underlying Mexican-hat magnon dispersion. Furthermore, helicity-driven nonlinear dynamics give rise to dispersive magnon bands with nontrivial Chern numbers within the first magnon gap. These findings provide fundamental insights into magnon behavior in complex spin environments and establish frustrated magnets as a versatile platform for manipulating spin excitations at the atomic scale.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00363v1 公告类型：新 摘要：动态且稳定的磁性纹理为在更广泛的自旋电子学背景下控制磁振子态提供了强大的平台。在这项工作中，我们发现了现实空间中一类新颖的动态、类似晶体的定位模式，这种模式是由磁振子与拓扑上非平凡的自旋纹理的杂交产生的，这些自旋纹理具有螺旋性作为内部自由度。通过调整磁振子波长以匹配这些纹理的大小，特别是中心对称受挫磁体中的原子级斯格明子，我们实现了强烈的干涉效应。这导致了磁振子超晶格的出现，其由内部斯格明子结构和底层的墨西哥帽磁振子色散形成。此外，螺旋驱动的非线性动力学在第一个磁振子间隙内产生具有非平凡陈数的色散磁振子带。这些发现为复杂自旋环境中的磁振子行为提供了基本见解，并将受挫磁体建立为在原子尺度上操纵自旋激发的多功能平台。
