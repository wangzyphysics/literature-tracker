## Abstract
Functional coatings based on amphiphilic polymer co-networks (ACNs) offer a versatile alternative to hydrogel coatings in biomedical research, providing enhanced mechanical stability and a broad range of tunable properties. This study systematically explores ACN thin films composed of star-shaped poly(ε-caprolactone) (PCL) and poly(ethylene glycol) (PEG) with varying PEG content to identify coatings with optimized amphiphilic balance for cell adhesion. Films with low PEG content (9 and 15 wt-%) promote significant protein adsorption and cell attachment, whereas PEG-rich films (38 and 47 wt-%) exhibit negligible protein adsorption and effectively inhibit cell adhesion. An ultrathin decellularized and digested extracellular matrix (dECM) coating was used to restore cell adhesiveness on PEG-rich ACN films while preserving their intrinsic material characteristics. Fibroblast studies showed that stiffness and surface roughness modulate cellular metabolic activity but do not affect initial adhesion. Time-dependent cell numbers on the samples reached levels comparable to or exceeding those on tissue culture polystyrene controls. Drug-proxy uptake depended strongly on network composition, with the highest uptake of Lucifer Yellow in PCL10_PEG10 films (~180 ng cm-2) and markedly higher uptake of Nile Red (~10 µg cm-2). These findings demonstrate the potential of ACNs as versatile functional materials for biointerface engineering in the biomedical field.

## 摘要（中文）
基于两亲聚合物共网络 (ACN) 的功能涂层为生物医学研究中的水凝胶涂层提供了一种多功能替代品，可提供增强的机械稳定性和广泛的可调性能。本研究系统地探索了由不同 PEG 含量的星形聚（ε-己内酯）（PCL）和聚（乙二醇）（PEG）组成的 ACN 薄膜，以确定具有优化细胞粘附两亲平衡的涂层。低 PEG 含量（9 和 15 wt-%）的薄膜可促进显着的蛋白质吸附和细胞粘附，而富含 PEG 的薄膜（38 和 47 wt-%）则表现出可忽略不计的蛋白质吸附并有效抑制细胞粘附。使用超薄脱细胞和消化的细胞外基质 (dECM) 涂层来恢复富含 PEG 的 ACN 薄膜上的细胞粘附性，同时保留其固有的材料特性。成纤维细胞研究表明，硬度和表面粗糙度可调节细胞代谢活动，但不影响初始粘附。样品上随时间变化的细胞数量达到与组织培养聚苯乙烯对照相当或超过的水平。药物代理的摄取很大程度上取决于网络组成，PCL10_PEG10 薄膜中荧光黄的摄取量最高（~180 ng cm-2），尼罗红的摄取量明显更高（~10 µg cm-2）。这些发现证明了乙腈作为生物医学领域生物界面工程的多功能功能材料的潜力。
