## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09331v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Oscillations are ubiquitous wave phenomena in physical systems ranging from electromagnetic and acoustic to gravitational waves. The behavior of finite-size systems is traditionally understood to be governed by fundamental oscillatory modes arising from bulk physics and boundary conditions. A paradigmatic example is the particle-in-a-box model introduced with the advent of quantum mechanics, in which confinement leads to discrete resonances and quantized energy levels. Such quantization underpins phenomena including semiconductor quantum dots, where electronic waves are confined in all three spatial dimensions, producing standing-wave modes analogous to the vibrational states of a guitar string. These modes are characterized by integer quantum numbers corresponding to the number of envelope oscillations fitting within the cavity. Recently, counter-intuitive modes have been observed in finite systems with Dirac dispersion, including states that do not oscillate spatially, yet a general theoretical framework for modes in such cavities has been lacking. Here, we discover the phenomenon of arbitrary fractional quantization in wave physics and show that, in finite-size boxes with linear dispersion, the quantum number need not be an integer but can take any real value, including zero. Using Bloch theory in a Dirac-cone photonic crystal with a controllable fractional number of unit cells at its boundaries, we demonstrate continuous control of the cavity-mode envelope wavenumber. We introduce a unified nomenclature for these unconventional modes and derive their corresponding wavefunctions. These counter-intuitive states in open Dirac potentials challenge conventional notions of quantization and open new avenues across wave physics.

arXiv:2601.09331v1 公告类型：新 摘要：振荡是物理系统中普遍存在的波现象，从电磁波、声波到引力波。传统上，有限尺寸系统的行为被理解为受体物理和边界条件产生的基本振荡模式的控制。一个典型的例子是随着量子力学的出现而引入的盒中粒子模型，其中限制导致离散共振和量子化能级。这种量子化是包括半导体量子点在内的现象的基础，其中电子波被限制在所有三个空间维度中，产生类似于吉他弦振动状态的驻波模式。这些模式的特征是整数量子数，对应于腔内安装的包络振荡的数量。最近，在具有狄拉克色散的有限系统中观察到了反直觉模式，包括不空间振荡的状态，但此类空腔中模式的通用理论框架一直缺乏。在这里，我们发现了波动物理学中的任意分数量化现象，并表明，在具有线性色散的有限尺寸盒子中，量子数不必是整数，而是可以取任何实值，包括零。在狄拉克锥光子晶体中使用布洛赫理论，其边界处具有可控的晶胞分数，我们证明了腔模包络波数的连续控制。我们为这些非常规模式引入了统一的术语，并推导了它们相应的波函数。开放狄拉克势中的这些反直觉状态挑战了传统的量子化概念，并开辟了波物理学的新途径。
