# Improvement of a focused ion beam fabricated diamond pillar for scanning ensemble nitrogen-vacancy magnetometry probe using an ultrapure diamond

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07188v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Scanning diamond nitrogen-vacancy probe microscopy (SNVM) is an important tool for studying nanoscale condensed-matter phenomena. Ga$^+$-ion-focused-ion-beam (FIB) milling has been introduced as an available method for fabricating SNVM, while the probe diameter is limited to a few micrometers due to the Ga$^+$-induced damage. We report a method for improving the SNVM probes' quality, with an 800-nm diameter probe of ultrapure diamond, through polyvinyl alcohol and Pt/Pd capping, followed by UV/ozone exposure. The effectiveness of the method is confirmed by NVs' spin-coherence property measurements and magnetic domain structure imaging with a few-hundred-nanometer resolution and a 6.7 $\mu$T/Hz$^{1/2}$ sensitivity.

arXiv:2601.07188v1 公告类型：新 摘要：扫描金刚石氮空位探针显微镜（SNVM）是研究纳米级凝聚态现象的重要工具。 Ga$^+$离子聚焦离子束（FIB）铣削已被引入作为制造SNVM的可用方法，而由于Ga$^+$引起的损坏，探针直径被限制在几微米。我们报告了一种提高 SNVM 探针质量的方法，使用直径为 800 nm 的超纯金刚石探针，通过聚乙烯醇和 Pt/Pd 封盖，然后进行 UV/臭氧暴露。该方法的有效性通过NVs的自旋相干特性测量和具有数百纳米分辨率和6.7 $\mu$T/Hz$^{1/2}$灵敏度的磁域结构成像得到证实。
