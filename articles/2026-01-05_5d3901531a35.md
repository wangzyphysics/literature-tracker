## Abstract
Cellulose-based fluorescent materials enjoy advantages such as excellent biocompatibility, biodegradability, and environmental friendliness, making them potentially useful for chemical sensing, bioimaging, and information encryption. However, traditional cellulose-based fluorescent materials face issues of single functionality and aggregation-caused quenching, making the development of multifunctional fluorescent cellulose materials applicable in aggregated states desirable but challenging. Herein, we innovatively endowed fluorescent cellulose materials with an aggregation-induced emission characteristic through hydroxyl-yne click reaction. The developed ECTPEs exhibit breakthrough performance with high transparency (>95%), full ultraviolet (UV)-shielding efficiency (>99.9%), accompanied by fluorescence characteristics, thereby achieving simultaneous UV blocking and indicating functions for UV-sensitive materials. Additionally, ECTPEs demonstrate grafting ratio-dependent multicolor fluorescence, enabling their application in high-resolution fluorescent QR codes. The further development of acid-responsive ECTPE-MTPAPs through secondary grafting realizes fluorescence color switching under acidic stimulation and enables the creation of dynamic multicolor coding devices with enhanced data storage density and sophisticated encryption capabilities. Significantly, an amine exchange strategy based on dynamic enol ether bonds effectively resolves the inherent conflict between material functionalization and degradability. This work provides an innovative methodology for developing reversible multifunctional fluorescent cellulose materials, offering crucial guidance for advancing the development of sustainable fluorescent materials.

## 摘要（中文）
纤维素基荧光材料具有优异的生物相容性、生物可降解性和环境友好性等优点，使其在化学传感、生物成像和信息加密方面具有潜在的用途。然而，传统的纤维素基荧光材料面临功能单一和聚集引起的猝灭问题，使得开发适用于聚集态的多功能荧光纤维素材料是可取的但具有挑战性。在此，我们创新性地通过羟基炔点击反应赋予荧光纤维素材料以聚集诱导的发射特性。所开发的ECTPE具有突破性的性能，具有高透明度（>95%）、全紫外线（UV）屏蔽效率（>99.9%），并具有荧光特性，从而实现对紫外敏感材料同时实现紫外阻挡和指示功能。此外，ECTPE 表现出与接枝率相关的多色荧光，使其能够在高分辨率荧光 QR 码中应用。通过二次接枝进一步开发酸响应型 ECTPE-MTPAP，实现了酸性刺激下的荧光颜色切换，并能够创建具有增强的数据存储密度和复杂的加密功能的动态多色编码装置。值得注意的是，基于动态烯醇醚键的胺交换策略有效解决了材料功能化和可降解性之间的固有冲突。这项工作为开发可逆多功能荧光纤维素材料提供了一种创新方法，为推进可持续荧光材料的开发提供了重要指导。
