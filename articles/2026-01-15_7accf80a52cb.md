## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09585v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Compared to other stable isotopes of $\rm{Yb}^+$, $^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ has a richer hyperfine structure, which leads to more favorable clock transitions, spectroscopic techniques for probing new physics, and more sophisticated quantum computing architectures. However, to date, its electronic spectrum remains poorly characterized. Here, we report on efficient laser cooling, state preparation, and detection of a single trapped $^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ ion. The previously unobserved $^2\!S_{1/2} \rightarrow {}^2\!D_{3/2}$ electric quadrupole transition at 436 nm is coherently excited, and the isotope shift between $^{171}\rm{Yb}^+$ and $^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ on this transition is determined with an uncertainty of 1.4 Hz. Using microwave spectroscopy, we resolve the hyperfine structure (HFS) of the ${}^2\!D_{3/2}$ state with a relative uncertainty below $10^{-8}$. From the HFS measurement data, we infer for ${}^{173}$Yb a nuclear magnetic octupole moment $\Omega = -0.062(8)\,({\rm b} \times \mu_N)$ with uncertainty reduced by more than 2 orders of magnitude compared to previous studies. The data also allow us to determine hyperfine anomalies for the ${}^2\!S_{1/2}$ and ${}^2\!D_{3/2}$ states.

arXiv:2601.09585v1 公告类型：新 摘要：与 $\rm{Yb}^+$ 的其他稳定同位素相比，$^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ 具有更丰富的超精细结构，从而带来更有利的时钟跃迁、用于探测新物理的光谱技术以及更复杂的量子计算架构。然而，迄今为止，其电子频谱的特征仍然很差。在这里，我们报告了有效的激光冷却、状态准备和单个捕获的 $^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ 离子的检测。先前未观察到的 436 nm 处的 $^2\!S_{1/2} \rightarrow {}^2\!D_{3/2}$ 电四极跃迁被相干激发，并且该跃迁上 $^{171}\rm{Yb}^+$ 和 $^{173}\rm{Yb}^+$ 之间的同位素位移由 1.4 Hz 的不确定性确定。使用微波光谱，我们解析了 ${}^2\!D_{3/2}$ 态的超精细结构 (HFS)，相对不确定度低于 $10^{-8}$。根据HFS测量数据，我们推断${}^{173}$Yb的核磁八极矩$\Omega = -0.062(8)\,({\rm b} \times \mu_N)$，与之前的研究相比，不确定性降低了2个数量级以上。这些数据还使我们能够确定 ${}^2\!S_{1/2}$ 和 ${}^2\!D_{3/2}$ 状态的超精细异常。
