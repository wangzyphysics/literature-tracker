## Abstract
Nanohoops with radial π-conjugation, such as cycloparaphenylenes (CPPs), exhibit intrinsically large Stokes shifts arising from excited-state symmetry breaking. However, their integration into functional electroluminescent platforms has remained virtually unexplored. Herein, we report the synthesis of a three-fold symmetric naphthalenediimide (NDI)-embedded CPP analogue and its application in inorganic powder electroluminescence (iEL) devices. The incorporation of electron-accepting NDI units extends the emission into the red region (λem = 632 nm, ΦF = 22%, in CHCl₃) with a large Stokes shift of 138 nm (4420 cm⁻¹), which is advantageous for suppressing self-absorption. AC field-driven iEL devices achieved reddish-purple emission via a one-step spectral down-conversion from ZnS emission. Notably, the nanohoop emitter is integrated into the device via a simple solution-coating process by mixing 1 wt% of the dye with ZnS, and the resulting devices retained stable emission even when cut, and mechanically reconfigured into “origami-” and “kirigami-” type architectures, demonstrating a level of mechanical flexibility and low-cost processability that is challenging to achieve with conventional OLEDs. This work establishes a design principle for red-emissive nanohoops and highlights the potential of radially π-conjugated macrocycles as promising red emitters for next-generation EL technologies with mechanical reconfigurability.

## 摘要（中文）
具有径向 π 共轭的纳米环，例如环对亚苯基 (CPP)，由于激发态对称性破缺而表现出本质上较大的斯托克斯位移。然而，它们与功能性电致发光平台的集成实际上尚未被探索。在此，我们报道了三重对称萘二亚胺（NDI）嵌入的CPP类似物的合成及其在无机粉末电致发光（iEL）器件中的应用。电子接受NDI单元的结合将发射扩展到红色区域（λem = 632 nm，ΦF = 22%，在CHCl₃中），具有138 nm（4420 cm -1）的大斯托克斯位移，这有利于抑制自吸收。交流场驱动的 iEL 器件通过 ZnS 发射的一步光谱下转换实现了红紫色发射。值得注意的是，通过将 1 wt% 的染料与 ZnS 混合，通过简单的溶液涂覆工艺将纳米环发射器集成到器件中，所得器件即使在切割时也能保持稳定的发射，并机械地重新配置为“折纸”和“剪纸”型架构，展示了传统 OLED 难以实现的机械灵活性和低成本可加工性。这项工作建立了红光发射纳米环的设计原理，并强调了径向 π 共轭大环作为具有机械可重构性的下一代 EL 技术有前途的红光发射器的潜力。
