## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01186v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In an effort to compete with the brain's efficiency at processing information, neuromorphic hardware combines artificial synapses and neurons using mixed-signal circuits and emerging memories. In ferroelectric resistive weights, the strength of the synaptic connection between two neurons is stored in the device conductance. During learning, programming pulses are applied to the synaptic weight, which reconfigures the ferroelectric domains and adjusts the conductance. One strategy to lower the energy cost during the training phase is to lower the duration of the programming pulses. However, the latter cannot be shorter than the self-loading time of the resistive weights, limited by intrinsic parasitics in the circuits. In this work, ferroelectric resistive weights are fabricated using a process compatible with CMOS Back-End-Of-Line integration, based on hafnia/zirconia nanolaminates. By laterally scaling the device area under 100 $\mu$m$^2$, the self-loading time becomes sufficiently short to enable 20 ns programming, which corresponds to a maximum of 3 picoJoules per pulse. Further, in this work, the weight update rule with 20 ns pulses is experimentally measured not only for different amplitudes but also for different initial conductance states. We find that the final weight is determined by the pulse amplitude, independent of the initial weight value.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01186v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：为了与大脑处理信息的效率竞争，神经形态硬件使用混合信号电路和新兴记忆将人工突触和神经元结合起来。在铁电电阻权重中，两个神经元之间的突触连接强度存储在设备电导中。在学习过程中，编程脉冲被应用于突触权重，从而重新配置铁电域并调整电导。在训练阶段降低能量成本的一种策略是缩短编程脉冲的持续时间。然而，受电路固有寄生效应的限制，后者不能短于电阻重量的自加载时间。在这项工作中，铁电电阻重量是使用与 CMOS 后端集成兼容的工艺制造的，基于氧化铪/氧化锆纳米层压板。通过将器件面积横向缩放到 100 $\mu$m$^2$ 以下，自加载时间变得足够短，可以实现 20 ns 编程，这对应于每个脉冲最大 3 picoJoules。此外，在这项工作中，不仅针对不同的幅度，而且针对不同的初始电导状态，对 20 ns 脉冲的权重更新规则进行了实验测量。我们发现最终重量由脉冲幅度决定，与初始重量值无关。
