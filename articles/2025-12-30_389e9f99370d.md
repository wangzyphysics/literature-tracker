## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22891v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report the influence of Ce$^{3+}$ substitution on the magnetic structures and phonon dynamics in SmCrO$_3$ perovskites. Magnetic landscapes are spanned by long-range canted anti-ferromagnetism, AFM with Neel temperatures $\sim$196 K accompanied by spin-reorientation transitions, T$_{SRPT}$ at 42 K. In Sm$_{0.9}$Ce$_{0.1}$CrO$_3$ (SCCO), Ce$^{3+}$ substitution at Sm$^{3+}$ sites transform the weak ferromagnetic (FM) $\Gamma_4$ state into robust AFM $\Gamma_1$ configuration through a gradual crossover. Such coexistence of magnetic spin configurations $\Gamma_1$(AFM) to $\Gamma_4$ (WFM) results in the enhanced high coercive field and a pronounced exchange bias-field, $H_{\mathrm{EB}}$ $\sim$ 2 kOe. Spin-only driven magneto-crystalline anisotropy of Cr$^{3+}$ and spin-orbit driven magnetic moment in Sm$^{3+}$, and Ce$^{3+}$ exhibits spin-phonon coupling through $A_{1g}(6)$ mode in SCCO are consistent with the temperature dependent spectral features of the isostructural magnetic systems and quite significant in SCCO which is in accordance with the higher structural distortion in SCCO. These results demonstrate that site-specific R$^{3+}$ substitution modulates lattice distortions, spin-phonon coupling, and spin-orbit interactions, offering pathways to optimize perovskites for diverse spintronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22891v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报道了 Ce$^{3+}$ 取代对 SmCrO$_3$ 钙钛矿中磁性结构和声子动力学的影响。磁景观由远程倾斜反铁磁性、AFM 跨越，尼尔温度 $\sim$196 K 伴随自旋重定向跃迁，T$_{SRPT}$ 为 42 K。在 Sm$_{0.9}$Ce$_{0.1}$CrO$_3$ (SCCO) 中，Sm$^{3+}$ 位点上的 Ce$^{3+}$ 替代会改变弱铁磁(FM) $\Gamma_4$ 通过逐渐交叉进入稳健的 AFM $\Gamma_1$ 配置。磁自旋构型 $\Gamma_1$(AFM) 到 $\Gamma_4$ (WFM) 的共存导致增强的高矫顽场和明显的交换偏置场，$H_{\mathrm{EB}}$ $\sim$ 2 kOe。 Cr$^{3+}$ 中仅自旋驱动的磁晶各向异性和 Sm$^{3+}$ 中的自旋轨道驱动磁矩和 Ce$^{3+}$ 在 SCCO 中通过 $A_{1g}(6)$ 模式表现出自旋声子耦合，与同结构磁系统的温度相关光谱特征一致，并且在 SCCO 中非常显着，这与 SCCO 中较高的结构畸变一致。这些结果表明，位点特异性 R$^{3+}$ 取代可调节晶格畸变、自旋声子耦合和自旋轨道相互作用，为各种自旋电子应用优化钙钛矿提供途径。
