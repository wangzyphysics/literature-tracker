## Abstract
Author(s): Franziska Kühn, Matthias R. Schweizer, Tamara Azevedo, Vitaliy I. Vasyuchka, Georg von Freymann, Victor S. L'vov, Burkard Hillebrands, and Alexander A. Serga In this combined theoretical and experimental study, the authors investigate angle-dependent parametric pumping of magnons in yttrium iron garnet. They demonstrate that the direction of the magnetic field governs magnon scattering pathways via kinetic instability. Parallel pumping lowers the threshold, whereas perpendicular pumping increases the population near the spectral minimum, thereby optimizing the flux of magnons into the Bose-Einstein condensate. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 014409] Published Thu Jan 08, 2026

作者：Franziska Kühn、Matthias R. Schweizer、Tamara Azevedo、Vitaliy I. Vasyuchka、Georg von Freymann、Victor S. L'vov、Burkard Hillebrands 和 Alexander A. Serga 在这项理论与实验相结合的研究中，作者研究了钇铁石榴石中磁振子的角度相关参数泵浦。他们证明磁场方向通过动力学不稳定性控制磁振子散射路径。并行泵浦降低了阈值，而垂直泵浦则增加了光谱最小值附近的粒子数，从而优化了进入玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体的磁振子通量。 [物理。 Rev. B 113, 014409] 2026 年 1 月 8 日星期四发布
