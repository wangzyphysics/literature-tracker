## Abstract
Author(s): Charly Beulenkamp, Krzysztof P. Zamarski, Robert C. Bird, C. Ruth Le Sueur, Jeremy M. Hutson, Manuele Landini, and Hanns-Christoph Nägerl The authors report the creation of an ultracold gas of bosonic 39 K 133 Cs molecules via magnetoassociation across an interspecies Feshbach resonance, providing the necessary starting conditions for the creation of ground-state molecules. They also perform Feshbach spectroscopy to observe several new resonances. [Phys. Rev. A 112, 062821] Published Mon Dec 22, 2025
