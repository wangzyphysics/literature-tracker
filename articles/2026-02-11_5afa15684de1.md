## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09614v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The local polarization of the electromagnetic field plays a crucial role in the interaction of light with spin- and valley-polarized quantum sources. Unlike free-space electromagnetic waves, whose polarization degeneracy enables flexible polarization manipulation, planar integrated optical structures lack such degree of freedom owing to intrinsic structural anisotropy. Here, we propose a planar optical ring resonator based on a subwavelength grating waveguide that supports two quasi-degenerate modes. We demonstrate that coupling of these modes in the ring resonator leads to the formation of the resonances with a predominant direction of electric-field rotation in the vicinity of the resonator, resulting in the non-zero transverse optical spin. The average degree of circular polarization in the proposed structures reaches values of up to 70%. The theoretical predictions are corroborated by experimental validation in the microwave spectral range. Our findings suggest a viable route toward realization of on-chip optical spintronic and valleytronic interfaces.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09614v1 公告类型：新 摘要：电磁场的局部偏振在光与自旋偏振和谷偏振量子源的相互作用中起着至关重要的作用。与自由空间电磁波不同，自由空间电磁波的偏振简并性能够实现灵活的偏振操纵，而平面集成光学结构由于固有的结构各向异性而缺乏这种自由度。在这里，我们提出了一种基于亚波长光栅波导的平面光学环形谐振器，支持两种准简并模式。我们证明，这些模式在环形谐振器中的耦合导致在谐振器附近形成具有电场旋转主要方向的谐振，从而产生非零横向光学自旋。所提出的结构中的平均圆偏振度达到高达 70% 的值。理论预测通过微波光谱范围内的实验验证得到证实。我们的研究结果提出了实现片上光学自旋电子和谷电子接口的可行途径。
