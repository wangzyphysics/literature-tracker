## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08904v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Stepwise signals are ubiquitous in single-molecule detections, where abrupt changes in signal levels typically correspond to molecular conformational changes or state transitions. However, these features are inevitably obscured by noise, leading to uncertainty in estimating both signal levels and transition points. Traditional frequency-domain filtering is ineffective for denoising stepwise signals, as edge-related high-frequency components strongly overlap with noise. Although Hidden Markov Model-based approaches are widely used, they rely on stationarity assumptions and are not specifically designed for signal denoising. Here, we propose a diffusion model-based algorithm for stepwise signal denoising, named the Stepwise Signal Diffusion Model (SSDM). During training, SSDM learns the statistical structure of stepwise signals via a forward diffusion process that progressively adds noise. In the following reverse process, the model reconstructs clean signals from noisy observations, integrating a multi-scale convolutional network with an attention mechanism. Training data are generated by simulating stepwise signals through a Markov process with additive Gaussian noise. Across a broad range of signal-to-noise ratios, SSDM consistently outperforms traditional methods in both signal level reconstruction and transition point detection. Its effectiveness is further demonstrated on experimental data from single-molecule Forster Resonance Energy Transfer and nanopore DNA translocation measurements. Overall, SSDM provides a general and robust framework for recovering stepwise signals in various single-molecule detections and other physical systems exhibiting discrete state transitions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08904v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：逐步信号在单分子检测中普遍存在，其中信号水平的突然变化通常对应于分子构象变化或状态转换。然而，这些特征不可避免地会被噪声掩盖，从而导致估计信号电平和转变点的不确定性。传统的频域滤波对于逐步信号去噪是无效的，因为与边缘相关的高频分量与噪声强烈重叠。尽管基于隐马尔可夫模型的方法被广泛使用，但它们依赖于平稳性假设，并且并不是专门为信号去噪而设计的。在这里，我们提出了一种基于扩散模型的逐步信号去噪算法，称为逐步信号扩散模型（SSDM）。在训练过程中，SSDM 通过逐步添加噪声的前向扩散过程来学习逐步信号的统计结构。在接下来的反向过程中，该模型从噪声观测中重建干净的信号，将多尺度卷积网络与注意力机制相结合。训练数据是通过使用加性高斯噪声的马尔可夫过程模拟逐步信号来生成的。在广泛的信噪比范围内，SSDM 在信号电平重建和转变点检测方面始终优于传统方法。单分子福斯特共振能量转移和纳米孔 DNA 易位测量的实验数据进一步证明了其有效性。总体而言，SSDM 提供了一个通用且强大的框架，用于恢复各种单分子检测和其他表现离散状态转换的物理系统中的逐步信号。
