## Abstract
In-situ soft X-ray spectroscopy provides direct insight into the electronic structure of electrocatalysts under realistic reaction conditions but remains technically challenging due to the need to combine aqueous electrochemistry with ultra-high-vacuum detection. Here we present a mesoporous carbon–membrane working electrode assembly (WEA) that enables window-free in-situ XPS and NEXAFS measurements during electrochemical reactions. The design integrates a Nafion™ proton-exchange membrane with a mesoporous carbon–ionomer contact layer and a thin IrOₓ catalyst layer, providing continuous electronic and protonic pathways and stable hydration through the membrane. By tuning the chamber water vapor pressure to 8 mbar, the WEA maintains a nanometer-thin water layer sufficient for the oxygen evolution reaction (OER) while preserving photoelectron detection efficiency. A robust peristaltic pumping system and closed-loop alumina-bed water vapor dosing maintain steady-state hydration at 6–10 mbar with <±0.1 mbar variation, enabling reproducible in-siut spectra over extended periods. In-situ Ir 4f and O 1s XPS reveal reversible oxidation of Ir³⁺/Ir⁴⁺ to Ir⁴⁺/Ir⁵⁺ and dynamic changes in hydroxyl and lattice oxygen species, while O K-edge NEXAFS identify the formation of μ₂–O and μ₁–O species at OER potential, corresponding to OER-active intermediates. The WEA thus provides a quantitative, window-free platform for probing electrochemical interfaces under near-ambient conditions and establishes a general methodology for in-situ soft X-ray studies of functional electrocatalysts, closely resembling the architecture and operation of industrial membrane-based water electrolysers. This approach establishes a reliable methodology for coupling electrochemistry with the element specific soft X-ray spectroscopy under realistic reaction conditions.

## 摘要（中文）
原位软 X 射线光谱可以直接洞察现实反应条件下电催化剂的电子结构，但由于需要将水相电化学与超高真空检测相结合，因此在技术上仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了一种介孔碳膜工作电极组件（WEA），可以在电化学反应过程中进行无窗原位 XPS 和 NEXAFS 测量。该设计将 Nafion™ 质子交换膜与介孔碳离聚物接触层和薄 IrOₓ 催化剂层集成在一起，提供连续的电子和质子路径以及通过膜的稳定水合作用。通过将腔室水蒸气压力调整至 8 mbar，WEA 可以维持足以进行析氧反应 (OER) 的纳米薄水层，同时保持光电子检测效率。强大的蠕动泵系统和闭环氧化铝床水蒸气计量可将稳态水合作用保持在 6-10 mbar，变化 <±0.1 mbar，从而能够在较长时间内实现可重复的原位光谱。原位 Ir 4f 和 O 1s XPS 揭示了 Ir3⁺/Ir⁴⁺ 到 Ir⁴⁺/Ir⁵⁺ 的可逆氧化以及羟基和晶格氧物种的动态变化，而 O K 边缘 NEXAFS 识别了在 OER 电位下 μ2-O 和 μ1-O 物种的形成，对应于 OER 活性中间体。因此，WEA 提供了一个定量、无窗口的平台，用于在近环境条件下探测电化学界面，并建立了功能性电催化剂原位软 X 射线研究的通用方法，与工业膜基水电解槽的架构和操作非常相似。该方法建立了一种在实际反应条件下将电化学与元素特定软 X 射线光谱耦合的可靠方法。
