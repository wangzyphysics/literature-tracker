## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21503v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The triangular Hofstadter-Hubbard model, realizable in moir\'e bilayers, provides a fertile ground for discovering correlated topological states. We investigate this model in the grand canonical ensemble by introducing a fermionic infinite projected entangled simplex state (iPESS) approach, which offers direct access to the stability of the emergent correlated states at the thermodynamic limit. Through numerically optimizing fermionic iPESS, we accurately capture the chiral spin liquid (CSL) phase in the Mott insulating regime, characterized by a uniform chiral order, entanglement spectrum and the appearance of gossamer correlation tails in spin channel. The intermediate-$U$ CSL is separated from the weak-$U$ Chern insulator by a Mott transition at $U_{c_1} \approx 11.5$, signaled by changes in the charge fluctuation and compressibility. Finite-correlation-length scaling of the magnetization reveals a transition into a large-$U$ $120^\circ$ N\'eel phase at $U_{c_2} \approx 22.5$. Remarkably, with finite hole doping $\delta$, we identify a uniform superconducting state with a finite pairing amplitude, whose order parameter displays a nearly universal phase winding across the $U$-$\delta$ phase diagram. Our work demonstrates robust chiral superconductivity in the thermodynamic limit through doping Chern insulator and CSL.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21503v1 公告类型：新 摘要：可在莫尔双层中实现的三角形 Hofstadter-Hubbard 模型为发现相关拓扑状态提供了肥沃的土壤。我们通过引入费米子无限投影纠缠单纯形态（iPESS）方法来研究大正则系综中的该模型，该方法提供了直接访问热力学极限下出现的相关态的稳定性的方法。通过数值优化费米子 iPESS，我们准确地捕获了莫特绝缘区中的手性自旋液体 (CSL) 相，其特征是均匀的手性顺序、纠缠谱和自旋通道中出现的游丝相关尾部。中间 $U$ CSL 通过 $U_{c_1} \approx 11.5$ 处的莫特跃迁与弱 $U$ Chern 绝缘体分开，由电荷波动和压缩性的变化表示。磁化强度的有限相关长度缩放揭示了在 $U_{c_2} \approx 22.5$ 处转变为大 $U$ $120^\circ$ N\'eel 阶段。值得注意的是，通过有限孔掺杂 $\delta$，我们识别出具有有限配对幅度的均匀超导态，其阶数参数显示了 $U$-$\delta$ 相图中几乎通用的相绕组。我们的工作通过掺杂陈绝缘体和 CSL 展示了热力学极限下强大的手性超导性。
