## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14155v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic skyrmions in chiral magnets typically arrange into hexagonal lattices, with their structural order influenced by factors such as temperature, external magnetic fields and geometric constraints. While translational defects in skyrmion lattices such as dislocations have been extensively studied, individual angular defects, or disclinations, remain largely unexplored. Here, we report on the stabilization of five-fold and seven-fold disclinations in pentagon- and heptagon-shaped FeGe nanocrystals created using focused ion beam milling. The magnetic and elastic structures of the disclinated lattices are investigated using Fresnel imaging and off-axis electron holography in a transmission electron microscope. The results are supported by micromagnetic simulations and analytical models based on linear elasticity theory.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14155v1 公告类型：新 摘要：手性磁体中的磁性斯格明子通常排列成六方晶格，其结构顺序受温度、外部磁场和几何约束等因素的影响。虽然斯格明子晶格中的平移缺陷（例如位错）已被广泛研究，但单个角度缺陷或向错仍然很大程度上未被探索。在这里，我们报告了使用聚焦离子束铣削制造的五边形和七边形 FeGe 纳米晶体中五倍和七倍向错的稳定性。在透射电子显微镜中使用菲涅尔成像和离轴电子全息术研究向错晶格的磁性和弹性结构。该结果得到了基于线弹性理论的微磁模拟和分析模型的支持。
