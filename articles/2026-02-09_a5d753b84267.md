## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06235v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Enhancing the conversion efficiency and maximum energy of laser-driven ion beams is a critical challenge for applications in hadron therapy and high-energy density physics. In this work, we present a comprehensive two-dimensional Particle-In-Cell (PIC) simulation study comparing Target Normal Sheath Acceleration (TNSA) from standard flat foils and novel annular sector (C-shaped) targets. Under identical ultra-intense laser irradiation (a0=10, tau=25 fs), the annular sector geometry demonstrates a substantial enhancement in acceleration performance driven by two synergistic mechanisms: electromagnetic cavity confinement and geometric plasma focusing. Our analysis reveals that the target void acts as an optical trap, sustaining oscillating electromagnetic fields for over 300fs via multiple internal reflections. This confinement results in a total laser energy absorption of 49% (compared to 16% for flat targets), which yields a peak electron temperature of 5.1 MeV more than double the 2.2MeV observed in flat targets. Furthermore, phase space diagnostics confirm that ion bunches accelerated from the converging cavity walls superimpose at the geometric center, creating a localized high-density focal spot. Consequently, the annular target increases the proton cut-off energy to 22MeV (vs. 12MeV for flat targets) and boosts Carbon ion energies beyond 60MeV. These findings establish that tailoring target curvature to exploit optical trapping and geometric focusing offers a robust pathway for developing compact, high-efficiency laser-ion sources.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06235v1 公告类型：新 摘要：提高激光驱动离子束的转换效率和最大能量是强子治疗和高能量密度物理应用的关键挑战。在这项工作中，我们提出了一项全面的二维细胞内粒子 (PIC) 模拟研究，比较了标准扁平箔和新型环形扇形（C 形）目标的目标正常鞘加速度 (TNSA)。在相同的超强激光照射下（a0 = 10，tau = 25 fs），环形扇形几何形状显示出由两种协同机制驱动的加速性能的显着增强：电磁腔限制和几何等离子体聚焦。我们的分析表明，目标空洞充当光陷阱，通过多次内反射维持振荡电磁场超过 300fs。这种限制导致总激光能量吸收为 49%（而平面目标为 16%），从而产生 5.1 MeV 的峰值电子温度，是平面目标中观察到的 2.2MeV 的两倍多。此外，相空间诊断证实，离子束从会聚腔壁加速，叠加在几何中心，形成局部高密度焦点。因此，环形靶将质子截止能量提高至 22MeV（而平面靶为 12MeV），并将碳离子能量提高到 60MeV 以上。这些发现表明，定制目标曲率以利用光学捕获和几何聚焦为开发紧凑、高效的激光离子源提供了一条可靠的途径。
