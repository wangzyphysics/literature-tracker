## Abstract
Author(s): Yiwei Bao, Ruo-Yu Liu, Gwenael Giacinti, Hai-Ming Zhang, and Yang Chen GeV gamma-ray emission is frequently observed both at large angular separation from pulsars and are not always centered around these pulsars. Using GPU-acceleration monte-carlo simulations, the authors provide a compelling explanation that the propogation of 100 GeV electrons that produce these gamma-rays in the turbulent interstellar magnetic field both leads to off-center peaks and wide separation, e.g., a gamma-ray mirage. These mirages can help constrain the properties of the interstellar magnetic field. [Phys. Rev. D 112, 123017] Published Fri Dec 05, 2025

作者：Yiwei Bao、Ruo-Yu Liu、Gwenael Giacinti、Hai-Ming Zhang 和 Yang Chen GeV 伽马射线发射经常在与脉冲星的大角距处观察到，并且并不总是以这些脉冲星为中心。作者使用 GPU 加速蒙特卡罗模拟提供了令人信服的解释，即在湍流星际磁场中产生这些伽马射线的 100 GeV 电子的传播会导致偏离中心的峰值和宽间隔，例如伽马射线海市蜃楼。这些海市蜃楼可以帮助限制星际磁场的特性。 [物理。 Rev. D 112, 123017] 2025 年 12 月 5 日星期五发布
