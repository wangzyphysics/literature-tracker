## Abstract
arXiv:2601.02013v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We offer a quantum chemical analysis of mono-halogenated borane molecules using DFT and TD-DFT theories, applying the PBE0/def2-SVPD and B3LYP/6-311+G(d) methods as implemented in ORCA, and explore how solvent effects influence electronic transition properties. The comparable benchmarks are the archetype anti-\ce{B18H22} denoted as (1) against hypothetical halogenated derivatives: 7-F-anti-\ce{B18H21} (2), 4-F-anti-\ce{B18H21} (3), and the recently synthesized 4-Br-anti-\ce{B18H21} (4). The analysis includes an optimization of the ground and first singlet excited states, vibrational frequency analysis, and a comprehensive spectroscopic profile covering IR, Raman, UV-Vis absorption, and emission spectra. The IR spectra of the fluorinated compounds feature a characteristic B-F stretching peak, while the Raman spectra closely resemble the parent molecule. UV-Vis spectral analysis shows a redshift and oscillator strength enhancement for F at position B7, indicating altered electronic properties due to substitution with lighter halogen. Furthermore, solvent effects enhance the probability of electronic transitions. Halogene presence led to a decrease of the energy gap EG(LUMO-HOMO) due to the stabilization of LUMO, which implied a redshift in the emission/absorption wavelength spectra, with the largest EG change at around 14\% occurring for the (4)$^{th}$ benchmark compound.. Notably, all compounds emit light within the visible spectrum, underscoring their potential for optoelectronic applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.02013v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用 DFT 和 TD-DFT 理论，应用 ORCA 中实施的 PBE0/def2-SVPD 和 B3LYP/6-311+G(d) 方法，对单卤代硼烷分子进行量子化学分析，并探索溶剂效应如何影响电子跃迁特性。可比较的基准是原型 anti-\ce{B18H22} 相对于假设的卤化衍生物表示为 (1)：7-F-anti-\ce{B18H21} (2)、4-F-anti-\ce{B18H21} (3) 和最近合成的 4-Br-anti-\ce{B18H21} (4)。该分析包括基态和第一单线激发态的优化、振动频率分析以及涵盖红外、拉曼、紫外-可见吸收和发射光谱的综合光谱曲线。氟化化合物的红外光谱具有特征 B-F 伸缩峰，而拉曼光谱与母体分子非常相似。紫外-可见光谱分析显示 B7 位置 F 的红移和振荡器强度增强，表明由于用较轻的卤素取代而改变了电子特性。此外，溶剂效应提高了电子跃迁的可能性。由于 LUMO 的稳定，卤素的存在导致能隙 EG(LUMO-HOMO) 减小，这意味着发射/吸收波长光谱发生红移，其中 (4)$^{th}$ 基准化合物的最大 EG 变化约为 14\%。值得注意的是，所有化合物都发射可见光谱内的光，强调了它们在光电应用中的潜力。
