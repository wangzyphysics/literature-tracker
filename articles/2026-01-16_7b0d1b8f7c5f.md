## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09922v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We employ state-of-the-art first-principles calculations to investigate the shandite compounds Co3Sn2S2, Co3Sn2SeS, and Co3Sn2Se2, which host Weyl fermions and complex magnetic textures. Their magnetic structures are governed primarily by exchange interactions and magnetocrystalline anisotropy, whereas the symmetry-allowed alternating-layer Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction (DMI) is found to be negligible. We identify a previously unrecognized spin-chiral interaction (SCI) arising from the kagome lattice topology, which plays a decisive role in stabilizing the experimentally observed magnetic textures. The extracted magnetic parameters reproduce experimental trends, with the SCI emerging as a novel and dominant contribution. The calculated SCI strengths are 0.78 meV, 0.86 meV, and 0.87 meV for Co3Sn2S2, Co3Sn2SeS, and Co3Sn2Se2, respectively. Furthermore, we demonstrate that short-wavelength magnetic textures drive phase transitions of the Weyl nodes, resulting in band flattening and the opening of an emergent gap. This newly identified SCI, together with the associated electronically driven phase transitions, provides a promising route for manipulating transport properties in spintronic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09922v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们采用最先进的第一原理计算来研究闪锌矿化合物 Co3Sn2S2、Co3Sn2SeS 和 Co3Sn2Se2，它们含有 Weyl 费米子和复杂的磁性结构。它们的磁性结构主要受交换相互作用和磁晶各向异性控制，而对称性允许的交替层 Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya 相互作用 (DMI) 被发现可以忽略不计。我们发现了一种以前未被识别的自旋手性相互作用（SCI），这种相互作用是由 kagome 晶格拓扑产生的，它在稳定实验观察到的磁性纹理方面起着决定性作用。提取的磁参数再现了实验趋势，SCI 成为一项新颖且占主导地位的贡献。对于 Co3Sn2S2、Co3Sn2SeS 和 Co3Sn2Se2，计算出的 SCI 强度分别为 0.78 meV、0.86 meV 和 0.87 meV。此外，我们证明短波长磁性纹理驱动外尔节点的相变，导致能带平坦化和出现间隙的打开。这种新发现的 SCI 以及相关的电子驱动相变为操纵自旋电子器件中的输运特性提供了一条有前途的途径。
