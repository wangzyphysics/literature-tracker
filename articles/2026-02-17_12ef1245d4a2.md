## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13924v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Antiferromagnets typically host spin-wave (magnon) excitations in the terahertz (THz) regime, offering a promising platform for high-speed magnonic information technologies. Harnessing these excitations requires sensitive control of their spectral properties. Here we use resonant x-ray diffraction and Raman scattering to demonstrate uniaxial-strain control of the antiferromagnetic (AFM) ground state and THz magnon excitations in the layered Mott insulator Ca$_2$RuO$_4$. Although the states separated by the strain-induced phase transition differ only by the sign of the weak and partially frustrated interlayer interaction, their magnon energies differ by more than 10% (~ 0.3 THz). Our theoretical analysis explains this surprising observation by tracing the origin of both the sign reversal of the interlayer coupling and the magnon energy to the spin-orbital composition of the Ru valence electrons. The extreme strain sensitivity of the THz magnon energy near a magnetic phase transition opens up pathways towards a new generation of transition-edge magnonic devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13924v1 公告类型：新 摘要：反铁磁体通常在太赫兹 (THz) 范围内产生自旋波（磁振子）激发，为高速磁振子信息技术提供了一个有前途的平台。利用这些激发需要对其光谱特性进行灵敏的控制。在这里，我们使用共振 X 射线衍射和拉曼散射来演示层状莫特绝缘体 Ca$_2$RuO$_4$ 中反铁磁 (AFM) 基态和太赫兹磁振子激发的单轴应变控制。尽管由应变诱导相变分离的状态仅因较弱且部分受抑的层间相互作用的符号不同而不同，但它们的磁振子能量相差超过 10% (~ 0.3 THz)。我们的理论分析通过追踪层间耦合的符号反转和磁振子能量与Ru价电子的自旋轨道组成的起源来解释这一令人惊讶的观察结果。磁相变附近太赫兹磁振子能量的极端应变敏感性为新一代过渡边缘磁振子器件开辟了道路。
