## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09465v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Following earlier theoretical prediction, intercalated V2Se2O-family altermagnets such as RbV2Te2O and KV2Se2O have now been experimentally confirmed as d-wave altermagnets, representing the only known van der Waals layered altermagnetic systems. By combining crystal-symmetry-paired spin-momentum locking (CSML) with the layered structure, the V2Se2O-family offers a suitable platform for studying low-dimensional spintronic responses and exploring the interplay among multiple quantum degrees of freedom. To establish a concrete theoretical foundation for understanding and utilizing these materials, we investigate six representative members of the V2Se2O-family and construct a realistic tight-binding model parameterized by first-principles calculations, which is benchmarked by experimental measurements. This model accurately captures essential altermagnetic electronic properties, including CSML and noncollinear spin-conserved currents. It further incorporates strain-coupling parameters, enabling the simulation of strain-tunable responses such as the piezo-Hall effects. This realistic model allows systematic exploration of multiple degrees of freedom (like spin, valley, and layer) within a single system, and lays the groundwork for understanding their coupling with other quantum materials, such as topological insulators and superconductors, thereby advancing both the fundamental understanding and potential device applications of this novel class of layered altermagnets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09465v1 公告类型：新 摘要：根据早期的理论预测，插层 V2Se2O 系列交变磁体（例如 RbV2Te2O 和 KV2Se2O）现已通过实验证实为 d 波交变磁体，代表了唯一已知的范德华层状交变磁体系统。通过将晶体对称配对自旋动量锁定 (CSML) 与层状结构相结合，V2Se2O 系列为研究低维自旋电子响应和探索多个量子自由度之间的相互作用提供了合适的平台。为了为理解和利用这些材料奠定具体的理论基础，我们研究了 V2Se2O 系列的六个代表性成员，并构建了一个通过第一性原理计算参数化的现实紧束缚模型，该模型以实验测量为基准。该模型准确地捕获了基本的交变磁电子特性，包括 CSML 和非共线自旋守恒电流。它进一步结合了应变耦合参数，能够模拟应变可调响应，例如压电霍尔效应。这种现实的模型允许系统地探索单个系统内的多个自由度（如自旋、谷和层），并为理解它们与其他量子材料（如拓扑绝缘体和超导体）的耦合奠定了基础，从而推进了这种新型层状交替磁体的基本理解和潜在的设备应用。
