# Enabling high giant magnetoresistance in ultrathin-free-layer spin valves

## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22726v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Emerging spin-orbit-torque devices based on spin valves require an ultrathin (e.g., $\lesssim$2 nm) magnetic free layer to maximize the torque per moment. However, reducing the free-layer thickness deteriorates the giant magnetoresistance (GMR) signal for electrical readout. Here, we demonstrate that the addition of a 1-nm Cu seed layer enables high GMR ratios of 5-7% at free-layer thicknesses of $\lesssim$2 nm by promoting high-quality, textured growth of spin valves. Our work offers a pathway for engineering high-signal GMR readout in spin-orbit-torque digital memories and neuromorphic computers.

arXiv:2512.22726v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基于自旋阀的新兴自旋轨道扭矩器件需要超薄（例如 $\lesssim$2 nm）磁性自由层，以最大化每力矩的扭矩。然而，减少自由层厚度会恶化用于电读出的巨磁阻（GMR）信号。在这里，我们证明，添加 1 nm Cu 种子层可以通过促进自旋阀的高质量、纹理化生长，在 $\lesssim$2 nm 的自由层厚度下实现 5-7% 的高 GMR 比。我们的工作为在自旋轨道扭矩数字存储器和神经形态计算机中设计高信号 GMR 读出提供了途径。
