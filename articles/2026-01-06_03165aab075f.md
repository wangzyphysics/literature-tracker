# Deep versus Broad Technology Search and the Timing of Innovation Impact

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00871v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study offers a new perspective on the depth-versus-breadth debate in innovation strategy, by modeling inventive search within dynamic collective knowledge systems, and underscoring the importance of timing for technological impact. Using frontier machine learning to project patent citation networks in hyperbolic space, we analyze 4.9 million U.S. patents to examine how search strategies give rise to distinct temporal patterns in impact accumulation. We find that inventions based on deep search, which relies on a specialized understanding of complex recombination structures, drive higher short-term impact through early adoption within specialized communities, but face diminishing returns as innovations become "locked-in" with limited diffusion potential. Conversely, when inventions are grounded in broad search that spans disparate domains, they encounter initial resistance but achieve wider diffusion and greater long-term impact by reaching cognitively diverse audiences. Individual inventions require both depth and breadth for stable impact. Organizations can strategically balance approaches across multiple inventions: using depth to build reliable technological infrastructure while pursuing breadth to expand applications. We advance innovation theory by demonstrating how deep and broad search strategies distinctly shape the timing and trajectory of technological impact, and how individual inventors and organizations can leverage these mechanisms to balance exploitation and exploration.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00871v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项研究通过在动态集体知识系统中建模创造性搜索，并强调技术影响时机的重要性，为创新战略中深度与广度的争论提供了新的视角。我们使用前沿机器学习在双曲空间中投影专利引用网络，分析了 490 万项美国专利，以研究搜索策略如何在影响积累中产生不同的时间模式。我们发现，基于深度搜索的发明依赖于对复杂重组结构的专业理解，通过在专业群体中的早期采用来推动更高的短期影响，但随着创新被“锁定”而传播潜力有限，它们面临回报递减的情况。相反，当发明基于跨越不同领域的广泛搜索时，它们会遇到最初的阻力，但通过接触认知多样化的受众而实现更广泛的传播和更大的长期影响。个别发明需要深度和广度才能产生稳定的影响。组织可以战略性地平衡多项发明的方法：利用深度来构建可靠的技术基础设施，同时追求广度来扩展应用程序。我们通过展示深度和广泛的搜索策略如何明显地塑造技术影响的时间和轨迹，以及个人发明者和组织如何利用这些机制来平衡利用和探索来推进创新理论。
