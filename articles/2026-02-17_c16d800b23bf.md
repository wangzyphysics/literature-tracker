## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14429v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantifying spin-orbital-to-charge conversion efficiency is crucial for spin-orbitronics. Two widely used methods for determining these efficiencies are based on ferromagnetic resonance (FMR), spin pumping FMR for the inverse effect, and spin-torque FMR for the direct effect. A key parameter to achieve accurate quantification, especially for spin-pumping FMR, is the RF field strength, $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$. We present a comprehensive theoretical model and experimental protocol that allow a correct quantification of $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$. It was validated by extensive experimental results and it was rigorously tested across various antennas geometries and ferromagnetic systems. We demonstrate that odd-symmetric Lorentzian voltages-which perfectly mimic spin-pumping or spin-torque FMR signals-can arise purely from rectification effects (due to anisotropic magnetoresistance) when $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$ orientation is parallel to the ferromagnetic surface. Through a systematic study of various 10-nm-thick ferromagnetic layers, such as Ni, NiFe, Fe, and CoFeB, we find that while Fe and CoFeB exhibit minimal rectification, Ni and NiFe generate strong rectified signals that must be corrected. We further demonstrate that these rectification effects become negligible for ferromagnetic thicknesses $\leq$ 6 nm, as validated in NiFe/Pt bilayers, providing an important guideline for the design of future heterostructures.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14429v1 发布类型：新 摘要：量化自旋轨道到电荷的转换效率对于自旋轨道电子学至关重要。两种广泛使用的确定这些效率的方法基于铁磁共振 (FMR)、用于逆效应的自旋泵 FMR 和用于直接效应的自旋扭矩 FMR。实现准确量化（尤其是自旋泵浦 FMR）的关键参数是 RF 场强度 $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$。我们提出了一个全面的理论模型和实验协议，可以正确量化 $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$。它经过了广泛的实验结果的验证，并在各种天线几何形状和铁磁系统中进行了严格的测试。我们证明，当 $h_{\mathrm{RF}}$ 方向平行于铁磁表面时，奇对称洛伦兹电压（完美模拟自旋泵浦或自旋扭矩 FMR 信号）可以纯粹由整流效应（由于各向异性磁阻）产生。通过对各种 10 nm 厚的铁磁层（例如 Ni、NiFe、Fe 和 CoFeB）的系统研究，我们发现虽然 Fe 和 CoFeB 表现出最小的整流，但 Ni 和 NiFe 产生必须校正的强整流信号。我们进一步证明，对于铁磁厚度 $\leq$ 6 nm，这些整流效应可以忽略不计，这在 NiFe/Pt 双层中得到了验证，为未来异质结构的设计提供了重要指导。
