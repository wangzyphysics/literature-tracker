## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06803v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The expansion of two circular rarefaction waves in vacuum or in a thin ambient plasma is examined with particle-in-cell simulations that resolve two spatial dimensions. In the simulation with no ambient plasma, the rarefaction waves interpenetrate near the symmetry line between both rarefaction wave centers. The exponential density decrease of rarefaction waves with distance implies that the sum of their density does not lead to a density maximum near the symmetry line. The absence of a density maximum, which would yield a repelling electric potential for the inflowing rarefaction wave ions near the symmetry line, and the high interpenetration speed of the ion beams lead to ion-ion instabilities rather than shocks in the overlap layer. The simulations with ambient plasma show that the rarefaction waves pile up the ions of the ambient plasma near the symmetry line. A localized piston of hot ambient ions forms. If its density is large enough, its thermoelectric field allows reverse shocks to grow in the rarefaction waves. These reverse shocks move slowly in the simulation frame and enclose a slab of downstream plasma. A decrease in the speed of the rarefaction wave ions upstream of the shocks with time leads to their collapse.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06803v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通过解析两个空间维度的细胞内粒子模拟来检查真空或稀薄环境等离子体中两个圆形稀疏波的扩展。在没有环境等离子体的模拟中，稀疏波在两个稀疏波中心之间的对称线附近相互穿透。稀疏波的密度随距离呈指数下降意味着它们的密度之和不会导致对称线附近的密度最大值。密度最大值的缺失会在对称线附近对流入的稀疏波离子产生排斥电势，并且离子束的高互穿速度导致离子-离子不稳定性而不是重叠层中的冲击。对环境等离子体的模拟表明，稀疏波在对称线附近堆积了环境等离子体的离子。形成热环境离子的局部活塞。如果它的密度足够大，它的热电场就会允许反向激波在稀疏波中增长。这些反向激波在模拟框架中缓慢移动，并包围下游等离子体板。随着时间的推移，激波上游的稀疏波离子速度降低，导致其塌缩。
