## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00485v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Our Sun is the closest X-ray astrophysical source to Earth. As such, it makes a formidable case study to better understand astrophysical processes. Solar flares are in particular very interesting as they are linked to coronal mass ejections as well as magnetic field reconnection sites in the solar atmosphere. Flares can therefore provide insightful information on the physical processes at play on their production sites, but also on the emission and acceleration of energetic charged particles towards our planet, making it a formidable forecasting tool for space weather. While solar flares are critical to understanding magnetic reconnection and particle acceleration, their hard X-ray polarization -- key to distinguishing between competing theoretical models -- remains poorly constrained by existing observations. To address this, we present the CUbesat Solar Polarimeter (CUSP), a mission under development to perform solar flare polarimetry in the 25-100 keV energy range. CUSP consists of a 6U-XL platform hosting a dual-phase Compton polarimeter. The polarimeter is made of a central assembly of four 4x4 arrays of plastic scintillators, each coupled to multi-anode photomultiplier tubes, surrounded by four strips of eight elongated GAGG scintillator bars coupled to avalanche photodiodes. Both types of sensors from Hamamatsu are respectively read out by the MAROC-3A and SKIROC-2A ASICs from Weeroc. In this manuscript, we present the preliminary spectral performances of single plastic and GAGG channels measured in the laboratory using development boards of the ASICs foreseen for the flight model.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00485v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们的太阳是距离地球最近的 X 射线天体物理源。因此，它是一个令人敬畏的案例研究，可以更好地理解天体物理过程。太阳耀斑特别有趣，因为它们与日冕物质抛射以及太阳大气中的磁场重联位点有关。因此，耀斑不仅可以提供有关其生产现场的物理过程的深入信息，还可以提供有关高能带电粒子向地球发射和加速的信息，使其成为太空天气的强大预测工具。虽然太阳耀斑对于理解磁重联和粒子加速至关重要，但它们的硬 X 射线偏振（区分相互竞争的理论模型的关键）仍然很少受到现有观测的限制。为了解决这个问题，我们推出了 CUbesat 太阳偏振计 (CUSP)，这是一项正在开发的任务，用于在 25-100 keV 能量范围内进行太阳耀斑偏振测量。 CUSP 由托管双相康普顿旋光计的 6U-XL 平台组成。该旋光计由四个 4x4 塑料闪烁体阵列的中央组件组成，每个塑料闪烁体阵列都耦合到多阳极光电倍增管，周围环绕着四个与雪崩光电二极管耦合的八个细长 GAGG 闪烁体条。 Hamamatsu 的两种类型传感器分别由 Weeroc 的 MAROC-3A 和 SKIROC-2A ASIC 读取。在这份手稿中，我们介绍了在实验室中使用飞行模型预见的 ASIC 开发板测量的单塑料和 GAGG 通道的初步光谱性能。
