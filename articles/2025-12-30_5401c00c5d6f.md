## Abstract
arXiv:2408.13163v5 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In this paper, we study excitations and dissipation in two infinite parallel metallic plates undergoing relative motion. The degrees of freedom of the electrons in both plates are modeled using the 1+2 dimensional Dirac field, and a nonlocal potential is selected to describe the interaction between the two plates. The internal relative motion is introduced via a Galilean boost, with one plate assumed to slide relative to the other. We then calculate the effective action of the system and derive the vacuum occupation number in momentum space using a perturbative method. Numerical plots reveal that the vacuum occupation number, as a function of momentum, is isotropic for a motion speed $v = 0$ and anisotropic for nonzero $v$. The relative motion induces energy transfer between the plates, leading to on-shell excitations in a manner analogous to the dissipative process of the Schwinger effect. Consequently, we study the motion-induced dissipation effects and the dissipative forces through the quantum action. Numerical results demonstrate that both the imaginary part of the quantum action due to the motion boost and the dissipative force exhibit a threshold as functions of $v$, and both are positively correlated with $v$.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2408.13163v5 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在本文中，我们研究了两个无限平行金属板进行相对运动时的激发和耗散。两个板中电子的自由度均使用 1+2 维狄拉克场进行建模，并选择非局域势来描述两个板之间的相互作用。内部相对运动是通过伽利略升压引入的，假设一个板相对于另一个板滑动。然后，我们计算系统的有效作用，并使用微扰方法推导出动量空间中的真空占据数。数值图表明，真空占据数作为动量的函数，对于运动速度 $v = 0$ 是各向同性的，对于非零 $v$ 是各向异性的。相对运动引起板之间的能量转移，以类似于施温格效应的耗散过程的方式导致壳上激发。因此，我们通过量子作用研究运动引起的耗散效应和耗散力。数值结果表明，由于运动增强和耗散力而产生的量子作用的虚部都表现出作为 $v$ 函数的阈值，并且两者都与 $v$ 正相关。
