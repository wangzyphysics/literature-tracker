## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00681v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Nonlocal metamaterials (MTMs) have recently attracted significant research attention across different areas of wave physics, owing to their ability to translate long-range interactions among meta-atoms into a wide array of wavevector-dependent responses and functionalities. In this work, we introduce nonlocal transmission line metamaterials (TL MTMs) as a versatile platform to investigate and engineer nonlocality in the microwave frequency regime. We first establish a concise theoretical framework for nonlocal TL MTMs based on circuit and network theory, from which we derive the general dispersion relation for TL MTMs having arbitrarily complex nonlocal coupling configurations. Building upon this foundation, we demonstrate how such structures can be used to synthesize nearly arbitrary, even, dispersion functions within their first Brillouin zone, effectively linking nonlocal circuit parameters to prescribed dispersion profiles. Finally, we introduce time-switched nonlocal TL MTMs, a new class of metamaterials with time-varying nonlocality in which the nonlocal branches are dynamically activated as an electromagnetic pulse propagates through the structure. This platform enables complex, nearly arbitrary frequency-momentum transformations on a propagating pulse, as well as the simultaneous excitation of modes with positive, negative, and zero group velocity within the first Brillouin zone. Our results offer new physical insights into the behavior of nonlocal MTMs, a versatile platform to investigate the interplay of frequency dispersion, spatial dispersion and time modulation, and a general theoretical foundation for the design of more advanced nonlocal and time-varying electromagnetic and photonic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00681v1 公告类型：新 摘要：非局域超材料（MTM）最近引起了波物理学不同领域的广泛研究关注，因为它们能够将元原子之间的长程相互作用转化为各种波矢量相关的响应和功能。在这项工作中，我们引入非局域传输线超材料（TL MTM）作为一个多功能平台来研究和设计微波频率范围内的非局域性。我们首先基于电路和网络理论建立了一个简洁的非局域 TL MTM 理论框架，从中我们推导出具有任意复杂非局域耦合配置的 TL MTM 的一般色散关系。在此基础上，我们演示了如何使用此类结构在其第一个布里渊区内合成几乎任意的、均匀的色散函数，从而有效地将非局部电路参数与规定的色散曲线联系起来。最后，我们介绍了时间切换非局域 TL MTM，这是一种具有时变非局域性的新型超材料，其中当电磁脉冲通过结构传播时，非局域分支会动态激活。该平台能够在传播脉冲上进行复杂的、几乎任意的频率动量变换，以及在第一布里渊区内同时激发具有正、负和零群速度的模式。我们的研究结果为非局域 MTM 的行为提供了新的物理见解，是研究频率色散、空间色散和时间调制相互作用的多功能平台，并为设计更先进的非局域和时变电磁和光子系统提供了一般理论基础。
