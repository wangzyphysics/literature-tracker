# Layer Hall effect induced by altermagnetism

## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03937v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In this work, we propose a scheme to realize the layer Hall effect in the ferromagnetic topological insulator Bi$_2$Se$_3$ via proximity to $d$-wave altermagnets. We show that an altermagnet and an in-plane magnetic field applied near one surface gap the corresponding Dirac cone, yielding an altermagnet-induced half-quantized Hall effect. When altermagnets with antiparallel N\'{e}el vectors are placed near the top and bottom surfaces, giving rise to the layer Hall effect with vanishing net Hall conductance, i.e., the altermagnet-induced layer Hall effect. In contrast, altermagnets with parallel N\'{e}el vectors lead to a quantized Chern insulating state, i.e., the altermagnet-induced anomalous Hall effect. We further analyze the dependence of the Hall conductance on the orientation of the in-plane magnetic field and demonstrate that the layer Hall effect becomes observable under a perpendicular electric field. Our results establish a route to engineer altermagnet-induced topological phases in ferromagnetic topological insulators.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03937v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在这项工作中，我们提出了一种通过接近 $d$ 波交流磁体在铁磁拓扑绝缘体 Bi$_2$Se$_3$ 中实现层霍尔效应的方案。我们表明，在相应狄拉克锥的一个表面间隙附近施加交变磁体和面内磁场，产生交变磁体感应的半量子化霍尔效应。当具有反平行N\'{e}el矢量的交变磁体放置在顶部和底部表面附近时，会产生具有消失的净霍尔电导的层霍尔效应，即交变磁体引起的层霍尔效应。相反，具有平行 N\'{e}el 矢量的交流磁体会导致量子化的陈绝缘态，即交流磁体引起的反常霍尔效应。我们进一步分析了霍尔电导对面内磁场方向的依赖性，并证明在垂直电场下可以观察到层霍尔效应。我们的结果建立了一条在铁磁拓扑绝缘体中设计交流磁体感应拓扑相的路线。
