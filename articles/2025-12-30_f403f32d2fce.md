## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23295v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Physics-Informed Neural Networks with hard constraints (HC-PINNs) are increasingly favored for their ability to strictly enforce boundary conditions via a trial function ansatz $\tilde{u} = A + B \cdot N$, yet the theoretical mechanisms governing their training dynamics have remained unexplored. Unlike soft-constrained formulations where boundary terms act as additive penalties, this work reveals that the boundary function $B$ introduces a multiplicative spatial modulation that fundamentally alters the learning landscape. A rigorous Neural Tangent Kernel (NTK) framework for HC-PINNs is established, deriving the explicit kernel composition law. This relationship demonstrates that the boundary function $B(\vec{x})$ functions as a spectral filter, reshaping the eigenspectrum of the neural network's native kernel. Through spectral analysis, the effective rank of the residual kernel is identified as a deterministic predictor of training convergence, superior to classical condition numbers. It is shown that widely used boundary functions can inadvertently induce spectral collapse, leading to optimization stagnation despite exact boundary satisfaction. Validated across multi-dimensional benchmarks, this framework transforms the design of boundary functions from a heuristic choice into a principled spectral optimization problem, providing a solid theoretical foundation for geometric hard constraints in scientific machine learning.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23295v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：具有硬约束的物理信息神经网络（HC-PINN）因其通过试验函数 ansatz $\tilde{u} = A + B \cdot N$ 严格执行边界条件的能力而越来越受到青睐，但控制其训练动态的理论机制仍未得到探索。与边界项充当加性惩罚的软约束公式不同，这项工作揭示了边界函数 $B$ 引入了乘法空间调制，从根本上改变了学习景观。建立了严格的 HC-PINN 神经正切核 (NTK) 框架，推导了显式核组成定律。这种关系表明边界函数 $B(\vec{x})$ 充当谱滤波器，重塑神经网络原生内核的特征谱。通过谱分析，残差核的有效等级被确定为训练收敛的确定性预测因子，优于经典条件数。结果表明，广泛使用的边界函数可能会无意中引起谱崩溃，从而导致优化停滞，尽管边界精确满足。该框架经过多维基准验证，将边界函数的设计从启发式选择转变为有原则的谱优化问题，为科学机器学习中的几何硬约束提供了坚实的理论基础。
