## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00719v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We propose a new two-fluid model for a partially ionized magnetoplasma under gravity, where electrons and neutrals are treated as a single fluid, and singly charged positive ions are a separate fluid. We observe that the classical result of gravitational instability (also known as Rayleigh-Taylor instability) in fully ionized plasmas is significantly modified by the influence of ion-neutral collisions (with frequency $\nu_{\rm{in}}$) and transverse wave numbers ($k_x$ and $k_y$). The instability growth rate can be enhanced or decreased depending on the values of the ratios $\kappa\equiv k_x/k_y$ and $f\equiv\nu_{\rm{in}}/\Omega_{\rm{ci}}$, where $\Omega_{\rm{ci}}$ is the ion-cyclotron frequency. We also estimate the growth rates relevant to the ionospheric E-region and solar atmosphere, noting that such growth rates can be maximized for $\kappa,~f\ll1$, or for $\kappa>1$ and $f\sim0.64$, and minimized for $f\gg1$ irrespective of the value of $\kappa$. Furthermore, the timescale of instability ranges from $1$ minute to $2$ minutes in the solar atmosphere, while in the E region, it ranges from $1$ minute to $80$ minutes. The latter can be a satisfactory result for the reported lifetime of solar prominence threads.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00719v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们提出了一种新的重力下部分电离磁等离子体的双流体模型，其中电子和中性粒子被视为单一流体，单带电正离子被视为单独的流体。我们观察到，完全电离等离子体中重力不稳定性（也称为瑞利-泰勒不稳定性）的经典结果受到离子中性碰撞（频率 $\nu_{\rm{in}}$）和横波数（$k_x$ 和 $k_y$）的影响而显着改变。不稳定性增长率可以根据比率 $\kappa\equiv k_x/k_y$ 和 $f\equiv\nu_{\rm{in}}/\Omega_{\rm{ci}}$ 的值来增强或降低，其中 $\Omega_{\rm{ci}}$ 是离子回旋加速器频率。我们还估计了与电离层 E 区和太阳大气相关的增长率，注意到对于 $\kappa,~f\ll1$ 或 $\kappa>1$ 和 $f\sim0.64$ 来说，这种增长率可以最大化，而对于 $f\gg1$ 来说，无论 $\kappa$ 的值如何，这种增长率都可以最小化。此外，在太阳大气层中，不稳定的时间尺度从 1 美元分钟到 2 美元分钟不等，而在 E 区域，不稳定时间尺度从 1 美元分钟到 80 美元分钟不等。对于所报道的太阳日珥线的寿命来说，后者可能是一个令人满意的结果。
