## Abstract
arXiv:2602.07580v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This study presents the first ex-vivo positronium imaging of human tissues using the modular J-PET scanner with the $^{44}$Sc radionuclide. The $^{44}$Sc isotope was produced via the $^{44}$Ca(p, n)$^{44}$Sc nuclear reaction and used to perform positronium imaging of phantom composed of human adipose tissue, cardiac myxoma tissue, thrombi blood clot, and also porous polymer XAD4, and a certified reference material (CRM) made from fused silica. The experiment demonstrates the suitability of $^{44}$Sc as a positron source for positronium imaging. The performance of J-PET for positronium imaging with $^{44}$Sc was validated by proper reconstruction of the mean orthopositronium lifetime for CRM material and XAD-4 polymer. The mean ortho-positronium (oPs) lifetimes determined for adipose tissue, cardiac myxoma tissues and thrombi were consistent with results of previous experiments. The study highlights the potential $^{44}$Sc radionuclide for positronium lifetime imaging (PLI).

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.07580v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项研究首次使用模块化 J-PET 扫描仪和 $^{44}$Sc 放射性核素对人体组织进行离体正电子成像。 $^{44}$Sc 同位素是通过 $^{44}$Ca(p, n)$^{44}$Sc 核反应产生的，用于对由人体脂肪组织、心脏粘液瘤组织、血栓血凝块以及多孔聚合物 XAD4 和由熔融石英制成的经过认证的参考材料 (CRM) 组成的体模进行正电子成像。该实验证明了 $^{44}$Sc 作为正电子成像的正电子源的适用性。通过正确重建 CRM 材料和 XAD-4 聚合物的平均正电子素寿命，验证了 J-PET 使用 $^{44}$Sc 进行正电子素成像的性能。脂肪组织、心脏粘液瘤组织和血栓的平均正正电子（oPs）寿命与之前的实验结果一致。该研究强调了 $^{44}$Sc 放射性核素用于正电子寿命成像 (PLI) 的潜力。
