# The Stochastic-Quantum Theorem

## 中文标题
随机量子定理

## 基本信息

## Abstract
arXiv:2309.03085v2 Announce Type: cross Abstract: This paper introduces several new classes of mathematical structures that have close connections with physics and with the theory of dynamical systems. The most general of these structures, called indivisible stochastic processes, collectively encompass many important kinds of stochastic processes, including Markov chains and random dynamical systems. This paper then states and proves a new theorem that establishes a precise correspondence between any indivisible stochastic process and a unitarily evolving quantum system. This theorem therefore leads to a new formulation of quantum theory, alongside the Hilbert-space, path-integral, and quasi-probability formulations. The theorem also provides a first-principles explanation for why quantum systems are based on the complex numbers, Hilbert spaces, linear-unitary time evolution, and the Born rule. In addition, the theorem suggests that by selecting a suitable Hilbert space, together with an appropriate choice of unitary evolution, one can simulate any indivisible stochastic process on a quantum computer, thereby potentially opening up an extensive set of novel applications for quantum computing.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2309.03085v2 公告类型：cross 摘要：本文介绍了与物理学和动力系统理论密切相关的几类新的数学结构。这些结构中最常见的称为不可分割的随机过程，它们共同包含许多重要的随机过程，包括马尔可夫链和随机动力系统。然后，本文陈述并证明了一个新定理，该定理在任何不可分割的随机过程与统一演化的量子系统之间建立了精确的对应关系。因此，除了希尔伯特空间、路径积分和准概率公式之外，该定理还带来了量子理论的新公式。该定理还为为什么量子系统基于复数、希尔伯特空间、线性酉时间演化和玻恩规则提供了第一性原理解释。此外，该定理表明，通过选择合适的希尔伯特空间以及适当的酉演化选择，人们可以在量子计算机上模拟任何不可分割的随机过程，从而有可能为量子计算开辟一系列广泛的新颖应用。
