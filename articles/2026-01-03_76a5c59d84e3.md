## Abstract
Amid the alarming threat of pollution, the electrocatalytic pathway stands out as a frontrunner, offering a promising strategy for clean energy conversion and environmental remediation. However, despite its immense potential, the practical substitution of the industrial process remains an elusive target. Despite conducting considerable research for over a century, the primary problem remains the engineering of an efficient and durable electrocatalyst to overcome bottlenecks and convert electrochemical pathways into viable, scalable technologies. Herein, this work transcends a comprehensive review by bridging material breakthroughs with their fundamental mechanistic insights and, through a meticulous analysis of existing experimental studies, reveals how either a small magnetic field or a fictitious magnetic field orchestrates complex catalytic pathways, positioning itself as a cornerstone work to propel the future of magneto-electrochemistry. The material aspects cover not only the already tested materials but also draw a new roadmap for advanced quantum materials that can change the landscape of spin-based catalysis research. Ultimately, the integration of complex materials with dynamic mechanistic understanding, rational cell engineering across diverse applications, and AI- and ML-driven predictive design is poised to revolutionize magnetic-field-assisted catalysis. This unified and adaptive framework for magneto-electrocatalysis not only delineates existing challenges but also illuminates sustainable and scalable pathways toward next-generation energy conversion, environmental remediation, and green chemical manufacturing.

## 摘要（中文）
在令人震惊的污染威胁中，电催化途径脱颖而出，成为清洁能源转换和环境修复的有前景的策略。然而，尽管潜力巨大，工业流程的实际替代仍然是一个难以实现的目标。尽管进行了一个多世纪的大量研究，但首要问题仍然是设计高效且耐用的电催化剂，以克服瓶颈并将电化学途径转化为可行的、可扩展的技术。在此，这项工作超越了全面综述，将材料突破与其基本机制见解联系起来，并通过对现有实验研究的细致分析，揭示了小磁场或虚拟磁场如何协调复杂的催化途径，将其自身定位为推动磁电化学未来的基石工作。材料方面不仅涵盖已经测试的材料，还为先进量子材料绘制了新的路线图，可以改变基于自旋的催化研究的格局。最终，复杂材料与动态机制理解、跨不同应用的合理细胞工程以及人工智能和机器学习驱动的预测设计的集成有望彻底改变磁场辅助催化。这种统一且适应性强的磁电催化框架不仅描绘了现有的挑战，而且阐明了通往下一代能源转换、环境修复和绿色化学制造的可持续和可扩展的途径。
