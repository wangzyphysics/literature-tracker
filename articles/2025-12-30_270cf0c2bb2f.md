## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22577v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: In the outer core of neutron stars, $^3$P$_2$ superfluid neutrons and $^1$S$_0$ superconducting protons are deemed to exist, forming quantum vortices and magnetic fluxtubes, respectively. Those quantum vortices and fluxtubes play an important role in explaining observed sudden changes of rotational frequency, known as pulsar ``glitches.'' While the most of conventional glitch models rely on pinning/unpinning dynamics of neutron $^1\text{S}_0$ superfluid vortices in the inner crust, contributions of the outer core have not been ruled out. However, the latter possibility has been less explored so far and further thorough investigations are desired. In this study, we are thus developing a microscopic model based on spin-2 Gross-Pitaevskii equation (GPE) for neutron $^3$P$_2$ superfluid vortices coupled with Ginzburg-Landau equation (GLE) for fluxtubes associated with superconducting $^1\text{S}_0$ protons. In this contribution, we outline our theoretical framework and report tentative results showing how shape of quantum vortices could be affected by the presence of a proton fluxtube.

arXiv:2512.22577v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：在中子星的外核中，$^3$P$_2$超流中子和$^1$S$_0$超导质子被认为存在，分别形成量子涡旋和磁通管。这些量子涡流和磁通管在解释观察到的旋转频率突然变化（称为脉冲星“毛刺”）方面发挥着重要作用。虽然大多数传统毛刺模型依赖于中子 $^1\text{S}_0$ 内地壳中超流体涡流的钉扎/脱钉动力学，但并未排除外核的贡献。然而，到目前为止，后一种可能性的探讨较少，需要进一步彻底的调查。因此，在这项研究中，我们正在开发一个基于中子 $^3$P$_2$ 超流体涡旋的自旋 2 Gross-Pitaevskii 方程 (GPE) 以及与超导 $^1\text{S}_0$ 质子相关的通量管的 Ginzburg-Landau 方程 (GLE) 的微观模型。在这篇文章中，我们概述了我们的理论框架并报告了初步结果，显示了质子通量管的存在如何影响量子涡旋的形状。
