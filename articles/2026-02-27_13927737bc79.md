## Abstract
Nature Machine Intelligence, Published online: 27 February 2026; doi:10.1038/s42256-026-01196-x Zhong et al. introduce Uni-HamGNN, a graph neural network model that predicts spin–orbit-coupled electronic structures quickly and accurately, enabling fast screening and the discovery of advanced quantum materials across the periodic table.
