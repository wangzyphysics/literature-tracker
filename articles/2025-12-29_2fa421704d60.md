## Abstract
arXiv:2506.04898v3 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Turbulence poses challenges for numerical simulation due to its chaotic, multiscale nature and high computational cost. Traditional turbulence modeling often struggles with accuracy and long-term stability. Recent scientific machine learning (SciML) models, such as Fourier Neural Operators (FNO), show promise in solving PDEs, but are typically limited to one-step-ahead predictions and often fail over long time horizons, especially in 3D turbulence. This study proposes a framework to assess the reliability of neural operator models in turbulent flows. Using three-dimensional forced homogeneous isotropic turbulence (HIT) as a benchmark, we evaluate models in terms of uncertainty quantification (UQ), error propagation, and sensitivity to initial perturbations. Statistical tools such as error distribution analysis and autocorrelation functions (ACF) are used to assess predictive robustness and temporal coherence. Our proposed model, the factorized-implicit FNO (F-IFNO), improves long-term stability and accuracy by incorporating implicit factorization into the prediction process. It outperforms conventional LES and other FNO-based models in balancing accuracy, stability, and efficiency. The results highlight the importance of prediction constraints, time interval selection, and UQ in developing robust neural operator frameworks for turbulent systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2506.04898v3 公告类型：替换 摘要：湍流由于其混沌、多尺度性质和高计算成本而给数值模拟带来了挑战。传统的湍流建模常常难以保证准确性和长期稳定性。最近的科学机器学习 (SciML) 模型，例如傅里叶神经算子 (FNO)，在解决偏微分方程方面显示出前景，但通常仅限于一步预测，并且经常会在很长一段时间内失败，尤其是在 3D 湍流中。本研究提出了一个评估湍流中神经算子模型可靠性的框架。使用三维强制均匀各向同性湍流 (HIT) 作为基准，我们从不确定性量化 (UQ)、误差传播和对初始扰动的敏感性方面评估模型。误差分布分析和自相关函数 (ACF) 等统计工具用于评估预测稳健性和时间相干性。我们提出的模型，因式分解隐式 FNO (F-IFNO)，通过将隐式因式分解纳入预测过程来提高长期稳定性和准确性。它在平衡精度、稳定性和效率方面优于传统的 LES 和其他基于 FNO 的模型。结果强调了预测约束、时间间隔选择和 UQ 在为湍流系统开发稳健的神经算子框架中的重要性。
