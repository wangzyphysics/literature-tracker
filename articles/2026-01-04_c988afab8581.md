## Abstract
Aramid fiber (AF) reinforced epoxy resin (EP) composites are widely regarded as promising candidates for fabricating advanced composites. However, the inherently weak interfacial adhesion between AF and EP considerably restricts the overall performance of AF/EP composites. Therefore, the effective interfacial optimization of is crucial for realizing high-performance AF/EP composites. In this work, an amino-terminated hyperbranched polyimide (HBPI) was synthesized via low-temperature solution polycondensation and utilized as a novel AF sizing agent. Thereafter, the AF/EP interfacial interactions were enhanced through the combined effects of chemical bonding between the HBPI sizing agent and EP and physical interactions (hydrogen bonding and π–π interactions) between HBPI sizing agent and AF, thereby benefiting stress transfer at the interfacial region. As a result, the 90° tensile strength and NOL-ring interlaminar shear strength (ILSS) of AF0.04/EP composites (with a 0.04 wt% HBPI sizing concentration) were improved by 38.45% and 14.31%, respectively, compared with the AFunsized/EP specimen. Furthermore, the tensile strength and elongation at break of the impregnated filament increased by 17.86% and 14.73%, respectively. These findings demonstrate that the HBPI sizing agent offers significant potential for enhancing the interfacial performance of AF/EP composites, providing valuable theoretical insights for the development of high-performance composite materials.

## 摘要（中文）
芳纶纤维（AF）增强环氧树脂（EP）复合材料被广泛认为是制造先进复合材料的有前途的候选材料。然而，AF 和 EP 之间固有的弱界面粘合力极大地限制了 AF/EP 复合材料的整体性能。因此，有效的界面优化对于实现高性能AF/EP复合材料至关重要。在这项工作中，通过低温溶液缩聚合成了氨基封端的超支化聚酰亚胺（HBPI），并将其用作新型AF施胶剂。此后，通过HBPI施胶剂与EP之间的化学键合以及HBPI施胶剂与AF之间的物理相互作用（氢键和π-π相互作用）的综合作用，增强了AF/EP界面相互作用，从而有利于界面区域的应力传递。结果，AF0.04/EP复合材料（HBPI施胶浓度为0.04 wt%）的90°拉伸强度和NOL环层间剪切强度（ILSS）比AFunsized/EP样品分别提高了38.45%和14.31%。此外，浸渍长丝的拉伸强度和断裂伸长率分别提高了17.86%和14.73%。这些研究结果表明，HBPI施胶剂在增强AF/EP复合材料的界面性能方面具有巨大的潜力，为高性能复合材料的开发提供了宝贵的理论见解。
