## Abstract
Author(s): Jin Wang, Ali Khosravi, Andrea Vanossi, and Erio Tosatti Friction at highly lubric interfaces of two-dimensional materials is important yet incompletely characterized. This Colloquium discusses sliding and pinning between two-dimensional layers, using simulations of twisted graphene interfaces as a prototypical system. The resulting insights are of potential relevance for a larger category of bilayer and multilayer systems as well. [Rev. Mod. Phys. 96, 011002] Published Wed Feb 07, 2024
