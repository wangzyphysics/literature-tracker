## Abstract
This study explores the electrochemical properties of Perindopril Erbumine (PE) via cyclic voltammetry (CV) and their relationship to electronic structure, assessed through HOMO energies computed using ORCA simulations. CV experiments, conducted with a three-electrode system (glassy carbon working electrode, Ag/AgCl reference, Pt counter), revealed distinct oxidation and reduction peaks, which were correlated with HOMO and LUMO energies to probe drug’s redox behavior. The effects of scan rate (10–100 mV/s) on peak oxidation current and the reversibility of the reaction were also investigated. Computational results from ORCA (B3LYP/6-31G(d,p)) yielded a HOMO energy of -6.048eV, -6.243eV, -6.715eV. These findings validate the use of combined electrochemical and computational methods to elucidate PE’s redox properties, offering insights into its stability and potential biological interactions.

## 摘要（中文）
本研究通过循环伏安法 (CV) 探索培哚普利艾丁胺 (PE) 的电化学特性及其与电子结构的关系，并通过 ORCA 模拟计算的 HOMO 能量进行评估。使用三电极系统（玻碳工作电极、Ag/AgCl 参比电极、Pt 计数器）进行的 CV 实验揭示了明显的氧化和还原峰，这些峰与 HOMO 和 LUMO 能量相关，以探测药物的氧化还原行为。还研究了扫描速率（10-100 mV/s）对峰值氧化电流和反应可逆性的影响。 ORCA (B3LYP/6-31G(d,p)) 的计算结果得出 HOMO 能量为 -6.048eV、-6.243eV、-6.715eV。这些发现验证了使用电化学和计算相结合的方法来阐明 PE 的氧化还原特性，从而深入了解其稳定性和潜在的生物相互作用。
