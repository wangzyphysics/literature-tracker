## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03613v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Traditional experimental and numerical approaches for fluid dynamics problems often suffer from high computational cost, mesh sensitivity, and limited capability in capturing complex physical behaviors. Moreover, conventional physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) frequently struggle in chaotic and highly unsteady flow regimes. In this work, we propose \textit{PhysicsFormer}, a fast and efficient transformer-based physics-informed framework that incorporates multi-head encoder-decoder cross-attention. Unlike multilayer perceptron-based PINNs, PhysicsFormer operates on sequential representations constructed from spatio-temporal data, enabling effective learning of long-range temporal dependencies and improved propagation of initial condition information. A data-embedding strategy is employed to convert spatio-temporal points into pseudo-sequences, while a dynamics-weighted loss function replaces the standard PINNs formulation. Owing to its parallel learning structure, PhysicsFormer demonstrates superior computational efficiency compared to existing transformer-based approaches. The framework is validated on Burgers' equation and flow reconstruction governed by the Navier-Stokes equations, achieving mean squared errors on the order of $10^{-6}$. In addition, an inverse problem involving parameter identification in the two-dimensional incompressible Navier-Stokes equations is investigated. For clean data, PhysicsFormer achieves zero identification error for both $\lambda_1$ and $\lambda_2$; under $1\%$ Gaussian noise, the errors are $0.07\%$ for $\lambda_1$ and $0\%$ for $\lambda_2$. These results demonstrate that PhysicsFormer provides a reliable and computationally efficient surrogate modeling framework for time-dependent fluid flow problems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03613v1 公告类型：新 摘要：流体动力学问题的传统实验和数值方法通常面临计算成本高、网格敏感性高以及捕获复杂物理行为的能力有限的问题。此外，传统的物理信息神经网络（PINN）经常在混乱和高度不稳定的流态中挣扎。在这项工作中，我们提出了 \textit{PhysicsFormer}，这是一种快速高效的基于 Transformer 的物理信息框架，它结合了多头编码器-解码器交叉注意力。与基于多层感知器的 PINN 不同，PhysicsFormer 对时空数据构建的顺序表示进行操作，从而能够有效学习远程时间依赖性并改进初始条件信息的传播。采用数据嵌入策略将时空点转换为伪序列，同时动态加权损失函数取代了标准 PINN 公式。由于其并行学习结构，与现有的基于 Transformer 的方法相比，PhysicsFormer 表现出了卓越的计算效率。该框架在 Burgers 方程和由 Navier-Stokes 方程控制的流动重建上进行了验证，实现了约 10^{-6}$ 的均方误差。此外，还研究了二维不可压缩纳维-斯托克斯方程中涉及参数辨识的反问题。对于干净的数据，PhysicsFormer 对 $\lambda_1$ 和 $\lambda_2$ 都实现了零识别错误；在$1\%$高斯噪声下，$\lambda_1$的误差为$0.07\%$，$\lambda_2$的误差为$0\%$。这些结果表明，PhysicsFormer 为瞬态流体流动问题提供了可靠且计算高效的替代建模框架。
