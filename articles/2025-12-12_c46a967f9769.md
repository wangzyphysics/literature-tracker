## Abstract
Author(s): Kaipeng Liu, Shiwei Dai, and Lixin Ge The authors investigate here quantum vacuum friction acting on a rotating nanosphere near a graphene sheet and demonstrate that the friction force can be substantially tuned by a magnetic field. This tunability originates from the mode coupling between graphene’s magnetoplasmon polaritons and the localized surface phonon polaritons in nanospheres. Interestingly, the authors reveal that the axial Casimir force—a dissipationless friction—exhibits quantized steplike features as the magnetic field and/or the chemical potential increases. This phenomenon is attributed to the quantum Hall effect of graphene at low temperatures. [Phys. Rev. B 112, 235412] Published Fri Dec 12, 2025

## 摘要（中文）
作者：Kaipeng Liu、Shiwei Dai 和 Lixin Ge 作者在此研究了作用在石墨烯片附近旋转纳米球上的量子真空摩擦，并证明摩擦力可以通过磁场进行大幅调节。这种可调谐性源于石墨烯的磁等离子极化激元与纳米球中的局域表面声子极化激元之间的模式耦合。有趣的是，作者揭示了轴向卡西米尔力（一种无耗散摩擦力）随着磁场和/或化学势的增加而表现出量子化的阶梯状特征。这种现象归因于石墨烯在低温下的量子霍尔效应。 [物理。 Rev. B 112, 235412] 2025 年 12 月 12 日星期五发布
