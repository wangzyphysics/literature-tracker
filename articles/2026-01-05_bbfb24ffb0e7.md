## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00101v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In the metallic magnet NbFe2, the low temperature threshold of ferromagnetism can be investigated by varying the Fe concentration within a narrow homogeneity range. NbFe2 is one of a number of compounds where modulated order is found to mask the ferromagnetic quantum critical point. However, here we report the rare case where the masking modulated magnetic order has been fully refined. Spherical neutron polarimetry and high-intensity single-crystal neutron diffraction reveal the first case of a longitudinal spin-density wave masking the ferromagnetic quantum critical point. The spin-density wave is characterised by a large-wavelength incommensurate modulation of its low average moment. It is formed from ferrimagnetic building blocks with antiparallel ferromagnetic sheets. The existence of ferromagnetic sheets and cancellation of the magnetisation only over mesoscopic length scales show local similarity between the spin-density wave and the ferromagnetic parent phase and indicate the spin-density wave's unconventional nature as emerging from underlying ferromagnetic quantum criticality.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00101v1 公告类型：新 摘要：在金属磁体 NbFe2 中，可以通过在较窄的均匀性范围内改变 Fe 浓度来研究铁磁性的低温阈值。 NbFe2 是许多化合物中的一种，其中发现调制顺序可以掩盖铁磁量子临界点。然而，我们在这里报告了掩蔽调制磁序已被完全细化的罕见情况。球形中子极化测量和高强度单晶中子衍射揭示了纵向自旋密度波掩盖铁磁量子临界点的第一种情况。自旋密度波的特点是其低平均矩的大波长不相称调制。它由具有反平行铁磁片的亚铁磁构件形成。铁磁片的存在和仅在介观长度尺度上磁化强度的抵消显示了自旋密度波和铁磁母相之间的局部相似性，并表明自旋密度波的非常规性质是从潜在的铁磁量子临界性中出现的。
