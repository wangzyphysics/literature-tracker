## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09471v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Superconducting quantum devices, from qubits and magnetometers to dark matter detectors, are influenced by magnetic flux noise originating from paramagnetic surface defects and impurities. These spin systems can feature complex dynamics, including a Berezinskii-Kosterlitz-Thouless transition, that depend on the lattice, interactions, external fields, and disorder. However, the disorder included in typical models is not materials-specific, diminishing the ability to accurately capture measured flux noise phenomena. We present a first principles-based simulation of a spin lattice consisting of paramagnetic O$_2$ molecules on an Al$_2$O$_3$ surface, a likely flux noise source in superconducting qubits, to elucidate opportunities to mitigate flux noise. We simulate an ensemble of surface adsorbates with disordered orientations and calculate the orientation-dependent exchange couplings using density functional theory. Thus, our spin simulation has no free parameters or assumed functional form of the disorder, and captures correlation in the defect landscape that would appear in real systems. We calculate a range of exchange interactions between electron pairs, with the smallest values, 0.016 meV and -0.023 meV, being in the range required to act as a two-level system and couple to GHz resonators. We calculate the flux noise frequency, temperature, and applied external magnetic field dependence, as well as the susceptibility-flux noise cross-correlation. Calculated trends agree with experiment, demonstrating that a surface harboring paramagnetic adsorbates arranged with materials-specific disorder and interactions captures the various properties of magnetic flux noise observed in superconducting circuits. In addition, we find that an external electric field can tune the spin-spin interaction strength and reduce magnetic flux noise.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09471v1 公告类型：新 摘要：超导量子器件，从量子位和磁力计到暗物质探测器，都受到顺磁表面缺陷和杂质产生的磁通量噪声的影响。这些自旋系统可以具有复杂的动力学特征，包括依赖于晶格、相互作用、外部场和无序的别列津斯基-科斯特利茨-托利斯转变。然而，典型模型中包含的无序并非特定于材料，从而降低了准确捕获测量的通量噪声现象的能力。我们提出了基于第一原理的自旋晶格模拟，该自旋晶格由 Al$_2$O$_3$ 表面上的顺磁 O$_2$ 分子组成，这是超导量子位中可能的通量噪声源，以阐明减轻通量噪声的机会。我们模拟了具有无序方向的表面吸附物的集合，并使用密度泛函理论计算了方向相关的交换耦合。因此，我们的自旋模拟没有自由参数或假定的无序功能形式，并且捕获了实际系统中出现的缺陷景观中的相关性。我们计算了电子对之间的一系列交换相互作用，最小值为 0.016 meV 和 -0.023 meV，处于充当两级系统并耦合到 GHz 谐振器所需的范围内。我们计算磁通噪声频率、温度和施加的外部磁场依赖性，以及磁化率-磁通噪声互相关。计算出的趋势与实验一致，证明具有材料特定无序和相互作用排列的顺磁性吸附物的表面捕获了在超导电路中观察到的磁通量噪声的各种特性。此外，我们发现外部电场可以调节自旋-自旋相互作用强度并降低磁通量噪声。
