## Abstract
Author(s): Davide Ghio, Fabrizio Boncoraglio, and Lenka Zdeborová In the inference problem for epidemic spreading processes on graphs in the case of correlated initial states (i.e., epidemic sources), the initial states can be given as the output of a neural network. This paper presents a framework to study this case. #AdvancingField #MachineLearningSpotlight [Phys. Rev. E 113, 015301] Published Wed Jan 07, 2026
