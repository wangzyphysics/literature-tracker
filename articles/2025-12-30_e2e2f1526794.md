## Abstract
arXiv:2503.00587v5 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Positron range (PR) blurring degrades positron emission tomography (PET) image resolution, particularly for high-energy emitters like gallium-68 (68 Ga). We introduce Dual-Input Dynamic Convolution (DDConv), a novel computationally efficient approach trained with voxel-specific PR point spread functions (PSFs) from Monte Carlo (MC) simulations and designed to be utilized within an iterative reconstruction algorithm to perform PR correction (PRC). By dynamically inferring local blurring kernels through a trained convolutional neural network (CNN), DDConv captures complex tissue interfaces more accurately than prior methods. Additionally, it also computes the transpose operator, ensuring consistency within iterative PET reconstruction. Comparisons with a state-of-the-art, tissue-dependent correction confirm the advantages of DDConv in recovering higher-resolution details in heterogeneous regions, including bone-soft tissue and lung-soft tissue boundaries. Experiments across digital phantoms and MC-simulated data show that DDConv offers near-MC accuracy and outperforms the state-of-the-art technique, namely spatially-variant and tissue-dependent (SVTD), especially in areas with complex material interfaces. Results from real phantom experiments further confirm DD-Conv's robustness and practical applicability: while both DD-Conv and SVTD performed similarly in homogeneous soft-tissue regions, DDConv provided more accurate activity recovery and sharper delineation at heterogeneous lung-soft tissue interfaces. Our code available at https://github.com/mellak/ddconv-prc.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2503.00587v5 公告类型：替换 摘要：正电子范围 (PR) 模糊会降低正电子发射断层扫描 (PET) 图像分辨率，特别是对于镓 68 (68 Ga) 等高能发射体。我们引入了双输入动态卷积 (DDConv)，这是一种新颖的计算高效方法，使用来自蒙特卡罗 (MC) 模拟的体素特定 PR 点扩展函数 (PSF) 进行训练，并设计用于迭代重建算法中以执行 PR 校正 (PRC)。通过训练有素的卷积神经网络 (CNN) 动态推断局部模糊核，DDConv 比现有方法更准确地捕获复杂的组织界面。此外，它还计算转置运算符，确保迭代 PET 重建的一致性。与最先进的组织相关校正的比较证实了 DDConv 在异质区域（包括骨-软组织和肺-软组织边界）恢复更高分辨率细节方面的优势。数字体模和 MC 模拟数据的实验表明，DDConv 提供接近 MC 的精度，并且优于最先进的技术，即空间变化和组织相关 (SVTD)，特别是在具有复杂材料界面的区域。真实模型实验的结果进一步证实了 DD-Conv 的稳健性和实际适用性：虽然 DD-Conv 和 SVTD 在同质软组织区域中表现相似，但 DDConv 在异质肺-软组织界面提供了更准确的活动恢复和更清晰的描绘。我们的代码可在 https://github.com/mellak/ddconv-prc 获取。
