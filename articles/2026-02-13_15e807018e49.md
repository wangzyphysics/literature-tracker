# Harmonic-to-anharmonic thermodynamic integration made simple using REG TI

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12240v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Standard harmonic-to-anharmonic thermodynamic integration (TI) is known to develop a near singularity in the integrand for solids exhibiting diffusive degrees of freedom, such as rotating functional groups or migrating defects. This pathology results in numerical challenges for estimating absolute free energies within a single thermodynamic cycle. In this work, we introduce a simple regularization that removes this singularity and yields a well-behaved integrand that can be accurately evaluated on a uniform grid. The approach -- termed Regularized End-point Gradient (REG) TI -- is demonstrated on a model system and on predicting the relative stability of paracetamol polymorphs for which quasi-free methyl rotations lead to a near singularity in standard TI. We expect REG TI to simplify anharmonic free energy calculations for solids and to potentially enable their automation.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12240v1 公告类型：新 摘要：众所周知，标准谐波到非谐波热力学积分 (TI) 会在表现出扩散自由度的固体（例如旋转官能团或迁移缺陷）的被积函数中产生接近奇异性。这种病理学导致了估计单个热力学循环内的绝对自由能的数值挑战。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种简单的正则化，可以消除这种奇点并产生一个表现良好的被积函数，可以在均匀网格上准确地进行评估。该方法被称为正则化终点梯度 (REG) TI，在模型系统和预测对乙酰氨基酚多晶型物的相对稳定性上得到了证明，对于这种多晶型物，准自由甲基旋转导致标准 TI 接近奇点。我们期望 REG TI 能够简化固体的非谐波自由能计算，并有可能实现其自动化。
