## Abstract
arXiv:2402.18542v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: In the solid state, a large variety of single-photon emitters present high quality photophysical properties together with a potential for integration. However, in many cases, the host matrix induces fluctuations of the emission wavelength in time, limiting the potential applications based on indistinguishable photons. A deep understanding of the underlying spectral diffusion processes is therefore of high importance for improving the stability of the light emission. Here, we theoretically investigate the photon statistics of an emitter driven by a resonant laser, and subject to either of two qualitatively different stationary spectral diffusion processes - a continuous diffusion process and a process based on discrete spectral jumps, both of which being known to model the spectral diffusion of various solid-state emitters. We show that the statistics of light emission carries several experimentally accessible signatures that allow to discriminate between the two classes of models, both at short times in the intensity correlation function, and at long times in the fluctuations of the integrated intensity. These results establish that resonant excitation combined with photon statistics offers a rich access to the spectral diffusion processes, yielding information that goes beyond the bare characterization of the inhomogeneous shape and noise correlation time. Incidentally, our findings shed a new light on recent experimental results of spectral diffusion of B centers in hexagonal boron nitride, providing more insight in their spectral diffusion mechanisms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2402.18542v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在固态下，多种单光子发射器呈现出高质量的光物理特性以及集成潜力。然而，在许多情况下，主基质会引起发射波长随时间的波动，限制了基于不可区分光子的潜在应用。因此，深入了解潜在的光谱扩散过程对于提高光发射的稳定性非常重要。在这里，我们从理论上研究了由谐振激光器驱动的发射器的光子统计，并受到两种性质不同的固定光谱扩散过程中的任何一个的影响——连续扩散过程和基于离散光谱跳跃的过程，这两种过程都已知用于模拟各种固态发射器的光谱扩散。我们表明，光发射的统计数据带有几个实验上可访问的特征，可以区分两类模型，无论是在短时间内的强度相关函数，还是在长时间的积分强度波动中。这些结果表明，共振激发与光子统计相结合，为光谱扩散过程提供了丰富的途径，产生的信息超出了非均匀形状和噪声相关时间的单纯表征。顺便说一句，我们的研究结果为六方氮化硼中 B 中心光谱扩散的最新实验结果提供了新的线索，提供了对其光谱扩散机制的更多见解。
