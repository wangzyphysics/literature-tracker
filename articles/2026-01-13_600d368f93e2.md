## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07781v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Magnetic proximity effects in Co/hBN/graphene heterostructures are systematically analyzed via first-principles calculations, demonstrating a pronounced localized spatial variation of the induced spin polarization of graphene's Dirac states. The proximity-induced exchange coupling, magnetic moments, and tunneling spin polarization (TSP) are shown to depend sensitively on the atomic registry at the interfaces. We analyze more than twenty distinct stackings, including high- and low-symmetry configurations, and reveal that the spin splittings of graphene's Dirac bands span a wide range from 1 to 100 meV, depending on the local hybridization of Co $d_{z^2}$, hBN $p_z$, and graphene $p_z$ orbitals. The strongest proximity effects emerge at geometric resonances, or "proximity hot spots", where the three orbital states overlap maximally. The local spin polarization also depends sensitively on energy: Dirac states aligned with resonant Co orbitals experience the most pronounced exchange interaction. At these energies, the pseudospin Hamiltonian description of magnetic proximity effects breaks down. Outside these resonances, the pseudospin picture is restored. Our findings highlight the intrinsically local nature of proximity effects, governed by the spectral resonance and interlayer wavefunction overlap. We further quantify how additional hBN layers, interlayer twist, and multilayer graphene modify the proximity exchange and TSP, offering microscopic insight for designing spintronic van der Waals heterostructures with engineered interfaces and optimized spin transport.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07781v1 公告类型：新 摘要：通过第一原理计算系统地分析了 Co/hBN/石墨烯异质结构中的磁邻近效应，证明了石墨烯狄拉克态诱导自旋极化的显着局部空间变化。邻近引起的交换耦合、磁矩和隧道自旋极化（TSP）被证明敏感地依赖于界面处的原子登记。我们分析了二十多种不同的堆叠，包括高对称性和低对称性配置，并揭示了石墨烯狄拉克能带的自旋分裂跨越了从 1 到 100 meV 的广泛范围，具体取决于 Co $d_{z^2}$、hBN $p_z$ 和石墨烯 $p_z$ 轨道的局部杂化。最强的邻近效应出现在几何共振或“邻近热点”处，其中三个轨道态重叠最大。局部自旋极化也敏感地取决于能量：与共振 Co 轨道对齐的狄拉克态经历最明显的交换相互作用。在这些能量下，磁邻近效应的赝自旋哈密顿量描述失效了。在这些共振之外，赝自旋图像被恢复。我们的研究结果强调了邻近效应的本质局部性质，该效应受谱共振和层间波函数重叠的控制。我们进一步量化了额外的六方氮化硼层、层间扭曲和多层石墨烯如何改变邻近交换和 TSP，为设计具有工程化界面和优化自旋输运的自旋电子范德华异质结构提供了微观见解。
