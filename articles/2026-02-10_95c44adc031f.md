## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08105v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Estimating the dimensionality of the latent representation needed for prediction -- the task-relevant dimension -- is a difficult, largely unsolved problem with broad scientific applications. We cast it as an Information Bottleneck question: what embedding bottleneck dimension is sufficient to compress predictor and predicted views while preserving their mutual information (MI). This repurposes neural MI estimators for dimensionality estimation. We show that standard neural estimators with separable/bilinear critics systematically inflate the inferred dimension, and we address this by introducing a hybrid critic that retains an explicit dimensional bottleneck while allowing flexible nonlinear cross-view interactions, thereby preserving the latent geometry. We further propose a one-shot protocol that reads off the effective dimension from a single over-parameterized hybrid model, without sweeping over bottleneck sizes. We validate the approach on synthetic problems with known task-relevant dimension. We extend the approach to intrinsic dimensionality by constructing paired views of a single dataset, enabling comparison with classical geometric dimension estimators. In noisy regimes where those estimators degrade, our approach remains reliable. Finally, we demonstrate the utility of the method on multiple physics datasets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08105v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：估计预测所需的潜在表示的维度（与任务相关的维度）是一个困难的、在很大程度上尚未解决的问题，具有广泛的科学应用。我们将其视为信息瓶颈问题：什么嵌入瓶颈维度足以压缩预测器和预测视图，同时保留它们的互信息（MI）。这将神经 MI 估计器重新用于维度估计。我们证明，具有可分离/双线性批评器的标准神经估计器系统地膨胀了推断的维度，并且我们通过引入混合批评器来解决这个问题，该混合批评器保留显式维度瓶颈，同时允许灵活的非线性跨视图交互，从而保留潜在的几何形状。我们进一步提出了一种一次性协议，该协议可以从单个过度参数化混合模型中读取有效维度，而无需清除瓶颈大小。我们验证了具有已知任务相关维度的综合问题的方法。我们通过构建单个数据集的配对视图来扩展内在维度的方法，从而能够与经典的几何维度估计器进行比较。在这些估计器退化的嘈杂环境中，我们的方法仍然可靠。最后，我们展示了该方法在多个物理数据集上的实用性。
