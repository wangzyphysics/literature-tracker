## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00424v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate far-field radiation of energy, linear momentum, and angular momentum from two-dimensional electron systems, focusing on metallic thin films described by the Drude conductivity. Using the Keldysh formalism within the non-equilibrium Green's function framework, we derive analytical expressions for radiative power, force, and torque. To enable angular momentum radiation, an out-of-plane magnetic field is applied to break reciprocity, resulting in gyrotropic terms in the permittivity tensor. By approximating the emitter as a thin film, the photon Green's functions can be solved analytically. Expressions for the Poynting vector and Maxwell's stress tensor can subsequently be extracted from the lesser Green's function, which governs the field correlations. The final radiation formulas can be expressed in terms of Fresnel coefficients, revealing an insightful connection to energy conservation via Kirchhoff's law. Using the Wigner transform, the analytical expression for the radiative torque can also be related to the generalized Fresnel coefficients. Numerical calculations based on the optical conductivity of bismuth are presented to corroborate the analytical results. These results provide a unified framework for energy, momentum, and angular momentum radiation in gyrotropic thin films.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00424v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究二维电子系统的能量、线性动量和角动量的远场辐射，重点关注德鲁德电导率描述的金属薄膜。在非平衡格林函数框架内使用凯尔迪什形式主义，我们推导出辐射功率、力和扭矩的解析表达式。为了实现角动量辐射，应用面外磁场来破坏互易性，从而在介电常数张量中产生陀螺项。通过将发射器近似为薄膜，可以解析地求解光子格林函数。随后可以从控制场相关性的小格林函数中提取坡印廷矢量和麦克斯韦应力张量的表达式。最终的辐射公式可以用菲涅耳系数表示，通过基尔霍夫定律揭示了与能量守恒的深刻联系。使用维格纳变换，辐射扭矩的解析表达式也可以与广义菲涅尔系数相关。基于铋的光学电导率的数值计算证实了分析结果。这些结果为回旋薄膜中的能量、动量和角动量辐射提供了统一的框架。
