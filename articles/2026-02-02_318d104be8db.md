## Abstract
arXiv:2412.10498v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Periodically driven Floquet quantum many-body systems have revealed new insights into the rich interplay of thermalization, and growth of entanglement. The phenomenology of dynamical freezing, whereby a translationally invariant many-body system exhibits emergent conservation laws and a slow growth of entanglement entropy at certain fixed ratios of a drive amplitude and frequency, presents a novel paradigm for retaining memory of an initial state upto late times. Previous studies of dynamical freezing have largely been restricted to a high-frequency Floquet-Magnus expansion, and numerical exact diagonalization, which are unable to capture the slow approach to thermalization (or lack thereof) in a systematic fashion. By employing Floquet flow-renormalization, where the time-dependent part of the Hamiltonian is gradually decoupled from the effective Hamiltonian using a sequence of unitary transformations, we unveil the universal approach to dynamical freezing and beyond, at asymptotically late times. We analyze the fixed-point behavior associated with the flow-renormalization at and near freezing using both exact-diagonalization and tensor-network based methods, and contrast the results with conventional prethermal phenomenon. For a generic non-integrable spin Hamiltonian with a periodic cosine wave drive, the flow approaches an unstable fixed point with an approximate emergent symmetry. We observe that at freezing the thermalization timescales are delayed compared to away from freezing, and the flow trajectory undergoes a series of instanton events. Our numerical results are supported by analytical solutions to the flow equations.

arXiv:2412.10498v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：周期性驱动的 Floquet 量子多体系统揭示了对热化和纠缠增长的丰富相互作用的新见解。动态冻结的现象学，即平移不变的多体系统表现出涌现的守恒定律，并且在驱动幅度和频率的某些固定比率下纠缠熵的缓慢增长，提出了一种将初始状态的记忆保留到后期的新范式。先前对动态冻结的研究很大程度上局限于高频 Floquet-Magnus 展开和数值精确对角化，无法以系统的方式捕捉缓慢的热化方法（或缺乏热化方法）。通过采用 Floquet 流重正化，其中哈密顿量的时间相关部分使用一系列酉变换逐渐与有效哈密顿量解耦，我们揭示了在渐进晚期动态冻结及其他情况的通用方法。我们使用精确对角化和基于张量网络的方法分析了在冻结和接近冻结时与流动重整化相关的定点行为，并将结果与​​传统的预热现象进行了对比。对于具有周期性余弦波驱动的通用不可积自旋哈密顿量，流动接近具有近似涌现对称性的不稳定不动点。我们观察到，在冻结时，热化时间尺度与远离冻结时相比有所延迟，并且流动轨迹经历了一系列瞬子事件。我们的数值结果得到了流动方程解析解的支持。
