## Abstract
arXiv:2512.12934v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The pseudopotential lattice Boltzmann method (LBM) is a prominent approach for simulating multiphase flows, valued for its physical intuitiveness and computational tractability. However, existing immiscible pseudopotential methods for modeling dynamic multi-component immiscible fluid systems involving open boundaries face persistent challenges, notably the influence of spurious currents on interface formation and breakup, as well as the effects of inlet and outlet boundary configurations on simulation stability. Therefore, this paper proposes a corrected open boundary framework based on Multiple-relaxation-time (MRT) for the immiscible pseudopotential model. Our method includes three key improvements: firstly, introducing correction coefficients to reconstruct the distribution function, in order to accurately recover the macroscopic quantities at the inlet boundary. Secondly, based on real-time mass flow rates at the inlet and outlet, the outlet boundary velocity is adjusted to ensure global mass conservation in the computational domain. Finally, the relaxation coefficient related to numerical stability is adjusted based on the viscosity of two-phase fluids to reduce spurious currents. To validate the reliability of the proposed corrected method, four benchmark cases were simulated: Laplace tests and Taylor deformation, two-phase Poiseuille flow, migration of droplets in microchannels, as well as droplet generation in T-shaped and co-flow devices. The results demonstrate that the corrected approach accurately captures various dynamic complex immiscible multiphase flows.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.12934v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：赝势格子玻尔兹曼方法 (LBM) 是模拟多相流的一种重要方法，因其物理直观性和计算易处理性而受到重视。然而，现有的用于模拟涉及开放边界的动态多组分不混溶流体系统的不混溶赝势方法面临着持续的挑战，特别是杂散电流对界面形成和破裂的影响，以及入口和出口边界配置对模拟稳定性的影响。因此，本文针对不混溶赝势模型提出了一种基于多重弛豫时间（MRT）的修正开放边界框架。我们的方法包括三个关键改进：首先，引入校正系数来重建分布函数，以便准确地恢复入口边界处的宏观量。其次，根据入口和出口的实时质量流量，调整出口边界速度，以确保计算域中的全局质量守恒。最后，根据两相流体的粘度调整与数值稳定性相关的松弛系数，以减少杂散电流。为了验证所提出的校正方法的可靠性，模拟了四个基准案例：拉普拉斯测试和泰勒变形、两相泊肃叶流、微通道中液滴的迁移以及T形和同流装置中的液滴生成。结果表明，修正后的方法可以准确捕获各种动态复杂的不混相多相流。
