## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03827v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Assigning individuals with hearing impairment to auditory profiles can support a better understanding of the causes and consequences of hearing loss and facilitate profile-based hearing-aid fitting. However, the factors influencing auditory profile generation remain insufficiently understood, and existing profiling frameworks have rarely been compared systematically. This study therefore investigated the impact of two key factors - the clustering method and the number of profiles - on auditory profile generation. In addition, eight established auditory profiling frameworks were systematically reviewed and compared using intrinsic statistical measures and manifold learning techniques. Frameworks were evaluated with respect to internal consistency (i.e., grouping similar individuals) and cluster separation (i.e., clear differentiation between groups). To ensure comparability, all analyses were conducted on a common open-access dataset, the extended Oldenburg Hearing Health Record (OHHR), comprising 1,127 participants (mean age = 67.2 years, SD = 12.0). Results showed that both the clustering method and the chosen number of profiles substantially influenced the resulting auditory profiles. Among purely audiogram-based approaches, the Bisgaard auditory profiles demonstrated the strongest clustering performance, whereas audiometric phenotypes performed worst. Among frameworks incorporating supra-threshold information in addition to the audiogram, the Hearing4All auditory profiles were advantageous, combining a near-optimal number of profile classes (N = 13) with high clustering quality, as indicated by a low Davies-Bouldin index. In conclusion, manifold learning and intrinsic measures enable systematic comparison of auditory profiling frameworks and identify the Hearing4All auditory profile as a promising approach for future research.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.03827v1 公告类型：新 摘要：将听力障碍人士分配到听觉档案可以帮助更好地了解听力损失的原因和后果，并促进基于档案的助听器验配。然而，影响听觉轮廓生成的因素仍然没有得到足够的了解，并且现有的剖析框架很少被系统地比较。因此，本研究调查了两个关键因素（聚类方法和配置文件数量）对听觉配置文件生成的影响。此外，还使用内在统计测量和多种学习技术系统地审查和比较了八个已建立的听觉分析框架。框架的评估涉及内部一致性（即对相似的个体进行分组）和聚类分离（即群体之间的明确区分）。为了确保可比性，所有分析均在通用的开放获取数据集（扩展的奥尔登堡听力健康记录 (OHHR)）上进行，该数据集包含 1,127 名参与者（平均年龄 = 67.2 岁，SD = 12.0）。结果表明，聚类方法和选择的配置文件数量都会极大地影响最终的听觉配置文件。在纯粹基于听力图的方法中，Bisgaard 听觉曲线表现出最强的聚类性能，而听力表型表现最差。在除听力图之外还包含超阈值信息的框架中，Hearing4All 听觉配置文件具有优势，它将接近最佳数量的配置文件类别 (N = 13) 与高聚类质量相结合，如低 Davies-Bouldin 指数所示。总之，多种学习和内在测量能够对听觉分析框架进行系统比较，并将 Hearing4All 听觉分析确定为未来研究的一种有前景的方法。
