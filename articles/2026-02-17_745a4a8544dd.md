## Abstract
arXiv:2602.15016v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Recent experiments in moir\'e quantum materials exhibit quantized Hall states without an external magnetic field, motivating continuum mechanisms based on smooth moir\'e-periodic pseudospin textures. We present a controlled theory of skyrmion Chern bands generated by such textures. An exact local $SU(2)$ transformation reveals an emergent non-Abelian gauge field; for large branch splitting we perform an operator-level Schrieffer-Wolff expansion, yielding a single-branch Hamiltonian together with systematically dressed physical operators that define the projected interacting theory beyond strict adiabaticity. The leading dynamics is governed by a $U(1)$ Berry connection whose flux is set by the skyrmion density, while controlled non-adiabatic corrections are fixed by the texture's real-space quantum geometric tensor. In a Landau-level representation built from the averaged emergent field, moir\'e-periodic modulations induce Umklapp-resolved deformations of Girvin-MacDonald-Platzman kinematics and microscopic sources of excess optical quantum weight above the topological lower bound. Assuming a gapped Hall phase, we further derive a skyrmion-crystal effective field theory with a universal Berry-phase term and a noncommutative magnetophonon. Our results provide experimentally accessible signatures for twisted transition-metal dichalcogenide homobilayers and rhombohedral graphene aligned with hexagonal boron nitride.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.15016v1 公告类型：新 摘要：莫尔量子材料的最新实验在没有外部磁场的情况下表现出量子化霍尔态，激发了基于平滑莫尔周期赝自旋纹理的连续介质机制。我们提出了由这种纹理生成的斯格明子陈能带的受控理论。精确的局部 $SU(2)$ 变换揭示了一个新兴的非阿贝尔规范场；对于大分支分裂，我们执行算子级 Schrieffer-Wolff 展开，产生单分支哈密顿量以及系统化的物理算子，定义超出严格绝热性的投影相互作用理论。主要动力学由 $U(1)$ Berry 连接控制，其通量由斯格明子密度设置，而受控非绝热校正由纹理的真实空间量子几何张量固定。在从平均涌现场构建的朗道级表示中，莫尔周期调制引起了 Girvin-MacDonald-Platzman 运动学的 Umklapp 解析变形以及拓扑下限之上的过量光学量子重量的微观来源。假设有间隙霍尔相，我们进一步推导了具有通用贝里相项和非交换磁声子的斯格明子晶体有效场理论。我们的结果为扭曲的过渡金属二硫属化物同双层和与六方氮化硼排列的菱形石墨烯提供了实验上可访问的特征。
