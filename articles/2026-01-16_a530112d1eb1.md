## Abstract
Multicomponent crystals (MCCs) enable property tuning with growing impact across pharmaceuticals, energetics, agrochemicals, and optoelectronics. Besides well‑studied cocrystals, salts and solvates are increasingly important due to unique properties and frequent occurrence in cocrystallization, yet current screening and prediction focus on cocrystals only. In this study, we introduce MCC–Graph Convolutional Network (MCC‑GCN), the first AI framework for comprehensive MCC classification, predicting formation and distinguishing among salts, cocrystals, solvates, and negative samples. We curate 33,569 MCC entries and 1,052 negatives and train GCN with transfer learning, pretraining on large chemical databases and fine‑tuning on MCC‑specific data. Benchmarked against knowledge‑based, quantum chemistry methods and competitive ML baselines, MCC‑GCN delivers strong accuracy and interpretable attributions on target molecules unseen in dataset. Moreover, through comprehensive solid-state characterization of 64 cases of two molecules lacking previously reported MCCs, 9 cocrystals, 13 salts, and 25 solvates were identified. This mechanism‑aware, multiclass framework reduces salt/solvate misclassification, enables higher‑throughput coformer selection, and advances rational design.

## 摘要（中文）
多组分晶体 (MCC) 可实现性能调节，对制药、能源、农用化学品和光电领域的影响越来越大。除了经过充分研究的共晶之外，盐和溶剂化物由于其独特的性质和共结晶中的频繁出现而变得越来越重要，但目前的筛选和预测仅集中于共晶。在这项研究中，我们介绍了 MCC-图卷积网络（MCC-GCN），这是第一个用于全面 MCC 分类、预测形成并区分盐、共晶体、溶剂化物和负样本的人工智能框架。我们整理了 33,569 个 MCC 条目和 1,052 个负例，并通过迁移学习、大型化学数据库预训练以及 MCC 特定数据的微调来训练 GCN。 MCC-GCN 以基于知识的量子化学方法和有竞争力的 ML 基线为基准，对数据集中未见的目标分子提供极高的准确性和可解释的归因。此外，通过对 64 例缺乏先前报道的 MCC 的两种分子进行全面的固态表征，鉴定出 9 种共晶、13 种盐和 25 种溶剂化物。这种机制感知的多类框架减少了盐/溶剂化物的错误分类，实现了更高通量的共形成物选择，并推进了合理设计。
