## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09836v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Next-generation high-energy electron-positron colliders, operating as Higgs factories, require an unprecedented jet energy resolution for precision measurements of Higgs and Z/W bosons. To address this challenge, a conceptual design is presented for a novel high-granularity crystal electromagnetic calorimeter that combines the superior intrinsic energy resolution of a homogeneous calorimeter with the fine segmentation required for particle-flow reconstruction. The crystal electromagnetic calorimeter design is based on orthogonally arranged long scintillating crystal bars read out by silicon photomultipliers (SiPMs) at both ends. Key design specifications were established through comprehensive simulation studies. Critical technical considerations, including crystal choices, photosensors, electronics, mechanical support, and radiation damage, are discussed. A dedicated digitisation framework was developed to realistically model effects from the crystal, SiPMs, and readout electronics. The performance of a single calorimeter module was evaluated using simulated electron showers. Simulation results for a single module demonstrate an excellent electromagnetic energy resolution of $1.12\%/\sqrt{E(\mathrm{GeV})}\oplus0.24\%$ and an energy linearity within $\pm0.5\%$ for electrons from 3 GeV to 100 GeV. The performance significantly exceeds the design requirement of $\leq 3\%/\sqrt{E(\mathrm{GeV})}\oplus1\%$. The results establish the feasibility of the proposed high-granularity crystal calorimeter concept and point to a promising pathway toward the precision calorimetry required for future high-energy electron-positron collider experiments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09836v1 公告类型：新 摘要：作为希格斯工厂运行的下一代高能正负电子对撞机需要前所未有的射流能量分辨率来精确测量希格斯和 Z/W 玻色子。为了应对这一挑战，提出了一种新型高粒度晶体电磁量热计的概念设计，该设计将均质量热计卓越的固有能量分辨率与粒子流重建所需的精细分割相结合。晶体电磁热量计设计基于正交排列的长闪烁晶体棒，由两端的硅光电倍增管 (SiPM) 读出。关键设计规范是通过全面的模拟研究确定的。讨论了关键的技术考虑因素，包括晶体选择、光电传感器、电子、机械支撑和辐射损伤。开发了专用的数字化框架来真实地模拟晶体、SiPM 和读出电子器件的效果。使用模拟电子簇射评估单个量热器模块的性能。单个模块的仿真结果表明，对于 3 GeV 至 100 GeV 的电子，其电磁能量分辨率为 $1.12\%/\sqrt{E(\mathrm{GeV})}\oplus0.24\%$，能量线性度在 $\pm0.5\%$ 范围内。性能显着超出$\leq 3\%/\sqrt{E(\mathrm{GeV})}\oplus1\%$的设计要求。结果证明了所提出的高粒度晶体量热仪概念的可行性，并为未来高能正负电子对撞机实验所需的精密量热仪指明了一条有前途的途径。
