## Abstract
arXiv:2407.00254v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Networks with two nodes are previously grouped into either two classes (mutually interactive, master-slave) or five classes (mutualism, competition, predator-prey, commensalism, amensalism). By allowing self-loops, the number of signed regulatory graphs increases to 39. We provide a complete summary of dynamical behaviors of the 39 two-node McCulloch-Pitts models when the link weights are constrained to three values [$-1$,0,$+1$] and Boolean node variables. Depending on whether the Boolean values are [$-1,1$] (bipolar) or [0,1] (binary), we show that the dynamics could also be different with the same signed regulatory graphs. We demonstrate that slight variations in the McCulloch-Pitts model (called variants) may lead to fundamentally different dynamics. We study the full model space and three kinds of robustness or stability: a) of a rule against parameter change on its overall dynamics, b) for a given state against parameter change on its final state, and c) against an initial state change on its final state. All these stability properties are loosely related to a model's limiting dynamics, with the fixed-point rules to be more stable in the first two types of robustness, but less stable in the third robustness type. These analyses pave the way towards a better understanding of a minimum complex system.

arXiv:2407.00254v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：具有两个节点的网络先前被分为两类（相互交互、主从）或五类（互惠、竞争、捕食者-猎物、共生主义、共生主义）。通过允许自循环，签名监管图的数量增加到 39 个。我们提供了当链接权重限制为三个值 [$-1$,0,$+1$] 和布尔节点变量时 39 个两节点 McCulloch-Pitts 模型的动态行为的完整摘要。根据布尔值是 [$-1,1$] （双极性）还是 [0,1] （二进制），我们表明，相同符号的监管图的动态也可能不同。我们证明，McCulloch-Pitts 模型的微小变化（称为变体）可能会导致根本不同的动态。我们研究完整的模型空间和三种鲁棒性或稳定性：a）针对其整体动态的参数变化的规则，b）对于给定状态针对其最终状态的参数变化，以及c）针对其最终状态的初始状态变化。所有这些稳定性属性都与模型的极限动态松散相关，定点规则在前两种类型的鲁棒性中更稳定，但在第三种鲁棒性类型中不太稳定。这些分析为更好地理解最小复杂系统铺平了道路。
