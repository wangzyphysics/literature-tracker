## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00931v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We introduce Grokene, a novel two-dimensional superlattice derived from graphene, which was identified through an AI-guided materials discovery workflow utilizing a large language model. Grokene is predicted to exhibit ambient-pressure, room-temperature superconductivity, with computational simulations revealing a high electron-phonon coupling constant and a substantial logarithmic-averaged phonon frequency (~1650 K), leading to a mean-field critical temperature of approximately 325 K. Full isotropic Eliashberg solutions further support a critical temperature around 310 K, underscoring its strong potential for room-temperature superconductivity. However, the strict two-dimensional nature of Grokene introduces phase fluctuations, limiting the observable superconducting transition to a Berezinskii-Kosterlitz-Thouless (BKT) temperature of about 120 K in monolayers. To elevate TBKT toward room temperature, strategies such as few-layer stacking, substrate or gate engineering, and optimization of superlattice structure and doping levels are proposed. Our integrated workflow, combining AI-driven materials discovery with advanced many-body theories (DFPT/EPW, Eliashberg, and RPA), provides a systematic and reproducible framework for exploring novel superconductors. We suggest that experimental synthesis and comprehensive characterization of Grokene will be essential to assess these computational predictions and to explore routes toward practical superconductivity under ambient pressure.

arXiv:2601.00931v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们介绍了 Grokene，一种源自石墨烯的新型二维超晶格，它是通过利用大型语言模型的人工智能引导材料发现工作流程来识别的。 Grokene 预计将表现出环境压力、室温超导性，计算模拟显示较高的电子声子耦合常数和相当大的对数平均声子频率（约 1650 K），导致平均场临界温度约为 325 K。完全各向同性的 Eliashberg 解决方案进一步支持 310 K 左右的临界温度，强调了其室温超导的强大潜力。然而，Grokene 严格的二维性质引入了相位波动，将可观测的超导转变限制在单层中约 120 K 的 Berezinskii-Kosterlitz-Thouless (BKT) 温度。为了将 TBKT 提升至室温，提出了诸如少层堆叠、衬底或栅极工程以及超晶格结构和掺杂水平优化等策略。我们的集成工作流程将人工智能驱动的材料发现与先进的多体理论（DFPT/EPW、Eliashberg 和 RPA）相结合，为探索新型超导体提供了系统且可重复的框架。我们认为，Grokene 的实验合成和综合表征对于评估这些计算预测并探索在环境压力下实现实用超导的途径至关重要。
