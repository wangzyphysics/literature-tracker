## Abstract
arXiv:2512.18724v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The classical $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising model on the honeycomb lattice is important for understanding frustrated magnetic phenomena in materials such as FePS$_3$ and Ba$_2$CoTeO$_6$, where diverse phases (e.g., striped, zigzag, armchair) and magnetization plateaus have been experimentally observed. To explain the experimental results, previous mean-field studies have explored its thermal phase transitions, identifying armchair phases and striped phases, but their limitations call for more reliable numerical investigations. In this work, we systematically revisit the classical $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising model using the Wang-Landau algorithm. We find that the armchair (AC) phase, previously reported in mean-field and experimental studies, actually coexists with the spiral (SP) phase, with their combined degeneracy reaching 20-fold (4-fold for the AC states and 16-fold for the spiral states). The phase transitions and critical exponents are studied at different interaction values. We observe first-order phase transitions, continuous phase transitions, and even the multipeak phenomenon in frustrated systems. These results clarify the nature of phases and phase transitions in frustrated Ising systems and their exponents, and additionally provide inspiration for experimental efforts to search for the spiral state and specific-heat multipeak phenomenon.

arXiv:2512.18724v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：蜂窝晶格上的经典 $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising 模型对于理解 FePS$_3$ 和 Ba$_2$CoTeO$_6$ 等材料中的受挫磁现象非常重要，在这些材料中，已经通过实验观察到了不同的相（例如条纹、锯齿形、扶手椅形）和磁化平台。为了解释实验结果，之前的平均场研究已经探索了其热相变，识别了扶手椅相和条纹相，但它们的局限性需要更可靠的数值研究。在这项工作中，我们使用 Wang-Landau 算法系统地回顾了经典的 $J_1$-$J_2$-$J_3$ Ising 模型。我们发现，之前在平均场和实验研究中报道的扶手椅（AC）相实际上与螺旋（SP）相共存，它们的综合简并性达到20倍（AC态为4倍，螺旋态为16倍）。研究了不同相互作用值下的相变和临界指数。我们观察到一阶相变、连续相变，甚至受挫系统中的多峰现象。这些结果阐明了受挫伊辛系统及其指数中相和相变的性质，并为寻找螺旋态和比热多峰现象的实验工作提供了灵感。
