## Abstract
Author(s): Pratyay Mukherjee and Ritwik Mondal Achieving fast and deterministic control of antiferromagnetic order remains a central challenge for next-generation spintronic devices. Here, the authors show that circularly polarized terahertz magnetic field pulses can deterministically switch antiferromagnetic order via field-derivative torque. By exploiting the time derivative of the applied field, their work significantly reduces switching thresholds and establishes a promising route toward ultrafast antiferromagnetic spintronics. The results reveal how tailoring pulse shape and damping can be used to optimize switching efficiency on sub-picosecond timescales. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 054407] Published Thu Feb 05, 2026

作者：Pratyay Mukherjee 和 Ritwik Mondal 实现反铁磁序的快速、确定性控制仍然是下一代自旋电子器件的核心挑战。在这里，作者表明，圆偏振太赫兹磁场脉冲可以通过场导数扭矩确定性地切换反铁磁阶。通过利用应用场的时间导数，他们的工作显着降低了开关阈值，并为超快反铁磁自旋电子学建立了一条有前途的途径。结果揭示了如何使用定制脉冲形状和阻尼来优化亚皮秒时间尺度的开关效率。 [物理。 Rev. B 113, 054407] 2026 年 2 月 5 日星期四发布
