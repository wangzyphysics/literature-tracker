## Abstract
arXiv:2601.03770v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Nanoparticles of plasmonic metals have significantly to the development of spectroscopic techniques, enabling strong confinement of electromagnetic fields at the nanoscale and corresponding signal amplification. However, to date, plasmonic applications have been limited mainly to the visible and near-infrared range, as materials supporting ultraviolet resonances typically exhibit poor chemical stability and lack robust surface functionalisation methods. In this work, we address these limitations by introducing a fully programmable approach to UV plasmonics based on rhodium nanocube dimers assembled using DNA origami templates. We have developed a reliable ligand exchange strategy that allows the functionalisation of rhodium nanocubes with DNA while maintaining their colloidal stability. These DNA-modified nanocubes act as modular building blocks that can be assembled into dimers with 69% efficiency and an average gap size of 10 nm. The DNA origami design also allows for the deterministic placement of a single streptavidin protein in the plasmonic gap, unlike previous methods based on stochastic diffusion. Experiments with single-molecule autofluorescence in UV, supported by numerical simulations, show an increase in brightness of up to 22, a reduction in fluorescence lifetime, and a more than tenfold increase in the total number of detected photons. By creating a robust and versatile platform for the production of UV-resonant plasmonic nanoantennas, this work extends the functionality of plasmonics to the deep UV spectrum and opens up new possibilities for labelling-free single-protein spectroscopy.

arXiv:2601.03770v1 公告类型: cross 摘要：等离子体金属纳米粒子对光谱技术的发展具有重大意义，能够在纳米尺度上强限制电磁场并实现相应的信号放大。然而，迄今为止，等离子体应用主要限于可见光和近红外范围，因为支持紫外共振的材料通常表现出较差的化学稳定性并且缺乏可靠的表面功能化方法。在这项工作中，我们通过引入一种完全可编程的 UV 等离子体激元方法来解决这些限制，该方法基于使用 DNA 折纸模板组装的铑纳米立方体二聚体。我们开发了一种可靠的配体交换策略，允许铑纳米立方体与 DNA 功能化，同时保持其胶体稳定性。这些 DNA 修饰的纳米立方体充当模块化构建块，可以组装成二聚体，效率为 69%，平均间隙尺寸为 10 nm。 DNA折纸设计还允许将单个链霉亲和素蛋白确定性地放置在等离子体间隙中，这与之前基于随机扩散的方法不同。在数值模拟的支持下，紫外下单分子自发荧光实验表明，亮度增加了高达 22，荧光寿命缩短，检测到的光子总数增加了十倍以上。通过创建一个用于生产紫外共振等离子体纳米天线的强大且多功能的平台，这项工作将等离子体的功能扩展到深紫外光谱，并为无标记单蛋白光谱开辟了新的可能性。
