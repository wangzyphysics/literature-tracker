## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06108v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Analog quantum simulators and digital quantum computers are two distinct paradigms driving near-term applications in modern quantum science, from probing many-body phenomena to identifying computational advantage over classical systems. A transformative opportunity on the horizon is merging the high-fidelity many-body evolution in analog simulators with the robust control and measurement of digital machines. Such a hybrid platform would unlock new capabilities in state preparation, characterization and dynamical control. Here, we embed digital quantum control in the analog evolution of a synthetic quantum material by entangling the lattice potential landscape of a Bose-Hubbard circuit with an ancilla qubit. This Hamiltonian-level control induces dynamics under a superposition of different lattice configurations and guides the many-body system to novel strongly-correlated states where different phases of matter coexist -- ordering photons into superpositions of solid and fluid eigenstates. Leveraging hybrid control modalities, we adiabatically introduce disorder to localize the photons into an entangled cat state and enhance its coherence using a many-body echo technique. This work illustrates the potential for entangling quantum computers with quantum matter -- synthetic and solid-state -- for advantage in sensing and materials characterization.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06108v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：模拟量子模拟器和数字量子计算机是推动现代量子科学近期应用的两种不同范例，从探测多体现象到识别相对于经典系统的计算优势。即将到来的变革机遇是将模拟模拟器中的高保真多体进化与数字机器的稳健控制和测量相结合。这种混合平台将释放状态准备、表征和动态控制方面的新功能。在这里，我们通过将 Bose-Hubbard 电路的晶格势图与辅助量子位纠缠在一起，将数字量子控制嵌入到合成量子材料的模拟演化中。这种哈密顿级控制在不同晶格配置的叠加下引发动力学，并引导多体系统达到物质不同相共存的新颖的强相关状态——将光子排序为固体和流体本征态的叠加。利用混合控制方式，我们绝热地引入无序，将光子定位到纠缠猫状态，并使用多体回波技术增强其相干性。这项工作说明了将量子计算机与量子物质（合成的和固态的）纠缠在一起的潜力，以在传感和材料表征方面发挥优势。
