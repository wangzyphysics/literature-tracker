## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00903v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Contrary to widespread belief, magnetostatic field lines do not ordinarily form closed loops. Why, then, are they in fact closed for so many familiar examples? What other topologies are possible, and what current configurations generate them?
