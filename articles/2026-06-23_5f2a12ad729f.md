## Abstract
Author(s): D. Ourdani, C. Gourdon, J. A. Arregi, V. Uhlíř, Y. Roussigné, and L. Thevenard FeRh is best known for its metamagnetic transition and giant volume magnetostriction. Here, the authors reveal remarkably strong phonon–magnon coupling in epitaxial FeRh. Combining Brillouin light scattering experiments with magnetoacoustic modeling, they demonstrate giant magnetoelasticity and show that Love surface acoustic waves provide an efficient mechanism for coherent magnon-polaron formation in magnetic thin films, highlighting FeRh as a promising platform for spin-acoustic information processing. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 214450] Published Tue Jun 23, 2026

## 摘要（中文）
作者：D. Ourdani、C. Gourdon、J. A. Arregi、V. Uhlíř、Y. Roussigné 和 L. Thevenard

FeRh 以其变磁转变和巨体积磁致伸缩效应而最为人所知。本文中，作者揭示了外延 FeRh 中异常强的声子–磁振子耦合。通过结合布里渊光散射实验与磁声建模，他们证明了巨磁弹性，并表明 Love 型表面声波为磁性体系中相干磁振子–极化子的形成提供了一种高效机制。
