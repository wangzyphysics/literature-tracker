# On the vortex transport and blade interactions in a reversible pump-turbine

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.08687v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Pumped storage type hydropower plants play an important role in mitigating real-time energy flexibility. Reversible pump-turbines undergo extreme operating conditions such as runaway and speed-no-load. Very limited studies are undertaken to understand the stochastic flow under these conditions in the reversible pump-turbine. The present study investigates the unsteady vortical flow, its transportation, and interaction with the blades at speed-no-load. Large eddy simulations are conducted in both turbine and pump modes. The computational domain contains 120 million nodes. Numerical results provided evidence of a large longitudinal vortex that develops on the high-pressure side of the blade, and transports into the blade passage and develops the unsteady "string of swirls". The results also showed another "string of swirls" in the draft tube, where flow in the center is reversible (pumping). The resulting flow instability is very high, and it has the potential to induce fatigue damage to the blades.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.08687v1 公告类型：新 摘要：抽水蓄能型水电站在缓解实时能源灵活性方面发挥着重要作用。可逆水泵水轮机可承受极端运行条件，例如失控和空载速度。为了了解可逆水泵水轮机中这些条件下的随机流动，进行了非常有限的研究。本研究研究了非定常涡流、其传输以及空载速度下与叶片的相互作用。大涡模拟在涡轮和泵模式下进行。计算域包含1.2亿个节点。数值结果提供了叶片高压侧产生大纵向涡流的证据，该涡流传输到叶片通道并形成不稳定的“涡流串”。结果还显示尾水管中存在另一个“漩涡串”，其中中心的流动是可逆的（泵送）。由此产生的流动不稳定性非常高，并且有可能引起叶片的疲劳损坏。
