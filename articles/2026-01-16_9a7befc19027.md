## Abstract
arXiv:2601.10174v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Gallium oxide (Ga2O3) is a wide-bandgap semiconductor with promising applications in high-power and high-frequency electronics. However, its complex polymorphic nature poses substantial challenges for fundamental studies, particularly in understanding phase-transformation behaviors under nonequilibrium conditions. Here, we develop a robust, accurate, and computationally efficient machine-learning interatomic potential (MLIP) for Ga2O3 based on the neuroevolution potential (NEP) framework combined with an energy-dependent weighting strategy. The resulting NEP potential demonstrates clear advantages over the state-of-the-art tabGAP potential with respect to both accuracy and computational efficiency. Furthermore, we introduce a physically process-oriented sampling strategy to systematically augment the training dataset, thereby enhancing the MLIP performance for targeted physical phenomena. As a representative application, a dedicated NEP potential is constructed for swift heavy-ion (SHI) irradiation simulations of \b{eta}-Ga2O3. The simulated results are in quantitative agreement with experimental observations and provide a consistent physical explanation for the reported experimental discrepancies regarding phase transformations in the ion track of \b{eta}-Ga2O3.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.10174v1 公告类型：新 摘要：氧化镓 (Ga2O3) 是一种宽带隙半导体，在高功率和高频电子领域具有广阔的应用前景。然而，其复杂的多态性给基础研究带来了巨大的挑战，特别是在理解非平衡条件下的相变行为方面。在这里，我们基于神经进化势（NEP）框架与能量依赖加权策略相结合，为 Ga2O3 开发了一种稳健、准确且计算高效的机器学习原子间势（MLIP）。由此产生的 NEP 势在准确性和计算效率方面表现出明显优于最先进的 tabGAP 势的优势。此外，我们引入了一种面向物理过程的采样策略来系统地增强训练数据集，从而提高针对目标物理现象的 MLIP 性能。作为一个代表性应用，构建了专用的 NEP 势，用于 β-Ga2O3 的快速重离子（SHI）辐照模拟。模拟结果与实验观察结果在定量上一致，并为所报告的有关 β-Ga2O3 离子轨道相变的实验差异提供了一致的物理解释。
