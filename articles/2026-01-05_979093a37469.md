## Abstract
Author(s): Kainan Chang, Yan Meng, Yanyan Qian, Yuwei Shan, Luxia Wang, and Jin Luo Cheng Here, the authors explore giant and electrically tunable second-harmonic generation (SHG) in black phosphorene. Notably, its effective nonlinear susceptibility surpasses AgGaSe 2 by orders of magnitude and is widely tunable via gate voltage and chemical potential. They clarify the link between conventional SHG and electric field induced SHG, showing that they coincide at low gate voltages. These nonlinear optical properties establish black phosphorene as a superior platform for active photonic devices. [Phys. Rev. B 113, 045408] Published Mon Jan 05, 2026
