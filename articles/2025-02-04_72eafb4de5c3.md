## Abstract
Author(s): Zheng-Meng Zhai, Mohammadamin Moradi, and Ying-Cheng Lai Using data-driven machine learning models, this work demonstrates a method to map the dynamical state of a full power grid from limited observations, enabling the user to locate disruptions with information only from other areas of the grid. [PRX Energy 4, 013003] Published Tue Feb 04, 2025
