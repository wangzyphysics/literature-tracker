## Abstract
arXiv:2602.13911v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Predicting the rheological behavior of associative polymers bridging colloidal particles into transient networks is fundamentally challenging because the coupled spatiotemporal scales prevent efficient molecular-fidelity modeling. We address this through a novel, unified multiscale simulation framework for telechelic polymer-colloid suspensions integrating: explicit-chain Brownian dynamics resolving polymer-particle association kinetics; active learning metamodels compressing kinetics into efficient surrogates; and Population Balance-Brownian Dynamics (Pop-BD) computing network-scale dynamics from metamodel predictions. Validated against explicit-chain Brownian dynamics, our framework accurately reproduces time-and frequency-dependent stress relaxation moduli, enabling simulations of larger systems over longer timescales. Systematic investigations reveal that network connectivity exhibits critical transitions at specific chain-to-particle ratios, with bond density and lifetime correlating to enhanced relaxation times and moduli. Higher particle volume fractions yield more persistent bonds and slower relaxation. This framework connects chain-level dynamics to macroscopic rheology, enabling computationally efficient rational design of associative colloidal materials for waterborne coatings and soft-matter applications.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.13911v1 公告类型：新 摘要：预测将胶体颗粒桥接成瞬态网络的缔合聚合物的流变行为从根本上具有挑战性，因为耦合的时空尺度阻碍了有效的分子保真度建模。我们通过一个新颖的、统一的多尺度模拟框架来解决这个问题，该框架用于遥爪聚合物胶体悬浮液，集成了：显式链布朗动力学解决聚合物-颗粒缔合动力学；主动学习元模型将动力学压缩为有效的替代物；人口平衡-布朗动力学（Pop-BD）根据元模型预测计算网络规模动态。我们的框架经过显式链布朗动力学验证，可以准确地再现时间和频率相关的应力松弛模量，从而能够在更长的时间尺度上模拟更大的系统。系统研究表明，网络连通性在特定的链与粒子比率下表现出关键转变，键密度和寿命与增强的弛豫时间和模量相关。颗粒体积分数越高，键合越持久，弛豫速度越慢。该框架将链级动力学与宏观流变学联系起来，从而能够对水性涂料和软物质应用的缔合胶体材料进行高效、合理的计算设计。
