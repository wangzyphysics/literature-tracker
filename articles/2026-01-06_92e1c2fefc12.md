## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01499v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We identify hexagonal YMnO$_3$ as a material realization of the elusive $\beta$-phase of unconventional magnetism, a noncollinear, noncoplanar antiferromagnetic state defined by intrinsic spin-momentum locking and a topological spin texture. First-principle calculations reveal that this unique electronic structure enables a perpendicular electric field to generate a transverse pure spin current, a response that occurs without requiring relativistic spin-orbit coupling. Symmetry analysis demonstrates that this spin current is intimately related to the material's ferroelectric polarization that breaks the inversion symmetry and is rigorously forbidden at domain walls where electrical polarization vanishes. This provides a blueprint for a non-volatile transistor where a gate voltage switches the spin current conductivity by controlling domain wall density, enabling all-electrical control for energy-efficient antiferromagnetic spintronics.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01499v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们将六方 YMnO$_3$ 确定为非常规磁性难以捉摸的 $\beta$ 相的材料实现，这是一种由固有自旋动量锁定和拓扑自旋纹理定义的非共线、非共面反铁磁状态。第一原理计算表明，这种独特的电子结构使垂直电场能够产生横向纯自旋电流，这种响应无需相对论自旋轨道耦合即可发生。对称分析表明，这种自旋电流与材料的铁电极化密切相关，铁电极化破坏了反演对称性，并且在电极化消失的磁畴壁处被严格禁止。这为非易失性晶体管提供了蓝图，其中栅极电压通过控制畴壁密度来切换自旋电流传导性，从而实现节能反铁磁自旋电子学的全电气控制。
