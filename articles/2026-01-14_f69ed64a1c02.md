## Abstract
arXiv:2503.16303v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Mode mixing in optical fibers caused by mechanical bending induces perturbations that distort the spatial field profile of coherent beams as they propagate through few-mode or multimode fibers. The observed output from a bent fiber commonly appears as complex speckle, which is challenging to relate directly to the underlying deformation, particularly in continuously varying systems such as aerially deployed fibers or fiber-integrated sensors in mechanical structures. We introduce a novel method for constructing a complete deformation-resolved orthonormal modal basis that captures the optical response of a multimode fiber across a range of controlled mechanical deformations. The basis is derived via a two-stage singular value decomposition framework that initially constructs deformation-specific orthonormal mode sets from speckle pattern correlation matrices and subsequently decomposes the aggregated sets to produce a unified functional basis that comprehensively spans the deformation-induced modal subspace supported by the fiber. This hierarchical framework yields an energy-balanced representation that isolates statistically dominant field components across all deformation states, approximates superpositions of the fiber's propagation-invariant modes, systematically encodes deformation-induced perturbations, and supports robust decomposition of output fields across varying mechanical conditions. Such a basis enables tracking of mechanically induced modal evolution in deployed fibers, supporting distributed sensing, network resilience, and predictive fault diagnostics, with potential for integration into mode-division multiplexing systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2503.16303v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：由机械弯曲引起的光纤中的模式混合会引起扰动，当相干光束通过少模或多模光纤传播时，这些扰动会扭曲相干光束的空间场轮廓。从弯曲光纤观察到的输出通常表现为复杂的散斑，这很难与潜在的变形直接相关，特别是在连续变化的系统中，例如空中部署的光纤或机械结构中的光纤集成传感器。我们介绍了一种构建完整的变形解析正交模态基础的新方法，该方法可以捕获多模光纤在一系列受控机械变形范围内的光学响应。该基础是通过两阶段奇异值分解框架导出的，该框架最初从散斑图案相关矩阵构建变形特定的正交模式集，然后分解聚合集以产生全面跨越光纤支持的变形引起的模态子空间的统一功能基础。这种分层框架产生了一种能量平衡的表示，可以隔离所有变形状态下的统计主导场分量，近似光纤传播不变模式的叠加，系统地编码变形引起的扰动，并支持在不同机械条件下对输出场进行鲁棒分解。这样的基础能够跟踪已部署光纤中的机械诱导模态演化，支持分布式传感、网络弹性和预测性故障诊断，并具有集成到模分复用系统中的潜力。
