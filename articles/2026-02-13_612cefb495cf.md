## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11579v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Cation-disordered solids offer a rich chemical landscape where local coordination, lattice responses, and configurational disorder collectively, yet often implicitly, govern ion transport. In cation-disordered rocksalt oxides, Li+ diffusion has conventionally been rationalized by the static 0-transition-metal (0-TM) percolation rule, which assumes an ideal, passive lattice and thus fails to capture experimentally accessible capacities. Here, we show that lattice distortion is an essential, previously overlooked degree of freedom that actively reshapes Li+ percolation networks. By developing a lattice-responsive framework combining Monte Carlo sampling of cation configurations with machine-learning-accelerated molecular dynamics, we quantitatively predict Li+ percolation and electrochemical capacities within 5% of experiment. Our results reveal a causal coupling between lattice distortion and cation short-range order: enhanced local distortions precede and suppress short-range ordering, activating Li+ migration through nominally inaccessible 1-TM channels, fundamentally extending percolation beyond the 0-TM paradigm. Guided by this, we design and synthesize a high-entropy oxide, Li1.2Mn0.2Ti0.2V0.2Mo0.2O2, which exhibits enhanced distortion and achieves a 71.9% Li+ percolation network, surpassing 65.8% in Li1.2Mn0.4Ti0.4O2, delivering 256.3 mAh/g capacity, closely matching our prediction of 255.1 mAh/g. These findings establish lattice distortion as an active control parameter for ion transport, revising percolation concepts and offering a general design principle beyond metal-ion cathodes.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11579v1 公告类型：新 摘要：阳离子无序固体提供了丰富的化学景观，其中局部配位、晶格响应和构型无序共同但往往隐含地控制着离子传输。在阳离子无序岩盐氧化物中，Li+扩散通常通过静态0-过渡金属（0-TM）渗滤规则合理化，该规则假设理想的被动晶格，因此无法捕获实验上可获得的容量。在这里，我们证明晶格畸变是一个重要的、以前被忽视的自由度，它可以主动重塑 Li+ 渗流网络。通过开发一个晶格响应框架，将阳离子构型的蒙特卡罗采样与机器学习加速的分子动力学相结合，我们在实验的 5% 内定量预测 Li+ 渗滤和电化学容量。我们的结果揭示了晶格畸变和阳离子短程有序之间的因果耦合：增强的局部畸变先于并抑制短程有序，通过名义上不可访问的 1-TM 通道激活 Li+ 迁移，从根本上将渗透扩展到 0-TM 范式之外。在此指导下，我们设计并合成了一种高熵氧化物Li1.2Mn0.2Ti0.2V0.2Mo0.2O2，它表现出增强的畸变并实现了71.9%的Li+渗流网络，超过了Li1.2Mn0.4Ti0.4O2的65.8%，提供了256.3 mAh/g的容量，与我们预测的255.1 mAh/g非常接近。这些发现将晶格畸变确立为离子传输的主动控制参数，修正了渗流概念，并提供了超越金属离子阴极的通用设计原理。
