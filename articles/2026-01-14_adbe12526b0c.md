## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07883v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: We explore the experimental predictions of the local scale invariant, non-Hermitian pilot-wave (de Broglie-Bohm) formulation of quantum theory introduced in arXiv:2601.03567. We use Weyl's definition of gravitational radius of charge to obtain the fine-structure constant for non-integrable scale effects $\alpha_S$. The minuteness of $\alpha_S$ relative to $\alpha$ ($\alpha_S/\alpha \sim 10^{-21}$) effectively hides the effects in usual quantum experiments. In an Aharonov-Bohm double-slit experiment, the theory predicts that the position probability density depends on which slit the particle trajectory crosses, due to a non-integrable scale induced by the magnetic flux. This experimental prediction can be realistically tested for an electrically neutral, heavy molecule with mass $m \sim 10^{-19} \text{g}$ at a $\sim 10^6 \text{ esu}$ flux regime. We analyse the Weyl-Einstein debate on the second-clock effect using the theory and show that spectral frequencies are history-independent. We thereby resolve Einstein's key objection against local scale invariance, and obtain two further experimental predictions. First, spectral intensities turn out to be history-dependent. Second, energy eigenvalues are modified by tiny imaginary corrections that modify spectral linewidths. We argue that the trajectory dependence of the probabilities renders our theory empirically distinguishable from other quantum formulations that do not use pilot-wave trajectories, or their mathematical equivalents, to derive experimental predictions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07883v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：我们探索 arXiv:2601.03567 中引入的量子理论的局域尺度不变、非厄米导波（德布罗意-玻姆）公式的实验预测。我们使用外尔对电荷引力半径的定义来获得不可积尺度效应 $\alpha_S$ 的精细结构常数。 $\alpha_S$ 相对于 $\alpha$ ($\alpha_S/\alpha \sim 10^{-21}$) 的微小性有效地隐藏了通常量子实验中的效应。在阿哈罗诺夫-玻姆双缝实验中，该理论预测，由于磁通量引起的不可积尺度，位置概率密度取决于粒子轨迹穿过哪个狭缝。该实验预测可以在 $\sim 10^6 \text{ esu}$ 通量状态下对质量为 $m \sim 10^{-19} \text{g}$ 的电中性重分子进行实际测试。我们利用该理论分析了韦尔-爱因斯坦关于第二时钟效应的争论，并表明光谱频率与历史无关。由此，我们解决了爱因斯坦对局部尺度不变性的主要反对意见，并获得了两个进一步的实验预测。首先，光谱强度与历史有关。其次，能量特征值通过微小的虚数校正来修改，从而修改光谱线宽。我们认为，概率的轨迹依赖性使我们的理论在经验上可以与其他不使用导波轨迹或其数学等价物来得出实验预测的量子公式区分开来。
