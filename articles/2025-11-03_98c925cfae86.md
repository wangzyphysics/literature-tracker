## Abstract
ABSTRACT Degradable sulfur-containing polymers leverage the controllable cleavage of thioester and thioether bonds under external stimuli, coupled with closed-loop recyclability, to significantly mitigate plastic pollution. The dynamic covalent adaptability of sulfur bonds within the polymer backbone, combined with the high refractive index arising from sulfur’s high polarizability, enables the integration of stimuli-responsive degradation and multifunctionality. Advanced polymerization techniques, such as ring-opening polymerization and reversible addition-fragmentation chain-transfer polymerization, permit precise control over the architecture of the backbone, yielding high-performance materials with tunable thermal, mechanical, and optical properties. These materials demonstrate significant potential in biomedicine, environmental remediation, and energy storage. Focusing on molecular design, degradation control, and functional diversification, this review systematically elucidates synthetic strategies, degradation mechanisms, and frontier applications, while providing perspectives on future developments.

摘要 可降解含硫聚合物利用硫酯和硫醚键在外部刺激下的可控裂解，加上闭环可回收性，可显着减轻塑料污染。聚合物主链内硫键的动态共价适应性，加上硫的高极化性所产生的高折射率，使得刺激响应性降解和多功能性相结合。先进的聚合技术，例如开环聚合和可逆加成断裂链转移聚合，可以精确控制主链的结构，从而产生具有可调热、机械和光学性能的高性能材料。这些材料在生物医学、环境修复和能源储存方面表现出巨大的潜力。该综述重点关注分子设计、降解控制和功能多样化，系统阐明了合成策略、降解机制和前沿应用，同时提供了未来发展的展望。
