## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04067v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Wave breaking injects turbulence and bubbles into the upper ocean, modulating air-sea exchange of momentum, heat, gases, and sea-spray aerosols. These fluxes depend nonlinearly on sea state but remain poorly represented in coupled atmosphere-wave-ocean models, where air-entrainment velocity is often parameterized using wind speed or significant wave height alone. We develop a global machine-learning parameterization of Va trained on a 43-year WAVEWATCH III simulation that resolves the breaker-front distribution and associated energetics. A multilayer perceptron with seven physically motivated predictors (wind speed, wave height, wave age, steepness, direction, and depth) reproduces spectral-reference Va with high skill. The model reduces longstanding biases in bulk formulas, notably overestimation in swell-dominated low latitudes and underestimation in storm tracks. Applied globally, it improves bubble-mediated CO2 transfer velocity and sea-salt aerosol emission, reducing errors by an order of magnitude. Validation against independent HiWinGS observations supports robust deep-water performance.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04067v1 公告类型：新 摘要：波浪破碎将湍流和气泡注入上层海洋，调节海气交换动量、热量、气体和海雾气溶胶。这些通量与海况呈非线性关系，但在大气-波浪-海洋耦合模型中仍然表现不佳，其中空气夹带速度通常仅使用风速或有效波高进行参数化。我们开发了 Va 的全局机器学习参数化，经过 43 年的 WAVEWATCH III 模拟训练，解决了断路器前沿分布和相关能量学问题。具有七个物理驱动预测器（风速、波高、波龄、陡度、方向和深度）的多层感知器以高技能再现光谱参考 Va。该模型减少了批量公式中长期存在的偏差，特别是对涌浪主导的低纬度地区的高估和对风暴路径的低估。在全球范围内应用，它提高了气泡介导的二氧化碳传输速度和海盐气溶胶排放，从而将误差减少了一个数量级。针对独立 HiWinGS 观测的验证支持强大的深水性能。
