## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11313v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Machine learning (ML)-based models have demonstrated high skill and computational efficiency, often outperforming conventional physics-based models in weather and subseasonal predictions. While prior studies have assessed their fidelity in capturing synoptic-scale atmospheric dynamics, their performance across timescales and under out-of-distribution forcing, such as +3K or +4K uniform-warming forcings, and the sources of biases remain elusive, to establish the model reliability for Earth science. Here, we design three sets of experiments targeting synoptic-scale phenomena, interannual variability, and out-of-distribution uniform-warming forcings. We evaluate the Neural General Circulation Model (NeuralGCM), a hybrid model integrating a dynamical core with ML-based component, against observations and physics-based Earth system models (ESMs). At the synoptic scale, NeuralGCM captures the evolution and propagation of extratropical cyclones with performance comparable to ESMs. At the interannual scale, when forced by El Ni\~no-Southern Oscillation sea surface temperature (SST) anomalies, NeuralGCM successfully reproduces associated teleconnection patterns but exhibits deficiencies in capturing nonlinear response. Under out-of-distribution uniform-warming forcings, NeuralGCM simulates similar responses in global-average temperature and precipitation and reproduces large-scale tropospheric circulation features similar to those in ESMs. Notable weaknesses include overestimating the tracks and spatial extent of extratropical cyclones, biases in the teleconnected wave train triggered by tropical SST anomalies, and differences in upper-level warming and stratospheric circulation responses to SST warming compared to physics-based ESMs. The causes of these weaknesses were explored.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11313v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基于机器学习 (ML) 的模型已表现出高技能和计算效率，在天气和次季节预测方面通常优于传统的基于物理的模型。虽然之前的研究已经评估了它们在捕获天气尺度大气动力学方面的保真度，但它们在跨时间尺度和分布外强迫（例如+3K或+4K均匀变暖强迫）下的表现，以及偏差的来源仍然难以捉摸，以建立地球科学的模型可靠性。在这里，我们设计了三组针对天气尺度现象、年际变化和分布不均的均匀变暖强迫的实验。我们根据观测结果和基于物理的地球系统模型 (ESM) 评估神经大气环流模型 (NeuralGCM)，这是一种将动力核心与基于 ML 的组件集成在一起的混合模型。在天气尺度上，NeuralGCM 捕获温带气旋的演变和传播，其性能与 ESM 相当。在年际尺度上，当受到厄尔尼诺-南方涛动海面温度（SST）异常的影响时，NeuralGCM 成功地再现了相关的遥相关模式，但在捕获非线性响应方面表现出缺陷。在分布不均的均匀变暖强迫下，NeuralGCM 模拟了全球平均温度和降水的类似响应，并再现了与 ESM 中类似的大尺度对流层环流特征。值得注意的弱点包括高估了温带气旋的路径和空间范围、热带海温异常引发的遥连波列的偏差，以及与基于物理的ESM相比，高层变暖和平流层环流对海温变暖的响应存在差异。对这些弱点的原因进行了探讨。
