## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14807v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: This work presents the development and testing of a novel radio-frequency (RF) gasket with a Lenz lens surface geometry for contactless measurements in diamond anvil cells (DACs). Conventional RF approaches, which fabricate the Lenz lens onto the diamond anvil itself, preclude the placement of electrical circuits. Our method overcomes this limitation by transferring the RF sensor to a composite Ta-based gasket. The sensor consists of single-turn microcoils that are formed by magnetron sputtering a gold film onto an insulating Ta$_2$O$_5$ layer. The Lenz lens topology is then patterned using focused ion beam etching. We validated this technique using polycrystalline Cu1234 and Bi2212 high-$\textit{T$_c$}$ superconductors at ambient and high pressures. The measurements consistently identified the superconducting transition temperature across carrier frequencies from 111 kHz to 200 MHz. This new gasket technique establishes a reliable and sensitive tool for contactless studies of superconductivity under high pressure.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14807v1 公告类型：新 摘要：这项工作介绍了一种新型射频 (RF) 垫片的开发和测试，该垫片具有 Lenz 透镜表面几何形状，用于金刚石砧座 (DAC) 中的非接触式测量。传统的射频方法将楞次透镜制造到金刚石砧本身上，无法放置电路。我们的方法通过将射频传感器转移到复合钽基垫片上克服了这一限制。该传感器由单匝微线圈组成，这些微线圈是通过磁控管将金膜溅射到绝缘 Ta$_2$O$_5$ 层上而形成的。然后使用聚焦离子束蚀刻对楞次透镜拓扑进行图案化。我们在环境压力和高压下使用多晶 Cu1234 和 Bi2212 高$\textit{T$_c$}$ 超导体验证了这项技术。测量结果一致地确定了 111 kHz 至 200 MHz 载波频率范围内的超导转变温度。这种新的垫片技术为高压下超导性的非接触式研究建立了可靠且灵敏的工具。
