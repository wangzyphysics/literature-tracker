## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22325v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We study samples and a dipolar model of magnetic rods arranged on twisted polygonal clusters in terms of the twist angle. We find that the relative twist between polygons induces noncollinear chiral phases, ranging from flux vortex closure to hedgehog like radial configurations. Chirality, quantified in terms of a bond order parameter, is an emergent property that behaves here as an Ising variable. The chiral configurations of the systems can be understood in terms of chirality and clock index order parameters, whose evolution with twist occurs through two types of first order phase transitions. Within a fixed Ising chiral sector, the clock index, rooted in the $C_N$ invariance of the polygons, characterizes chiral textures that share chirality. As the twist increases, it continuously shifts the preferred relative clock phase, but the Nfold anisotropy only allows discrete orientations; the competition produces a tilted Nfold energy landscape whose global minimum hops discontinuously between clock sectors. As the number of sites in the polygon grows, the resulting response displays a nonlinear crossover from rigid, Ising like behavior to an almost $\rm U(1)$ invariant regime, governed by a twist induced suppression of the emergent $Z_N$ clock anisotropy. A Landau phenomenology captures these trends and naturally extends to bilayer lattices, where we show that twisted honeycomb systems realize an effective sine-Gordon theory with twist-controlled transitions between isolated domain walls and domain wall lattices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.22325v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们根据扭转角研究了排列在扭转多边形簇上的磁棒的样本和偶极模型。我们发现多边形之间的相对扭曲会引起非共线手性相，范围从通量涡旋闭合到刺猬状径向配置。以键序参数量化的手性是一种突现性质，在这里表现为伊辛变量。系统的手性配置可以用手性和时钟指数阶参数来理解，其随扭曲的演化通过两种类型的一阶相变发生。在固定的伊辛手性扇区内，时钟索引植根于多边形的 $C_N$ 不变性，表征共享手性的手性纹理。随着扭曲的增加，它会不断地改变首选的相对时钟相位，但 Nfold 各向异性只允许离散方向；竞争产生了倾斜的 Nfold 能源格局，其全球最小跳跃在时钟扇区之间不连续。随着多边形中站点数量的增加，产生的响应显示出从刚性的伊辛式行为到几乎 $\rm U(1)$ 不变状态的非线性交叉，由新兴 $Z_N$ 时钟各向异性的扭曲引起的抑制控制。朗道现象学捕捉了这些趋势，并自然地扩展到双层晶格，我们证明扭曲蜂窝系统实现了有效的正弦戈登理论，在孤立的畴壁和畴壁晶格之间具有扭曲控制的过渡。
