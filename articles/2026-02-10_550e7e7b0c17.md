## Abstract
arXiv:2510.09891v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Seasonal forecast of Arctic sea ice concentration is key to mitigate the negative impact and assess potential opportunities posed by the rapid decline of sea ice coverage. Seasonal prediction systems based on climate models often show systematic biases and complex spatio-temporal errors that grow with the forecasts. Consequently, operational predictions are routinely bias corrected and calibrated using retrospective forecasts. For predictions of Arctic sea ice concentration, error corrections are mainly based on one-to-one post-processing methods including climatological mean or linear regression correction and, more recently, machine learning. Such deterministic adjustments are confined at best to the limited number of costly-to-run ensemble members of the raw forecast. However, decision-making requires proper quantification of uncertainty and likelihood of events, particularly of extremes. We introduce a probabilistic error correction framework based on a conditional Variational Autoencoder model to map the conditional distribution of observations given the biased model prediction. This method naturally allows for generating large ensembles of adjusted forecasts. We evaluate our model using deterministic and probabilistic metrics and show that the adjusted forecasts are better calibrated, closer to the observational distribution, and have smaller errors than climatological mean adjusted forecasts.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.09891v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：北极海冰浓度的季节性预测是减轻海冰覆盖范围快速下降带来的负面影响和评估潜在机会的关键。基于气候模型的季节性预测系统通常会出现系统偏差和复杂的时空误差，这些误差会随着预测的增加而增加。因此，运营预测通常会使用回顾性预测进行偏差纠正和校准。对于北极海冰浓度的预测，误差校正主要基于一对一的后处理方法，包括气候平均值或线性回归校正以及最近的机器学习。这种确定性调整最多仅限于原始预测中运行成本高昂的有限数量的集合成员。然而，决策需要对事件的不确定性和可能性进行适当的量化，尤其是极端事件。我们引入了基于条件变分自动编码器模型的概率误差校正框架，以在给定有偏差的模型预测的情况下映射观测值的条件分布。这种方法自然允许生成调整后的预测的大型集合。我们使用确定性和概率性指标评估我们的模型，并表明调整后的预测校准得更好，更接近观测分布，并且比气候平均调整预测具有更小的误差。
