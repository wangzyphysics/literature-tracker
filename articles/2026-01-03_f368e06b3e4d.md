## Abstract
Embedding mechanophores into polymer matrices to activate mechanochemical reactions has been extensively employed to realize diverse functional applications. Spiropyran (SP) is a widely used mechanophore that undergoes force-induced isomerization into its activated merocyanine (MC) form, accompanied by a color change. To accurately simulate the SP-MC isomerization process under tension, the present work introduces a large-scale hybrid coarse-grained/atomistic (CG/AA) model in which poly(methyl metacrylate) (PMMA) monomers are represented by in dividual beads, while SP remains fully described at the AA level. We used force field parameters derived from quantum chemical (QC) data, employing the fully atomistic framework previously developed by our group. The underlying CG PMMA force field was reparameterized to match experimental bulk properties and compared with a corresponding atomistic force field. This approach enabled deformation simulations of significantly larger systems than possible with a pure AA force field, at reduced computational cost, thereby providing novel insights into force transduction and mechanophore activation. Furthermore, a parabolic stress distribution was observed along individual PMMA chains, resulting in preferential SP activation near chain centers. Non specific chain scission events occurred far less frequently than SP activations. The developed hybrid model offers a powerful framework for investigating more complex mechanophore doped polymer networks.

## 摘要（中文）
将力团嵌入聚合物基质中以激活力化学反应已被广泛用于实现多种功能应用。螺吡喃 (SP) 是一种广泛使用的机械载体，可经历力诱导异构化为其活化的部花青 (MC) 形式，并伴有颜色变化。为了准确模拟张力下的 SP-MC 异构化过程，目前的工作引入了一种大规模混合粗粒/原子 (CG/AA) 模型，其中聚甲基丙烯酸甲酯 (PMMA) 单体由单个珠子表示，而 SP 仍然在 AA 级别上进行充分描述。我们使用从量子化学（QC）数据导出的力场参数，采用我们团队之前开发的完全原子框架。底层 CG PMMA 力场被重新参数化，以匹配实验体特性，并与相应的原子力场进行比较。这种方法能够以降低的计算成本对比纯 AA 力场大得多的系统进行变形模拟，从而为力传导和力力团激活提供新的见解。此外，沿着各个 PMMA 链观察到抛物线应力分布，导致链中心附近的 SP 优先激活。非特异性断链事件发生的频率远低于 SP 激活。开发的混合模型为研究更复杂的机械力掺杂聚合物网络提供了强大的框架。
