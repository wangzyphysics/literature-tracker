## Abstract
arXiv:2602.06497v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: While epidemiological modeling is pivotal for informing public health strategies, a fundamental trade-off limits its predictive fidelity: exact stochastic simulations are often computationally intractable for large-scale systems, whereas efficient analytical approximations typically fail to account for essential short-range correlations and network loops. Here, we resolve this trade-off by introducing Tensor Network Dynamical Message Passing (TNDMP), a framework grounded in a rigorous property we term \textit{Susceptible-Induced Factorization}. This theoretical insight reveals that a susceptible node acts as a dynamical decoupler, factorizing the global evolution operator into localized components. Leveraging this, TNDMP provides a dual-mode algorithmic suite: an exact algorithm that computes local observables with minimal redundancy on tractable topologies and a scalable and tunable approximation for complex real-world networks. We demonstrate that widely adopted heuristics, such as Dynamical Message Passing (DMP) and Pair Approximation (PA), are mathematically recoverable as low-order limits of our framework. Numerical validation in synthetic and real-world networks confirms that TNDMP significantly outperforms existing methods to predict epidemic thresholds and steady states, offering a rigorous bridge between the efficiency of message passing and the accuracy of tensor network formalisms.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.06497v1 公告类型：新 摘要：虽然流行病学建模对于告知公共卫生策略至关重要，但一个基本的权衡限制了其预测保真度：对于大型系统来说，精确的随机模拟通常在计算上难以处理，而有效的分析近似通常无法考虑基本的短程相关性和网络循环。在这里，我们通过引入张量网络动态消息传递（TNDMP）来解决这种权衡，这是一个基于我们称为 \textit{Susceptible-Induced Factorization} 的严格属性的框架。这一理论见解揭示了易受影响的节点充当动态解耦器，将全局演化算子分解为局部组件。利用这一点，TNDMP 提供了双模式算法套件：一种在易处理拓扑上以最小冗余计算局部可观测值的精确算法，以及针对复杂现实世界网络的可扩展且可调的近似值。我们证明了广泛采用的启发式方法，例如动态消息传递（DMP）和对近似（PA），在数学上可以恢复为我们框架的低阶限制。合成网络和现实网络中的数值验证证实，TNDMP 显着优于预测流行阈值和稳态的现有方法，在消息传递效率和张量网络形式的准确性之间架起了严格的桥梁。
