# Quantum-Coherent Thermodynamics: Leaf Typicality via Minimum-Variance Foliation

## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12212v1 Announce Type: cross Abstract: Equilibrium statistical ensembles commute with the Hamiltonian and thus carry no coherence in the energy eigenbasis. We develop a thermodynamic framework in which energy fluctuations can retain genuinely quantum-coherent contributions. We foliate state space into "minimum-variance leaves," defined by minimizing the average energy variance over all pure-state decompositions, with the minimum set by the quantum Fisher information. On each leaf we construct the least-biased state compatible with normalization and mean energy, defining a leaf-canonical ensemble. The Gibbs ensemble is recovered on the distinguished commuting leaf, while generic states are organized by their leaf label. This structure provides a natural setting to extend eigenstate thermalization beyond equilibrium via a "leaf typicality" hypothesis. According to that hypothesis, under unitary time evolution local observables depend only on the leaf and energy and, at all times, are reproduced by evolving a representative (pure) state drawn from the optimal ensemble.

arXiv:2602.12212v1 公告类型：cross 摘要：平衡统计系综与哈密顿量交换，因此能量本征基不具有相干性。我们开发了一个热力学框架，其中能量波动可以保留真正的量子相干贡献。我们将状态空间叶化为“最小方差叶”，通过最小化所有纯态分解的平均能量方差来定义，其中最小值由量子费希尔信息设置。在每个叶子上，我们构建与归一化和平均能量兼容的最小偏差状态，定义叶子规范系综。吉布斯系综在杰出的通勤叶子上恢复，而通用状态则按其叶子标签组织。这种结构提供了一个自然的设置，通过“叶典型性”假设将本征态热化扩展到平衡之外。根据该假设，在酉时间演化下，局部可观测量仅取决于叶子和能量，并且在任何时候都通过演化从最优系综中提取的代表性（纯）状态来再现。
