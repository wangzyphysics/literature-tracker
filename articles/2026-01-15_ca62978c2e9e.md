## Abstract
arXiv:2601.09284v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Heterostructures combining graphene with 3d transition metal ferromagnets (FMs) enable various spin-based phenomena at ultrafast timescales. However, challenges such as the interfacial impedance mismatch, FM deposition-induced defect generation, and interface modification by interfacial coupling or hybridization can impede their functionalization for spin-orbitronics. In this work, we utilize insulating TiOx barrier layers (BLs) to modify the interfacial spin conductance structurally, disentangle spin pumping and magnetic proximity effects (MPE), and establish external control over ultrafast magnetization dynamics in single-layer graphene/TiOx/Co systems. All-optical time-resolved magneto-optical Kerr effect measurements of femtosecond to nanosecond spin dynamics reveal systematic tunability of ultrafast magnetic parameters via barrier engineering. The thickness-dependent damping modulation in Co indicates strong spin pumping, with interfacial spin transparency close to half its physical limit in the presence of an ultrathin BL, where MPE is eliminated. Our results show that appropriately chosen ultrathin BLs can prevent interfacial alterations from ferromagnetic metals, facilitating efficient spin detection in graphene and enhancing control over spin angular momentum dissipation in graphene/FM interfaces.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.09284v1 公告类型：新 摘要：石墨烯与 3d 过渡金属铁磁体 (FM) 相结合的异质结构在超快时间尺度上实现了各种基于自旋的现象。然而，诸如界面阻抗失配、FM沉积引起的缺陷产生以及界面耦合或杂交引起的界面修饰等挑战可能会阻碍其自旋轨道电子学的功能化。在这项工作中，我们利用绝缘TiOx势垒层（BL）从结构上改变界面自旋电导，解开自旋泵浦和磁邻近效应（MPE），并建立对单层石墨烯/TiOx/Co系统中超快磁化动力学的外部控制。飞秒至纳秒自旋动力学的全光时间分辨磁光克尔效应测量揭示了通过势垒工程的超快磁参数的系统可调性。 Co 中与厚度相关的阻尼调制表明强自旋泵浦，在超薄 BL 存在的情况下，界面自旋透明度接近其物理极限的一半，其中 MPE 被消除。我们的结果表明，适当选择的超薄BL可以防止铁磁金属的界面变化，促进石墨烯中有效的自旋检测，并增强对石墨烯/FM界面中自旋角动量耗散的控制。
