## Abstract
Nature Computational Science, Published online: 22 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s43588-025-00932-4 This Review explores the integration of deep learning in first-principles electronic structure calculations, addressing the accuracy–efficiency dilemma of traditional algorithms and extending first-principles methods to unprecedented scales and complexity.

《自然计算科学》，在线发布：2025 年 12 月 22 日； doi:10.1038/s43588-025-00932-4 这篇综述探讨了深度学习在第一性原理电子结构计算中的集成，解决了传统算法的准确性-效率困境，并将第一性原理方法扩展到前所未有的规模和复杂性。
