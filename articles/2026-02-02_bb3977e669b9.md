## Abstract
arXiv:2510.13970v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Periodically driven quantum systems can host non-equilibrium phenomena without static analogs, including in their entanglement dynamics. Here, we discover $temporal$ $entanglement$ $transitions$ (TET) in a Floquet spin chain, which correspond to a quantum phase transition in the spectrum of the entanglement Hamiltonian and are signaled by dynamical spontaneous symmetry breaking. We identify the symmetry principles underlying these transitions: they appear when the driven Hamiltonian preserves global symmetry (here, $\mathbb{Z}_2$), the initial state respects this symmetry, and the reduced density matrix carries weight in both subsystem-parity sectors, with TET occurring precisely when the sector weights become equal (given the previous two conditions are also satisfied). Intriguingly, we find these transitions across a broad range of driving frequencies (from adiabatic to high-frequency regime) and independently of drive details, where they manifest as periodic, sharp entanglement spectrum reorganizations marked by the Schmidt-gap closure, a vanishing entanglement echo, and symmetry-quantum-number flips, while remaining invisible to conventional local observables. At high frequencies, the entanglement Hamiltonian acquires an intrinsic timescale decoupled from the drive period, rendering the transitions genuine steady-state features. Finite-size scaling reveals universal critical behavior with correlation-length exponent $\nu=1$, matching equilibrium Ising universality despite its emergence from purely dynamical mechanisms decoupled from static criticality. Our work establishes TET as novel features in Floquet quantum matter.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2510.13970v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：周期性驱动的量子系统可以在没有静态类似物的情况下承载非平衡现象，包括在其纠缠动力学中。在这里，我们在 Floquet 自旋链中发现了 $temporal$ $entanglement$ $transitions$ (TET)，它对应于纠缠哈密顿量谱中的量子相变，并通过动态自发对称性破缺来表示。我们确定了这些转变背后的对称原理：当驱动哈密顿量保持全局对称性（此处为 $\mathbb{Z}_2$）时，它们就会出现，初始状态尊重这种对称性，并且降低的密度矩阵在两个子系统奇偶校验扇区中都具有权重，而 TET 恰好在扇区权重相等时发生（假定也满足前两个条件）。有趣的是，我们发现这些转变跨越了广泛的驱动频率（从绝热到高频状态），并且与驱动细节无关，它们表现为周期性的、尖锐的纠缠谱重组，以施密特间隙闭合、消失的纠缠回波和对称量子数翻转为标志，同时对传统的局部可观测值保持不可见。在高频下，纠缠哈密顿量获得与驱动周期解耦的内在时间尺度，使转变具有真正的稳态特征。有限尺寸标度揭示了相关长度指数 $\nu=1$ 的普遍临界行为，匹配均衡伊辛普遍性，尽管它是从与静态临界性脱钩的纯动态机制中出现的。我们的工作将 TET 确立为 Floquet 量子物质的新特征。
