## Abstract
Vitrimers are polymer networks that can rearrange their topology via thermally-triggered dynamic covalent bonds. While their versatility offers interesting paths for developing new applications, one needs, first, to understand how their dynamics depends on the topology of the network to exploit their large potential. These last years, boronic esters, especially dioxaborolanes, have been widely studied as moieties to create reversible covalent bonds. Recently, dioxazaborocanes, moieties featuring a labile N–B bond, have been also proposed, showing much faster dynamics at high temperatures compared to dioxaborolanes when incorporated in vitrimers. Herein, we study the viscoelastic properties of vitrimers obtained from linear precursors bearing complementary dioxazaborocane and dioxaborolane moieties. By playing with the density of these two different moieties, we can accurately control the proportion of free functional groups present in the vitrimers and, therefore, study their influence on the dynamics of the reversible network. It is found that the network dynamics can be either slowed down or accelerated in presence of dioxaborolane or dioxazaborocane free functional groups, respectively, regardless of the total density of functional groups. The results also show a non-monotonic dependence of the network relaxation time as a function of the cross-linking density, that we attribute to the antagonistic effects of the sub-diffusion process of the dynamic moieties and the restricted mobility of the polymer chains within the network.

## 摘要（中文）
Vitrimer 是一种聚合物网络，可以通过热触发的动态共价键重新排列其拓扑结构。虽然它们的多功能性为开发新应用程序提供了有趣的途径，但首先需要了解它们的动态如何依赖于网络拓扑，以开发它们的巨大潜力。近年来，硼酸酯，尤其是二氧硼杂硼烷，作为形成可逆共价键的部分被广泛研究。最近，还提出了二氧杂硼烷，即具有不稳定 N-B 键的部分，与二氧杂硼烷掺入 vitrimer 时，在高温下表现出更快的动力学。在此，我们研究了从带有互补的二氧杂硼烷和二氧杂硼烷部分的线性前体获得的vitrimer的粘弹性。通过调整这两个不同部分的密度，我们可以准确控制玻璃体中存在的自由官能团的比例，从而研究它们对可逆网络动力学的影响。研究发现，无论官能团的总密度如何，在存在二氧硼杂硼烷或不含二氧杂硼烷的官能团的情况下，网络动力学可以分别减慢或加速。结果还显示网络弛豫时间作为交联密度的函数的非单调依赖性，我们将其归因于动态部分的子扩散过程和网络内聚合物链的受限流动性的拮抗效应。
