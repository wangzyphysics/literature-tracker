## Abstract
Photogenerated spin-correlated radical pairs (SCRPs) in donor–bridge–acceptor (D–B–A) systems represent a promising platform for developing molecular quantum technologies using their sensitivity to magnetic and electric fields. For SCRPs to be sensitive to weak magnetic fields, they need to have sufficiently long lifetimes for efficient spin evolutions. We recently identified that H8–BINOL, a partially reduced form of BINOL, can act as a bridge to enable SCRPs of >100 ns lifetimes, enabling a large magnetic modulation of molecular emission in solutions at room temperature (J. Am. Chem. Soc. 2025, 147, 13, 11062). The present study explores the role of chiral BINOL as a bridging segment in D–χ–A molecules, where boron dipyrromethene (BODIPY) and triarylamine (TAA) derivatives act as acceptors and donors, respectively. We systematically varied both the connectivity of the BINOL bridge and the BINOL scaffold itself, finding that these structural and electronic changes significantly influence the charge-transfer dynamics. These findings highlight the potential of BINOL-based bridges to facilitate spin-selective phenomena and controlled charge transfer processes. The insights gained from this work contribute to the development of advanced chiral molecular architectures for quantum sensing and spintronics.

## 摘要（中文）
供体-桥-受体（D-B-A）系统中的光生自旋相关自由基对（SCRP）代表了利用其对磁场和电场的敏感性开发分子量子技术的有前景的平台。为了使 SCRP 对弱磁场敏感，它们需要具有足够长的寿命以实现有效的自旋演化。我们最近发现，H8-BINOL（BINOL 的部分还原形式）可以充当桥梁，使 SCRP 的寿命超过 100 ns，从而能够在室温下对溶液中的分子发射进行大的磁调制 (J. Am. Chem. Soc. 2025, 147, 13, 11062)。本研究探讨了手性 BINOL 作为 D–χ–A 分子中桥接片段的作用，其中硼二吡咯亚甲基 (BODIPY) 和三芳胺 (TAA) 衍生物分别充当受体和供体。我们系统地改变了 BINOL 桥和 BINOL 支架本身的连接性，发现这些结构和电子变化显着影响电荷转移动力学。这些发现凸显了基于联醇的桥在促进自旋选择性现象和受控电荷转移过程方面的潜力。从这项工作中获得的见解有助于开发用于量子传感和自旋电子学的先进手性分子结构。
