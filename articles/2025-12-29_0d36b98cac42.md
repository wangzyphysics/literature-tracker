## Abstract
arXiv:2505.00920v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Van der Waals (vdW) materials, with their unique combination of electronic, optical, and magnetic properties, are emerging as promising platforms for exploring excitonic phenomena. Thus far, the choice of materials with exceptional excitonic response has been limited to two-dimensional (2D) configurations of vdW materials. At the same time, large interlayer distance and the possibility to create a variety of heterostructures offers an opportunity to control the dielectric screening in van der Waals heterostructures and van der Waals 3D materials, thus engineering the excitonic properties. Here, we reveal that bulk vdW crystal CrCl3 answers this quest with a record exciton binding energy of 1.64 eV owing to a delicate interplay of quasi-2D electronic confinement and local magnetic correlations. We also suggest that the non-local magnetic correlations play an important role in the temperature dependence of photoluminescence intensity. Furthermore, we observe colossal binding energies in vdW crystals NbOCl2 (0.66 eV) and MoCl3 (0.35 eV) and formulate a universal exciton binding energy dependence on bandgap for 2D and 3D vdW materials. Hence, our findings establish a fundamental link between the layered structure of vdW materials and their excitonic properties.

arXiv:2505.00920v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：范德华 (vdW) 材料以其独特的电子、光学和磁特性组合，正在成为探索激子现象的有前景的平台。到目前为止，具有特殊激子响应的材料的选择仅限于 vdW 材料的二维 (2D) 配置。同时，大的层间距离和创建各种异质结构的可能性提供了控制范德华异质结构和范德华3D材料中的介电屏蔽的机会，从而设计激子特性。在这里，我们揭示了块状 vdW 晶体 CrCl3 以创纪录的 1.64 eV 激子结合能回答了这一问题，这归因于准二维电子限制和局域磁关联的微妙相互作用。我们还认为，非局域磁关联在光致发光强度的温度依赖性中起着重要作用。此外，我们观察到 vdW 晶体 NbOCl2 (0.66 eV) 和 MoCl3 (0.35 eV) 中的巨大结合能，并制定了 2D 和 3D vdW 材料的通用激子结合能与带隙的关系。因此，我们的研究结果建立了 vdW 材料的层状结构与其激子特性之间的基本联系。
