## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00440v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Culture is not just traits but a dynamic system of interdependent beliefs, practices and artefacts embedded in cognitive, social and material structures. Culture evolves as these entities interact, generating path dependence, attractor states and tension, with long-term stability punctuated by rapid systemic transformations. Cultural learning and creativity is modelled as coherence-seeking information processing: individuals filter, transform and recombine input in light of prior acquisitions and dissonance reduction, thereby creating increasingly structured worldviews. Higher-order traits such as goals, skills, norms and cognitive gadgets act as emergent metafilters that regulate subsequent selection by defining what counts as coherent. Together, these filtering processes self-organise into epistemic niches, echo chambers, polarised groups and institutions that channel information flows and constrain future evolution. In this view, LLMs and recommender algorithms are products of cultural embeddings that now act back on cultural systems by automated filtering and recombination of information, reshaping future dynamics of cultural systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00440v1 公告类型：新 摘要：文化不仅仅是特征，而且是嵌入认知、社会和物质结构中的相互依存的信仰、实践和人工制品的动态系统。随着这些实体的相互作用，文化不断发展，产生路径依赖、吸引子状态和张力，并伴随着快速的系统转型而保持长期稳定。文化学习和创造力被建模为寻求连贯性的信息处理：个人根据先前的习得和减少不和谐来过滤、转换和重新组合输入，从而创造出日益结构化的世界观。目标、技能、规范和认知工具等高阶特征充当新兴的元过滤器，通过定义什么是连贯的来调节随后的选择。这些过滤过程一起自组织成认知生态位、回音室、两极分化群体和机构，引导信息流动并限制未来的演变。从这个角度来看，法学硕士和推荐算法是文化嵌入的产物，它们现在通过信息的自动过滤和重组来反作用于文化系统，重塑文化系统的未来动态。
