## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00628v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Accurate hindcasting of extreme sea state events is essential for coastal engineering, risk assessment, and climate studies. However, the reliability of numerical wave models remains limited by uncertainties in physical parameterizations and model inputs. This study presents a novel calibration framework based on Bayesian Optimization (BO), leveraging the Tree structured Parzen Estimator (TPE) to efficiently estimate uncertain sink term parameters, specifically bottom friction dissipation, depth induced breaking, and wave dissipation from strong opposing currents, in the ANEMOC-3 hindcast wave model. The proposed method enables joint optimization of continuous parameters and discrete model structures, significantly reducing discrepancies between model outputs and observations. Applied to a one month period encompassing multiple intense storm events along the French Atlantic coast, the calibrated model demonstrates improved agreement with buoy measurements, achieving lower bias, RMSE, and scatter index relative to the default sea$-$state solver configuration. The results highlight the potential of BO to automate and enhance wave model calibration, offering a scalable and flexible approach applicable to a wide range of geophysical modeling problems. Future extensions include multi-objective optimization, uncertainty quantification, and integration of additional observational datasets.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00628v1 发布类型：新 摘要：极端海况事件的准确后报对于海岸工程、风险评估和气候研究至关重要。然而，数值波浪模型的可靠性仍然受到物理参数化和模型输入的不确定性的限制。本研究提出了一种基于贝叶斯优化 (BO) 的新颖校准框架，利用树结构 Parzen 估计器 (TPE) 来有效估计 ANEMOC-3 后报波浪模型中的不确定汇项参数，特别是底部摩擦耗散、深度诱导破碎和强反向流引起的波浪耗散。所提出的方法能够联合优化连续参数和离散模型结构，显着减少模型输出和观测值之间的差异。应用于法国大西洋沿岸发生多次强烈风暴事件的一个月期间，校准模型显示出与浮标测量值的一致性得到改善，相对于默认的sea$-$state求解器配置实现了较低的偏差、RMSE和散射指数。结果凸显了 BO 在自动化和增强波浪模型校准方面的潜力，提供了适用于各种地球物理建模问题的可扩展且灵活的方法。未来的扩展包括多目标优化、不确定性量化以及附加观测数据集的集成。
