## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14870v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: In order to achieve its ambitious luminosity target, the SuperKEKB collider must achieve and sustain high beam currents on the order of Ampere in its storage rings. This requires continuous top-up injection and operation with a two-bunch injection scheme, injecting two 96 ns spaced bunches in a single injection cycle. An important input for tuning the injection beam is the position reading from a dedicated beam position monitor (BPM), located after the septum magnets, slightly upstream of where the injected and stored beams converge. Previously, the readout electronics used for these special BPMs were not capable of independent measurement of both bunches in the two-bunch injection mode and modification of the concerned devices and their firmware was not feasible. The opportunity was taken to develop a new readout device based on the AMD/Xilinx RF System on a Chip (RFSoC) platform with the goal of not only providing a sufficiently flexible and performant readout solution for the concerned BPMs, but also to evaluate and gain experience with the platform for beam monitor electronics applications. This paper is concerned with the details of this development as well as evaluation and operation of the developed device.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14870v1 公告类型：新 摘要：为了实现其雄心勃勃的光度目标，SuperKEKB 对撞机必须在其存储环中实现并维持安培量级的高束流。这需要连续补充注入并采用两束注入方案进行操作，在单个注入周期中注入两个间隔为 96 ns 的束。调整注入光束的一个重要输入是来自专用光束位置监视器 (BPM) 的位置读数，该监视器位于隔膜磁体之后，注入光束和存储光束会聚位置的稍上游。此前，用于这些特殊 BPM 的读出电子设备无法在双束注入模式下独立测量两个束，并且对相关设备及其固件进行修改也是不可行的。我们借此机会开发了一种基于 AMD/Xilinx 射频片上系统 (RFSoC) 平台的新型读出设备，其目标不仅是为相关 BPM 提供足够灵活和高性能的读出解决方案，而且还评估用于光束监测电子应用的平台并获得经验。本文涉及该开发的细节以及所开发设备的评估和操作。
