## Abstract
arXiv:2506.11834v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Recently, one can observe a renewed interest in coupling of spin waves (magnons) and collective charge oscillations (plasmons), especially in two-dimensional systems. Several mechanisms of the magnon-plasmon hybridization in ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic systems have been proposed. Here, we consider another mechanism of magnon-plasmon hybridization, which is based on the linear magnetoelectric interaction. As a specific system we consider a monolayer of vanadium-based diselenide with perpendicular easy-axis magnetic anisotropy and Dzialoshinskii-Moriya interaction. The derived parameter of magnon-plasmon coupling is proportional to the magnetoelectric constant. Assuming for this constant an adequate experimental value, we calculate dispersion relations of the hybridized magnon-plasmon mods. Moreover, we also show that an external electric field normal to the layer (due to a gate voltage) can be used as a tool to tune the magnon modes and this way also hybridized magnon-plasmon coupling. A specific case of magnon-plasmon coupling based on tuning Dzialoshinskii-Moriya interaction is also considered.

arXiv:2506.11834v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：最近，人们可以观察到人们对自旋波（磁振子）和集体电荷振荡（等离子体激元）的耦合重新产生了兴趣，特别是在二维系统中。已经提出了铁磁和反铁磁系统中磁振子-等离子体杂化的几种机制。在这里，我们考虑另一种基于线性磁电相互作用的磁振子-等离子体杂化机制。作为一个特定的系统，我们考虑具有垂直易轴磁各向异性和 Dzialoshinskii-Moriya 相互作用的钒基二硒化物单层。磁振子耦合的导出参数与磁电常数成正比。假设该常数有足够的实验值，我们计算了混合磁振子-等离子体模的色散关系。此外，我们还表明，垂直于层的外部电场（由于栅极电压）可以用作调整磁振子模式的工具，这样也可以混合磁振子-等离子体耦合。还考虑了基于调节 Dzialoshinskii-Moriya 相互作用的磁振子-等离子体耦合的具体情况。
