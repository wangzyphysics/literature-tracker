## Abstract
Anion chemistry plays a critical role in determining interfacial structure and charge storage in electric double-layer capacitors, yet its microscopic influence remains unclear. Here, constant-potential molecular dynamics simulations are employed to examine anion-specific effects in potassium-ion supercapacitors using graphene electrodes and propylene carbonate-based organic electrolytes. By fixing the cation (K⁺) and solvent while varying the anion (TFSI⁻, PF6⁻, and ClO4⁻), we isolate the impact of anion geometry and ion-pairing behavior on electric double-layer formation. The results reveal pronounced anion-dependent differences in ion adsorption, interfacial structure, and capacitive response. The KTFSI electrolyte exhibits the highest integral capacitance, arising from enhanced accumulation of K⁺ ions at the electrode surface due to weakened ion-pair correlations. In contrast, PF6⁻ and ClO4⁻ form stronger ion pairs with K⁺, limiting cation availability and reducing capacitance. These findings demonstrate charge storage can be optimized by tunning the geometry and chemical form of anions in K-ion storage systems

## 摘要（中文）
阴离子化学在确定双电层电容器的界面结构和电荷存储方面发挥着关键作用，但其微观影响仍不清楚。在这里，采用恒电位分子动力学模拟来检查使用石墨烯电极和碳酸丙烯酯基有机电解质的钾离子超级电容器中的阴离子特异性效应。通过固定阳离子 (K⁺) 和溶剂，同时改变阴离子（TFSI⁻、PF6⁻和 ClO4⁻），我们隔离了阴离子几何形状和离子配对行为对双电层形成的影响。结果揭示了离子吸附、界面结构和电容响应方面明显的阴离子依赖性差异。 KTFSI 电解质表现出最高的积分电容，这是由于离子对相关性减弱而导致 K⁺ 离子在电极表面的积累增强所致。相比之下，PF6⁻和ClO4⁻与K⁺形成更强的离子对，限制了阳离子的可用性并降低了电容。这些发现表明，可以通过调整钾离子存储系统中阴离子的几何形状和化学形式来优化电荷存储
