## Abstract
arXiv:2602.14711v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We report the discovery of a new compound, Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$, and reveal the hidden time-reversal-like degenerate states within it. Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$ is an antiferromagnet with the distorted kagome lattice of Ce atoms, in which several fractional magnetization plateaus emerge with the increase of magnetic field. At the 1/2 magnetization plateau, obvious hysteresis has been observed in the magnetoresistance and Hall resistivity during the rise and fall of the magnetic field. However, hysteresis vanishes in the corresponding measurements of magnetization, indicating the existence of degenerate states with the same net magnetization but different electronic transport properties. The degenerate states can be connected by the time-reversal-like operation. In addition, by comparing with HoAgGe, it is suggested that the special crystal structure in Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$ may have a shielding effect on the time-reversal-like operation, thereby affecting the distinction of degenerate states. Our work establishes Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$ as an example of utilizing electronic transport properties to identify and distinguish hidden symmetries in frustrated magnetic systems.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.14711v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们报告了一种新化合物 Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$ 的发现，并揭示了其中隐藏的类似时间反转的简并态。 Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$是一种具有扭曲的Ce原子戈戈美晶格的反铁磁体，其中随着磁场的增加出现几个分数磁化平台。在1/2磁化平台处，在磁场上升和下降过程中，磁阻和霍尔电阻率观察到明显的磁滞现象。然而，磁滞在相应的磁化强度测量中消失，表明存在具有相同净磁化强度但电子传输特性不同的简并态。简并态可以通过类似时间反转的操作来连接。此外，通过与HoAgGe的比较，表明Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$中特殊的晶体结构可能对类时间反转操作产生屏蔽作用，从而影响简并态的区分。我们的工作以 Ce$_3$MgBi$_5$ 为例，利用电子输运特性来识别和区分受挫磁系统中隐藏的对称性。
