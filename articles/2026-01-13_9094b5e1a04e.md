## Abstract
Spectroelectrochemical platforms that integrate electron paramagnetic resonance (EPR) with electrochemistry offer unprecedented insight into electron transfer and catalytic mechanisms, yet their potential has been constrained by electrode materials that compromise sensitivity and stability. There is a fundamental challenge to provide high electrochemical electrode conductivity whilst minimising microwave losses in the EPR cavity. We use reductive ‘nanotubide’ processing to prepare high-quality buckypapers from long, undamaged single-walled carbon nanotubes. The resulting electrodes provide a unique combination of high conductivity, low microwave loss, and low EPR background. With their excellent mechanical robustness and high accessible surface area, these free-standing electrodes enable film-electrochemical EPR (FE-EPR) spectroelectrochemistry under operando conditions across a 2V potential window and broad pH range. Redox active species can be readily attached to the electrode surface, as illustrated by diazonium-mediated TEMPO-grafting. This model system demonstrates exceptionally stable spectroelectrochemical performance, providing a Pourbaix diagram of all three redox states (TEMPO+, TEMPO•, TEMPOH). The application to redox catalysis is illustrated using TEMPO-mediated alcohol oxidation; we track radical intermediates in real time and extract kinetic parameters, revealing how surface properties govern electron transfer and catalytic turnover. By providing a stable platform for operando spectroelectrochemistry, carbon nanotube buckypapers enable mechanistic insight into electron-transfer reactions.

## 摘要（中文）
将电子顺磁共振（EPR）与电化学相结合的光谱电化学平台为电子转移和催化机制提供了前所未有的见解，但其潜力受到电极材料的限制，而电极材料会损害灵敏度和稳定性。提供高电化学电极电导率同时最大限度地减少 EPR 腔中的微波损失是一个根本性的挑战。我们使用还原“纳米管”工艺，用长的、未损坏的单壁碳纳米管制备高质量的巴基纸。由此产生的电极具有高电导率、低微波损耗和低 EPR 背景的独特组合。这些独立式电极凭借其出色的机械稳定性和高可接触表面积，可在 2V 电位窗口和宽 pH 范围内的操作条件下实现薄膜电化学 EPR (FE-EPR) 光谱电化学。氧化还原活性物质可以很容易地附着到电极表面，如重氮介导的 TEMPO 接枝所示。该模型系统表现出异常稳定的光谱电化学性能，提供所有三种氧化还原态（TEMPO+、TEMPO•、TEMPOH）的普贝图。使用 TEMPO 介导的醇氧化来说明氧化还原催化的应用；我们实时跟踪自由基中间体并提取动力学参数，揭示表面特性如何控制电子转移和催化周转。通过为操作光谱电化学提供稳定的平台，碳纳米管巴基纸能够深入了解电子转移反应的机理。
