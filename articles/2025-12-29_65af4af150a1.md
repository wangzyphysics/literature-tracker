## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21790v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate quantum tunneling of charge carriers through a periodic superlattice in twisted bilayer graphene (TBG) with rectangular potential barriers, including the presence of a defect, using a low-energy continuum model. Transmission probabilities are numerically analyzed depending on the parameters of the problem, highlighting the roles of twist angle, number of barriers, barrier geometry, and the presence of a defect barrier within the superlattice. Our numerical results reveal that transmission is highly sensitive to these parameters: reducing the twist angle changes the number, depth, and position of transmission gaps and resonance peaks. The presence of defect affects the transmission, leading to the appearance of tunneling states inside transmission gaps with energy position can be tuned by the well width. At low incident energy, the transmission for normally incident electrons is perfect or nearly perfect, independent of the twist angle and the number of barriers. However, at large incident energy, the transmission becomes distinctly anisotropic, reflecting the separation of Dirac cones induced by twist angle variations. The presence of defects, particularly at smaller twist angles, provides additional control of tunneling behavior, allowing complete suppression of Klein tunneling under certain conditions. These findings extend the established understanding of miniband transport in periodic graphene systems and open new possibilities for twist-tunable nanoelectronic and quantum devices.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21790v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们使用低能连续介质模型研究了电荷载流子穿过具有矩形势垒（包括缺陷存在）的扭曲双层石墨烯 (TBG) 中的周期性超晶格的量子隧道效应。根据问题的参数对传输概率进行数值分析，强调扭转角、势垒数量、势垒几何形状以及超晶格内缺陷势垒的存在的作用。我们的数值结果表明，传输对这些参数高度敏感：减小扭转角度会改变传输间隙和共振峰的数量、深度和位置。缺陷的存在会影响传输，导致传输间隙内出现隧道态，并且能量位置可以通过阱宽度来调节。在低入射能量下，垂直入射电子的传输是完美或接近完美的，与扭转角和势垒数量无关。然而，在大入射能量下，透射变得明显各向异性，反映了扭转角变化引起的狄拉克锥的分离。缺陷的存在，特别是在较小扭转角下，提供了对隧道行为的额外控制，允许在某些条件下完全抑制克莱因隧道。这些发现扩展了对周期性石墨烯系统中微带传输的既定理解，并为扭转可调纳米电子和量子器件开辟了新的可能性。
