## Abstract
arXiv:2602.12915v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We proof the non-renormalization of the Hall viscosity by Coulomb interactions for integer and fractional quantum Hall Jain states building on previous results obtained for the Hall conductivity. We employ Wigner-Weyl calculus in order to represent the Hall viscosity in terms of a topological invariant comprised of Green functions and work within the composite fermion field theory model of Jain states of the fractional quantum Hall fluid presented by Lopez and Fradkin. The topological expression is first derived within the free field theory of electrons and explicitly calculated for this case as well as in the mean field approximation of the composite fermion theory Jain states. The topological orbital spin of composite fermions distinguishes their mean field treatment from that of electrons resulting in an additional topological contribution. We then argue that the introduction of Coulomb interactions does not lead to perturbative corrections of the Hall viscosity in both integer and fractional quantum Hall fluids. The proof relies on the assumptions of homogeneity and rotational invariance of an underlying sample modulo the vector potential giving rise to the homogeneous external magnetic field. These conditions imply a Hall viscosity per emergent quasiparticle number density quantized in units of one half times the average quasiparticle orbital spin or one quarter times the Wen-Zee shift. The latter features a contribution from the composite fermion topological orbital spin relative to that of electrons.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.12915v1 公告类型：新 摘要：基于先前获得的霍尔电导率结果，我们通过整数和分数量子霍尔 Jain 态的库仑相互作用证明了霍尔粘度的非重正化。我们采用维格纳-韦尔微积分，以由格林函数组成的拓扑不变量来表示霍尔粘度，并在 Lopez 和 Fradkin 提出的分数量子霍尔流体 Jain 态的复合费米子场理论模型中工作。拓扑表达式首先在电子自由场理论中导出，并针对这种情况以及 Jain 所说的复合费米子理论的平均场近似进行了明确计算。复合费米子的拓扑轨道自旋将其平均场处理与电子的平均场处理区分开来，从而产生额外的拓扑贡献。然后我们认为库仑相互作用的引入不会导致整数和分数量子霍尔流体中霍尔粘度的微扰校正。该证明依赖于对产生均匀外部磁场的矢量势进行模的基础样本的均匀性和旋转不变性的假设。这些条件意味着每出现准粒子数密度的霍尔粘度以平均准粒子轨道自旋的二分之一或文泽位移的四分之一为单位进行量化。后者的特点是复合费米子拓扑轨道自旋相对于电子的贡献。
