## Abstract
arXiv:2505.20436v3 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Within the symmetric mass generation (SMG) approach to the construction of lattice chiral gauge theories, one attempts to use interactions to render mirror fermions massive without symmetry breaking, thus obtaining the desired chiral massless spectrum. If successful, the gauge field can be turned on, and thus a chiral gauge theory can be constructed in the phase in which SMG takes place. In this paper we argue that the zeros that often replace the mirror poles of fermion two-point functions in an SMG phase should be ``kinematical'' singularities. We conjecture that the SMG interactions generate opposite-chirality bound states, which combine with the gapped elementary mirror states to form massive Dirac fermions. The propagator zeros can then be avoided by choosing an appropriate set of interpolating fields that contains both elementary and composite fields. This allows us to apply general constraints on the existence of a chiral fermion spectrum which are valid in the presence of (non-gauge) interactions of arbitrary strength, including in any SMG phase. Using a suitably constructed one-particle lattice hamiltonian describing the fermion spectrum, we formulate a generalized no-go theorem which establishes the conditions for the applicability of the Nielsen-Ninomiya theorem to this hamiltonian. If these conditions are satisfied, the massless fermion spectrum must be vector-like. We add some general observations on the strong coupling limit of SMG models. We also elaborate on the qualitative differences between four-dimensional and two-dimensional theories that limit the lessons that can be drawn from two-dimensional models. Finally, we compile a list of open questions which must be addressed in any SMG model in order to determine whether or not it is subject to the generalized no-go theorem.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2505.20436v3 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：在构建晶格手性规范理论的对称质量生成（SMG）方法中，人们试图利用相互作用使镜面费米子在不对称破缺的情况下具有质量，从而获得所需的手性无质量谱。如果成功，规范场就可以打开，从而可以在SMG发生的阶段构建手性规范理论。在本文中，我们认为在 SMG 相位中经常取代费米子两点函数的镜像极点的零点应该是“运动学”奇点。我们推测 SMG 相互作用会产生相反的手性束缚态，这些束缚态与带隙的基本镜像态结合形成大量的狄拉克费米子。然后可以通过选择一组适当的包含基本场和复合场的内插场来避免传播器零。这使我们能够对手性费米子谱的存在应用一般约束，该谱在存在任意强度的（非规范）相互作用的情况下（包括在任何 SMG 相中）都是有效的。使用适当构造的描述费米子谱的单粒子晶格哈密顿量，我们制定了广义 no-go 定理，该定理为尼尔森-二宫定理对该哈密顿量的适用性建立了条件。如果满足这些条件，无质量费米子谱必定是矢量状的。我们添加了一些关于 SMG 模型强耦合极限的一般观察结果。我们还详细阐述了四维和二维理论之间的质的差异，这些差异限制了从二维模型中吸取的教训。最后，我们编制了任何 SMG 模型中都必须解决的开放问题列表，以确定它是否遵循广义不可行定理。
