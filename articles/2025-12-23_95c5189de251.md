## Abstract
We studied and characterized structural, spectroscopic and dynamic properties of the triangular ring isomer of water hexamer anions (H2O)6- at low temperatures. This species binds an excess electron in a solvent cavity surrounded by six dangling hydrogen atoms in a bulk hydrated electron fashion. We find using high level quantum chemistry and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations that the species represents a local minimum on the potential energy surface (PES). Structurally, the anion is composed of two cyclic water trimers held together by a single excess electron that acts as a weak chemical bond. The most spectacular and dominant feature of the dynamics of the species is a 7 cm-1 wavenumber quasi-translational motion periodically approaching and moving away the water trimer units. Since the local potential well of the isomer is extremely shallow, we predict the species to be metastable and very fragile. As a consequence, at temperatures higher than $\sim$60 K, the isomer thermally dissociates to two stable neutral trimers and a free electron. At low temperatures, compression of the inter ring distance may lead to isomerization of the species and/or the concomitant electron detachment via quantum tunneling, resulting either a stable anionic or neutral water hexamer or two neutral trimers. Based on simple quantum mechanical model calculations, in this temperature regime, we place the minimal lifetime of the triangular ring isomer in the microdecon domain.

## 摘要（中文）
我们研究并表征了水六聚体阴离子（H2O）6-的三角环异构体在低温下的结构、光谱和动力学性质。该物质在溶剂空腔中结合多余的电子，该溶剂空腔以大量水合电子的方式被六个悬空氢原子包围。我们通过高级量子化学和分子动力学 (MD) 模拟发现，该物质代表势能表面 (PES) 上的局部最小值。在结构上，阴离子由两个环状水三聚体组成，两个环状水三聚体通过一个作为弱化学键的多余电子结合在一起。该物种动力学最引人注目和最显着的特征是周期性接近和远离水三聚体单元的 7 cm-1 波数准平移运动。由于异构体的局部势阱非常浅，我们预测该物种是亚稳态的并且非常脆弱。因此，在高于 $\sim$60 K 的温度下，异构体热解离成两个稳定的中性三聚体和一个自由电子。在低温下，环间距离的压缩可能导致物质的异构化和/或伴随的电子通过量子隧道脱离，从而产生稳定的阴离子或中性水六聚体或两个中性三聚体。基于简单的量子力学模型计算，在此温度范围内，我们将三角环异构体的最小寿命置于微解域中。
