## Abstract
arXiv:2601.01534v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Tibetan Plateau (TP), known as the "Asian Water Tower," is currently undergoing a rapid wetting trend. While this moisture increase is often viewed as beneficial for water availability, it remains unclear whether the hydrological system itself is becoming more resilient or drifting toward instability. Here we apply an entropy-based framework to quantify the changing structural organization of the TP's soil moisture system. We show that from 2000 to 2024, regional wetting has driven a long-term decline in entropy, reflecting an increase in system order and stability due to enhanced hydrological buffering capacity. This stability is modulated by the El Ni$\~n$o-Southern Oscillation (ENSO), which regulates regional heterogeneity via a distinct spatial dipole. Crucially, however, CMIP6 climate projections reveal an alarming reversal: future warming triggers a rise in entropy. This transition signals a loss of systemic resilience, characterized by intensified spatial disorder and potential abrupt regime shifts by the mid-century. Our findings suggest that while current wetting provides a stabilizing buffer, continued warming is projected to amplify spatial heterogeneity, thereby destabilizing the Asian Water Tower, with significant risks for downstream water security.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.01534v1 发布类型：新 摘要：素有“亚洲水塔”之称的青藏高原（TP）目前正经历快速湿润趋势。虽然湿度的增加通常被认为有利于水的可用性，但目前尚不清楚水文系统本身是否变得更有弹性或走向不稳定。在这里，我们应用基于熵的框架来量化TP土壤水分系统结构组织的变化。我们发现，从2000年到2024年，区域湿润导致了熵的长期下降，反映了由于水文缓冲能力增强而导致的系统秩序和稳定性的增加。这种稳定性受到厄尔尼诺南方涛动（ENSO）的调节，它通过独特的空间偶极子调节区域异质性。然而，至关重要的是，CMIP6 气候预测揭示了一个令人震惊的逆转：未来变暖会引发熵的上升。这一转变标志着系统弹性的丧失，其特点是空间无序加剧，到本世纪中叶可能出现突然的政权更迭。我们的研究结果表明，虽然当前的湿润提供了稳定缓冲，但持续变暖预计会放大空间异质性，从而破坏亚洲水塔的稳定，给下游水安全带来重大风险。
