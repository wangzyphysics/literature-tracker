## Abstract
arXiv:2512.21240v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: High-energy blazar light curves, in X-rays and beyond, have historically preferred a log-normal flux distribution, signifying multiplicative processes either in the jet itself or due to connection(s) with accretion. Here we present 18 year archival Fermi-LAT light curves (0.1-100 GeV) of the flat spectrum radio quasar (FSRQ) CTA 102 from August 2008 to November 2025, which underwent a huge flare in 2017, with a $\sim$ factor of 100 jump in $\gamma$-ray flux, along with similar flaring in X-rays. Our statistical analyses confirm that neither the pre nor the post-flare total GeV light curves follow a strictly log-normal distribution. Instead, we observe a statistically significant reduction in skewness from the pre to the post-flare light curves, which implies the blazar transitioned from an energetic state with frequent flaring to a more plateaued state with occasional flaring. We further find that this state transition can be explained through magnetic relaxation, where many reconnection events caused the 2017 flare, after which the magnetic field was ordered and its energy reached a minimum. To explain this further, we use a Monte Carlo simulation of a modified minijets-in-a-jet model where GeV flares are produced only when a maximum number of minijets move toward the broad line region and towards the line of sight, in the context of an external Compton model. The flux distributions (both observed and simulated) could be fit by a modified log-normal power-law distribution, implying our minijets model can reproduce the GeV flares in CTA 102 as well as their flux distributions.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.21240v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：X 射线及其他领域的高能耀变体光变曲线历来更喜欢对数正态通量分布，这意味着喷流本身或由于与吸积的连接而产生的倍增过程。在这里，我们展示了 2008 年 8 月至 2025 年 11 月期间平坦光谱射电类星体 (FSRQ) CTA 102 的 18 年档案费米-LAT 光变曲线 (0.1-100 GeV)，该类星体在 2017 年经历了一次巨大的耀斑，$\gamma$ 射线通量的 $\sim$ 因子跃升了 100，同时 X 射线中也出现了类似的耀斑。我们的统计分析证实，耀斑前和耀斑后总 GeV 光变曲线均不遵循严格的对数正态分布。相反，我们观察到从耀斑前到耀斑后的光变曲线在统计上显着减少，这意味着耀变体从频繁耀斑的高能状态转变为偶尔耀斑的稳定状态。我们进一步发现这种状态转变可以通过磁弛豫来解释，其中许多重联事件导致了2017年的耀斑，之后磁场变得有序，其能量达到最小值。为了进一步解释这一点，我们使用改进的微型喷气机模型的蒙特卡罗模拟，其中仅当最大数量的微型喷气机在外部康普顿模型的背景下向宽线区域和视线移动时才会产生GeV耀斑。通量分布（观察到的和模拟的）可以通过修改后的对数正态幂律分布来拟合，这意味着我们的小型喷气机模型可以重现 CTA 102 中的 GeV 耀斑及其通量分布。
