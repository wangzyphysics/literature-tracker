## Abstract
Author(s): Sami S. Khamis et al. Black hole mergers may emit bursts of unknown particles. A search of data from a global magnetometer network during a gravitational-wave event, while not turning up such signals, sets the first lab-based limits on exotic emissions tied to dark matter. [Phys. Rev. X 15, 031048] Published Wed Aug 20, 2025
