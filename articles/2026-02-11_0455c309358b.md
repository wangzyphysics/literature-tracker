## Abstract
arXiv:2602.09545v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Interaction effects are dramatically enhanced in flat-band systems due to quenched kinetics, facilitating the binding of single excitations into composite quasiparticles. In this work, we present a comprehensive study of spin polarons over the entire momentum and energy space within the Mielke-Tasaki model using projected exact diagonalization. We identify distinct low-energy spin polarons at momenta q=0 and q=\pi, and also find multiple high-energy branches of spin polaron. It is demonstrated that the interaction-induced Hartree dispersion plays a decisive role in determining the momentum sector of low-energy spin polarons. Furthermore, by introducing a finite bandwidth, we unravel the underlying binding mechanisms: the formation of low-energy spin polarons is governed by the conventional virtual exchange mechanism, whereas the high-energy spin polarons arise from a joint effect of the effective attraction and virtual exchange. Our results suggest promising avenues for realizing spin polaron crystals and exploring novel superconducting pairing mechanisms in moir\'e materials like twisted WSe2 and MoTe2.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.09545v1 公告类型：新 摘要：由于淬灭动力学，平带系统中的相互作用效应显着增强，促进单激发与复合准粒子的结合。在这项工作中，我们使用投影精确对角化对 Mielke-Tasaki 模型中整个动量和能量空间的自旋极化子进行了全面研究。我们在动量 q=0 和 q=\pi 处识别出不同的低能自旋极化子，并且还发现了自旋极化子的多个高能分支。结果表明，相互作用引起的哈特里色散在确定低能自旋极化子的动量扇区方面起着决定性作用。此外，通过引入有限带宽，我们揭示了潜在的结合机制：低能自旋极化子的形成是由传统的虚拟交换机制控制的，而高能自旋极化子是由有效吸引力和虚拟交换的共同作用产生的。我们的研究结果为实现自旋极化子晶体和探索扭曲 WSe2 和 MoTe2 等摩尔纹材料中新型超导配对机制提供了有前景的途径。
