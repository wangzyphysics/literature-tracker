## Abstract
arXiv:2601.07346v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We investigate the ferromagnetic $q$-state Potts model on spherical Fibonacci graphs. These graphs are constructed by embedding quasi-uniform sites on a sphere and defining interactions via a chord-distance cutoff chosen to yield a network approximating four-neighbor connectivity. By combining Swendsen-Wang cluster Monte Carlo simulations with graph convolutional networks (GCNs), which operate directly on the adjacency structure and node spins, we develop a unified phase-classification framework applicable to both regular planar lattices and curved, irregular spherical graphs. Benchmarks on planar lattices demonstrate an efficient transfer strategy: after a fixed binarization of Potts spins into an effective Ising variable, a single GCN pretrained on the Ising model can localize the transition region for different $q$ values without retraining. Applying this strategy to spherical graphs, we find that curvature- and defect-induced connectivity irregularities produce only modest shifts in the inferred transition temperatures relative to planar baselines. Further analysis shows that the curvature-induced shift of the critical temperature is most pronounced at small $q$ and diminishes rapidly as $q$ increases; this trend is consistent with the physical picture that, in two dimensions, the Potts model undergoes a transition from a continuous phase transition to a weakly first-order one for $q>4$, accompanied by a pronounced reduction of the correlation length.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.07346v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们研究球面斐波那契图上的铁磁 $q$ 状态 Potts 模型。这些图是通过在球体上嵌入准均匀位点并通过选择的弦距离截止来定义交互来构建的，以产生近似四邻居连接的网络。通过将 Swendsen-Wang 集群蒙特卡罗模拟与直接对邻接结构和节点自旋进行操作的图卷积网络 (GCN) 相结合，我们开发了一个适用于规则平面晶格和弯曲、不规则球面图的统一相分类框架。平面晶格的基准测试证明了一种有效的转移策略：在 Potts 自旋的固定二值化为有效的 Ising 变量之后，在 Ising 模型上预训练的单个 GCN 可以定位不同 $q$ 值的过渡区域，而无需重新训练。将此策略应用于球面图，我们发现曲率和缺陷引起的连通性不规则性仅使推断的转变温度相对于平面基线产生适度的变化。进一步分析表明，曲率引起的临界温度变化在较小的 q$ 时最为明显，并随着 $q$ 的增大而迅速减小；这一趋势与物理图像一致，即在二维中，Potts 模型经历了从连续相变到 $q>4$ 的弱一阶相变的转变，同时伴随着相关长度的显着减小。
