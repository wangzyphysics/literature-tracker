## Abstract
arXiv:2512.22426v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Reconstructing time-resolved flow fields from temporally sparse velocimetry measurements is critical for characterizing many complex thermal-fluid systems. We introduce a machine learning framework for uncertainty-aware flow reconstruction using sparse variational Gaussian processes in the Kolmogorov-Arnold network topology (SVGP-KAN). This approach extends the classical foundations of Linear Stochastic Estimation (LSE) and Spectral Analysis Modal Methods (SAMM) while enabling principled epistemic uncertainty quantification. We perform a systematic comparison of our framework with the classical reconstruction methods as well as Kalman filtering. Using synthetic data from pulsed impingement jet flows, we assess performance across fractional PIV sampling rates ranging from 0.5% to 10%. Evaluation metrics include reconstruction error, generalization gap, structure preservation, and uncertainty calibration. Our SVGP-KAN methods achieve reconstruction accuracy comparable to established methods, while also providing well-calibrated uncertainty estimates that reliably indicate when and where predictions degrade. The results demonstrate a robust, data-driven framework for flow field reconstruction with meaningful uncertainty quantification and offer practical guidance for experimental design in periodic flows.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.22426v1 公告类型：新 摘要：从时间稀疏测速测量中重建时间分辨流场对于表征许多复杂的热流体系统至关重要。我们引入了一种机器学习框架，用于在 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络拓扑 (SVGP-KAN) 中使用稀疏变分高斯过程进行不确定性感知流重建。这种方法扩展了线性随机估计 (LSE) 和谱分析模态方法 (SAMM) 的经典基础，同时实现了有原则的认知不确定性量化。我们对我们的框架与经典重建方法以及卡尔曼滤波进行了系统比较。使用脉冲冲击射流的合成数据，我们评估了 0.5% 至 10% 的分数 PIV 采样率的性能。评估指标包括重建误差、泛化差距、结构保留和不确定性校准。我们的 SVGP-KAN 方法实现了与现有方法相当的重建精度，同时还提供了经过良好校准的不确定性估计，可以可靠地指示预测何时何地退化。结果展示了一个强大的、数据驱动的流场重建框架，具有有意义的不确定性量化，并为周期性流的实验设计提供了实用指导。
