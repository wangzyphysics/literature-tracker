## Abstract
Gold-catalyzed tandem reactions have been shown to be an all- round synthetic approach to the production of heterocyclic systems, which are of great relevance in the pharmaceutical and materials industries. The purpose is to describe previously unknown over 140 studies regarding the gold-catalyzed tandem reactions and review the progress since the beginning of the decade of 2010th. Main results focus on the formation of biologically active species, for instance, 2-Indenylindole (3-(1H-inden-2-yl)-1H-indoles) with noted activity against different cancer cells; this shows the versatility of the reactions under discussion in medicinal chemistry. This emphasizes that gold (I) catalysts are useful for several transformations such as 1,2-indole migration, C3-propargylation of indoles, synthesis of oxazoles, and fluorinated pyrazoles with moderate to good yields 55– 87%. Dataset at the mechanistic level is remarkably informative towards the catalytic cycle and intermediate species where it has been confirmed that gold(I) catalysis fixes reactive intermediates highly effectively or in other words the species necessary for the bond construct. For example, the 1,2- indole migration that has been prompted by propargylic indoles have been found to furnish intricate indoles that play an essential role in medicinal chemistry. This review is therefore novel in that it provides a state-of-the-art account of gold catalysis with a concern on gold (II) complexes that could lead to new catalytic manifolds and possible improvements in reactivity on tandem methods. Further, it considers the consequences of designing the catalyst and the optimization methods that are used to overcome the problems, for instance, catalyst deactivation and substrate constraint. More potential work should be directed to the improvement of these reactions and the stability of the reaction conditions to broaden the use of gold catalysis in chemical reactions.

## 摘要（中文）
金催化串联反应已被证明是一种生产杂环系统的全面合成方法，这在制药和材料工业中具有重要意义。目的是描述 140 多项有关金催化串联反应的先前未知的研究，并回顾自 2010 世纪初以来的进展。主要结果集中于生物活性物质的形成，例如对不同癌细胞具有显着活性的 2-茚基吲哚 (3-(1H-inden-2-yl)-1H-indoles)；这显示了药物化学中讨论的反应的多功能性。这强调了金 (I) 催化剂可用于多种转化，例如 1,2-吲哚迁移、吲哚的 C3-炔丙基化、恶唑的合成和氟化吡唑，产率中等至良好，产率为 55-87%。机械层面的数据集对于催化循环和中间体物种具有非常丰富的信息，其中已证实金（I）催化可以高度有效地固定反应性中间体，或者换句话说，固定键结构所需的物种。例如，已发现由炔丙基吲哚引起的 1,2-吲哚迁移可提供在药物化学中发挥重要作用的复杂吲哚。因此，这篇综述的新颖之处在于它提供了金催化的最先进的描述，并关注金（II）络合物，这可能导致新的催化歧管和串联方法反应活性的可能改进。此外，它还考虑了设计催化剂的后果以及用于克服问题的优化方法，例如催化剂失活和底物约束。更多潜在的工作应该针对这些反应的改进和反应条件的稳定性，以扩大金催化在化学反应中的应用。
