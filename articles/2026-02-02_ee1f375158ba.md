## Abstract
arXiv:2601.22933v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Plastic deformation in amorphous solids is carried by localized shear transformations that self-organize into avalanches. In amorphous carbon modeled with a machine-learned interatomic potential, we find that the energetics and organization of these avalanches can be resolved by systematically following the underlying energy landscape. With a pseudo-arclength numerical continuation framework, we decompose avalanches into constituent shear transformations and determine their strain-dependent energetics. Our analysis shows that, prior to onset, avalanches have a latent structure that consists of well-separated local minima. We further demonstrate that arclength continuation yields an event driven framework for following avalanche dynamics, eliminating time-step effects on statistical avalanche properties such as distributions of stress drops.

arXiv:2601.22933v1 公告类型：新 摘要：非晶固体中的塑性变形是通过自组织成雪崩的局部剪切变换来实现的。在用机器学习的原子间势建模的无定形碳中，我们发现这些雪崩的能量和组织可以通过系统地遵循潜在的能量景观来解决。通过伪弧长数值延拓框架，我们将雪崩分解为成分剪切变换并确定其应变相关的能量学。我们的分析表明，在雪崩发生之前，雪崩具有由分离良好的局部最小值组成的潜在结构。我们进一步证明，弧长连续产生了一个事件驱动框架，用于跟踪雪崩动力学，消除了统计雪崩特性（例如应力降分布）的时间步长影响。
