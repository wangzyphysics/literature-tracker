## Abstract
arXiv:2601.00199v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: We develop a reduced-order operator-learning framework for forward and inverse band-structure design of two-dimensional photonic crystals with binary, pixel-based $p4m$-symmetric unit cells. We construct a POD--DeepONet surrogate for the discrete band map along the standard high-symmetry path by coupling a POD trunk extracted from high-fidelity finite-element band snapshots with a neural branch network that predicts reduced coefficients. This architecture yields a compact and differentiable forward model that is tailored to the underlying Bloch eigenvalue discretization. We establish continuity of the discrete band map on the relaxed design space and prove a uniform approximation property of the POD--DeepONet surrogate, leading to a natural decomposition of the total surrogate error into POD truncation and network approximation contributions. Building on this forward surrogate, we formulate two end-to-end neural inverse design procedures, namely dispersion-to-structure and band-gap inverse design, with training objectives that combine data misfit, binarity promotion, and supervised regularization to address the intrinsic non-uniqueness of the inverse mapping and to enable stable gradient-based optimization in the relaxed space. Our numerical results show that the proposed framework achieves accurate forward predictions and produces effective inverse designs on practical high-contrast, pixel-based photonic layouts.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.00199v1 公告类型：新 摘要：我们开发了一种降阶算子学习框架，用于具有二元、基于像素的 $p4m$ 对称晶胞的二维光子晶体的正向和逆向能带结构设计。我们通过将从高保真有限元能带快照中提取的 POD 主干与预测减少系数的神经分支网络耦合，构建了沿着标准高对称路径的离散能带图的 POD-DeepONet 代理。该架构产生了一个紧凑且可微分的正向模型，该模型是针对底层 Bloch 特征值离散化而定制的。我们在松弛设计空间上建立了离散能带图的连续性，并证明了 POD（DeepONet 代理）的均匀近似属性，从而将总代理误差自然分解为 POD 截断和网络近似贡献。在此前向代理的基础上，我们制定了两个端到端的神经逆设计程序，即色散到结构和带隙逆设计，其训练目标结合了数据失配、二值提升和监督正则化，以解决逆映射的内在非唯一性，并在松弛空间中实现稳定的基于梯度的优化。我们的数值结果表明，所提出的框架实现了准确的前向预测，并在实际的高对比度、基于像素的光子布局上产生了有效的逆向设计。
