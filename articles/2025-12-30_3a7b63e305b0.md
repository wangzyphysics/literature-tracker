## Abstract
arXiv:2512.23034v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The aim of this study was to investigate the impact of ambient air plasma treatment on both sunflower seed germination and early steps of seedling development under water stress. Dry seeds were exposed to a cold atmospheric plasma (CAP) generated in a dielectric barrier device where excited molecular nitrogen and ozone were detected by optical emission spectroscopy and mass spectrometry respectively. Interestingly, we explain the crucial role of gap's accuracy when treating seeds with CAP, especially to improve interaction between seeds and plasma and therefore ensure an efficient treatment. CAP significantly improved the germination rates of seeds of dormant and non-dormant sunflower genotypes under water stress, demonstrating its efficiency in alleviating seed dormancy and in improving germination under suboptimal conditions. Furthermore, greenhouse experiments demonstrated that plasma treatment also stimulated seedling development under water stress conditions. These findings highlight the potential of CAP treatment as an effective approach to promote the whole process of emergence of crop species.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.23034v1 公告类型：新 摘要：本研究的目的是调查水分胁迫下环境空气等离子体处理对向日葵种子萌发和幼苗发育早期阶段的影响。将干燥种子暴露于介电屏障装置中产生的冷大气等离子体（CAP），其中分别通过光学发射光谱法和质谱法检测激发的分子氮和臭氧。有趣的是，我们解释了间隙准确性在用 CAP 处理种子时的关键作用，尤其是改善种子和血浆之间的相互作用，从而确保有效的处理。 CAP显着提高了休眠和非休眠向日葵基因型种子在水分胁迫下的发芽率，证明了其在缓解种子休眠和改善次优条件下发芽方面的效率。此外，温室实验表明，等离子处理还可以刺激水分胁迫条件下的幼苗发育。这些发现凸显了 CAP 处理作为促进作物物种出苗整个过程的有效方法的潜力。
