## Abstract
Author(s): Carsten Hartmann, Philipp C. Böttcher, David Gross, and Dirk Witthaut To avoid power disruptions, all generators in a power grid must be synchronized. Here, the authors propose a mathematical approach to calculate all stable states of the lossless real power flow equations, which provide insights into the widely used linear power flow approximation and factors that limit the stability of power grids. [PRX Energy 3, 043004] Published Thu Oct 31, 2024
