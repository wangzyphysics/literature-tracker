## Abstract
arXiv:2601.06960v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: A recently proposed generalised continuum theory of curved dislocations describes the spatial and temporal evolution of statistically stored and geometrically necessary dislocation densities as well as the curvature. The dynamics follow from a scalar plastic potential that constrains the allowed velocity fields and leads to a phase field like formulation with a nontrivial mobility function. Although conceptually related to strain gradient plasticity, the theory differs by introducing an intrinsic, evolving length scale given by the dislocation spacing. In this paper, we determine three key material independent parameters of this continuum theory by quantitatively comparing its predictions with discrete dislocation dynamics (DDD) simulations. To achieve this, we impose a narrow impenetrable wall inside the simulation volume, which blocks dislocation motion and generates characteristic spatial variations of the dislocation density fields under external loading. We show that for this geometry, the continuum equations reduce to a form that can be solved efficiently via direct numerical integration. The resulting stationary distributions of total and geometrically necessary dislocation densities are then compared to extensive 2D and 3D DDD simulations. This comparison allows us to extract the parameters that govern the back stress, the density gradient coupling, and the flow stress relation. Our results demonstrate that the continuum theory quantitatively captures the DDD observed structure of the dislocation pile up near the wall and therefore provides a reliable mesoscale description. The wall loading setup further serves as a benchmark problem to validate numerical implementations of the continuum theory in more general geometries.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2601.06960v1 公告类型：新 摘要：最近提出的弯曲位错的广义连续统理论描述了统计存储的和几何上必要的位错密度以及曲率的空间和时间演化。动力学遵循标量塑性势，它限制了允许的速度场，并导致类似相场的公式具有非平凡的迁移率函数。尽管在概念上与应变梯度塑性相关，但该理论的不同之处在于引入了由位错间距给出的内在的、不断变化的长度尺度。在本文中，我们通过将其预测与离散位错动力学（DDD）模拟进行定量比较，确定了该连续介质理论的三个关键材料独立参数。为了实现这一目标，我们在模拟体积内施加了一个狭窄的不可穿透的壁，它阻止位错运动并在外部载荷下产生位错密度场的特征空间变化。我们证明，对于这种几何形状，连续介质方程可以简化为可以通过直接数值积分有效求解的形式。然后将所得总位错密度和几何必要位错密度的平稳分布与广泛的 2D 和 3D DDD 模拟进行比较。这种比较使我们能够提取控制背应力、密度梯度耦合和流动应力关系的参数。我们的结果表明，连续介质理论定量地捕获了 DDD 观察到的壁附近位错堆积的结构，因此提供了可靠的介观尺度描述。壁加载设置进一步作为基准问题来验证连续介质理论在更一般的几何形状中的数值实现。
