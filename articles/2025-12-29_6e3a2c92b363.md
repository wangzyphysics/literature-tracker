## Abstract
arXiv:2512.06651v4 Announce Type: replace Abstract: An RF helical deflector is a type of electron and ion optics device that applies a time-dependent rotating transverse electric or magnetic field by means of time-dependent RF voltage applied on two opposite conducting helical structures (wires, ribbons or other) to deflect charged particles (a single, bunch or beam) in a circular or spiral path. It is a perspective indirect timing system being concurrent for reaching picosecond time resolution, and have promise being excellent candidate for high precision time-of-flight detection. As a timing system, it converts the temporal structure of an electron beam into a spatial pattern -- particularly, an ellipse in the case of a single-frequency RF voltage and continuous electron pencil beam. I propose a capacitor model of an RF helical deflector and compare it with the existing textbook model \cite{ZhigarevBook, Gevorgian2015}, interpret them and provide understanding of them. Furthermore, I analyze the latter, finding analytical formulas for the applied electric field, ellipse sizes (semi-axes) and rotation angle, lengths of the ellipse line, corresponding to the duration of electron pencil bunches or beams. The present article touches the topics of getting circle on resonance limit and of deflection sensitivity.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2512.06651v4 公告类型：替换 摘要：射频螺旋偏转器是一种电子和离子光学器件，它通过在两个相对的导电螺旋结构（导线、带状或其他）上施加随时间变化的射频电压来施加随时间变化的旋转横向电场或磁场，以在圆形或螺旋路径中偏转带电粒子（单个、束或束）。它是一种并行的透视间接计时系统，可达到皮秒时间分辨率，并有望成为高精度飞行时间检测的优秀候选者。作为一个定时系统，它将电子束的时间结构转换为空间图案——特别是在单频射频电压和连续电子笔束的情况下为椭圆形。我提出了射频螺旋偏转器的电容器模型，并将其与现有教科书模型\cite{ZhigarevBook，Gevorgian2015}进行比较，解释它们并提供对它们的理解。此外，我分析了后者，找到了与电子笔束或电子束的持续时间相对应的施加电场、椭圆尺寸（半轴）和旋转角度、椭圆线长度的分析公式。本文涉及共振极限圆和偏转灵敏度的主题。
