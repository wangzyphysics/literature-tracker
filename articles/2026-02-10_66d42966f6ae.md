## Abstract
arXiv:2511.08267v2 Announce Type: replace-cross Abstract: Superconducting transmon qubits are a promising platform for quantum computation, yet they face significant fidelity degradation due to connectivity noise, particularly in the intermediate coupling regime where noise levels are substantial. While prior works suggest that high fidelity requires operating in regimes with strongly suppressed noise, maintaining such conditions under practical experimental constraints remains challenging. To address this, we investigate quantum gate operations in fully connected transmon rings, examining both SWAP and general circuits. Our study reveals that fidelity can be significantly enhanced by tuning gate operation durations, with local maxima emerging even under strong noise conditions. These fidelity enhancements occur consistently across different qubit numbers and operation types, and for specific initial states -- particularly those with favorable symmetry or entanglement properties -- the achieved fidelities approach quantum error correction thresholds. Furthermore, we develop a supervised machine learning model that accurately predicts the optimal operation durations for new devices, enabling efficient optimization without extensive experimental simulations. These results provide a pathway toward robust quantum circuit design in noisy experimental environments.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2511.08267v2 公告类型：replace-cross 摘要：超导传输量子比特是一种很有前途的量子计算平台，但由于连接噪声，它们面临着显着的保真度下降，特别是在噪声水平很高的中间耦合区域。虽然先前的工作表明高保真度需要在噪声受到强烈抑制的情况下运行，但在实际实验限制下维持这种条件仍然具有挑战性。为了解决这个问题，我们研究了完全连接的传输环中的量子门操作，检查了交换和通用电路。我们的研究表明，通过调整门操作持续时间可以显着提高保真度，即使在强噪声条件下也会出现局部最大值。这些保真度增强在不同的量子位数量和操作类型中一致发生，并且对于特定的初始状态——特别是那些具有有利的对称性或纠缠特性的——所实现的保真度接近量子纠错阈值。此外，我们开发了一种监督机器学习模型，可以准确预测新设备的最佳运行持续时间，从而无需进行大量实验模拟即可实现高效优化。这些结果为在嘈杂的实验环境中实现稳健的量子电路设计提供了一条途径。
