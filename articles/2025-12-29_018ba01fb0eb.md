## Abstract
arXiv:2512.14795v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: The present theoretical study focuses on the structural, electronic and thermoelectric properties of PbTe, PbSe and their ternary alloys PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$, using the density functional theory (DFT) by the full potential linearised augmented plane wave (FP-LAPW) method implemented in Wien2k code. Structural properties were performed by using the generalized gradient approximation of Perdew Burke and Ernzenhof (GGA-PBE) scheme. The results show that the calculated lattice parameters are in good agreement with theoretical data previously obtained. For electronic properties, we noticed that for all the compounds of PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$, we have a direct band gap in L point. For thermoelectric properties, we used BoltzTraP2 code and Gibbs2 code. Our results show that the PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$ compounds have reached a value of 2.55 for the figure of merit, which indicates that our material is a good thermoelectric candidate.

arXiv:2512.14795v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：目前的理论研究重点是 PbTe、PbSe 及其三元合金 PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$ 的结构、电子和热电性能，采用 Wien2k 代码中实现的全势线性化增强平面波 (FP-LAPW) 方法的密度泛函理论 (DFT)。通过使用 Perdew Burke 和 Ernzenhof (GGA-PBE) 方案的广义梯度近似来计算结构特性。结果表明，计算的晶格参数与先前获得的理论数据吻合良好。对于电子性质，我们注意到对于 PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$ 的所有化合物，我们在 L 点有一个直接带隙。对于热电性质，我们使用了 BoltzTraP2 代码和 Gibbs2 代码。我们的结果表明，PbSe$_{x}$Te$_{1-x}$ 化合物的品质因数已达到 2.55，这表明我们的材料是一种良好的热电候选材料。
