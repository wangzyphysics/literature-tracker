## Abstract
arXiv:2602.11593v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Quantum spin rings represent fundamental model systems that exhibit distinctive quantum phenomena-such as quantum critical behavior and quasiparticle excitations-arising from their periodic boundary conditions and enhanced quantum fluctuations. Here, we report the on-surface synthesis and atomic-scale characterization of antiferromagnetic S=1/2 quantum spin rings composed of pristine and unmodified [2]triangulene units on a Au(111) surface. Using stepwise on-surface synthesis followed by STM tip-induced dehydrogenation, we precisely constructed cyclic five- and six-membered spin rings and investigated their spin states via scanning probe microscopy and multireference calculations. Nc-AFM imaging reveals that the six-membered ring retains a planar geometry, whereas the five-membered ring exhibits pronounced structural distortion. The six-membered ring hosts a uniform excitation gap that can be accurately described by a Heisenberg spin model and multireference CASCI calculations. In contrast, the distorted five-membered ring displays spin ground states with asymmetric spatial distributions due to degeneracy lifting induced by structural distortion. Our findings establish a versatile molecular platform for exploring correlated magnetism and quantum spin phenomena in cyclic organic magnetic architectures with disorder.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.11593v1 公告类型：新 摘要：量子自旋环代表了基本模型系统，这些系统表现出独特的量子现象，例如量子临界行为和准粒子激发，这些现象源于其周期性边界条件和增强的量子涨落。在这里，我们报告了反铁磁 S=1/2 量子自旋环的表面合成和原子尺度表征，该反铁磁 S=1/2 量子自旋环由 Au(111) 表面上原始且未修饰的 [2]triangulene 单元组成。通过逐步表面合成和 STM 尖端诱导脱氢，我们精确构建了环状五元和六元自旋环，并通过扫描探针显微镜和多参考计算研究了它们的自旋状态。 Nc-AFM 成像显示六元环保留了平面几何形状，而五元环则表现出明显的结构变形。六元环具有均匀的激发间隙，可以通过海森堡自旋模型和多参考 CASCI 计算准确描述。相比之下，扭曲的五元环由于结构扭曲引起的简并提升而显示出具有不对称空间分布的自旋基态。我们的研究结果建立了一个多功能分子平台，用于探索无序循环有机磁性结构中的相关磁性和量子自旋现象。
