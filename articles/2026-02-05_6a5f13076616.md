## Abstract
arXiv:2602.04301v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The Rashba-Edelstein effect (REE), which generates spin accumulation under an applied electric current, quantifies charge-to-spin conversion (CSC) efficiency in non-centrosymmetric systems. However, systematic investigations of REE in two-dimensional (2D) materials remain scarce. To address this gap, we perform a comprehensive symmetry analysis based on the 80 crystallographic layer groups, elucidating the relationship between materials' symmetries and the geometric characteristics of the REE response tensor. Our analysis identifies 13 distinct symmetry classes for the tensor and reveals all potential material candidates. Considering the requirement of strong spin-orbit coupling for a large REE response, we screen the C2DB database and identify 54 promising 2D materials. First-principles calculations demonstrate that the largest REE response coefficients in these materials exceed those reported for other 2D systems by an order of magnitude, indicating exceptionally high CSC efficiency. Focusing on three representative materials, including HgI2, AgTlP2Se6 and BrGaTe, we show that their large response coefficients can be well explained by effective kp models and the characteristic spin textures around high-symmetry points in momentum space. This work provides a systematic framework and identifies high-performance candidates, paving the way for future exploration of REE-driven CSC in 2D materials.

## 摘要（中文）
arXiv:2602.04301v1 公告类型：新 摘要：Rashba-Edelstein 效应 (REE) 在施加电流下产生自旋累积，可量化非中心对称系统中的电荷自旋转换 (CSC) 效率。然而，对二维 (2D) 材料中稀土元素的系统研究仍然很少。为了解决这一差距，我们基于 80 个晶体层组进行了全面的对称性分析，阐明了材料的对称性与 REE 响应张量的几何特征之间的关系。我们的分析确定了张量的 13 个不同的对称类别，并揭示了所有潜在的候选材料。考虑到大稀土响应需要强自旋轨道耦合，我们筛选了 C2DB 数据库并确定了 54 种有前途的二维材料。第一原理计算表明，这些材料中最大的 REE 响应系数比其他 2D 系统报告的响应系数高出一个数量级，表明 CSC 效率极高。重点关注三种代表性材料，包括 HgI2、AgTlP2Se6 和 BrGaTe，我们发现它们的大响应系数可以通过有效的 kp 模型和动量空间中高对称点周围的特征自旋纹理得到很好的解释。这项工作提供了一个系统框架并确定了高性能候选材料，为未来在二维材料中探索稀土驱动的 CSC 铺平了道路。
