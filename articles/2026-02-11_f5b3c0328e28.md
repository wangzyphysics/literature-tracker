## Abstract
arXiv:2511.23462v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: Control of the temporal waveform of photons produced during spontaneous emission from single quantum emitters provides a crucial tool in the establishment of hybrid quantum systems, optimization of quantum state transfer protocols and mitigation of effects due interferometric instability for network architectures based on flying qubits. We describe a method to generate photons of any temporal waveform from emitters of any excited state lifetime, limited only by the timing resolution of control hardware. We show how the temporal waveform of photons can be controlled by deterministically varying the population of an excited state which undergoes spontaneous emission. Our broadly applicable approach has only two requirements for a candidate quantum emitter: modulation of the (1) amplitude and (2) relative phase of a field coupling a ground state to the excited manifold. We detail how to identify optimal excitation pulses by employing variational algorithms to feed back on atomic populations. Additionally, we develop Quantum Monte Carlo based tools to determine photon-number statistics and establish techniques to identify optimal excitation strengths and post-selection thresholds for photon generation protocols. We situate our work in the context of other prior research on bespoke single photon sources and networking including post-emission pulse shaping, temporal gating and cavity-based methods. In comparison, our free-space process has greater flexibility in producing any waveform, requires less infrastructure, and can be readily applied across a wide range of quantum emitters. We discuss the applications and limits of this technique, including how increasing photon emission probabilities affects achievable temporal-mode overlap fidelities between emitted and target photon waveforms.

arXiv:2511.23462v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：控制单个量子发射器自发发射过程中产生的光子的时间波形，为建立混合量子系统、优化量子状态转移协议以及减轻基于飞行量子位的网络架构的干涉不稳定性带来的影响提供了关键工具。我们描述了一种从任何激发态寿命的发射器生成任何时间波形的光子的方法，仅受控制硬件的定时分辨率的限制。我们展示了如何通过确定性地改变自发发射的激发态的数量来控制光子的时间波形。我们广泛适用的方法对候选量子发射器只有两个要求：调制（1）振幅和（2）将基态耦合到激发流形的场的相对相位。我们详细介绍了如何通过采用变分算法反馈原子群来识别最佳激发脉冲。此外，我们开发了基于量子蒙特卡罗的工具来确定光子数统计数据，并建立了确定光子生成协议的最佳激发强度和后选择阈值的技术。我们将我们的工作置于其他先前关于定制单光子源和网络的研究的背景下，包括发射后脉冲整形、时间选通和基于腔的方法。相比之下，我们的自由空间过程在产生任何波形方面具有更大的灵活性，需要更少的基础设施，并且可以轻松应用于各种量子发射器。我们讨论了该技术的应用和限制，包括增加光子发射概率如何影响发射和目标光子波形之间可实现的时间模式重叠保真度。
