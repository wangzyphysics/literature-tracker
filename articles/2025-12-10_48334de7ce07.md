## Abstract
Nature Reviews Chemistry, Published online: 10 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41570-025-00787-3 Ahead of his 80th birthday, Steven V. Ley, Professor of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge, discussed his career from drawing chemicals by hand to his work generating complex natural product architectures using machines.
