## Abstract
arXiv:2511.22592v2 Announce Type: replace Abstract: High-Temperature Superconductors (HTS) such as YBa2Cu3O7-delta (YBCO) are essential for next-generation Tokamak fusion reactors, where Rare-Earth Barium Copper Oxides (REBCO) form the functional layers in HTS magnets. Because YBCO's superconductivity depends strongly on oxygen stoichiometry and defect structure, atomistic simulations can provide crucial insight into radiation-damage mechanisms and pathways to maintain material performance. In this work, we develop and benchmark four Machine-Learned Interatomic Potentials (MLPs) for YBCO: the Atomic Cluster Expansion (ACE), the Message-Passing Atomic Cluster Expansion (MACE), the Gaussian Approximation Potential (GAP), and the Tabulated Gaussian Approximation Potential (tabGAP), trained on an extensive Density Functional Theory (DFT) database explicitly designed to include irradiation-damaged-like configurations. The resulting models achieve DFT-level accuracy across a wide range of atomic environments, faithfully capturing the interatomic forces relevant to radiation damage processes. Among the tested models, MACE delivers the highest accuracy, although at greater computational cost, while ACE and tabGAP provide an excellent balance between efficiency and fidelity. These machine-learned potentials establish a robust foundation for large-scale molecular dynamics simulations of radiation-induced defect evolution in complex superconducting materials

arXiv:2511.22592v2 公告类型：替换 摘要：YBa2Cu3O7-δ (YBCO) 等高温超导体 (HTS) 对于下一代托卡马克聚变反应堆至关重要，其中稀土钡铜氧化物 (REBCO) 形成 HTS 磁体的功能层。由于 YBCO 的超导性在很大程度上取决于氧化学计量和缺陷结构，因此原子模拟可以提供对辐射损伤机制和维持材料性能的途径的重要见解。在这项工作中，我们为 YBCO 开发了四种机器学习原子间势（MLP）并进行了基准测试：原子簇扩展（ACE）、消息传递原子簇扩展（MACE）、高斯近似势（GAP）和表格高斯近似势（tabGAP），并在明确设计的广泛密度泛函理论（DFT）数据库上进行训练包括类似辐射损坏的配置。由此产生的模型在广泛的原子环境中实现了 DFT 级精度，忠实地捕捉与辐射损伤过程相关的原子间力。在测试的模型中，MACE 提供了最高的准确度，但计算成本较高，而 ACE 和 tabGAP 在效率和保真度之间提供了出色的平衡。这些机器学习的势为复杂超导材料中辐射引起的缺陷演化的大规模分子动力学模拟奠定了坚实的基础
